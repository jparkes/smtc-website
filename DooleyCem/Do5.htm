Paris Dooley memorial

DOOLEY, Paris F., 23 Aug 1800 - 12 Apr 1830. Tombstone reads: 
"Sacred to the Memory of Paris Dooley who was born August 23, 1800 and departed this life April the 12 1830"
Paris F Dooley was the son of James Dooley and his second wife Rachel Rutledge Moore Dooley. He was born in Lincoln County, KY and migrated to Maury County, TN with his parents about 1806. He married Cynthia Eliza Davidson 8-12-1822 in Maury County TN and they had four children before he died at the age of 30.
