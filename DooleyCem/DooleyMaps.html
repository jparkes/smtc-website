DOOLEY CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Go south on Highland Avenue to the church property at 2302 Highland Street, take the private drive called Grace Lane beside the parking lot. At the last house on the left, turn left down the driveway and you will see the fenced cemetery on your right. By Lauri Miller, 2007.
Photo & Information contributed by Lauri Dooley Miller of Texas, 5/21/2007.
