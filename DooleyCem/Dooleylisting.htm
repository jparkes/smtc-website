DOOLEY CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Directions 1986:
- Located in Columbia. Go south on Highland Avenue to the Church of the Nazarene. The cemetery is behind the church in a grove of trees almost surrounded by residences but in an enclosed fence on the Church Property. By Fred L. Hawkins.
Directions 2007:
- Located in Columbia. Go south on Highland Avenue to the church property at 2302 Highland Street, take the private drive called Grace Lane beside the parking lot. At the last house on the left, turn left down the driveway and you will see the fenced cemetery on your right. By Lauri Miller, 2007.

The Dooley Cemetery of Columbia, Maury County, TN
Dooley Cemetery was the burial ground of the James Dooley family beginning in the early 1800's. William Dooley, son of James was a founding Justice of Maury County, TN. The cemetery is located behind a church at 2302 Highland Ave Columbia, TN. It holds the remains of James Dooley, a revolutionary war patriot, William Dooley, a patriot of the war of 1812 and Martin Pemberton Dooley (son of William) a Civil War patriot and many other Dooley relations.
