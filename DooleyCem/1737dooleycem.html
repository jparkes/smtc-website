Wayne, These are the best shots of my ancestor's Grave in Columbia, TN.

Dooley Cemetery, Columbia, Maury County, TN
Tombstone reads "Sacred to the Memory of James Dooley who departed this life June 17, 1824 in his 78th year of age"
