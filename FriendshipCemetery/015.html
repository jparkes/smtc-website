FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


WARDEN, W. S. (William Samuel), 20 Jul 1864 - 24 Nov 1894. married to Susan Mitchell until William's death and then Susan married Lee Holt and had children by that marriage also. (son of James N. & Mary A. Ramsey Warden) , died of Consumptive Chill, Section 1.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 10/21/2003.
