FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


WELLS, John F. (Franklin)., 25 Feb 1842 - 18 Dec 1917. "Father", (Son David & Elizabeth P. Fitzpatrick Wells; (3rd Infantry, C. S. A.), husband of Martha L. Williams Wells), Sec 1 
and
WELLS, Martha L., 17 Dec 1846 - 5 Sep 1917. "Mother", (daughter of W. J. & Martha L. Gilmer Williams.), Sec 1.
Would be glad to share what I (John Woods) know: John & Martha L. Wells had nine children. Martha's maiden name was Williams, and she was born in Giles Co., Tennessee; my great grandfather was born in Maury Co., Tennessee; he died in Navarro Co., Texas. His parents: David & Eliz.. (Fitzpatrick) Wells; her parents: Wm. J. & Martha Louise (Gilmer) Williams. All of John & Martha's children were born in Maury County.  
[James Wood 11/29/2007]
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 10/21/2003. Update by John Woods 11/29/2007. Revised photo dated 27 Dec 2006.
