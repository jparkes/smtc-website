FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HOLT, Katherine, 16 Dec 1909 - 21 Jun 2004, (same marker base with Lucy Holt), Sec 2
HOLT, Lucy, 29 Apr 1912 - 2 Mar 1999, (same marker base with Katherine Holt), Sec 2
Katherine Holt was a Maury Co. native: daughter of Lee Holt & Susie Mitchell Holt; preceded in death by three sisters, Alvy Richardson, Lucy Holt & Mae Straughan; and three brothers, John Holt, Lee Holt & Jerry Holt.
