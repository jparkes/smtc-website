WEBB, Clarence Andy, Jr., 5 Oct 1931 - 21 Jun 2013. Section 3. (s/o Clarence Webb Sr. & Josie Vaughan Webb.) obituary

Clarence Andy Webb Jr., age 81 and a resident of Parkview Drive in Columbia, TN, passed away Friday, June 21, 2013 at Maury Regional Medical Center.
Clarence was born on October 5, 1931 in Lewis County to the late Clarence Webb Sr. and the late Josie Vaughan Webb. He worked for many years as assistant plant manager of Pride Die Casting in Chicago, Illinois. After retiring, he was working maintenance at Burchell Properties. He was a hard-working "Mr. Fix-it man" and a wonderful father, grandfather, and great grandfather who was always there whenever needed. He was a pitcher, "lefty" for the Mynders School and was an avid Atanta Braves fan.

Clarence is survived by his 2 daughters: one of Columbia, and one of Lavergne, Tennessee; a son and daughter-in-law of Augusta, Georgia; 12 grandchildren; a close friend, 14 great grandchildren, and a host of nieces and nephews.
In addition to his parents, he is preceded in death by his sisters: Bertha Webb Bradford, Virginia Webb McCrary; brother: Harvie Webb; and one grandson: Donnie Pickard.
