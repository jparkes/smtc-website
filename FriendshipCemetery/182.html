RICHARDSON, Sally Hays, 23 Feb 1940 - 13 Aug. 2015. ( married 2 Jan 1959 to Don Gaylon Richardson, Sec 2 obit

Sally Hays Richardson Barnes, age 75 died quietly Thursday, August 13, 2015 at her home in Lewisburg after an extended illness.
She was a lifelong resident of Marshall County, Tennessee. She was preceded in death by her parents, James Harvey and Vadie Tennison Hays, her brother, James Tennison Hays, her first husband, Gaylon Richardson, son, James B. (Jim) Richardson and grandson, Gaylon James Richardson.
She is survived by her second husband, Earnest Colie Barnes, one daughter, Kimberly Richardson (Steve) Thomason both of Lewisburg, and one step-daughter, Beth (Zach) Mitchell of Murfreesboro, five grandchildren, Angela Summers of Martin, Tennessee, Gaylon Thomason, Katie Hargrove, and Hays Richardson all of Lewisburg, and Maliah Vasquez of Mt. Pleasant, four step-grandsons, Caleb, Christopher, and Connor Mitchell and Benji Barnes all of Murfreesboro, and eight great grandchildren.
Mrs. Barnes was a 1957 graduate of Marshall County High School and attended David Lipscomb College. She began her career in banking in 1958 at First National Bank in Lewisburg and retired in 2002 from Farmers Bank in Lewisburg.
She was a member of East Commerce Baptist Church for greater than fifty years where she taught children�s Sunday School for many years.

Visitation will be held from 4:00 p.m. until 8:00 p.m. Saturday and Sunday from 12:00 p.m. until the funeral hour.
