FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


From the road we are looking up the hill at the sign. The sign denotes the date 1833 when Mr. John Toombs gave six acres of land to the congregation to also establish this cemetery. Behind the sign is section 2. Section 1 is behind that further up the hill. Section 3 is on our left and further back. The local caretakers and folks watch closely over this cemetery.  Photo & Info by WA 10/10/06
