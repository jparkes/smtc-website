FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

CHEATHAM, Frank, 2 Sep 1853 - 25 Dec 1929 
and
CHEATHAM, Josie, 22 Jul 1862 - 23 Nov 1926, Section 1.
The Frank Cheatham's lived on Covey Hollow Road on the McCains (east) side of the hill in an old southern white house. Just down from the house was a spring that gushed cold fresh water. The house was on the right (south) side of the road just east (400 or so yards) from the intersection of Canterbury and Covey Hollow Road. The old house was blown away by a killer tornado that swept thru there in the 1970s or 1980s. Of course as you can see they did not reside there at that time. [WA 10/11/06
Information & photo by Wayne Austin, 9/16/2006.
