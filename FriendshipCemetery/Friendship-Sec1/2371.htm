FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

MASSEY, James, 13 Sep 1839 - 2 May 1902. (C.S.A.) "Enlisted in Peter Turney's 1st Tenn Reg, 1861, served to close of 1865; died 2 May 1902 at Reunion of National Assoc. of Conf. Soldiers at Dallas, Texas.", Sec 1
MASSEY, Martha M. Denham, 30 Dec 1845 - 2 Aug 1922. "Wife of James Massey.", Sec 1
The photographer missed imaging the other three sides of this memorial where I believe the inscriptions for James & Martha Massey reside. Inscription we have: "In grateful remembrance of our Confederate Dead, In my Fathers House are many mansions. MASSEY"
