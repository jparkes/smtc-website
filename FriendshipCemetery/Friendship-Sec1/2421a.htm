FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The stone on the right next to the road is their memorial. These ladies were sisters:
RENFRO, Delia May, 8 Dec 1880 - 25 Nov 1956. (daughter of W. C. & Emma Davis Renfro.), Sec 1
RENFRO, Minnie S., 18 Apr 1892 - omitted death date, Sec 1

The Graves memorial is the stone behind the front row of Renfro family memorials in the top photo:
GRAVES, G. G. (George Gabriel), 9 Nov 1849 - 11 Apr 1924. (Son John & Rosa Collier Graves of Giles Co.), Sec 1
GRAVES, Mrs. Ruth D., 28 Feb 1855 - (13 Sep 1932.) (Daughter of Henry & Celia Fitzpatrick Cheatham; wife of G. G. Graves.), Sec 1

The Hattie Lee Graves memorial is just to the left of G.G. & Mrs R.D. Graves memorial in the top photo:
GRAVES, Hattie Lee, 21 Dec 1888 - 5 Mar 1919. 'Daughter of G. G. & R. D.", Sec 1
