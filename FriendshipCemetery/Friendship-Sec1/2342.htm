FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

CREWS, Margaret, 17 Apr 1840 - 13 Jan 1922. (daughter of Thomas & Mary F. Brown Ashton; wife of Polk Crews.), Sec 1
From Rosalie Ann Beasley: "Margaret's full name is Margaret Elizabeth Jane Ashton. She married William Polk Crews (also known as Polk Crews) on 11 March 1866, and they had three children: Judith Ann Frances Crews who married E. W. Richardson, Josephine Albina Crews who married Samuel Jones Graves, and William Oscar Crews who married Maggie S. Wells."
Information & photo by Wayne Austin, 27 Dec 2006.Added 18 Dec 2008. Update from Rosalie Ann Beasley 4 Feb 2009. Sent in by MB McClain.
