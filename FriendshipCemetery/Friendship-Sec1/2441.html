FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Two Funeral Markers from Oaks & Nichols, one with Alice Brown 1933 - 2004 & another with just the surname Brown remaining which has been there much longer than 2004. These are between James Jett & Luther D. Anderson. Could the fallen marker on our right be that for H. H. Brown Mr Fred Hawkins found in 1986?
