(Washington, D.C. native; d/o Dr. Leland DePue and Marie Robinson DePue; mar. 15 Jun 1985 to John Wayne II; art teacher a Culleoka Unit School; Bachelor of Fine Arts from Old Dominion University and Masters in Education from Nova Southeastern University; preceded in death by son, Joseph Wayne.) NW corner of Sec 3
Dr. Carol Lee DePue Wayne, Ed.D, age 55, a resident of Norma Court in Culleoka, passed away on Tuesday, March 9, at Maury Regional Medical Center.

Biography
Funeral services for Carol were conducted Monday at 2:00 P.M. in the chapel of Williams Funeral Home in Columbia with Bro. Derek Hunter, Pastor Roebin Tyler and Bro. Larry Purvis officiating. Burial followed in the Friendship Cemetery in Culleoka.


Carol was born July 18, 1954 in Washington, DC, the daughter of the late Dr. Leland DePue and Marie Robinson DePue. On June 15, 1985 she was married to John Wayne II who resides in Culleoka. Carol was an art teacher at Culleoka Unit School for 11 years. She received her Bachelor of Fine Arts from Old Dominion University and her Masters in Education from Nova Southeastern University and was completing work on her doctorate with Nova Southeastern University and they will be awarding her degree posthumously. Carol was an active member of Grace Church of the Nazarene. In addition to her parents, Carol was preceded in death by a son, Joseph Wayne.

Survivors in addition to her husband include her children: Tami Rhodes of Mesa, AZ, John Wayne III of Casper, WY, Misti McAniff of Ft. Pallbearers were Stephen Weems, Stephen Wayne, Brian McKenzie, Larry Purvis, Byron Perry and Keith Farmer. Honorary pallbearers were the teachers at the Culleoka Unit School.
