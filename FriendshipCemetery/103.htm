FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Unknown upright stone crumbled with time, near the west central section. There is a large open area with many fieldstones marking graves near here. Might have been slave graves or not? During the time prior to ending slavery the slaves and owners were interred in separate sections of the same cemetery. I am unaware of the history of the layout respectful of that information in this cemetery. Section 1 (Old section).
