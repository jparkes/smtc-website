ANDERSON, Luther Dixon, 19 Feb 1902 - 7 Apr 1987. (Native of Hickman Co. and the ; son of Newt & Emily Scott Anderson; husband of Clara Ethel Matthews Anderson), Sec 1.

Mrs. Clara Ethel Matthews Anderson, 96, widow of Luther Anderson and a resident of the Culleoka Community, died Sunday, July 18, 2004 at Maury Regional Hospital.
Funeral services will be conducted Tuesday at 2:00 p.m. at Oakes & Nichols with Brother Tim McHenry officiating. Burial will be in the Friendship Cemetery in Culleoka. The family will visit with friends Monday from 4:00 to 8:00 p.m. at the funeral home.
The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Ben Matthews and Alberta Head Matthews. She was a member of the Culleoka Church of Christ.
Survivors include two daughters, Edna (Bill) Ambrose of Culleoka and Evelyn Ring of Lewisburg; a son, Thomas (Dot) Anderson of Columbia; daughter-in-law, Ella Anderson of Columbia; nine grandchildren; fourteen great-grandchildren; five great-great-grandchildren.
