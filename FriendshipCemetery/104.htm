FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Unknown stones, near the lower western section. There is a large open area with many fieldstones marking graves near here. I did not photograph them but can count several in the above photo. Might have been slave graves or not? During the time prior to ending slavery the slaves and owners were interred in separate sections of the same cemetery. I am unaware of the history or the layout respectful of that information in this cemetery. Section 1 (Old section).
Information & photo by Wayne Austin, 9/16/2006.
