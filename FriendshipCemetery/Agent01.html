FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

AGENT, Thomas Wayne, 25 May 1955 - 11 Oct 2012. (Maury Co. native; s/o Lester Nathaniel & Willie Mae Fitzgerald Agent.) obit

Mr. Thomas Wayne Agent, age 57 of Lewisburg, died Thursday morning, October 11, 2012 at his residence.

Funeral services will be conducted Saturday at 1:00 PM from the Lord's House of Prayer in Lewisburg. The family will receive friends from 10:00 AM until the funeral hour at the church. Burial will follow in Friendship Cemetery at Culleoka. The family is being assisted by Heritage Funeral Home.

Mr. Agent was a native of Maury County and son of the late Lester Nathaniel and Willie Mae Fitzgerald Agent.

He is survived by two daughters: Laura Mae Agent Aviles of Lewisburg and Audrey Anne Agent of Mt. Pleasant; 4 sisters: Joyce Fautt of Lewisburg, Ruby Cates of Chapel Hill, Loreene Collins and Linda Hester of Columbia; and his step-father: Jeff Buell of Columbia.

Information & photo compiled and sent by MB Richardson, 11 Jan 2015.
