RUNIONS, Donald A., 18 Mar 1948 - 6 Jun 2013. Sec 3 (s/o Mack & Rebecca Runins.)

Donald Allen "Spark Plug" Runions, age 65, of Hohenwald, passed away Thursday, June 6, 2013 after an injury from a gunshot wound.
Donald was born March 18, 1948 in Columbia, TN to the late Mr. Mack and Rebecca "Becky" Runions. He loved to fish when he found time. Most of all he loved to tinker with motors of all sorts. Donald loved to farm and garden and spent a great deal of time doing it. Donald loved helping those who were in need.
Donald is survived by his son, Donald ( Leeanna) Runions of Five Points, TN; ex-wife, Barbara Runions of Hohenwald, TN; Six siblings: Martha Escue of Summertown, Daisy Harvillie of Mt. Pleasant, Bill (Joyce) Robbin Runions of Hohenwald, TN, Roy W. Runions of Hampshire, Joe Runions of Mt. Pleasant, Lillian Elois Scott of Mt. Pleasant. He is preceded in death by two sisters: Margarett Gibson and Elaine Harris.
