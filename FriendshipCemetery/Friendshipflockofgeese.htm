FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Could not resist capturing this flock of Canadian Geese on photo as they winged their way toward the northeast on that cold December 2006 day. I counted at least 22 of them. Photo from inside the grounds of the Friendship Cemetery Section 1 looking across section 1 & 3.
