FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is a larger scale aerial view.

To find this cemetery go east from Culleoka Tennessee out Hwy 373 one mile and look up the hill to your left and you will see the cemetery just west of the church. The white trails above are the roads thru the different cemetery sections. Section one is the oldest and Section two is next with the section three being opened up recently. Go there to enjoy the beauty of the cemetery. Go there for mischievous reasons and you will be strung up.  The local caretakers and folks watch over this cemetery like they should. I should know they were even suspicious of an out-of-towner like me.  WA 10/10/06
