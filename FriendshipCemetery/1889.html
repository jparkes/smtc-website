FRIENDSHIP BAPTIST CHURCH/CULLEOKA CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


BRYSON, Richard, 25 Jun 1920 - 30 Aug 1995, US Army WWII, Sec 3
BRYSON, Sara Owens, 30 May 1921 - no date, married Richard Bryson 6 May 1938, Sec 3

Sarah Rosie Owens Bryson, died 24 Nov 2006 in Columbia, Tennessee. She was a Maury County native and was the daughter of Smith Owens and Hallie Mae Howell Owens Young. She was a member of Godwin-Chappell United Methodist Church; was preceded in death by three children, Ruby Bryson, Ruth Bryson and Frank Bryson who are buried in Section 1 of this cemetery. Grandchildren are: Cheryl Ann Newcomb White, Tim Newcomb, and Glenda Jean Bryson, sister, Masel Owens, and stepfather, Sherman Young.
This is on the north side Section 3.
Information & photo by Wayne Austin, 27 Dec 2006.
