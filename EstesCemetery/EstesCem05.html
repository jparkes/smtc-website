ESTES CEMETERY, Mt Pleasant Pike, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BENDERMAN, Eva Estes, 4 Nov 1846 - 31 Oct 1900. (Mar. Ozni Benderman, 5 Dec 1869; TS tumbled.)
Mrs. O. Benderman died last night at 12:30 o'clock at her home in Mt. Pleasant, aged about 45 years. The funeral services were conducted this morning by Rev. F. J. Tyler at the residence on Columbia avenue. Deceased leaves to mourn her death a husband, four daughters and three sons.
Columbia Daily Herald, Thursday, 1 Nov 1900. Submitted by Judy B. Forgos 8/9/2009
