COOPER CEMETERY,  CRANFORD HOLLOW RD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Miss H. S. Cooper memorial.  

COOPER, Miss H.S., who departed this life June 18, 1865.
Miss Hester Stewart Cooper, born Thursday, 11 Feb 1822 in Franklin, Williamson Co., Tn - died Sunday, 18 Jun 1865. Age 42 yrs 4 mos. 7 days. Daughter of Jonathan and Elizabeth Cooper.
Compiled by Mary Bob McClain who descends from the brother of this Cooper family. Photo by Wayne Austin who visited here 13 Jan 2009.
