GOODING CEMETERY-LYTLE CREEK & HICKMAN LANE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mary Elizabeth Gooding. Photo by WA.


GOODING, Mary Elizabeth, 11 May 1832 - 1 Aug 1852. "Daughter of John B. & Jane Gooding." 
Inscription: "MARY ELIZABETH -- daughter of -- JOHN B. & JANE GOODING, -- Born May 11th 1832 -- Died Aug 1st 1852. Thou are gone to the spirit land -- To the home of the truly blest -- Where neither sin nor pain can come -- To disturb thy blessed rest." 
Photo by Mary Belle Duvivier of New York 14 Apr 2008. Folks this is a new listing never before revealed to historical circles. Thanks to all the hard work from the team of Marc & Mary Belle Duvivier and myself. We dug up many stones here but were saddened that so many of them were shattered and pieces lost by the works of the Vandals. Accompanying Mary Belle & Marc Duvivier that day was Wayne Austin who also made photos.
