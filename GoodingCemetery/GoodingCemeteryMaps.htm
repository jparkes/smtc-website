GOODING CEMETERY-LYTLE CREEK & MURPHY LANE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


The Map coordinates: Latitude, 35 degrees 32.415', Longitude 87 degrees 2.207' You should not try to find this cemetery in the Summer in the high weeds because it is near
impossible to do so.  The other small cemetery shown near the bottom of the map (as Cem) is the Patterson Cemetery. Wayne Austin, 7 May 2008.

Go four miles south of Columbia on Pulaski Pike, turn left (east) on Hickman Lane. Go about 4 tenths of a mile and turn north into a farm road at an old house place and circle around to the east and the cemetery is on your right on a high place in a small grove of big trees.
The new owner Mr. Anderson has recently cleaned it.
