UNNAMED CEMETERY-LYTLE CREEK & HICKMAN LANE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Location Maps

Go 1/4 mile south of Murphy Lane on Pulaski Pike, to the place owned by Mr. & Mrs. Victor Rasberry on the left who have lived there since Mr. Fred Hawkins visited in 1988. The small cemetery is near Lytle Creek in a small grove of trees. A rock wall encloses a space large enough for four to six graves. No stones are now in place and the Rasberrys know nothing about persons who might have been buried there in the past.
