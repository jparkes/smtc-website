GOODING CEMETERY-LYTLE CREEK & HICKMAN LANE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GALLAHER, Jas., "Son of S. P. Mc...., Feb , d. 30 Jul 1828, months." (This stone was broken into many pieces and scattered about. I was able to assemble it and read the information given above. Fred Lee Hawkins,1987) 
I was unable to confirm this stone. For one thing it seems from the information Mr. Fred gave our subject was a son of SP McKee and how could then the surname be Gallaher unless he was adopted or something. Well I don't know everything, so we are publishing as is. 
Information by Wayne Austin 6 May 2008.
