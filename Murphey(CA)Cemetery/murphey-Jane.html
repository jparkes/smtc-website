MURPHEY (CHARLES ALBERT) CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD.

MURPHEY-Jane R. (Robison), 8 Dec 1820 - 4 May 1883, "Jane R. , Wife of C. A. Murphey Born Dec 8, 1820 Died May 4, 1883, O Mother dear a short farewell. That we may meet again above. And roam where angels love to dwell, Where trees of life bear fruits of love." This Murphey cemetery is reporting is from the gracious nature of Claudia Jean Williams Baker who is a gg-granddaughter of Charles Albert Murphey and Jane Robison Murphey whose memorials are shown here. Her ancestral chart is as follows: 1. Claudia Jean Williams Baker, daughter of 2. Virginia Benderman Williams and George Claude Williams, Virgina was the daughter of 3. Lela Ambie Walker Benderman and Otis Orel Benderman. Lela was the daughter of 4. Alexander Franklin Walker and Mary Jane Murphey Walker. Mary Jane was the daughter of Charles Albert and Jane Robison Murphey. It's somewhat odd that CA and his wife were buried on the family farm when other relatives were buried at Ebenezer during that time frame.
