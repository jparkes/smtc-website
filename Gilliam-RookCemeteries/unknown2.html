CRAIG - ROOK CEMETERY (Also known as the Gilliam Cem) -MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



All of the above are unknown fieldstone markers scattered around graveyard marking unknown graves in Craig Cemetery. This is a very old cemetery which may explain all of the unmarked graves. It is very near one of the earlier best places migrant settlers could cross (ford) the Duck River coming from the north and became prime settlement land.
