CRAIG - ROOK CEMETERY (Also known as the Gilliam Cem) -MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



All of the above are unknown fieldstone markers scattered around graveyard marking unknown graves in Gilliam Cemetery. This is a very old cemetery which explains all of the unmarked graves. It is very near one of the earlier places migrant settlers could cross (ford) the Duck River coming from the north. Since there were few markers in the region prior to 1815 then it follows that many if not all of these burials could have been before then.
