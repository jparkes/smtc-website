CATHEY FAMILY CEMETERY, LOVE BRANCH RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Susan's stone is the one on our left in front. Notice the lichen white spots on these stones. That makes them easily recognized from a distance if we make a mental note of the size and location of the spots.
