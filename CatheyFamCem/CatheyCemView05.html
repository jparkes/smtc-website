This view is one of the more important ones as we see most of the heavily populated areas of the Cathey Family Cemetery. It looks northwest across the cemetery from the southeast corner.  The two most identifiable stones in this cemetery from a distance can be seen here. That is the box tomb in the near bottom of the photo and the red granite memorial out behind some of the others and not as easily seen.
