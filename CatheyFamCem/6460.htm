CATHEY, Jane, no Tombstone. (b. 1756 ; Daughter of Griffith Rutherford; wife, James Cathey; mother, Griffith Cathey; mar. 2nd a Mr. White.)
There is a long stretch of area between Griffith Cathey and the next marked grave in this row so there is no telling who is buried there. Likely one grave is that for Jane Rutherford Cathey. The next stone down in this row is for Suzannah Cathey wife of Griffith Cathey whose stone is on our immediate right. There is a small worn & broken tombstone between Susannah & Griffith with no remaining inscription.
Photo and information by Wayne Austin. 18 Nov 2008. (parenthetical data from MTCT.)
