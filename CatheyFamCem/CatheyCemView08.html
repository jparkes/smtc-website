This view looks southeast across the cemetery while standing just in front of the Kennedy Memorial. This as a back side view of other photos you will see here. Inscriptions are all on the other side of the stones facing east toward the "second coming of the Lord", an old Judeo-Christian tradition. 
As you note the location of the Red Granite Kennedy memorial, one can get a feel for where the photographer is standing & shooting. We are looking east really panning the camera around from the above photo.
