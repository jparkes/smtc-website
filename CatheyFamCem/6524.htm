CATHEY FAMILY CEMETERY, LOVE BRANCH RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

    Mabel Lynn Holt.

HOLT, Mabel Lynn, 16 Aug 1904 - 27 Jul 1915. I don't see the surname on this stone but it is in the Holt Lots or row. I suppose that is enough evidence for me. Previous researchers agreed with calling this surname Holt.
