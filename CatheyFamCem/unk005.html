CATHEY FAMILY CEMETERY, LOVE BRANCH RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Unknown footstone with inscription A.A.K. Could this be for Albert Kennedy?
 KENNEDY, Albert Alexander, 14 Jan 1823 - 14 Jan 1903. (Albert Alexander Kennedy husband of Mary Ann Biffle Kennedy interred here in the same cemetery)
Style of footstone sure agrees with 1903. This is over in the southern central section of the cemetery out by itself. On the other hand there is a Alice A. Kennedy Green interred here, so it also may be for her though the stone is out of place if so.
Photo and information by Wayne Austin. 18 Nov 2008. (Update 5 Oct 2010: Albert Kennedy should be - Albert Alexander Kennedy, husband of Mary Ann Biffle Kennedy in the same cemetery j-rose@umn.edu.)
