This cemetery is called the Cathey Family Cemetery from olden times.
Go west on Hwy 412/99 several miles and veer right (or northwest) onto Taylors Store Rd. Keep that for about 2.5 miles and turn left into Love Branch Road. Follow that for just under a mile and pass the Southpoint Ridge Road on your right. Look ahead about 100 yards and the cemetery will be upon the small hill on your right about 50 yards from the road.
