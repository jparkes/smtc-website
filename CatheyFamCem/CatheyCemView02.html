known also as the Cathey Cemetery.

This view looks south across the cemetery from the north edge. Here we see among the small inscribed stones many fieldstones marking graves without inscription. This is a newer section than the old section to our left where all the older stones are located. This may simply means times were hard in the period of the 1880s and many deaths went unmarked due to the economic hardship.
Looking back east at the main area of the cemetery from a place in the western part.
