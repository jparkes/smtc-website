CATHEY FAMILY CEMETERY, LOVE BRANCH RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
known also as the Cathey Cemetery.

This view looks southeast across the cemetery from the northwest edge. Stones visible here are some of the first stones I photographed; that of Allice Cathey on our left. Shown also is the  prominent red modern granite stone of the Kennedy family shown back aways.
As you note the location of the Red Granite Kennedy memorial one can get a feel for where the photographer is standing. We are looking south. Notice the unmarked grave area in the open area where the grasses are tall from the sunken places of lost graves. Well not lost to God and that is all that really matters.
Photos by Wayne Austin 18 Nov 2008.
