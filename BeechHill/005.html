BEECH HILL CHURCH & CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Hinson, Winnie Frances Boshers, 16 Sep 1855 - 6 Jan 1941 (last child to die of 25 children born to Jason Wilson "Wilkes" Boshers and his 2 wives; her mother was his 2nd wife, Winnie Weaver; wife of George Washington Hinson; one of the earliest members of Beech Hill Church).
