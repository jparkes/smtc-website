Eastep, William Henry "Harry", 20 Jun 1941 - 2 Feb. 2009.

EASTEP, William Henry
William Harry Eastep, 67, passed away on Monday, February 2, 2009. He was a native of Mt. Pleasant, TN and a 1960 graduate of Hay Long High School there. He was of the Church of Christ faith. He played guitar and had his own band in the Milwaukee, WI area for several years. He retired from Gwaltney of Smithfield, in Virginia, as a maintenance supervisor.
William leaves behind to cherish his memory his sons, William Harry Eastep, Jr. and Mark Allen Wayne Eastep; daughters, Sharry Lynn Lunsford and Rebecca Lynn Eastep; 9 grandchildren and brother, Buford Allen Eastep.
William served his country honorably in the USAF from 1960-1964. He will be greatly missed by all who loved and knew him.
