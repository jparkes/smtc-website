 Obituary: Mrs. Anna Kelly Rail Mrs. Anna Kelly Rail, 89, of 311 Edgewood Drive, Mt. Pleasant, died Tuesday morning at Columbia Health Care Center. Services were 10:30 a.m. Thursday at Williams-Mt. Pleasant Funeral Home with Danny Douglas, minister of Beech Hill Church of Christ, officiating. Burial was in the church cemetery. A native of County Mayo, Ireland, she was a daughter of the late John and Teresa Lundy Kelly, she was the widow of Henry Urbin Rail, who died in 1933. She was a housewife and a member of Beech Hill Church of Christ. Survivors include a daughter, Mrs. Frances Bradburn, Columbia; five sons, Richard J. Rail, Reading, Mich., Henry Urbin Rail, Gary, Ind., William J. Rail, Selmer, Tenn., Thomas William Rail, Arlington Heights, Ill., and Harry Rail, Summertown; 10 grandchildren and 10 great-grandchildren. Grandsons will serve as pallbearers.
