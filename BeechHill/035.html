Eastep, Oner Jewell Duboise, 12 Nov 1911 - 21 Dec 1994 (wife of William Marion Eastep; daughter of Porter Duboise & Myrtal Tucker) 
 
EASTEP, Oner Jewell

Oner Jewell Duboise Eastep O'Dair passed away Wednesday Dec. 21st 1994 in Maury Regional Hospital, Columbia Tenn.
following a brief illness.
She was a long time resident of Mt. Pleasant. The latter part on the family farm on Camp Branch Road. She was 83.
Services were at 1 p.m. Friday at Beech Hill Church of Christ Hueston Marshall Officiating. She was buried in the Beech Hill Cemetery
A native of Limestone County Ala., She was born Nov 11, 1911 to Porter Alec and Myrtle Tucker Duboise.
She was the wife of Jasper O'Dair of Mt. Pleasant.
Her first husband, William Marion Eastep died in 1945. she was a homemaker and member of the Beech Hill Church of Christ.
She is survived by her husband, Two sons, Buford Allen Eastep of Harlingen Texas, and William Harry Eastep of Hampton Va.; one brother John Duboise of Florida, two sisters Vona Parish of Arley Ala., and Lacy Duboise of Nashville; six grandchildren and 2 great-grandchildren.
She was preceded in death by her parents and one sister Leona Waldrop.
