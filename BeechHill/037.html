EASTEP, Gladys Louise Hendrix

Mrs. Gladys Louise Hendrix Eastep, 84, a resident of Third Avenue in Mt. Pleasant, Tennessee, died Saturday, July 10, 2004 at Maury Regional Hospital. Funeral services for Mrs. Eastep will be conducted, Tuesday July 13, 2004 at 3:00 p.m. in the chapel of Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant with Brother Franklin Williams officiating. Burial will be in Beech Hill Cemetery. The family will visit with friends Monday from 5:00 until 9:00 p.m. at the funeral home. The Maury County native, was the daughter of the late, John Harvey and Katherine McClain Hendrix. She was the widow of Marvin Thomas Eastep who died in April 2001. She was a homemaker and a member of the Beech Hill Church of Christ. She is survived by a son, Wayne (Carolyn) Eastep of Mt. Pleasant, her grandchildren, Randy (Tammy) Eastep, Stanley (Pat) Eastep Both of Mt. Pleasant, Barry (Melanie ) Eastep of Columbia, Kenny Eastep, Michael (Shelia) Eastep and Scottie (Beth) Eastep all of Mt. Pleasant; twelve great-grandchildren an two great-great grandchildren; two sisters, Katherine (Franklin) Williams of Thompson Station, TN and Linda (Jackie) Boshers of Mt. Pleasant; one brother, Jimmy (Deane) Hendrix of Mt. Pleasant. She was preceded in death a son, Johnny Eastep who died in 1995 and an infant daughter, Jo Anne Eastep who died in 1942; a sister, Dorothy Lowery; two brothers, Mark and Clarence �Feller� Hendrix.
