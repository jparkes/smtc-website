Smith, Raymond L., Jr., 9 Oct 1945 - 20 Aug 2008. (Maury Co. native; son of Frances Johnson Smith and Raymond L. Smith, Sr.; grad. of Hay Long High School; carpenter and farmer.)

SMITH, Raymond Lewis

Mr. Raymond Lewis "Sonny" Smith, Jr., age, 62, a resident of Camp Branch Road in Mt. Pleasant passed away Wednesday, August 20, at his residence.
Funeral services for Mr. Smith will be conducted Saturday, August 23rd at 10:00 A.M. in the chapel of Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant with Brother Ed Brown officiating Burial will follow in the Beech Hill Church of Christ Cemetery.
The family will receive friends on Friday from 4:00 ï¿½ 8:00 P.M. at the funeral home.
The Maury County native was the son of Frances Johnson Smith and the late Raymond L. Smith, Sr. He was a graduate of Hay Long High School. Mr. Smith was a former employee of Stauffer Chemical Company, a carpenter, and a farmer. He was a member of Beech Hill Church of Christ.
