Eastep, Clemon Ray, 29 Jul 1943 - 20 Mar 2007. (Maury Co. native; son of Jessie and Maggie Dugger Eastep; mar. to Mary Irene Johnson Eastep; preceded in death by brother Junior Eastep.)
 
EASTEP, Clemon Ray

Mr. Clemon Ray Eastep, age 63, a resident of Scott Hollow Road in Culleoka, passed away on Tuesday, March 20, at his residence.
Funeral services for Mr. Eastep will be conducted Sunday at 2:00 P.M. in the chapel of Williams Funeral Home in Columbia with Butch Martin officiating. Burial will follow in Beech Hill Cemetery.
The family will visit with friends on Saturday from 4:00 � 8:00 P.M. at the funeral home.
The Maury County native was the son of the late Jessie and Maggie Dugger Eastep. He was the husband of Mary Irene Johnson Eastep of Culleoka. He was a self employed contractor and roofer. He attended the Graymere Church of Christ.
Survivors in addition to his wife include three daughters: Mary (Dickie) Voorhies of Culleoka, Becky Eastep of El Paso, TX and Lisa (Chris) Mayo of Mt. Pleasant; one son: Bobby (Amanda) Eastep of Nashville; one sister: Sue (Dean) Chase of Wisconsin; one brother: Tommy Eastep of Summertown; 11 grandchildren and 1 great-grandchild. He was preceded in death by one brother: Junior Eastep.
