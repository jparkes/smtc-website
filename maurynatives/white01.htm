MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.


WHITE, Edward Douglass, 3 Mar 1795 - 18 Apr 1847. Born in Maury County, Tennessee; son of James White, father of Edward Douglass White 1845-1921. State court judge in Louisiana, 1825, U.S. Representative from Louisiana 1st District, 1829 - 34, 1839 - 43, Governor of Louisiana, 1835-49. Catholic. Died in New Orleans, Orleans Parish, La. Interment at St. Joseph's Catholic Cemetery, Thibodaux, La.
