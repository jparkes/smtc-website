M. P. EDWARDS

EDWARDS, M.P., dates not known but (abt 1842 -1863), 9th Tenn Cav. Co. B. C.S.A. This is a placque at Emory and Henry Cemetery, Emory Washington Co. Virginia and his name is on it. M. P. Edward fought in the a unit that included most Maury County boys. Their graves are marker with a small square marker with a number that matches the large memorial plaque at Holston Conference, Emory and Henry Cemetery, Emory Washington County, Virginia
