MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.
 

	
Woods, Samuel Davis  b. Sept 19, 1845 - Dec 24, 1915,  Born in Mt. Pleasant, Maury Co., Tennessee.  Republican & 
 Lawyer, U.S. Representative from California 2nd District, 1900 - 1903.  Died in San Francisco, California.  Cremated ashes interred at Olivet Memorial Park, Colma, California.
 Information by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 6/15/2007.
