MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.

Wilkes, Peter Singleton, b. 1827 - 2 Jan 1900. Born in Maury County, Tennessee. Delegate to Democratic National Convention from Missouri, 1860, Representative from Missouri in the Confederate Congress, 1864-1865. Law partner of David S. Terry. Died in Stockton, San Joaquin County, Calif. Interment at Rural Cemetery, Stockton, Calif.
