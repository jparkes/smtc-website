HOWELL, Major 1835 - 1906, Major Howell was caught up in the guerilla style warfare that occurred around Mt. Pleasant 
Tennessee during the time of the Civil War. He was accused of being one of the killers of William F.  Coffee. After Samuel 
Morrow the other accused killer was murdered Major and his family fled to western Kentucky where he lived out his life 
and died there. This stone can be found somewhere in Western Kentucky. Written by Wayne Austin a third great nephew 
of William F Coffee. Photo image sent by Don Howell a nice person who is a descendent of Major who still lives 
in Western Kentucky.
