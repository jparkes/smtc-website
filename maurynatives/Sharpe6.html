MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE

 SHARP, James Austin, 25 Dec 1928 - 29 Jun 1991. Sgt US Marine Corp Korea.
 Obituary appeared in the Tennessean (Nashville) Newspaper: 
Mr. James A. SHARP of Fairview Died June 29, 1991, in Parkview Hospital. He was a native of Maury County Tn and was preceded in death by his parents James Washington and Ruby Media Turner Sharp. Survived by his wife, Faye Sharp, Fairview; sons, Ray Sharp, Kerry Sharp, John Sharp and Eddie Sharp all of Nashville; brothers, Donald B. Sharp and David Sharp both of Nashville; sisters, Ann Cole, Hilda Overstreet both of Nashville, Neuva Sears of Bay St. Louis, Miss, Carolyn Hicks of Huntsville, ALA and Linda Yates of Nashville. He as three grandchildren. 
Chapel Services will be 2 p.m. Tuesday at the Harpeth Hills Funeral Home. The Reverend James Patton will be officiating. Interment will be in the Harpeth Hills Cemetery. 
Pallbearers: Steve Sharp, Leon Overstreet, Arnie Yates, Mark Sharp, Lloyd Beach and Ben Parman. Honorary Pallbearers: The Adult Men's, Sunday School Class of First Baptist Church of Fairview.

Photos and information provided by Linda Sharp Yates 3/21/2006.
