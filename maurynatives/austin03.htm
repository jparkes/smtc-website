MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.


AUSTIN, Clyde Elbert, 8 Aug 1901 - 5 Apr 1979 (son of Charles Harris & Otie Eva Kerr Austin.) native of Maury County, Tn Lived his adult married life on farm on south side of Waco to Yokley Road in Giles County Tn.

AUSTIN, Eula Anna Story,  8 Sep 1904 - 7 Sep 1993. Giles County Native. (She was orphaned early in her life and lived with her brother Carl in the home of Ella Burkett & her husband James Tyler who raised these two children, Ella Burkett was the sister of these two children's mother.)
