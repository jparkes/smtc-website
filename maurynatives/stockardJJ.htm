John Johnstone Stockard was born in 1818 at Mt. Pleasant, Tn. In 1836 at 18 years of age, after his mother's death, he went to Picken Co. Ala., where he was a court clerk at Carrolton and then was in business and banking
there and at nearby Macon, West Point and Columbus Miss., until his death in 1905. In 1849 he married Victoria A. Rupert of Macon, and they had eight children, six sons and two daughters, but I know about only the 
following 5: Dr. Richard Rupert, about 1849 - 1930, Bennet Arthur, who died of tuberculosis in Los Angeles Calif, in Dec., 1919, and left three daughters; Dr. Charles Cecil; Thomas Walter, and Daisy, the youngest.
[Mary Bob McClain Richardson 14 Aug 2011]
