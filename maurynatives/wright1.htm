MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.
 

	WRIGHT, Leroy "Boss" Wright was born Jan 1904 in Maury County, Tennessee and died Mar. 1938 
in Oklahoma. He was the son of James Lewis and Cora Dean Kennedy Wright. He was buried near 
his parents at the Wards Chapel Cemetery in Atoka, Atoka County, Oklahoma.
