MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.


Zollicoffer, Felix Kirk,  b. May 19, 1812 0 January 19, 1862. 
Born in Bigbyville, Maury County, Tenn.  Member of Tennessee State Senate. 
1849 fought a pistol duel with rival editor John I. Martin, in Nashville, Tennee,
1852, U.S. Representative from Tennessee 8th District, 1853-49;  general in the
Confederate Army during the Civil War.  Killed in battle near Mill Springs, Wayne
County, Ky.  Interment at Nashville City Cemetery, Nashvile, Davidson Co., Tennessee.
 Information by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 6/15/2007.
