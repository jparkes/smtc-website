 WILTSHIRE, Robert Earnest, 3 Dec 1931 - 16 Oct 2008, 76, (b. in Mt Pleasant Tn; d. in Titusville Fla; Son of Charlie Joe and Martha Wiltshire; he moved his family in 1962 to Titusville, FL, where he pursued a career at Kennedy Space Center. 
WILTSHIRE, June, 5 Apr 1935 - 14 Mar 2008. Daughter of Katie McAfee Hinson & Johnny Hinson; married to June Wiltshire 50 years.)

JUNE WILTSHIRE,  TITUSVILLE FL.
June Wiltshire, who was born in Mt. Pleasant, TN on April 5, 1935, passed away at her home on Friday, March 14, 2008. She moved to Titusville Florida in 1962. She is survived by her husband of 50 years, Robert E. Wiltshire; two daughters, Emily Thompson (Travis) and Julie Stinnett (Tim); four grandchildren, Zachariah Short, Jennifer Kahl, Tonya Thompson and Cameron Stinnett; and four great grandchildren; four sisters, Angeline Harris (Richard), Hilda Plummer, Joneva Lambert (Richard) and Martha Livermore; and many more family and friends. Memorial services will be held at North Brevard Funeral Home, Titusville, Fl. on March 20, 2008 at 5 p.m.

June Hinson Wiltshire was the daughter of: Katie McAfee Hinson and Johnny Hinson of Mt. Pleasant, Tn. They had 5 daughters, Angeline, June, Hilda, Johneva and Martha Gail.

ROBERT EARNEST WILTSHIRE, TITUSVILLE, Fla. 
Robert Earnest Wiltshire, 76, was called home Thursday, October 16, 2008 to join his wife of 50 years, June, who preceded him in death on March 14, 2008. Robert was born December 3, 1931 in Mt. Pleasant, TN to Charlie Joe and Martha Wiltshire. In 1962 he moved his family to Titusville, FL, where he pursued a career at Kennedy Space Center, and retired after many years of service. He served his country with the United States Navy as a gunner aboard the USS Biaroko during the Korean War. Robert received a B.S in electronics from Tennessee Tech at Cookville Tn, and later received a Masters from Nova University, FL. He coached girls softball, traveled, and was an avid outdoorsman who loved to hunt and fish. He is survived by daughters, Emily Thompson (Travis) and Julie Stinnett (Tim); four grand children, four great-grandchildren, and brother, Donald W. Wiltshire. Services will be held on Thursday, October 23, 2008 at 5 p.m. Sand Point Park Pavilion in Titusville. North Brevard Funeral Home is handling arrangements. North Brevard Funeral Home and Crematory.
