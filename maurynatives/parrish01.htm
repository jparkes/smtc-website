MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.

PARRISH, James T., died 21 Jul 2007, Age 59. (Son of  James T. Parrish and Katherine Sowell Parrish;  interred in Great Lakes National Cemetery, Holly Michigan.)

Mr. James T. Parrish, III, age 59, a resident of Taylor, Michigan, passed away Saturday July 21.

Funeral services for Mr. Parrish were conducted Friday July 27, at 4 :00 P.M. in the chapel of Kernan Funeral Home in Taylor Michigan. Burial will follow Monday in the Great Lakes National Cemetery in Holly, Michigan.

The Maury County native was the son of the late James T. Parrish and Katherine Sowell Parrish.

He was survived by two sons: Gillis Parrish and Brian Parrish both of Columbia, TN; grandchildren: Jason Parrish, Katherine Parrish and Michael Parrish all of Columbia; one sister: Edna Ruth Parrish of Columbia; two nieces and one nephew; one aunt: Mary Bennett of Indiana; special friend: Kathleen Baughman of Taylor, Michigan.

He was preceded in death by one brother: Timothy A. Parrish.

Memorials may be made to Northside Baptist Church, 127 Theta Pike, Columbia, TN 38401.

Williams Funeral Home in Columbia was in charge of local arrangements.

Information compiled and sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 12 Jan 2009.
