MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.
 

	GRAMMAR, Anna Pearl Wright was born 1901 in Maury County, Tennessee to James Lewis 
Wright and Cora Dean Kennedy. She was married to Rex Grammar in 1920 in Atoka County, Oklahoma. 
She died in Oklahoma in 1963 and was buried in Wards Chapel Cemetery in Atoka, Oklahoma. Her 
husband Rex was born in 1897 and died during 1967.
