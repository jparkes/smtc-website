MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.

ORR, Sample,  b. 26 Nov 1816 - 14 Aug 1900.  Born in Maury Co., Tennessee.  Candidate for Governor of Missouri, 1860, Independent candidate for Delegate to U.S. Congress from Montana Territory, 1878.  Died in Blackfoot, Bingham County, Idaho, interment at Grove City Cemetery, Blackfoot, Idaho.
