MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.

ARNELL, Samuel Mayes, 3 May 1833 - 20 Jul 1903. Born in Maury County, Tennessee. Republican. Delegate to Tennessee State Constitutional Convention, 1865, member of Tennessee State House of Representative, 1865-1866. U.S. Representative from Tennessee 6th District, 1866-1871, delegate to Republican National Convention from Tennessee, 1868. Died in Johnston City, Washington County, Tennessee. Interment at Monte Vista Cemetery, Johnson City, Tennessee.
