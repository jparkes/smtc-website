MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE

WILLCOXON, Jul 1855 - 21 Feb 1926, son of Granville P. & Charlotte Catherine Coffee Willcoxon. 
Interred in Electra Cemetery, Wichita County, Texas. His mother Catherine Coffee was related by marriage to W.N.'s wife Lula West. Mr. Willcoxon furnished 
the tombstone for Calvin Coffee his grandfather's grave. At least he had his name inscribed on the 
stone as W.W. Wilcoxin. He was a native of Maury County removed to Texas with his family.
Photo dated 3 Mar 2001 provided by the Juanita & Bill Hyde, close relatives.
