MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE

WILEY/WYLIE, James Hugh 26 Jan 1822 - 10 Apr 1906, 9th Tennessee Battalion, Confederate States of America.
and
WILEY/WYLIE, Easter Elizabeth Barnett, Oct 1824 - 8 Sep 1904. (daughter of Hugh Moore & Martha Johnston Barnett of Culleoka Tn. 
James & Elizabeth are interred in Huckabay Cemetery, Erath Co., TX. 
James H. became sick with Typhoid fever in Louisiana while serving during the Civil War and was shortly thereafter discharged. Sometimes after 
the Civil War he and his wife and several children & their families removed to Texas from Maury County Tennessee.
