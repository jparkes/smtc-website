MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.


JONES, Jim D., 1906 - 1988, Math Professor and Civil Engineer. Lived and died in Troy Tn., interred West Hill Cemetery Obion County Tn.
JONES, Lutie Kerr Austin, 15 Feb 1908 - 11 Jul 1995. Teacher & Maury Native. Married Jim Jones in Knoxville, Tn about 1934, West Hill Cemetery Obion County Tn.
This couple taught school in Tennessee, Texas and Louisiana during their long spanning career and retired in the 1970s.They met at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville where they were graduates. Jim at one time was one of the best design engineers of his time, working on projects such as the Memphis bridges across the Mississippi River, etc.
Information provided by Nephew Wayne Austin administrator of this site. 3/20/2005.
