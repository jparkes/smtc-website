MAURY COUNTY NATIVES AND FORMER RESIDENTS INTERRED ELSEWHERE.

BLAIR, Elizabeth Ruth McClain, 6 Mar 1919 - 27 Sep 1967, Wife of Paul Blair, daughter of John Turley and Allie Mai Kennedy McClain. native of Maury County, Mt. Joy area. Sister to Edna Earl McClain Brooks, James Ward McClain, Robert M. McClain, Ara Alton McClain, Mildred Illene McClain Weatherly, Helen Jane McClain Pinkley, David Leo McClain and Jerry McClain. Ruth died of cancer and is buried in the Nashville National Cemetery with her husband. She died first. They are buried in the same grave space, in the vertical stacked vault method.
Information by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 6/15/2007.  updated 10/27/08
