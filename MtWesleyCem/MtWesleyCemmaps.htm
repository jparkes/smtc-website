MT. WESLEY AKIN CHURCH CEMETERY (Santa Fe Pike) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The red check shows the location of the Mt Wesley Akin Church Cemetery. The GPS Latitude & Longitude are correct as shown above.


From Columbia Tennessee go northwest on Santa Fe Pike. Church is on left side on top of ridge, midway between Athendale and Santa Fe. The cemetery is on the left and just behind church. 
This Aerial view shows the approach to Mt Wesley Akin Nazarene Church Cemetery on Highway 7 (Santa Fe Pike) on the left.
