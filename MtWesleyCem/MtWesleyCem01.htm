MT. WESLEY AKIN CHURCH CEMETERY (Santa Fe Pike) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 HOOD, J. D. (James Darrell Mangrum), 15 Jan 1843 - 1 Jul 1917. Father. (Son D. L. & Parthenia Mills Hood.)

James Darrell Mangrum was born 15 Jan 1843. He was the son of M. D. Lafayette & Parthenia Mills Hood. James served in Maury Light Artillery C.S.A. during the Civil War and returned to Maury County to marry Miss Martha Anderson. The children of the couple included seven sons and two daughters. He was a farmer all his life, passing away on 1 Jul 1917.
