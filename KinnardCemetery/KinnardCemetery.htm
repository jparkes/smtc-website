KINNARD CEMETERY, NEAR EXIT 46, I-65, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 Turn off of Old Bear Creek Pike north onto Blackburn Rd.  Next turn left onto Clara Mathis Rd. toward 
Kedron.  The cemetery is about 1/2 mile down the road, on the left.
