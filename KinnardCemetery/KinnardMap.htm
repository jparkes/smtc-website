KINNARD CEMETERY, NEAR EXIT 46, I-65, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map from Topozone modified for this site but shows the Lat & Long coordinates.


From Columbia turn off of Old Bear Creek Pike (north) onto Blackburn Rd. Next turn left onto Clara Mathis Rd. toward  Kedron. The cemetery is about 1/2 mile down the road, on the left.
