GRAY OR MYRICK CEMETERY, MOORESVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The cemetery can be found in the center of this map on our left side of Mooresville Map. It will be back on the ledge under the trees to the left and back of a historic house. Going south on the Mooresville Pike it will be about 1/2 mile on the right past the junction of what is shown as Cross Road but listed on other maps as Phinney Murphy Road.
