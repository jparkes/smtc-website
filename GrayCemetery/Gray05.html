GRAY OR MYRICK CEMETERY, MOORESVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
GRAY, Eliza K., 8 Jun 1811 - 1 Nov 1857. (46-04-13.) 'Wife of Matthew Gray.' In her will Eliza provided the land for his cemetery. For further details see the book "Maury County Cemeteries" page 494, which mentions details of the will. Accordingly her father is mentioned as formerly living on this place.
