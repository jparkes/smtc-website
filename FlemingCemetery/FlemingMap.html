PLEASANT GARDEN/FLEMING CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Here we see the GPS Coordinates of the Flemming Cemetery

Find the Fleming/Pleasant Garden Methodist Church Cemetery by going north on Mooresville Pike from Culleoka toward Columbia a couple of miles. Turn left on Phinny Murphy (called Cross Rd on some maps) Road and go 3 tenths of a mile and the road will bend left or south. Look to your right into the thicket and back into there is the cemetery. It is thought the church building stood in front or nearer the road than the cemetery.

This aerial view shows the cemetery marked by a red check in the clump of trees near the bend in the Phinney Murphy Road.
