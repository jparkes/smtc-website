LEE RAINEY, Anna Lise M. Lee,  2 Dec 2014 - 31 Dec 2015. (d/o Derek Crafton & Emily Rainey.) obit
 
Anna-Lise M. Lee-Rainey, one year old infant, passed away on Thursday, December 31, 2016 at Maury Regional Medical Center. 
Burial in Adkisson Cemetery.
Heritage Funeral Home is assisting the family with arrangements.
Born on December 2, 2014 in Maury County, Anna-Lise was the daughter of Derek Crafton and Emily Rainey. She was a cheerful, loveable baby who was loved by many. 
In addition to her parents, she is survived by her paternal grandmother: Ivy Beard; maternal grandmother: Jenny Tatum; and foster father: Ron Burgess and family. 
She was preceded in death by her foster mother: Cynthia Burgess.
