ADKISON CEMETERY, SANTA FE, (West) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

  
McKEE, CPL Robert Rudolph, 2 Jul 1930 - 4 Oct. 2008. (s/o Elmer & Ruby Fainn McKee.) obit
 Robert R. McKee, 78, retired mechanic for Union Carbide Corporation and resident of Sulphur Springs Branch Road in the Theta Community, died Saturday, October 4, 2008 at Maury Regional Hospital...
Burial will be in Attkisson Cemetery in the Santa Fe Community with military honors provided by Herbert Griffin American Legion Post #19...
The Maury County native was the son of the late Elmer McKee and Ruby Fain McKee. He served in the U. S. Army during the Korean War and was a member of the Church of Christ.
