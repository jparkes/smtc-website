BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Bell, F. Marion, 25 Sep 1837 - 12 Dec 1878 (son of William H. Bell and Elizabeth K. Hall). 
The stone is broken into three peices. However the inscription appears clear. Needs a good cleaning, bonding with Sikadur 
35 and after bonding a sealing job using a mix ratio of 1:2:4 parts white Portland cement, Hydrated 
Lime and white sand. Looks like the base is solid. Take extra pains with the fracture joints to seal those. 
Information and photo by Rick Gray, 11/2/2004. Procedure by Wayne Austin by recommendations of Dan Sumner Allen IV.
