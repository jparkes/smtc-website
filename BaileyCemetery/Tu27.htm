BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 



Turnbo, J. A., 4 Feb 1849 - 8 May 1878. The stone is buried in the midst of the Baileys, right to the left of Urastus Bailey. I am not sure how J. A. Turnbo is related or if he is related to the Baileys. He is buried right in the midst of the Bailey family though.
