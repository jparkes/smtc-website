Nancy A. Turnbo (Turnbow), daughter of James and Felicia Coffee Turnbow, was born 20 Feb 1824 in Maury County, TN. She married John H. (Harvey) Bailey in Maury County, TN on 3 Dec 1846. There is evidence that Nancy lived in Brown, Columbia AR for a time, but she is buried beside her husband John H. Bailey in the Bailey Cemetery, in Maury County Tennessee. She did live another 30 years after his death. Nancy A. Turnbo(w) Bailey died on 19 Aug 1899 at the age of 75. John H. Bailey as stated is buried in Bailey Cemetery, Mt. Joy community, Maury, TN. John H. Bailey and Nancy A. Turnbo Bailey were my paternal third great-grandparents.
