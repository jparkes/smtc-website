BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Beckum, Lettie King, 9 Oct 1828 - 18 Feb 1899 (wife of James T. Beckum; married him on 10 Jun 1847 in Lewis County, Tennessee)
(Death Notice, Lettie King Beckum birth & death dates not listed in this cemetery: "born 9 Oct 1828, died 18 Feb 1899; wife, James T. Beckum; joined Cumberland Presbyterian Church, 1842." (Columbia Herald, 24 Feb 1899) Likely she is buried in this cemetery or other sources say is buried here.)[added by Mary Bob McClain 10 Nov 2008]
