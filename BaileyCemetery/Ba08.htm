BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 

Bailey, Frances C. Williams, 1853 - 1939 (wife of James L. Bailey; daughter of Wiley B. Williams and Mary Arington)
and
Bailey, James L., 1851 - 1916, (husband of Francis C. Williams; son of John H. & Nancy A. Bailey).


Francis C. Williams Bailey married James L. Bailey on 5 Jan 1871 in Lewis Co., Tn. by Lewis W. Grimes.
She joined the Mt. Joy Cumberland Presbyterian Church in 1886.[added by Mary Bob McClain 10 Nov 2008]
