BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 

Four memorials of the Sims families. The two for Billie and Mary Mash share a common base and the other two memorials of the sons are further out on our left.

Sims, Roy Lee, 4 Aug 1927 - 2 Nov 1988 (son of Archie Lee Sims and Mary Mash)


Sims, Archie Lee, 17 Feb 1890 - 13 Feb 1951 (husband of Mary Mash; son of Hugh Sims and Tennessee "Tennie" Fuller; Tenn., Pvt. 108 Inf. 27 Div., WWI).

Sims, Mary Mash, 11 Feb 1902 - 12 Feb 1968 (wife of Archie Lee Sims) 

Sims, Billie Hugh, 16 Apr 1930 - 20 Jun 1968, (son of Archie Lee Sims and Mary Mash)
