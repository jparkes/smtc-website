BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 

Above is a picture of John R. Perry who is posted and buried at Bailey Cemetery. He is at the home of Mary Melvina Cooper Kilpatrick Whitwell, just off the Mt. Joy Road going toward West Fork. The lady in the forground is my Grandma Whitwell, she is my gggrandmother, and is buried at McClain Cemetery in Lewis Co., Tenn. [Mary Bob Richardson 11/21/2004]
