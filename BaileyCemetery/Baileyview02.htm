BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 


This is a shot taken at the (middle) back of the cemetery. It is panned out over the Brown graves and some of the Johnston/Johnson graves. That is my mother sitting on the bench that is in front of her gg uncle, Tom Johnston's grave. Three graves down is his niece and Mom's grandmother, Mary Alice Lindsey Brown.

This shot is shot toward the middle and right side of the cemetery. I took it to show that in the middle there are a lots of unmarked graves and many marked by fieldstones. The right side of the cemetery has more of the most recent graves than any the left side.

Information and photo by Rick Gray, 11/2/2004.
