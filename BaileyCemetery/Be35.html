BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 

The Fractured stone of L. Z. Beckum. It will be tough to repair this one, but can be done if all 
the parts are there. It is fractured in several places. The many pieces are in danger of being 
scattered and lost or sinking out of sight below the ground.  

Beckum, L. Z., 21 Dec 1855 - 1 Feb 1883 "Son of R. G. & H. Beckum" (son of R. G. Beckum 
and Hulda Hay) Note: Most likely this is the couple's son Alonzo who lived in the Mt. Joy area. 
The L and Z can be made out in the name and the tombstone could have had Alonzo had one time. 
He was married to Mary Elnora Williams, daughter of Wiley B. Williams and Mary Arington. 
Stone on the ground and broken.
