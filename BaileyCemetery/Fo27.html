BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 





This is a photo of a stone that I could not find anything on the last time I was there. Yesterday I gently scraped the stone after seeing the faint outline of a letter. It revealed the letters or word: LAD The first photo is of the stone showing the LAD. The second picture is showing the stone with another small stone behind it. It could have been a headstone at one time, I am not sure on that one. There is nothing faint at all on that stone. If it was ever engraved with anything it is blank now. The third picture shows part of the relation of this stone to others in the cemetery. Just to the right of it are the stones of Bliss Beckum and James H. King. The fourth picture shows another part of its relation to other stones. The two stones right behind it are the stones of Ruth S. Pickard Williams and Rebekah S. Pickard.
Information and photo by Rick Gray, 2/5/2005.
