BAILEY CEMETERY, MT JOY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


KING - LINDSEY Miss Cora Lee King, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Argles King and Mr. Elbie Lee Lindsey were quietly married Sunday afternoon. Dec 30. Rev. G.W. Phillips officiating. The bride is a charming young lady and has a hosts of friends. The groom is a young man well known in this and adjoining counties. This newspaper clipping is not dated.
 
Information submitted by Mary Bob McClain, 6/15/2007.
