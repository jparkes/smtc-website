HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

Mary Smith,Photo by Wayne Austin 12 Mar 2008

SMITH, Mary A., 1779 - 7 Mar 1858. "Wife of Stephen Smith." (Cooper memorial in Background. This stone has been cleaned by someone in order to read it. This stone needs to be sealed to prevent water from the large fissures from breaking the stone. 4 parts Hydrated Lime to 1 part White Cement to 8 parts White Sand mixed with water to form a mortor and smeared into the cracks will protect this stone from breaking up. [WA 4/12/05]
