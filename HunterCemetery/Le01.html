HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

LENHART, J. (John) W., d. 3 Sep 1851. Aged 51 yrs.

John W. LENART died 3 Sep 1851, and rests in this cemetery. He was 51 years old, and my Great Great grandpa. John Lenhart was a miller in Mount Pleasant, and a member of the Sons of Temperance.
Ref: e-mail from: Don Rose, Mariposa, California darose47@gmail 2 Sep 2009

He was married to Harriet HULL. Many of my Hulls settled in Mt. Pleasant between 1843 and 1848. Most of this family moved on to southeast Missouri by 1860. However, Harriet's brother, Rev Rowland Randolph Hull raised his family of 9 children there. I've been told that he is buried in the Baptist Cemetery in Theta. He died in 1906.
