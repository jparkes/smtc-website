HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE


SMITH, John W., 8 Oct 1798 - 17 Feb 1837. (enclosed by a rock wall.)
In memory of JNO. W. SMITH Who was born Oct. 8th 1798, & departed this life Feby. 17th 1837
And when you hear my heart strings brake, How sweet my moments roll. A mortal paleness on my cheeks, And glory in my sorrow.
