HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

 
CRAIG, Elender, d. 13 Nov 1831. In 88th yrs. of her age. (The stone you see on the ground all broken up is the original slab stone of Elender Craig. The new inscription; "Eleanor Johnston Craig,1743 - Nov 13 1831, Wife of David Craig." The large monument in the background is the McGee memorial.  
Information and photo by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, Visited there 4/2/2005.
