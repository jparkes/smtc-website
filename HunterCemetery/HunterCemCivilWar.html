HUNTER CEMETERY (Historic)-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

Civil War Soldiers buried in Hunter Cemetery

HUNTER  774

COOPER, J.C. (James Carlisle),  d.  11 Nov 1897.  70-11-24.  Mason.  (Mexican War, C.S.A.;  was on way to see gold mine in Mexico when he died.; MCTC).  See bio on this site.  Buried HUNTER CEMETERY.  48th (Nixon's) Regiment, Tennessee Infantry, Co. E, Confederate side, rank in 1st Lt. rank out Captain.  (Civil War Soldiers and Sailors)

ELLETT,  John H.,   no dates.  (Son Thomas P. & Susan Griffith Ellett;  b. 29 Oct 1834;  Co. D, 3rd Tn Inf., C.S.A.; d. 1880;  MCTC)  Buried HUNTER CEMETERY.  3rd (Clack's) Regiment, Tennessee Infantry Co. D, Confederate side, rank  in Pvt. rank out Pvt.  (Civil War Soldiers and Sailors)

HARRIS, Thomas A.,  b. 20 Jun 1820 - 5 Jul 1891.  (Mexican War;  C.S.A.; MCTC).  Buried HUNTER CEMETERY.  I was unable to identify THOMAS A HARRIS in Sailors and Soldiers Systems, there were so many.  The only one I found with the exact name served 2nd (Biffle's) Tennessee Calvary, unknown company #, Confederate side, rank in 2nd Lt. rank out 2nd Lt.  mb   No records were found matching this exact search criteria.  (Civil War Soldiers and Sailors)

KITTRELL,  Jacob Hunter,  26 Oct 1828 - 16 Apr 1867.  (Son Seth R. & Eliza J. Hunter Kittrell;  Co. D, Tn Inf., C.S.A.;  wounded in action at Jackson, Miss.;  mar. Agnes Dobbins.;  MCTC).  Buried HUNTER CEMETERY, there is a memorial stone for the KITTRELL's at Lawrence Cemetery in Mt. Pleasant, Tn. a few miles away.   3rd (Clack's) Regiment, Tennessee Infantry, Co. D, Confederate side, rank in Pvt. rank out Pvt.  (Civil War Soldiers and Sailors)

JARMON,  Willie T.,  5 Apr 1847 - Nov 1864.  (Son B.F. & R.S. Jarmon;  Co. E, McDonald's Bn, Forrest Cav., C.S.A.;  MCTC).  Buried HUNTER CEMETERY.  3rd (Forrest's) Tennessee Calvary, Co. E., Confederate side, rank in Pvt. rank out Pvt.  (Civil War Soldiers and Sailors)

RICKETTS,  Rev. R.D.  26 Dec 1824 - d.  Dec 5, 1897.  (Minister, C.P. Church;  Country Magistrate; mar. Mary Smith; Home Guard, C.S.A.: MCTC).  Buried HUNTER CEMETERY.  No records were found matching this exact search criteria.  (Civil War Soldiers and Sailors)
