HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE


STOCKARD, William, Esq., 11 Aug 1775 - 17 Aug 1837. 62nd yr. of his age. (Orange Co., N.C. marriages; Wm Stockard to Lear Mann, 28 Jan 1797; wife later moved to Mississippi.) This is a very large tombstone that has broken in half and laying on the ground of the base. Inscribed as: "Sacred to the memory of William Stockard Esq. who was born aug 11 A.D. 1775 departed this life aug 17 AD 1837 in the 62 year of his age."
