HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

LOHORN, Ann, born 21 Aug 1842. "36th year of her age Consort of Wm. Lohorn; daughter of James & Dorothy Busby." I cannot vouch for the accuracy of this inscription. Maybe it says: "SACRED to the memory of Ann Lohorn consort of Wm Lohorn, daughter of James & Dorthy Busby, was born August 21 1842. died in the 36th year of her age" Cannot confirm the birth date. It may be a death date. [WA 5/17/06]
