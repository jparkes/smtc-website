HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

DICKSON, Elenor, 12 Sep 1783 - 6 Aug 1823. (inscribed on a fieldstone.)
Eleanor Dickson 9/12/1783-8/6/1823 this is probably d/o John Gordon & Elizabeth Trousdale (my ancestors) who came from Orange Co., N.C. A Nellie Gordon m. William Dickson in Williamson County, Tn. about the time Maury was created out of Williamson. Nellie is nickname for Eleanor. Not sure how those other Dicksons fit in with Eleanor Gordon Dickson as most of them left Maury County before 1850. Ref: Dora Mai Gordon 10 Apr 2008.
Information and photo by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 4/23/2006.
