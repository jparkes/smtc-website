Vincent Ridley. Photo by Wayne Austin 12 mar 2008


RIDLEY, Vincent, Oct 1776 - 16 Nov 1852. Aged 76 years. (War of 1812.) (new stone 
has "Lieut W Tenn Militia ,War of 1812." mb.) The birth date on the main stone is 1776 whereas the birthdate on the military stone is 1778.
Information and photo by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, Visited there 4/2/2005.
