HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE


ODIL, Samuel Albert, 24 Oct 1852 - 15 Aug 1908. Once enclosed by an iron fence; corner posts remain. I did not see the orginal stone. Since Mr. Fred Hawkin's book "MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE CEMETERIES a new stone has been placed at this grave. The little orange flag in front of the stone may have been a marker placed there until this new stone was set. There are many such little flags in the cemetery where no stone is.
and
ODIL, Annie E., 16 May 1861 - 7 May 1917. (Wife of Al Odil; dau. William and Sarah Walker Craig of N.C.)
 Information and photo by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, Visited there 4/2/2005.
