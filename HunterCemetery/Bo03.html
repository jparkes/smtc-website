BOND, Robert Lee, 16 Oct 1862 - 14 Dec 1865. 03-01-28. "Son of James L. & M. V. Bond." "Aged 3 Ys (1 month / 28 dys.)" This stone is still on the long base . The stone of his mother Mary Virginia has broken and fallen off of the base on the left of Robert L. Bond's stone.
