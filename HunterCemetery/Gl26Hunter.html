HUNTER CEMETERY MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

HUNTER CEMETERY, MT Pleasant Tennessee:
GILCHRIST, MALCOLM, Tombstone Inscription: "Sacred to the memory of Malcolm Gilchrist, Sr. who was the son of Angus Gilchrist was born in Cantyre, Scotland on the 8th day of Feb. 1744. Came to the United States of America in the year 1770. Died on the 12th day of April 1821 aged Seventy Seven years two months and four days".

Listing from the book "Hunter's Meeting House", 1983, in Hunter Cemetery, Mt Pleasant, Tennessee: GILCHRIST, Malcolm, Sr., 8 Feb 1744 - 12 Apr 1821. "Son Angus Gilchrist; b. Cantire, Scotland; came to U.S. of America , 1770: Revolutionary War."

GILCHRIST, CATHARINE,  Tombstone Inscription, "Sacred to the memory of Catharine Gilchrist wife of Malcolm Gilchrist who was born in Cumberland County North Carolina June 30 1752, died Oct. 5 1839 aged 87 years 3 months and 5 days".

Listing from the book "Hunter's Meeting House", 1983, in Hunter Cemetery, Mt Pleasant, Tennessee: GILCHRIST, Catherine, 30 Jun 1752 - 5 Oct 1839. 87-03-05. "Wife Malcolm Gilchrist; b. Cumberland Co., N.C."
