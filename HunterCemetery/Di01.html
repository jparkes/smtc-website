HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

Sarah J. Dickson, Photo by Wayne Austin 12 mar 2008.
 
DICKSON, Sarah J., 27 Sep 1824 - 29 Jul 1890. " Our Mother "; (Hunter Meetinghouse - "Our Mother", & "Asleep in Jesus, blessed sleep. From which none ever wake to weep.") There are two lines under the death date that I could not read. This stone looks to me as though it has been professionally put back on the base hopefully with the latest epoxies and appears to be in good condition.  
Information and photo by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, Visited there 4/2/2005.
