HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE

DRUMMOND, Maria Louisa, 25 Oct 1822 - 2 Feb 183-. "Daughter of A. B. & M. B. Drummond; b. Brunswick City, Va.", Stone is breaking up. It will continue to peel from the bottom until it crumbles to nothing proving that stone returns to soil within enough years. It was just soft limestone and not the hard granite of today.
