HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE



McGEE, Chiles, d. 22 Sep 1838 . Aged 72 years. (This is the largest monument in 
the cemetery. It sits on a four-tier pedestal, with a winged design at the top. It is inscribed on three 
sides. Stonecutter - W. Gland. Graffetti is carved on the side of the McGee stone - M. Harwell, Joe Wilkes, 
J.E.H., B. Woodard.- Hunter Meetinghouse, 1983.) This large four tiered monument is in very serious condition. 
There is yellow police tape around the base, because it looks like it is going to cave in. The yellow tape has been 
there since 2004 Spring.  

McGEE, Mrs. Jemima, d. 20 Feb 1815. Aged 50 years.  
McGEE, Mrs. Ann, d. 19 Jan 1851. Aged 79 years. (Wake Co., N.C. marriages: Chiles McGee to 
Jemimah Joiner, 30 Mar 1785; Large four-tiered spire.)
