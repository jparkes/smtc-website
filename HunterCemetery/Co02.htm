HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE



 COLEBURN,Parmelia S., 23 Aug 1842 - 13 Aug 1877. "Wife of T.L. Coleburn."
also
COLEBURN, William F., 25 Oct 1876 - 1 May 1877. "Son T. L. & P.S. Coleburn." (Hunter Meetinghouse says the William F. inscription is on same stone with his mother and I might add that is obvious here, but; this appears to be a tragedy related to childbirth. Death of mother and child was common in those days but rarely happens today. [WA 4/27/06]
