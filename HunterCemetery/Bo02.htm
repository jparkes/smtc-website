HUNTER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT, TENNESSEE 


BOND, Mary Virginia, 9 Jan 1839 - 23 Nov 1867. 29-10-14. "Wife of James L. Bond." This stone is broken in several places and off the base. It is leaning on a tree.
