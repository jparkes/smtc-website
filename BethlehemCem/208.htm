BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
ALDRIDGE, Aaron A., 15 Feb 1837 - 29 Dec 1898.
On 22 March 1864, he married Louisa B. Carrigan, daughter of John Sample and Nancy Carrigan. They had two known children: Charles R., born April 1865 in Tennessee and James, born about 1870 in Tennessee.
Aaron Aldridge served in the 4th Regiment, Tennessee Cavalry (McLemore's) C.S.A., during the Civil War.
