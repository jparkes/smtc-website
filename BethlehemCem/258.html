BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
DARK, Mary J., 17 Aug 1839 - 10 Feb 1912. Mother

Dark, Mary J. - Aug 17 1838 - Feb 10 1912 - Plot 226

Lewisburg Tribune Obituary Notice February16, 1912: Mary Jane Dark died near the Rock Spring/Leftwich Communities in Maury County, her death being the result of the infirmities of age. Buried at Bethlehem Cemetery in Marshall County. Survived by two children, Tom Dark of Leftwich Community of Maury County and Miss Sue Dark of the Rock Spring Community.

Information & Photo Wayne Sharp 10 Oct 2008. Obit provided 6 Mar 2012
