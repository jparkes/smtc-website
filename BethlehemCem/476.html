BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

WHITEHEAD, Fannie May, 20 Apr 1881 - 5 Sep 1910. 'Wife of Ben Whitehead."

 

Plot 259 - Whitehead, Fannie May Gillespie - Apr 20 1881 Sep 5 1910

Lewisburg Tribune Obituary Notice, September 6, 1910: Mrs. Fannie Mai Whitehead wife of B.W. Whitehead of Columbia aged 29 years, died yesterday morning about 5 o'clock at the home of her father Mr. John H. Gillespie near Berlin. Her remains were laid to rest in the family plot at the Bethlehem Cemetery. She leaves a husband, father and five brothers.

Information & Photo Wayne Sharp 10 Oct 2008.  Obit provided 6 Mar 2012
