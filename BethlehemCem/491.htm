BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
WILLIAMSON, David, Sr., 5 May 1777 - 25 Feb 1870, Age 93, Born Halifax Co. NC. Moved to Ohio 1832 then on to Illinois 1852. Moved to Tennessee to live
with his son after the death of sons James and David, Jr. Died at home of his son John J. Williamson's in the Bethlehem Community in Maury County. Plot 179
Information & Photo Wayne Sharp 10 Oct 2008. Updated 16 Dec 2011 to add birth date. and other info.
