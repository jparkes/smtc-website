BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
WHITESELL, John, 15 Nov 1908 - 24 Jun 1910. "Son G. O."

Plot 156 - Whitesell, John - Nov 15 1908 Jun 24 1910

Lewisburg Tribune Obituary Notice June 28, 1910: John J. Whitesell, infant son of Mr and Mrs G.O. Whitesell of Lillards Mills, died Friday morning, aged 19 months. Buried in the Bethlehem cemetery.

Information & Photo Wayne Austin, 17 Apr 2008.  Obit provided 6 Mar 2012
