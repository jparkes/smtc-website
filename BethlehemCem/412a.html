Bette Jean Davis Liggett / Julian Mack Liggett
LIGGETT, Julian Mack, Jan 8 1936 - Feb 15 2014, Son of Ruth McCarley and Thomas Julian Liggett (Plot 25F)
and
LIGGETT, Bette Jean Davis, 17 May 1938 - 9 Sep 2009 (Plot# 25E) (Wife of Julian M. "Mack" Liggett, married 22 Nov 1956.) died in Columbia, Tn. (Maury Co. native; dau. of James Mash and Marie Davis; 1956 grad. of Columbia Central High School, "Queen of Sports" her senior year, coach of Columbia City League Basketball; preceded in death by sister Janice Lampley, brother Lewis Gunnells.)
Obituary information sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 10 Sep 2009. Updated Tombstone photo & burial data sent by Wayne Sharp 17 Sep 2009 and again 17 Feb 2014.
