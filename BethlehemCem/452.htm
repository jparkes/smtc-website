BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

RUTLEDGE, G. W. 1860 - 1940.
RUTLEDGE, Bettie (Elizabeth C.) Bercheen, 1860 - 1954, age 94, Wife of G. W. Rutledge, Daughter of Eliza Jane Tindell and Josiah (Joseph?) H. Bercheen. The Bercheen's are buried in Jackson Cemetery (#2) on Old Sowell Mill Pike near Leftwich, Maury County TN.
