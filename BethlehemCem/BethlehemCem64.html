BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
DAVIS, Sam McLean, 23 Jun 1890 - 22 Feb 1911.

Plot 161 - Davis, Sam McLean - Jun 23 1890 Feb 22 1911

Lewisburg Tribune Obituary Notice February 24, 1911: Mr. Sam Davis aged 21 years died Wednesday of typhoid fever in Seattle, Washington, where he had gone only six weeks ago. He is the son of Mr and Mr. Henry Davis of Lillards Mill. The remains will be brought home for burial and will reach here some time next week. Rev. T.M Hendrix will conduct the funeral services after which the remains will be buried in Bethlehem Cemetery.

Information & Photo Wayne Austin, 17 Apr 2008.  Obit provided 6 Mar 2012
