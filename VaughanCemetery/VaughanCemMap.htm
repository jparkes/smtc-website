VAUGHAN CEMETERY, SOWELL MILL RD., DUCK RIVER, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



Maps showing the location of the Vaughan Cemetery on the bank above the bluff on the Duck River. The Longitude and Latitude on the TOPOZONE site as shown above are accurate as are most others in Maury County.


To find this cemetery go east on Sowell Mill Pike to point where Duck River makes its turn east. Enter the old road bed that crosses the concrete bridge and meanders along river bluff. Go about 1/4 mile. The cemetery is in a grove of large trees which spans into the field from the river on right of trail. There is a locked cable across the road, prohibiting the entrance of vehicles. Have a nice walk.
