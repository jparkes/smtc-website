VAUGHAN, Sarah (Sallie) Osborn, 16 Nov 1858 - 3 Jun 1861. (02-06-19). "Daughter of N. J. & F. E.".  
There are two stones for this child the one shown in the bottom photo is a replacement stone in the late 1800s. It is my opinion that some of or all of the children's memorials were replaced at that time and the old stones were taken up and leaned against trees on the south side of the cemetery near the rock wall. This replacement included the footstones also.
Photos by Mary Belle Duvivier & Wayne Austin. Visited there accompanied with Marc & Mary Belle (Mary B., a descendent of this cemetery) Duvivier of upstate New York. 14 Apr 2008.
