RICHARDSON CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This topographical map from Topozone is not correct. I have drawn a blue arrow pointing to the actual place this cemetery can be found. The corrected coordinates of this cemetery are 35.4542N X 87.0355W. Actually the North Coordinate may be correct as stated using (NAD 27 calculating methods) 
Directions: Go south on Pulaski Pike to Valley Creek Rd. Turn east and stop at second house on left. That is before the road goes down a slope and crosses the small creek. The cemetery is in the back yard of this house, which is owned by Mr. Scott.

This aerial view shows a yellow pointer indicating the cemetery location under the trees behind the house. Driveway is on the east side of the house and permission is required to visit here.

This aerial view shows a yellow pointer indicating the cemetery location under the trees behind the house (shows as a bluish top above). Driveway is on the east side of the house and permission is required to visit here.
