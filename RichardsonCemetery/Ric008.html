RICHARDSON CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The fallen shaft style memorial of Mary Ann Richardson. The various parts lye scattered over a 15+ foot area. It was tumbled by the roots of the large tree rendering the base out of balance.

Other side of the Mary Ann Richardson memorial:
"The deceased had been several years a member of the Church of Christ & died triumphant in the faith, leaving an infant son who survived her a few months, who was born on 20 May 1853 and who died the 10th day of August of 1854, aged 1 yr., 3 mos. & 21 days & who lies on her left side. His name was James Mason Richardson." Â

Mary Ann Richardson, Consort of William C. Richardson & Daughter of Dr J.S. Hunt
INSCRIBED AS: "Erected in memory of Mary Ann Richardson, Consort of William C. Richardson & Daughter of Dr J.S. Hunt, was born June 19th 1831 & Departed this life December 13th 1853 aged 22 years 5 Months & 24 days."
RICHARDSON, Mary Ann, 19 Jun 1831 - 13 Dec 1853. aged 22 years 5 Months & 24 days. "Consort of William C. Richardson; daughter of Dr. J. S. Hunt. (Shaft memorial). "The deceased had been several years a member of the Church of Christ & died triumphant in the faith, leaving an infant son who survived her a few months, who was born on 20 May 1853 and who died the 10th day of August of 1854, aged 1 yr., 3 mos. & 21 days & who lies on her left side. His name was James Mason Richardson."
