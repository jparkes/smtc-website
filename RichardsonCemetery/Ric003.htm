RICHARDSON CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The fallen and broken memorials of the Richardson cemetery.

John M. Richardson. 

RICHARDSON, John M., 23 May 1832 - 27 Sep 1833. 1 year 4 months & 4 Days "Son James M. & Lucy Richardson."
Inscribed as: "JOHN M. RICHARDSON, son of James M. & Lucy Richardson, born May 23rd 1832 died Sept 27th 1833, aged 1 year, 4 mo. & 4 days. Suffer little children to come unto me & forbid them not for of such is the kingdom of Heaven."
