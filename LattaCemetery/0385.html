LATTA, CEMETERY (Snow Creek Rd.) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Samuel Latta 1783 - 1855, son of John & Margaret Latta.

LATTA, Samuel, 10 Jan 1783 - 29 Aug 1855. "Son of John & Margaret Latta. He Emigrated from S.C. & Settled on Snow Creek in 1817 where he lived until his death, Aged 72 yrs, 7 mo., & 19 Days. "Esteemed, he lived. Lamented he Died."
Complete Inscription: "IN memory of SAMUEL Son of JOHN & MARGARET LATTA Born Jan 10.th 1783. He Emigrated from S.C. & Settled on Snow Creek in 1817 Where he lived until His Death Aug 29th 1855 Aged 72 yrs, 7 mos & 19 Days -- Esteemed, he Lived Lamented, he Died."Â
