CAVE SPRING CEMETERY, (Angie Sewell Rd), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the cemetery at N 35.80065 X W -87.08633.

From Theta (at the junction of Snow Creek Rd and Chestnut Ridge Rd) take Chestnut Ridge Road about 3 & 1/2 miles to the Angie Sewell Road. Turn North (left) and go one mile to Cave Spring Church. The cemetery is on your left beside the church.
