PERRY CEMETERY, McCAINS, TENNESSEE

UNKNOWN, fieldstone clearly marking a grave. The headstone is at the top center of the photo and the footstone is near the bottom center. These are common rocks stood on end and buried in a way they would stand. This is between the area of Milton & Lydia Scott and the main area of the Perry cemetery south of this spot. There may still be someone on this earth that knows who is interred here.
