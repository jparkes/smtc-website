PERRY CEMETERY, McCAINS, TENNESSEE


PERRY, Elizabeth, 14 Apr 1776 - 19 May 1847. 71-01-05. (Daughter of John B. & Frances Shaw; married Wm. Perry on 1 May 1792.) "HERE LIES the body of Elizabeth Perry born april 14th 1776 died juay 19th 1847 aged 71 years 1 month 5 days".
