PERRY CEMETERY, McCAINS, TENNESSEE

MACK, Nathan Green Perry, 18 Jul - 29 Jul 1849. 11 days. Son of (John William De La Fletcher Mack, who is buried in Hazelwood Cemetery in Springfield Greene Co., Missouri) and Sarah E. Mack.); (Nathan lived just 7 days after the death of his mom obviously from something related to being a newborn in his case, and childbirth in her situation. Such tragedy, but it was common in the old days.
