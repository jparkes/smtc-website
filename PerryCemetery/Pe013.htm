PERRY CEMETERY, McCAINS, TENNESSEE


PERRY, Elizabeth, 14 Nov 1797 - 12 May 1880. Mother. ‘Wife of Simpson Perry.' (Daughter of Robert Thompson of Belle Meade, Davidson Co.; mar. Simpson Perry on 6 Aug 1812 who was a leader in founding the Bigbyville Methodist Church. Stone is off its base but standing.
