PERRY CEMETERY, McCAINS, TENNESSEE.


From Highway 31 South (Pulaski Pike) at McCains turn west (right) onto Bigbyville Road for 1/4 mile and then turn left onto (Perry) Cemetery Road. Continue south until the road bends west and goes by the old Perry Home your right. Shortly after that turn north around a tree line for about 100 yards and the cemetery will be in a thicket about 200 yards northwest of the home.
