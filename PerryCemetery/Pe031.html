PERRY CEMETERY, McCAINS, TENNESSEE

MACK, Sarah Elizabeth, 28 Nov 1826 - 22 Jul 1849. "aged twenty two years, seven months and twenty six days." (Nee: Sarah Elizabeth Murphy; d/o Nathaniel Green & Charlotte Perry Murphy; w/o Judge John William De La Fletcher Mack, mar. 2 May 1843 in Maury Co. Tn., he is buried at Hazelwood Cemetery in Springfield Missouri. Ref: Patricia Heath americanterrortwin@yahoo.com)
Sarah lived four days after the birth of her son and Nathan Green Perry Mack died seven days after his mom. Such tragedy, but it was common in the old days.
