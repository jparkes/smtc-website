HILLIARD CEMETERY, Natchez Trace Parkway, (Northwest) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
A.K.A. ASHWORTH CEMETERY 

Fred Hawkin's instructions of 1987: From Natchez Trace go 3/4 mile east of Johnson's Chapel traveling old Natchez Trace Rd.
Latitude:35.818�N, Longitude:87.148�W, Elevation 912 Ft
This shows the cemetery along the Natchez Trace Parkway. You might find yourself first traveling north on Dutch Potts Hollow Rd to get there. Next turn right on the old trace road before you cross the Trace and go a few feet and immediately turn right into a road that contains the cemetery.

With this aerial view one can actually see the little cemetery outlined by the fence.
