HILLIARD CEMETERY, Natchez Trace Parkway, (Northwest) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
A.K.A. ASHWORTH CEMETERY 
Locator Map of the cemetery

This is a small, well-fenced and well-kept family cemetery. The gate is made of ornate wrought-iron and has the name "HILLIARD CEMETERY' over it. F.L.H.

Sources: MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE CEMETERIES Page 680 - 682, by Fred Lee Hawkins 8 Mar 1986; THEY PASSED THIS WAY, Page B-14, by Evelyn Lightfoot and Marise Shackelford. For more notes and info see the book by Fred L. Hawkins. This cemetery has not yet been updated for burials after 1987. Published here before revisions 4 Apr 2009 by Wayne Austin.
