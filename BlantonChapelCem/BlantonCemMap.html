BLANTON CHAPEL METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, OAK VALLEY DR., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Go out Bear Creek Pike almost to I-65 and turn northeast onto Blackburn Road and go about a a miles and turn left on Clara Mathis Road for about a mile and turn left on Big Oak Drive. When that shortly terminates on Oak Valley Drive turn right and go about 3 tenths of a mile and the cemetery is visible on your left out 200 feet left in a grove of tall forest with new housing development all around. In the summer the memorial stones are hard to spot from the road driving by. There is no fence nor other indication of a graveyard.

The white + "Plus" shows the clump of trees where the cemetery is located. 

This tall map shows Bear Creek Pike. One should turn off Bear Creek Pike onto Blackburn Lane and then turn left on Clara Mathis Rd. Next turn left on Big Oak Dr. and a final right turn onto Oak Valley Dr.
