BLANTON CHAPEL METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, OAK VALLEY DR., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
DODSON, John, d. 17 Oct. 1899. unmarked grave.
John Dodson died at the home of Thomas Allen, on Bear Creek, Tuesday night, and the remains were buried at Blanton's Chapel Wednesday. -- The Tennessean, 20 Oct. 1899

Information sent in by Mary Bob Richardson 8 Jul 2016.
