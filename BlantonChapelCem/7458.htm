BLANTON CHAPEL METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, OAK VALLEY DR., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



DOBBIN, Mrs. S.H., died 17 Aug 1876 - Age 55 years. "wife of Wm A. Dobbin, She was the subject of great affliction for many years but was submissive to the Master's will. Embraced religion in youth and was a living member of the M.E. Church South, died in glorious Triumph" 
Obit: "Wife of Rev. Alex Dobbins, d. 17 Aug 1876, age 55; of Fairview; died at home of J.A. Sanders; she was the daughter of John Crawford and she was the former Sarintha Harriet Crawford, b. 29 Mar 1821. Her mother died when she was three. She married Rev. Dobbins on 23 Jul 1837; had 4 step-children and 15 year old daughter." (Columbia Herald, 29 Sep 1876.)
Information and photograph by Wayne Austin 30 Dec 2008. Obit from MCTC.
