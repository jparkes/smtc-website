BLANTON CHAPEL METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, OAK VALLEY DR., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Caldwell memorial in three pieces. This is another stone whereby the base with a little leveling would be level enough to stand if someone strong enough to set it up came along. I think that stone has been recently tumbled probably by a vandal since there is no evidence lying around that a tree fell on it.

CALDWELL, John S., 10 Feb 1811 - 23 Dec 1890.
CALDWELL, Sarah A., 15 Nov 1820 - 10 Dec 1899. (wife of John S. Caldwell.)
Information and photograph by Wayne Austin 30 Dec 2008.
