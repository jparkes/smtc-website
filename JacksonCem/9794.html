GULLETT, Samuel, 14 Nov 1781 - 23 Dec 1829. 
Inscription: "SACRED -- To -- the memory of -- SAMUEL GULLETT -- born -- Nov. 14 AD 1784 and -- died Dec 23, 1829 -- in -- the 49th year of his age. -- Next to truth he loved -- friends"
