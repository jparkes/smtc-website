HARGROVE (David) CEMETERY, Kedron Pike, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Go Kedron Pike from the Saturn Parkway about 5 miles crossing under/(or over) I-65. Then go 1/2 mile and pass Ollie Chunn Road on your left. Then go about 2 tenths of a mile and look for the road into the old farm & cemetery. The cemetery sign is visible from the road on the left of the farm/cemetery road. The cemetery is at the top of the hill at the end of the road.
