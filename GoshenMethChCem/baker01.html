GOSHEN CEMETERY aka Goshen Methodist Church Cemetery, (west) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


 BAKER, James C., 18 Mar 1854 - 4 Dec 1926. (Son Robert & Mary Hunphrey Baker, shares stone with Louise H. Baker.) 0492/

 Obit shared by James Triplett
The Maury Co. Democrat, Columbia, Tennessee (December 9th 1926).

James Baker aged 72 years, highly esteemed citizen of Santa Fe community died Saturday morning at his home following a short illness. Mr. Baker was a member of the Methodist church and a man who enjoyed the esteem and confidence of a wide circle of friends. He is survived by his wife and six sons, B. H., J. C., Jr., Woodrow, Wallace, Robert, Sam and Eugene Baker, and the following daughters, Mrs. Irene Shelton of Nashville, Mrs. Tennie Jack of Theta, Mrs. Carrie McKee of Hohenwald, Dorothy Baker of Santa Fe and Annie Mae McKee of Hohenwald. Funeral Services were conducted by Rev. W. E. Walker and Rev. Paul Lanius with internment in the Goshen Cemetery, Oakes and Nicholas Funeral Directors, in charge.
