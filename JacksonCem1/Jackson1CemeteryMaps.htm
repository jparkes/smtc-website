JACKSON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Shows the cemetery using a red check in the Jackson Bend of the Duck River.

Shows it north of Sowell Mill Pike more than a mile.

 This cemetery is located off of Old Sowell Mill Pike.
Go east on Sowell Mill Pike. About two miles beyond the 1-65 overpass keep left on the old Pike. Where road forks, take another left on dirt road. The cemetery is about a mile and one half further on, overlooking the bottom lands of Jackson Bend. This cemetery is very hard to find. The people in the last house on the road into the area (the Terry Parrish family, in 1987) could have given instructions. We are uncertain if that knowledge resource still exist today in 2009.
