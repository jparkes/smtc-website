BLOCKER'S CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This is an aerial view of the area where the cemetery is believed to be. You can get there from Columbia by taking Williamsport Pike to the Sawdust Community. 100 yard before you reach Craig Bridge Road which turns right stop your car and look left under the trees. There you will see the last of the old Blocker's Shop (Blacksmith). The stone remnants of the firing pit can be seen near the road next to a large tree. The Cemetery and Cabin are shown above. The cabin remains will be about 300 yards south east of where you stopped your car. Now be careful because this is private land. You must secure permission before exploring these sites in person.
