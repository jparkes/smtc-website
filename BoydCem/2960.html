BOYD CEMETERY,  HIGHWAY 231, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


 BOYD, Dr. T. M., 21 Apr 1833 - 2 Jun 1890.
Dr. T. M. Boyd was a native of Williamson Co, Tennessee. He came to Maury County prior to the Civil War and set up his medical practice here in the Rally Hill Community where he remained until his death. This beautiful rock-walled cemetery bears his name.
