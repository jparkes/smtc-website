BOYD CEMETERY,  HIGHWAY 431, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is the view of the cemetery enclosed in a well built rock wall. I believe the wall was added since the invention of concrete mortar. 
Looking at the cemetery from inside the walls one notices there are only two rows of graves. That is the far northwest corner we are looking at which has the J. W. Boyd memorial. Other Boyd family memorials can be seen here.
Photos by Wayne Austin 29 Nov 2005.
