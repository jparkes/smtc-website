BOYD CEMETERY,  HIGHWAY 231, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Our subject stone is the third one photographed from the southwest corner and next to the west wall of the cemetery. I believe there is enough room on both sides (actually in front) of this stone for two more graves leaving this stone occupying space for three graves. It sets at the head of the center grave. I could not tell if the two spaces on either side were unmarked graves or open spaces.

BUCHANAN, Lou Alta, 2 Jul 1870 - 5 Nov 1898. (Front Row 2,4)
