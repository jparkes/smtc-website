BOYD CEMETERY,  HIGHWAY 431, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Go south on Hwy 431 out of Rally Hill (Junction of Hwy 431 & Kedron Road) about 1/3 mile. The Cemetery is on your right behind and just off of the right of way inside a stone wall.
 Information by Wayne Austin 9 Aug 2008. Map from MS Terra mapping service modified for this site.
