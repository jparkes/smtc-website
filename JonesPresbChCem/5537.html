JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

LUSK, Melia R., 30 Mar 1817 - 5 Feb 1839. "Consort of S. B. Lusk; married 27 Nov 1833."
Inscription: SACRED -- To -- the mem -- ory of MEL -- IA R. LUSK  Late Con -- sort of S.B. LUSK. Born -- March 30th 1817 -- Mari -- ed Nov 27th 1833. and -- departed this life -- for the Mansions of -- Glory Feb 5th 1839. -- -- Farewell vain world -- I'm going Home. -- My Savior Smiles -- and bids me come -- Bright Angels becon -- me away -- To sing Gods prais - in endlis day. -- -- In youth Form For -- tune we both -- were blest. -- Can Fancy Form -- more finished -- happiness.
