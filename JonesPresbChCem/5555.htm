JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Margaret Whiteside memorial enclosed in a fence.

This stone has been recently repaired and someone is looking after it.

WHITESIDE, Margaret, died 28 Jan 1830. Aged 87 years. (Nee Lusk; wife of Thomas Whiteside; Tombstone very faded.). I am staying with Mr. Fred Hawkins in the transcription of this memorial even though the death date looks like 1839 and the age looks a bit like 37 instead of 87. You can be the judge here.
Photos and info by Wayne Austin. 28 Oct 2008. Parenthetical info from MCTC.
