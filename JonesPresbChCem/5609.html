JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The McClanahan family memorial inside the McClanahan Family fenced Lots. This side is the Mother, Father on the opposite or south side and the children on the west side. Seven Footstone against the east fence, one for each.
