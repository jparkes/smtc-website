JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Elizabeth Jones

JONES, Elizabeth A., 9 Dec 1821 - 13 Apr 1894. "Wife of James Jones."
This stone wedged between a wrought iron fence and a tree and had iron fencing obstructing the view. It was hard to photograph and read. It is also fallen and stood up beside its base.
