JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

With this photo you can see the head and footstone of infant Johnathan Wylie.

WILEY/(WYLIE), Johnathan M., 16 Apr 1814 - 25 Sep 1818, "son of J. H. & Mary W., Wylie" (Located in the Wiley/Fariss family Areas. These two families co-mingled their stones.)
I am listing this surname as above if anyone can remotely disconnect this child to the John Wiley buried a  few graves to the south then I will happily change that back to Wylie.
