JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This stone is fallen and wedged under the stone of Jane Fariss. It is the stone for W.F. which is probably W. Fariss her husband who was born 4 years before her in 1785.

With this photo you can read the inscription as: "W.F. -- Died Oct -- 9, 1825 -- AG'd 40 Yrs."

FARISS, W., died 9 Oct 1825 - Aged 40 years. (Tombstone with no inscription for surname, beside the Jane Fariss' grave. See notes above under the photos.)
FARISS, Jane, 1 Apr 1789 - 3 Mar 1854.
