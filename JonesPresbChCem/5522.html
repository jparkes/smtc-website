WILEY, John, 1 Mar 1781 - 24 Feb 1857 (Son of Robert & Sarah (?) Wiley.)
WILEY, Mary W., abt. 1795 - death date unknown. married to John Wiley abt. 1815) There are two field stones one on either side of the John Wiley memorial. Chances are very great one of them belongs to his wife Mary W. (?) Wiley)
I trace my ancestry to James Hugh Wiley/(Wylie) the early Wileys of Maury County and still have not placed James Hugh Wiley in the genealogy charts with this family though it is highly likely he is related. James Hugh Wiley is the oldest ancestor I have proven, b. 1822,  Mecklenburg NC, d. Huckaby, TX, 1906.
This John Wiley fits well to be in the generation preceding James but I do not have the information to connect this. I recall in early records brothers Moses and other Wiley kin mentioned but I am careful with the old records as they are often inaccurate.
