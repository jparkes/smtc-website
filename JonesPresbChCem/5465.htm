JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

G W. Wilson and Mary Lusk Wilson.


WILSON, G.W., 2 Feb 1857 - 12 Jan 1912. (front side of memorial with Mary Lusk Wilson or Mary Lusk)
WILSON, Mary Lusk, 17 Aug 1813 - 22 Jul 1894. (Footstone: M.L.; back side of same memorial as G. W. Lusk.) 
Shaft with both names on it and a middle section with surname WILSON inscribed on that. I believe the age differences are too great to be a marriage here. More like a son and mother.
