JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The stone in the middle with the top leaning against its left side. In front is the two footstones.

KIRKPATRICK, Robert W., 1 Oct 1830 - 5 Sep 1893. (Son of Elihu & Nancy Wiley Kirkpatrick; Co. E, 48th Tenn. Inf., C.S.A.)
KIRKPATRICK, Sarah T., 26 Oct 1840 - 5 Aug 1886. "Wife of R. W. Kirkpatrick." (Dau. W.C. & Sarah Wiley Raines; mar. on 22 Jan 1856; Tombstone broken.)
