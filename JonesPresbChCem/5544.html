JONES PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, ISOM, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

William Noles

NOLES, William, 4 Dec 1825 - 19 Aug 1875. Mason.
There were many men of this age that died around this time from guerilla warfare. Bad feelings and policies of the Reconstruction era were responsible irrespective of the fact that the Civil War had ceased ten year earlier the killing did not stop. I am unaware if William Noles was one of those, but he was a Mason somewhat of a hint of evidence.
