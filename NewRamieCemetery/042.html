NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Tall stone in the row of two leaning far (east) to our right. It is next to the tall straight one one on the end. The small stones are footstones marking the foot of the grave. The two tall head stones have footstones that are out of the photo to our right. Of the five headstones on our left there are is one without a footstone.


DUGAR, C. J. (Charles Johnson), 10 Apr 1821 - 25 Sep 1873. (He was the son of Joseph & Elizabeth Dugger. He married Ethalinda L. Bumpass in Lawrence County Tennessee on December 17 1840.) [Parenthetical info- MCTC.]
