NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Tall stone in the row of two on our right. It is standing straight up (on the end)  next to the (right) leaning one. The small stones are footstones marking the foot of the grave. The two tall head stones have footstones that are out of the photo to our right. Of the five headstones on our left there are is one without a footstone.


DUGER, Sophia T., 28 Sep 1858 - 21 Sep 1871. 'Daughter of C. J. & E. L." (Notice the spelling variations: Dugar, Duger, Dugger; all in the same family lot.)
