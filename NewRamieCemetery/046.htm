NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

At the base of the large tree on this side lies a Box Tomb. Just in front of that is the base of a small box tomb and just in front of that is another large box tomb very close to the ground. It is our subject stone below.

PULLEN, Ann, d. 29 Apr 1843. (53-01-22.) 'Consort of E.(Elisha) Pullen; dau. Thomas Richardson; d. in the Predistinarian Faith.' (Box tomb.) Inscription complete: "Sacred to the memory of Ann Pullin, Consort of E. Pullin and daughter of T. Richardson who Departed this life in the Predistinarian Faith April the 29, 1843 Aged 53 years 1 months and 22 days" Could the word above be Predestination. If so the old congregation here could have been connected to the Primitive Baptist faith.
