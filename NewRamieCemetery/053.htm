NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The box tomb visible in this photo center left of the photo and right of the Thomas & Sarah Moore memorial is the subject stone.

MOORE, Caroline M., 28 Jul 1823 -27 Mar 1846.(22-08-00.) 'Daughter of Thomas & Sarah Moore.'
Inscription: "Caroline M. More Daughter of Thomas & Sarah Moore who was born July the 28th 1825 & departed this life March the 27th 1846 Aged 22 years & 8 Months. She was mild in her manner and courteous in her _ _ _ "(temperance?)
