NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The next over from us short stone with the wide base next to the tall stone center of the photo between us and the two trees is our subject.

This is the bottom of the Nancy M. Bell memorial. The top sits in the wide base to the right. That means we are missing the top to the unknown base on the right. It is probably below the ground and can be found by probing the ground near the base if so.


BELL, Nancy M., 2 Dec 1833 - 4 Jul 1864. 'Wife of Thomas A. Bell."
