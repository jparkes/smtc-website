DUGGER, Maggie C., 2 Nov 1896 - 11 Mar 1929, Daughter of J. T. & Ann Hickman Dugger; schoolteacher.
 
March 12, 1929 NASHVILLE TENNESSEAN ________ MISS MAGGIE DUGGER Columbia, Tenn., March 11..(..Special)
Miss Maggie Dugger 32, daughter of Mrs, J. T. Dugger, died at her home at Southport today at 12 o'clock. Miss Dugger was a member of the Christian church. She is survived by her nother and the following brothers and sisters: C. W., S. E., M. C., Joe, Albert, Thomas, Lona and Eva Dugger. The funeral will be conducted tomorrow at 1 o'clock, by elder Ben F Harding, pastor of the Stiversville Christian Church, with burial in the new Ramey cemetery at that place.
