NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

To the left of the large dying beech tree is the open place pointed out by Jack Dugger as the location of the unmarked graves of Alexander & Ann and David & Katherine Dugger.

Subject: unmarked graves in New Ramey (Ramie or Ramah) Baptist Church Cemetery. 

Alexander and Ann Dugger as well as David and Katherine Dugger are supposed to be buried in New Ramey in unmarked graves. Virginia Dugger Tyree showed us the location where they are supposed to be buried. When we were down there last Saturday a huge limb or part of a tree had fallen over the site where they were buried. It will be cleaned up shortly according to James Glenn Dugger. I might mention that The New Ramey Cemetery Association, (not a formal organization-just a voluntary group) maintains the cemetery by voluntarily donated funds. The hat is passed at each Dugger reunion. There is a surplus of funds that have been donated in a CD of $ 5,000. Virginia Tyree is more familiar with the details about who is buried there. Jack Dugger
