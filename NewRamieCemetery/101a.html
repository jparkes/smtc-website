NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Notice the little stone in the far right of the photo between us and the large tree. It finally happened. I missed a stone. The photos I make allow me normally to thoroughly investigate the cemetery, but I am not perfect and I get no second chance. I failed to photograph this stone so I don't have a clue who it belongs to.

The little round top stone in the bottom left (same stone as above different angle of photography) forefront of the photo was overlooked. I failed to photograph it. It is broken and the two broken parts of the headstone are resting against what is probably the footstone. As stated above I have not a clue what the inscription says. It is a problem when I am 80 miles away and discover an error. I do not get a second chance to correct the omission in photography.
