NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Della Moore memorial erected by Joe M Moore, her husband.

"Death has robbed me of my treasure. This world holds no charm for me since My Dearist One is Gone. Worlds of the Lonely Companion, Joe M." (East side of memorial)

MOORE, Della, 16 Nov 1871 - 30 Jan 1892. (20-02-14.)'Wife Joe Moore.'
Erected in kind and lasting rememberance of Della the Beloved Wife of Joe Moore, Born Nov 16, 1874, Died Jan 30 1892 Age 20 Yrs 2 Mo 14 Days." (North side of memorial)
Photos 5/30/2005 and publication by Wayne Austin May 12 2006.
