NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Just in front of the pile of stone in the bottom of the photo is a blue looking new granite stone.  It is our subject stone below.

PULLEN, Nancy Elizabeth Scott Pullen, 1826 - 1876, "2nd wife of Elisha Pullen"
Hello Mary Bob McClain, The correct spelling should be Nancy Elizabeth Scott Pullen. It is not known where Nancy is buried. This marker was placed, at New Ramie, about 4 years ago by descendants to honor Nancy. An error was made in etching the stone. She was born 1826 and died March 1876. Since the placing of this stone, new evidence surfaced that leads us to think she may be buried near her parents and other family members at Chaffin Cemetery, Maury Co. info from her gg-granddaughter, Pat Cribbet Sullivan, IL 
New stone has inscription error. Given name should be Nancy, not Nacy)
