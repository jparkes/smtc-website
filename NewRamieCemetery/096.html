NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 
First stone to our right and back of the big tree.

Mr fred says the stone embedded in the elm tree is the replacement stone for Shadrack Dugger. The readable date 1896 confirms that when I looked at the photo of the underside.[WA 5/16/06] 


DUGGER, Shadrack S., 20 Feb 1814 - 7 Nov 1896. (82-08-18.) (When double stone was set, older single stone was leaned against elm tree. It is now deeply embedded in trunk of tree. 
and
DUGGER, Martha Jane Leneave, 31 Oct 1851 - 23 Dec 1910. (Daughter of Howell & Elizabeth Epperson Laneave.)
