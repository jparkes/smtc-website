NEW RAMIE CEMETERY, STIVERSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
UNMARKED grave, next to the south fence of the cemetery in the Dugger, Richardson, Scott area. (bottom photo). It is actually marked with common field stones.This tree has the old replaced Lucy Blankinship stone lying against it along with numerous footstones. Wish I had taken the time to get all the initials to see if there was one without a headstone. Can see an AED and MD? and that is all because grass covers the others. Most of those I see can be dated using styles of stone as after 1880. [WA 4/23/2006]
