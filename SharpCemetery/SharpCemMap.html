SHARP CEMETERY (WILLIAM ROBERDS, JR.)-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

From this map we find the GPS of the Sharp Cemetery as 35.62579 -86.86874. It is on the right side of the road turning east off of Carpenter Bridge Rd.
 
 This cemetery according to the Maps can be found on the left side of Carpenter Bridge Road going south for 1.5 miles from Highway 412. Look to your left for the Cemetery Sign then take an unimproved lane left for 200 yards. Do not visit this cemetery for any other reason than to honor the memory of the deceased who are interred here.

This is the road at your turnoff to the Sharp Cemetery. The roadbed is gravel so it is actually improved after all.
