SHARP CEMETERY (WILLIAM ROBERDS, JR.)-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Columbia herald., July 12, 1901- T. H. Carpenter died Thursday at the home of his father, Mr. T. B. Carpenter, near Sharp's Corner. Deceased was 26 years of age, and leaves a wife and one child. The funeral services were conducted Friday morning by Elder Newton Derryberry, and the interment was in the graveyard at that place.
