LIBERTY CHURCH OF CHRIST CEMETERY, Parsons Bend Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

Benjamin F. & Franklin B. Warfield, the only stone found inscribed in this cemetery today. The headstone of Franklin shown as a pile of crumbled rock on our right side of Benjamin F. Warfield memorial is crumbling badly and the inscription is partially lost. Johnie Eskew must have discovered this stone. It is not listed by any known previous family historian.

WARFIELD, Benjamon F., died 15 Oct 1848. Aged 13 years, 9 months. (brother of Franklin B. Warfield. It is believed these brothers died in a drowning on the Duck River at a place near here called Suck Island.
