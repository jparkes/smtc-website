LIBERTY CHURCH OF CHRIST CEMETERY, Parsons Bend Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Liberty Church of Christ Cemetery located on Parsons Bend Road in the western part of Maury County in the Parsons Bend of the Duck River.
14 Oct 1987: Across road from Burcham Pond on Parson's Bend Road behind Montsano Company property. The pond has now dried up and the area is being mined.
24 Mar 2009: From the intersection of Hampshire Pike & Williamsport Pike go about 8 miles and turn right on Craig Bridge Road. Follow that northeast about 1 & 1/2 miles and turn right on Parsons Bend Road. The cemetery is about 2 miles east on Parsons Bend Road on the left in the trees. Look for a new well made cattle gap entrance and the cemetery entrance will be the next one. One cannot drive into the cemetery area. It has many unmarked graves. It is being restored by Johnny Eskew, the Caretaker.
