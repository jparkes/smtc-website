McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


BENDERMAN, Mary E.(Evaline) McKissick, 13 Nov 1875 - 28 Jul 1946. Mother. Mary was born in Maury County, TN; and died in Corpus Christi, Nueces, Texas; buried Aug. 1, 1946. She was the daughter of William C. and Sophronia A. (Crosthwait) McKissick. She married John William Benderman and had eight children; he is buried in Reese's Chapel Cemetery. inscription - 'Prepare to meet me in heaven'.
The inscription also reads: "MOTHER" MARY E. (MC) BENDERMAN, NOV 13 1875 - JULY 28, 1943. The stone is next to and of the same style as her brother John William McKissick.
