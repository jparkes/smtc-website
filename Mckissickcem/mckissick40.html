McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McKISSICK, Robert Coleman., 31 Dec 1880 - 6 Jul 1943. Robert Coleman McKissick, born and died Maury County, buried July 8, 1943. Son of William C. and Sophronia A. (Crosthwait) McKissick. Never married. inscription - 'Prepare to meet me in heaven'
