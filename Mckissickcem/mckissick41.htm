McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McKISSICK, John William, 6 Jul 1883 - 16 Sep 1943. "Husband". Born and died in Maury County, TN; buried Sep. 17, 1943. Son of William C. and Sophronia A. (Crosthwait) McKissick. Lived in Decatur, Texas; married twice to widows with children, but apparently had no children of his own. inscription - 'Gone but not forgotten'.
 His stone is next to his sister Mary E. McKissick Benderman, 13 Nov 1875 - 28 Jul 1946.
