McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McKISSICK, Pvt. George M., 16 Mar 1891 - 16 Nov 1918. (Died In Camp Sheridan, Ohio; son of Mr & Mrs W. C. McKissick). 
35th Regiment 9th Division Depot Brigade (Training Battalion); died of bronchial pneumonia at Camp Sherman, Chillicothe, Ross, Ohio. 
Source is Great World War Gold Star Records. 
inscription reads - 'Here lies a brave soldier who died that his country might live'.
