McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McKISSICK, John S., 3 Mar 1871 - 25 Sep 1915. Woodmen of the World. 
John Smith McKissick, Sr., son of David V. and Eliza A. (Williams) McKissick. Husband of Mary Winnie (Fleming) McKissick (she is buried in Wheatland Cemetery, Dallas, TX). Tennessee Death Certificate says he died at Vanderbilt Hospital, Nashville, Davidson, TN from pistol shot wounds inflicted about 12 weeks prior to his death.
