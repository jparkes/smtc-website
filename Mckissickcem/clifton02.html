McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


CLIFTON, Mabel King, 1882 - 1912.
CLIFTON, Mabel Estelle King, 3 Feb 1887 - 21 Mar 1912. (Dau. of Edward Joshua King and Mary Ellenor McKissick King; "Obituary Nashville Banner, Thursday, 21 Mar 1912, pg. 14: Mrs. Mabel King Clifton, wife of H.F. Clifton, died this morning at a local infirmary. The remains are at Dorris, Karsch & Co.'s chapel, from which the funeral will take place tomorrow. The body will be taken over the Louisville & Nashville Railroad Train at 7:40 o'clock to Mt. Pleasant, Tenn., where the funeral and interment will take place. note tombstone say 1882 - 1912. Reference: Judy B. Forgos, 1 Mar 2009.)
