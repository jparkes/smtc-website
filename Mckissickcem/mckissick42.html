McKISSICK CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McKISSICK, Andrew Hershel, 16 Jan 1889 - 14 Aug 1929, (Son of William C. and Sophronia A. (Crosthwait) McKissick. Married Olene Gentry.) 
Andrew Hershel McKissick, born Maury County, TN, died Nashville, Davidson, TN, buried Aug. 16, 1929. Son of William C. and Sophronia A. (Crosthwait) McKissick. Married Olene Gentry in Erath County, TX, had two children. inscription - 'Dearest daddy, thou hast left us, Hear thy loss we deeply feel; But, tis God that hath bereft us, He can all our sorrows heal'.
