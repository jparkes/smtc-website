FARRAR CEMETERY, Rock Springs Rd., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is the Farrar Cemetery. The Farrar graves are outside the fence and the Jones Graves are inside, but the far side of the fence is collapsed. There are many graves marked by fieldstones. They are mostly located inside the chain linked fence where they are more likely to be left standing.

We are looking southeast at the fallen Farrar memorials. These are largely box tombs that are fallen. Four of them to be exact. As far as I could see there were no inscriptions on the tops of them.Â 

This photo was snapped while standing on the Rock Springs Road and shooting up across the cemetery. The cemetery is among the cedars and pines or the dark shadowed trees where their foliage remains in place in the winter.
