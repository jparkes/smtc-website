WORKMAN, Tildon W., 1876 - 1959 (husband of Ada F. "Addie" Kennedy; son of Isaiah Elson Workman and Ellen Williams) 
and
WORKMAN, Ada F. "Addie" Kennedy, 28 Dec 1877 - 10 Feb 1947 (wife of Tildon W. Workman; daughter of Andrew Knox Polk Kennedy and Easter Louella Henderson). 
Someone here has made this  inexpensive memorial from concrete. If the concrete used is considered "Hydraulic" (high strength) then the stone might outlast a granite memorial. Actually the lasting properties of a stone depends more on how it handles cold, heat, water & ice or whether it is porous or not.
