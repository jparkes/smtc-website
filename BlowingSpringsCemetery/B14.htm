BENNETT, James R., 1794 – 1827

DAVIS, Rachel Alexander Bennett Davis, but no relationship is listed or dates for her, but all available research has it that she is the wife of James R. Bennett. Rachel Alexander Bennett married John Davis on 23 Feb 1828 in Maury County and had six more children.
