BLOWING SPRINGS CEMETERY, (SOUTH WEST), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

Mt Pleasant Record 12 Aug 1932.


Tennessee death Certificate dated 2 Sep 1932.
PIERCE, Fred, 8 Aug 1871 - 8 Aug 1932. (son of John & Elizabeth Ann Osborne Pierce; 1st married to Mandy Seagraves & 2nd to Dolly Kennedy; died from a fall from a truck.)
Fred Pierce married first to Mandy Seagraves, 2nd to Dollie Kennedy. Fred is buried at Blowing Springs in an unmarked grave. You will notice on his death certificate it states Chumley Cemetery. Some people would refer to Blowing Springs Cemetery as Chumley Cemetery. I work with one of Fred's granddaughters, Wanda Stewart. Wanda said Fred was buried at Blowing Springs, but the family is not sure of the location. She said when her grandmother Dollie passed away, they buried her in the area where they thought Fred was buried. Unlike Fred's grave Dollie has a headstone.
Charlotte Colvett, 3 Jun 2009.
Information and documents sent in by Charlotte Colvett.  News Article published in the Mt. Pleasant Record 12 Aug 1932.
