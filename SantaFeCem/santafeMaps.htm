SANTA FE CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY


This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Santa Fe Cemetery. 

Santa Fe Cemetery, an aerial view showing the cemetery just south & across the road from the high school spanning more than 200 yards north and south along old Santa Fe Pike. That is along the east side of the road and about 120 yards up the slope. The yellow arrow points to the center and the entrance road & sign is just to the south of the yellow arrow tip. One can see the faint memorials here as small rough dots of north south rows on this map.
