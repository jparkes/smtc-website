SANTA FE CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY


This view from the road looks southeast across the southern area or newer areas of the cemetery. The cemetery tended to expand to our right or to the south. This is a very large city cemetery of at least 850 burials.

Santa Fe Cemetery  from the entrance road looking back northwest. One can see the Santa Fe High School across the road.
