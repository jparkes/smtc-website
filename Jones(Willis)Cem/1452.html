JONES (WILLIS) CEMETERY, (Santa Fe) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Rebecca Jones inscribed on the our left side

Rebecca A. Jones. When the first granite monuments like this one started appearing the inscribers did not know how to inscribe the stone deeply enough for it to be easily read, thus hard to read stones in the early 1900s. Granite is believed to be the best stone of for grave monuments known to be very hard.

JONES, Rebecca A., 4 Jul 1830 - 25 Dec 1866. (nee Satterfield; 2nd wife James Willis Jones.)
