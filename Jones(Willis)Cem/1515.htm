JONES (WILLIS) CEMETERY, (Santa Fe) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Sarah Ellen, daughter of Willis & Elizabeth Jones., stone fragmented into many pieces. If the pieces are not gathered and glued back together soon they will float away in the hard rain washes and sink into the ground far from the remaining stone and be lost. Sometimes I see this happening and make an attempt to gather the parts and I find them as far away as 30 feet. 

Sarah Ellen, daugher of Willis & Elizabeth Jones.
JONES, Sarah Ellen, 26 Aug 1834 - (5 Jun 1838) "Aged 3 years 9 months & 9 Days, Dau. Willis & Elizabeth Jones." (death date broken off into the ground, copied from MCTC)
