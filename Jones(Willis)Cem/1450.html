JONES (WILLIS) CEMETERY, (Santa Fe) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The large monument in the center is for James Willis Jones & his three wives. Just to the right we see his "Confederate Cross of Honor"

James Jones inscribed on the our right side

C.S.A. iron cross monument given to soldiers who remained loyal to the Confederacy thru out the Civil War, 1861-1865.

James Jones inscription 

 Inscription highlighted in black
