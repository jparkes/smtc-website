Harriet J. Jones, stone lying on the ground on its right side propped against the stone behind it, broken and out of place. 

Sarah Jane Jones

JONES, Harriet J., 15 Oct 1816 - 29 Jun 1849. "Consort of William J.(Joseph) Jones." (the nine of 1849 is very faint almost shaled off). 
This stone is showing major signs of failure and will not last much longer. I will transcribe it here: "IN -- memory -- of --Harriet J. Consort -- of William J. Jones -- born Oct the 15th -- 1816 Died June the -- 29th 184(9?)"
