JONES (WILLIS) CEMETERY, (Santa Fe) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Elizabeth Jones, stone leaning into the ground barely standing.

Elizabeth Jones, wife of Willis Jones.
JONES, Elizabeth, 25 Mar 1792 - 9 Nov 1834. (42-07-15.) "Wife of Willis Jones." (nee Ghee or Gee.) Inscribed as follows: "In memory of ELIZABETH JONES Born the 25th of Mar 1792 Died the 9th of November 1834. Aged 42 Years 7 Months & 15 Days."
