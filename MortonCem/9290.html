MORTON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

CARPENTER, Bennie A., 13 Jul 1888 - 11 Sep 1924.
CARPENTER, Ollie M., 16 Nov 1889 - 13 Sep 1979. Maury County native died in Lewisburg, Marshall County.

Ollie Carpenter, 89, died Thursday at Lewisburg Community Hospital. Funeral services were conducted this afternoon at Bills and McGaugh Funeral Home, with burial in Morton Cemetery in the Berlin Community. He was a Maury County native. Survivors include a daughter, Mrs. Clifford Harris, Lewisburg; two grandchildren; two great-grandchildren; and one great-great-grandchild.
The Daily Herald, Columbia, TN, Fri., 9/14/1979, pg. 14.
