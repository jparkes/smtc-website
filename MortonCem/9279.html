GREGG, Johnny Ray, 12 May 1954 - 19 May 2014.

Johnnie Gregg, age 60 of Lewisburg, died Monday, May 19, 2014 in Vanderbilt Medical Center.
Mr. Gregg was a native of Marshall County, Tennessee and he had been a packer with the Marshall County Impact Center for over thirty years.
He is survived by one brother, James Gregg of Oregon, longtime friends, Pam Ward, Jerry Bunn and Paul Taylor and caregiver for six years, Joyce Asiuwhu.
The family wishes to thank the staff of the Marshall County Impact Center and their program director, Lynne Holt for the watchful care of Johnnie over the years.
Graveside services will be conducted by Minister, Martha Hargrove Saturday, May 24, 2014 at 12:00 p.m. in Morton Cemetery.
The family will be at London Funeral Home Saturday from 10:00 a.m. until 11:45 a.m. for visitation.
