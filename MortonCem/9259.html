THORNHILL, Raymond E., 12 Dec 1905 - 23 Feb 1990. (son of the Luther & Fannie Mae Thornhill. 1st married to the Ruby

THORNHILL, Ruby L., 11 Oct 1908 - 6 Dec 1960.
Mr. Raymond Thornhill
Retired farmer, 84
COLUMBIA -- Mr. Raymond E. Thornhill, 84, died Friday in Maury Regional Hospital. (No date - some time in 1990.)
Services was held at 2 p.m. today at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home. Burial was in Morton Cemetery.
Mr. Thornhill was a retired farmer.
He was a native of Maury County and a son of the late Luther and
Fannie Mae Thornhill. He was first married to the former Ruby
Hardison, who died in 1960. He was later married to the former
Gladys Holt Cross. Mr. Thornhill was a member of Eastside Church of Christ.
Survivors besides his wife include a stepdaughter, Norma Jean
Rush of Columbia; five stepgrand-children; and a stepgreat-grand-
child.
