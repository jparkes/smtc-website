MORTON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HARDISON, A. J. (Andrew J.), 1 Nov 1860 - 11 Jul 1943. (Son Marshall E. & Eliza Olds Hardison.
The Hardisons' were early settlers in Maury Co., and have a long list of descendants in this section. One of them Mr. Andrew J. Hardison was born in Nov. 1, 1856, is a son of Marshall E. and Eliza (Olds) Hardison, and has been a lifelong farmer. He married Millie, dau. of Ira and Mary (Franks) Hardison. Their children are Clarence, Maxwell, Fannie May, Mary, Rubie, Chesley, and Dixie. Mr. Hardison has a good home at Leftwich, and a 225-acre farm devoted to general agriculture and stock.
