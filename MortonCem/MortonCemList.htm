MORTON CEMETERY,  HIGHWAY 431, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
(aka HARDISON FAMILY CEMETERY.)

This cemetery was surveyed beginning in the front (east side) and progressing back west along the north south rows. The lower photo number (correspond with the file number) are on the east side. 

Go out New Sowell Mill Pike about two miles past Antioch Church of Christ, turn right on A. Sampson Rd.; go three miles, turn right into Morton Cemetery road and cross Cedar Creek and before you is the cemetery. Do not visit this cemetery unless your intentions are honorable.
