MORTON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HARDISON, T. C. 'Squire Tom", 30 Dec 1829 - 9 Nov 1912, (Son, Joel & Jane Howe Long Hardison.)
"Squire Tom" lived his entire life on Cedar Creek in eastern Maury Co., Tennessee. He was a prosperous farmer and served his county as magistrate for 40 years, much of this time being during the hectic days of the Civil War and following. He married, 1st, Frances "Fannie" Fox and 2nd, Temple Carpenter. He fathered nine children.
