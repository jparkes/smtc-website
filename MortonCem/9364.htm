MORTON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Hannah E. Tate. This broken stone has the same inscription as the one below. They are not near each other so the above broken & repaired one is probably located at the head of the grave and the one below is a replacement probably near her parents.

TATE, Hannah E., 23 Dec 1879 - 12 Aug 1881. "dau. of J. H. (James H.) & M .F. D. Tate."
