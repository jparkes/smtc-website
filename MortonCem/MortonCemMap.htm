MORTON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows us the Morton Cemetery GPS location.

 Go out New Sowell Mill Pike about two miles past Antioch Church of Christ, turn right on Arthur Sampson Road. Go three miles, turn right into Morton Cemetery road and cross Cedar Creek and before you is this modern well kept cemetery. Do not visit this cemetery unless your intentions are honorable.
 Information by Wayne Austin 31 Aug 2008. Map from MS Terra mapping service modified for this site.
