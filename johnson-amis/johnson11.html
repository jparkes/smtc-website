JOHNSON-AMIS CEMETERY-CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

JOHNSON, William, 27 Dec 1749 - 8 Feb 1830 (80-01-12.) "To the Memory of William johnson. born Dec the 27th 1749 & departed this life on the 8th of Feb 1830. Aged 80 years 1 month & 12 days." As you can see this stone is peeling away at the bottom. Won't be long before the inscription will begin to peel away. William & Sarah's stone sits on the western side of the area inside the rock wall with Williams on our left and Sarah's stone on the right. The two stones face east. They are about five paces from the edge of the rock wall.
