JOHNSON-AMIS CEMETERY-CULLEOKA TENNESSEE


A view looking northwest across the cemetery from the west wall. The cemetery has been lightly weeded and stones uncovered and photographed. Out in the center is the stand up stone of William Johnson and his wife Sarah. Between us and the William Johnson family stones in our near view is the fallen memorial of Alexander & Mary Johnson composed of four parts: (1) the large base resting on the older abandoned Box tomb and (2) a small cap further left almost covered in brush and (3) a block that contains the inscription just the left of the large base, and (4) the shaft lying on the ground behind the base.
