JOHNSON-AMIS CEMETERY-CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

AMIS, Amanda Malvena, 23 Jul 1835 - 31 Aug 1840 (3-1-8.) "This stone is erected in the memory of AMANDA MALVENA AMIS A Beloved daughter of Lewis & Louisa Y. Amis. Born July 23rd 1835 _ _ _ _ _ "
The death date is shown in Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries by Fred Hawkins as 31 Aug 1840. This stone has been shattered by vandals. It is hard rock with no appearance of weakness or cracks that cause stones to break up naturally.
