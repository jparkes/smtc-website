A view looking northwest across the cemetery from the southern position.

This is a (2009) restored view of the cemetery with most if not all the memorials standing up. The tall monument on our left actually stands upon the stair step base on our right, but it is a considerable project to level and strengthen such a base to properly hold the heavy top in place without future shifting and again falling.
