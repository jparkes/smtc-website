JOHNSON-AMIS CEMETERY-CULLEOKA TENNESSEE

This is a view of the cemetery looking east at the thick brush and rocks. This cemetery is spread over a small area about 60 feet square surrounded by a rock fence that is covered by vines. The rock fence is tumbling down in places. There are memorials in this photo that do not show up due to the thick brush. I did some trimming with a RYOBI weed whacker before starting the photography otherwise some of the stones would never have been found.
Photo by WA. 27 Oct 20003.
