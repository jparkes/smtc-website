JOHNSON-AMIS CEMETERY-CULLEOKA TENNESSEE


A view looking north across the cemetery from the  south wall. Far out we see the box tomb of Jonathan David Hunt an infant. The other box tombs in that row are for the small children of Lewis & Louisa Y. Amis. The box tomb to the right is for the Walker family infant.
