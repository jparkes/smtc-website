JOHNSON-AMIS CEMETERY-CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

AMIS, Op...., 26 Jun 1843 (01-10-21.) "Op_ _ _  Johnson the 4th child of Lewis & Louisa Y. Amis Died in infancy at the age of one year 10 months 21 days June 26th 1843. This is the next to last (south) of a row of children's (small) box tombs that are lined up north to south. Only old name I can think of above that fits is "Ophelia."
