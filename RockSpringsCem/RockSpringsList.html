ROCK SPRINGS CEMETERY, ROCK SPRINGS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The photographer started on the north side of the cemetery on the row next to the cemetery road and progressed south on the first row but went thru all the stones east and west and continued that Zigzag approach for this cemetery (to minimize walking) finishing up in the far southwestern most section next to the highway. The first memorial surveyed was for Chad Thomas Turnage. As stated, to save time where the stones were skimpy I covered multiple rows in sequence.

Located on the new TVA road, about 1/4 mile south of Rock Springs
Baptist Church on the eastern side of the road.
