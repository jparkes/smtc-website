ERVIN, Gerry Dwight, 22 May 1958 - 5 Feb 2013. (s/o Herman and Lula Mae Ervin; Veteran Army National Guard.) obit

Obituary

Gerry Dwight Ervin, age 54, a resident East Sheepneck Road in Culleoka, passed away Tuesday, February 6, 2013.

Funeral services will be Sunday, February 10, 2013 at 2:00 PM in the chapel of Heritage Funeral Home with Rev. Dwain Brown officiating. Burial will follow in Rock Springs Cemetery. Visitation will be Saturday, February 9, 2013 from 4:00 � 7:00 PM at Heritage Funeral Home. Heritage Funeral Home is assisting the family and in charge of the arrangements.

A native of Columbia, Gerry was born on May 22, 1958 to the late Herman and Lula Mae Ervin. He was of the Baptist faith and worked as a farmer. Gerry served our country in the Army National Guard. He loved working on heavy equipment and enjoyed being with his family.
