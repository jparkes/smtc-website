ROCK SPRINGS CEMETERY, ROCK SPRINGS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

JONES, Rayburn Gordon, 18 Nov 1938 - 26 Sep 2014. (s/o Ward Carmack and Irene Dooley Jones.)

Obituary

Rayburn Gordon Jones, 75, retired welder, maintenance worker, and resident of Nashville, died Friday, September 26, 2014 at Mt. Pleasant Healthcare & Rehab.

Burial will be in Rock Springs Cemetery.
The Maury County native was the son of the late Ward Carmack Jones and Irene Dooley Jones. Mr. Jones was a twenty-five year employee of Do-All Tennessee Company and retired from Belmont University. He was of the Baptist faith.

Survivors include two sisters, Ann Carpenter and Dorothy Lawrence, both of Florence, Alabama; sister-in-law, Norma Younger of Santa Fe; lifelong friends, Charles & Judy Lee of Culleoka; and several nieces and nephews

He was preceded in death by his brother, James Ward Jones; sisters, Alice Faye Pearson, Mary Scott "Scottie" George, and Mildred Elaine Jones; and brothers-in-law, J. L. Carpenter, Laurel Edwin Lawrence, and Dr. C. D. George.

Active pallbearers will be Charles Lee, Tim Lee, Colton Wilson, Ricky Peden, Ervin "Ed" Lee, and Troy Derryberry. Honorary pallbearers will include Chris Gorbatoff, Jannette McKennon of Avalon Hospice, and members of Rock Springs Baptist Church.

 Obit sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 3 Oct 2014.
