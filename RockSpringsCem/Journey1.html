ROCK SPRINGS CEMETERY, ROCK SPRINGS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

JOURNEY, Jean Marie Graham, 1933 - 6 Feb 2011. (w/o Thomas W. Journey, Sr..)

Obituary

Jean Marie Graham Journey, age 77, of Tucker, GA passed away Sunday, February 6, 2011. She was preceded in death by her husband Thomas W. Journey, Sr. in 2010.

She is survived by, children, Thomas W. Journey, Jr., Virginia Journey, Sandra Swann, Shannon Dixon and Roxanne Nieman; grandchildren, Jessica Journey, Miranda Swann Haley, and Justin Nieman; sisters, Marian Dearing and Betty Gatewood.

Mrs. Journey was a member of Lawrenceville Church of Christ, and taught piano in Tucker, GA for over 50 years.

Funeral services will be held Wednesday, February 9, 2010 at 5:00 P.M. at Wages & Sons Stone Mountain Chapel with Minister Alan Sheppherd officiating. A graveside service will be held Thursday, at 3:00 P.M. CST at Rock Springs Cemetery in Columbia, TN. The family will receive friends on Tuesday from 6-8PM at Wages & Sons Funeral Home, Stone Mountain, GA Published in The Atlanta Journal-Constitution on February 7, 2011

Obit sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 3 Oct 2014.
