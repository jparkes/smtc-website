ROCK SPRINGS CEMETERY, ROCK SPRINGS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BARKER, Rayburn Charles "Charlie", 14 Aug 1921 - 26 May 2008. (Maury Co. native; son of C.U. Barker and Annie Hardison Barker; 1939 grad of Central High School; 15 Sep 1947 married Mary Lynn Barker; Veteran U.S. Army Air Corp, WWII; preceded in death by son, Charles Barker.)
