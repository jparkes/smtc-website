This publication is based on an on-site visit to the Dockery 8 Apr 2009 by Cecil Wayne Austin. The cemetery was photographed complete, but as noted one stone Mr Fred & previous researchers found was missing and may be below the ground. Other publications where information can be found on this cemetery are: Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries by Fred Lee Hawkins Jr. listed 4 Feb 1987 on page 245. Also published in  the book "They Passed This Way", 1963, by Evelyn Shackleford & Marise Lightfoot, page B-46.
