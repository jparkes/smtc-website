HUNTER CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is the view of the cemetery as you climb the slope of the hill from the private road below.

This cemetery certainly highlights the difficulties of childbirth and being the bearer of motherhood was for the ladies. Lined up are three stones of deceased wives of a father J. M. Hunter, b. 1863 and his son J. Norris Hunter b. 1895. The small stone is an infant of J. Norris Hunter.
