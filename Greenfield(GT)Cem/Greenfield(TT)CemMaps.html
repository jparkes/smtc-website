GREENFIELD (THOMAS T.) CEMETERY, Greenfield Bend Road (West) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the locator coordinates for this cemetery.
Map from Topozone modified for this site by Wayne Austin. 5 May 2009.

This aerial map above shows where the cemetery is located pointed out by a yellow arrow. It is actually a thicket unless it is cleaned up and then one can see the Brick & Limestone Wall surrounding the cemetery. From Williamsport Pike (Hwy 50) in Williamsport, just past the Duck River Bridge, turn (left) southwest onto Greenfield Bend Road and go 1 & 1/2 miles. The cemetery is located in a Soy Bean field about 200 yards from Greenfield Bend Road on left. The red arrow above shows the location along Greenfield Bend Road.
