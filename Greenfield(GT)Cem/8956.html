GREENFIELD (THOMAS TRUMAN) CEMETERY, Greenfield Bend Road (West) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Capt Walter K. Wheeler memorial.

WHEELER, Capt. Walter K., native of Maryland who died on 19 Sep 1833 in his 60th year. Many years a member of Presbyterian Church. Inscription given him by his sincere friend, Dr. G.T. Greenfield. An honest man, the noblest work of God.
Photos dated 27 Jan 2009 and published here 6 May 2009 by Wayne Austin.
