GREENFIELD (THOMAS TRUEMAN) CEMETERY, Greenfield Bend Road (West) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

In this photo we see the footstone out from the center for the grave of G.T. Greenfield. Nearer the bottom we see the corner of a stone protruding from the ground marking the grave of an unknown interment. Due to the ground being covered in brush I did not get a good view, but believe there were no other unknown fieldstone markers depicting graves of lost souls at least lost as to who they were to us.
 Photos & information developed 27 Jan 2009. Published here 4 May 2009 by Wayne Austin.
