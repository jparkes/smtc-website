BROWN (Henry B.) CEMETERY (Joe Peay Cemetery Road) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates where the cemetery can be found. It is marked here about 40 feet to the east of the actual place.

This Aerial view shows the various roads nearby and the route to the cemetery. To get there:
In the western area of Maury County & on the west side of I-65 and visible in the winter time from I-65.
From Kedron Go east 1 miles and turn left or north on Joe Peay Cemetery Road. Go 1/4 mile and turn right into the road to the cemetery. Continue as this road bends left then back right and on around to the cemetery on your right. This leg of Joe Peay Cemetery road does not cross I-65.

This is a little clearer map using a long yellow arrow to point the cemetery out. It is located in the little clearing visible as a tiny lighter green square on this map.
