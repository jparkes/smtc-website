WATSON, (JOHN W.) CEMETERY, (Fred white Rd), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Columbia follow Hwy 50 several miles and turn north (left) onto Fred white Road before it goes under I-65. Go 1 3/4 miles north and look east (right) at a trailer house 200 yards off the road. The cemetery is 50 yards in back of that home under trees. The family living there have a large dog so be careful. There was a tense moment getting permission, and keeping the dog friendly, but all was well in the end. Mr. Fred Hawkins mentioned in 1987 the cemetery was in the middle of a junk yard. That is no longer so.
