ZION PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

John James Stephen

STEPHENSON, John James, 10 Sep 1811 - 26 Aug 1838. (Son of James White & Mary Fleming Stephenson.) "John James Stephenson. Who was born Sept. 10th 1811 and departed this life August 26th 1838. Aged 26 years 11 months and 15 days. Life how short. Eternity how long."
