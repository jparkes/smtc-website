ZION PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Webb Family Memorial

Erected to the memory of the wife & children of Wm. H. Webb.

John & Ben

Mary, Mrs Elizabeth & Wm Henry

Louisa, Ann & Virginia.
WEBB, John Pomphrett, died 30 Aug 1852. Age 4 days, (son of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB, Benjamin Franklin, died 12 Jul 1853. 10 years. (son of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB, Mary Frances, died 24 Jul 1853. 19 years. (daughter of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB. Mrs. Elizabeth W., died 24 Jul 1853. 40 years. (wife of William H. Webb)
WEBB, William Henry, died 26 Aug 1853. 3 years. (son of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB, Louisa Catherine, died 20 Jul 1853. 8 years. (daughter of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB, Ann Smith, died 21 Jul 1853. 14 years. (daughter of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB, Virginia Carolina, died 21 Jul 1853. 17 years. (daughter of William H. & Elizabeth W. Webb)
WEBB: This large monument "Erected to the memory of the children & wife of Wm. H. Webb".: 
(Note: This family was wiped out by an epidemic of typhoid fever. See Blackman and Tate Cemeteries listed in, "Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries", by Fred Hawkins for other families that died in the same time period.)
