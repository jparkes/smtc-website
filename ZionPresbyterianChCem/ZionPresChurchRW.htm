ZION PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

A photo of the bronze memorial of the Rev. War Soldiers buried at Zion Presbyterian Church Cemetery.

Inscribed as:
THESE REVOLUTIONARY WAR SOLDIERS ARE BURIED IN ZION CEMETERY. MANY WERE INVOLVED IN THE ESTABLISHMENT OF ZION CHURCH, WHICH ENSURED THAT FUTURE GENERATIONS WOULD HEAR THE GOSPEL.

JAMES ARMSTRONG           JOSEPH LINN            JOHNS MATTHEWS            
JOSHUA  FRIERSON           WILLIAM LINN         DR. SAMUEL MAYES
ROBERT FRIERSON             WM. LITTLEFIELD      JAMES W. STEPHENSON
WM. FRIERSON JR.             JOHN MACON            WILLIAM WALSTON
BENJAMIN HARPER             DAVID MATTHEWS   WILLIAM WATKINS.

DEDICATED JANUARY 30, 1993 TNSSAR
