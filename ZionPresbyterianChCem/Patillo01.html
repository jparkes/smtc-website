Early information is sometimes confusing on the Pattillo's. I have the following notes for John Franklin Pattillo:

John Franklin Pattillo was born 1770 or 1778 and was the son of Reverend Henry Pattillo, a well know Presbyterian Minister, and Mary Mindred "Mille" Anderson. Reverend Pattillo was from Kinnesswood, Portmoak parish, Scotland. He immigrated along with his brother, George Alexander Pattillo, in about 1740 to Virginia. John was one of eight children.

He married Harriet Hyde in 1826 probably in Thompson Co., New York. He and Harriet had four children: Harriet Caroline Pattillo, and Robert Goodloe Pattillo.

Harriet Hyde was the daughter of Robert Hyde and Elizabeth Goodloe Harper.

Don't have much more. I hope this is helpful. My husband Eddie is a descendant of George Alexander Pattillo.
Mary Jane Hobbs Pattillo
