ZION PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

FRIERSON, Robert Luther, 1 Jan 1803 - 13 Mar 1857. "b. in Williamsburg Dist., S.C.;' moved to Maury Co. 1812."
INSCRIPTION: "SACRED -- to the memory of -- ROBERT LUTHER FRIERSON -- who was born in Williamsburg -- District South Carolina Jany 1st -- 1803. He removed to Maury Co. Tenn. -- A.D. 1812 where he resided till the -- time of his death March 13th 1857. -- He was a man of unflinching integ -- rity, honest and truthful; an oblig -- ing neighbor; a good citizen; a firm -- friend; a kind husband; and father -- and a sincere christian. For many -- years a member of the Presbyterian -- Church he lived trusting in Christ -- for salvation and rejoicing in hope -- of the glory of God. He realized and -- exemplified the truthfulness  -- of the declaration of the Psalmist "Bles -- sed is the man that walketh not in -- the counsel of the ungodly, nor -- standeth in the way of the sinners, nor -- sitteth in the seat of the scornful, -- but his delight is in the law of the -- Lord; and in his law doth he medi -- tate day and night"
