JENNINGS CEMETERY (Victor Road off of Hwy 243) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The red check shows the location of the Jennings Cemetery. The black retangle and squares are the facility assets of Stauffer Chemical Company.


This map shows the Jennings Cemetery southeast of Mt Pleasant Tennessee. It is located on what used to be the Stauffer Chemical Company Property. The cemetery is shown with the red dot as all others are on the above map.
 Location 1988: South of Mt. Pleasant take Mt. Joy Rd. to Stauffer Chemical Co. plant. Take road that goes to right of plant and
continue for almost a mile. The cemetery is in a well-kept plot, across the road from the Organic Products Plant.
Location 2010: Go south on Mt Pleasant Bypass and turn east or right on E. Plant Entrance Road (aka Victor Road) and go about a mile. The cemetery is on the right of the road at what was the East Entrance Gate to the Stauffer Chemical Plant.
Location as found by J.B. & Ann Lamar 8 May 2011:Located off Hwy 243 on Mt. Joy Rd turn right on Victor Rd go .7 miles, cemetery on right up a small hill just a few feet from road, no gates to unlock or pass thru. It is a public road now.
