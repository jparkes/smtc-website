CROSS BRIDGES METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The main highway is Hampshire Pike, Big Bigby Creek is shown, Community of Crossbridges.

Located in Cross Bridges, next to (or just west) of the Methodist Church on Hampshire Pike. The cemetery is shown at the tip of the yellow arrow. The Church is in the trees 1/2 inch from the cemetery on the right.
