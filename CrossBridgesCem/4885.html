CROSS BRIDGES METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


CECIL, John Wilkerson, III, 5 Nov 1923 - 27 Sep 2006, Pvt US Army WWII. (Maury Co. native; born on Cecil Family Farm in Cross Bridges; son of John Wilkerson Cecil, II, and Lula Clementine Lovell Cecil; mar. 53 years to Florence Edity Duke Cecil who died 10 Aug 2001; 1942 grad. Central High School; owned Cecil's Store, farmer; preceded in death by brother, William Scott Cecil, brothers-in-law, William Rainey and Ralph Duke, Sr.)
