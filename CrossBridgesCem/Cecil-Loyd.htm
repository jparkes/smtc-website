CROSS BRIDGES METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



CECIL, Loyd, 24 Aug 1833 - 1 Nov 1920. "Son of Wm. Ingram & Elizabeth Ribble Cecil; C.S.A. Veteran, grandson Samuel Cecil, ggrandson of Zachariah Cecil, and gggrandson of Samuel Cecil, of England."
CECIL, Virginia W. "Jennie" Porter, 2 Nov 1839 - 24 Feb 1893. "Dau. Dr. S. S. & C. W. Porter; mar. to Loyd Cecil on 2 Apr 1860. "Our Children: William Porter, Kate Ribble. Maury B., Bessie Todd, & Nettie May Cecil."


Loyd Cecil was the son of William Ingram & Elizabeth Ribble Cecil. (see Cecil Cemetery) He was born in Indiana on 24 Aug 1833. He married Virginia "Jennie" Winslow Porter and she bore him two sons and three daughters. During the Civil War Loyd served as a Private in Company E, 6th Tenn. Cavalry. He died on 1 Nov 1920, at the age of 87 years.
