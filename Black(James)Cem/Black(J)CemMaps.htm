BLACK (James) CEMETERY, Spring Hill, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the locator coordinates for this cemetery.
Map from Topozone modified for this site by Wayne Austin. 21 Sep 2009.

From Spring Hill, take Beechcroft Road (about 1 mile) (Hwy 247 west) to Kleburn/(Claiborne) Rd. Turn left (or south) and go about 8 tenths of a mile and turn right into the private farm (with permission) road. Go 300 yards and the cemetery is on your left about 200 yards due south of you out in an open field under a giant red oak tree. The tree has grown up in the midst of the graves.
