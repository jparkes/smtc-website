SHAW CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Inside the fence in the far corner on the other side of the Hackberry Trees is the Professor Shaw Memorial.

The fallen stone of Professor R. Barclay Shaw.

SHAW, Prof. Barclay R., 28 Jan 1865 - 15 Oct 1895, (Son of J. P. & Margaret Shaw: Note: Census records indicate he was born in 1868; also note s/o William F. A. & Mary A. E. Renfro Shaw.)
 This stone is fallen and very large.  The base must be dug up and leveled and then supported by a sub base of stone and then the stone can be set up.
