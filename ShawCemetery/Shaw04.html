SHAW CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Inside the fence just to the left at the base of the crooked tree leaning to our left is the Mary Shaw memorial. This stone is broken and was actually lying out side the fence. I could find no evidence of the remaining part of the stone so I set it up back inside the fence where it would be protected from the foraging livestock. 

SHAW, Mary E., 12 Feb 1856 - 27 Oct 1893. "Wife of W. F. A. Shaw." (Nee: Mary E. McDonald; w/o William F. A. Shaw; Stone broken.)
