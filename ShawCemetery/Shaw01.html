SHAW CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Outside the fence is the Cordie Shaw memorial. The base is over in front of the corner of the wrought iron fence with the other two pieces of the memorial lying on its back further to your left (photo below). There is an unmarked fieldstone on its left and one on the right marking other graves.

SHAW, Cordie P., 4 Feb 1885 - 6 Jul 1887. (Stone broken into three pieces.)
Information and photos by Wayne Austin 12/30/2005.
