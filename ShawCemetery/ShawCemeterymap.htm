SHAW CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Topographical Map showing the location of Shaw Cemetery.
From Culleoka go east on Mooresville Pike (Hwy 373) for one mile passing Friendship Church on your left. The cemetery is on your left on the hillside behind the Andrews house. This land owner was so in 1987 when Fred Hawkins visited. I was unable to ask their names in 2005. After permission climb the hill going up from the house about 450 yards as shown on these maps. You will find yourself among groves of cedar trees. Look for a thicket with three crooked Hackberry Trees surrounded by a wrought iron fence in need of repair at the top edge of the line of grove of Cedars.
Actually The owners have a farm road going around the east side (your right) of the house. Follow that and it will come to a gate that turns west. Go thru the gate and (close it behind you) immediately turn back up the hill (going north) and continue thru the Cedar Tree thickets until you reach the cemetery. When you exit the Cedar groves the cemetery is along that area.

Marked here on this map is a check where the few trees are standing over the graveyard. This depiction in the right lower corner shows the GPS: Latitude N 35.47472 & Longitude: W 86.96398.
