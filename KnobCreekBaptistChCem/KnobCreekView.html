KNOB CREEK BAPTIST CHURCH CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


We are looking at the sign, gate and out over the Knob Creek Baptist Church Cemetery. Many of Maury Counties finest are interred here. The founding of settlements in this valley goes all the way back prior to when Maury County existed. The Knob Creek Valley is rich in soils and resources to support the many families that have flourished here and that will continue to be so.
