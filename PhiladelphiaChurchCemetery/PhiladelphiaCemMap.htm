PHILADELPHIA CHURCH OF CHRIST CEMETERY, FRED WHITE RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Two maps above show the location in a red X. Directions: Turn north off of the Old Lewisburg Pike (Highway 50) onto Howard's Bridge Road. Most maps and signs call this road Fred White Road) Go about two miles. The cemetery is behind the Philadelphia Church of Christ on the left.
