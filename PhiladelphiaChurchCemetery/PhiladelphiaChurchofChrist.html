PHILADELPHIA CHURCH OF CHRIST CEMETERY, FRED WHITE RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Philadelphia Church of Christ.


This is a photo of the Philadelphia Church of Christ. It was not named after the City Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, but after the Philadelphia Church in the New Testament section of the Bible. Behind the building is our subject cemetery.
Visit here for worship, memories and reverence and you are a welcome soul. Go there with mischievous aims and you will be figuratively strung up to the nearest tree by the folks who keep close watch here.
Photo by Wayne Austin 12/27/2006.
