HAYWOOD CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
  
This map shows us the GPS Coordinates of the Haywood Cemetery.

 Go north on Knob Creek Rd. and take first road that turns right, which is Dowell Branch Rd. Go one mile and enter private residential road on left. Cross Dowell Branch Creek and bend toward right then back left and up the drive toward the house. Cemetery is on your right just before you reach the house. Be sure to get permission.
