WEST (John S.) MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Mapping the Location

WEST, John S., 1798 - 2 Sep 1856.
WEST, Susannah, 8 May 1798 - 1885
WEST, T. P., birth date unknown - 9 Aug 1914.

According to Mr Fred Hawkins' survey there are five box tombs, with two of them being inscribed. Three others exist but are broken and evidently unreadable. There are enough assets there to provide a great opportunity to find the cemetery. Especially in winter months.
 

Compiled by C. Wayne Austin 26 Dec 2014. Added here 27 Dec 2014. Included in the book by Fred Hawkins Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries, Page 173. Not visited by C. Wayne Austin. From the maps it is clearly marked & now easy to find if I ever get back up that way to visit it.
