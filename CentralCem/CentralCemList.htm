CENTRAL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

In Culleoka go north to the Central Church and turn east or right on Scribner's Mill Road. This is actually midways between Glendale & Culleoka. The cemetery is just after you turn into Scribner's Mill Road out in the field in back of the church. It is about 100 yards from the church and 50 yards from Scribner's Mill Road.
