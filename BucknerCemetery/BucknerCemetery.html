BUCKNER FAMILY CEMETERY, GIPSON HOLLOW ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
These instructions rely here on the instructions Mr. Fred Hawkins made in 1987 & the USGS mapping of the Lat & Long coordinates.  Locator Maps 


CATES, Infant daughter of G. W. & H. J., d. in embryo, 19 Mar 1859.
CATES, Leonidas Buckner, 28 Jul 1860 - 22 May 1861. 0-9-4. "Son G. W. & H. J Cates."
CATES, Infant son of G. W. & H. J., d. in embryo, 20 Nov 1864.
BUCKNER, Alfred P., 13 Feb 1808 - 12 Jul 1855. 47-05-29.
BUCKNER, Leodas Z., 17 Oct 1836 - 14 Nov 1855. 19-00-29. "Son Alfred & Nancy L." (Tombstone grown into large hackberry tree.)
WILLIAMS, Nancy L., 30 May 1817 - 24 Mar 1859. "Wife of Alvis Williams & relict of A.P. Buckner."

Mr Fred Howkins says: "4 or 5 fieldstones mark other gravesites. Mr. McAnally said that there were once several more fieldstones here."

 Listed 22 Feb 1988 by Fred Hawkins in the book Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries Page 762. Also listed in They Passed This Way, Page A-124 about 1967.
Published here 20 Nov 2008, by Wayne Austin. Preliminary locator visit has occurred but no photography.
