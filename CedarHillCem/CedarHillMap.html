The Red Arrow is pointing northward at the Cedar Hill Cemetery. Columbia is on our right out Hwy 412 and Hampshire (near county line) is on our left. This is located on the south side of Hwy. 99/412, about half way between Cross Bridges and Hampshire. 
Map from MS Streets and Trips modified for this site by Judy Forgos of Ohio. 10/6/2006.


As you can see this is in the Curry Branch  - East Fork Valley with high hills all around hwy 412. In your Auto trip you have climbed part way up English Hill and rounded the curve southward, then back westward, and then started a gradual incline downward before the cemetery will appear in your sights. Notice this map shows a church on the north side of the road. That is the Cedar Hill Church of Christ an active congregation today.
Go to this cemetery with love and appreciation for your/the heritage of this great community. Going there for any other reason will invoke the fierce loyalty of this community and you will wind up in deep trouble.
[WA 8 Oct 2008.]
