WINN CEMETERY, FRANK MOORE RD CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Ella A. Winn

WINN, Ella A., d. 14 Jan 1856. (01-09-27.) "Daughter of W. A. & S. Winn"
 Inscription: ELLA A WINN -- Daughter of W. A. -- & S. Winn Died -- Jan 14th 1856 Aged 1 Year 9 Mo 21 Days." (When reading this stone don't confuse the "&" for an "S" as some have.)
