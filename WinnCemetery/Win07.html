WINN CEMETERY, FRANK MOORE RD CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Andrew Alonza Winn, an infant. Stone no longer readable. Do not disturb-fragile stone.

WINN, Andrew Alonzo, inf. of John R. (no other info discernable).
 Memorial is crumbling into tiny bits. We can read this much: "SACRED", "drew". 
I believe the "drew" is the lettering in Andrew. Thanks for the Authors of T.P.T.W. &  M.C.T.C.'s Fred Hawkins for preserving this much of this lost memorial back in 1963 & 1987 respectively. This stone might still be readable by collecting all the bits of stone and reassembling the the top plate for those pieces that have inscription on them. That is at least a 10 hour job that I do not wish to tackle. There is a fresh fracture on the top that has occurred this year. Just flaking off in the center. It does not contain any inscription.
