WINN CEMETERY, FRANK MOORE RD CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

J. B. Winn.

Another view.

WINN, J. B., 6 Dec 1816 - 9 Jan 1828. Large Box tomb. Very hard to read. It was wet and because of that it could not be cleaned without damaging the stone. Transcription: "SACRED -- to the memory of -- J. _ _ _ _Winn _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _  _ _ _ _ 1828." That amounts to all that I can make out above, so once again we rely on previous historians.
