WINN CEMETERY, FRANK MOORE RD CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The sign posted on a Hackberry Tree adjacent to cemetery, Southside.

WINN CEMETERY
Restoration and Maintenance Performed by:
Marty & Nancy Winn  Lyles, Tn.
Walter & Ellen Winn  Decatur, Ga.
Great-Great-Grandsons 
& their wives: Descendents of
Archelaus Winters Winn
&
Sussanna Bellanfant Winn.

 Note: A recent restoration was done on this cemetery by the above folks. My findings are that they did a great job including a fence that keeps out the livestock. Nice winter grasses abound also. All stones are upright to the best of everyone's ability. Now we know the full names of A. W & Sussanna Winn. 
This is an example of kinfolks that took charge and made a difference.
Photograph & information by Wayne Austin, 26 Feb 2008.
