Sign that graces the Billington Cemetery since Jan 2003 when James Brent Winn of Missouri posted it and also a military marker was set up. Graphics to make the sign readable by C. Wayne Austin. 6 Jun 2011.
BILLINGTON, Ezekiel, Mar 22, 1759 - Sep 29 1848, Private New Jersey Militia, Revolutionary War.

This stone is found in the Billington Cemetery in Marshall County Tennessee. It is not in a well kept environment for a cemetery but is typical for rural cemeteries on private land in the south. Billington cemetery is near Lewisburg, TN.
Birth was 22 Mar 1759 in Newark, Essex, New Jersey. His death was 29 Sep 1848, Marshall County Tennessee. Burial: Billington Family Cemetery, 801 Highway 99, Lewisburg, TN 37091
