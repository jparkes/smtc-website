ROUND HILL CEMETERY, (Belfast) MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

SWEENEY, Sara Neely, Age 90, died 4 Sep 2010 interred in Round Hill Cemetery,  Belfast, Marshall Co. Tn.

Sara Neely Sweeney

 Mrs. Sara Neely Sweeney, age 90 of Lewisburg, formerly of Cornersville, died Saturday, September 4, 2010 in Maury Regional Hospital.

Mrs. Sweeney was a Marshall County native and daughter of the late William Knox and Sadie Pickle Glenn. She was a member of the Belfast Union Presbyterian Church. She was retired from Genesco Corporation.

In addition to her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Cecil Brown Sweeney, and two sons-in-law Randal Sands and Charles Hickerson. She is survived by two daughters, Cecilia Sands of Franklin and Glynda Jacobs of Germantown, one brother, Harold Glenn of Lewisburg, sister, Louise Nelms of Belfast, three grandchildren, two step grandchildren, three great grandchildren, four step great grandchildren, and one step great great grandchild.

Funeral services will be conducted by Steve Thomas Wednesday, September 8, 2010 at 1:00 p.m. from the chapel of London Funeral Home with burial to follow in Round Hill Cemetery at Belfast.

Serving as pallbearers will be Randy Sands, Glen Hickerson, Chas Hickerson, Shaun Harbison, Clay Glenn, Jim Jacobs, and Caleb Allen. Honorary bearers are members of the Belfast Presbyterian Church and Bobby Sands.

The family was at London Funeral Home Tuesday from 4:00 p.m. until 8:00 p.m. and Wednesday until the funeral hour for visitation.
