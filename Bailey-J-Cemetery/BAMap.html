BAILEY (J.) CEMETERY, NEEDMORE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Take Hampshire Pike out of Mt. Pleasant to Needmore. Turn north an Gibson Hollow Rd. and go 1/2 mile to the old Boshears place (1988). When the road forks take the right fork. The graveyard is located about 1/4 mile from the fork in the road, on a lane that goes thru a farm home area and continues back. The cemetery is just the other side of two new barns. One barn is on either side of the road and the cemetery is on the right side of the lane beside a stump and a few hackberry trees.

This map is the clearest showing a tall tree shadowing the road to the north.
