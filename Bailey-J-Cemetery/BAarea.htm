BAILEY (J.) CEMETERY, NEEDMORE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This is the Bailey Cemetery as I found it near a stand of Hackberry trees. Notice there are two box tombs and several stones piled upon the box tombs. One appears to be inscribed, but opps I failed to get the inscription.
