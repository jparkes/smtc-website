BAILEY (J.) CEMETERY, NEEDMORE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This is the Bailey Cemetery as I found it near a stand of Hackberry trees. The two box tombs belong to James & Elizabeth Bailey. James has the near one. On top of Elizabeth Bailey's tomb is an inscribed stone that thru oversight I failed to confirm the inscription. Undoubtly it is one of memorials for the Ricketts children interred here.

The James Bailey Memorial.

 BAILEY, James, 24 May 1787 - 12 May 1866. (Box tomb.)
BAILEY, Elizabeth, 12 Mar 1786 - 8 Sep 1842. (wife of James Bailey, Box tomb.)
RICKETTS, Sarah E., 6Aug 1845 - 22 Jan 1850. 
RICKETTS, G. W., 31 Jul 1850 - 25 Aug 1851.
