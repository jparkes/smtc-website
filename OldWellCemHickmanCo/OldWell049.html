Richard's memorial.

049 ANDERSON, Richard "Kettle Dick", Sr., 28 Mar 1777 - 19 June 1858 (1st mar. Margaret Craig Anderson, 2nd mar. Sarah Elizabeth Cutbirth; 3rd great grandfather of Lettie Pearl Anderson who married Manley Walker Morrow)
049 ANDERSON, Margaret, Died 14 July 1828 In about the 45th year of her age (d/o Henry Craig (1728 - 1807) who was a Patriot and Soldier of the American Revolution and is buried at Bethel Presbyterian Church Cemetery in Clover, S.C.; & Mary Erwin Craig (1736 - 1818), w/o Richard "Kettle Dick" Anderson Sr.)
