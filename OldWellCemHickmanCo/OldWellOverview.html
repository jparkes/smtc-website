OLD WELL CEMETERY, Natchez Trace, HICKMAN COUNTY,  TENNESSEE

This view looks across the cemetery at a majority of the memorials. In addition to the memorials we see four old Box Elders Shrubs near the road. They are big enough to be more than 50 years old.  
This place is lovingly cared for by folks who obviously have a high regard for those gone before, great self esteem for themselves and a sense of wellness in the community.
