EVERGREEN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

COFFEY, Sherby Elmor 11 Mar 1886 - 5 Nov 1892 "Son of J.M. & M.J." 
(Tall shaft tumbled.) On the opposite side of this stone is the Rosa Hudson Coffey 
inscription. The stone was stood up temporarily to photograph it. Inscription reads: "To the
memory of Sherby Elmor Coffee, son of J. M. & M. J. Coffee was born March 11, 1886.
Departed this life November 5 1892". The stone will not stand up because the base is 
tilted and livestock that roam here would knock it down while rubbing against it.
