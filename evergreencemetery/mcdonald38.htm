EVERGREEN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

McDONALD, Temperance, 23 Mar 1805 - 30 Oct 1848. "Wife of Allen McDonald." 
"To the memory of TEMPERENCE, wife of Allen McDonald born March 23rd 1805 Died Oct 30th 1848.
 As she had lived with hope, strong faith, bright & Heaven in full view, O grave where is thy victory."
