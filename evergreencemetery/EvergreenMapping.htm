EVERGREEN CEMETERY-DODSON GAP ROAD, CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


With this map we can see the GPS location commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.

Here we can see the roads we need to take to get to the Evergreen Cemetery.
This aerial view shows a tacking horse track between the road and the cemetery but just slightly northeast of the cemetery. Go down Dodson Gap Road until it bear southward. Follow southward more than a half mile until you come to the concrete bridge where the stream crosses over to the other side of the road and the cemetery is just northwest of that crossing about 100 yards on the west (right) side of the road.
