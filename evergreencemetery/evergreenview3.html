EVERGREEN CEMETERY-DODSON GAP ROAD, CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 View looking west at the central section of the cemetery. Back of the above is fenced with a decorative wrought iron fence that is smashed by fallen trees. To your left in the center of the cemetery is a mass of fallen trees left from the storms of May 2003. Fortunately none of the stones were damaged from this but some were covered by debris and hard to photograph. Photo by WA.
