ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

HART, Charlotte Lewis, 14 May 1947 - 19 Jul 2010.

HART, Charlotte Lewis Age 63, died peacefully on July 19th.
She was preceded in death by her father, Frederick Donald Hart.
Charlotte was an inspiration to many with her smile and unrestrained joy for the simplest things in life. Even with her disabilities, she was exuberant about everything she did, and for all the people she met, including many, loving caregivers.
The fellowship with her church was an important part of her life, and through the support of Progress, Inc. and her family, she enjoyed involvement in various community activities.
Abridged from The Tennessean, published July 20, 2010. Obituary sent by Mary Bob Richardson 28 Jul 2016
