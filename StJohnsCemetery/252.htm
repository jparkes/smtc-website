ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Side # 1 and Cross of Honor.


POLK, General Lucius Eugene POLK Sr. 10 Jul 1833 d. 1 Dec 1892. husband of Sallie Moore (POLK) POLK (she was the daughter of Rufus King POLK). He was a Confederate States Army Brigadier General during the Civil War. Lucius was the son of William J. & Mary Rebecca Long Polk; b. in Salisbury, N.C.. 

POLK, Sally Moore, 1844 - 1925. (Daughter of Rufus King & Sarah Moore Jackson.)
