Box Tomb of John and Elizabeth Polk.


POLK, John, d. 24 May 1845. age 78 years. (Revolutionary War soldier.)

POLK, Elizabeth Anderson, d. 24 Nov 1829. (Wife of John Polk, age 63 years.)  Photos by "Jamie" and also by Mary Bob McClain Richardson.
