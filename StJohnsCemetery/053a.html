ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


CRAWFORD, Robert E., 26 Jun 1982 - 5 Oct 2006. (The Aurora, IL., native was the son of Richard E. Sr., and Barbara Hoffmann Crawford. He was a 2001 graduate of Spring Hill High School and was a volunteer for the Kids Fighting Aids Corporation. He was a member of St. Peter's Episcopal Church.)
