ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

HARDISON, Winifred Higgins, 16 Jan 1918 - 8 Jan. 2006.
Mrs. Winifred Higgins Hardison, 87, retired manager of S & H Honey Farms and resident of the Nashville Highway, died Sunday, January 8, 2006 at Maury Regional Hospital.Funeral services will be conducted at 12:00 noon Wednesday at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home with Calvin Hardisonofficiating. Burial will be in St. John's Churchyard. The family will visit with friends Tuesday from 5- 8 p.m. at the funeral home.Memorials may be made to Church of Christ Disaster Relief Effort, Inc., 410 Allied Drive, Nashville, TN 37211.
Obituary sent by Mary Bob Richardson 28 Jul 2016
