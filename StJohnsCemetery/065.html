ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
 
DORTCH, Margaret Anderton, 1 Nov 1919 - 29 Oct 2002 in Nashville, Tn. (Richmond, Va. native; dau. of Edward Covey Anderton and Virginia Cauthorne Anderton; grad. of St. Margaret's School in Tappahannock, Va., attended Sweet Briar College in Sweet Briar, Va; mar. in 1940 to O. Lawrence Dortch, who died 20 Nov 1991; member of Jr. League of Nashville, Garden Club of Nashville, National Societ of the Colonia Dames of America, James K. Polk Memorial Association and Belle Meade Country Club.)
