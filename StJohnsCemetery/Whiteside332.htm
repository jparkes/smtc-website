ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
WHITESIDE, Joseph L., 27 Jul 1914 - 22 Sep 2010. (Oxford, Al. native; s/o late Joseph Lister Whiteside Sr., and Eloise Orr Whiteside; degree in Chemical engineering from Auburn University in 1936 and worked for Monsanto Chemical Co. for 38 yrs.; Veteran U.S. Army, European Theatre from 1942 - 1946; awarded three Battle Stars, the Bronze Star and Purple Heart; Aug. 1946 he married Mary Jane Yeatman she died 14 Apr 2001; member of Kiwanis Club and Columbia Rotary Club; served a term on the Board of Trustees of Maury Co. Hospital and President of the Maury Co. Chamber of Commerce; preceded in death by a sister, Sara Frances Hodge.)
 Photo and Bio compiled and sent in by Mary Bob Richardson. 25 Sep 2010.
