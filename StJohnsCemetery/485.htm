ST JOHNS CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Andrew Jackson Polk memorial

POLK, Andrew Jackson POLK Jr. b. 24 Sep 1850 here in Ashwood, Maury Co., TN. son of Andrew Jackson POLK Sr. and Rebecca (VAN LEER) POLK. He died 28 Apr 1852. "died after an illness of 15 days."
also:
POLK, Infant son of Andrew Jackson & Rebecca (VAN LEER), died 1846.
also but not seen in this inscription:
POLK, Kitty Kirkman, 18 Sep 1852 - 13 Feb 1857. "Dau of Andrew J. & Rebecca; b. Ashwood Hall; d. of Scarlet Fever." (She is listed on the Andrew J. POLK grave big photo as Ketty.)

Cemetery photo & info by Patti Heath. Sent in 2/8/2007.
