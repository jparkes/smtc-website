HARDISON, William Humphrey, 1841 - (19 Feb) 1870, Pvt., Co H 1st Tenn Inf., C.S.A (Son of Asa &

HARMON, Susan Gray McLemore (Nicholson) Hardison, 13 Dec 1845 - 28 May 1923, "Dau of Robert N. & Sarah Nicholson". She married William Humphrey Hardison in 1870 then she married; Thomas Harmon 1839 - 1909. 
The date Wm. H. Hardison and Susan Gray Nicholson married is 6 Nov 1862. She married Thomas Harmon on 14 Mar 1875. Thomas was born 12 Jun 1839 or 1840 and died 15 Nov 1909.  I thought since his marker is kind of hard to read you might want to include this information.
