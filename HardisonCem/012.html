HARDISON (CHARLES) CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
JACKSON, Nannie, 1870 - 1908. Here is a prime example of why we need to preserve fieldstones that mark graves. Here someone knew the fieldstone that marked this graves and went back years later and placed this marker. Had the stone been pulled up and thrown away the grave would location would have been lost.
