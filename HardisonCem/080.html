HARDISON (CHARLES) CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
UNDERWOOD, William L., (18 Jun) 1848 - (29 Jan) 1917. (marked with a funeral marker) the son of David and Nancy Bradley Underwood, source; the obituary. (husband of Mary L. Nicholson.)
*UNDERWOOD, Mary L. Nicholson Williams ,ca. 1841 - July 1919. (unmarked grave; daughter of James Williams and Frances Nicholson Williams. She married William Underwood on 4 Jan 1866. Underwood was the son of David and Nancy Bradley Underwood. He was born 18 Jun 1848.)
