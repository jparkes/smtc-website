HARDISON (CHARLES) CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
DERRYBERRY, A. H. (Alfred Horsley), (27 Aug) 1878 - (23 Nov) 1943, (son of William Alexander & Frances Eugenia Hardison Derryberry).
DERRYBERRY, Poca A. (Pocahontas Hardison), (14 Mar) 1882 - (18 Mar) 1962. (Wife of A. H.; Daughter of Ezra & Mary Hardison.)
I have recently received an email from a great granddaughter of Alfred and Poca Derryberry and would like to ask you to make the following change to this listing:

 Was: Derryberry, A. H. (Alt H.) 1878 - 1943.

Change to: Derryberry, A. H. (Alfred Horsley), 27 Aug 1878 - 23 Nov 1943, (son of William Alexander & Frances Eugenia Hardison Derryberry).

On the wife she states I don't know if she was actually named Pocahontas or if that was a nickname. On the 1910 census, Maury Co TN, she is found with Alfred as "Pokie". She is on his WWI draft registration record as "Pokahontas Angeline Derryberry". They are on the 1920 census as "Alph S and Pauline"; and then on the 1930 census as "Alfred H. and Poca A."
