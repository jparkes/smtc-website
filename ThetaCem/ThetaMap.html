THETA CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
aka Sunset Hills Cemetery

Theta Cemetery topographical map locator.

Theta Cemetery.  From Columbia go out Carters Creek Pike several miles to Les Robinson Road. Turn left and go about 2 & 1/2 miles. The cemetery is on your right across the road from the Theta Methodist Church. The cemetery in the map above is the red dot on your left in the square. The other red dot is the Theta Baptist Church Cemetery.
