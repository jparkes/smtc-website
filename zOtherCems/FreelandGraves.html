Mr. Austin, I have information on the cemeteries in Maury County. I have done a lot of genealogy and fieldwork on my families and have a few additions for your website. Bill Thrasher.

The Freeland Cemetery is on Scribner Mill Road (Brush Creek Road) across the road from the Haynes Cemetery. This is the burial place of James Gipson Freeland and his wife , possibly others. However this is not documented anywhere that I know of. Bill Thrasher
