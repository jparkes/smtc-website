MOORE CEMETERY, CULLEOKA TENNESSEE
A north view shows the Nath & Eliza Moore memorial on our far upper left just beyond the first tree with dark bushy bark lying on its
back.

A north west view shows the many boulders scattered around that belong on the rock wall. Some of them may even mark graves but that needs to be studied to tell which is which at this point.
