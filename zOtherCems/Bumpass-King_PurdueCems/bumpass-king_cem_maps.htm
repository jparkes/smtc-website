BUMPASS-KING CEMETERY, (Johnson Hill Rd near Hwy 43) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Topozone placement of this cemetery at the correct Longitude & Latitude coordinates in the center of the above map. I have not been there to verify this so it is strictly from the USGS records of the 1950s.

Take Highway 43 south to Johnson Hill Road or Sheepneck Road. Turn left and go about a mile noticing the road is bending gradually back eastward. It soon take a steep turn back due east. Stop and get out there and walk due south (but not without permission) 200 yards to a grove of trees jutting out from the tree line. The cemetery should be in that clump of trees if the mapping is correct.
 Notice the line of trees sticking out northwest from the other tree line. The cemetery is supposed to be in the line jutting northward.
