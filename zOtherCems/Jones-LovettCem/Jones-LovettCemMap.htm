JONES-LOVETT CEMETERY, LEE ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This old Topographical map dated in the 1950s shows the area of the cemetery layed out several yards from the road and occupying many square feet of ground. It is just on the edge of another drop in elevation from the 800 foot line. I think the new housing development lies on the east side of that brown elevation line for the Jones-Lovett Cemetery but the Foster-Lee Cemetery east and across from there is inside the development.

We have marked in red the spot about where the one stone is still standing for this cemetery, but believe the area encompassing the cemetery lies largely to the north if the mapping is correct.
Information by Wayne Austin 30 Dec 2008. Maps from Topozone & Mapquest mapping service modified for this site.
