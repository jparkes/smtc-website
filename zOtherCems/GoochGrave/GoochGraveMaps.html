GOOCH GRAVE, Mc Benderman Rd, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This Map shows the Latitude/Longitude or GPS coordinates of the area that needs to be searched to find this lost grave as 35.46692 X -87.114306. 

 This road map shows the location and road needed to get to the Gooch Cemetery. From Columbia go out Hwy 245 aka Campbellsville Pike about 7+ miles and turn right on Millers Lake Rd. Follow that for less than a half a mile and turn right on Mack Benderman known in some places as Holt Road. Stop at Bethel Church for permission and registration to visit this cemetery. Then follow that road for about a half mile and when the road bends back right the cemetery will be on your left in a cedar tree area up the slope about 100 yards from the road.

This aerial view shows the forestry layout of the area where this cemetery is found. Notice the dark colored treed area as being Cedars that mark the location of this cemetery The Bethel Church of Christ is back to your right on this map.
