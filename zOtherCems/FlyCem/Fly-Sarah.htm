FLY CEMETERY, (Primm Springs) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Sarah Fly - Cropped version of a tombstone photo added to Findagrave by N. Wickam 27 Aug 2009. Tombstone probably set by John Fly, 1802 -1884
long after the death of his mother because the style & type of stone is not consistent with the 1808 era.
 FLY, Sarah, 1767 - 1808, "Mother of John Fly". This is possibly the first death in Maury County that is inscribed on a tombstone. However we believe it was set in place much later by her son John Fly. It is not of the style of stone set in 1808, but of a style that was used mostly between 1870 & 1900.)
Photo compiled and sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 4 Sep 2010. Commentary by W. Austin 26 Sep 2010.
