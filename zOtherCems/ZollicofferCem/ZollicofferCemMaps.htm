ZOLLICOFFER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Zollicoffer Cemetery.

Located on the John Abernathy place (1988), behind the house in a pasture. From Columbia bypass (Hwy 50) go south on Campbellsville Pike to the Golf Course. From there continue two and a half miles and turn east, cross the creek and go to the end of the lane. 1988 Instructions of Fred Hawkins.

This Aerial Map based on the Topozone and USGS coordinates shows the cemetery in a small clump of trees behind a house upon the slope.

Zooming in using this map with a red check shows the cemetery behind what I think is an old house. All these directions are based on the USGS coordinates. I have not been here and cannot confirm this. The chances of this being correct is about 85% based on my experience with other remote cemeteries. [C Wayne Austin 5 Apr 2011.]
