MITCHELL CEMETERY-PARK STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Locator Map

Mr. Austin, I just have a little info on cemeteries in Maury. I have done a lot of genealogy and fieldwork on my families and have a few additions for your website. Bill Thrasher.

Location: Mitchell Cemetery is on land formerly owned by Luther Lamar now owned by his daughter Ruth and husband Jonathan Bates. It is just past the AME church on right side of Hwy. 50 before reaching Park Station. 

Several people have told me of this one but I was unable to find it. It is said to have marked memorial stones for the Mitchell family.
