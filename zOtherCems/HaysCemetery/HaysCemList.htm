HAYS CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Locator Map of the cemetery

FOX, Elizabeth L. Hays, 21 Aug 1839 - 25 Sep 1871. (Wife of P. Fox, Jr.)
HAYS, David Kilcrease, 24 Jul 1815 - 20 Jan 1895.
HAYS, Manerva, 2 Feb 1819 - 18 Mar 1891. (Wife of David K. Hays)

Listed in "Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries", 15 Mar 1986, by Fred Lee Hawkins, page 94.
Listed in "They Passed This Way", 1963, by Evelyn Shackleford & Marise Lightfoot, page C-49.)
Only two or three fieldstones are now visible of what was once a large graveyard. Mr. James Byrnes said that when the field was cleared some years ago, the loggers dragged logs through the cemetery and destroyed the simple markers (fieldstone marked graves). FLH. Jr. 1986.
