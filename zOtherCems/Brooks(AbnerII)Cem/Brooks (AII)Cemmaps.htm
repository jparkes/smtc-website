BROOKS (ABNER II) CEMETERY (Snow Creek Road) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The yellow pointers indicate prime places to look for this cemetery. One of them points to the Nelson Brooks cemetery which I did find. This cemetery is not detailed on the USGS records so I have provided this improvised map to attempt to locate it. It makes sense that this cemetery would be in an area within 1/2 mile from the Nelson Brooks Cemetery since Nelson Brooks was donated 35 acres of land from the Abner II Brooks estate. It was on the Woodrow Johnson property in 1987 south of Snow Creek Road. 


 This map has the Brooks (Abner II) cemetery somewhere in its locations, but it is not indicted in the detail. Consequently, I was unable to find it 8 Apr 2009. I hesitate to say it is lost just because I was unable to get the time to locate someone who knew where it was. So another try later maybe.
