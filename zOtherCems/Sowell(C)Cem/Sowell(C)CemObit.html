SOWELL (CHARLES) CEMETERY, south of Williamsport near Duck River), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

DEDMAN, W T., Birth: Oct. 10, 1868, Hickman Co., TN, Death: May 23, 1920, Maury Co., TN

Husband of Ida Dale, son of John Thomas Dedman and Lydia Elizabeth Forehand. Had five children: Robert Dedman, Mary Florence Dedman, M. C. Dedman, J. H. Dedman, and Bessie J. Dedman 
 
Public Domain. The Columbia Herald May 28, 1920
DEATH CLAIMS RESPECTED MAN

W. T. Dedman, aged fifty-one years.
respected citizen of the Century
Mines community died at 4 o'clock
Sunday afternoon at his home after
an illness of several days.
Mr. Dedman was a member of the
Methodist church and was widely
known and respected. He is survived
by his wife and several children.
A prayer service was conducted at
5 o'clock Monday afternoon at the
Sowell cemetery. Interment was in
the family lots The Maury Undertaking
Company in charge.
