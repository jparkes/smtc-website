SOWELL (CHARLES) CEMETERY, south of Williamsport near Duck River), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Location: From Williamsport, go south on Cross Bridges Rd. aka Jimmy Gray Robinson Rd for one mile and enter Russell Lane on the right (gated private property) that leads to Sowell-Russell place, now (1987) owned by Mr. Jimmy Russell. The house was built by the great-grandfather of Mr. Russell. The cemetery is in the field just behind an air strip on top of the hill, but airstrip is no longer visible. This cemetery is at GPS North 35.67535 X West -87.24306 as calculated by C. Wayne Austin 10 Jul 2017.
