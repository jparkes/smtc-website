ISOM FAMILY CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Take Dry Fork Rd. west of Hampshire and go to where it forks. Take Whiteside Rd. south and go 1/4 mile. The
graveyard is 100 yards from road, on right.
   
Locator Map     Cemetery photo view

ISOM, Martha A., 30 Dec _ _ _ _ - 4 Oct 1834. (Box tomb with very weathered inscription.)

There were probably other graves here but no trace of them remains. [1987, per Mr. Fred].

This cemetery listing was  listed in the various books of the past. Mr. Fred Lee Hawkins in the book Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries listed this cemetery on page 690. He visited  there 4 Nov 1987. Before that it was listed in the book They Passed This way page A-134, about 1967.  [Wayne Austin 30 Nov 2008]
