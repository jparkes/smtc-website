HARRIS CEMETERY, (Blue Springs Rd), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

At the place I stopped to investigate the dead ended Blue Springs Road That was high upon a bluff over looking the junction of Silver & Fountain Creek. I heard in the distance a wild sound I had never heard before. That was a flock of strange sounding birds setting up a noisy chatter. It sounded like a flock of Parrots, and sent me investigating wondering why Parrots would be there. Upon closer inspection it proved to be a large flock of Robins (Robin Red Breast) but the sound echoing across the holler was unique. I did not know they flocked together that way, but was familiar with their individual sounds.
