CRANFORD CEMETERY, Cranford Hollow Rd., (Eastern) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Locator Maps

CRANFORD, A.H. ( Arthur H.), 19 Dec 1790 - 3 mar 1877. ( Veteran War of 1812.)
CRANFORD, Frances Barker, 17 Nov 1791 - 23 Feb 1873. "Wife of A.H.," ( Frances "Frankey" Barker was the daughter of George and Frances Kerr Barker, pioneers of the Barker family that settled in Rock Springs area prior to 1820.)
TUCKER, Fannie, 14 Oct 1829 - 7 Feb 1873. "Wife of Jas. M. Tucker, dau. of A.H. & Frances Cranford)

Those known by Newpaper records to be buried here - No inscribed markers found in any survey.
 + HEDRICK, G.B., died Tuesday of paralysis, was tenant on Ninian Evans farm on Bear Creek; was originally from Tullahoma, Tn. Five children survive. Burial in the Old Cranford graveyard.," (Dailey Herald, 6 Dec 1904.)
+ JACKSON, William D., married, born 4 Dec 1838 in Tenn., died 3 Feb 1918, of old age; age 81-2-2; (age does not calculate with dates), son of David Jackson, born in Va., and Jemima Hardison, born in Tenn; buried in Cranford Cemetery.) (Death Notice, Daily Herald.)
+JACKSON, David, married, birth not given, nor age, died 24 Oct 1917, born in Maury Co., son of Bill Jackson.
