PARK GRAVES-PARK STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Not sure I can place this cemetery on a map. Park Station is not marked but is west of I-65 Exit 37. The cemetery likely is on one of the hill tops to the west and south of I-65 on Hwy 50 as Bill stated. It can be found probably in a clump of trees as farmers know there are burials there and will not usually clear the land there. Parks Station was a post office and depot on the Duck River Valley Railroad in the Silver Creek area at a time when the rails came thru this place. The post office was discontinued in 1903. Wayne Austin 7 Apr 2008.
