OLD KNOB CREEK CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 CAUGHRON, Mary E. Hayes, (1870 - (Oct. 2, 1898). No marker reported. (d/o James Pinkney & Eliza Jane Smithson Hayes; w/o John L. Caughron.)

We are sorry to report the sad death of Mrs. Mary Caughron, who died in Nashville the 2nd of October, and was laid to rest in the Knob Creek grave-yard. She leaves a husband and two little children to mourn her loss. -- Columbia Herald, 14 Oct. 1898.
