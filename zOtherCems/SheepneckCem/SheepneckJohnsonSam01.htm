SHEEPNECK CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE- MT. PLEASANT AREA


Above is a copy of an obit and a death certificate for Sam H. Johnson who is buried in the Sheepneck Cemetery in Sandy Hook, Maury County. The obituary is from The Mt. Pleasant Record March 11, 1932. Sam Johnson is not listed in the Maury County Cemetery Book nor the this website until 30 May 2009.

Tennessee death certificate filed 16 mar 1932 S.H. Johnson.

JOHNSON, Sam H., 6 Jun 1860 - 9 Mar 1932. (husband of Sarah K. Gibson Johnson.)
JOHNSON, Sarah K. Gibson, 15 Nov 1860 - 29 Nov 1923, age 63, (wife of Sam H. Johnson; daughter of John & Lizzie Grimes Gibson.)
All information and imagery sent in by Charlotte Colvett 29 May 2009.
