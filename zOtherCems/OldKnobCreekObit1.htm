OLD KNOB CREEK CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 HAYES, Eliza Jane Smithson, (Feb., 1839) - (Dec. 17, 1901). No marker reported. (w/o James Hayes.)

Mrs. Hayes, wife of James Hayes of Santa Fe, died Tuesday morning after an illness of several weeks, and was buried Wednesday at 1 o'clock at Knob Creek. Rev. Mr. Allison, pastor of the Methodist church at Santa Fe, conducting the services. Mrs. Hayes was a good Christian woman, highly esteemed in her neighborhood, and will be missed by her family and the community at large. -- Columbia Herald, Dec. 20, 1901. reported by Kendall
