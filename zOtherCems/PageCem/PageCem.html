PAGE CEMETERY, FLY, MAURY, COUNTY TENNESSEE 
Mapping the location

In Fly, go up Page Branch Rd. for about a mile. Enter a farm gate, on right of road, and continue for 1/4 mile on narrow lane through the woods, The cemetery is difficult to find and gate is generally padlocked.

BAKER, Infant, May 1885. 'Infant of Robert P. & Amanda L. Page Baker.
BAKER, Infant, Aug 1893. 'Infant of Robert P. & Amanda L. Page Baker.
FLY, Sarah C., 21 Jan 1849 - 21 Sep 1873. 'Wife C. W. Fly.'
PAGE, Greenie (?), dates unknown. (This was possibly the last person buried here.)

There are several other graves marked with fieldstones. Mr. Vester Humphries takes care of the cemetery (1986), at least he has it mowed from time to time. It was in very bad condition when we went to see it. Listed by Fred Hawkins 27 Sep 1986.

Sources: Listed Maury County Cemeteries, by Fred Lee Hawkins Jr. 27 Sep 1986, page 207, Vol I.
Not listed in They Passed this Way by Marise P. Lightfoot & Evelyn B. Shackelford.
