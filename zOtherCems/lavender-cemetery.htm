LAVENDER CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Turn north off of Kedron Road, onto Joe Peay Road, just beyond I-65 overpass, and go about 2 miles. The cemetery is on Waddington farm, in the middle of a large field just after passing the house and barn (1986 instructions, by FLH).

 This cemetery is listed in Maury County, Tennessee Cemeteries on page 7. It was not listed in They Passed This Way.
