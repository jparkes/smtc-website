BLACKBURN-KINZER CEMETERY (Leiper's Creek Rd) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the location of the Blackburn-Kinzer Cemetery GPS which indicates the cemetery location.


 This map shows the roads needed to get to this cemetery. 1986 instructions: From Water Valley go west to Alexandria Nazarene Church;
turn south on Leiper's Creek Rd. and go 1 & 1/2 miles to old Kinzer place (now owned by Hooker Chemical Co.) The cemetery is on hill above barn.)
The yellow pointers indicate prime places to look for this cemetery. The top most pointer is on top of the small hill but really the hill spans parallel with Leipers Creek Road so we may need to look further if the GPS is wrong.
