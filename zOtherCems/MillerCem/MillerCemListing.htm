MILLER CEMETERY, (east side, Hwy 31 near Sagewood Subdivision) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

   Mapping the unknown location


HANKS, Esther, Died 29 Dec 1868, Aged 62 years "wife of E. Hanks" (nee Williams; 2nd wife of Rev Elijah Hanks, pioneer Baptist preacher. (This stone is broken and most of it is gone F. L. H., 1986)

MILLER, Vinson, 5 Apr 1796 - 20 Jun 1848. Aged 52yrs, 2 mo. & 15 days.(Son of Fredrick & Mary Frances Carter Miller; Halifax Co. Va. native; apparently moved to Maury Co. Tn. with parents about 1814; mar. 14 Jul 1828 to lst cousin Mildred Miller.) Tombstone Photo / Photo1 by Tanner Babb 30 Oct 2012

NEVILS, B. . . . . , born the 25th . . . . . and departed this life 5 Feb 1840, Age 49 yrs, 9 mos. and 11 days. (This stone is broken into fragments, most of which are gone. Footstone with initials B. N. is still standing F. L. H., 1986)

This was once a large cemetery, as there are a good number of graves marked by fieldstones scattered over a large area. F. L. H., 1986

The Vinson Miller stone is the only one standing complete in the entire cemetery.

This cemetery listing was  condensed from the book Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries by Fred Lee Hawkins, page 85, (Mr. Hawkins was accompanied by Mr. Stephenson to this cemetery). Also presented in the book They Passed This Way, Vol II, Page 347. If you know where this cemetery is located please let us know. There is confusion on Findagrave as to its location.
