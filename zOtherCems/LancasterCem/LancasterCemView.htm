LANCASTER CEMETERY, (Blue Springs Rd), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This quick snap shot taken (where Lancaster Cemetery will be found) as I traveled northwest on Blue Springs Road is across the field and across Silver Creek from the Lancaster Cemetery mapping location. Silver Creek is just where the field above meets the tree line. I was not about to try to find this cemetery that day. Further up the road revealed just how high the bluffs and how deep the creek was. So it would be very difficult to ford in the winter time. This cemetery overlooks the high bluffs of Silver Creek on the south side in a very remote area and that cemetery will as be a very hard one to find when I get up the guts to attempt that.
Photo and information by Wayne Austin 22 Jan 2009.
