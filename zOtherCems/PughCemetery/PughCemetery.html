PUGH CEMETERY, Whiteside Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

West of Hampshire. Take Dry Fork Road to Whiteside Rd. Turn south and go one mile. The cemetery is in bushes on the west side of road, on place owned by Mrs. Roy Dycus. (1987)

   Locator Map     Cemetery photo view


PUGH, Andrew Jackson, born ca. 1817, died after 1880. (Father of Andrew Milton Pugh. Known to be buried here with no marker.)
PUGH, Annie B. Nolen, wife, Andrew Jackson, b. ca. 1812.  (Mother of Andrew Milton Pugh. Known to be buried here with no marker.)
PUGH, Andrew Milton, 1847 1908. 
PUGH , Ellen Perkins, 1854 - 1921. Wife.
VINCENT, Nehemiah, 1835 - 15 Feb 1868. 
VINCENT, (wife of Nehemiah, dates & given name unknown.)

There are several graves marked with fieldstones. The area is badly grown-up and it is hard to find all of the stones. 

This cemetery listing was  shown in the various books of the past. Mr. Fred Lee Hawkins in the book Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries listed this cemetery on page 690. He visited  there 4 Nov 1987. Prior to that it was listed in the book They Passed This Way page A-134, about 1967.  [Wayne Austin 30 Nov 2008]
