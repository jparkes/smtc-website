PUGH CEMETERY, Whiteside Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Cemetery is located west of Hampshire. Take Dry Fork Road to Whiteside Rd. Turn south (left) and go about one mile. The cemetery is in bushes on the (right) west side of road, on place owned by Mrs. Roy Dycus. (1987). This map from Topozone is probably accurate. The perimeter placement of the cemetery is drawn out indicating someone spent the time necessary to get it correctly placed.

The red check is my best guess of where I think the cemetery can be found. If wrong stay tuned as I fix this after an actual locating of the cemetery. Hopefully the stones are still standing and can be found.  [WA 28 Nov 2008]
