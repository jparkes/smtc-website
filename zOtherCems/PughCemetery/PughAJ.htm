PUGH CEMETERY, Whiteside Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

PUGH, Andrew Jackson, b. ca. 1817, died after 6 Aug 1896 listed in the 1880 census. (Father of Andrew Milton Pugh. Known to be buried here with no marker.)
PUGH, Annie B. Nolen, wife, Andrew Jackson, b. ca. 1812.  (Mother of Andrew Milton Pugh. Known to be buried here with no marker.)

"Mr. & Mrs. A. J. Pugh of Hampshire are over 80...there has never been a death in the family. Mrs. Pugh passed through Columbia 75 years ago and has never been back." (Maury Democrat, 6 Aug 1896.) (info published in the book Maury County Cemeteries by Fred Hawkins page 690. Go there for more information.)
