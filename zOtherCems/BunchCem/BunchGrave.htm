BUNCH GRAVE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Locator mapping of the cemetery


BUNCH, Mrs. Elizabeth, departed this life 2 Sep 1822. Aged 24 Years, 4 Months & 9 Days. (stone is fallen, 1987 F.L.H.)

Mr. Fred Hawkins says there are several dozen fieldstones marked graves scattered over about an acre of land that is probably marking the graves of slaves prior to 1865. 


 Added here 30 Nov 2009 by Wayne Austin with locator instructions, but no visit.
The above listing is mostly from Cemeteries of Maury County by Fred Hawkins. Listed 5 Jan 1987, on Page 83.
Also this cemetery was included in the book They Passed This Way by Marise P. Lightfoot & J.C. Parrish, aided by Robert Parks & Boyd Johnson for this cemetery. Published on page C-55. The visit here took place 25 Oct 1964.
