BYRD CEMETERY, (Kinderhook) Maury County Tennessee
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the location of Byrd Cemetery.

Go through Kinderhook on Dog Creek Road, cross Natchez Trace and go a mile further. Enter small farm lane to left, cross Dog Creek & go up hill to left. Cemetery is beyond house in edge of woods, on bluff above creek, on Westmoreland place.

This Aerial map places the Byrd Cemetery to the best of my ability. The red cross was positioned using map interpolations. There is no guarantee the cemetery is at this location.
