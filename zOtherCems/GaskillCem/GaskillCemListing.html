GASKILL CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE - N. W. CORNER OF COUNTY
 

Mapping the location

Listing:
GASKILL, Robert J., 1 Mar 1793 - 26 Mar 1856. ((h/o Elizabeth Haynes Gaskill; Native of Virginia; Veteran of War 1812.)
GASKILL, Elizabeth Haynes, 30 Dec 1796 - 12 Aug 1874. (Georgia native; w/o Robert J. Gaskill. Ref: Donald Miller.)
GASKILL, Robert J., 22 Jan 1826 - 17 Feb 1879. (Son Robert J. & Elizabeth Gaskill; Co. K, 48th Tenn. Inf., C.S.A.)
GASKILL, Margaret, 4 Feb 1828 - 24 Oct 1903. (nee Fox.)
GASKILL, James, (Bible record says: b. 27 Feb 1838. [This stone is likely covered by an enormous rose bush. I could just see the corner FLH 1987.]
GASKILL, T. (Thomas) C. (Christopher), Sr., 25 Jan 1843 - 5 Dec 1915. Father. (Santa Fe, Maury Co. native; s/o Robert & Elizabeth Haynes Gaskill; Mason, Veteran Civil War, Corporal in Co D 3rd Regiment, Missouri Cav.: Ref: Fred Hawkins.)
GASKILL, Fannie Maud, 7 Nov 1837 - 13 Oct 1894. Mother. (1st wife of Thomas C. Gaskill; d/o Thomas & Eliza Jerman Simmons.)
GASKILL, J. M. "Mack", 7 Jan 1850 -8 Jul 1897. (s/o Robert & Margaret Fox Gaskill; Stone tumbled.)
GASKILL, James Alonzo, (10 Jul) 1894 - (23 Oct) 1917. (s/o J. M. & Mary J. Morrow Gaskill.)
HARBISON, Andrew Bunch. (Age 75, died Monday near Fly's Store, paralysis, 5 children. (Maury Democrat, 27 Jan 1927; Ref: Fred Hawkins..)
HARBISON, Nancy Elizabeth Walters. (no dates, grave of two Harbison graves identified in 1961 by granddaughter; (Ref: Fred Hawkins..)
KIRKPATRICK, Infant son of A.S. & Charlotte Kirkpatrick, 15 Aug 1884 - 28 Aug 1884.
KIRKPATRICK, Infant daughter of A.S. & Charlotte Kirkpatrick, 15 Aug 1884 - 28 Aug 1884.
McKNIGHT, Golden J., 27 Jun 1863 - 26 Jul 1884. "Daughter of Joseph & Elizabeth Walters; wife of W. J. McKnight."
SIMMONS, Eliza A., 15 Jul 1808 - 3 Jun 1888. "Wife of Thomas A. Simmons." (Stone broken.)
WALTERS, C. C. (Christopher), 20 Jun 1813 - 11 Jul 1895. (Stone fallen.)
WALTERS, Mary W. (Polly), 30 Dec 1816 - 14 Oct 1880. (nee Mary Woodson Gaskill.)
WALTERS, E. J., 11 May 1822 - 4 Feb 1882. (nee Elizabeth Jane Gaskill; w/o Joseph Walters.)
WALTERS, W. B. (William Byrd), 3 Dec 1836 - 27 Sep 1902. (s/o C. C. & Mary W. Gaskill Walters; C.S.A.; Enlisted in Co. D 3rd Missouri Calvary and 48th Ten. Cav.; Ref: Fred Hawkins.) )
WALTERS, D. C., 4 Jan 1850 - 4 Jun 1858. "Son C. C. & Mary Walters.)
WALTERS, Emerintha, 16 Jul 1856 - 22 Jan 1857. "Dau. C. C. & Mary Walters."

Mr. Fred Lee Hawkins said this 4 Feb 1987: This cemetery has been abandoned for almost a century and is in very bad condition. There are several fieldstones that evidently mark other gravesites.


This cemetery was presented in the book Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries by Fred L. Hawkins 1989, page 195. It was also presented in the book They passed this way, Page B-25, About 1961. Listing sent here by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 26 Feb 2012.
