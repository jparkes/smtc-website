MORTON (J.T.) CEMETERY, NW area of MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

The above cemetery with the red check is shown on this map as the Morton Cemetery. This cemetery is not detailed by the USGS so we have estimated the GPS above based on Mr. Hawkins directions and mapping.

 The above yellow arrows points to the place one might find this cemetery.

From Kinderhook go northwest on Gordon Lawrence Road for 3 miles. The cemetery is on Mr. Allen Beard's place (1986), about 1/4 mile beyond his barn.
