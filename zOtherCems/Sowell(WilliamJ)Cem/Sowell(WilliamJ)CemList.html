SOWELL (WILLIAM J.) CEMETERY, (Sowell Bend of Duck River-east Maury County), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Location Mapping


FRANK, Mary G., 15 Oct 1844 - 18 Apr 1895, ag 51 yrs, 6 mos, 3 days, (2nd wife of Ira Hardison, later maried Mr. Frank.)
HARDISON, Ira, 12 May 1806 - 30 Sep 1875, (son of Edward & Millie Stubbs Hardison)
HARDISON, Penelope, 27 Apr 1810 - 16 Sep 1861, (wife of Ira Hardison, daughter of James & Mary Smithwick Hardison.)
REYNOLDS, Charles S., 30 Aug 1854 - 6 Aug 1888.
SCOBEY, Infant son of J. E. & F. S. Scobey, 17 May 1892 - 22 May 1892.
SOWELL, William J., 21 Jul 1824 - 14 Aug 1884.
SOWELL, Emily J., 1 Nov 1829 - 7 Sep 1910, "wife of W. J. Sowell." (Daughter of Ira & Penelope Hardison).
SOWELL, Alice J., 27 May 1853 - 25 Oct 1861, "Daughter of W. J. & Emily J. Sowell".
SOWELL, Carrie E., 17 Mar 1866 - 5 Feb 1883, "Daughter of W. J. & Emily J. Sowell".
SOWELL, A. Barclay, 18 Jun 1871 - 20 Apr 1884. "Son. of W. J. & Emily J. Sowell." (Stone not found, by F.L.H. 1986.)
SOWELL, Lebert, 21 Dec 1879 - age 3 yrs, 4 mos 4 days., "Son of W. J. & S. J Sowell." (The initial of mother's name is an "S". Reason for this: ??)
SOWELL, Jimmy D., died 14 Sep 1880, age 12 yrs, 1 mo., 3 days, "Son of W. J. & Emily J. Sowell".
SOWELL, Barclay, 30 Aug 1884 - 1 Sep 1884, "Son T. W. & Jennie Sowell".
