BOOKER CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The yellow pointers indicate places where this cemetery might be found. This is a reflection that this cemetery is not plotted in the USGS Mapping survey and the GPS coordinates are unknown. Fred Hawkins says in 1987, from Columbia, turn off Carmack Boulevard onto Polk Street, go two blocks and turn right. The cemetery is at edge of the black subdivision, near New Smyrna Cumberland Presbyterian Church. I pegged three places with yellow arrows that might be where this cemetery is located.
Map from MS Virtual Earth modified for this site by Wayne Austin 10 May 2009.
