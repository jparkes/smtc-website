Rick Gray of Mt Pleasant originally compiled this transcription 6 Jul 2004 from many sources and stated it as 100% complete to the best of his ability, but the listing was not updated for tombstones set after 1990. Rick has special family knowledge and information about burials here both with and without headstones and has related that to us above. On 30 Dec 2008 Wayne Austin visited and photographed this cemetery. The photos were sent to Mary Bob McClain Richardson of Birmingham Alabama and used to by her to update the cemetery listing. Revised for her efforts 26 Sep 2009 by Wayne Austin. Accordingly, all marked graves are updated as of 30 Dec 2008. After thousands of photos and the long day the nightfall caught me and the photos show up as dark.
