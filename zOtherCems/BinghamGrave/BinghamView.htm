BINGHAM GRAVE, WEST POINT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Bingham grave is shown as the little dark rectangular object between the house & farm lane in the left center of the photo. 
The Bingham memorial standing as a lone memorial in the yard of the old house. The house is probably as old as this stone set in the 1850s. It has features that belies it age such as the rock chimney on the far side.
 Photos & Commentary by Wayne Austin 25 Sep 2008.
