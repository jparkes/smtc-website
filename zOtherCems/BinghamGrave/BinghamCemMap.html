BINGHAM GRAVE, WEST POINT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Located at the tip of the yellow arrow at the corner of the drive to the house and on back to the barns.Â 

To get here follow West Point Church Road past Crafton Foster Rd and look to the right at a metal locked gate which is the lane to the old house and farm. After the permissions the grave is about 100 yards up the road on the right between the house and the old tobacco barn.

Information by Wayne Austin 25 Sep 2008. Maps from MS Terra & Mapquest mapping service modified for this site. I peg this gravestone as N35 degrees 35.437' and W87 degrees 14.557'. That is an error range of about 20 yards.
