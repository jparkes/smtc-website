BINGHAM GRAVE, WEST POINT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Heading north on Roy Thompson Road turn right on West Point Road and go one mile. The stone is located in the yard of an old house on the right, 100 yards off the West Point Road on the right side of the farm lane to the house.
