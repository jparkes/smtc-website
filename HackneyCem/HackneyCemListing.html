HACKNEY CEMETERY, (Scotts Mill, Southport) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mapping the Location


HACKNEY, Joseph, 7 Dec 1778 - 7 Apr 1849.
HACKNEY, Mary, 24 Feb 1781 - 26 Jun 1842, Aged 61 Years, 4 Months & 2 Days.
HACKNEY, Newton, 19 Aug 1819 - 27 Aug 1850.
LONG, N. J., 17 Apr 1795 - 22 May 1850, Born in Halifax County N.C.
 

There are fieldstones in the outlying areas of this cemetery which may indicate slave graves since this cemetery is dated prior to the freeing of slaves in 1860. There is a record of Joseph Hackney purchasing slaves on Ancestry.com and the census of 1840 confirms there were 18 slaves.
