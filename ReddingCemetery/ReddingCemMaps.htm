REDDING CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Go south out of Culleoka on Graham Road. After a mile or so the road makes a sharp turn to the left. The cemetery is behind a private house (on the north side of the road. The place is/was owned by Tommy Brown. This is near the railroad. See the map below I now know the above map showing the Redding Cemetery on the south side of the road is not correct.

 The actual GPS coordinates of this cemetery are: North 35.45926 X  West 86.98415. Most maps have a Redding Cemetery pegged about a mile north of here. I have never heard of another Redding Cemetery so my conclusion is the national mapping is incorrect, which means the USGS & Google mapping has it wrong.
Locatin: 1.4 miles S of Culleoka TN, 5.8 mi NxNE of Lynnville TN, 10.6 mi NW of Cornersville TN, 11.0 mi W of Lewisburg TN & about 8 miles southeast of Columbia Tennessee.
