Full view of memorial

Inscribed as: "SARAH G. WIFE OF GEN. W. D. WILLIAMS. Born Aug 29, 1812 Died Oct 23 1884. Our father and mother are gone. They lay beneath the sod: Dear parents tho we miss you much We trust you rest with God."
