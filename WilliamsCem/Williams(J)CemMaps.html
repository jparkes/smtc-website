WILLIAMS (Joshua) CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Williams Cemetery in Santa Fe.

This Aerial view shows a yellow pointer pointing to the cemetery in the middle of a large field. On our left is the old Santa Fe Pike.

This aerial view zooming in a bit shows the Williams & the McLean Cemetery (incorrectly shown as the Vestal Cemetery on maps) further to the west.
