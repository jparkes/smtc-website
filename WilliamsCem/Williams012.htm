WILLIAMS (Joshua) CEMETERY, SANTA FE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Monument in far left of the photo. Just a thin upright sliver is visible here. Looks like a fence post.

Full view of memorial looking back toward Santa Fe.

Inscribed as: "TO THE MEMORY of SARAH J. WILLIAMS daughter of joshua H. & sarah williams was born july 19th 1812 & departed this life november 27th 1815 aged 3 years."
