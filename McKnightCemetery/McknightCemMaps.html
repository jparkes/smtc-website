McKNIGHT CEMETERY, Hwy 245, Campbellsville Pike, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This Map shows the Latitude/Longitude or GPS coordinates of the area that needs to be searched to find this lost grave as 35.46924 X -87.09715. GPS Coordinates as provided by Andrew Tomlin.

 This road map shows the location and roads needed to get to the McKnight Cemetery. From Columbia go south out Hwy 245 aka Campbellsville Pike about 8+ miles and pass on by the Southport Road on your right. The cemetery is opposite across from the junction where Miller Lake Road terminates into Campbellsville Pike on the east side of the road but slightly northward from a due east line.
This aerial view shows the area heavily forested where this cemetery is found. As you notice the cemetery is above a large gorge probably caused by strip mining. That gorge may be hard to cross. My estimation is the cemetery is about 75 yards west from the highway.
