SMISER-BOOKER CEMETERY, just west of Columbia out new Hwy 50, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Columbia go east on Hwy 50 (James Campbell Blvd.) and turn north (left) on Tom J. Hitch Parkway. Go a mile+ and turn right (east) on Mapleash Ave. Go about a 100 yards and turn right into the driveway with permission. The cemetery is thought to be in back of the house near a power pole. From the looks of Maps the area is well groomed, but the stones are all fallen and fractured. Maybe this is just one of those cemeteries that do not warrant a visit because of the decline in its state. I cannot say for sure because I have no proof of anything except a subjective map evaluation. I do know the status of most of these old cemeteries is sad.
