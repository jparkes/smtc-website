HAYNES CEMETERY (A.K.A FLOWERS CHAPEL or SYMRNA CEMETERY.), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

In Glendale, turn south on Culleoka Rd (Hwy 373). Go two miles to Central Church and turn east on Brush Creek Rd. known also as Scribner's Mill Road. Go about five miles to the well-kept cemetery located on a hill on right side of road.
