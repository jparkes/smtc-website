HAYNES CEMETERY (a.k.a. FLOWERS CHAPEL or SYMRNA CEMETERY.), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Unknown grave & stones not inscribed, 20 feet from John Baldridge memorial. This is the same setup as for the John Baldridge Memorial but the headstone above does not appear to have been inscribed. It also has the large slap covering the grave and installed on the ground.
Photographed on 12/27/2006 by Wayne Austin.
