HAYNES CEMETERY (a.k.a. FLOWERS CHAPEL or SYMRNA CEMETERY.), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Up next to the Cedar Tree is the Baldridge memorials. Seen closer is the Haynes memorials. Both Baldridge and Haynes were Revolutionary Soldiers.
