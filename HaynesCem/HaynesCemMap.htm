HAYNES CEMETERY (a.k.a. FLOWERS CHAPEL or SYMRNA CEMETERY.), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

In Glendale, turn south on Hwy 373, Culleoka Rd (not shown on above map). Go two miles to Central Church and turn east on Brush Creek Rd. known also as Scribner's Mill Road. Go about five miles to the well-kept cemetery located on a hill on the right side of road.
