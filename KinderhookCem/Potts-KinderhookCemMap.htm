POTTS (I) - KINDERHOOK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This Topozone map shows the GPS Lat. and Long. coordinates. Based on 1950s roads, but the coordinates are still correct.

The roads have been changed since 1987 but this cemetery should still be easily found using the same directions from New Hwy 7 instead of Kinderhook Road. Looks like labeling of the roads above may also be incorrect as there are several New Hwy 7. I believe Gordon Lawrence Rd is shown correctly on this map though. Labeled as Potts Cemetery here.
