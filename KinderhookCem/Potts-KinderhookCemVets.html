POTTS (I) - KINDERHOOK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Potts Cemetery War Veterans

Civil War
POTTS, Miles, 8 Jun 1833 - 26 Jan 1915. "Pvt. Co. B. Holman's Cav. Bn, C.S.A." (Son of John Calvin Potts of N.C. & Patsy Humphrey Potts of Va.; gave land for this cemetery. Obit give birth date: b. 24 Apr 1835. C.S.A. Soldier Pension #S13996 - 10 Cav.; Holman's Battalion, Partisan Rangers, Tennessee Co. B, rank out Pvt.; mar. to Missouri Lavender Potts. Ref: Dept. of Tenn. State Library and Archives & Civil War Soldiers and Cheryl Wilson a descendant.)

KELLEY, Andrew "Jack", 28 Jul 1892 - 9 Aug 1976. (Son of Andrew J. & Martha Kelley; Pvt. U.S. Army, WWI.)

World War II
