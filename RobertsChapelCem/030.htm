ROBERTS CHAPEL CEMETERY, Roberts Bend Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.
ROBERTS, Infant of Mr & Mrs T. W. Roberts, 1893. (infant of T.W. & Eva Foster Roberts)
This stone was one of the last one photographed being up against the back fence. This baby lies beside her mother Eva Foster Roberts, wife of T. W. Roberts. Eva died three years after her infant child.
