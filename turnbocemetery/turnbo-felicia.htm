TURNBO (JAMES) GRAVES, ENTERPRISE, TN

TURNBO, Felicia (Coffee), 3 Mar 1787 - 18 May 1865 (78-02-15). Spelling is "Turnbow" on her stone. Felicia is thought to be a sister of Nathan Coffee a prominent businessman & landowner in early (1840s) Maury County times. She is believed to be the daughter of Chesley Jr. and Margaret (Baldwin) Coffee who lived a mile or so to the east of this place and are buried there in the hills.
