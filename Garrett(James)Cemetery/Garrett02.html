GARRET (JAMES) CEMETERY, NEAR CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



GARRETT, Mary, 1784 - 19 May 1849. "Consort of James.". 
Inscription: "In memory of -- MARY GARRETT, -- Consort of JAMES GARRETT -- who was born in the -- year 1784 and departed -- this life May 19th 1849."
Photo & inscription by Wayne Austin. Visited there accompanied with Marc & Mary Belle (descendent) Duvivier of New York. 14 Apr 2008.
