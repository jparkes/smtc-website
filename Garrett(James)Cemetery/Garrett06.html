GARRET (JAMES) CEMETERY, NEAR CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Arney Memorial (fallen) it was face down (1987) when Mr Fred Hawkins visited here. That means the inscription was facing west when the stone was standing and someone flipped it over since then. It is in the western edge of the cemetery away from the Garrett family memorial.

ARNEY, J. J., 15 Feb 1840 - 31 Jul 1914. Father. 
ARNEY, E. F., 12 Nov 1842 - (Jul, 1929, Death Date not inscribed). Mother. (Stone tumbled. At west edge of cemetery.)
