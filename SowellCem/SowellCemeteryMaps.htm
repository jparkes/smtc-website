SOWELL GRAVES, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Go east on Hwy 99 to Rip Steel Road which is the first road past Berea Church of Christ. Turn left (north) and go 1/2 mile. When the road bends back right go into the farm road (with locked gate, permission needed) on left leading to what was in 1986 the Wayne Derryberry farm. The single monument is located about 500 yards (1536 feet) or three tenths of a mile down that road 20 yards on the left of the road.
