Looking south from the end of main run of the memorials one can see other memorials scattered in the above photo.


This interesting photo shows the Oak Tree that has swallowed the stone on the left side into the tree. Notice the decaying trunk indicating this tree is declining. On our left is the Mary Dixon memorial. Just on the other side of he Oak tree is the Calvin Haines Memorial. I presume the swallowed memorial to be that of Calvin Haines' wife as it has the footstone initials SWH.
