LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).

Lot of detail for a stone dated 1828. It may have been set later.

 Hudson, Priscilla, July 4, 1778 - Aug 26, 1828, formerly Priscilla Thomas, 53 years 1 month 22 days
Inscription:
SACRED To the memory of -- PRISCILLA HUDSON -- formely PRISCILLA THOMAS -- who was born on the 4th of July 1778 -- and died August -- 26th 1828 -- Aged 53 years  one month and 22 days. 
Had she been born two years earlier she would have had the same birthday as our country.
