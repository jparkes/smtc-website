LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).

The Joseph P Caruthers memorial facing its normal easterly direction. The bottom image was made by turning the stone toward the western sunshine thereby capturing the shadows of the sunlight on the face of the inscription. After the photo the stone was returned to its normal easterly facing direction and reset. Amazing the difference, one would call the inscription worn looking above when in actuality it is just the lighting.

 Caruthers, Joseph P., Jan 26, 1821 - Sept 14, 1823, son of Robert & Elizabeth Caruthers
Inscription:
SACRED -- To -- the memory of -- Joseph P. Caruthers -- Son of Robert & Elizabeth Caruthers -- was born 26th of Jany -- 1821, died the 14th of -- September 1823.
