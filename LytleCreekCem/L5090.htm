LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).

Shelton, infant girl, 5 Apr 1843 - 29 Apr 1843 "girl infant of G. W. & J. P. Shelton".

Inscription:
This the infant -- of G. W. and J. P. -- SHELTON. She -- was Born April -- the 5th & died -- the 29th 1843. Notice the fallen stone beside this one which is unidentified. There are many in this cemetery like that.
