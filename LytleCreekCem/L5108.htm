LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).

 Porter, Joseph B., Dec 7, 1770 - Oct 21, 1828, Thomas J. & Parry W. Porter to their father; An honest man. The noblest work of God.
Inscription:
Erected by -- THO'S J. and PARRY.W.PORTER -- To the memory of their Father -- JOSEPH B. PORTER. -- Who was born Dec. 7th 1770. -- and died on the 21st October -- 1828.
--- An honest man, The noblest work of God.---
