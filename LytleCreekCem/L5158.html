LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).


Brown, Mrs. Sarah, died 15 Jun 1840, 66 years old. (wife of Col Joseph Brown, she was a Thomas prior to her marriage to Brown) (Boxtomb) (5158)
Inscription: SACRED to the memory of Mrs. Sarah Brown who departed this life 10th June 1840 aged 66 years lacking two days. Who profesed religion in the revival of 1800 and lived in full communion in the church to her death.
