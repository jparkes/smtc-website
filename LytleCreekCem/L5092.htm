LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).



 Breckinridge, Alexander, Aug 14, 1815 , 16 years (5092,93,94)

Inscription: AUG 14 -- 1815 -- IN MEMORY OF --
ALEXANDER -- BRECKINRIDGE -- Aged 16 years -- _ _? Days. This may be the oldest stone in this cemetery. You will not find them much older than this anywhere in the south.
