LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).


Anderson, Mrs. Ann, died 12 Feb 1839, aged 78 years. (wife of Capt. William Anderson) (5160)
Inscription: SACRED to the memory of Mrs. Ann Anderson, who departed this life 12th of Feb 1839, aged 78 years. a boute 50 years a profesor of religion and dyed in full assurance of a happy Immortality.
