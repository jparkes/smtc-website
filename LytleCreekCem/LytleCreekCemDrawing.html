LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).


This is a May 1, 2003 drawing from a survey done by Ronnie Erwin and family. The Erwins' are relatives & descendents of many of the interred.  Ronnie has marked the approximate area of the Erwin ancestors. The print is a bit small but the center listings read: William Edward Erwin, Sr. & Elizabeth Dixon Thomas Erwin whereas the listings for the upper burials above these are for: Jonas E. Erwin & Mary Hamilton Erwin.  
 Drawing & info by Ronnie Erwin January, 2006.
