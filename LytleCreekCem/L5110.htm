LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).

 Porter, Isaac Newton, Oct 17, 1802 - Oct 1, 1825.
Inscription:
To the memory of -- ISAAC NEWTON PORTER -- born on the 17th of October 1802 -- died on the 1st of October 1825.
---- He was civil and courteous in habit -- Honorable in his ways -- Industries in his habit.....
