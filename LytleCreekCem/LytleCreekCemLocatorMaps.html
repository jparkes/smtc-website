LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).



These  are maps depicting the location of the Lytle Creek also called Old City Cemetery and Brown Cemetery. Maps were graphically modified to better reflect the location. If you go there the cemetery is in the forks of two creeks. One has to cross a creek to reach the cemetery from Morrow Lane and Mooresville Pike.
