LYTLE CREEK/BROWN/OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Southeast of Columbia).

 Porter, Elizabeth Thomas, Jan 5, 1772 - Dec 26, 1852, Thomas J. & Parry W. Porter to their mother
Inscription:
Erected by -- THO'S J. and PARRY.W.PORTER -- To the memory of their Mother -- ELIZABETH THOMAS PORTER -- Who was born the 3rd of Jan -- uary 1772.And died the 26th -- of Dec. 1852.
---- She loved her Children and they loved her. ----
