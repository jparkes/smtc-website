GODWIN CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

 George C., Godwin, "80, the oldest citizen of Union died; s/o George W. & Elizabeth Moore Godwin, who were among the first settlers of Santa Fe area; died in the same house in which he was born; married 8 Jan 1872
to Miss Benina Polk Sedberry and they had 8 children, 4 survived"
(Maury Democrat, 3 Mar 1932.)
