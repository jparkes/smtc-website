GODWIN CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This Map shows the GPS coordinates of the Godwin Cemetery. Conversion to Decimal: 35 Degrees.76033 (45.62 divided by 60.) X 87 degrees.08433 (5.06 divided by 60.). 

On Snow Creek Road, 2 tenths of a mile north and further up Snow Creek Rd from Union Church on left. Union Church is at the junction of Snow Creek & Godwin Roads.

As you approach the cemetery look to your left. The yellow arrow on the above map marks the place where you sill see the Godwin cemetery.
