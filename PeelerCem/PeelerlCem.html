Peeler Cemetery Location    Peeler Cemetery signage    Peeler Cemetery lost graves

PEELER, John, 10 Apr 1860 - 13 Apr 1878. (Tombstone is tumbled and broken right through the death date. Looks like born "1860" died "1848", if so this is an obvious inscription error.

There are 5 or 6 graves marked with fieldstones. Someone has recently lovingly restored this cemetery in many aspects. The fieldstones and other unmarked graves were all marked with a wooden crosses and the rock wall has been restored along with a neat sign set in place. [WA 16 Apr 2009]
