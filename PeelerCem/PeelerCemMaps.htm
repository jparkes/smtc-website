PEELER CEMETERY, Columbia, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 Peeler Cemetery Coordinates.
From Columbia By-Pass, go south on Campbellsville Pike for 1/2 mile and turn west on Due St. Go 1/4 mile to where the road starts up the hill. The little cemetery is on the shoulder of the left side of the road after the second sharp curve to the right.
This Aerial Map shows the location as on the east edge and in the bend of the road and in the trees.
