Joe Mack Benderman military marker



BENDERMAN, Joe Mack, 23 Mar 1928 - 25 Jul 2003, 1st Lt. US Army, Korea. (Maury Co. native; son of J. Mack Benderman and Rena Brunson Benderman; 1947 grad. of Hay Long High School, and 1951 grad. of U.T., taught agriculture in Maury Co. for 35 yrs., coached football at hay Long, principal at Hampshire School, golf coach at Central High School; mar. 49 yrs. to Olivia "Libby" McClain Benderman.)
and
BENDERMAN, Olivia McClain, 15 Oct 1932 - no date, married Joe Mack Benderman 6 Mar 1954.
