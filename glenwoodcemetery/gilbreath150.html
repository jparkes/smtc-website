GILBREATH, Herbert A., 14 Jan 1894 - 27 Jun 1894, Son of J. W. & M. A. Gilbreath. This stone is fallen and the base is lying under the main stone. It was photographed from under an umbrella on a rainy Dec 19, 2002. Photo and information by Wayne Austin 9-30-2003.
