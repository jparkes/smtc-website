GLENWOOD CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE
  
Across the Campbellsville Pike from the cemetery stands the old Glenwood Cumberland Presbyterian Church. Its colorful history has long since faded from our memories. Soon the structure will be gone if action is not taken. Cows graze at random now all around its once majestic grounds where children played and studied about Jesus. The adults worshipped their creator, and caught up on the latest in the community. 
If you know the history of this church write in and we will include it here. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 10-1-03 According to MCTC this is was a Cumberland Presbyterian Church. Many of the old interred in the Glenwood Cemetery were members and attended here.
