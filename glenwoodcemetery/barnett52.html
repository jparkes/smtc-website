BARNETT, Caroline, 4 Nov 1826 - 9 Jan 1898 "Aunt Linnie", Daughter of Hugh M. & Martha E. (Johnston) Barnett. I believe Aunt Linnie remained unmarried, but from what I read a well respected lady.
