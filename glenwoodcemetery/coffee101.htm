GLENWOOD CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE



COFFEE, Minnie F.,24 Jan 1892 - 12 Oct 1902, 1st born daughter of W. F. & S. V. Coffee. She was the
 daughter of William Fine and Samantha Virginia (Wiley) Coffee. Minnie was stricken by Meningitis. Her mother Samantha in an attempt to nurse her back to health succumbed a year or so after Minnie's death to what was thought of at the time to be nurses fever. Per the family legend of William F. Coffee as told to Joyce Austin Grigsby by the sister of Minnie who was Ethel Nellie (Coffee) (Chaffin) Barlar, 1895 - 1981, during the 1950's.
