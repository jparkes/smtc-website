GLENWOOD CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE


 

WILEY, William Young, 3 Dec 1843 - 18 May 1923, Company B, 9th Tn Cavalry Bn C.S.A. Son of James Hugh & Easter Elizabeth (Barnett) Wiley who migrated to (Huckabay) Erath County Texas after the Civil War. 
and 
WILEY, Caledonia Chaffin, 22 Jul 1845 -  18 Sep 1917. (Married July 26 1866). She was the daughter of Hillary G. & Susan (Kerby) Chaffin. This couple had many offspring and they held large family reunions spanning several years. Some of the offspring were quite successful.
