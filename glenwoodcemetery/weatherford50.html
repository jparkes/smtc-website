GLENWOOD CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE
 

WEATHERFORD, James L. "Jim", 24 May 1931 - 10 Sep 2010. (Maury Co. native; s/o late Cleveland Sullivan Weatherford and Dessie Lee Ozburn Weatherford; h/o Diane Martin Weatherford; dated for 22 years before marrying in 1999;  attended public school in Maury Co. public school and MTSU; Veteran U.S. Army, serving from 1952-1954, Sgt., receiving National Defense Service Medal, Good Conduct Medal, an honorable discharge; then graduated pre-law at Northwestern, entered Cumberland Law School graduating in 1957, passing the bar in Dec. 1958; practicing in Lawrence Co., elected Circuit Judge of the 11th (now 22nd) judicial district for 3 - 8 yr. terms and retired in 2006; preceded in death by sister, Peggy Weatherford Poag and son William Bailey "Bill" Weatherford, who died in 2008.)

Update sent by Mary Bob Richardson 30 Dec 2010.
