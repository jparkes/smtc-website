GLENWOOD CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE
 
Standing in this photo is not an angel silhouetted in the sunlight on the box tomb of William & Caledonia Wiley but my (now grown) 8 year old daughter Kristi. The trip in 1990 to take these photos was special for me. This would turn out to be the only family history trip back from Jackson TN whereby both my son and daughter joined me on a Saturday escapade to learn of the family history. Photo taken in 1990. [Wayne Austin, October 1, 2003]
