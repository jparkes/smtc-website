STOCKARD, John, 1828 - 1895. (s/o James Andrew Stockard II & Susannah Polk Reese Stockard; mar. "Tennie" West 2 Nov 1864 in Maury Co. Tn.
and
STOCKARD, Tennie (West), (8 Oct) 1841 - (8 Sep) 1930. (daughter of Samuel A. West & Minerva Ann Samuels West interred in the West family cemetery on Blue Branch Giles Co Tn.)
Mary Tennessee "Tennie" (West) Stockard was born 8 Oct 1842 in Giles Co., TN. She was d/o Samuel West & Minerva Samuels. 21051425
She married John Stockard s/o Janes Andrew Stockard II & Susannah Polk Reese 2 Nov 1864 in Maury Co., TN. They had the following: Emma Louisa, John Martin, Susan, Reese A., and Samuel M. Stockard.
She died 3 Sep 1930 in McCains, Maury Co., TN. She was buried Sep 1930 in Glenwood Cemetery in Southport, Maury Co., TN.

I (Wayne Austin) remember my mom (Ruby Barlar Austin, b. 1912) affectionately mentioning her Great Aunt Tennie Stockard in the 1950s to us children. She had a high regard for Tennie. So we see how someone's good reputation can live on for many years (at least for my life span) beyond their life. Kids are you listening.
