GLENWOOD CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE

COFFEE, Mary A. "Agnes"  1868 - (23 Mar) 1948. Nee Mary Agnes Watson. She was the second wife of William Fine Coffee. There were no children by this marriage. However Mary largely raised the children of Samantha since Samantha died seven years after the birth of her fourth & last child. 
and 
COFFEE, William F. "Fine", (18 Mar) 1868 - (14 May) 1954. He was the son of Major Isaac Hamilton & Emma Frances (West) Coffee. William was a native of MC and a manager of a logging operation in the early years of his life down in Giles County and later a store owner in the Bear Creek area of the County. 
and
COFFEE, Samantha Virginia, (21 June) 1869 - (21 Mar) 1903. She was Samantha Virginia Wiley the daughter of William Young & Caledonia (Chaffin) Wiley also interred in this cemetery. Samantha was the first wife of William F. Coffee and the birth mother of his children who were Minnie, Lela, Ethel Nell & Marvin Y. The W. Y. & C. D. Wiley memorial & box Tomb can be seen in this photo in back of this parents of Samantha.
