CROWE CEMETERY,  RALLY HILL, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

DERRYBERRY, Regina, 24 Feb. 1918 - 6 May 2014.
 
Miss. Regina Derryberry, age 96 of Spring Hill, TN, passed away peacefully at her home, May 6, 2014. Daughter of the late Cromer & Lillian Crowe Derryberry. Member of Glenn's Chapel United Methodist Church. "Gener" was a mother to many, a great cook, and always had a smile. She was known throughout Middle Tennessee for her exceptional talent as a seamstress.
In addition to her parents, she was preceded in death by sisters, Mabel Warren & Rachel Gillis. Survived by brothers, John (Martha) Derryberry of the Rally Hill Community and Edwin Derryberry of Columbia, TN; sister and caregiver, Margaret Reed of Spring Hill, TN and several nieces, nephews, great & great great nieces & nephews.
Funeral services will be held 3:00 PM Saturday, May 10, 2014 at Spring Hill Memorial Funeral Home, Rev. Randy Brown officiating. Burial will follow in Crowe Cemetery. Serving as pallbearers will be Kenny Sweeney, Johnny, Mark & Larry Derryberry, Larry & Randy Warren. Memorials may be made to Glenn's Chapel United Methodist Church or Crowe Cemetery. Visitation with the family will be 4-8 PM Friday and 12-3 PM Saturday at SPRING HILL MEMORIAL PARK, FUNERAL HOME AND CREMATION SERVICES, 5239 Main St., Spring Hill, TN
