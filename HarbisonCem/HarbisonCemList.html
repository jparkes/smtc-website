As you can see from the photos this small family cemetery was found well maintained when I surveyed it on 8 Apr 2009. W.A.

Historical note reported in MCTC:
A lot was deeded "to the public for a lot or parcel for ground for a public burying ground or graveyard" by Matthew Harbison, Sr., 26 Mar 1856. 

Listed in "Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries", page 246 & skip to page 255, 4 Feb 1987, by Fred Lee Hawkins, Also listed in "They Passed This Way", 3 Feb 1964, page B-45, by Evelyn Shackleford & Marise Lightfoot.  Added here 15 Jul 2009 based on a 8 Apr 2009  visit & resurvey of this cemetery.
