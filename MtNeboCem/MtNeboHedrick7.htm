MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HEDRICK, M. Frances Goodwin, 25 Oct 1925 - 31 Dec 2012. (d/o George Coleman & Lottie Willie Howell Goodwin; w/o Goerge Hendrick.)

Frances Goodwin Hedrick, 87, widow of George Hedrick and retired from Publix Manufacturing Company, died Monday, December 31, 2012 at Maury Regional Medical Center.
Graveside services will be conducted Thursday at 11:00 a.m. at Mt. Nebo Cemetery with Chaplain Eddie Rogers officiating. The family will visit with friends from 9:30 to 10:30 a.m. at Oakes & Nichols. Notes of sympathy may be extended at oakesandnichols.com.
The Maury County native was born 25 Oct 1925, the daughter of the late George Coleman Goodwin and Lottie Willie Howell Goodwin and attended the Baptist Church.
Survivors include her daughters, Linda Kaye Hedrick and Georgene (Leon) Hinson, both of Columbia; grandchildren, Keith (Ann) Hinson and Deborah (Keith) Poarch; five great-grandchildren; one great-great-grandchild; and two special nieces, Jewell Sutherland and Shirley Kennedy.
Family and friends will serve as pallbearers.

Condensed by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 15 Oct 2015. Posted by C. Wayne Austin 16 Oct 2015
