ROBERTS, Dorothy Eura, 24 May 1918 - 25 Sep. 1918. (d/o Horace & Dlea Covey Adams Roberts.)

Tennessee Death Certificate has Dorothy Roberts, born 24 Jun 1918 to Horace Roberts of TN and Dela Ciley of AL, died 25 Aug 1918 at age 4m in Maury County due to marasmus. Buried in Mt Nebo Cemetery on 26 Aug 1918. Informant is Will Hain.
