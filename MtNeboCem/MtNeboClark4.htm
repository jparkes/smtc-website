MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

CLARK, Paul Clarence, 1945 - 2 Jan 2010. (s/o Velton Clarence & Myrtle Pruitt Clark.)

Paul Clarence Clark, 64, resident of West 13th Street, died Saturday, January 2, 2010 at Maury Regional Medical Center.
Funeral services will be conducted Tuesday at 12:00 noon at Oakes & Nichols with Rev. Rick Points officiating. Burial will follow in Mt. Nebo Cemetery. The family will visit with friends Monday from 4:00 to 8:00 p.m. and after 10:00 a.m. Tuesday at the funeral home. Notes of sympathy may be sent to www.oakesandnichols.com.
The Maury County native was the son of the late Velton Clarence Clark and Myrtle Pruitt Clark.
Mr. Clark was retired from the operations department of Lasko Corporation in Franklin. A dedicated worker all his life, he was an employee of Columbia Construction Company for several years and owned and operated Clark Tree Trimming Company for many years.
Survivors include sons, Herschel (Stacy) Brennan, Clarence Clark, and William Clark, all of Columbia; sisters, Velma McGaw of Mt. Nebo, Rosie (Henry) Kennedy of Mt. Pleasant, Pearlene Clark and Dorothy (Marion) Wilhoite, both of Columbia; grandsons, Chris Brennan, James Brennan, and Joseph Brennan, all of Columbia; nieces and nephews, Bessie, Abbie, Dora, Shelia, Sandra, Charles, and Sarah; several great-niec6es and great-nephews. He was preceded in death by a niece, Joyce.
Family and friends will serve as pallbearers.
