HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the accurate GPS based on the Actual Imagery of Google mapping. I have it plotted as 35.65273 X 87.16609 of in the center behind the Mt Nebo Church.

Go northwest several miles on Williamsport Highway (Hwy 50) to Brown Hollow Road and turn (right) north. Go 1/4 mile to the Mount Nebo Methodist Church on the left. The cemetery is behind the church building.
This topographical map shows the cemetery on Brown Hollow Road.
