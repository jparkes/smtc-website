MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

SNIPES, Lucille (Holt), 26 Dec 1916 - 20 Sep 2004. (d/o John & Izora Oakley Holt; w/o Marvin Snipes and shares same stone.)

Lucille Holt Snipes, 88, of Columbia, died Monday, Sept. 20, 2004, at Maury Regional Hospital.
Burial in Mt. Nebo Cemetery.
She was preceded in death by her parents, John Holt and Izora Oakley Holt; husband, Marvin Snipes.
Columbia Daily Herald obituary 26 Sep 2004.
