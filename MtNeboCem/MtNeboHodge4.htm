MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HODGE, Lizzie Roberts, Feb. 1871 - 16 Aug 1915. (d/o Isham & Eliza Roberts; w/o Henry Hodge.) census

1880 Census, taken 11 Jun 1880, has Isham Roberts, 50, farmer, married to Eliza, 40, living in 4th Civil District, Williamson County, TN. Children are: James, 17, farm laborer; Isham, m, 15; George, m, 12; Lizzie, f, 10; Sam, m, 8; Eol R, m, 6; Sunny, m, 4; and Mary, f, 2.
Tennessee Marriage Records have Henry Hodge married Miss Lizzie Roberts on 10 Apr 1898 in Maury County, TN.
1900 Census, taken 2 Jun 1900, has Henry Hodge, 20, born Jul 1879, miner, married for 12 years to Lizzie, 29 born Feb 1891, living in 10th Civil District, Maury County, TN. They have a son Charley, m, 10m, born Jul 1899.
1910 Census, taken 28 Apr 1910, has Henry Hodge, 28, miner, married for 12 years to Lizzie, 39, who had 5 children with all surviving, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Charley, m, 11; George M, m, 7; Lena, f, 6; Mary F, f, 3; and Annie, f, 2.
