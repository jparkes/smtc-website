ARMSTRONG, Fred Jackson, 19 Jul 1883 - 9 Jun 1936. (s/o Ben & Tammie Ann Armstrong; h/o Lou Willie Steward Armstong Snipes.)

Tennessee Death Certificate has Fred Armstrong, born 1884 to Ben and Tammie Ann Armstrong, born in TN; he was a farmer, married to Lou Willie Armstrong; died 9 Aug 1936 in Columbia, Maury County, TN at age 52 due to spinal meningitis. Contributing cause not related to principal cause: chronic Myocarditis. Buried 11 Aug 1936 in Mt Nebo Cemetery. Informant was Mrs Lou Willie Armstrong of Columbia, TN.
