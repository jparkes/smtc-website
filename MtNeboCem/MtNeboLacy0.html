MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

LACY, William Austin, 1900 - 1970. (h/o Gussie E. Lawrence, mar.. 10 Jul 1917 in Maury Co., Tn.; h/o Tavie Simpson Lacy mar. 29 May 1945 in Williamson Co., Tn.)

Tennessee Marriage Records have Will Lacy married Gussie E Lawrence on 10 Jul 1917 in Maury County.
US City Directories for Nashville, Davidson County, TN has:
1930: Lacy Wm (c; Gussie) driver
1931, 35, 38: Lacy Wm A (c; Gussie) driver
1937: Lacy Wm A (c; Gussie) laborer
Tennessee Marriage Records have William Austin Lacy, 45, married Tavie Simpson, 42, on 29 May 1945 in Williamson County.
US Social Security Death Index has William Lacy, born 15 Aug 1898, died Dec 1970. Last residence was Chagrin Falls, Cuyahoga County, TN.
