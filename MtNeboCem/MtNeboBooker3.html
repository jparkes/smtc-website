BOOKER, Jannie Pearl Lawrence, 29 Apr. 1918 - 11 July 2015. (d/o Gus & Fannie Thomas Lawrence; w/o Walter Henry Booker.)

Mrs. Jannie Pearl Lawrence Booker age 97 of Columbia, TN. died Saturday, July 11, 2015 at Cumberland Manor in Nashville, TN.
Interment is in Mt. Nebo Community Cemetery.
The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Gus and Fannie Thomas Lawrence. She attended the Maury County school system and was a faithful member of Mt. Nebo Original Church Of God where she served on the Motherï¿½s Board, Church Secretary and sang in the Choir and was the oldest member of the Church.
Although she had no children of her own she took pride and joy in caring and assisting rearing of her great nephews John Jr. and Llewendell and great great niece Keanea.
She was preceded in death by her husband, Walter Henry Booker, and all of her siblings: Gussie, Mary Sue, James, Robert, Bethel, Minnie, Lena, Walter and Lucille.
