MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

JOHNSON, Gideon Polk, 16 Nov 1850 - 7 Dec. 1940. (s/o Richard & Katie Mangrum Johnson; h/o Adelia Sandefur Johnson.)

Gideon Polk Johnson was born on November 16, 1850 in Maury County, Tennessee, the son of Richard Johnson and Katie (Mangrum) Johnson.
He was married to Adelia Sandefur (1851ï¿½1906).
A retired farmer, he was living at 1610 Holley Street, Nashville, was a widower and 90 year old when he died at home of a coronary occlusion on December 7, 1940. Burial was the next day in Mount Nebo Cemetery, Maury County.
Most of the above is from his Death Certificate, with Mrs O L McBride of 1610 Holley Street, Nashville as the informant.
