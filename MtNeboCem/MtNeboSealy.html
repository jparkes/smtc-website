MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

SEALY, A.C. (Amos Curtis), 2 Mar 1834 - 30 Jan 1898. (s/o Enoch & Hester Sealey; C.S.A.) 6729

Mr A C Sealey was found dead at his barn last Sunday at 10 am. He was 63 years of age, and leaves surrounding him a wife and two children, Mr Henry Sealey and Mrs Georgie Howell. He has been a member of the Methodist Church for 43 years. 
The remains were interred in Mt Nebo Cemetery.

The Columbia Herald, 4 Feb 1898.
 
 
Sent Mary Bob McClain Richardson 15 May 2017
