MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

MILLER, Lucille Legg, 1927 - 8 Aug 2015.

Mrs. Lucille M. Miller, 88, of Mt. Pleasant passed Saturday August 8, 2015 at Maury Regional Medical Center.
The body will lie in state Wednesday, August 12, 2015, 2:00 til 7 p.m. at Roundtree, Napier and Ogilvie Mt. Pleasant Funeral Home.
Visitation with the family will be Thursday August 13, 2015, 12noon-1:00 p.m. at Mt. Nebo Missionary Baptist Church with funeral service to follow. Rev. Dennis Smith Eulogist. Interment Hunters Cemetery.
Lucille was the daughter of the late Rev. James M. Legg and Queen E. Vinson Legg. She was a faithful member of Fuqua M.B. Church until her health declined.
She was preceded in death by her husband Calvin Miller.
Sister Miller leaves to cherish her loving memories: Devoted sons James (Angela) Miller and Dedrick (Patricia) Miller; Devoted daughter Portia Miller Richard. 4 grandchildren, 9 great grandchildren, aunt Mary Madden, uncle Morgan Vinson, a host of nieces, nephews other relatives special friends Pearline Whitaker and Lena Mae Butler.
Roundtree, Napier and Ogilvie Mt. Pleasant Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements.
Columbia Daily Herald 10 Aug 2015.
