MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

LACY, Ada Parker, 20 May 1867 - 7 Mar. 1950. (w/o Ephrium Lacy; d/o Steve & Ada Parker.) dr & census

1900 Census, taken 21 Jun 1900, has Ephrium Lacy, 35, farm laborer, married for 10 years to Ada, 27, who's had 5 children with all surviving, living in 14th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Fannie M, f, 9, born Jul 1890; John, m, 7, born Aug 1892; Andrew, m, 5, born Oct 1894; Lee Mat, m, 3, born Aug 1896; Willie, m, 1, born Jan 1899.
1910 Census, taken 28 Apr 1910, has Ephrium Lacy, 55, farm laborer, married for 19 years to Ada, 38, who's had 9 children with all surviving, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Fannie M, f, 19; John H, m, 16, feeder at mine dryer; Andrew, m, 15, deraper man at mines; Lee Mat, m, 13, cansyer water at mines; Willie, m, 10; George, m, 9; Curley, m, 6; Crystan, f, 4; and Richard Allen, m, 1y 3m.
1920 Census, taken 23 Jan 1920, has Ephrium Lacy, 62, phosphate mine laborer, married to Ada, 44, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Lee, m, 22, phosphate mine laborer; George, m, 19, phosphate mine laborer; Christine, f, 13; Allen, m, 10; and Paul, m, 8.
1930 Census, taken 25 Apr 1930, has Ephrium Lacy, 65, farmer on own farm, married at 30 to Ada, 50, married at 16, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Curly, m, 24, phosphate mine laborer; Allen, m, 20, phosphate mine laborer; and Paul, m, 17. Also in the household is granddaughter Esther L, 7.
Tennessee Death Certificate has Ada Lacy of Columbia, Maury County, TN, born 20 May 1867 in Maury County, TN to Steve and Ada Parker; widow; died 7 Mar 1950 in Davidson County, TN at age 82 due to organic heart disease. Buried 10 Mar 1950 at Nebo Cemetery in Columbia, TN. Informant was Lee Lacy of Mt Pleasant, Maury County, TN.
