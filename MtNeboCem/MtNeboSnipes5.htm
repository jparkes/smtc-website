MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

SNIPES, Lou Willie Armstrong, 1903 - 1975. (w/o Fred Armstong,, mar. 17 Mar. 1915 in Maury Co., Tn.; w/o Alfred J. Snipes mar. 24 Feb 1945 in Maury Co., Tn.)

Tennessee Marriage Records have Fred Armstrong married Lou Willie Steward on 17 Mar 1915 in Maury County.
1930 Census, taken 22 Apr 1930, has Fred Armstrong, 50, farmer on own farm, married at 35 to Lou W, 30, married at 15, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Ernest C, m, 13; Jun, f, 12; Fred Jr, m, 10; Mary M, f, 8; James A, m, 6; Walter, m, 4y 6m; and John H, m, 2y 3m.
1940 Census, taken 4 Apr 1940, has Lou Willie Armstrong, 38, widow, home laundress, living in Sawdust, Maury County, TN. Children are: Irene, f, 21, home laundress; Fred, m, 19, farm laborer; Mary, f, 17; John W, m, 11; Ben R, m, 9; Annie L, f, 6; and Barbara J, f, 1.
Tennessee Marriage Records have Alfred J Snipes, 63, born 3 Aug 1881, and Lou Willie Armstrong, 47, both from Columbia, Maury County, TN, married on 24 Feb 1945 in Maury County.
US Social Security Death Index has Lou Snipes, born 10 Feb 1898, died Feb 1975. Last residence was Columbia, Maury County, TN.
