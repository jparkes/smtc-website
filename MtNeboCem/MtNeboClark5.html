MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

CLARK, Pearlene, 26 Jan 1943 - 13 Oct 2013. (d/o Velton Clarence & Myrtle Pruitt Clark.)

Miss Pearlene Clark, 70, died Sunday, October 13, 2013 at her residence on Winding Way.
Burial in Mt. Nebo Cemetery.
The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Velton Clarence Clark and Myrtle Pruitt Clark. She had been employed at Miss Andrews Day Care Center and helped rear her nieces and nephews as if they were her own children. She also sat with the sick and the elderly. Miss Clark was a former resident of West 13th St. She enjoyed watching television especially the Hallmark Channel which played her favorite shows, Little House on the Prairie, The Waltons and Golden Girls and she talked everyday to her sister, Rosie. She was of the Baptist faith.
She was preceded in death by a brother, Paul Clarence Clark.

Condensed by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 15 Oct 2015. Posted by C. Wayne Austin 16 Oct 2015
