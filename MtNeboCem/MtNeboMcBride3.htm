MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

McBRIDE, William D. (Davidson) 21 Nov. 1877 - 13 Jun 1950. (h/o Willia A. Sargent in 1897; s/o Robert Nathaniel & Helena Jennings Williams McBride.)

1910 Census, taken 28 Apr 1910, has Will D McBride, 35, farmer on a general farm, married for 12 years to Willie, 30, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Also in the household are brother-in-laws: Albert N, 25, merchant at general merchandise store; Roy, 22, house carpenter; and Engram, 20, odd job laborer.
1920 Census, taken 6 Jan 1920, has Will McBride, 45, operator on a general farm, married to Willie, 43, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Also in the household is brother-in-laws Roy, 31, building carpenter.
1930 Census, taken 10 Apr 1930, has W D McBride, 55, constable for civil district, married since he was 23 to Willie A, 52, married since she was 20, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN.
1940 Census, taken 10 Apr 1940, has William McBride, 65, constable for 8th district, married to Willie, 63, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN.
Tennessee Death Certificate has William Davidson McBride, married retired farmer living in Maury County, TN, born 21 Nov 1874, died 13 Jun 1950 due to cerebral hemorrhage due to chronic myocarditis due to chronic nephritis. Parents were Robert McBride and Helena Williams. Buried 14 Jun 1950 at Mt Nebo. Informant was Mrs W D McBrideof Columbia, TN.
