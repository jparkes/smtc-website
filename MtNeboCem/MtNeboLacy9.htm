MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

LACY, Gussie E. Lawrence, 1902 - 1944. (d/o Gus Baker & Fannie Lawrence; w/o William Austin Lacy)

Tennessee Death Certificate has Gussie Lacy of Davidson County, TN, born in 1902 to Gus Lawrence and Fannie Thomas, both born in Maury County, TN; married to William Lacy; died 18 Oct 1944 in Hubbard Hospital in Nashville, Davidson County, TN at age 42 due to acute hemorrhage due to abruptis placentae. Buried 22 Oct 1944 at Mt Nebo Cemetery. Informant was Mrs Lee Willa Hancock of Davidson County, TN.

Condensed by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 15 Oct 2015. Posted by C. Wayne Austin 16 Oct 2015
