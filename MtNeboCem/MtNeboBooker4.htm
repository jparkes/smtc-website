MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BOOKER, Walter Henry, 27 Jan. 1927 - 6 Sep, 1963. (h/o Alice _____; h/o Jennie Pearl Lawrence, mar. 7 Aug. 1948.)

1930 Census, taken 16 Apr 1930, has Robert Booker, 68, farmer on a general farm, married at 20 to Alice, 48, married at 30, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Ariter J, m, 5; James L, m, 4, farm laborer; and Walter H, m, 2y 3m.
1940 Census, taken 11 Apr 1940, has Robert Booker, 70, farmer, married to Alice, 50, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: R J, m, 15; James L, m, 14, farm laborer; and Walter H, m, 13.
Tennessee Marriage Records have Walter H Booker, 21, born 3 Jan 1927, and Jennie Pearl Lawrence, 30, born Apr 29, 1918, both from Columbia, Maury County, TN, married on 7 Aug 1948. Next of kin for groom is Robert Booker of Columbia, TN. Next of kin for bride is Gus Baker Lawrence of Columbia, TN.
US Social Security Death Index has Walter Booker, born 1 Jan 1926, died Sep 1963.
