MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HOLT, John, 8 Oct 1879 - 15 Apr. 1931. (s/o Sam Oakley & Lizzie Stammans Holdt.)

Tennessee Marriage Records have John Holt married Izora Oakley on 27 Feb 1907 in Maury County.
Tennessee Death Records have Izora Holt, born 10 Jan 1892 to Sam Oakley and Lizzie Stammans, died 11 Apr 1930 in Mount Pleasant, Maury County, TN due to tuberculosis. Buried in Columbia, Maury County, TN on 13 Apr 1930.
1930 Census, taken 28 Apr 1930, has John Holt, 50, widower, laborer at phosphate mine, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Minnie, f, 16; Frank, m, 19, laborer at phosphate mine; Pete, m, 18, laborer at phosphate mine; Lucille, f, 14; Edie, m, 10; Charles, m, 9; Annie M, f, 7; and Pauline, f, 3y 2m.
Tennessee Death Certificatehas widower John Holt, born 8 Oct 1879 in TN to Ben Holt (and unknown mother) of TN, died 15 Apr 1931 at age 52 in Maury County, TN. Buried at Nebo Cemetery on 16 Apr 1931. He was a miner. Informant was John Holt of Columbia, Maury County, TN.
