MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

JONES, Mary E., Jun, 1843 - 9 Nov. 1915. (d/o Andrew & Berthena P. Culberson Jones.)

Mary's parents were Andrew Headen and Berthena P. (Culberson) Jones. She was born in either Chatham or Randolph County, North Carolina.
According to her death certificate, she was buried at Mt. Nebo. No headstone has been located for her.
