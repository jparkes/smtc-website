MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

ROBERTS, Beulah Cook, 25 Aug. 1906 - 23 Jan. 1983. (d/o Tomas & Florence Cook; w/o Sam Roberts, mar. 10 Dec. 1922 in Maury Co. Tn.)

1920 Census, taken 20 Jan 1920, has Tomas Cook, 46, farm laborer, married to Florence, 36, living in 3rd Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Beulah, f, 13; Sarah, f, 12; Lannie, f, 10; Gilbert, m, 8; and Sam, m, 3.
Tennessee marriage Records have Sam Roberts married Beulah Cook on 10 Dec 1922 in Maury County.
1930 Census, taken 22 Apr 1930, has S M Roberts, 45, laborer at phosphate mines, married to Beulah, 25, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN. They have a son Sam Jr, m, 3y 4m.
1940 Census, taken 15 Apr 1940, has Sam Roberts, 67, farmer, married to Beulah, 32, living in 8th Civil DIstrict, Maury County, TN. Children are: Sam Jr, m, 12; Claude, m, 9; Haral, m, 6; and James, m, 4.
