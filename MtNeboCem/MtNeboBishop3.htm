MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BISHOP, William "Bill" Cleveland, 20 Jun 1941 - 6 Jul 2011. (Maury Co. native; s/o late William Bishop and Rosetta Potts Bishop; Veteran U.S. Navy serving 20 years)

Member of Herbert Griffin Am. Legion Post 19, Naval Fleet Reserve and Vietnam Veterans of America, ful military honors at graveside provided by the U.S. Navy Honors Unit; h/o Diane Morel Bishop for 40 years; preceded in death by son, Martin Eugene Bishop in 1984 and sisters, Judy Bishop in 1970 and Rose Bishop in 2010.
