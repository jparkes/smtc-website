MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 
WEBB, George, 1900 - 1968. (h/o Virginia Brown mar. 21 Sep. 1936 in Hickman Co., Tn.)

Tennessee Marriage Records have George Webb married Virginia Brown on 21 Sep 1936 in Hickman County.
1940 Census, taken 11 Apr 1940, has George Webb, 35, farmer, married to Virginia, 25, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN.
US Social Security Death Index has George Webb, born 6 May 1896, died May 1968. Last residence was Columbia, Maury County, TN.
