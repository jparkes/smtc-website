MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

WEBB, Virginia, 1914 - (1 Nov) 2005. (d/o Wide C. & Delia Brown; w/o George Webb mar. 21 Sep 1946 in Hickman Co., Tn.)

Mrs. Virginia Mai Webb, 91, of the Williamsport Community, died Friday, Nov. 1, 2005, at Maury Regional Hospital.
Burial is in Mt. Nebo Community Cemetery.
Columbia Daily Herald 5 Nov 2005.
1920 Census, taken 16 Jan 1920, has Wide C Brown, 45, farm laborer, married to Delia, 41, living in 1st Civil District, Maury County, TN. Children are: Harlan, m, 21, farm laborer; Kugar, m, 17, farm laborer; Annie L, f, 14; Pinie, f, 12; Wide C, m, 10; Virginia, f, 6; and Sammy, m, 4m.
Tennessee Marriage Records have George Webb married Virginia Brown on 21 Sep 1936 in Hickman County.
1940 Census, taken 11 Apr 1940, has George Webb, 35, farmer, married to Virginia, 25, living in 8th Civil District, Maury County, TN.
