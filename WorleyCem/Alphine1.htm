WORLEY CEMETERY, Isom, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE Also known as the WHITESIDE CEMETERY

ALPHIN, Birney Wagner, 22 Nov 1919 - 16 Dec. 2013.

Mr. Birney Wagner Alphin, 94, former resident of Columbia, died Monday, December 16, 2013 at Williamson Medical Center.
Funeral services will be conducted Friday at 1:00 p.m. at First Presbyterian Church, 101 Legends Club Lane, Franklin, TN with Rev. Chris Joiner officiating. The family will visit with friends Friday from 12:30 p.m. until time of service at the church. A graveside service will follow at 3:30 p.m. in Worley Cemetery in the Hampshire Community with David Davidson officiating. Military honors will be provided at the graveside by Herbert Griffin American Legion Post 19. Oakes & Nichols Funeral Directors are assisting the family with arrangements.
The family suggests memorials to Alzheimers Association of Middle Tennessee, 4825 Trousdale Drive, Suite 220, Nashville, TN 37220. Condolences may be offered online at www. oakesandnichols.com.
