THETA BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Feb 8, 1930
NASHVILLE TENNESSEAN

MRS. R. V. SELLERS
Columbia, Tenn., Feb. 7.- (Spl) - Mrs. Willie Jane Sellers, 69, wife of R.V. Sellers, died Thursday afternoon at 5 o'clock at her home at Theta.
The daughter of the late Thomas Dodson and Jane Fitzgerald Dodson, she was a member of a prominent Maury county family. She was a member of the Baptist Church since early in life. Surviving her are her husband, and the following children. Mrs. E. R. Fitzgerald, of Columbia; Mrs. Otey Thurman of Tampa, Fla., E. B. Sellers and W. H Sellers of Franklin, and Mrs. A. N. Hutchinson of Detroit, Michigan; two brothers, E. R. Dodson, of Columbia, and Arch Dodson of Bowling Green, Ky. and a sister, Mrs. Lola Hutchison of Columbia. The funeral services were held this afternoon at 2:30 o'clock at the Theta Baptist church conducted by the Rev. W. J. Stuart and the Rev. J. E. Hight. Burial was in the Theta cemetery.
The following nephews served as pallbearers. Laurence Dodson, Madison Dodson, Ross Dodson, Smith Dodson, Walter Hutchison and Eugene Hutchison.

 Information sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 7/16/07.
