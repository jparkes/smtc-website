BAXTER CEMETERY,  (West) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This cemetery is on Southpoint Road. It is 3/4 of a mile west of Southpoint Cemetery on the same road. During 1987 there was a large Elm Tree on the cemetery premises. Not sure if it is there in 2008.
