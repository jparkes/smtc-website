DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Photo of Mayfield Delk (son of Howard), John A. Delk (son of John G), James Porter Delk (son of Howard), and Jake Delk (son of Dan) made at the old Kettle Mills church of Christ bldg (toward Stanfill Gap from current bldg) in about the winter of 1941). Notice the road mud on the cars and the coats.
