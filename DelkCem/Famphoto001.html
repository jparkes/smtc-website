DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Sam Gill Delk Reunion: front row: Josie Farris, w. of Reps Farris; Samuel Gill Delk and wife Isabella W. Anderson Delk; Jacob "Jake" Delk.
back row: John Alexander Delk, Richard Delk, Wm. D. "Billy" Delk (this is Sam Kennedy's grandfather), Samuel H. Delk, Molly Delk Wiley, Nan Delk Voss, Joshua V "Dollar" Delk

This picture was made in 1894 at Samuel Gill Del's in the Kettle Mills community just beyond the Delk Cemetery. The names on this photo should be correct except for Isabella W. Anderson Delk's maiden name. JVD.
