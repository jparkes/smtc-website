DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


This is a view of the cemetery on a cold rainy 27 Jan 2009. We are looking west across the cemetery and into the distant hills. The Duck River is just over on our left. Most of the cemetery is visible from this angle. Notice a tall grain silo in the distance which could be easily confused as a tombstone in this photo.
Photo (27 Jan 2009) & information by Wayne Austin 28 Jun 2009.
