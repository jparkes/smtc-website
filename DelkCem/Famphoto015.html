DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Left to Right: Will Church-far left cutoff, Brown Shelby-on Stump, Young boy-unknown, Unknown, Elbert Dean, Edward Baker-behind post, Sam Tucker, Ewell Church, Joe Shelby, Bob Cummins, Unknown, Dave Totty-on edge of porch. One of the unknowns is Marcy Hoover. Photo made on a Sunday afternoon 3 Apr 1920 in Shady Grove on the porch of Iron Baker's first store building about where the Post Office is located now at Duck River, TN.
Photos & information were sent from the collection of James V. Delk and used with his permission. 2 Dec 2008.
It is known the two remaining "originals of the above picture are owned by Mrs. Jean Shelby and Mrs. Sue Baker. They are of the opinion there are no other originals. It is thought the labeling came from a published account in the Columbia Herald. Revised commentary and sourcing 8 Nov 2009.
