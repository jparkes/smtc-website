DELK CEMETERY, GREENFIELD BEND RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Columbia go out Williamsport Pike (Hwy 50) just beyond Williamsport turn left (south) on Greenfield Bend road. Go about 4 miles staying on Greenfield Bend Rd., and, after you pass the Morrow Rd on your right, turn left (south) onto the Delk Dr. Road. Almost immediately make another turn left into the 1st farm house road. This road terminates at the farm house but the Cemetery will be found 200 yards to the right or (Southwest) down the Duck River bank from the house. Locator Maps   Cemetery Photo view
