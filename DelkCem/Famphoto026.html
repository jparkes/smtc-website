DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This photo was made beside the Geo. Kennedy Store in the Kettle. More specifically just in front of the Jim Lovell grist mill in about 1939 or 40. The group photo was actually made in from the yard of George Kennedy, across the road at the side of the store building.
Names on the front row: John A Delk, (son of John G. Delk); Alice Kennedy (dau. of George Kennedy; died fairly recently); James Porter Delk (son of Howard & Nina Delk) marry Sara Cummings from Shady Grove. Names on the back row: Sarah Rachel Anderson (dau. of Rosco Anderson & Alma Farris; still living --I think-- at Shady Grove) married Everett Anderson; (behind her left shoulder) Jake Delk (son of Dan Delk); ___(I'm not sure who the middle girl is)__; Mayfield Delk (son of Howard & Nina Delk).
