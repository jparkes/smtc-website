DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Photo was made at Marcy Hoover's in about 1950-02.  The names are --L to R-- Mr. and Mrs. Bud Watson;  Mr. and Mrs. Dudley Hill; and Mrs. and Mrs. John A. Delk.  Mrs. Watson's maiden name was Hoover, she is still living;  Dudley Hill was in the real estate business in Columbia for many years as "L.D. Hill & Sons" and before that as "Hill and Greer"; Mrs. Hill was Mary Elizabeth Delk, dau. of John G. Delk.  John A Delk was the son of John G. Delk, Mrs. Delk was the former Mary Alice Whiteside (she's still living --the other four are dead)
