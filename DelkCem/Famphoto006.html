DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Photo of Howard and Nina Delk, at Ma Betty's, was made circa 1948 at Elizabeth Cathey Kennedy's birthday, This photo was made the same day with the larger reunion on this site of 1948 or 1949. Haynes Peery made these pictures. He was Carl Harris, Jr's father in law.
This copy of the photos & information given here are the property of James V. Delk and used with his permission. 2 Dec 2008.
