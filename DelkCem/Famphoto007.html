DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Photo of Howard, Floyd, Allen, Paul, Horace Delk. Made in Sanger (in Denton Co.), Texas in 1907. Pictured are Howard Delk, Floyd Delk, Allen Delk (baby, he was born March 1906), Paul Delk, and Horace Delk. These are the sons of Joshua Vernon "Dollar" Delk and Emma Jane Thompson Delk (one child, Harry, died in 1901 at age 1)
