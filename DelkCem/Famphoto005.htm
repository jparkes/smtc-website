DELK FAMILY PHOTOS, KETTLE MILLS, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Photo was made in about 1950-52 at Marcy Hoover's of the men of the Anderson/Delk & Hoover families. They are all deceased now (2008) except for Carl Harris, Jr., I think. JVD.
