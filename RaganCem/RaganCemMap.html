RAGAN CEMETERY,Â  KEDRON RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
1986 Instructions: On Kedron Road, behind barn of first house (on right) southeast of overpass, in edge of woods, about 500 ft. from barn.
2008 Instructions: Go northwest out Kedron Rd from Rally Hill (Junction of Hwy 431 & Kedron Road) about 2 1/4 miles. Just before ( 300 yards) the road passes under the Interstate 65 look to your left and behind a house & barn will be the cemetery. It is about 350 yards out from the road with a Concrete Block Wall around two of the graves. The concrete wall can be seen from the road. With permission turn into the driveway at the house in front of the cemetery and get out and walk to your left (east) beside the barn and turn back south toward the cemetery out in the pasture.
