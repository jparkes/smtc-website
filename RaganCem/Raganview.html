RAGAN CEMETERY,  KEDRON RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Ragan Cemetery. I am told it was much larger that what is shown here at one time. This Concrete Enclosure is only a part of the cemetery.

The Roberson memorial sits well out in front of the others but may also include other graves in alignment with the Roberson graves. I can see the stump of a large Cedar tree in the right of the photo. That tree may have been an active part of the cemetery at one time. It is large enough to be hundreds of years old.
Photos & information by Wayne Austin 5 Aug 2008.
