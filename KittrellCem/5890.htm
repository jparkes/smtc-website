KITTRELL CEMETERY, STEPHENSON SCHOOL HOUSE RD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GALLOWAY, Enoch, 2 Jul 1840 -17 Jul 1927. (Enoch Galloway was born in Tennessee, the eldest child of three children of Jack and Edna Galloway. Enoch's father died sometime prior to 1847 and Edna married James McKeel. James McKeel brought six children from a prior marriage to the family. The James and Edna had five children together.
Enoch Galloway married Hannah Judd Vestal, a widower with two children: Hannah Elizabeth Vestal b. 1858 (married W.L. Delk) and Annie Vestal b. 1857 (married W.A. Kitrel). Enoch married Hannah sometime between 1860 and 1862.
Children of Enoch and Hannah are:
Mary Alice b. 1863 d. 1900, m. William Alonzo Brooks, Dan b. 1865, d. 1919 m. Betty Stanfield Whiteside, Lura Pauline "Lou" b 1867 d. 1934, m. Joseph Finley Whiteside,
Martha Porter "Matt" b. Dec 10, 1869 m. James D. Cathey.
