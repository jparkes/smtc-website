The Black Arrow on the above map is pointing at the Kittrell Cemetery. This is north of Hwy 412 and Hampshire (near county line) is on our left with Columbia being on our right. 
Go west on Hwy 412/99 several miles and veer right or northwest onto Taylors Store Rd. Keep that for about a mile and turn west or left on Catheys Creek Road for about 2 miles and turn north across the Catheys Creek onto Stephenson Schoolhouse Rd and go about 1/4 mile to the Cathey's Creek Church of Christ. Look up the hill straight ahead at the tree line and go straight ahead into a private drive and the cemetery will be on that road about 50 yards up the road on the slope on your right. Look to your right at the Chain Link fence and gate into the cemetery. 
Go to this cemetery with love and appreciation for your/the heritage of this great community. Going there for any other reason will invoke the fierce loyalty of this community and you will wind up in deep trouble.
Map from MS Streets and Trips modified for this site by Wayne Austin. 3 Nov 2008.


 Kittrell Cemetery is near Cathey's Creek Church of Christ across the road above the church building on the side of a hill on Stephenson School House Road. It is on the side of a slope and has a enclosed fence around it with a separately enclosed iron fence around the north part of the cemetery enclosing the Staley Memorials.
