HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

William Elijah Harlan.

HARLAN, William Elijah, 22 May 1842 - 28 Aug 1843. "Son of Jehu/(John?) & Pricilla Harlan." (5048)
Inscription:
SACRED
To
the Memory of
William Elijah Harlan
Son of Jehu & Priscilla
T. Harlan who was born
May the 22nd 1842 and
Departed this life August
the 28th 1843.
