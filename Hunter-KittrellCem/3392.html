HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


BOND, David, d. 23 Aug 1855. 9 days old. "Son J. H. & S.E. Bond". (Stone lost until it was dug up 6 Jul 2012 by the restoration crew.)
Inscription: 
DAVID
son of
 J. H. & S. E. BOND
Died Aug.
23rd, 1855
aged 9 days.
David Bond's mother is Sallie Elizabeth Bond 1837-1861 buried @ Old Hunter's. Father J. H. Bond probably was there also but I understand that lots of graves/headstones were destroyed when they mined there also for phosphate. But I found a connection to the Hunter's going back to 1730- from Chowan Co court records deposition of Isaac Hunter appraiser of estate of Richard Bond. Richard Bond's direct descendant John B. Bond also buried @ Old Hunter's.
