HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


PATTERSON, Ann Priscilla, 28 May 1841 - 10 Apr 1865. "Daughter of D. T. & M. W. Moss & wife of John R. Patterson." (4983)
Inscription:
ANN PRISCILLA
daughter of, 
D. T. & M. W. Moss
and wife of,
 John R. Patterson
Born May 28, 1841.
Died April 10 1865.
Blessed are the dead who die n the Lord.
This stone must have thousands of hair line fractures. I have never seen one as bad as this which is still intact. It could be repaired with a mix of white concrete mortar, hydrated lime and sand smeared over the cracks to seal them with porous strength.
