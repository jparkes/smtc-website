HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Willie H, Orr in front of the box tomb for Eudora E. A. Kittrell.

ORR, Willie H., 6 Jan 1861 - 6 Aug 1862. "Son of A. & L. K. Orr." (Tombstone broken.) (5058). 
The small memorials of the Orr children are generally broken or misplaced and found lined up next to the box tombs of the Kittrells, They are actually a part of the row to the east of the box tombs.
