HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The stone in the distance pointed out with a "V" away from the others.

BURKET, Velaria A. P., 24 Sep 1826 - 18 Aug 1869. "Wife of A. Burket & Daughter of N. & G. Wright." (4999)
Inscription: 
VALERIA A.P. wife
of A. Burket & Daughter
of N & G. Wright born
Sep 24.1826 died
Aug 18.1819. 
This stone is located over next to the western edge of the cemetery next to the phosphate pit.
