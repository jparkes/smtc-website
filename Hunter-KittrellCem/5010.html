HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The tumbled memorial of Mary Smith. The base is in the center of the photo with the mid section leaning against that and the shaft is on the ground in the lower part of the photo.

SMITH, Mary, died 9 Apr 1889. Aged 74 years. "Wife of Lawrence Smith." (Tall shaft, toppled.) (5010)
