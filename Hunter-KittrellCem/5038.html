HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Box tumbled tomb of Martha F. Hunter.

HUNTER, Martha F., 11 Jul 1842 - 19 Oct 1844. "Daughter of Wm. & Sarah M. Hunter." (Box tomb.) (5038)
Inscription:
IN memory of
Martha F. 
Hunter, daughter of Wm.
 & Sarah M. Hunter born
July 11th 1842 and 
Departed this life the 19th 
of October 1844.
