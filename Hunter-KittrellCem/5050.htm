HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Martha L. G. Kittrell.

KITTRELL, Martha L. G., 10 Dec 1825 - 7 Jun 1844. "Daughter of Micklejohn & Ann A." (Box tomb.) (5050)
Inscription:
Martha L. G. Kittrell the
daughter of Micklejohn
& Ann A. Kittrell was
born Dec. the 10th
1825. departed
this life June
the 7th
1844.
