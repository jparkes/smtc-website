HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


KITTRELL, Eliza H., 11 Oct 1804 - 3 Jul 1851. "Wife of Seth R. Kittrell.' (nee Eliza J. Hunter; married to Seth Kittrell, 1 Jun 1826; Tall shaft, largest monument in this cemetery, spherical top has fallen and lies on the ground next to the stone. Inscription on the east side next to the tree.) (5027)
Inscription:
SACRED
to the Memory
of
ELIZA H.
KITRELL,
wife of
Seth R. Kittrell
Born Oct 11th 1804
Died July 3rd 1851.
