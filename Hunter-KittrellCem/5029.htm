HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Patience Hunter memorial.

HUNTER, Patience, 1764 - 7 Aug 1830. Age 66. "Consort of Jacob Hunter." (5029)
Inscription:
SACRED
TO
the Memory of
PATIENCE HUNTER
Consort of 
JACOB HUNTER
Born 1764 and departed
this life on Aug 7th 1830.

Note: Ann Alston Hunter and Elvira J. Hinton Hunter were sisters. Their mother is also buried here. She was Patience Williamson who married Jacob Isaac Hunter. I don't know where he is buried. Alex Orr, 84 yrs old as of Jan 2011
