HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The box tomb for Eudora E. A. Kitrell in the center of the photo

KITTRELL, Eudorah E. A., 19 Sep 1833 - 10 Oct 1853. 'Dau. Micklejohn & Ann." (Box tomb.) (5059)
Inscription:
Eudora E. A. Kittrell. the
daughter of Micklejohn 
& Ann A. Kittrell. was
born Sept. the 19th
1833. departed
this life Oct.
the 10th
1853.
