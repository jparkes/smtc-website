HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

IRWIN, Sallie Elizabeth, 3 Oct 1859 - 6 Oct 1862. "Daughter of W. M. & F. A. Irwin." (Shaft toppled.) (4984)
Inscription:
SALLIE
ELISABETH
Daughter of W.
M. & F. A. Irwin
born Oct 3, 1859
died Oct 6 1862.
Sallie Elizabeth Irwin is connected to the Hunters. Mary Polly (Priscilla) married John Wm. Irwin son of Wm. Montgomery Irwin and Francis Augusta Moss Irwin. Same initials on Sallie Eliz. Irwin's stone W. M. and F. A.
