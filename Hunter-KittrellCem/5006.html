HUNTER-KITTRELL CEMETERY, REESE'S CHURCH ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


OVERSTREET, James, 15 Jun 1766 - 14 Aug 1846. 69-02-00. (5006)
Inscription:
IN
memory of
James Overstreet
who was born
June 15th 1766 and 
departed this life
August 14th 1846
Aged 69 years
and 2 months.
Previous researchers thought this name was misspelled as "OVERTREET" but the small missing "S" in OVERSTREET is shown elevated above the other letters as a correction by the inscriber thus this becomes a proven surname prominent in Maury County History.
