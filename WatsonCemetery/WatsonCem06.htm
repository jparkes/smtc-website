WATSON-PINKSTON CEMETERY, SMYRNA CHURCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Wm Wesley Watson & Mary S Pinkston.

PINKSTON, Mary Steele, 13 Jan 1822 - 15 Mar 1870. (Daughter of John & Marget "Peggy" Pinkston Watson. Married to Wm. Wesley Pinkston on 25 Jun 1840; killed in accident when her horse ran away with the buggy attached and her in it. Source, Jill Garrett, Historical Sketches.)
