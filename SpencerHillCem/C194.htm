SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CHAPMAN, Luna "Looney", 1 Jan 1903 - 30 Jul 1976. (Son of John W. Chapman and Mary Agness Thomason; married Bertha Alma Allen in 1923; native of Giles County. Great Aunt of Rick Gray.)
and
CHAPMAN, Beartha (Bertha), 9 Oct 1906 - Date not inscribed. (Daughter of Emmett Allen and Vicki Bell Hall; wife of Looney Chapman - married 1923; native of Marshall County.)
Mrs. Bertha Allen Chapman, 95, resident of the Watts Hill Community, died Friday, June 14, 2002, in Heritage Manor. Funeral services for Mrs. Chapman will be conducted Sunday at 2:00 p.m. in the chapel of Williams Mt. Pleasant Funeral Home with Dalton Cole and Billy Dean Felker officiating. Burial will follow in the Spencer Hill Cemetery. She was a native of Marshall County, the daughter of the late Emmitt and Vicki Bell Hall. She was the widow of Looney Chapman who died in 1976. She was a homemaker and was a member of the Mt. Zion Church of Christ. Survivors include one daughter, Minnie Nelson of Ethridge; four sons, Marvin (Maxine) Chapman, Hazel (Jean) Chapman and Carl (Brenda) Chapman, all of Summertown, and Forace (Toni Gale) Chapman of Columbia. 25 grandchildren, several great-grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. She was preceded in death by 6 children, Elwood Chapman, Alton Eugene Chapman, Virgil Chapman, Horace Chapman, John Harlan Chapman, and Melvin Wayne Chapman. Pallbearers will be Doug Chapman, Mike Chapman, David Chapman, Vance Chapman, Allen Chapman and Keith Chapman. Honorary pallbearers will be Jeff Chapman and Garry Chapman.
