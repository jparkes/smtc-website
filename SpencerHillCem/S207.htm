SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE



SPENCER, John F., 1854 - 1929. 
and
SPENCER, Mary L., 15 Sep 1853 - 7 Sep 1901."Wife of J. F. Spencer"
SPENCER, Susie, 30 Aug 1871 - 27 Nov 1915. "Wife of J. F.", "Beloved one Farewell". Was Susie the second wife of J. F. Spencer or were there two different J. F. Spencer's? The dates suggest that Susie outlived Mary L. and could have been a 2nd wife.
