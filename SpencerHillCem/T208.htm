SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE



THOMASON, P.L., Mr. & Mrs. no dates. (inscribed by hand on this large fieldstone. Must be Porter L. Thomason, who died April 6, 1887 of a gunshot wound. He was born about 1847. He married Mollie Spencer.) With a rough MR/MRS inscribed here there is indication that she is interred here also.
