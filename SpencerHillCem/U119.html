SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Unknown stone marking a grave between William A. Davis and Abner Cummings/(Cummins). Appears to me to be a hand shaped limestone rock and not a decaying marked stone. Two Cummings family memorials show up in this photo in our immediate background.
