SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

THOMASON, Agnes M., 1921 - 1975, (Oaks & Nichols Funeral marker.) This is a very durable funeral marker and is designed to last in the weather for hundreds of years if the lawnmowers will just be careful and not knock it over. To our left is the Thomason Funeral marker devoid of any given name and dates. This marker was not listed in the book MCTC or TPTW.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there March 20, 2004.
