LOGUE,  John B.,  (1 Mar) 1842 - d. (20 Jun) 1933.  (Co. B, 9 Tenn. Cav., C.S.A.; MCTC).  Buried  SPENCER HILL CEMETERY.   9th Battalion (Gantt's) Co. B, Tenn. Calvary, Confederate side, rank in Pvt. rank out Pvt.  (NARA)

William S. Robb,  28 Aug 1845 - 12 Aug 1923.
Buried at Spencer Hill is in an unmarked grave, according to Daily Hearld 1907.
Conf. Sol. & Pat. of Maury Co., p. 296, states that William S. Robb served in the Confederate Army as a steward in a hospital.
