SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CUMMINS, John Wesley, 10 Nov 1881 - 14 Dec 1935. John W. (Wesley) is my grandfather and husband of Florence R. Young (buried at Arlington). He is the son of Rube and Manomey. W. O. is the child of Florence Young and John Wesley.
