SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 


 The Bottom photo is looking into the older part of the cemetery. We are looking northwest. The cemetery has actually expanded in burials to the east and west from the center. Inscribed stones can be found dated as early as 1888 to 1895 across several surnames. From the top photo one can see the Spencer Hill Church building in the distance thru the trees. These are the only two photos (made in winter of 1992) of Spencer Hill Cemetery that I have. Most of the graves are located back to our left.  It was raining in March of 2004 when I was there. Digital cameras do not make good area photos in the rain.
Information and photos by Wayne Austin, 6/6/2005.
