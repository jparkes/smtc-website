SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

HENSON, Cal B., 28 Nov 1862 - 28 Dec 1924. 
Some photos do such a great job of capturing the background as this one surely does. We still have all you see in the background to photograph and some not seen to our right and left. It is beginning to rain and the photographer did not drive an hour and a half to be denied photos by mother nature so I put up the old jumbo umbrella. Now that complicates the photography as one must hold the umbrella and make to photo also. Also the stones get wet and the photos do not show up as well. Old well nothing stops an old military head. WA 5/21/2005
