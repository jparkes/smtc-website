SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CUMMINS, Manomey, 20 Mar 1855 - 11 mar 1895. Abner was the father of Manomey Cummins who married Rube C. Cummings - they were 1st cousins once removed - genealogy programs show Rube as the grandnephew of Manomey (Omey). Rube was the son of Lindsay, son of Jacob (according to family info and a census).
 Info and listing by Judy Cummins French, Editing by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Photo dated 3/20/2004 by WA. Judy also sent in photos of the Cummins memorials.
