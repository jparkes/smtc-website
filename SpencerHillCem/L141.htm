SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

LOGUE, James E., 28 Nov 1916 - 7 Apr 1945. (Tenn Cpl. 945 Field Arty Bn., WWII.) James was a war casualty. He is listed among those on the Columbia War Memorial Wall for those Maury County natives who made the supreme sacrifice for our freedom.
