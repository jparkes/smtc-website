SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

ROBB, Dr. W.S., MD., (28 Aug) 1845 - (12 Aug) 1923. (Native of Sumner Co.; son W.J. & Maria Smith Robb.) 
and
ROBB, Maggie E., 1860 - 1933. (Native of Jefferson Co., Ala.)
Civil War record: William S. Robb, 28 Aug 1845 - 12 Aug 1923. Buried at Spencer Hill is in an unmarked grave, according to Daily Herald, 1907. Confederate Soldiers & Patriots of Maury Co., p. 296, states that William S. Robb served in the Confederate Army as a steward in a hospital.
