POWELL, Amanda E., 25 Aug 1828 - 6 Apr 1885 There is a listing in MCTC for an Amanda E. Brumit with the same birth & death dates. I am not aware if that was a simple error or if Amanda Powell was at one time Amanda Brumit and the authors of MCTC were noting that.
