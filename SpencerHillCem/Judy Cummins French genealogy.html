Here is some of what I have.  Some is a repeat of earlier info, but wanted to be sure that I had it right.
Information from 1870 Lawrence Co., Census shows Louisa and Abner's Family.  From Kenneth Warise: "ELISA" WAS LOUISA GRISHAM.  ABNER MARRIED LOUISA, A WIDOW WITH TWO CHILDREN, IN LAWRENCE COUNTY ON FEB. 4, 1868.  Louisa's daughter, Frances married Houston Cummins.  Houston was a brother to my great-grandmother, Manomey.  Louisa Gresham Cummins was Houston's step-mother as well as his mother-in-law.

1880 Census of Maury Co., Tn shows Louisa living with Houston and Frances.  Note from Kenneth Warise: LOUISA - WIDOW OF HOUSTON'S FATHER (ABNER) AND MOTHER OF HIS WIFE (FRANCES).  HER LISTING IN THIS CENSUS EFFECTIVELY CONTRADICTS THE 1886  DEATH DATE ON ABNER'S TOMBSTONE.  HE OBVIOUSLY DIED BEFORE 1880.
 
Ruben Cummins was first married to Manomey (his first cousin, daughter of Abner) and then to Maggie Young, daughter of Wm. L. and Rhoda Scribner Young.  Maggie was a 1/2 sister to my grandmother, Florence Young Cummins.  This made Maggie my grandmother's 1/2 sister as well as her step-mother-in-law and Heaven knows what else.  My family tree is a circle.  :-)
 
I have never been able to figure this out, but will do more research and let you know what I find.
