SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CHAPMAN, Daniel Paul, 8 Dec 1978 - 15 Jan 1995. "A little boy was born into this world full of strangers. Sixteen years later loved by all who knew him. A young man left a world filled with friends. He's not meeting friends in Heaven."
