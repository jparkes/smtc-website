SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


McMILLION, Minnie, 19 No 1944 
also
McMILLLION, Jennie, 19 Nov 1944. 
also
McMILLION, M.E., 22 Mar 1929 - 1 Jul 1929. 
Jennie & Minnie have Oaks & Nichols Funeral Markers well preserved. To our right and above is the memorial inscribed by a home made artist for M.E. McMillion another infant born in 1929 and died during the same year. I am unaware of the relationship of M.E. to Jennie & Minnie being 15 years between their births. 
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there March 20, 2004.
