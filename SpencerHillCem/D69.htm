SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


DAVIS, John W., d. 31 Dec 1931.
DAVIS, Mary Margaret, 3 Jul 1855 - 17 Dec 1919. "Wife of J.W. Davis." (Dau Bill Hammond.) I hope there is not an error in this record by the placing these two stones together as if they are husband and wife. The stones and graves were side by side among other evidence.
