SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


HENSON, Robert Flem, 1867 - 1938. 
and
HENSON, Lucy A., 7 Dec 1871 - 29 Jul 1901. "Wife of Flem." The memorial for Robert Flem Henson 1867-1938 is shown in the background. I believe that is the memorial for the person Lucy was married to. She died young so I wonder if Flem might have remarried?
