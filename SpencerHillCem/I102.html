SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE



INGRAM, small stone with initials M. M. I., & 2 infant graves. The flowers are our clue that these are not footstones. All of the flowers are the same type flowers indicating the same person set them suggesting a common bond.
