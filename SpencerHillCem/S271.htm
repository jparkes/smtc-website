SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE








STEWART, William, 1882 - death date not readable assuming the above date is the birth date. I believe I see the name William Stewart stenciled with a steel punch on the last memorial. There are eight concrete markers with embedded steel plates marking graves all lined up in a row is this cemetery. The only one readable is for Mr. William Stewart detailed above. This is an inexpensive method of creating a memorial, but evidently there was no follow through and seven of the steel plates were not inscribed. Might I presume these are all for the Stewart family? If you know of the details of this family of memorials please let us know and we can list the correct information here.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there March 20, 2004.
