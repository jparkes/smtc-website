SPENCER, G. F. "Buck", 13 Sep 1885 - 28 Dec 1960. Father. 
and
SPENCER, Fannie E., 14 Mar 1883 - 5 May 1918. Mother
"I (Jack Dugger, born 1918,  today June 12, 2005) remember "Buck" Spencer. He used to lead singing and ran a singing school a time or two at Bethel Church of Christ at Southport. He stayed in our home, then."
