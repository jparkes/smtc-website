SPENCER HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


CUMMINS, Andrew J., 16 Jun 1876 - 5 Nov 1926. 
and
CUMMINS, Maud M. Akins, 15 Jul 1884 - 16 Jan 1969. Funeral marker says name: "Maud Akins Cummins"
Andrew is the son of Houston and Frances. Houston is the son of Abner and brother of Manomey. J. I. is one I have not identified his lineage. Charley is a brother to Rube (Reuben) and a son of Lindsay (murdered in Ala and buried somewhere down there).
