LAWRENCE CEMETERY-MT PLEASANT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 ALLEN, Alberta Louise Kittrell, 3 Apr 1918 - 18 Jan 2011. (d/o Hinton Greene and Alberta Martin Kittrell)
Mrs. Alberta Allen was born on April 3, 1918 in Mt. Pleasant, Tennessee. She was the daughter of the late Hinton Greene and Alberta Martin Kittrell. Alberta worked for many years at the University of Arizona. She enjoyed volunteering at the Community Food Bank. She loved flowers and playing bridge. Alberta had a deep love for the arts. She was a very giving and unconditionally loving Christian woman.
Mrs. Allen is survived by her daughters, Virginia Kwolek and Ann Allen; grandson, Queston Kwolek; brother, Hinton Kittrell; and many other family and friends.
Mrs. Allen was 92 years old and a resident of Tucson, Arizona. She passed away on Tuesday, January 18, 2011 at Peppies House Hospice in Tucson.
Graveside services for Mrs. Allen will be conducted at 2:00 P.M. on Saturday, January 22, 2011 at Lawrence Cemetery in Mt. Pleasant with Rick Points officiating.
The Allen Family will receive friends from 1:00 P.M. until service time at Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant.
Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant is assisting the family with arrangements
