LAWRENCE CEMETERY-MT PLEASANT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


BAYER, John Clarence, 25 Dec 1885 - 28 Jun 1963.
John Clarence Bayer is the eldest son of Joseph Bayer and Sarah Arrevia Bedwell Bayer. He was a native of Cumberland City, Stewart County, Tennessee.
