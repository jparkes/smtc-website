BUFORD CEMETERY, near Spring Hill,(N.Eastern) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates for the Buford Cemetery.


From I-65 take Saturn Parkway west & go north on Port Royal Road for 3/4 mile and turn right on Lovell Lane. The cemetery is inside a fence on the right about 1/4 mile down the road. The road ends at the cemetery but that is not shown on the above map.

This aerial view shows the fence line of the cemetery lined with trees making the cemetery not visible from a distance. The public road ends at that tree lined fence line running north & south.
