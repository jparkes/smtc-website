HARRIS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, right on old TVA Rd, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
The Selinie Harris & her Infant, memorial.

HARRIS, Selinie, 16 Jul 1834 - 13 Jul 1856. "Wife of Edward Harris." (Dau. Christopher & Elizabeth Elizabeth Chappell Harris.
HARRIS, Infant of Edward & Selinie Harris, "Interred with Selinie Harris, its mother"
Selinie Harris was the wife of Edward Harris. After the death of Selinie, Edward moved to Texas, where he died there. Inscribed as: "SELINIE wife of Edward Harris, Born July 16, 1834, Died July 13, 1856. Also an infant of above."
