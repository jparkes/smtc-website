HARRIS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, right on old TVA Rd, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The Christopher C. & Elizabeth Harris memorial on our left, and the Charlotte J. Sealy Harris memorial is on our right. This is in the middle of the rock enclosure.
HARRIS, Charlotte J. Sealy, 5 Dec 1858 - 31 Jan 1887. "Wife of T. H. Harris."
Thomas H. Harris was the son of Edward & Selinie Harris. He was born in 1854. After the death of Selinie, his father Edward moved to Texas, where he died there. Thomas H. remained on the farm near Knob Creek, wedding Charlotte "Lottie" Sealey in 1886. She died in 1887. From this marriage they had one son, named Curtis Harris. In 1895, Thomas H. married Mildred Houser (1860-1940.) He died in 1939 and he and his second wife are buried at Godwin-Chappel Cemetery.
