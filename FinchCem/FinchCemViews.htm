FINCH CEMETERY, Williamsport, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The two memorials of the Payne Children are in our immediate view with the Finch family (5 each) stones further down the hill. Other small stones you see are footstones.

We are standing near Hwy 50 looking at the signage pointing out the cemetery north up the slope where the gate is seen with the white supporting cross bar. Someone keeps all that area mowed.

This nearby antique home is likely the place the Finch Family lived at one time.
