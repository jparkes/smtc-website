FINCH CEMETERY, Williamsport, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

With this map we can see the Latitude & Longitude (GPS) coordinates.

Here we see the long & narrow clearing spanning up to the cemetery upon the hill under the trees. At a crest in the hill is the cemetery at the tip of the yellow pointer.
Directions: From Williamsport, cross the river and go 1/4 mile. The cemetery is on north side of highway 50 on land owned by Hooker Chemical Company (1987). The cemetery is up the slope in the trees about 100 yards from the road.
