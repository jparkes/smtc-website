HILLS CHAPEL CEMETERY, CROSS BRIDGES,HWY 99/412, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


PARKER, John E., 24 May 1894 - 24 Feb 1946.
PARKER, Mrs. Bell Kelley, "70, died Thursday 25 Mar 1954 (actually 21 Jan 1954); daughter of Rufus Morgan and Martha Jane Holt Kelley; native of Maury Co.; married to John E. Parker (Daily Herald, 29 Mar 1954.) She is interred as stated below in this Certificate of Death. Evidently Bell does not have a marker. We conveniently list her with her husband in this cemetery. Hopefully this is what she would have wanted.
