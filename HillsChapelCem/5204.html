HILLS CHAPEL CEMETERY, CROSS BRIDGES,HWY 99/412, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Little stone and a Funeral Marker for Bobby Prince.

PRINCE, Bobby, 1939 - 1939. (Funeral Marker.). Mr Fred Hawkins found this record in 1987. He too concluded this was an infants grave. 
The caretakers here are to be commended for being careful enough to allow this funeral marker to stand at least 20 years if not 69 years. [WA 16 Nov 2008]
