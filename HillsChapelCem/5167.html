HILLS CHAPEL CEMETERY, CROSS BRIDGES,HWY 99/412, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

8th and last stone we can see nearest to us on the row on our right coming this way. There are others but they were not captured in the photograph.

Mother and Father stones for the Rosson memorial were graphically altered moving them closer to make the image more readable. Left and right relationship is accurate.

ROSSON, William Robert, 8 Feb 1913 - 1 Dec 1972.  "Father"
ROSSON, Garland E., 30 Jan 1918 - 26 Feb 1996. "Mother" 
M.V. Poarch has an interest in this interment.
