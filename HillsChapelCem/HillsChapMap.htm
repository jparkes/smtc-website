HILLS CHAPEL CEMETERY, CROSS BRIDGES, HWY 99/412, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Go south from Cross Bridges on South Cross Bridges Road 1/2 mile and the cemetery is on the right with a sign marked Cross Bridges Cemetery. The Black Arrow is pointing at the Hills Chapel Cemetery. Columbia is on our right out Hwy 412 and Hampshire (near county line) is on our left a few miles. Map from MS Streets and Trips modified for this site by Wayne Austin. 10/20/2008.
