SOUTH POINT CEMETERY, SOUTH POINT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
From Columbia traveling Hampshire Pike west go about 10 miles and veer right off onto Taylor Store Road. After a couple of miles turn left on Love Branch Road. Travel Love Branch Road for about 3/4 mile and turn right on South Point Ridge Road. Follow South Point Ridge Road for something less than a mile and look left and you will see the cemetery near the road as you round a slight curve to the left.
