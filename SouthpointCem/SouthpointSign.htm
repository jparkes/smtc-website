SOUTH POINT CEMETERY, SOUTH POINT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is the sign for the cemetery. This sign might be suggesting the name to be "The Edwards Cemetery at Southpoint".
As I was photographing this cemetery a nearby pack of hunting dogs were setting up a clamor of howling & barking. I felt a like I was on a Raccoon/Possum/Fox or Rabbit Hunting expedition instead of conducting Cemetery research here. Being a city dweller for last the 44 of 62 years this was amusing as it brought back childhood memories.
