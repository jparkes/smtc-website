BROOKS, (ABNER), Santa Fe, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the Latitude & Longitude Coordinates of this Cemetery using another NAD27 methodology. The Plotting in relation to the Herman Church Road is incorrect anyway for this cemetery. I found it at approximately N 35.75106 X W 87.13522 which is several hundred feet north of where the above map places the red check for this cemetery. The topography appears correct for the cemetery to be located where our directions specified as in the bend of the Herman Church Road shown as a dotted line unimproved road on the above map.

In Santa Fe, go north on Fly Road for 1 and 1/2 miles. Turn west on Herman Church Rd. The cemetery is on the right just after the road bends (right) to the north in the trees about 50 yards from the road. Hard to spot in the summer thickets.)
