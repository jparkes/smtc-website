CECIL FAMILY CEMETERY, MT PLEASANT, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The Mrs. C.V. Warren in the background in front here is the Shelvey W. Cecil memorial.

CECIL, Shelvey W., 26 Jun 1824 - 23 Jun 1849. 25 years. "Born Montgomery Co., Va.; married Mary J. Bradon on 27 Apr 1843." Mr Fred thinks the name is Mary C. Brown. I see an "a" in the name and the initial J.. You decide. This stone leans sharply back to the west.
