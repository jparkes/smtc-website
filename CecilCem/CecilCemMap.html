CECIL FAMILY CEMETERY, MT PLEASANT, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


From Mt Pleasant take Cross Bridges Road north and west for about 2 miles and turn left on the Roy Thompson Road. Bend back north and take the right fork into Rippey Lake Road and go until it bends and take the farm road straight ahead for 200 yards to the end of the hedge row where the gate goes into the next field on your right. With permission to get thru the locked gate follow the path up the hill east but slightly southward toward the tree line. Go into the trees and the cemetery will be there in the forest surrounded by a three foot high rock wall about 2 feet thick. The rock wall is still in good shape and therefore the cemetery is also well preserved except for underbrush.
