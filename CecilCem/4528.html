CECIL FAMILY CEMETERY, MT PLEASANT, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Footstone with initial M.E.H. for Mary E. Howard. It is the footstone for the broken stone shown below.

The Mary E. Howard with the top broken off and missing.

HOWARD, Mary E., d. 17 Jun 1866. Age 19 yrs 9 mo & 7 ds., "Wife of Rev. J. W. Howard & daughter of J. W. & Sarah Cecil." Rev J. W. Howard probably remarried (though there is no other name on his memorial but his) and is buried in the Ebenezer Cemetery south of Mt Pleasant (this is north.) His listing is also on this site.
