CECIL FAMILY CEMETERY, MT PLEASANT, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The big tree right beside this stone will eventually push this stone over and it may crash on some of the smaller stones breaking them also.There is also a problem in felling this tree now. It will also crush the wall if someone just cuts it and lets it fall.


CECIL, William Ingram, 9 Jan 1809 - 28 Jan 1877. "Born in Pulaski Co., Va.; married in Delaware Co., Ind. on 8 Nov 1832; died in Maury Co., Tn.'
CECIL, Elizabeth Ribble, 9 Oct 1811 - 28 Jun 1894. "Born Pulaski Co., Va.; wife of William Ingram Cecil." (This is a tall spire.)
