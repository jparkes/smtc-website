JACOBS-FITZGERALD (B. H.) CEMETERY, SANTA FE
 

You will notice that the name used here is the Jacob Cemetery. It is almost at the top of the crest of a ridge. Following the Electrical Transmission line on the Topo map northwest one can count 6 steel transmission towers north west of where the Tom Fitzgerald Road crosses under the TVA Electrical Transmission Lines. The cemetery sits under the 6th one but that tower will not be visible at that place in the Tom Fitzgerald Road due to the hill sloping downward on the other side. The closest point and better approach is to use Mr. Hawkins directions on the main menu but the house owner is probably different today than in 1987.
