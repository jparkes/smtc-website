CENTER POINT CEMETERY, Twin Springs Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Center Point Cemetery as N 35.25841 X W -86.65483.

Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431 to Bidwell and turn left (west) on Old Petersburg Pike. Follow that until it cross or terminates into S. Fishing Ford Road and turn left (or south) then turn back west on Twin Springs Road. Twin Springs bends back south and just before it bends back west again look to your left next to the road to see the cemetery, a modern well kept cemetery.
