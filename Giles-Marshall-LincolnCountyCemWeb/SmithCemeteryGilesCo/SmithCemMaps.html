SMITH CEMETERY, (Hwy 166 on east side) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
aka Pulley Cemetery.
Use this map to find the Longitude and Latitude location or GPS of the Smith or Pulley Cemetery.

Location: Take Bethel Road (Hwy 166), 1 1/2 miles beyond Kedron; and turn left into a side residential road which goes by the cemetery on the left and one residential house on a bank of a hill. The cemetery is about 100 yards back & before you get to that house. This cemetery is near the site of the old Pulley home. Evidently the Smith family lived near here at one time too.
