BEASLEY CEMETERY, (Crook Run Road) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS coordinates of the Beasley Cemetery. I believe the GPS coordinates to be accurate.
This road map shows the cemetery off of Highway 166 on the east side of Crooked Run Road. Take Bethel Road (Hwy 166) to Crook Run Road and go past Fogg Hollow Road. Access to the Cemetery is behind a locked gate and rest with the permission of the Lindsey family on the left down their driveway on a steep decline about 7 tenths of a mile past Fogg Hollow or John Brownlow Roads.
This aerial view shows the Cemetery the on the right (south) side of the Lindsey's driveway. The part under the trees was highlighted in yellow and a red check was placed in the center of the cemetery.
