AYMETT CEMETERY (Hwy 64 East, North side of Road, past Leatherman Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Aymett Cemetery inside a rock wall behind a barn - N 35.18058 X W 86.96047

Location: On old Aymett place now owned by Dr. Joe Fite near Aymett Town. Take Highway 64 go past the (Leatherwood) road where you see the lumber mill. Joe Fite lives
in the large house upon the hill. Another set of instructions: Go East on Hwy 64 to Leatherwood Creek Rd and turn north and go to Cobb Lane and turn right.Â  Follow that as it snakes around and (with permission) turn right into the driveway of Dr Fite's home and park near the back side of his driveway and walk southeast toward the barn. You will find the cemetery behind & northwest of the barn some 20 yards.
This aerial map shows the cemetery behind the barn but you can also notice the route to this cemetery by traveling Cobb Lane to the land owner's home.
