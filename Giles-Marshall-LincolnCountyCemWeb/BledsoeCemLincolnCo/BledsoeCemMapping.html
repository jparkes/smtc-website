BLEDSOE CEMETERY, Foster Hollow Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Bledsoe Cemetery as N 35.34477 X W -86.57797.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 to Petersburg & turn right (East) on Hwy 129. Follow that several miles and turn north on Bledsoe Road and follow that for about 2 miles and turn right (east) into Foster Hollow Rd. After about 200 yards turn left (north) into a field road and go toward a clump of trees in the distance on the hill. This little family cemetery is in that clump of trees about 300 yards off of Foster Hollow Road.Â
