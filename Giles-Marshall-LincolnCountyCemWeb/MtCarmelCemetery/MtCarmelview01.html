This photo shows a good sampling of the many memorials in this cemetery. The photographer's track was up the hill from row to row. Look closely at the detail of each stone and you should be able to identify the foot print of the stone by noticing its many color variations, different sizes and shapes to help you pick the stone of interest from this lineup. Why do we want to do that? Burial place denoted genealogical significance.
