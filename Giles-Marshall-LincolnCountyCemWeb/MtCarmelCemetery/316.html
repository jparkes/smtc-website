MT CARMEL CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Gracy memorials, John, third from the far end with the steel stake and Rachel, next (left) over without the stake.

John Gracy, 1759 - 1839, Revolutionary War Soldier.

GRACY, John, 30 Jan 1759 - 8 Oct 1839, Revolutionary Soldier. Married 5 Mar 1793.
GRACY, Rachel, 5 Mar 1773 - 23 Jun 1848. Married 5 Mar 1793. 
Inscription for John Gracy: SACRED to the memory of JOHN GRACY Revolutionary Soldier Born the 30th Jan 1759 Married March the 5 1793 Departed this life 8th of Oct 1839 Age 80 Y. 8 M. 18 D.
Inscription Rachel Gracy: SACRED to the memory of RACHEL GRACY Born March the 5th 1773 Married March the 5 1793 Departed this life 23 of June 1848 Age 75 Y 3 M 18 D.
