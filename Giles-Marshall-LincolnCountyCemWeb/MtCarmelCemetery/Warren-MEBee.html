MT CARMEL CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
WARREN, Mary Elizabeth "Betty" Bee, 1834 - 25 Jun 1864 (the 1st wife of William W. Warren who is interred in the Lynnwood Cemetery; no tombstone has been found for her in any survey. The memorial is likely to be broken and no longer readable. She could be interred in an unmarked grave near the other Warren memorials.
From Sue Davis a Giles Co researcher, 15 Jul 2009.
