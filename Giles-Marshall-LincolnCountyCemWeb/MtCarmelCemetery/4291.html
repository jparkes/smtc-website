MT CARMEL CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Sarah Morrison memorial is the one on our left next to the broken memorial of John D. Warren.

The broken plastic memorial to Sarah A. Warren Morrison.

MORRISON, Sarah A. Warren, 9 Dec 1840 - 18 Nov 1881, married to John Morrison 22 Sep 1867. This memorial was found broken. Half (the brown part) of it was on the ground below. I set it in place to make the photo and then buried the fallen dirty part next to the steel post below to keep the lawn mower from breaking it. This memorial is not going to last much longer. The plastic splits and burst in the hot summer sun. It is next north to the John D. Warren Memorial as can be seen in the photo above.
