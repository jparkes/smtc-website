PHAGAN CEMETERY, Old Molino Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Phagan Cemetery as N 35.12540 X W -86.67221.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and thru south on Hwy 273 (old Elkton Pike) follow that for a couple of miles and turn left on Old Molino Rd. Follow that for about 3 miles and just before Luttrell Road on your left is a field road that takes you to the cemetery.

This map shows the (right) turn off into the field road about the last curve in Molina Road before approaching Luttrell Road. With permission the road goes south of and beside a barn, but if you follow the road into the second big field you will be faced with a fence between you and the cemetery. So just before you cross into the 2nd big field meander right along and between the trees to the cemetery northwest of you. You can see that with the yellow highlighted pathway. If you have done it right there will be a tree lined hedgerow on your left thru the whole trip off Old Molino Road.
