This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Austin Family Cemetery as N 34.99513 X W -86.83530. I think this is in the city limits of Ardmore Tennessee.

Location: From the center of Ardmore go east on Hwy 53 toward Huntsville (Main Street) to Hwy 110 (Ardmore to Fayetteville Hwy) and turn left. Go about 1/2 mile and look left at the cemetery in a well groomed area.
