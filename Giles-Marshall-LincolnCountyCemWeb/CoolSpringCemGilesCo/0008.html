The Pulaski Citizen March 10, 2005
HELEN MAULTSBY HOLT
Mrs. Helen Maultsby Holt, 80, of Pulaski, died Tuesday, March 8, 2005, at the Hillside Hospital.
A Giles County native, she was born March 29, 1924, and was the daughter of the late Roy Maultsby Sr. and Amy Lou Bailes Maultsby. Mrs. Holt was a member of East Hill Church of Christ for 51 years.
Funeral services were to have been held today (Thursday) at the East Hill Church of Christ. Burial was to have been in Cool Springs Cemetery.
She was preceded in death by a brother, William Andrew Maultsby; and a sister, Emma Jean Price.
Survivors include her husband of 62 years, James Edward Holt of Pulaski; a son, Bill Holt of Pulaski; daughters, Debbie Carroll of Mt. Pleasant and Donna Millican of Pulaski; brothers, Morris Maultsby of Pulaski and Roy Maultsby Jr. of Nashville; two grandchildren; a great-grandson and two step-grandson Obituary added by C.
