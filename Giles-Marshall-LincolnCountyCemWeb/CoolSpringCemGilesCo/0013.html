COOL SPRING CEMETERY, (Minor Hill Hwy on west side of Hwy) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Mary Elizabeth Locke died yesterday. She was a resident of Pulaski and 71 years old.

Survivors include a son Timothy Miller of Pulaski and daughter Vivian Coleman of the Goodspring community. She is survived by brothers Robert Morse of Nashville, William Morse of Elkton and sister Patricia Elias of Huntsville. Four grandchildren and six great grandchildren also survive.
