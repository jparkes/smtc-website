SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 Husband
GLOVER, Grady Roosevelt 9 Jul 1907 25 Sep 1999
(h/o Maggie Elizabeth Locke Glover)
(s/o James Samuel and Sarah Fox Doggett Glover)
Obituary:
Mr. Grady Roosevelt Glover, 92, of Goodspring died Sept. 24, 1999, at Baptist Hospital. A Tennessee native, he was the son of the late James Samuel and Sarah
Glover and was married to the late Maggie Locke Glover. He was a retired farmer and school bus driver. Funeral services were held Sept. 26 at Carr and Erwin
Funeral Home. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery. Survivors include daughters, Lorene Campbell of Pulaski and Nellie Karshner of Nashville; six grandchildren,
10 great grandchildren and six great-great-grandchildren.

Wife
GLOVER, Maggie Elizabeth 16 Jun 1910 2 Apr 1993
(w/o Grady Roosevelt Glover)
(d/o Sam and Bertha Jones Locke)
Obituary:
Mrs. Maggie Elizabeth Glover, 82, of Goodsprings, died Friday April 2, 1993, at Maury Regional Hospital in Columbia. Funeral services were held on Sunday
April 4, at Carr and Erwin Chapel with Bernard Konig officiating. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery. Mrs. Glover was the daughter of the late Sam and Bertha
Jones Locke. Survivors include her husband, Grady Glover, Pulaski; one son, Edward S. Glover, Louisiana; two daughters, Lorene Campbell, Pulaski, Nelly
(Nellie) Karshner, Nashville; four sisters, Louise Johnson, Goodsprings, Ruth Banner, Pulaski, Cloie Webb, Lewisburg, Bernice Cates, Minor Hill; six
grandchildren, 8 great-grandchildren, 1 great-great-grandchild.
