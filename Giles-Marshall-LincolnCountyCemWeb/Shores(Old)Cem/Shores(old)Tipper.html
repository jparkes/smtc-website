SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

TIPPER, Florence 9 Jun 1895 11 Aug 1971 (d/o George Washington and Nancy Louisa Tabitha Norwood Tipper)
Obituary:
Mrs. Florence Tennessee Tipper, 76, died Tuesday, August 17 at Taylor�s Hospital in Lewisburg. Services were held Thursday, August 19, at the Bennett-
May Chapel with burial in the Shores Cemetery. A native of Giles County, she was the daughter of the late Babe and Nancy Norwood Tipper. Survivors include
one daughter, Mrs. Hughey Luttrell, Pinewood; two grandchildren; two sisters, Mrs. Olga Lucurs, Mrs. Frank Prince and one brother, Charlie Tipper, Delina.
