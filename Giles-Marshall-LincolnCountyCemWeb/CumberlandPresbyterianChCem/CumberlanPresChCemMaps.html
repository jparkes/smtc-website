CUMBERLAND PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, (Petersburg) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS coordinates of the cemetery called Cumberland Cemetery on USGS mapping.

From Fayetteville travel the Lewisburg Highway to Petersburg and merge right onto South High Street. Continue on High St traveling north for a 1/2 mile and the cemetery is on the right next to an old church building.

This aerial map shows the Cumberland Presbyterian Church Cemetery indicated with the red cross. Look right just after you pass the church building and you will see it. This cemetery is not maintained except for the grass. The monuments are about all fallen and broken and piled together with missing parts including missing tops & bases.
