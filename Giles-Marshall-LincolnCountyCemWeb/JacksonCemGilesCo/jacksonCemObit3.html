JACKSON CEMETERY, (SE Minor Hill 2 Mi.), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

JACKSON, Wilburn 19 Sep 1886 15 Feb 1905 s/o Joseph Samuel and Eunice Alsup Jackson From The Pulaski Citizen.

Wilburn Jackson, son of J. S. Jackson died at his home at Minor Hill, Thursday after an illness with typhoid fever complicated with pneumonia. Wilburn Jackson was about 18 years old and a promising young man. The burial was at the family graveyard on Friday.
