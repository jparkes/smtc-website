MILAM CEMETERY, (Hwy 110, Milam Rd), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Milam Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go southwest on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Highway) about 7 miles. Go past Skinem, Kirkland Chapel Baptist Church & Mary's Grove Community. Turn right (or bear right) into Milam Road and after the road bends back right continue straight into the field & cemetery road toward the distant clump of trees where the cemetery is located.

This Aerial Map shows, marked with a red check. There are some farm assets along the field road to the cemetery so permission may be needed here.
