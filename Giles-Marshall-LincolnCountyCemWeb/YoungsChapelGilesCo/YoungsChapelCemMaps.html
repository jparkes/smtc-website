YOUNG'S CHAPEL CEMETERY, Youngs Chapel Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Young's Chapel Cemetery.Â 

Location: From I-65 Exit 14 at Highway 64 go west toward Pulaski to Bunker Hill Rd and turn left (south). Continue on that until you go under I-65 and turn left (east) onto Youngs Chapel Rd. Follow that for something over a mile and the Church and cemetery is on your right before the road bends sharply to the right.
