MOUNT PISGAH U. METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, Pisgah Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Mt Pisgah Cemetery.Â 

From the junction of Hwy 31 and the Hwy 64 southern by-pass go east toward I-65 several miles and turn right (south) on Pisgah Ridge Road and go just over a mile to the Pisgah United Methodist Church. The Cemetery is behind the church.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained as outlined in a graphic yellow border. It is fenced. The roads on the east side doubles as residential road. Go here with deliberate upscale intentions.
