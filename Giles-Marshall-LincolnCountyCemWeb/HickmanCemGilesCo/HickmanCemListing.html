 Judy King Sanders (the source author) and visitor of this cemetery in 1996 says the cemetery is not in good condition. It is an open cemetery and cows have been allowed to roam over it.  Another source is the Giles County Cemetery Historical Society book.  The GSC book say also that Elizabeth Hickman and husband J.B. Hickman (or Morrow) is interred here without headstones. They offered no listings for any other burials.  Photo by John Colvett. Revised to add John's photos in May 2015
