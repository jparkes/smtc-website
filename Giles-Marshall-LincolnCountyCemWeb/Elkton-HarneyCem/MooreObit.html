Obituary:

SAMUEL MORGAN MOORE
Mr. Samuel Morgan Moore, 75, of Ardmore, Tenn., died June 12, 1995 at St. Thomas Hospital in Nashville.
Mr. Moore was a native of Tennessee and the son of the late Samuel Buckner and Verna Bevels Moore.
Services were held June 14 at Carr & Erwin Chapel; Sam Phipps and William Suddarth officiated.
Burial was in Elkton Cemetery. Survivors include wife, Virginia Moore of Ardmore; five sons, Donnie Moore and Billy Wayne Moore, both of Ardmore and Malcolm Moore, Eddie Moore and
Joseph Moore, all of Elkton; one brother, Luther Moore of Elkton; 11 grandchildren and three great grandchildren.
