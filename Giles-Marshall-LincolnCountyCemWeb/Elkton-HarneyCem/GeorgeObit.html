Orleana Bryan George, 92, of Elkton, Tn. died Thursday, March 14, 2002 at SunBridge Nursing Home, Ardmore, Tn.

Funeral services were held Sunday, March 17, at 3:00 p.m. at Bennett-May Funeral Home. Burial was in the Elkton Cemtery with Bennett-May Funeral Home in charge.

Survivors include one daughter, Dolores George of Nashville, TN; two sons, Phil George of Baugh Station, Elkton, TN, and Sam George of Pulaski, TN; two grandchildren, Mike George of Murfreesboro, TN, and Stephanie Langford of Bryson, TN; one sister, Pauline Fuanigan of Pulaski, TN; one brother, Robert E. (Julia) Bryan of Elkton, TN; one sister-in-law, Mamie Bryan of Elkton, TN; and several nieces and nephews.
(Your Community Shopper, 12 March 2002.)
