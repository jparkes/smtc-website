McCANLESS CEMETERY, Friendship Community, Beech Hill Rd. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the McCanless Cemetery as N 35.23257 X W 86.91967.

 Location: Go east out old Hwy 15 aka Beech Hill Rd several miles to the Friendship Church Community. As you arrive in Friendship and when the Road starts to bend back to the right the cemetery is behind the house belonging (today) to the Monteros family. While they will welcome you as friendly likeable folks make sure their dog is under harness before getting out of your vehicle.
Alternate instructions: Old Hwy 15 at Friendship Community, about 2½ miles west of Beech Hill, on the Glen Worsham place, now the Monteros residence.
