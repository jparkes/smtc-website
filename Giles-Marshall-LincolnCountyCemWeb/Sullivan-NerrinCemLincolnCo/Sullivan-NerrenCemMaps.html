SULLIVAN-NERREN CEMETERY (Barnes Hollow Rd.), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sullivan-Nerren Cemetery as N 35.19366 X W -86.68742.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 several miles to Barnes Hollow Road and turn left and after the road bends back south look on the right for the cemetery road up the slope.

This photo shows you the view in this cemetery. It is fenced and well kept.
