FRY CEMETERY, (near Old Lynnville) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS location of the Fry Cemetery.  This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. It is my belief the GPS Latitude & Longitude are correct as stated.


 Location: Go out of Pulaski on Hwy 31 north to Waco (Old Lynnville). Turn left on Fry Branch Road. Cemetery is on the left across from old Fry home.  
This Aerial view shows the cemetery under the trees north west of Waco (or Old Lynnville)
