WAGGONER-FRIENDSHIP UNITED METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, (Lynchburg-Boonville Road) MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Waggoner-Buckeye/Friendship U. Meth. Church Cemetery GPS location.
