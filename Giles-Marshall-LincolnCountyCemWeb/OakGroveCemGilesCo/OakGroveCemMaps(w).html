OAK GROVE CEMETERY, (Oak Grove Community, near Hwy 11) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Oak Grove Cemetery as 35.09569 X -87.11703

 Location: Go south on Minor Hill Hwy to Oak Grove road and follow that until it dead ends into Cowhorne Hollow Rd. Turn left and go abt 600 yards to the cemetery on right.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the right in the corner of a junction in the road.
