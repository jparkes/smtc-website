HENRY TODD BUTLER CEMETERY, (just S. of Hwy 64) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Henry Todd Butler Cemetery.Â 

This road map which shows only the old Hwy 64 is slightly incorrect but the cemetery is still off of Bubb Road. Directions: Go east from Pulaski By pass on New Hwy 64 to Aymett Town Road and go thru Aymett Town and pass Leatherwood Creek Road on your left and bear right into Bubb Rd. The cemetery is in the right corner of the driveway of the first residential road on your right after you bear right into Bubb Road. It was behind a tall fence with no gate when I was there forcing you to go all the way into the farm and double back. There is no gate and an electric fence awaiting you. Well just another obstacle for an old country boy who could clear 6 foot fence at one time without touching it. Now I do good to clear a 6 inch fence.

This aerial map shows the cemetery in the corner of Bubb & a residential road. It is fenced with electrical shock protection on two sides and a high wire fence on the other two sides.
