FERRELL-JUDKINS CEMETERY, (Fall River Rd to Agnew Rd.), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Ferrell-Judkins Cemetery.Â 

 Location: Go southwest on Minor Hill Highway (11) to Anthony Hill and turn right (on Fall River Road. Follow that for abut a mile as it curves around and turn northwest on Agnew road. Go about 1/4 mile and turn left into the long driveway of Mr. McGee who is a logger. The McGee family has owned and lived here many years. The cemetery is northwest of his home and work buildings in the pasture under a new fence and wooden gate.
