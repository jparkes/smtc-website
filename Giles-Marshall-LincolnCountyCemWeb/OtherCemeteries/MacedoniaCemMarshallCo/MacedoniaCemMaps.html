MACEDONIA CEMETERY, (NE corner of) MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS of the Macedonia Cemetery.

From Hwy 431/106 go east on Kelley Farris/Moses Road and turn right (south) when the road bends left. Cemetery is on the left on that road after turning about 300 yards.
