ALLEN/CARPENTER CEMETERY, (Apple Hill Road), GILES COUNTY

 This map shows the GPS coordinates of the cemetery.

This Google Aerial view shows the cemetery just north of the bend in the road next to a house on the east side where some of the vicious dogs in Giles County reside.

This map shows the cemetery out Minnow Branch Road when in actuality this road is labeled Apple Hill Road at the junction of Ball Hollow and Minnow Branch Road. The Carvell Cemetery shown on this map  is actually on the Apple Hill Road.
