GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE CEMETERIES NOT LISTED BY CEMETERY


WEST, Nancy B. "Nannie" Anderson, dates unknown born about 1840 died prior to 12 Oct 1924, wife of John Walter West. She lived in Dist 7, Pulaski, of Giles County. Interred in Maplewood Cemetery - likely in an unmarked grave.

Nancy B. "Nannie" Anderson West
Birth: unknown
Death: unknown

Wife of John Walter West (1834-1924)
Birth & death dates unknown - no cemetery listing has been reported yet - This could be an unmarked grave.
Born about 1840 died prior to 1824 when her husband reportedly died as a widower.

Censuses show Nancy B. (?) West as deceased wife's name. Further investigation by Linda Munroe find a death certificate with her name as Nancy B. (Anderson) West
So far no gravestone has been reported in Maplewood Cemetery for either John Walter West or his wife. Either he is in an unmarked grave or it was missed by transcribers thus far.

Sources: Death cert. sent here by Linda Munroe. 21 Nov 2011. Transcribed and Posted here by 21 Nov 2011 by C. Wayne Austin
