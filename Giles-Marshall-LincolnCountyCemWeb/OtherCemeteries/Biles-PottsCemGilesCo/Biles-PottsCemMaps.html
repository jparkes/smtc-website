BILES-POTTS CEMETERY, (Prospect Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE aka Potts Cemetery (on maps)

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Biles-Potts Cemetery shown as the Potts Cemetery on the above maps.

This map shows the roads to take to get to the Biles-Potts Cemetery.
Location: Take Hwy 31 to Prospect Road and turn right in Elkton. Go about 3 miles and turn right (north) on Liberty Road. Follow that for 2 miles and the cemetery will be on your left very near the road.

This aerial view shows the cemetery on the south side of the road across the road from the Tucker Bend in the Elk River.
