MACKLIN/(MACLIN) (African American) CEMETERY, (Little Dry Creek Road), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Macklin grave.

From Campbellsville travel down Little Dry Creek Road about 4 miles and the cemetery is on the left (west) at the top of the hill about 50 to 75 yards from the road. Alternate location: On the Maclin/(Macklin) farm at Morristown Community: Little Dry Creek
Road, at the top of the hill on the east side of the road. One grave.
