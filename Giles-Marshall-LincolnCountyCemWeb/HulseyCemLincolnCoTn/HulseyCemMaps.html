HULSEY CEMETERY (Providence Rd), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hulsey Cemetery as N 35.17480 X W 86.51751. This shows a very straight access road to the cemetery which does not exist.

 Location: From Fayetteville go east on Hwy 64 to Hwy 50 and turn (left) north. Go less than 1/2 mile and turn east (right) on Providence Rd. then back north on Providence Rd. again. When the road starts to bend to the east go into the road into that neighborhood and go about 2/3 of a mile to the last house on the right who can direct you to this cemetery. It is further on north past the buildings and pond of that home and into the woods enough to make it a bit hard to spot.

This map shows better the small pond in relation to where the cemetery is located in the woods. In other words 30 yards northeast of the pond. Do not confuse this pond for the larger one south of it about 100 yards.
