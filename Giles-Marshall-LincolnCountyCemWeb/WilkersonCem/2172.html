WILKERSON CEMETERY, (Hwy 166) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The upright memorial of Mary Hughes, daughter of W.P. & R.V. Wilkerson.

WILKERSON, Aleth, Jan 1804 - 14 Feb 1858. 
Inscribed as: "IN Memory of ALETH WILKERSON, Born January 1804 and Departed this life February 14, 1858.". 
There is evidence here that Aleth is the wife of Allen Y. Wilkerson. Her memorial is beside his & her birth date is close.
