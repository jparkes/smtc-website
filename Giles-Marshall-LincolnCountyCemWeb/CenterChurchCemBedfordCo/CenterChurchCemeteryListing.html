The note no stone found in 2011 is largely a testimony of the declining condition of this cemetery. Many broken and declining monuments were found and others were fallen broken and in a serious state of decline. A few of the listing with that note may be known unmarked graves where some reporters were not careful to indicate that.

In the visit that day I was both surprised and shocked the the old historic church across the road was gone as I did not investigate. No sign of what happened to it. There is even a map on this site showing the building still there as late as the 2005 or after. No black charred area to indicate it burned so it was removed and where it went is not known by me. I have old photos of it for display here.
