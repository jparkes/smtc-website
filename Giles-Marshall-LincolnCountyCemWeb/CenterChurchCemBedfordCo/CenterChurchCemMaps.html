CENTER CHURCH CEMETERY, Center Church Road) BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Center Church Cemetery in Bedford County Tennessee.

 Location: From Shelbyville go south on Hwy 231 several miles to the Center Church Road. Turn left there and go about 1/2 mile and the cemetery is on your left after you round the curve and back from the road.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly back from the road. It occupies about all that area back to the tree line. The old Center Church is not there anymore. Its disposition I know nothing of except I know it did not burn down.
