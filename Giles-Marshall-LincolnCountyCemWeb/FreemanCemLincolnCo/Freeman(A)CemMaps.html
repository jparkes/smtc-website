FREEMAN (Abner) CEMETERY (Poor Grab-Chestnut Ridge Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
Shown as the Freeman Cemetery on USGS mapping. The GPS coordinates are corrects as far as I can tell.
From Fayetteville go north out Hwy 231 into Moore County and upon Chestnut Ridge. While traveling along Chestnut Ridge turn left on Poor Grab Chestnut Ridge Road and go back down the hill. When that road junctions with Pierce Road about a mile along the way, turn north (right) into Pierce Rd and go to the Freeman Cemetery on the right. With permission climb the hill to the top west of you and somewhat north to the cemetery with no formal headstones only fieldstones. I have not been here and cannot tell you that you will find this lost cemetery using these instructions.
