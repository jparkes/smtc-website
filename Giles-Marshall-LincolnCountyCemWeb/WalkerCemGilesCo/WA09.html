WALKER CEMETERY, Highway 129, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Tennie Walker Dabney

DABNEY, Tennie Walker, 17 Jan 1850 - 25 Feb 1926. "Mama".
Tennie Walker Dabney was the daughter of Henry Jefferson Walker and Sarah Stone; Wife of Samuel D. Dabney. Ref: Debbie Cromwell. Tennie was married to Samuel Day Dabney from Giles Co. and a Veteran of the Civil War. Pension #S8700 11th Cav. Tennie filed for Widow's penion. #W7426)
