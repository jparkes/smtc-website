Died in the performance of a faithful service on the morning of September 24, 1864.
The Seventh Kentucky Mounted Infantry. Forrest's Cavalry, Confederates States Army Engaged the enemy on this field and the following is a list of its dead whose remains repose near this stone.
CAPTAIN JOEL T. COCHRAN  - Company E
CAPTAIN DAVID L NOWLIN   - Company G
SERGEANT JACK WADDELL    - Company I
PRIVATE THOMAS HANDBERRY - Company I
PRIVATE THOMAS HATCHELL  - Company E
PRIVATE JOHN HANELINE    - Company E
PRIVATE JOHN OLIVER      - Company K
PRIVATE JOHN WILSON      - Company K
And an unknown Mississippian
    
ERECTED BY A COMRADE  - 1911
