Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and when you get to within 2 tenths of a mile back from Tarpley (Junction of Hwy 31 & Prospect to Tarpley Rd) look right (west) at the edge of the road but slightly down a bank at the wrought iron fence and monuments. Look for the Historical Marker depicting the battle.
