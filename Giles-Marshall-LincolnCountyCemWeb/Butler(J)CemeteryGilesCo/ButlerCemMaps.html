BUTLER (John) CEMETERY (Tarpley to Prospect Rd.) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the John J. Butler Cemetery.Â 

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and when you get to Tarpley turn west (or right) and Prospect Road. Go a few miles and cross the creek and look for a residential lane into an old house place. The cemetery is straight into this lane at the end of what is labeled Petty Lane, but you will not see public road signs. The cemetery is just this side of an old garage/barn and under the trees. It was lightly kept up when I was there.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the trees It is on the southeast side of a residential house at the end of a garden enclosed in a fence.
