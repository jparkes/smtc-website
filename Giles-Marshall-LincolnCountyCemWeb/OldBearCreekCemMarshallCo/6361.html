OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.


This photo shows this stone in the distance lying nearly flat on the ground near the livestock entrance where the gate is broken down. One can see the path where the livestock follow single file into and out of this cemetery. We are looking south and the photo was made with a camera to the left.

 PARTENHEIMER, J. F., 1834 - 18 Feb 1891
