OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

The Boxtomb on our right. Wm McGee Orr memorial.

An unknown stack of carved stones with no inscription further out, next toward us is the fallen stone of Mary King Orr, and then here in front is the box tomb of Wm Orr.

Another view of the Wm McGee Orr memorial.
