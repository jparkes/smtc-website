OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Our focus is on the stone broken top of a stone leaning against another in the far right edge just to the right and back from the James Orr memorial. It is for Elizabeth Orr his wife. I did not see the base of the stone.
ORR, Elizabeth, 23 Nov 1787 - 24 Feb 1863. 'Wife of James Orr." (Tombstone broken, we take the date info information from the book M.C.T.C."
