OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

MOORE, Elizabeth, died 1838. (Tombstone broken.) This little footstone has two sets of initials E.M. which may be for Elizabeth Moore and another below that I cannot read because of the debris. It is an old footstone of a style used between the years 1820 and 1850.
Photo, commentary and transcription by Wayne Austin, 26 Feb 2008.
