We notice from this view the Samuel Moore stone on our left center. Out to the right from that are several stones that mark graves. One in particular may be just a large outcrop stone left there from before this was a cemetery.


In this photo we see the memorials of Levina & Andrew Jackson & Thomas Moore. In the center bottom of the photo we see another fieldstone pointing upward. All this is separated by the muddy livestock path.

This is another fieldstone and without knowing where it is this is a bit hard to tell if this interment may have been related to anyone in this cemetery. It is close to the eastern edge of the cemetery.
