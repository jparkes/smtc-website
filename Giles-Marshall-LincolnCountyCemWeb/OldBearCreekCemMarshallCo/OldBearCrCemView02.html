View looking north in the upper area (western) of the cemetery. Stones are sparse up here but the ground is clear where the Cedar trees shade out the weeds.
This view was taken standing in the upper area of he cemetery looking southeast. We can see the edge of the broken fence and the open area where once a gate stood. The gate has fallen and now trampled into the ground.

This is the first view where one can see a large number of stones. We are looking east. This is a typical old cemetery with the graves scattered around, but all facing east with most of the stones grouped into family areas. This cemetery cover about 2 acres.
