MORROW (Daniel) CEMETERY, Hwy 166,  GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
 Maps that depict the location of this cemetery:
 Sugar Valley Farm     Map 2, 1,227 Acres


This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Morrow Family Cemetery in a grove of trees in a field on the old Luther Morrow farm, known, in 1996, as the Sugar Valley Farm. Take Hwy 64 W. then go north (right) on Highway 166, go more than 10 miles and pass thru Campbellsville, Giles County, Tennessee and the cemetery is past Campbellsville several more miles along Hwy 166 aka Mt Pleasant Road and about a mile further along Hwy 166 past pst the Hwy 245 turn off. This is a large 1,227 acre farm.


This aerial map shows the cemetery in a thicket west of a pond.

This road shows you another way to this cemetery but the gate may be locked and Brownlow creek may not be passable. The cemetery is beyond the first row of trees in the distance.
