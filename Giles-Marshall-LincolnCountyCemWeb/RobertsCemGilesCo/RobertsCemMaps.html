ROBERTS CEMETERY,  (Hwy 166, Greenfield Rd.) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Roberts Cemetery. 

Take Highway 166 South to Greenfield Road and turn left. The cemetery is on the left upon a bank between residential roads.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained and easily accessible from Greenfield Road.
