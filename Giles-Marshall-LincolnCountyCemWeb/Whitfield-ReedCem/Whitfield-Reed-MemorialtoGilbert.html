WHITFIELD-REED CEMETERY, (Reed Rd s. of Hwy 273) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Prospect United Methodist Church - Prospect Tennessee.
This is the three windows side by side with the middle one designating Father & Mother the two on either side being for Carey and Elizabeth Gilbert This church is a couple of miles northwest of Whitfield-Reed Cemetery in Prospect Tennessee.

This is a close up of the stain glass windows in the Church. The graves of Carey and Elizabeth Reed Gilbert are here at Whitfield Cemetery in Giles Co. Tn.
Stained Glass inscribed as:
CAREY GILBERT,  1818 - 1893.
ELIZABETH GILBERT, 1822 - 1873.
