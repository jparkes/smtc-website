SANDLIN CEMETERY (Camargo, Old Camargo Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sandlin Cemetery as N 35.09413 X W -86.62946.

Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 and turn right on Ardmore Hwy (Hwy 110). Go thru Skinem and on to Camargo Rd and turn right. Follow that for about 1 1/2 miles and turn right Old Camargo Rd and almost immediately turn left on Sandlin Cemetery Road and that dead ends next to the cemetery on your right after the road bends back (left) west.

With this map we see an approximate outline of the premises of the Sandlin Cemetery.
