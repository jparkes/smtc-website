This road map shows the cemetery on the north side of Hwy 64 about half way between Pulaski and Interstate 65. This is about 7 miles from Pulaski. As of today it is up under the trees inside a small rock wall about 150 feet from the highway.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the trees. It is in front of a house. The owners have posted keep out signs.
