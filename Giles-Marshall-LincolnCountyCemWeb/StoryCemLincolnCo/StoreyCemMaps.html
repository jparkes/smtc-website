STOREY CEMETERY, (Bee Springs Road, near Giles Co Line) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Storey Cemetery.

 Location: From Fayetteville go around the eastern bypass to Hwy 64. Turn right on Hwy 64 toward Winchester. Go a couple of miles and turn left (north) on the Marrs Road and circle around about 400 yards to the third house on the left. This is the first house past Old Mulberry Road junction on the right. ) With permission go up the driveway to the end and over the fence. The Gate is at the end of the concrete driveway. You are sitting beside another building behind the house and the gate is locked so don't even go here with advance notice to the owner. 

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery under the trees but behind a secondary building behind the main house. I cannot over emphasize permission, because this cemetery is in someone's back yard and is being maintained as part of the premises.
