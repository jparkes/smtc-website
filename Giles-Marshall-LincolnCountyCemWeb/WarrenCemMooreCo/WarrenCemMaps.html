WARREN CEMETERY, (on Bartlett Hollow near Hwy 231), MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Warren Cemetery GPS location. It is on the Bartlett Hollow Road just inside the drainage basin for the West Fork of Mulberry Creek. 

Location: From Shelbyville go south on Hwy 231 several miles and turn left on Bartlett Hollow Road and go about about 3/4 of a mile and the road into the cemetery is on the right in front of a house and the caretaker of the cemetery. The cemetery is on that almost field road about 100 yards on the left. I felt for an elderly lady that met me there . She apologized for the condition of the cemetery, and said her husband had passed away, and she did not have the health anymore to maintain it. If you can get by there and do some grounds maintenance please do. The fence is falling down and the grass is waist high now. Better yet, maybe you can donate to a fund to the upkeep of this cemetery.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery marked with a red check in a clearing among the trees. On this map the road to this cemetery is covered in trees, but generally follows the pattern of the trees coming southwest from the road to the west side of the cemetery. All this is co-mingled with farm assets so notification of neighbors of your intentions is a good idea before visiting.
