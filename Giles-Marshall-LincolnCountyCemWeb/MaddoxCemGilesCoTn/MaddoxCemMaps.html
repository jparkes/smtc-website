MADDOX-JORDAN CEMETERY (Lofton Hall Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Maddox-Jordan Cemetery. The Cemetery is found at GPS N 35.03474 X W -86.85023. However, I do not believe this is a correct plotting though this focal point will not be hundreds of yards in error.

From Ardmore go (north) out Ardmore Ridge Rd to Lewter Hollow Rd and turn right. Go about 1/4 mile and the cemetery is in the northeast corner (but not down next to the road) of the junction of Lofton Hall Rd and Lewter Hollow Rd on the property of Mr. Edge. Later I went back and found it.
 Directions in 1985: Highway 31S to Elkton. Off Ardmore Ridge Road, between Sinking Creek and railroad on old Currin place, back of house. Map shows plotting in front of current house because the house that was there is fallen and in a heap of rubble under brush.

Mapping from ACME Mapper 2.1 & MS Streets & Trips 2004. Modified and added here 12 Jan 2013, modified for a visit which occurred 17 Mar 2016 by C. Wayne Austin.
