WAGGONER CEMETERY, (Gimlet Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Wagoner aka McGee-Waggoner Cemetery GPS location.

Location: From Fayetteville take the Bypass to Hwy 64 and and go east toward Chattanooga. After a mile or so turn (left) north on the Lynchburg Hwy (Hwy 55) and go about 3 and 1/2 miles to the Booneville Road and turn left (or north). Go 1 mile and turn left or northwest on Gimlet Road. Go about 2 and 1/2 miles to the cemetery on your right inside a dense Privey Hedge thicket. The brush is so thick it is impossible to see all the cemetery assets in that environment.

This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next to Gimlet Road and on the other side might be a dog (vicious) breeding operation which that part is private but makes it very annoying to visit this cemetery when you wonder if you are in danger of a dog attack.
