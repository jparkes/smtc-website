OAK GROVE CEMETERY, (Oak Grove Community, near Hwy 11) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Oak Grove (African American) Cemetery as 35.09272 X -87.11108. This was the old Baptist Church Cemetery until that relocated or disbanded.

 Location: Go south on Minor Hill Hwy to Oak Grove Road and turn left. Follow that until it dead ends into Cowhorne Hollow Rd. Turn left and go abt 600 yards Past the Oak Grove white Cemetery and on another 1/2 mile to the Cemetery on the right under the trees.
