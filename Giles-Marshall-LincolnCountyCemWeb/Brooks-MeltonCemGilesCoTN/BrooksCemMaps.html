BROOKS-MELTON CEMETERY,  Old Stage Coach Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Brooks-Melton Cemetery. @ N 35.11803 X W -86.89935 in a pasture under a cedar thicket.

 Location: Go south on Hwy 31 14+ miles to Hardy Road and turn left. Climb up the hill and dead end into Bunker Hill/Old Stage Coach Road, and turn right. Follow that for a about 1 1/2 miles passing King road on your right. Go to the next road turning north (left) into that and crossing the bridge. Follow that Rd until it bends back to the right. With permission park there. Crawl under or over the fences without getting shocked or damaging the fences or disturbing the livestock. From there the cemetery is north of you up the slope further beyond a large treed lot. It is located in a small cedar and hackberry thicket under a fallen rock wall as seen in the below photo.
