This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Old Salem Cemetery as N 35.57958 X W -86.35728.

Location: From Shelbyville go north on Hwy 231 about 7 1/2 miles and turn right (east) on Webb Rd (Hwy 82) going toward Bell Buckle. Go into Bell Buckle and turn right (south) on Hwy 269 (Bell Buckle to Wartrace Rd). Follow 269 for something less than a mile and the cemetery will be on your left above the road. The parking/entrance is in north area of cemetery.
