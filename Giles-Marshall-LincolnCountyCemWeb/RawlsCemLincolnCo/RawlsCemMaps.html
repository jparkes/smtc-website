RAWLS CEMETERY, Red Hill Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Rawls Cemetery as N 35.10555 X W -86.67176.

Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 and turn right on Molino Rd. (before the Hwy 110 turn off also to the right). Follow Molino Rd more than 5 miles and turn south (left) on Red Hill Road. Go a few hundred yards and turn (with permission/notification) right (west) into the driveway of two homes and the cemetery is on the left side of the driveway of the home on the right under the trees on a rise.

There is a new home being built just north of the cemetery and by now is completed, but the same roads as shown above still apply. Mr Boxer Bulldog will greet you from the house on the left with lots of noisy barking and rushing toward you, but it my experience was, he really doesn't want trouble in the end as long as you stay away from his home.
