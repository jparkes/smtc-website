ABERNATHY-PITTARD CEMETERY, Chicken Creek Rd, west side, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Abernathy-Pittard Cemetery as N 35.17779 X W 87.10180. 

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn west (right) on the 64 bypass and then back left (south) on Chicken Creek Road. Pass Richardson Road on the right and Short Road on the left. Go another 1/2 mile and the cemetery is behind and NW of the Waldon family home which used to be the Kennedy family farm. Mr. Waldon's wife inherited the place from her mother who was a Kennedy and I think somewhere back in the line were the Abernathys'. Mrs. Waldon said that the Pittards' were friends of the Abernathy's in the community. I suspect if one follows the information trail there is a marriage somewhere that helped seal a family connection. This is the graves of the Charles Grandison Pittard b. 1809 and the William D. Abernathy b. 1800 families. To get there from standing at the Waldon home looking at the home one goes left across the creek; to the left of the barn, and up the slope. Cross over a tree line (thru an opening going northwest). Then follow a small narrow cleared area back to the cemetery located to the right 50 feet of a giant Pin Oak tree. This is northwest of the home about 400 yards. This cleared area is mowed by Mr. Waldon. If he ever gets to the point he is unable to do that then all the vegetation will change.
