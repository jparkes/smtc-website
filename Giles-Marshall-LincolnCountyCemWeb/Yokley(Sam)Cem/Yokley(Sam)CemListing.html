YOKLEY (Samuel) CEMETERY, (Locke Road, Cambpellsville) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mapping the location     Area photos of the cemetery

YOKLEY, Samuel, 20 Dec 1819 - 11 May 1901.
YOKLEY, Mary Hannah, 10 Apr 1822 - 1 Jun 1888
YOKLEY, Mary M. Yokley, wife of Samuel Yokley 10 Apr 1822 - 1 Jun 1880 (Probably a duplicate stone for Mary M. Hannah Yokley, but maybe a different death date; It is unknown which is correct. 1880 Census may tell us. This is not a second marriage by Samuel.)
SMITH, infant of W.C. & S.C. Smith, 18 Apr 1878 - 14 Jul 1878 (same stone; grandchild of Sam & Mary Yokley.)
SMITH, infant of W.C. & S.C. Smith, 10 July 1879. (Only Date) (same stone; grandchild of Sam & Mary Yokley.)
CAROTHERS, Mary Laura, 29 Mar 1847 - 18 Oct 1872 (daughter of Samuel & Mary Hannah Yokley.)
CROSSNOE, J. Thomas, died 1903, (husband of Alice Yokley; Alice was the daughter of Samuel & Mary Hannah Yokley.) A fieldstone marker existed at one time. It is thought  he was buried on the family plot during 1903.)
