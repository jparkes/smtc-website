MCOLLUM-BUCHANAN CEMETERY, Round Square Road, Howell, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the McCollum-Buchanan-Martin Cemetery as N 35.22863 X W -86.62188.

Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431 a few miles and turn left at Howell onto Shields Road. Follow that 1/3 mile to Brown Teal Road and turn right then back left on Round Square Road. When Round Square Road starts to bend back right or north look to your left for the field/farm road which doubles as a road back to the cemetery. At the end look to your right at the cemetery under the grove of trees behind the barn 100 yards away.Â  With permission when at the end of the farm road go thru the 2 iron cattle gates on your right while still facing west, but to the left (west) of the barn. Be sure to close all gates behind you. Finally cross into the iron gate leading into the cemetery. The cemetery entrance gate is at the northwest corner of the cemetery.

This cemetery is well fenced but grown up in privet hedge, small & giant trees and other growth. Some of the Moore monuments are near the entrance over from the gate. The McCollum monuments are in the south east quad of the cemetery and the Buchanan progenitor Mausoleum is in the center with the balance of the stones in the western area spanning along the north edge of the cemetery.
