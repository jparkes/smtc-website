CAMPBELL (H.C.) CEMETERY, (Campbellsville, west side) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE 
The Campbell-Rittenberry Cemetery Signage aka HC Capmbell Cemetery on the front gate decorated with the Christmas Wreath.

The Campbell-Rittenberry originally was called the Hamilton Crockett Campbell Cemetery after the eldest progenitor here. That is the name of this cemetery in the Giles County Historical Society Book. Someone later decided to name it after the most prevalent surnames interred in this cemetery.

Entrance into the cemetery marked with decorative hand made stones showing someone's special thoughts and care of this cemetery and its ancestral inhabitants. In the background we see the bases of the giant Grain Silos that so easily mark the location of this burial ground.
