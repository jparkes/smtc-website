There are two other historic notes I failed to mention on the stones in Yokley Cemetery. There is a Franklin A., son of Andrew & Delia Yokley who died in 1852 at the age of 21. He professed religion at the Popular Grove Cumberland Presbyterian Church. Well to me (and it has been my experience) this means this cemetery was probably founded by the Cumberland Presbyterians and that there as a church nearby with that name. Now try to find that bit of history in the cemetery book. I doubt if they even read it on the stone. Photo #1859. The church may have sat down where that house is now. Have any knowledge of that? Mary Bob will love translating that long poet inscription. Challenge now is to find some history on that old church.
 
Another note on a stone was in the cemetery book. A Civil War soldier David G. Alexander was wounded at the battle of Resaca, Ga. 15 May 1864 and died 6 Jun 1864. Photo #1710.
