POWELL CEMETERY, Little Texas Rd. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Powell Cemetery GPS location as N 35.07910, W -87.20165.

 Location: From Minot Hill (Hwy 11) take Puncheon Branch Road west and go about 2 miles and turn right (west on Barnett Rd. Go about 1/2 mile and turn on Peach Road and follow that for about 1/2 mile and turn right on left on Shannon Road and go about 1/4 mile and turn right on Little Texas Road (not sure of the name) and the cemetery is in the bend of that road 1/2 mile further on on the left.
