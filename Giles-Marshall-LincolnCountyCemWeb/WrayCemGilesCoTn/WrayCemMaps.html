WRAY CEMETERY, (Kelly Creek SE) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map from Google shows the GPS coordinates of the Wray Cemetery as found by David Walker N 35.082755 X W -86.855182
From Elkton in Giles County take Baugh Road which is one of the east/west streets in the Elkton village and go east. Travel for about 3 miles crossing over I-65 and turn north on Eagin Cemetery Rd aka Adams Road. Follow that for about 6 tenths of a mile and and there will be a fork in the road. Take the left fork which is Eagin Cemetery Road (the right fork is Adams Rd) and go about 3 tenths of a mile to the Railroad tracks. Cross over the tracks and stop. With permission follow the field road to the left thru the gate and on out into the open field to finish the walk to the distant three studded thicket of Wray Cemetery. The cemetery is inside that oblong thicket. The location description given in the book (195) is this: Location: Hwy 31S, left at Elkton School, Baugh Station to Kelly Creek, on Rudus Evans' farm, but that version is historical and not so useful.

 This aerial view shows the Cemetery in a green (tree) thicket out in a large field just SE of the Elk River and north of the snaky Kellys Creek. I have drawn a yellow graphic path for you to follow. However, David Walker later informed me the best way to walk to the cemetery is thus:  Leaving from Eagan Cemetery Road, cross the railroad tracks and walk down the southerly path  (face left) parallel to the tracks, and over/through the gate; then on to the tree grove containing Wray Cemetery.
