GEORGE aka Amos SMALL CEMETERY, Hwy 231, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

The center of the map marks the location of the George Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. the GPS coordinates placement are N 35.26187 X W -86.56647.


 To get to this cemetery go up Hwy 231 from Fayetteville. Just after you pass Cold Springs Road the cemetery is upon the hilltop behind and above the 2nd house on the left. If you turn on Cold Springs road the couple living in the second house on right Road has a gate into the cemetery but they do not own the property around the cemetery. They are friendly toward visitors. (this is actually the third driveway. The first driveway does not have a house, but appears to be some sort of business.
