MACLIN-AYMETT CEMETERY (Hwy 64 East) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Maclin-Aymett Cemetery.Â 

Go east on Hwy 64 toward Fayetteville. Turn right on one of the roads that was old Hwy 64 toward Aymett Town (Community). After traveling 400 years on this road the cemetery will be on the left back up into field 50 yards before you reach a house on the left. The road to the cemetery is actually a mowed area where the road used to be. The blue arrow above points to a cemetery that has no stones. It is fenced separately and mowed but has no visible stones.

This aerial map shows the Maclin-Aymett Ccemetery just before you reach the first house on the left. I believe another cemetery, the Aymett Cemetery to be behind that house on the left 100 yards east of this cemetery, but I have not been there. I do think it is in this area sandwiched between Old Hwy 64 and the new Highway 64.
