WARREN CEMETERY (Providence Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Warren Cemetery GPS location as  N 35.17452 X W -86.47258.  

Location: Take Highway 64 east out of Fayetteville and turn north on Hwy 50 toward Lynchburg and go 1/4 mile and turn back right on Providence Rd. Follow that for several miles and the cemetery is near the road on the right behind a security fence.
 This is a large Cemetery, under a clump of brushy trees. It is behind a tall security style fence of a large food consortium with keep out signs. The cemetery is next to the road, but no entrance is provided or allowed across the fence except thru locked gates and warning signs. There are hazardous materials operation warnings of processes in progress. Understandable. I am all for business progress but this is overboard as far as the cemetery being off limits is concerned. The visitor can walk to within 5 feet of the cemetery and not go 50 more feet to visit it. Since this security issue probably involves food safety I advise anyone wanting to visit this cemetery not to break any laws or rules here. Believe it is Frito Lay Company who are owners here or one of the big chip companies.
