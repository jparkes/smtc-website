This topographical map shows the various hills and streams and homes and barns and cemeteries in the Stella Community about 1957. Small black squares are homes.

Stella Community Map showing the Valley traversed by the East Fork of Shoals Creek and the various historical assets many still in place. The symbols depicting a field road on the left traversing north and south was a busy road at one time when a large family the Brownlows' lived down it.Â It is known today as the T. Dawes Road. The Samuel Kerr (d. 1881) home also the home of his son Rev. Addison M. Kerr (d. 1872) is shown just after one turns onto the H. Smith Road. All these homes are private facilities today.
