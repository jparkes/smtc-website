STELLA CEMETERY (aka Pleasant Hill), Stella, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Stella Cemetery GPS location.
Location: From Pulaski take the highway 64 bypass turn south on Hwy 166 and go several miles and turn west on Stella Road. Go about 6 miles and the cemetery will be in Stella on your left upon the slope.

This Road Map shows the Stella Cemetery and several other cemeteries in that area. The Pleasant Hill Cemetery is African-American.
Location: Hwy 64 bypass left (south) into Bethel Road, right onto Stella Road. High upon slope on left next to historic Stella Presbyterian Church.
