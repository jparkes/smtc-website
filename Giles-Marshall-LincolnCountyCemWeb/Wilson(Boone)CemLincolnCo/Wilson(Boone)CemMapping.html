WILSON (Boone) CEMETERY, Parker Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Boone Wilson Cemetery as N 35.21034 X W -86.76110.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 past Boonshill and turn north or right on Parker Rd. Go all the way to the end of the road (abt 1/2 mile) and with permission turn left at the last house and drive by the house and out into the field road. Follow that and when the road comes out into an open field turn sharp right and follow the tree line on your right (south). Go about 200 yards to the cemetery which juts northward out into the field and has a noticeable rock wall and several cedar trees inside. There will be private property and private assets all around so leave that alone and don't touch. Camera are on this premises. Not sure they were active, but by all means do not touch them.
