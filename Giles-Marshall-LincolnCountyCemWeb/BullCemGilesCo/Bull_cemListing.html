This cemetery was visited and the memorials photographed by Wayne Austin 28 Sep 2010. The listing was then transcribed & edited by Mary Bob McClain. Preliminary typing by Faye Bradford. Formatted and added here 16 Mar 2011 by Wayne Austin. This cemetery also listed on page 64, Giles County Cemetery book by the Giles County Historical Society. 1989. This book continues to be an important source of cemetery and genealogy information for Giles County Tennessee because of the errors and incompleteness of cemetery information on the internet.
