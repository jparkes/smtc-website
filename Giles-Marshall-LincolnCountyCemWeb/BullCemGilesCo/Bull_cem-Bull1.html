Charles Ordway Bull and he was the son of Ursula and Adrian Davenport Bull. He married Clarissa Lambeth Nov 13, 1871, Lester Station, Tennessee. All the Bull's buried in the Bull Cemetery are affiliated with the Ohio Bull family who are buried in Greene County, Ohio. Adrian and Richard Bull were someone Great Great Grandfather's brothers, he died on the way to Texas in the Oklahoma Territory, This Bull family came from Wales to Pennsylvania and then settle in Ohio.
