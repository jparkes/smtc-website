BULL FAMILY CEMETERY (Elkton) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Here on this map we see the GPS of 35.05084 X W -86.88949 of the Bull Family Cemetery.

This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Bull Family Cemetery.

Location from Pulaski: Take highway 31 south to Elkton and turn east on Main Street and after one block turn back south (right) on Mulberry St. The cemetery is about one block on top of the hill in front of a residence on the left. You will see the big monuments.

This aerial view shows the cemetery marked with a red check on the east side of Mulberry St.
