This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Old Buntley Cemetery as N 35.24183 X W 86.53817.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 and turn right on Clarks Mill Rd and when that road dead ends into Mimosa Rd take that north (left). Continue on Mimosa Rd to a driveway just back from Paint Hollow Rd that goes to the south up a hill and curves left to a new home. You must negotiate with the owner for a visit behind (east of their home) in a grove of trees. From the looks of mapping the cemetery it is under the trees, but in a part of the yard kept under some kind of maintenance.
