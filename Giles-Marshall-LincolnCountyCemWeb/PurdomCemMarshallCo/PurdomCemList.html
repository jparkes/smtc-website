PURDOM CEMETERY, (New Lake Road North side), MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Mapping the Location


COLLINS, Elizabeth, 16 Mar 1827 -5  Sep 1898*
PURDOM, Infant of W. H. & Paralee Purdom (no dates; only grave marked with an inscription.)
PURDOM, Washington Haywood, 26 Jan 1826 - 28 May 1900*
PURDOM, Paralee (Collins), 10 Apr 1831 - 2 Mar 1894, Married 6 Feb 1850, wife of Washington Haywood Purdom*

Also buried in this Cemetery are members of the Bearden Family and other members of Purdom and Collins family. There are no marked headstones for them here.*

*Information from a family Bible (1979) of E. A. Holly of Lewisburg, TN.
 

Sourced  from the book Marshall County Tennessee Cemeteries book Copied Dec 4, 1979 by Ralph D. Whitesell, Helen and Tim Marsh, Added here 2 Jul 2014.
