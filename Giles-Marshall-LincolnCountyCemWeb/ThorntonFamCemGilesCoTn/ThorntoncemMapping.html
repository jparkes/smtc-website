THORNTON CEMETERY (Ardmore Hwy 31) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Thornton Family Cemetery as N 35.00023 X W -86.86266 as calculated by C Wayne Austin using ACME Mapper 2.0

 Location: From downtown Ardmore go west on old Highway 31 toward Pulaski for about a mile and turn into the driveway of the nursing rehab home. Go to the back parking lot. As you approach the back parking lot look to your right and you will see the plank fence surrounding the cemetery is located. It will be hidden somewhat by the parked cars and the stones are small and some broken.

This aerial view points toward the cemetery on the right of the road. It is unseen from this image, but is just past the nursing facility on the right of the backing parking lot.
