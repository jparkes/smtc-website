MT. MORIAH CEMETERY (Mt Moriah or Big Dry Creek Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

PILLOW, Roy M. (Mitchell), 17 Oct 1900 - 25 Dec 1972. (s/o late Thomas & Sam Ella Cashion Pillow; h/o Mable Englett Pillow.) Area A 1789

 Obituary, The Pulaski Citizen
ROY MITCHELL PILLOW
[Roy Mitchell Pillow, 72, Pulaski, died Monday, December 25, 1972 at Beverly Manor Nursing Home, Nashville. Funeral services were held at 10:30 a.m. Wed., Dec. 27 at Bennett-May Chapel with burial in the Mt. Moriah Cemetery. The Rev. Mack Pinkelton officiated. #A native of Giles county, he was the son of the late Thomas and Sam Ella Cashion Pillow. He is survived by a daughter, Mrs. Robert A. Durham of Nashville; a son, Roy Thomas Pillow, Nashville; 2 sisters, Mrs. Ira Englett and Mrs. Joe Tidwell of Pulaski; and 3 grandchildren.]
