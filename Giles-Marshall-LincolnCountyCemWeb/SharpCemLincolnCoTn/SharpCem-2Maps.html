SHARP CEMETERY (Hwy 231 N.) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sharp Cemetery as N 35.20241 X W 86.56470. This shows the field road to the cemetery which meanders from northeast back to south and does not go directly beside the cemetery which can be seen here marked with a white check.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 past Clarks Mill Rd on right and further on past Warden Rd on left. Just after Warden Rd look to your right and you will see the gate thru which takes one to the pasture and also to the cemetery on your right. With permission, and a key for the gate, go into the field road and follow the road northeast then back south to near the cemetery in a thicket. There are several thickets in that field but the cemetery is located inside the largest one today.
