KINCAID CEMETERY, BRICK CHURCH, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Nashville take I-65 South. Take Exit 22 on Hwy 31 Alt and go toward Pulaski 1.3 miles (southwest) to Buford Station Road and turn right. Go about 1/4 mile and turn right again on Hart Leonard Road. Go 1.7 miles on that road and turn left onto Jett Road and go to the top of the hill and cemetery can be seen on the right in the brush across a private road. Go to the next road and turn into that and turn back immediately right again into a private field road that runs parallel to Jett Road going east/west. Go 300 yards on the field road and the cemetery is in the grove on your left 20 yards and can be seen unless it is grown up again. See the Red Check on the map above.
