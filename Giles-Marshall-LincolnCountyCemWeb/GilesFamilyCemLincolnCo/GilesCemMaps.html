This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Giles Cemetery as N 35.23765 X W -86.57635.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 and turn left (west) on Giles Hollow Road. Go about 7 tenths of a mile and there is a big open field on your right that has a gate into it. The Cemetery is in the far Northwest corner of that big field about 300 yards from the road behind a new well built rock wall.
