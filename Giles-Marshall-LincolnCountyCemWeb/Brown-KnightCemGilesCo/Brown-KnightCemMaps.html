BROWN/KNIGHT CEMETERY, Hanna Ward Bridge Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Brown-Knight Cemetery on Hanna Ward Bridge Road - N 35.02912 X W 86.95309

 Location: From Pulaski take Highway 31S. Go all the way to Elkton (abt 15 miles) then right on Hwy 273 Prospect Road, then left into Poplar Hill Road then left into Hanna Ward Bridge Rd. Cemetery on west side of Hanna Ward Bridge Rd under a Black Walnut Tree, across but somewhat north from Mabron Campbell's home.
