MARSH-DAVIS CEMETERY, (Globe Rd. next to I-65) MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Marsh-Davis Cemetery.Â 

From Lewisburg go west on Hwy 373 to just before I-65 turn south (left) on Globe Road and go a just over a mile and the cemetery is east (left)on a hill about 1/4 mile with a well maintained road to visit. Turn into the road that doubles as a residential road and follow that due east until the road goes up a hill and the cemetery is just ahead of you on the right. The road to the cemetery is open but you may need permission. You will pass very close to a house on what appears to be a private road.
