KELSO CEMETERY, Kelso, Hwy 64W, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Kelso Cemetery as N 35.12825 X W -86.47007.

 Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 to Kelso and turn right on Teal Hollow Road. At the stop sign turn right on (Old) Fayetteville Rd and this large sprawling cemetery spans on your right.
