PLEASANT HILL CEMETERY,(Ardmore) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Pleasant Hill Cemetery location a couple of miles west from I-65 at Ardmore Tn. It is at GPS location 35.011 North X 86.902 West.

Location: Highway 31 south from Pulaski to Elkton; then continue traveling south until just before junction of I-65 and Hwy 31. Turn right (west to south) onto Pleasant Hill road. Go two miles and the cemetery is behind the Pleasant Hill Methodist Church on the right.

This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides behind the (dark roof seen here) of the Pleasant Hills United Methodist Church. It is fenced and well kept. Do not go there with any intentions except honorable ones.
