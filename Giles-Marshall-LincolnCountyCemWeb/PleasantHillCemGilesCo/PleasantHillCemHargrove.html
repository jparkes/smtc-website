PLEASANT HILL CEMETERY,(Ardmore) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Athens (AL) News Courier, December 12, 1974, p. 3

Funeral services for Willis Coleman Hargrove, 59, rt. 2, Prospect, Tenn, will be today, 2 p.m. at Pleasant Hill Methodist Church, Rev. Mitchell Sawyer, Rev. Thomas Davis, and Rev. Norman Coke will officiate. Burial will be in Pleasant Hill Cemetery, McConnell Funeral home, directing.

Mr. Hargrove died Tuesday at Baugh-Wiley-Smith Hospital in Decatur.

Survivors include his wife, Mrs. Omega W. Hargrove, Prospect; one daughter, Mrs. Judy Faulkner Prospect; one son, Norris Hargrove, Prospect; two brothers, Calvin Hargrove, Pulaski, Tenn, Isom Hargrove, Toney; four sisters, Mrs. Clara King, prospect, Mrs. Susie Clem, Elkmont, Mrs. Arie Woodfin, Elkmont, Mrs. Emmie Hargrove, Ardmore.
