Emmie Mae Hargrove - Obituary

Emmie Mae Hargrove, 92, of Route 1, Ardmore, TN, died September 24, 1988, in the Huntsville Hospital. Funeral services were held September 26, at 2:00 p.m. in the Taylor Funeral Home chapel with Rev. Mike O'Neal officiating. Burial was in the Pleasant Hill Cemetery with Taylor Funeral Home in charge. A native of Alabama, Mrs. Hargrove was the daughter of the late Tom and Mollie Robinson Hargrove. She was a member of Pleasant Hill Methodist Church. Survivors include one son, Calvin Hargrove of Ardmore; one brother, Isom Hargrove of Harvest; one sister, Clara King of Prospect; five grandchildren and six great-grandchildren.
