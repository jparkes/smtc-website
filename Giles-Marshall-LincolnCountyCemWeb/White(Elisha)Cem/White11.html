WHITE (ELISHA) CEMETERY, WACO, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE, Waco to Yokley Rd.

CHRISTY, J. H. 18 Nov 1916 - 13 Jul 1987. Obviously a sportsman. 
This one memorial cemetery is enclosed in a separate small area in a wooden fence on the south side and adjacent to the White Cemetery. It was grown up in weeds, but I believe there were no other graves.
