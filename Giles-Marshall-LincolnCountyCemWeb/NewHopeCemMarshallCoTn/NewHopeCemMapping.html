NEW HOPE CEMETERY, Ostella Rd, Cornersville, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the center of the New Hope Cemetery, as I have plotted.Â 

Directions from Lewisburg. Go southwest on Hwy 31 Alt/akaHwy11 past Cornersville to Ostella Road and turn left. Follow Ostella Road for over a mile and after the road bends back south (right) 90 degrees look to your left at the cemetery next to the road. Turn left into the cemetery road on the south west corner of the cemetery.

This aerial map shows the general borders of the New Hope cemetery. I have divided it into three sections. Section A is very old with some new graves. Section B is the generally developed area of recent times with Section C being populated with new graves mostly from after year 2000.
