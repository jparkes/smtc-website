YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Robert E. Lee Yokley, 25 Dec 1870 - 10 Jn 1904. Son of Jacob & Almonza A. Yokley. Stone on right - fallen from its base. The stone on the left is toppled on its face and as of now is unknown. The stone is similar in style to Sophronia A & Sarah E. Yokley's monument. All these grave lots are for Yokley kin. It is believed to also be a sister. Photo by Wayne Austin. 21 Dec 2009.
