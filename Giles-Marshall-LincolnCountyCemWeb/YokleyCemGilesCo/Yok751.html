YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Almanza A. (Ann) Yokley, 18 Feb 1829 - 16 Jan 1899, wife of Jacob Yokley, (daughter of John Y. & Matilda Sharp Alexander Abernathy.) This stone along with her husband's monument are both tumbled to together in a pile in a manner threatening to grind the inscription away. Since all of the bases are level it appears these monuments were knocked down by cattle or a very violet storm, or perhaps vandals. Photo by Wayne Austin. 21 Dec 2009.
