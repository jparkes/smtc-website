YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Jacob Yokley, 12 Jul 1822 - 5 Jan 1910, (son of Andrew & Delia Morris Yokley.) This stone along with his wife Almanza Yokley's monument are both tumbled to together in a pile in a manner threatening to damage the inscription. Since all of the bases are level it appears these monuments were knocked down by cattle or a very violet storm, or perhaps vandals. Photo by Wayne Austin. 21 Dec 2009.
