YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Matilda S. Daughter of Silas & Sarah Alexander & Consort of John Y. Abernathy - The stone next right of the stone on our left end of the row with the pointed top is our subject memorial.

"In memory of Matilda S. Daughter of Silas & Sarah Alexander & Consort of John Y. Abernathy born July 18th 1806 died July 29th 1856 age 50 years & 11 days. Blessed are the dead which die in the Lord" Photo by Wayne Austin. 21 Dec 2009.

"In memory of Matilda S. Daughter of Silas & Sarah Alexander & Consort of John Y. Abernathy born July 18th 1806 died July 29th 1856 age 50 years & 11 days Blessed are the dead which die in the Lord."

ABERNATHY, Matilda S. (Sharp), 18 Jul 1806 - 29 Jul 1856, wife of John Y. Abernathy, daughter of Silas & Sarah (Reese) Alexander.
Photos & inscription translated by Wayne Austin 21 Dec 2009, except the parenthetical reference.
