YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Vick Family lots are under the Hedge Shrub Bushes. Here we see the head & footstones for John H. Vick & his wife Annie Jane Barlar and headstones for three of their sons Kenneth on the south of their dual headstone and Johnnie & Charley (our subject here) on our right (north) side of their parents stone. Several other unmarked fieldstones show graves in line with these and immediately to the north down the north/south row. All this is in the southwest corner of the cemetery.
