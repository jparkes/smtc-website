YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

"SACRED to the memory of Martha Abernathy who was born Jan. 23. 1803. and Departed this life Dec.r 30th 1844. My flesh shall slumber in the ground Tell the last lingering trump shall sound. Then burst the barer? with sweet surprise And in my savionr in age rise."
