YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE.

This fieldstone marked grave with a head and footstone is likely for an infant child. It is located in the Vick area of the graveyard, about 15 feet northeast of the head stone of John H & Annie Jane Vick.
Vick family memorials under the tall shrub bushes out next to the fence. Here we also see the fieldstones that line the same row adjacent to the Vick memorials as we are looking south from over next to the gate. We will track them individually down the row going south down the north/south row.

This rough cut (2nd in our row) fieldstone is hand inscribed with the lettering W. A. (or J) Vick . Findagrave.com says it is Wesley J. Vick b. 1821.
