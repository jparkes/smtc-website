YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Footstone for E.M. Morrow. Photo by Terina Boyd 10 Oct 2009.

E.M. Morrow. 
Someone has left the carcass of a deceased Cow in this cemetery. The vultures paid their visit and were roosting on the stones as they fed on the carcass leaving these stones in a mess and potentially medically harmful to humans. By the time I visited there in Dec to help Terina with the photography the vultures had left and this stone was washed clean by rainwater, but much of the carcass was still there.
