YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Vick Family lots are mostly under the Hedge Shrub trees/Bushes but span back to the north (our right) which includes graves marked with fieldstones. Here we will find the graves, head & footstones for John H. Vick & his wife Annie Jane Barlar and headstones, for three of their sons Kenneth (our subject here) on the south of their dual but fallen headstone, and also Johnnie & Charley on our right (north) side of their parents stone. Several other unmarked fieldstones show graves in line with these and immediately to the north down the north/south row. All this is in the southwest corner of the cemetery.Â
