YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Robert C., son of John Y. & Matilda S. Abernathy
The little small stone with the little round top broken off. The stone is broken at the bottom too. It is propped against its base next to the big stone on the right.

"SACRED to the memory of Robert C., son of John Y. & Matilda S. Abernathy born Aug 29 died Oct 4, 1848."
