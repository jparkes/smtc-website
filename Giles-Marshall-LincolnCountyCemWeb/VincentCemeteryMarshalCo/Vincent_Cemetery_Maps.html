VINCENT CEMETERY, Near MOORESVILLE, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
The Cemetery is just west of Interstate 65.

From I-65 take Exit 32 toward Mooresville 2 tenths of a mile and turn right (North) onto Gupton Road. Go about 1 1/4 mile north on Gupton Road and look left and there will be a Fence Row (shown above as a green line) going away from the Road (west) leading first down into a hollow and continuing up a sharp hill to a ridge running parallel with the road. The cemetery is to the right (north) of that fence row upon the top of that ridge among a stand of Trees (mostly Cedar). The cemetery is at a distance of about 250 yards west of Gupton Road.
