VINCENT CEMETERY, Near MOORESVILLE, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
ORR, footstone with initials; M. M. O., no other information or stones found.

Footstones are mostly all that remains here. Folks this is the works of a vandal, better known as the "Scum of the Earth." A tree falling on a stone will not cause widespread random scattering and missing pieces that I found here. The works of a Vandal generally manifest itself this way. First they take stones and smash them together and break as many of them as they can. Next they engage in a throwing party, scattering the fragments around causing actually more damage than breaking them.
