ANDERSON & OTHER UNKNOWN CEMETERY, (west of Pulaski, on Hwy 64) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Anderson Cemetery.Â 

Go west from Pulaski on Hwy 64. Go past Hwy 166 and when you see a high area that has the appearance of an old Indian mound the cemetery is in that thicket. The only inscribed stone I found for an Anderson child is very near the road in the middle of the cemetery.

This aerial map shows the cemetery in the clump of trees next to the Highway.
