FORSYTH, James Edward, 19 Dec 1921 - 1 May 2014.
James "Bub" Forsyth

December 19, 1921 - May 1, 2014
James "Bub" Forsyth of Minor Hill, Tn. passed away on Thursday, May 1, 2014 at Southern Tennessee Regional Health System Pulaski.
He was born in Lawrence County on December 19, 1921 and was 92 years old.
He was a member of Puncheon Church of Christ, retired from Murray Ohio in Lawrenceburg, Tn. and a Veteran of the United States Army.
Visitation will be on Saturday, May 3rd from 12:00pm until 2:00pm at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home.
Funeral Service will follow visitation on Saturday at 2:00pm in the Chapel of Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home.
Burial will follow in Oxford Cemetery, Minor Hill, Tennessee with full military honors.
He was preceded in death by his, Wife, Lorell Townsend Forsyth,
parents, Jesse Washington and Laura Pratt Forsyth and a brother, Noah Forsyth.
 Obituary sent in by Mary Bob Richardson 26 Oct 2015
