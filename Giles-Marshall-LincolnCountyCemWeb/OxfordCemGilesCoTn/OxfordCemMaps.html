OXFORD CEMETERY, (Minor Hill Hwy on east side of Hwy) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Oxford Cemetery at N 35.00568 X W -87.20307. 

From 64 Bypass go south on Hwy 11 (Minor Hill Highway) about 7+ miles and look for the entrance into the cemetery on the right. You cannot see the cemetery from the road but the signage is apparent along with the big barn on your right at the road where you turn left.
