LUNA (Peter) CEMETERY, 1 Mi S. of Talley on Fishing Ford Rd., MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Peter Luna Cemetery GPS location as North 35.34457 X West -86.68375.

 This cemetery is located one mile south of Talley on Old Fishing Ford Road, one-fourth mile west of Highway 431.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery in view with the monuments showing up under a lone tree. This is private land and cattle are present.

Do not go to this place if your intentions are not honorable. Respect must be shown at all times for those gone before us by honoring their memorials.
