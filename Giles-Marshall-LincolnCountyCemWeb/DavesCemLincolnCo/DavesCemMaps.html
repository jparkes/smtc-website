DAVES CEMETERY (Hwy 231) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Daves Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 several miles to the Warden Road. The cemetery is beside the driveway of a residential house opposite of where the Warden Road junctions into the Hwy 231. Turn left into the Driveway and (with permission to visit) the cemetery will be on your right.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly located on the left side of the driveway to the residential house. Please notify the owner of the property of your visit to the cemetery to avoid alarming them.
