HARKINS CEMETERY, Hwy 129, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Harkins Cemetery as N 35.31849 X W -86.55422.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Highway 231 and go upon the Chestnut Ridge. Turn left on Hwy 129 Petersburg Highway and go about 1 and 1/2 mile and the cemtery is on the left (south) side of the road at the junction of Bolles Hollow Lane inside a rock fence and under a bunch of trees.

I have attempted to outline the cemetery with a yellow highlighter in the above map.
