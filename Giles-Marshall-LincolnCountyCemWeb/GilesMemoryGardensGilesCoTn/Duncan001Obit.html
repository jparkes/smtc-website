GILES MEMORY GARDENS, Hwy 64 Bypass/Bethel Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 DUNCAN, Cody Shayne, 22 Oct. 1990 - 25 Dec. 2011.


 PV2 Cody Shayne Duncan age 21 of Pulaski passed away Sunday, December 25, 2011 in an automobile accident.

He graduated from Giles County High School and attended Columbia State Community College. He was in the Army National Guard, graduated basic training at Ft. Jackson, South Carolina, and was currently enrolled as a student at Ft. Sam Houston Texas as a combat medic.

Visitation was Wednesday, December 28th from 3:00pm until 8:00pm at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home.
Funeral Service was Thursday, December 29th at 1:00pm in the chapel of Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home with Bro. Don Richey officiating.

Burial followed in Giles Memory Gardens with full military honors.

Memorial Donations are suggested to the Cody Duncan Scholarship Fund at the Bank of Frankewing, 1671 West College Street, Pulaski, Tennessee.

He was preceded in death by his, Grandfather, Army veteran Spec 5 Danny D. Duncan

He is survived by his, Parents, Sergeant 1st Class Danny Shayne Duncan and Kelley Gibson Duncan
Brother, Keaton Duncan of Pulaski Grandparents, Dot and Harold Glover of Pulaski
Grandmother, Joyce Duncan of Pulaski Grandfather, Harold Gibson of Pulaski Aunts and Uncles, Mike and Gwen Martin,
Jeff and Kelly Martin, Barry and Gelenda Martin, Stanley and Tonya Holley, Greg and Shon Lunn, Tasha Williams, and Beth Gibson all of Pulaski
