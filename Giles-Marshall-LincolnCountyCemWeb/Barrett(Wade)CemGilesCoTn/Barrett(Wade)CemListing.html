BARRETT (Wade) CEMETERY, Pickens Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE


Mapping the Location

Area Views of the Cemetery: 1640W/1627/1639/1640


BARRETT, Wade, 29 Feb 1800 - 10 Dec 1870. (s/o Thomas & M. Jane Christian Barrett) 1633/1641 / His Church influence
 In Memory of Wade Barrett, Born Feb 29, 1800 Died Dec 10, 1870. (Prominent Christian minister of the Gospel in the Christian Church which about 1895 became known as the Church of Christ.)

BARRETT, Emelia/(Amelia Jones), 14 Oct 1800 - 16 Jan 1886. (w/o Wade Barrett d/o William & Sarah Armstrong Jones) 1632/1640
 In Memory of Emelia Barrett, Born Oct 14, 1800 Died Jan 16, 1886.

BARRETT, William T., 29 Mar 1829 - 30 Jun 1872. (h/o Louisa Ann McCollum Barrett s/o Wade & Amelia Jones Barrett) 1638C/1638/1640
 IN MEMORY OF Wm T. BARRETT, Born Marc 29, 1829. Died June 30, 1872.
 
BARRETT, T.D., son of H. W. & M. J. Barrett, 28 Oct 1875 - 5 Oct 1876. (s/o H. W. and M. J. Barrett) 1637C/1637
 In Memory of T. D., son of H. W. & M. J. Barrett 28 Oct 1875 - 5 Oct 1876.

CASKEY, Samuel W.(Wilkerson), 11 Jul 1826 - 29 Nov 1898. (h/o Sarah Amelia Barrett Caskey s/o Samuel & Nancy Agnes Coffey Caskey) 1628/1629/1631
 In Memory of SAMUEL W. CASKEY, Born July 11, 1826, Died Nov 29, 1898.  A precious one from us is gone, A voice we loved is stilled, A place is vacant in our home that never can be filled.

CASKEY, Sarah Ann Amelia Barrett, 1 Mar 1833 - 1 Dec 1912, Aged 79 yrs. 9 mos. (w/o Samuel Wilkerson Caskey d/o Wade and Amelia Jones Barrett)1630/1631
 In Memory of SARAH ANN CASKEY, Born Mar. 1, 1833, Died Dec 1, 1912. Age 79 years, 9 months. We miss thee from our home dear Mother, we miss thee......

CASKEY, Samey Lenora, dau. of S. W. & S. A. Caskey, (born cir 21 Nov 1862) died, 23 May 1868, Aged 5 yrs., 6 mo. 2 days. (d/o Samuel Wilkerson and Sarah Amelia Barrett Caskey) 1635C/1635
 Samey Lenora daughter of S. W. & S. A. Caskey, died, 23 May 1868, Aged 5 yrs., 6 mo. 2 days (might be 22 days).

CASKEY, Lena, 15 Sep 1867 14 Oct 1940. (d/o James Wilkerson and Sarah Amelia Barrett Caskey) (grave unmarked)

FOLLIS, Alice Ella Caskey 26 Dec 1857 - 27 Dec 1921. (w/o Benjamin Franklin Follis d/o Samuel Wilkerson & Sarah Amelia Barrett Caskey) (grave unmarked.)

MARTIN, James T., son of W. G. & M. J. Martin, 5 Sep 1861 - 12 May 1869, Age 7 yrs. 8 mos. (s/o William Groves & Mary Jane Barrett Martin) 1636L/1636C/1636/Martin(WG)Census-1860
 In memory of James T. Martin, son of W.G. & M.J. Martin, Born September 5th 1861, died May 12th 1869, Age 7 years, 8 months & 7 days.
 
MOORE, Lucinda Caroline, 3rd dau. of Wade and Amelia Barrett, and wife of Samuel Moore, 13 Aug 1826 - 5 Aug 1855, 29, years, wanting 8 days. (w/o Samuel Moore d/o Wade & Amelia Jones Barrett) 1634C/1634
 To the memory of Lucinda Caroline, 3rd Daughter of Wade & Amelia Barrett & wife of Samuel Moore, Born Aug 13, 1826 Died Aug 5th 1855. being 29 years old wanting 8 days.
