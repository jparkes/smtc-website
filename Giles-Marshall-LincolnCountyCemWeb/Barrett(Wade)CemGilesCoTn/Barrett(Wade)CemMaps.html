BARRETT (Wade) CEMETERY, Pickens Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the coordinates of the Barrett Cemetery as N 35.40243 X W -86.96401. It is in the yard behind the home on 2074 Pickens Rd. This is the backyard of the Burgess House.

Location: Go Hwy 31N - Right into Lynnville Hwy - Go thru Lynnville then another 1.5 Miles to Pickens Road - turn left (north) and go 2 miles. Cemetery is on the right, behind the house, in the back (south) edge of
the yard.

This aerial map shows the cemetery. It can be seen at the back edge of the yard separated by a concrete barrier in the back yard of a home. Please notify the owner of your visit here.
