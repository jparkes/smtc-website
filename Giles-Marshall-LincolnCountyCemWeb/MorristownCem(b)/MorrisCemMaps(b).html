MORRISTOWN CEMETERY, (African American, Dry Creek Road) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of he Morristown Cemetery. Not sure if that is the White or Black area (or both) of the cemetery shown above.

 Location: Highway 64 West, right into Campbellsville Road, left on Dry Creek Road. Go 1/4 mile and turn left into the New Hope Cemetery Rd. - former Collins farm.

This map shows the local roads & field roads needed to get to this cemetery. I do not know where the New Hope Cemetery is but it could be the designate name of the Morristown Cemetery (Black) by the community.
