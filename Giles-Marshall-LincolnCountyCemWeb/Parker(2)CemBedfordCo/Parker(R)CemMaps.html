PARKER CEMETERY (Mt Hermon Rd) BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS locator coordinates of the Parker (Rosanna) Cemetery, located west of New Hermon.
To find this cemetery go southeast on Hwy 82 from Shelbyville Tennessee to Flat Creek. Turn right on Center Church Road and almost immediately turn back left on New Hermon Road and travel 2 miles and you will be in New Hermon. Next turn right on the Mt Hermon Road and go 3/4 mile and the cemetery is upon a hill above a residence on the left.
