PARKER (Unknown) CEMETERY (Amos Gammill Rd) BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS locator coordinates of the Parker (Unknown) Cemetery.
To find this cemetery go southeast on Hwy 82 from Shelbyville Tennessee to Flat Creek. Turn right on new Center Church Road and go to the Amos Gammill Road and turn (left) south. Travel about a mile and the historic house will be on the left. Across from that in a cow pasture is a clump of trees where this cemetery is located.
