KIMBROUGH (HENRY) CEMETERY, (Crooked Hill Road) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Henry Kimbrough Cemetery.Â 

Go Bethel Road (Hwy 166 South. Veer left on Crooked Hill Road and go 1 mile and 2 tenths and with permission turn left into the driveway. You will find the owners have clear access but the cemetery is north across the fence on and not on their property so to go that way you are treading lightly.

This aerial map shows the cemetery accessible from a residential road originating just south of & due west of the cemetery. The road goes to a house and passes by on the north side, but can be driven to a gate and thru the gate with permission drive around the first thicket and get close to the north fence (spanning east/west) where the cemetery is across that fence. Have a 5' ladder to cross the fence so no damage is done. The road to the cemetery also doubles as the road to the residence and farm operation so be sure you go here with deliberate upscale intention, permission is needed.
