JORDAN, William Cherry "Bill", 1867 - 19 Jul 1951, son of Jim & Dee Taylor Jordan. Obituary

Athens (AL) Limestone Democrat, July 24, 1951, unnumbered (counted p.16)

Last rites for William Cherry Jordan, 84, Toney. Route 2. were held Sunday at 11 a.m. at the Bee Springs Church. W. C. Steadman officiating. Burial for the deceased, who died several days earlier, followed in the Bee Springs Cemetery, McConnell-Service directing. Survivors include three daughters, Mrs. Audio Scott. Maggie Randall and Mrs. Myrtle McCown; five sons. Dan, Homer, Jasper, Shelly and Willie; 21 grandchildren; 33 great-grandchildren; and nine great-great- grandchildren.
