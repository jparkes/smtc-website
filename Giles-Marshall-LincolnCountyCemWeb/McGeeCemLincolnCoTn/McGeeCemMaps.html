McGEE CEMETERY, (Boonville Rd-Ashby Rd) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the McGee Cemetery. 

From Hwy 50 (Lynchburg Hwy) take Booneville Road north (left) to Ashby Road and again turn left. Go half mile & turn left into the field road that seems to be dedicated to the cemetery though it may have locked gates and/or fences. Cemetery about 170 yards down that road behind a barn. Someone recently cleared the cemetery before I was there and may be caring for it now. 

This aerial map shows with a red check the cemetery down what seems like a right-of-way road. Be sure you go here with deliberate upscale intentions and secure permission if needed as this is probably not a public cemetery.
