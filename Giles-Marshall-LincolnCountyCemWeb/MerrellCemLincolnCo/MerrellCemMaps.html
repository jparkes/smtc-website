MERRELL CEMETERY, (McLemore Rd) - LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Merrell Cemetery.Â 

Take the Ardmore Highway south of Fayetteville and go southwest. Go more than 14 miles and turn right on Blanche Road and back left on McLemore Road. Go about 1 & 1/2 mile and the cemetery is under a clump of tree upon the slope.

This aerial map shows the cemetery maintained. It is visible from the road marked here with a red check. Secure permission here if needed. As you can see there are nearby neighbors.
