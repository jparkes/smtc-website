WALDROP CEMETERY,  (Hwy 31 N. Giles/Maury County line), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Waldrop Family Cemetery. GPS West side of Road: N 35.41964 X W 87.03365.

Location: Highway 31 North. Cemetery  is divided by the highway just south of the Maury & Giles County lines.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the west side of the road, but if it is on the other side it is not so clear to me as I failed to locate the east side.
