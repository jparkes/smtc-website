BROWN-BAUGH CEMETERY, (Roy Rd off of Prospect Road from Hwy 31) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Brown-Baugh Cemetery.Â 

Directions: Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski to Tarpley and bear right onto Prospect aka Aspen Hill Road. Go less than a mile and turn right into Roy Road and follow that to a locked gate with "No Trespassing" Signs posted on it. With permission & a key go thru that to the house straight ahead. To the right of the house 50 yards you will see the cemetery partly fenced under the trees. It is labeled the Baugh Cemetery on GNIS Maps.

This aerial map shows the cemetery just north of the residential house. The Baugh section of the cemetery is fenced with the (older) Brown Box tombs in front of the fence to the east. While there I noticed the Lewis Brown box tomb appeared to have been recently vandalized. It was lying open & broken on the ground. The north side had caved off and the corner of the top broken off. The inscription is still in place. We can see the tomb is not harmed when this aerial photo was made a couple of years ago. It is showing up here as a tiny rectangular shaped object. This time phases the crime somewhat.
