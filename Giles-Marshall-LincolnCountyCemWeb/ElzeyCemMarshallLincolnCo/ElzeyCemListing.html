This cemetery was known by the Sorrells family. It is not in any of the Marsh cemetery books nor any national mapping sites such as USGS or Google. It was still hard to locate being inside a round grove of trees among other round groves of trees on the north side of that hill, but nearing the top. Located about 350 yards south east of the James E. Sorrells Cemetery up the slope.

Photographed on a Saturday evening 30 Jun 2015 with the help of John Sorrells & JD Mathena . The inscriptions were updated with exceptions found or known by C. Wayne Austin.
