GIBSONVILLE CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 Columbia to Campbellsville Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This is the road signage which will indicate the road to travel east to get to this cemetery off of Hwy 245. The cemetery is about 300 yards off of the road across a cattle gap.
