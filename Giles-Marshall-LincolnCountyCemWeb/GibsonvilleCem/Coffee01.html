GIBSONVILLE CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 Columbia to Campbellsville Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.    

COFFEE, Dannie Wright, 22 Feb 1916 - unknown. (daughter of Marvin Young & Emma Lillian Duncan Coffee; died in childhood; unmarked grave. Ref: Ella V. Coffee Stevens. No Photo
COFFEE, Mary Lurline, 18 May 1918- unknown. (daughter of Marvin Young & Emma Lillian Duncan Coffee; died in childhood; unmarked grave. Ref: Ella V. Coffee Stevens. No Photo
COFFEE, Christine, 31 May 1920 - 3 Nov 1930. (daughter of Marvin Young & Emma Lillian Duncan Coffee; unmarked grave)
While living at Stiversville, Marvin and Lillian Duncan Coffee had three children that were born and died in childhood. These were Dannie Wright & Mary Lurline & Christine Coffee all girls. One died of pneumonia near the time of the great flu epidemic (abt 1918); one died of burns when her dress caught fire at a heater and she ran out to her mother in the back yard. Ella Virginia Coffee Stevens, a sister of the above children, says she doesn't recall which of these two children burned or which child died of pneumonia. The third child Christine died while Marvin and Lillian were living in Lynnville. Christine was an invalid at birth. Linda's mother (Ella Virginia) is the oldest child who survived those hard early years.)
Information provided by Linda Munroe as related to her by her mother Ella Virginia Coffee Stevens, 11 Apr 2009. Added here 17 Dec 2009 by Wayne Austin.
