GATLIN CEMETERY, (just S of Cash Point on Asa Smith Road), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Gatlin Cemetery.

 Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 and left or west on Hay 110 (Ardmore Highway) to Cash Point Community and turn left into Asa Smith Road. Go about 2/3 mile and look left upon the bank of the road at the cemetery.

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly in a grove of trees on the bank of the Asa Smith Road.
