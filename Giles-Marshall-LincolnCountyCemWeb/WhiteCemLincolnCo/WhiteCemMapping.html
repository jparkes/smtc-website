WHITE CEMETERY, (Cash Point), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the White Cemetery as N 35.1203 X W 86.81361.Â 

Go south from Fayetteville & turn (right) southwest on the Ardmore Highway #110. Follow that more than 10 miles to the Cash Point. You will have pass by Skenum, Taft, Roper, & other villages. The cemetery is about 3 miles before you get to Ardmore Tennessee on the (right) north side of the road in a clump of trees behind a chain link fence.
