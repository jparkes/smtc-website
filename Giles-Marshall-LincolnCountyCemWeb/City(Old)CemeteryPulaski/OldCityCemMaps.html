OLD CITY CEMETERY, (Pulaski, between 1st & 2nd Streets, south side) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Location: Between 1st and 2nd Streets, South, in the city of Pulaski, Giles County Tennessee.

Between 1st and 2nd Streets, South, in the city of Pulaski, Tennessee. 1st Street becomes Hwy 31 South and you will find this cemetery on your right just before the street bend southeastward. 

 This aerial view shows the cemetery on the right of 1st Street just before it exits the south part of the city of Pulaski Tennessee. The large Maple Wood Cemetery for the city of Pulaski is further east of this place.
