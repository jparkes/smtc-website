CROCKETT (Andrew) CEMETERY, Crockett Rd, WILLIAMSON COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Crockett (Andrew) Cemetery as N 35.977323 X W -86.776014.

Location: From Nashville go south on I - 65 to the Ext 71 (Concord Rd Exit) and go east (left) less than 1 mile to Wilson Pike. Turn right (south) and go about 1 and 1/2 miles to Crockett Rd. The cemetery is at the junction of Wilson Pike & Crockett Rd in the southeast corner under a large rock wall.Â  Parking is just past the cemetery on Wilson Pike. Do not go to this cemetery with harsh chemicals or abrasives or paints to be used on these stones. I see evidence of damage from that already such as the Samuel Crockett monument and the paint recently applied inside the inscription.
