HORTON/(HAUGHTON) CEMETERY, Brown Shop Road, MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Horton Cemetery GPS location at Lat. 35.322106, Long -86.765136.
Location: Go north from Lewisburg on the Verona-Caney Road to Double Bridge Road and turn left (west) Go just over a mile on that road and the cemetery is on your right back off the road up a gentle slope.
Do not go to this place if your intentions are not honorable. Respect must be shown at all times for those gone before us by not disturbing their monuments & graves.
