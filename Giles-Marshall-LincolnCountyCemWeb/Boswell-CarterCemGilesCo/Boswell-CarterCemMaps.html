BOSWELL-CARTER CEMETERY, (Ardmore Ridge Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS location of the Boswell-Carter Cemetery.Â  This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. It is my belief the GPS Latitude & Longitude are correct as stated.


 Location: From Ardmore go north on Ardmore Ridge Road about 3 miles to the John Cribbs place. Turn left into his drive which doubles as the Cemetery Road. With permission from John go by his barn following the road he has designated for cemetery visitors.
This Aerial view shows the cemetery in a big open field. Mr. Cribbs maintains it and has done a phenomenal job of clearing and restoration since he has lived there. If you visit this cemetery be sure to see him. He has a firm grasp of the issues here and enjoys telling others about the cemetery.
