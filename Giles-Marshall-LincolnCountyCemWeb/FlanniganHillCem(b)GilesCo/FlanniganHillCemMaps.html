FLANNIGAN HILL CEMETERY, (Morrow Branch Road near Maury Co line) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This cemetery and Howell Cemetery is called Elkridge on this map. The Flannigan Hill Cemetery is just across the road and due south of the Howell Cemetery making the North Latitude 35.44841 incorrect by about 300 feet. The corrected Lat/Long for this cemetery is 35.4474 X 87.1311.
Directions Hwy 64 to Highway Campbellsville Rd (Hwy 166) right (north) and take Hwy 245 to Morrow Branch Rd. Turn left and Pass Pump Station Rd and Screamer Rd and the cemetery is on your right still in Giles Co., but before you reach the Maury County Line. 
Alternate directions: Highway 64W, right through Campbellsville; right on Columbia Road, then left on Morrow Branch Road. Cemetery on right near missionary Baptist Church. Close to the Maury County line. It is just back from the Howell Cemetery on the left. 

 This aerial view shows the cemetery on the right of Morrow Branch Road in a broom sage field. The Second Cemetery included on this page is the Flannigan Ridge Cemetery. It is located northeast of the Flannigan Hill Cemetery in the brown colored clearing.
