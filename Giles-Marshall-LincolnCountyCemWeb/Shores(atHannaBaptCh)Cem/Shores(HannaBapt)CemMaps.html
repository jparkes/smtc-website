SHORES CEMETERY AT HANNA BAPTIST CHURCH (Hanna Community), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Shores at Hanna Baptist Church Cemetery GPS location.
Location: From Pulaski take Highway 64 bypass and turn south on Hwy 166 and go several miles and turn west on Stella Road. Go about 2 miles and turn west or left into Dog Branch Road. Go one mile and turn right onto Kimbrough Road and the cemetery is there on the left across from the Hanna Baptist Church

This Aerial Map shows the Shores Cemetery just after one turns onto Kimbrough Road.
