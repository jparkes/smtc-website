DERRYBERRY (AJ) CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

This map plots the GPS Coordinates of the Derryberry (AJ) Cemetery in the northwest corner of Marshall County Tennessee as GPS N 35.68696 X W -86.79197.

From Rally Hill. Go north on Highway 431 to Tally Moore Road, turn right and go about 2 miles to end of road. Turn right on Hazelwood Rd and go until it terminates into Reynolds Road. Turn left on Reynolds Road and then turn back right on Derryberry Cemetery road incorrectly labeled on the Google map below as Yancy Rd. Cemetery on hill to right of road in the bend of the road, in northwest corner of Marshall County. If you are following this map above after turning on Talley Moore Rd after the third 90 degree bend in the road you will terminate into Hazelwood Road and turn right there. Follow Hazelwood Rd for about a mile and you will terminate on Reynolds Road. Almost immediately after turning left on Reynolds Road turn back on Derryberry Cemetery Road then follow the above instructions, but you will see the cemetery from there. 

This google map shows the cemetery on Yancy Road but it may be Derryberry Cemetery Road instead.

With this Mapquest Aerial view we see the stones lined up in this cemetery north/south and facing east/west.
