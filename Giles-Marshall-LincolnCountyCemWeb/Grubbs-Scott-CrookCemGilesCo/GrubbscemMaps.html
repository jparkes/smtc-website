GRUBBS CEMETERY, (Tarpley Shop Road)GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Grubbs Cemetery.

Location from Pulaski: Take highway 31 south toward Elkton, but in Tarpley turn east on the Tarpley Shop Road and go just over 3 miles and turn right (south) into the drive of the brick home. The cemetery can be seen in the yard on the far side (east) from the driveway of the home.

This aerial view shows the cemetery marked with a red check on the east side of the home but the cemetery is actually mostly under the trees next to the home.

This view shows the house better and the cemetery next to the home on the east side. The stones are not visible here. The cemetery is about 50 yards from the home. It is being mowed as part of the yard as you will see from the photos.
