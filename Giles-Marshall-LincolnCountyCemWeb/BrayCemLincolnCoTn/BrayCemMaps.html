BRAY CEMETERY (Corders Crossroad Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Bray Family Cemetery GPS location as 35.05671 X -86.47906.
Location: Take Highway 64 east out of Fayetteville abt 5 miles and turn south at Kelso and get on Teal Hollow Road going south. Go several miles and pass over Howell Hill Rd where the road becomes Corders Crossroads Road and go nearly 2 miles and look left at a residential road which takes you to a house whereby the cemetery is behind the house on another party's land. The folks there are friendly and will allow you permission if you ask.

This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery is located, across the fence, behind a home.
