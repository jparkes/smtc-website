SMITH (Hardy) CEMETERY, (North side Lynchburg Highway) - MOORE COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Mapping the Location


Area Photos of the Cemetery: 8139/8140/8144


 SMITH, Hardy H., 9 Oct 1815 -  12 Jul 1864. Father. He was a loving Husband, a kind father, and a friend to the distressed. 8142/8143
 SMITH, T. B., 19 Jul 1850 - 8 Aug 1907. Father To him we trust a place is given, among the Saints, with Christ in Heaven. 8141/8144

Signs of older graves, but without markers.
This cemetery is caught up in the expansion of Jack Daniels Distillery, but seems cared for, except for the weeds growing very tall. I may have missed one stone but there are many listings on Findagrave.com classified into this cemetery which are really Smith Chapel Cemetery located elsewhere.


Visited & photographed 12 Feb 2011 by C. Wayne Austin. Initial typing by Faye Bradford. Added to this site 28 Jul 2012 by C. Wayne Austin.
