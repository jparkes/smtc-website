ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.



You are looking east down Highway 129 with the Cemetery Sign Monument on the right of the road. I am told the old Elkridge Church stood on the north side of the road (our left) just beyond the clump of trees where the bank tapers downward. On our right and behind us is the cemetery.
