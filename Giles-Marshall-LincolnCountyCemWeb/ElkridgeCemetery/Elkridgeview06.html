We are standing in the upper center of the cemetery looking southeast toward the older section. However there are recent graves in the front of this area. Scroll to the right if you cannot see all of the photo.

This view standing near the road in the center of the cemetery is due south. It shows the open area between the new section and the old historic section.  Sometimes the wide open space contains the remains of unmarked graves and sometimes not. Isn't it interesting to study the patterns of the growth of various types of grass to see if that tells us anything about where unmarked graves are located.
