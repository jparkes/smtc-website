ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE, Hwy 129 east

From Interstate Highway 65 exit (Exit 27) onto Hwy 129 east toward Lynnville and go just about 5 miles until you top a hill and there will be the cemetery all along the road, mostly on your left. To find this cemetery from Lynnville go east on Highway 129 about 2 miles and there along the road will be the cemetery on your right or left for the black section.
