ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.

BARLAR/(BARLOW), Jasper N., 10 Sep 1844 - 7 Jul 1918. Son of John Barlow & (2nd wife) Elvira (?) Barlow, h/o 1st Elizabeth Hopkins; 2nd Sarah (?) who preceded him in death. 1880 Census had children Ann, Wiley & Howard. His wife's name was given as Ann Barlow (no marked graves has ever been found here so likely he is interred here in an unmarked grave. Chances are good if his grave is here then his wives are buried here also.

Information by Wayne Austin 6 Sep 2013.
