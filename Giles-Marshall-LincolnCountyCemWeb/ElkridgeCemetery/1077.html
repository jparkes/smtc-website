ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.

The little fractured stone to our right of the Archibald Crockett memorial in the drawing.


KERR, William Monroe, son of Robert & Moriah Kerr, 21 October 1828 - 17 August 1829.

This memorial is located in the row between the memorials of Rachel Kerr Crockett and Archibald Crockett. Robert & Mariah's surnames were Cochran. This child was born after the marriage of Robert Cochran to Mariah Kerr abt 1826.
