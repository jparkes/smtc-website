ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.


BRAY, Malinda Butler, died 16 January 1850, age 71 yrs, 1 day old

BRAY, Reuben, died 26 May 1850, age 85 years old. "Twin Sister Mary Butler Lamar Brown buried in New Ramie Cemetery Maury County Tn.".
 The stone on our left is the old Malinda Butler Ingram Bray memorial. The broken stone on our right is the old Reuben Bray memorial. This is on the far east end of the cemetery.
