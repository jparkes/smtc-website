ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.

COFFEY, Loy, son of J.M. & M. J. Coffey 22 March 1880 - 26 April 1881, age 1 yr, 1 mo, 4 days. 
also, but on the north side of the stone
COFFEY, Ollie, son of J. M. & M.J. Coffey 22 October 1881 - 12 September 1882, Age 10 mos, 10 days
These Coffee children are relatives of the Coffey families interred in Campbell Station a few miles north of here in Evergreen Cemetery

and also Shane Cemetery

in Maury County. Clues: they were all Presbyterian. They used the same style tombstones for the children and the children had similar but unique given names. I believe the J. is for Joseph M. Coffey.
