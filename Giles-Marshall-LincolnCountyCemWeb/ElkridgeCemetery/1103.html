ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.

UNKNOWN Grave outlined by installed Limestone stones. Near the Montgomery and Crockett family plots. My archeological judgment tells me these are graves dated prior to 1840. Notice the old clump of bear grass at the head of the grave. That plant may be more than a hundred and fifty years old.
