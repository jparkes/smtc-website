DUNCANVILLE CEMETERY, Duncanville MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Duncanville aka Pleasant Hill Cemetery.
Location from Lewisburg go west on West Commerce St and turn left on Hwy 373. Go about 3/4 of a mile to Duncanville and the cemetery is on the left adjacent to the highway.

This aerial view shows the cemetery marked with a red check on the south side of the highway. A close inspection of this map shows a new highway bypassing Duncanville to the north. In the future you will need to find Duncanville Tennessee, to find this cemetery. This revised highway is an attempt to better connect Lewisburg with Interstate 65/ as this sleepy little village will slow you down.
