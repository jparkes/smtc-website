CARTER HILLS CEMETERY, (Coldwater), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

BEDDINGFIELD, Henry Farrar, 29 Jul 1937 - 28 Feb 2012.

Graveside services for Henry Farrar Beddingfield, age 74 of Taft, will be conducted Thursday at 11 AM at Carter Hills Cemetery with Rev. David Durham officiating. Higgins Funeral Home is in charge of services. Mr. Beddingfield passed away Tuesday morning in Donalson Care Center.
He was a native of Lincoln County and the son of the late James Clarence and Mary Adline Cowan Beddingfield. Mr. Beddingfield was a farmer and a member of the Baptist Church.
Survivors include two sisters, Sarah Sims of Taft and Faye Bly of Coldwater, three brothers, Frank Beddingfield of Taft, Lewis Beddingfield of Park City and O. G. Beddingfield of Taft and nieces and nephews.
There will be no visitation at the funeral home prior to services.
Higgins Funeral Home is assisting the family of Henry Farrar Beddingfield.
Information provided by Higgins Funeral Home.
