LUCY-COLVETT-ROPER CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

From this map we see the GPS location of the Lucy Cemetery in the northwestern part of Giles County.

From Nashville take I-65 South to Exit 22. Go left or southwest on Highway 31 Alt to Pulaski. Turn left or south in Pulaski and then take the main Highway 64 west (right) out of Pulaski for a couple of miles and turn (right) north on Highway 166. Go thru Campbellsville and continue on 166 to Factory Creek Road. Turn left (or west) and go 2/3 of a mile and turn north on Anderson Creek Road. The road to the cemetery on the right is just after one turns into Anderson creek Rd.
