HOWELL CEMETERY, MAURY/GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE LINE

We are looking into the cemetery at the few formal memorials and some inscribed fieldstones and perhaps hundreds of fieldstones marking graves. No one knows who is buried here. Back to our right is the stone of Major C. Howell and wife and to our left is the stone of Seley C. Norman. 
 

Photo by Norman Howell 7-25-1987. Info by Wanda Howell 9/14/04.
