RICHMOND CEMETERY, Hannah Gap Rd, BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Richmond Cemetery as N 35.38128 X W -86.59225.

Location: From Shelbyville go south on Hwy 231 and turn right on State Route 64. Follow that for about 3 miles and turn left (west) on Richmond Road (Hwy 130). Follow that for about 7 miles and turn left (south) on Hannah Gap Road. Go less than a mile and turn into the Richmond Cemetery Road and the cemetery will be on both sides of that road.

This is my rough attempt to draw out the boundaries of this cemetery, but do not take it as a legal document. I know there is an east side of the road and a west side of the road. Also I understand there is an African American Section, but since I did not cover the whole area I failed to identify that or to find any of the African American graves.
