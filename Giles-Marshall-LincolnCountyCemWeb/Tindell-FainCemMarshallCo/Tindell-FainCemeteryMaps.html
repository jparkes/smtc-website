JACKSON CEMETERY, LEFTWICH, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


 This cemetery is located almost on the Marshall-Maury County lines on Highway 431, about a mile south of Hardison's Bridge, on right, on the right of what used to be the old Hazel Hardison house. It is now gone. All I saw remaining in 2008 was a piece of the porch stairs.
