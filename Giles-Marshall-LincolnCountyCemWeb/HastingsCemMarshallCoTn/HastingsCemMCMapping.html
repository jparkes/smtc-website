HASTINGS CEMETERY, Richmond Rd., MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the Hastings Cemetery GPS location of N 35.35422 X W -86.62271.
Location: From Petersburg go north on Richmond Road aka Hwy 130 about 2 miles and notice the cemetery on the east (your right) bank of the road. You will not be able to park there. a small ditch is on both sides of the road so find parking 100 yards north and walk back to the cemetery.

This Google Street View shows the cemetery on the east side fenced out of the pasture next to the highway.
