POWELL CEMETERY, (Kincaid/Globe Rd) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Powell Cemetery.Â 

From Lewisburg go east on west Commerce St and turn left onto Mooresville Hwy 373. Go almost under I-65 and turn south on Globe Road. Pass by the Marsh Cemetery far off the road on your left and the Houston Cemetery on your right a couple hundred yards from the road. Merge onto Kincaid Road after a couple of miles on Globe Road and go a few hundred yards and the cemetery is on the bank of the road on the right.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with a red check next to the road under a stand of trees. It was fenced and maintained when I was there.
