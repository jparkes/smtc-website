PARK CEMETERY, (near Elkton) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS Latitude & Longitude of the Park Cemetery. Dellrose is not the nearest town. Elkton and Ardmore Tennessee/Alabama are both closer. That is a USGS designation that specifies the map center.

Location: Go south on Hwy 31 several miles and as you approach Elkton turn left or east on Bryson Road. Take first black top road on right labeled as Long Road on most maps. The cemetery is on left of Long Road and on the left side of a residential driveway enclosed in an old wrought iron fence. The property owner whose home is there has the surrounding property.
