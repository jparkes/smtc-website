Lincoln County News Magazine (of businesses & Farams) - 1904 (about 500 Celebrated Persons/Families)
This map of Lincoln County in 1904 and earlier shows the Main streams: Elk River (W/E), Cane Creek (N/S), Swan Creek (N/S), Mulberry Creek (N/S)and the West Fork of Mulberry Creek (N/S)
Shows the Rail Lines: MT & ARR Railroad from Fayetteville to Ardmore, Nashville & Chattanooga from Petersburg to Elora. This map also shows mileage from various points. Can't really tell if some of those numbers are district #?, Villages mentioned are from the top down Left to Right: Petersburg, Bidwell, Boonville, Blakeville, Bellville, Howell, Redoak, Boonshill, Mimosa, Mulberry, Hughey, Ego, McBurg, Harms, Fayetteville (City of), Millville, Cyruston, Kelso, Smithland, Clardyville, Molina, Dellrose, Coldwater, Renegar, Oregon, Yukon, Camargo,Corder, Flintville, Taft, Argyle, Corder, Wendover, Blanche, Rowell, Lincoln, Elora, Roperton, and Goshen. Charity is above Boonville and inside Moore County.
