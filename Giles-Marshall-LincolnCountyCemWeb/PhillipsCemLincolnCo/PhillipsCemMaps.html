PHILLIPS CEMETERY (Hwy 231 N. west side) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Phillips Cemetery.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231/10 toward Shelbyville. Continuing on Hwy 231 go upon the Chestnut Ridge and after passing the Charity Road (Hwy 129) on your right go just over a mile and the cemetery is on your left after the highway curves back (right) northeast. It is out in a field across a fence and I think a (locked?) gate is present. You can see it from the highway in the winter, that will hold true until the thickets grow up & hide it. The Cemetery is about 100 yards from the highway.
