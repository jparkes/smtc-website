NEAL CEMETERY, Beech Hill Rd, Pulaski, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This is the lone stone found in this cemetery. I am calling it the Neal Cemetery.

NEAL, James H., died 21 Jul 1825 aged 21 years 7 months and 12 days.
Inscription: "In memory of James H. Neal who died July 21st 1825 Aged 21 years 7 months and 12 days. He moulders under this stone, but not forgotten.
