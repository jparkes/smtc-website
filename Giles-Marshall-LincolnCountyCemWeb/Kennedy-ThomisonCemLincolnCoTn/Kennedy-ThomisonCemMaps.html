This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Kennedy-Thomison-Warden Cemetery as N 35.19272 X W -86.56596. There is a gated entrance at the Clark's Mill Road that is kept locked. One can see the rock lined tombs from the above map.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 to Clarks Mill Rd on right and the cemetery is in the south east corner of that junction in a horse pasture. Someone has recently cleared the cemetery of much of the brush.
