BATTS CEMETERY (African American), Johnson Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Batts Cemetery.Â N 35.22823 X W 87.02955.

 Location: Go north on North 1st St and take Bledsoe Road to the right. Climb up to the top of the hill. When the road forks take the Johnson Rd left another 1/2 mile. Cemetery in small angled clearing on right side of road just back from the Peterson Cemetery. Above map does not show the Peterson Cemetery. The cemetery marked Pointer Cemetery on this map in the right legend is actually the Porter Cemetery, another black cemetery.
