CLIFTON CEMETERY (Weakley Creek area), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Clifton Family Cemetery.
Location: Hwy 64 West out of Pulaski to Weakley Creek Road. North on Weakley Creek Road to Dry Weakley Creek Road. Right on that to the Clifton Cemetery Road which is a residential road too. The cemetery is in the back yard of Parker home and the Parkers' care for it.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly behind the Parker home. Do not go there without permission from the Parker family. Clara Parker the matriarch is a well respected archivist and historian of Giles County.
