KEDRON CEMETERY (Highway 166) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
The center of the map marks the location of the Kedron Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records and I have no reason to believe the GPS coordinates are not correct as shown above.


 This map shows at the Kedron Cemetery location on Highway 166 between Pulaski Tennessee and the Alabama State line. It is on the west side of the road above the old Kedron Church building now serving as a community center.

This Aerial view shows the cemetery pointed out by a yellow graphic arrow.
