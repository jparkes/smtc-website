Cemetery Restoration photo. Use a long steel sharp probe to punch down as deep as about 8 inches to look for the other missing parts of the monuments for this cemetery: 1467L

This little family cemetery has been totally neglected for who knows how long. Only one whole inscribed stone can be found, but it is fallen. Others may be present but if so they are underground.

Photographed on a Rainey Tuesday about 1:30 PM in the presence of Bobby Prosser and Terry Tudor. The inscriptions were updated with exceptions found or known by C. Wayne Austin. This publication culminated from various sources. Listed on page 2 of the book Lincoln County County Cemeteries by Tim & Helen Marsh, about 1978. Added here 23 Dec 2017.
