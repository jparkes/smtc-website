NICHOLS CEMETERY, Bledsoe Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Nichols Cemetery as N 35.34894 X W -86.58254 It is directly behind a house in an open field on o hill top.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 to Petersburg & turn right (East) on Hwy 129. Follow that several miles and turn north on Bledsoe Road and follow that just less than 2 miles and look to your right at a house. Go into the driveway and the cemetery is on the hill behind the fence on the highest part of the hill. It is hard to see because all the stones are fallen and the high grass covers the stones.
