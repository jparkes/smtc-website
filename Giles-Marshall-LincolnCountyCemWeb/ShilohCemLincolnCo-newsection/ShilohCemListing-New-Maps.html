SHILOH CEMETERY (New Section), (Hwy 273, Old Elkton Pike) - LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Shiloh Cemetery - New Section.Â 

Coming out of Fayetteville Turn left on Hwy 273 (Old Elkton Pike) and go several (more than 10) miles. Turn right on Shiloh Cemetery road and go up the slope to the cemetery new section on the left and the old section on the right.

This aerial map shows the cemetery down the hill from the church building. Please do not go here unless your intentions are to honor the dead or you have business here.
