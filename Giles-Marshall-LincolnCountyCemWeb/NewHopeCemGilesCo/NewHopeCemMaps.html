NEW HOPE CEMETERY, (Weakley Creek Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the New Hope Cemetery just off of Weakley Creek Road. 

 Location: Go west on Highway 64 (toward Lawrenceburg) from Pulaski several miles and turn north (right) on the Weakley Creek Road. The cemetery is 1 1/2 miles up the road on the left and (north of) beside the New Hope Baptist Church.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the Church Road just after a steep left bend in the Weakley Creek Road.
