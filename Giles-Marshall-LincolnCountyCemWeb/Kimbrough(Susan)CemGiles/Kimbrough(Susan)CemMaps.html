KIMBROUGH (Susan) CEMETERY, (Crook Hill Road) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS coordinates of the Kimbrough (Susan) Cemetery. It is in the center of the map and marked as Cem with a red check. I believe the GPS coordinates to be accurate.
This road map shows the cemetery off of Highway 166 on the east side of Crooked Hill Road. Take Bethel Road (Hwy 166) south from Pulaski to Crook Hill Road and turn right. Go past Fogg Hollow Road about 1 mile. Access to the Cemetery is directly across from a house facing south. At the end of the driveway of the mentioned house and with the permission of the Lindsey family go (east) into the woods about 50 yards and the fallen stone of Susan Kimbrough can be seen lying in three separate parts.
This aerial view shows the Cemetery on our right (east) side of Crooked Hill Road. There are today two large trees towering over the cemetery.
