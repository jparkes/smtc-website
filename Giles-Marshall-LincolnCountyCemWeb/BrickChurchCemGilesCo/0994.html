BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE


ROTHROCK, Jonathan, 17 April 1802 - 5 May 1852. "aged 50 yrs.","Married 10 Nov. 1831". (s/o Philip Rothrock and Martha Rothrock; h/o Margaret Amanda Gordon Rothrock; he was born in Lewis town Mifflin Co. Penn, and died in Lawrence Co. Tn.)
ROTHROCK, M. (Margaret) A., consort of Jonathan Rothrock, 27 Jun 1811 - 1 Apr 1892, (nee Margaret Amanda Gordon)

Issue of the Pulaski or Giles County Newspaper, Thursday April 7, 1892:
Margaret A. Rothrock died at her home near Brick Church, this county, on last Friday, April 1, 1892. Mrs. Rothrock was aged 80 years. She was the mother of one daughter and four sons: Mr. G. M. Rothrock, of Pulaski, Dr. R. G. Rothrock, of Nashville, Rev. Thos. Rothrock of Trenton, W. A Rothrock of Brick Church and Miss Mattie Rothrock, of Trenton, all of whom survive her.

The above from the book Giles County Genealogy in Newspapers, 1840-1898.
