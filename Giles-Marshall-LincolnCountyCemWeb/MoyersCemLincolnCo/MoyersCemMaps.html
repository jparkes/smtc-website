MOYERS CEMETERY, (Hwy 110, Taft), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Moyers Cemetery GPS location. 

Location: Take Highway 231 South from Fayetteville and turn right on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Hwy). Go past Skinem & also the Kirkland Baptist Church & Cemetery on the right and look for the cedar grove next to the road a 1/2 mile further on the left.
This Aerial Map shows the cedar grove on the edge of the Ardmore Highway.
