WALKER CEMETERY, Old Huntsville Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Walker as N 35.03069 X W -86.59985.

Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 to W. Lincoln Rd and turn west for a mile and then turn North (right) and go about 1/2 mile and the cemetery is just past a drive-way upon a bank and hard to spot because of hedgerows along the road. Since there is no good place to park along this road parking in the residential drivewayÂ  shown in these maps is a given. This cemetery is located in a very dense privy hedge so you will likely have to cut your way into it with some kind of brush cutters.
