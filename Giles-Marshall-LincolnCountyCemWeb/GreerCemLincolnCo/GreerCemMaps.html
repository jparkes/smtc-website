This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Greer Cemetery as N 35.32022 X W -86.58852.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 to Chestnut Ridge and turn left on Hwy 129 (Petersburg Chestnut Ridge Rd. Go about 2 miles and turn north (right) on Bledsoe Rd and go a few hundred yards and turn left on 3 hundred dollar road and then again 90 degrees left and go another few hundred yards and after that road then bends back right the cemetery is out in a field with access from a gate near there. There is a hedge row along the left side of that road making it hard to see the cemetery so look for the gate.

This map shows the two wrought iron fences enclosing the two areas of this graveyard, but, there may be unmarked graves outside the two fenced areas.
