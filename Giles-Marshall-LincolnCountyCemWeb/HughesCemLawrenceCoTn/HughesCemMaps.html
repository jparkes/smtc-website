HUGHES CEMETERY, (Sunrise Dr.) LAWRENCE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hughes Cemetery, eastern edge of Lawrence County as N 35.20650 X W -87.21577.Â 

Location: From Pulaski go west on Hwy 64 and and cross into Lawrence County and the next left is West Side road. Go left or south on that and you will encounter 1 90 degrees bends in the road back to the left (east). At the next bend turn left on Sunrise Drive and the cemetery is around hte corner lest to the road on the right side still in the edge of Lawrence County. Directions from Lawrenceburg: Location: From Lawrenceburg, go East on Hyw. # 64 for 6 1/2 miles, turn right on Miller Rd., go 1 l 1/2 miles. The cemetery is on the left, East of the house,
in a cedar grove about 500 yds from the road. Directions applicable about 1965.
