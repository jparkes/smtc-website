NEELY CEMETERY, (Hwy 110), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Neely Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go southwest on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Highway) about 8 miles. Go past Skinem, Kirkland Chapel Baptist Church & Mary's Grove Community. The cemetery is on your left just past Scott Orchard Rd next to a residence.
With this Aerial view from Microsoft we can see the cemetery marked with a red check on the north side of the road. Notice you can see the stones looking like small obstacles in this view.
