SWAN CREEK CHURCH CEMETERY,  Hopper Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Swan Creek Cemetery as N 35.18599 X W -86.73481.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64   more than 10 miles and turn left (south) on Swan Creek Road, Follow that for a mile and turn left on Hopper Road. After Crossing the Creek turn back left (north) into a residential Driveway of a new home. The young family (Cunningham's) there can grant you permission to visit the cemetery on the left side of their driveway about 200 yards back from their home. This is the same family whose ancestors lie in this cemetery so they own the cemetery too.

This shows the cemetery in a grove of trees. The Cunningham's Driveway to their new home follows along the right side of the trees & Cemetery. Please be respectful of their privacy.
