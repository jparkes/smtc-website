EUBANK CEMETERY, (Hanna Branch Road, west off of Hwy 166) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
WILLIAMS, Elizabeth Ann, wife of S. L. Williams,  9 Aug 1830 - 26 Jun 1903  (d/o Thomas and Nancy A. Graves Eubank)

Obituary: The Pulaski Citizen 2 Jul 1903 - Heron
 Mrs. Elizabeth Williams died suddenly Friday morning. As she failed to get out of bed at the
usual time, one of the family went to arouse her and found her dead. She was about 74 years
old, being the oldest daughter of Thomas and Nancy Eubank. Her remains were buried at the
family grave yard near here.
