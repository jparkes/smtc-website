HARWELL (Hartwell) CEMETERY,  Solomon RD., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 The Harwell, (Hartwell, Sr.) Cemetery with only tw of the stones having inscriptions on the right back and one stone on the right front. That leaves of the nine stones six which are not inscribed. They are: one fieldstone; four small stones the size of footstones; one large headstone without an inscription.  One of the stones had been hit by a speeding bullet. That is the one on the far left just above the weed. It gouged deep then ricocheted off.
