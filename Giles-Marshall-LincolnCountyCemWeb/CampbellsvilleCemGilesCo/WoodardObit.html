CAMPBELLSVILLE CEMETERY (Campbellsville) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 JOE ALTON WOODARD

Funeral services for Joe Alton Woodard, 26, who died at 7:30 o'clock Tuesday evening, Sept. 5, of a heart attack at his home near Bristol, Tennessee were held at 2:30 o'clock Thursday afternoon at the Union Valley Baptist Church with Rev. Horace Woodard, pastor and Rev. Loyd Hickman officiating. Burial was in the Campbellsville Cemetery.

Mr. Woodard is survived by his wife, Mrs. Ella Mae Skeins Woodard; two daughters, Deanna Lynn and Dathy Darlene Woodard; his parents, Mr. and Mrs. Robert Woodard of Campbellsville; two sisters, Mrs. Elmer Rose of Campbellsville and Mrs. Rogers Russell of Dellrose; and two brothers, Robert Felix Woodard of Bristol, and Rufus Wilkes Woodard of Campbellsville.

Pulaski Funeral Home was in charge of the arrangements.

Printed in the PULASKI RECORD, PULASKI, TENNESSEE 1950
