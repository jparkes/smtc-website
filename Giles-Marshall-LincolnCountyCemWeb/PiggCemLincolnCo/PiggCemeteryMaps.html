PIGG CEMETERY, (Boonshill-Petersburg Rd.), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
The center of the map marks the location of the Pigg Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. The GPS coordinates are correct as shown above freshly calculated from Aerial views, slightly different from the old Topographical calculation.


 To get to this cemetery from Fayetteville, go out Hwy 64 west toward Pulaski and turn north or right on Boonshill-Petersburg Rd. Follow that road for nearly 4 miles and turn left on Pigg Cemetery Road. Bear right at the bend in the road. The cemetery is on the right of the road at the crest of the hill.

This Aerial view shows the cemetery marked on the right of Pigg Cemetery Road with a red check.
