CAMPBELL (Hugh) CEMETERY, Poplar Hill Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hugh Campbell Cemetery as: N 35.02767 X W 86.97210

 Location: Highway 31S. Go all the way to Elkton and turn right on Hwy 273 (Elkton to Prospect Road) Go several miles to the Poplar Hill Road and turn left. Follow that passing by Hanna Ward Bridge Road on the left and  the Old Poplar School Road on the left at the Poplar Hill Cemetery.  Continue another 1/2 mile and turn left into Robert Liles long driveway with permission. The cemetery is northwest of his house about 100 yards) between the house and pasture.
