BLAKE CEMETERY, westside of Hwy 431, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE.
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Blake Cemetery as 35.27704 X -86.62978.

 Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431 about 9 miles and the cemetery is behind a home and between a cattle feed lot and driveway on the left. Permission is necessary to prevent disturbing the neighbors. There is no easy way to get into this cemetery as the rock wall is about 3 feet high in all places where humans can access it.
