NEW BEAR CREEK CHURCH CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE, near Mooresville.

Sarah J. Pickens memorial beside a 2008 springtime blooming of the Daffodils. We call these Buttercups here in the south.

PICKENS, Sarah J., 12 Feb 1839 - 12 Sep 1903. (I think she is the wife of Samuel D., but they are not buried together. However, they have individual memorials of the times and that may be the reason. [WA 3/10/08])
