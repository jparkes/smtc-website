EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

One of two standing memorials in this cemetery. A heavy infestation of Fire Ants subsist at the bas of the stone.

RATHER, James Thomas, son of W. B. & L.R. Rather, 10 Mar 1833 - 8 Oct 1833. Aged 7 months and 28 days.
