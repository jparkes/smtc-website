EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

The fallen memorial of Sallie C. Cartre. It is surrounded by a Fire Ant hill.

 CARTRE, Sallie C., 23 Apr 1848 - 16 Apr 1870. (believe the surname is a corruption of "Carter.")
This stone has been pushed over by cattle in spite of its concrete base.
