BROWN CEMETERY, (Good Billy, Hwy 166, Al State Line), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Standing on the State Line of Tennessee and photographing the sign at the junction of Hwy  127/166 & Brown Cemetery Road also known as Alsup Road. We are looking north. 

Standing on the Tennessee/Alabama State Line near Hwy  127/166 photographing the area where the Brown Cemetery can be found in the distance about 600 yards out across the creek just beyond the tree line. We are looking east but to the north at the arrow. There is a road and  a bridge across the creek to the cemetery.
