BROWN CEMETERY, (Good Billy, Hwy 166, Al State Line), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE


Located on Highway 166/127 south nearly to state line. Turn left at the state line into shown as the Alsup or labeled as the Brown Cemetery Road a small gravel (still gravel) in 2009. Go about 400 yards and turn back (left) north into the cemetery road. Follow that across the concrete bridge and wind on around to the cemetery. Cemetery is fenced & pad locked -  permission is needed. 

With this aerial view we can see the tree line and the bridge across the creek and the path of the cemetery road.
