TACKER CEMETERY, Apple Hill Rd) GILES COUNTY TN

Since we are still looking for this cemetery on private land this map is good for discerning where the hills are located since most cemeteries are located upon higher ground & above the flood plain.
Location: Take Highway 31 north out of Pulaski and go about 10 miles and turn left on Apple Hill Road Take 1st dirt road to right. We offer no GPS location here because this cemetery has not been mapped in the past on USGS records. Thus we only know the vague directions printed in the book Giles County Tennessee Cemeteries by the Historical Society about 1986.
Perhaps this Aerial Map shows the dirt road in the curve of the road where this cemetery can be found but this is unproven and may not be helpful. While visiting the Tackett Cemetery I did ask Mr. Arnold Williams about the location of this cemetery and he was not aware of it. By the way since I made this map the yellow plot is not the location of this cemetery as that is in the creek bed and low ground - not a place to find a cemetery.
