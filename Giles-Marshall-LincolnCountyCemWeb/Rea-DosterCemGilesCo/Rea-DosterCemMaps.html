REA-DOSTER CEMETERY (Rea Branch Road), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Rea-Doster Cemetery GPS location.
Location: Take Highway 64 west toward Lawrenceburg. Turn north after about a mile on Hwy 166 or Campbellsville Road and go to Campbellsville then pass on by Campbellsville. Continue about another mile and turn left (west) on Rea Branch Road. Follow that for a half mile or so. When you can see a clump of trees back up a slope across the creek beside a house on your right the cemetery is back into those trees behind a broken down rock wall. The land owner lives on the left side of the road the house shown below and the house across the road is a rental house where the cemetery is northeast of.

This Aerial Map shows the red check marking the forest where the cemetery will be found.  The cemetery is about 175 yards from the road and with permission go into the drive, cross the creek, and park next to the gate.
