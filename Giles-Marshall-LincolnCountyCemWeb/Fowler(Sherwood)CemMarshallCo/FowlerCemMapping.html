FOWLER CEMETERY, Hwy 431, MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE
The GPS coordinates for the Sherwood Fowler Cemetery are shown on the above map as N 35.39602 X W -86.81493. 
Located on Highway 431 about 3 miles north of Lewisburg on the right (west side).

With this aerial view we can see that the grves are laid out east to west but the land area is perpendicular to the Highway. The graves are back side of this property around the large Yellow Poplar tree.
