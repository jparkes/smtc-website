ISOM (James)CEMETERY, (Mimosa Road), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Isom Cemetery.

 Location: From Fayetteville eastern bypass go north on 231 and after a short distance turn right Old Mulberry Road and follow that for a short distance and turn left on Mimosa Road and follow that several miles. After you pass Golf road on the left go 1/4 mile and turn into the driveway to a house and farm. Cross the creek and continue to go straight bearing out into the field and go straight toward the distant hay storage shed. The cemetery will be on the east side of the shed in the thicket.

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery marked with a red check. You can follow the outlined road above to get to this cemetery though it is further that way. Also the (short) other way may be trampling on someone's hay crop.

I have outlined the path in yellow I took to get to this cemetery. I was shown where this cemetery is located with this path but am not aware of the location of the actual right of way. This is private property permission is always necessary or at least a courtesy.
