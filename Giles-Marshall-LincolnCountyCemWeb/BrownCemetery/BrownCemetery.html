BROWN CEMETERY, (Hwy 31, Old Lynnville), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
Map of the Cemetery location


BROWN, J. M., 8 Sep 1824 - 15 Nov 1884, 60 yrs, 2 mo, 7 days.
 BROWN, Malinda, wife of J. M. Brown, 19 Nov 1827 - 14 Aug 1876, 48 yrs, 8 mo, 25 days.

 COMPTON, John I. (Isaac), 10 Aug 1852 - 9 Jul 1890. 37 yrs, 11 mo, 1 days. (Son of Berryman Compton & Elizabeth Jane Hamrick; mar. Mary S. Ann Brown on 3 Nov 1878 in Giles Co. Tn. Ref; Rick Gray.)
COMPTON, Mary A. (Brown), wife of J. I. Compton 4 Apr 1853 - 29 Jun 1888, 35 yrs, 2 mo, 25 days. (died around the same time as her infant daughter. Ref: Rick Gray)
COMPTON, Infant daughter of J. I. & M. A. Compton,  20 Jun 1888 - 25 of Jun 1888 (died around the same time as her mother Mary A. Compton.)
 KELTNER, Martha Ann, wife of B. F. Keltner, 6 Jan 1847 - 6 Sep 1895, 48 yrs, 8 no. (note: B. F. Keltner was buried in Manor, Texas)
