BROWN CEMETERY, (Hwy 31, Old Lynnville), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Our focus is on the small Compton infant stone inside the southwest corner of the wrought iron fence.

Compton Infant who died 4 days before her mother.

COMPTON, Infant daughter of J. I. & M. A. Compton, 20 Jun 1888 - 25 of Jun 1888 (died around the same time as her mother Mary A. Compton.)
