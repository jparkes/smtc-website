ROUTT-WELLS CEMETERY, just S of Fayetteville on E. side of Hwy 231) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Routt-Wells Cemetery. A more precise GPS reading based on the calculation from ACME Mapper 2.0 is: N 35.11054 X W 56.56546.
Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 past the Ardmore Hwy 110 turnoff on your left. Go another 2/3 of a mile and look to your left as the road bank becomes eye level and you will see a parking place which is the remainder of the old road bed. Stop and park there dn the cemetery is out east from there.
This Aerial Map shows, marked with a yellow graphic outline the cemetery with most of the few stones out in the upper right area.
