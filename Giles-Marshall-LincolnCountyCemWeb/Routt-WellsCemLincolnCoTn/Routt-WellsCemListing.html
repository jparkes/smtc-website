This graveyard is about a 3 acre area that may includes many graves unmarked. Some are monuments that fell and crumbled to dust. Others were only marked with fieldstone without inscriptions. The fieldstones that mark graves without inscription tend to fall and get picked up by folks that are not so astute and piled into a corner or by a tree.  This converts them back to common boulders which changes the graveyard makeup to cause it look much smaller. Often the maintenance crew moves them to make it easier to mow.
