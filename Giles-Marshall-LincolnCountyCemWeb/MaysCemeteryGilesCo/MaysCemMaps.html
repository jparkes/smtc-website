MAYS-McCRACKEN CEMETERY, (Bunker Hill Rd. next to I-65) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Mays-McCracken Cemetery. 

From Pulaski go east on Hwy 64 nearly to the I-65 and turn south on Bunker Hill Road. Continue on Bunker Hill Road going under I-65 and after that the road bends back to the south (right) go 200 yards and pass a house and look left into the open field and the cemetery will be inside a fence. I believe permission can be granted at the next house on the road.
