SWINEBROAD CEMETERY, Boonshill, Red Hat Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Swinebroad Cemetery as N 35.21872 X W -86.75779.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn right (north) on the the road that takes you to the Boonshill village when that road dead ends turn left (west) and go thru Boohshill, past the church and Cemetery on the left and continue on for nearly a mile and the cemetery is off the road in the west side front part of the yard of a residential home and horse farm.
