Louis Nicholson Cemetery
Additional information for Louis Nicholson Listing:

NICHOLSON, Louis, 15 Mar 1886 - 7 Jan 1978. Son of Jim Nicholson a former slave on the Nicholson Plantation in the Rock Springs area of Maury County.

The following are known to have been buried here without markers:

1) FITZPATRICK, Jim, No Dates - Local history tells us he gave the land for this cemetery
2) FITZPATRICK, Anne, No Dates, Wife of Jim Fitzpatrick. No Marker
3) MAYBERRY, Tip, No Dates. No Marker
4) MAYBERRY, ?????, No Dates - Wife of Tip Mayberry. No Marker
5) MAYBERRY , Kelly, No Dates - Son of Tip Mayberry. No Marker
6) MAYBERRY, ?????, No Dates - Wife of Kelly Mayberry. No Marker
7) MAYBERRY , Roscoe, No Dates - Son of Kelly Mayberry. No Marker
8) HARDISON, Hamp, No Dates. Son of Lettie and Sam Hardison (they are buried in Joshua Hardison Cemetery approx. a mile away) . No Marker (He died in an accident on the Humphrey Hardison (1804-1874) Farm in the Leftwich Community of Maury County.)
