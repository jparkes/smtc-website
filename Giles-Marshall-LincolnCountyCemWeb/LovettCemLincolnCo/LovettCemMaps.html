This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Lovett Cemetery as N 35.21760 X W -86.80421.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 about 8 miles and turn right (north on Chicken Creek Rd. Follow it and it will bend back west then back south as it makes a loop back to Hwy 64. After about 2 miles on that you will cross on your right Lovett Cemetery Road. Take that turning sharply back to the right (northward) about a 1/2 mile and the cemetery will be on your left next to the road.
