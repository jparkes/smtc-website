PAMPLIN CEMETERY (Hwy 50 & Providence Rd, Fayetteville) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Pamplin Cemetery as N 35.16735 X W 86.52512.

 Location: From Fayetteville take Hwy 64 east 1+ mile and turn north (left) on the Lynchburg Highway (Hwy 50) then after 1/2 mile turn right on Providence Rd and with permission turn into the first private drive on the right. If the owner is available, you will be shown the cemetery on the east side of the home about 100 yards out in a field just west of a small pond. It is under a large hackberry Tree inside a white washed wrought iron fence.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with a yellow cross out in a field beside a house. The home between Providence Rd and the cemetery is incomplete and abandoned.
