 MOORE, Margaret, (c 1825) - d. 1 Nov 1910. age 84y, 11m, 27d.  8435a

MARGARET STRONG MOORE
Fayetteville Observer
Nov. 10 1910

Yukon (Community)

The Death Angel has again been in our midst, and took away on Tuesday, Nov. 1, Mrs. Margaret Moore, widow of the late Robert Moore, who preceded her to the grave nearly two years ago. She lacked only three days of being 85 years of age, therefore old age was the principal cause of her death. Only a short time ago she had gone to live with her son, William, but God in his wisdom saw fit to call her to another and better home, as we believe, where she will be free from sickness and pain, and where she will need the hand of children, grandchildren and others to minister to her needs. For many years she was a constant member of the Prosperity church, where the remains were laid to rest on Wednesday afternoon after funeral services by Rev. I. N. Kennedy.)
Obit furnished by MB McClain Richardson (no relation) 5 Apr 2013
