LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

WALLS, James Clyde, 26 Jun 1944 - 7 Sep 2014. (mar. to Brenda L. Walls) section F 5294

Obituary

J.C. Walls, age 70 of Pulaski, passed away Wednesday September 17, 2014 at his home.
Mr. Walls was born June 26, 1944 in Pulaski, TN to Walter Leslie and Mary Suda Elaine Garner Walls. He was the retired owner of Walls Exxon and Auto Repair, member of the Army National Guard and preceded in death by his parents, his wife Brenda Walls and grandson Justin Ray (Stoney) Estes.
Visitation will be Friday September 19, 2014 from 3 till 8 p.m. at Carr & Erwin Funeral Home. Funeral will be 2 p.m. Saturday September 20, 2014 at Carr & Erwin Chapel with interment in Lynnwood Cemetery.
Survivors include his sons Keith Walls and wife Julie of Pulaski, Jason Walls and wife Amelia of Ardmore, Rodney Fry and wife Lachelle of Pulaski, Dawson Walls of Pulaski, daughters Tina Glover and husband Bobby of Goodspring, Mellissa Estes and husband Wendell of Pulaski, brothers Joe Walls of Pulaski, Leslie Walls of Minor Hill, sisters Juanita Jones of Pulaski, Alvie Wilburn of Prospect, Mary Zeigler of Chattanooga, grandchildren Matt Glover and wife Talia, Jacob Glover, Rachel Glover, Kerri Walls, Tyler Walls, Chuck Estes and wife Tanna, Chad Estes, Martha Fry, and three great grandchildren.
Carr & Erwin Funeral Home in charge of arrangements.
