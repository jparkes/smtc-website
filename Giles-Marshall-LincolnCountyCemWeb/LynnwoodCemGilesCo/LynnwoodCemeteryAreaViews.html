LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE


Looking northwest this 1994 Springtime scanned photo captures the beauty & colors of the cemetery realistically with a Canon AE1 film camera. Here we see a very large cemetery occupied by folks as far away as about 15 miles. That may be partly because the cemetery is well organized with adequate spacing between graves and nice even rows. Since then this cemetery has undergone a significant expansion.
