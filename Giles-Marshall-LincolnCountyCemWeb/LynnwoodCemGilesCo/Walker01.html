LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

WALKER, Andrew Kelsey, d. 30 Jun 2010. (Maury Co. native; s/o Fate & Susie Hobbs Guerrero Walker.)
Obituary - Marshall County Tribune

Andrew Kelsey Walker, 36, of Columbia, died Wednesday, June 30, 2010.
Mr. Walker was born in Maury County and grew up in Giles County. He was the son of Fate Walker of Lynnville and Susie Hobbs Guerrero of Lewisburg.
In addition to his parents, he is survived by his wife, Suzette Dunavant Walker, son, Lafayette Wade Walker, and daughter, Brittany Ann Walker, all of Columbia; one brother, Kevin (Meredith) Walker of Lynnville; and one sister, Kimberly (Chad) Roder of Linden.
He was preceded in death by his paternal grandparents, Edward and Mary Walker, and maternal grandparents, Dean and Ima Jean Hobbs.
The family will be at London Funeral Home from 4 until 8 p.m. Friday, July 2, for visitation.
Funeral services will be conducted at 11 a.m. Saturday, July 3, from the chapel of London Funeral Home with burial to follow in Lynnwood Cemetery.

Information submitted by Mary Bob McClain 8 Feb 2015.
