GREEN, Sara Hammond, 5 Aug 1844 - 25 Aug 1925. "Mother" (married to C.C. Green)

MRS. SARAH H. GREEN
Columbia, Tenn., Aug. 27 - Funeral services were held for Mrs. Sarah Hammond Green, 81, at the residence Wednesday. Mrs. Green, wife of Curtis C. Green, had been a resident where she died for nearly half a century. In addition to her husband she is survived by the following children: Mrs. J. Snowdey Hickman, Cross Bridges, Mrs. Will Dugger and Mrs. Luther Hubble of this (Giles) county; J. S. Green of Franklin, Dr. J. B. Green of Columbia, S.C.; W. M. Green of Broadview, and Robert C. Green of Columbia. Grandchildren were pallbearers.
Wayne Austin, Mary Bob McClain, Trish Hollaway,
