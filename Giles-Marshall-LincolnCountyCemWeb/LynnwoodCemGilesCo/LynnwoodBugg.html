LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Robert Malone Bugg Jr. 5 Mar 1838 - 5 Apr 1928
son of Robert Malone Bugg & Martha Laird
Married Fanny S. Owens in Hays, Texas on 24 Dec 1867

Robert lived in Texas from 1858 to 1868 and served during the Civil War in the Texas 23rd Cavalry, Co. H and in Texas' McCulloch's 1st Cavalry, Co. D. He was elected to the 53rd Tennessee General Assembly (1903-1905 as a Democratic Representative from Giles County, TN.
