LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

ALDRED, Kathleen C. (Cross), 18 May 1926 - 8 Aug 2014. (Eastern Star emblem) " Wed Nov 27, 1947" (mar. to William L. Aldred Sr.) section E

 Aldred, Kathleen C., Obituary

Kathleen (Cross) Aldred was born about 1925, the daughter of Melvin Carver Cross and Lou Ellen (Carpenter) Cross of Pulaski, Tennessee.
Married to William L Aldred Sr, she was a Girl Scout Leader for many years, member of the Eastern Star, and she and her husband were active members of The First United Methodist Church in Hermitage, Tennessee for 38 years.
Kathleen was a devoted housewife and mother who valued family. She and her husband enjoyed camping, traveling, boating, church activities, and time spent with family.
She was 88 years old when she passed away on August 8, 2014.
Preceded in death by her husband, she is survived by a married son; a daughter; three grandsons; three granddaughters; and seven great-grandchildren.
Burial was in Lynnwood Cemetery, where Kathleen will be buried beside her late husband.

Original obituary in The Tennessean on August 13, 2014 Information submitted by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 27 Jan 2015.
