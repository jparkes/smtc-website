ANTHONY CEMETERY, (Crook Hill Road, East side of road), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

KIMBROUGH, Infant daughter of F. F. & V. S. Kimbrough, 1925. (Two separate stones with same information) 9233/9234/9231C
The two infants' names are not on the stones and both of them were born and died in 1925. Here is the information:
1. Kimbrough infant daughter, Born January 1, 1925, Died January 1, 1925, named Susan Jane Kimbrough, d/o Floyd Forest and Venice Sally Miller Kimbrough.
2. Kimbrough infant daughter, Born September 21, 1925, Died September 22, 1925, named Mary Catherine Kimbrough d/o Floyd Forest and Venice Sally Miller Kimbrough.
We do not know which stone applies to which child.
