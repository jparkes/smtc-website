McDANIEL (John) CEMETERY, Delina-Boonshill Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the John McDaniel Cemetery as N 35.23550 X W -86.72446.

 Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 to Boonshill-Delna Road and turn left. Follow that and  it will bend back north. Then go about a mile and the cemetery is upon a bank above the road on your right. The property surrounding the cemetery is actually on the property of a home that is 300 yards further down the road. They or someone are maintaining the cemetery by keeping lime grave spread over the cemetery.
This Aerial Map shows, marked with a yellow graphic outline the cemetery behind a home that is not on the property the cemetery is located. The Owners home is further north up the road.
