CARVELL CEMETERY, (Apple Hill Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 The GPS coordinates for the Carvell Cemetery are shown on the above map. Obviously these people interred in this cemetery made their living farming the rich bottom land of the Minnow Branch Creek watershed shown spanning northeast in the same direction as Minnow Branch Road (shown on maps and road signage as the Apple Hill Road.)
Location: Highway 64 West right into Campbellsville Road. After many miles turn right on Minnow Branch Road. Turn right again at the fork in road onto Apple Hill Rd. Go 1 tenth of a mile and look left just past a grown up fence line spanning north from the road and the cemetery is in the northeast corner where the fence line meets the tree line inside a chain link fence. The many streams that come together here provided sustenance to many pioneers in olden days. This is highlighted indicated by the number of burial grounds in this area as may be seen on the above MS Streets Map. This is northeast of Campbellsville Tennessee about 3 miles.

In this aerial view we see the brushy fence line spanning northwest from the Apple Hill Road. The cemetery is on the right side of that fence row in the corner just before the forest line on private land. It is pointed out with a yellow pointer.
