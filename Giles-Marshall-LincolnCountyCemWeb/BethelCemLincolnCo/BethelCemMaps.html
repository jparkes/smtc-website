BETHEL CEMETERY (Old Bethel Rd, West), western LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Bethel Cemetery GPS location. Actually Fayetteville is the nearest Town about 5 miles northeast from here. 

Location: Take Highway 64 out of Fayetteville and go several miles and turn south (left) on Old Bethel Road. Go about 1 and 1/2 miles and the cemetery is on your right just before you reach Gunter Hollow Road.
This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next Old Bethel Road indicated with a yellow pointer. Most of the cemetery is in the clearing but parts are in the tree line west and south of the clearing. This is a very old and fragile cemetery with stones easily damaged by overzealous people so be gentle on these soft monuments if you have business here.
