Clear Creek Church (Christian) Cemetery, McNairy County, Tennessee


The tall but fallen (into four pieces) memorial of R.W. Michie.

Michie, R. W., Apr. 26, 1816 - Oct. 21, 1902. (married to Eliza Dameron; R.W. was a minister of the Clear Creek Church of Christ here at Stantonville. The census of 1860 confirms his occupation was Christian Clergy.)
