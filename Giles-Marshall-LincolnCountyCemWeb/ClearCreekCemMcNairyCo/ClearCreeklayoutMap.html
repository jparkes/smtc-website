Clear Creek Church (Christian) Cemetery, McNairy County, Tennessee

The front side is near the entrance to the road and the back side appears further across than across the front side. Area 1 includes a large tree and is the newer section. Area 2 is also new, but gets quickly back into the stones of the early 1900s. Area 3 has the oldest stones with death dates into the 1860s. Area 4 has a large cedar & pine tree thicket on its left edge and has some damage to the stones due to fallen trees breaking up the stones. There is one ornamental iron fence (the Kerr plots) with one stone with two inscriptions. There may be other graves in that fence but not many. The size would not justify that.
