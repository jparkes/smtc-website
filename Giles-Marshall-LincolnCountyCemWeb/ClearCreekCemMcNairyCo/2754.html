Clear Creek Church (Christian) Cemetery, McNairy County, Tennessee

Fullwood, Samuel M., Dec. 2, 1811 - Nov. 23, 1896.Born in NC, removed here about 1849.
Fullwood, Annie B. Howard, Feb. 10, 1812 - Jun. 10, 1884. married Samuel M. Fullwood Oct 17, 1831.Born in NC, moved here about 1849.

Inscription:
 "FULLWOOD
Samuel M. Fullwood Born in North Carolina Dec 2, 1811 - Jan 23, 1896. 
Annie B. Howard Fullwood. Born in North Carolina, Feb 10, 1812 - June 10 1884.
They were married Oct 17, 1831 in Mecklenburg Co. North Carolina. 
Samuel & Annie B. Settled here about 1849. Their descendents include most of those in this area with the surname Fullwood or Fulwood."
Photo and information by Wayne Austin, July 18, 2008.
