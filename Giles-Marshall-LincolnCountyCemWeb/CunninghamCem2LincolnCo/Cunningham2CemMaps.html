CUNNINGHAM CEMETERY #2, (Circle Dr., NE of Fayetteville), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

 This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Cunningham Cem #2 as N 35.17223 X W -86.55167.

 Location: From Fayetteville at junction of Hwy 64 & the Bypass (Thornton Taylor Pky) go north 1/4 mile and turn right (north) on Old Mulberry Road. Follow that more than 1/2 mile and after it bends back east you will turn north (left) on Longview Ave. Follow that subdivision road until it become Circle Dr. When Circle Dr bends starts to back to the right look left and you will see the rocks on the left side of the road. The cemetery is under those trees. None of the stones are standing so you will be unable to see the cemetery until you go out and examine the grounds where the two monuments lye on the ground. My opinion is there are two remaining memorials here with two headstones and one footstone for one of the parties. Looks like someone has removed all the fieldstones marking graves and piled them next to a tree.
