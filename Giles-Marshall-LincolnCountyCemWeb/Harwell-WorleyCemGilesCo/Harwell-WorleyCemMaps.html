HARWELL-WORLEY CEMETERY, I-65 Access Road, west side I-65@Hwy64, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Harwell-Worley Cemetery as N 35.19964 X W -86.87154. 

From Pulaski go east on Hwy 64 toward I-65. At the junction while still on Hwy 64 on the west side of I-65 take the access road left (north) and go 1/2 mile to a home. The cemetery is on the right (north) side of the home in the pasture just across the fence line. You will only see minimal evidence of a cemetery here as noted on the menu page mainly because the owner of the land keeps it mowed down as a pasture. He does not wish it to grow up as a thicket. This is the instructions given in the Giles County Tennessee Cemetery book about 1988 Location: Hwy 64E, left into access road at interstate on Fly Farm. The gentleman said he had lived here 60 years so might we assume he is Mr. Fly?
