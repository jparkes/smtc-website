 Here is information for Benjamin M. Scoggin. It is family info and then I include his obituary from The Academist in Lawrence County.
 
He married Martha Abernathy on 17 Dec 1814 in Brunswick County, Virginia. He was the son of Gardner and Martha Lucy Scoggin.

The Academist (Lawrence County, TN), 1 April 1846:

Died - At his residence, in this county on the 20th inst. of Pulmonary Consumption, Mr. Benjamin M. Scoggin, in the 57th year of his age. (Pulaski Whig Courier)
