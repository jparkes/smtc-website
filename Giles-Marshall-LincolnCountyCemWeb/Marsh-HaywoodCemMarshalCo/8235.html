MARSH-HAYWOOD CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE


Sarah B. Haywood. 
HAYWOOD, Sarah B., wife of Dr. G. W. Haywood, Oct 25, 1809 - Apr 16, 1894, Age 84 yrs, 5 mos. 22 days. I am uncertain if the Initials are CW or GW for Dr Haywood. Mr Whitsell believed it was GW. 
Photos dated 29 Nov 2005 & information by Wayne Austin, 14 Jul 2008.
