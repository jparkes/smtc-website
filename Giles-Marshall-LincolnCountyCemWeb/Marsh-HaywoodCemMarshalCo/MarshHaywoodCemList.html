Last known listing is on Page 210 of the book Marshall County Tennessee Cemeteries 1968, by Tim & Helen Marsh. This cemetery was last transcribed by Mr. Ralph D. Whitesell during 1958 and included by Tim & Helen in their book.
Whitesell said there were 55 unmarked fieldstones in this cemetery. That is no longer apparent. Livestock has freely roamed this cemetery in the past and dislocated the stones.
The listings above without a photo are from this book, but not found in 2005 or 2008. Visited here Nov 11, 2005, again 17 Jul 2008, Wayne Austin. Published here July 15, 2008.
