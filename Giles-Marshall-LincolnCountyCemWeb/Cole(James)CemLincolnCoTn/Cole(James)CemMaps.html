COLE (JAMES) CEMETERY (Bellview Rd, S. of Kirkland) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the James Cole Cemetery as N 35.05574 X W -86.62622.Â 

 Location: Go south on Hwy 231 from Fayetteville and turn right on the Ardmore Highway (Hwy 110). Follow that more than 5 miles to Kirkland and turn south on Bellview Rd. Go abt 1/2 mile & the cemetery is behind an old home on the right on private property. The cemetery to be on the next property south of the old home and better access is from their driveway, but be sure to get permission or your motives will be questioned.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under an old Cedar tree, among other Cedar trees around, and just west from the landowner's home.
