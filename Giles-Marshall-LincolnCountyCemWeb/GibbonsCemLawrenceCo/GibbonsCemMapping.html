GIBBONS CEMETERY, Revilo Road, LAWRENCE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Gibbons Cemetery. This is south east of Lawrenceburg near the Giles County line. The GPS as calculated by C. Wayne Austin: N 35.05740 W -87.26309.

Location: From Leoma, take the Rabbit Trail Rd., to Five Points. Take the road (left) going East and go 3/4 mile to a sharp curve. Go North 3/4 mile, turn east again, go 3/4 mile to forks of road, take right fork labeled above as Blooming-Revilo Road, go 1 1/4 mile, take left road continuing on the same road name. Go one mile to the Norwood farm on the right of road. Walk to the cemetery on the hill behind the barn.Â
