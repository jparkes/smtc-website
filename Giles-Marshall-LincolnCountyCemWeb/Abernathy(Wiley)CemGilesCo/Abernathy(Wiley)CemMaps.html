ABERNATHY (Wiley)- FRAN ZONA CEMETERY (Old Campbellsville Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Abernathy (Wiley) Family Cemetery.Â 

 Location: Go west on Highway 64 (toward Lawrenceburg) from Pulaski and turn north on Campbellsville Rd (Hwy 166). Go several miles and turn right on Hannah Road (or at least the left turn is the Hannah Road) pass the Old Campbellsville Road and continue on Gunter Smith Road 6/10 of a mile and turn right into the old Abernathy home place and the cemetery is on the left out in a field. With Permission walk thru the gate closing it behind you and across the field to the cemetery. The road is not active to the cemetery. I believe they have sheep farming in progress here.
