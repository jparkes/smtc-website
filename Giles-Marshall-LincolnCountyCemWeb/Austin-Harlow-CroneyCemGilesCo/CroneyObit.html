AUSTIN/HARLOW/CRONEY CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CRONEY, Delia, 4 Sep 1874 - 22 Jun 1937. "We go to dwell with Jesus, On that bright eternal shore." Footstone: "D.C." (shares stone with J.B. Croney.)

Â OBITUARY: The Pulaski Citizen, Wednesday, Jun 23, 1937
Mrs. J. B. Croney, 62, died Tuesday (June 22) morning at the home of her daughter, Mrs. O. S. Dollar at Elkton. Death followed a three months illness. She was a member of the United Methodist Church. Funeral at 10 o'clock Wednesday morning at the Dollar home by Rev. W. E. Couser. Burial at Elkton.
Mrs. Croney is survived by ten children: Mrs. O. S. Dollar, T. C. Croney, Elkton, Mrs. J. C. Ramsey, Delrose, J. C. Croney, Nashville, Mrs. T. F. Davis, Howard Croney, Mrs. W. V. Ferrell, Alton Croney, Sheffield, Mrs. Albert Pack, Toledo, Ohio, Joe D. Croney, Elkton. Three brothers, Atha Hobbs, Tanner, Ala., Ofus Hobbs and Cleban Hobbs, both of Ardmore.
