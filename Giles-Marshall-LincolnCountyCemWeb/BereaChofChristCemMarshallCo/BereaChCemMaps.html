From the above map we are given the Berea Church Cemetery GPS location.
Location: Located at Berea Church of Christ, one-half mile north of Verona on the right on Hwy 272 (or the Verona Caney Road).

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery in clear view with the monuments showing up as fuzzy white spots surrounded by the drive around the cemetery premises. Notice the church top showing up as a black shadow here with cars parked around probably related to a gathering the day this was photographed by satellite.
Do not go to this place if your intentions are not honorable. Respect must be shown at all times for those gone before us by respecting their monuments & memorials.
