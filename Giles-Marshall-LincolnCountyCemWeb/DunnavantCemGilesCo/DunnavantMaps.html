DUNNAVANT CEMETERY, Newman Rd, near Elkton, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Interstate 65 take Exit 6 and go south west about 1.5 miles to Elkton, Tn. Turn right (north) on Highway 31 and go about 2 miles and turn right on Newman Rd. Go about 1/2 (bearing right) mile and the cemetery is on your left under a large stand of Cedar Trees.

This clearly shows the stand of trees the cemetery is located in. Some maps incorrectly show this cemetery on the right side of Newman Road.
