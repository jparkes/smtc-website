SHULER-PARKER CEMETERY, Shuler Branch Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Shuler-Parker Cemetery. as N 35.35973 X W -87.16439. The old home can be seen northeast of the cemetery.

Location: Go west on Hwy 64 from Pulaski a mile or so and turn right (north) on Hwy 166. Travel that many miles thru Campbellsville and eventually passing the intersection of Hwy 245 on the right then just a little further up 166 look for Shuler Branch road on the left. Turn left there and go just over a mile and look for the driveway into the old Shuler Home back on the right (north) far off the road. It is private property. With permission go across the pasture on the left up the slope toward the clump of Cedar & other trees in the distance and the cemetery is inside that clump behind a barb wire fence. Seen as Parker Cemetery in the above map.
