SULLIVAN CEMETERY, (Hamestring Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sullivan Cemetery in the Mulberry Quadrant.Â 

From Fayetteville go north on Shelbyville Road and merge onto old Mulberry Road. Follow that for a mile or so and turn left on Mimosa Road. Go several miles and turn right on Hamestring Road and go about a mile and 1/2 and the cemetery is up the slope behind the old fallen Sullivan home and behind a modular home.

This aerial map shows the assets as I have them labeled. This is private property along the road to the cemetery so notify the residents to keep from alarming them.
