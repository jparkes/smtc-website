GARRISON CEMETERY, (Lofton Hall Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Garrison Family Cemetery.Â Notice it is in the clump of trees shown above on private land.

 Location: From Elkton go east on Baugh Road about 3 miles to Lofton Hall Rd. Turn south (or right). Go 1/2 mile and the cemetery is on the right out in a field about 60 yards. There is a gate there just north of the cemetery which has a field road to the cemetery. Be sure to close the gate behind you and not to trample on the crops or let out the cows getting there.

This aerial map shows the cemetery west of the road just after the 1st bend in the road to the right on Lofton Hall Road.
