KING CEMETERY, Hwy 129 adjacent to Harkins Cem, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Harkins Cemetery as N 35.31778 X W -86.55412.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Highway 231 and go upon the Chestnut Ridge. Turn left on Hwy 129 Petersburg Highway and go about 1 and 1/2 mile and the cemetery is on the left (south) side of the road at the junction of Bolles Hollow Lane It is about 100 yards due south of the Harkins Cemetery.

Here we see the Gammill Stone and others of the fallen stones of the Kings and their relatives.
