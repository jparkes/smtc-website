This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hall Cemetery as N 35.26979 X W -86.62224.

Location: From Fayetteville go north west on Hwy 431 about 7 miles and turn right on old Petersburg Pike. After that road turns back south look out into the field at a grove of trees behind a rock wall where the cemetery is located. There is a gate in the corner of the field which serves the cemetery but be sure to close it behind you at all times.
