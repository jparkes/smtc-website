BUCHANAN CEMETERY Hwy 64 East, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
 


WARD, Julia M. Reynolds, Dec 21, 1892 - Apr 21, 1913.  d/o Buckner Reynolds; w/o James Milton Ward, m.  May 21, 1909


She died at age 21 of infection secondary to cut on hand received at church outing. Daughter of Buckner Reynolds; married James Milton Ward May 21, 1909; had daughter, Madeline born March 21, 1910. James married secondly Eva Dunnivant and they are buried in Rose Hill Cemetery, Fayetteville,TN
