REYNOLDS, Eva Hoots, 1865 - Dec 28, 1908, (age not given)
 
Eva Hoots was the third child of John and Harriette Hill Hoots of Mulberry, TN where John was a blacksmith. Their children were Richard, Corrina, Eva, Cora, Julia and John,Jr.
Eva married George Buckner Reynolds in Lincoln County on 6 Feb 1884. Together they had five chiildren:
Mary Reynolds Honey 1854-1957
Ola Reynolds Honey 1889-1977
Julia Reynolds Ward 1892-1913
Clyde Reynolds 1897-1971 (Kelso Cemetery) Lincoln Cry TN
Henry Reynolds 1900-1983 (Mountain View Cemetery in Tacoma WA)
Eva, a widow living with one of her children, died 28 Dec 1908 in Fayetteville, TN of consumption at age 43.
