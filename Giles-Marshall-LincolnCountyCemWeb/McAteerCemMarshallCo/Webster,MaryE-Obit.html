McATEER CEMETERY, Hobo Shaw Rd., MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE


Mary A. Webster
Birth: 1813
Death: May, 1910, Yell, Marshall Co., TN

Mrs Mary A. Webster, aged 87 years, died at her home near Yell Saturday night of a complication of diseases due to old age. She was a highly respected and a Christian
woman. Her remains were laid at rest in McAteer graveyard. - Lewisburg Tribune, May 1910
