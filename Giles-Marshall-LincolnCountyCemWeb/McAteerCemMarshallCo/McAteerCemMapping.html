McATEER CEMETERY, Hobo Shaw Rd., MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates needed to find this cemetery as N 35.40041 X W -86.82769

From Lewisburg go southwest on Hwy 31 Alt (Hwy 11) to Hobo Shaw Rd and turn left (west). Cross the RR tracks and after a half mile and before the road bends back south (left) look for the road (right) north into the cemetery. Crossing locked gates is a necessity to visit this cemetery.

This map shows a rough outline set in a yellow graphic line of the cemetery. Notice the stones are back from the east side of the cemetery under the trees.
