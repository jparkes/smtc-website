SMITH (Wm G.), Old Pulaski Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the William G. Smith Cemetery as N 35.17856 X W -86.82066 as calculated by C. Wayne Austin from ACME Mapper 2.1

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 several miles to the McBurg Community and turn left on the Old Hwy 64. Go past the McBurg Methodist Church 500 yards and the cemetery is south of the road on the Stevenson farm about 1/3 of a mile from the road. The graves are inside a block wall. It is far across the road from theÂ  Rambo Cemetery. Access seems closer from the McBurg/Dellrose Road but the property is not owned by the families along the west side of that road. Mr Stevenson is a likeable person and if you can work around his busy farm schedule he will probably accompany you to this cemetery. Otherwise with his permission you can visit here, but there are gates, live cattle and small creeks to be properly dealt with in making the long trek from the public road.
