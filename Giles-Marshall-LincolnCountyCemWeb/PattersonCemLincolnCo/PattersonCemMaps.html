PATTERSON CEMETERY, Cheatham Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Patterson Cemetery. It is marked with a red check as GPS N 35.09245 X W -86.792005.
Location: Going toward Fayetteville turn S. (right) off of Old Elkton Pike onto Cheatham Rd and cross the Elk River Bridge and go about 1/2 mile further. Next look to your right out into the field and the cemetery will be about 1/4 mile due west of you in an L. shaped patch of trees. There is a cattle ranching operation going on here and probably a locked gate between you and the cemetery. I do not know who the land owner is.
