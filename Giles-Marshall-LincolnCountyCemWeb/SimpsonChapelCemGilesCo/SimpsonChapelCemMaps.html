SIMPSON CHAPEL CEMETERY, Clear Creek Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Simpson Chapel Cemetery. 

 Location: Go north out of Pulaski several miles on Hwy 31 to Clear Creek Rd and turn right. Travel about 3 miles and the cemetery is on your left up a slight slope behind a gate and hedge row.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the left going south up a slight incline. It is surrounded by a thicket, but is being mowed.
