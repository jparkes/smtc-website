DERRICK CEMETERY, Brown-Teal Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Derrick Cemetery as N 35.22269 X W -86.62702.

Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431 to Shields Road and turn left (northeast). After that turns into Brown Teal Road turn left and follow Brown Teal Road until it crosses two branches or about a mile and the cemetery is behind a white frame house a address 112 Brown Teal Road, on the right (north side) behind their barn buildings. You will need permission.
