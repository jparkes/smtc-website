MARTIN CHITWOOD CEMETERY, (Boonshill Community) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Martin-Chitwood Cemetery as 35.20873 X -86.65233

 Location: Go west from Fayetteville on Hwy 64 past Egam and turn north on Bradford Rd. Go abt 1 1/2 miles and the cemetery is about 75 yards back into the woods as shown by the red check below. Might be a bit hard to spot with the woods being thick.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the west side of the road across from a house.
