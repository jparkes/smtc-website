ADAMS CEMETERY, (west off of Hwy 31s South-Tarpley) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Adams Cemetery.Â 

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn west (right) on Crescent View aka Thompson Road. Go nearly a mile and the road turns back north or right. From there bear or turn left into a gravel road. After about 400 yards the gravel road turns back left (southeast). Continue on and go past a small creek getting your tires wet. After that look left and as you climb a slope the cemetery tree line can be seen way out in a wheat field about 300 yards under a small clump of trees as seen on the next two maps.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the small clump of trees out in a wheat field (today. The farmer here is subject to growing any crop.

Same map - not cluttered up with the yellow highlighter. If you visit please respect the crop being grown here and not cross into the cemetery when the crops are green and subject to being trampled upon.
