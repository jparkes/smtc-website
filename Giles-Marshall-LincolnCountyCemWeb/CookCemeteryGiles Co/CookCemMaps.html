COOK CEMETERY, (Petty or Cook Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the Cook Cemetery left side of Cook Hollow Road. With this map we also see the GPS Coordinates - Latitude and Longitude.  

Location: Highway 64 West, right into Campbellsville Road. Right into Minnow Branch Road; bear left at forks to Cook Hollow Road. Go another mile and the cemetery is on your left about 300 yards from the road upon a slope under a grove of trees.

 This aerial view shows the route to the Cook cemetery and the Joines Cemetery. Once you see a barn on the left of Petty Hollow Road look left out to the cemetery on the slope. You will need permission from the owner. There are two gates to cross to get to the cemetery; one at the barn and the other across a fence line and they both may be locked with cattle in the area. The cemetery is inside a barb wire fence. Please travel quietly and give the cattle ample space so they are not disturbed.
