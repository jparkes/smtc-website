ASHBY-TURNER-CURLEE CEMETERY (Clark Mill Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Ashby-Turner-Curlee Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go northeast on Mulberry Avenue and merge left on Shelbyville Hwy. Go about 1 mile and turn north (left) on Mimosa Road. Follow that about 1 6/10 miles and turn left on Clark Mill Rd. With permission turn into the driveway and go nearly to the house and the cemetery is on your right next to the private drive.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly in a grove of trees next to the private driveway.
