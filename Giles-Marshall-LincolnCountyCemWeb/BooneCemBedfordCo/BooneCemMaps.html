BOONE FAMILY CEMETERY (New Hermon/Flat Creek Road) BEDFORD COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Boone Family Cemetery. The nearest major town is Lynchburg in Moore County.Â 

Location: Go southeast from Shelbyville Tennessee on Hwy 82 to Flat Creek and turn west (right) on New Center Church Road and almost immediately turn back south (left) on New Hermon/Flat Creek Road. Go less than 2 miles and look right into a small field for the large stones used to mark the graves.

This aerial map from Google shows the cemetery on the west side of New Hermon Road in the open. As you go by it may be on a ledge above your head so keep a keen eye to avoid missing it. Also this map from Google has the cemetery plotted on the west side of that barn shown above so I corrected that using graphics.
