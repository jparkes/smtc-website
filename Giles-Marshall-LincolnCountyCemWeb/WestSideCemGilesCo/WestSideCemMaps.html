WEST SIDE CEMETERY, (West Side Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the West Side Cemetery as N 35.18959 X W -87.21284. 

Location: From Pulaski go west on Hwy 64 and and cross into Lawrence County and the next left is West Side road. Go left or south on that and you will encounter 5 each 90 degrees bends in the road the first left, then right then left,  then left then right at which you will see the cemetery on the left all that over about 3 miles. The next to last bend will put you back into Giles County.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well laid out next to what is labeled Good Hope Road but on some maps it is West Side Road.
