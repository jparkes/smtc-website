REVOLUTIONARY SOLDIERS INTERRED IN CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 

TO THE MEMORY OF THOSE REVOLUTIONARY WAR SOLDIERS BURIED IN MAURY CO. TENNESSEE

Pension #W341 - Butler, Zachariah

Born November 15, 1764, Amelia Co., Virginia, died 8 April 1842, Maury County, Tennessee.

Wife: Elizabeth Noble born abt 1762 and died abt 1854

Married in Amelia Co., Virginia, 9 December 1787

Moved to Tennessee in 1810 and to Maury County 5 March 1811.

Mentions there was several children but only one by name, William F. Butler, living in Sullivan Co., Tennessee. He may be the child that is mentioned as being born September 23, 1788. 

Sarah Pillow provided a sworn statement supporting the marriage date & location and the date of birth for first child- a son.

Transcribed from the original Revolutionary Pension Records by Pat Cribbet and sent to this site 13 Mar 2009.
