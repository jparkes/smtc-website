LOFTIN CEMETERY, LOFTIN ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Topographical map showing the Longfield Loftin Cemetery just on the south edge of the Loftin Road.
Road Map showing the Loftin Cemetery east of Columbia off of Iron Bridge Road.
 Follow out E. 7th St from Columbia on Iron Bridge Road and cross over the Duck River. Continue on about 1 1/4 mile and turn right or south on Loftin Road and after 1/2 mile bend back east and go until the paved road ends and the cemetery will be on your left. 
Loftin Road begins and ends on Iron Bridge Road so if you enter it on the west side you will travel on gravel road from the northeast and the cemetery will be on your right.
