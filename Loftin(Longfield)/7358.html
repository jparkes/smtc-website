LOFTIN, Lovick, 26 Feb 1842 8 Nov 1911.(soldier of the Confederate States Army; son of Alfred & Emily Loftin.)
LOFTIN, Ella Smith, 6 Aug 1843 - 9 Jan 1941. (Wife of Lovick Loftin; died in Nashville.)
Lovic Loftin, 69, died in infirmary in St. Louis, Mo. on Nov. 8; formerly of Maury; moved to Texas two years ago but was returning home and stopped in Missouri for treatment; wife and seven children survive; brought here for burial. (Maury Democrat, 9 Nov 1911.)
