LOFTIN, John & Sarah, 1846.
There are a couple of ways to see this stone. Either it is a couple John & Sarah Loftin who died in 1846 with no mention of birth dates. Also it might be twin infants who were born and died in 1846. Also it might be a brother and sister who died of one of the infectious diseases in 1846. Use of the censuses to analyze this may clarify this issue.
