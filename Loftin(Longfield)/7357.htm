LOFTIN CEMETERY, LOFTIN ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

LOFTIN, Ben R., 16 Jan 1880 - 25 Feb 1915. "Brother"
Ben Loftin "Shot in back in Tulia, Texas, he and his brother Henry were doing some fencing. A man named Hogan with whom they had some difficulty shot Ben & wounded Henry; he was 35 & the unmarried son of Mrs. Love Loftin. (Columbia Herald, 5 Mar 1915)
