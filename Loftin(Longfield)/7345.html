LOFTIN CEMETERY, LOFTIN ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

We found a small footstone with the initials A.M.L. in the area of the photo between the pile of rocks in the back and the two little footstones in the center of this photo behind the Longfield & Mary Loftin Memorial. The two other footstones in this photo are L.L. (Longfield Loftin) & M.L.(Mary Loftin).

LOFTIN, Annie McKinnie, 6 Aug - 25 Aug 1873. "Inf. daughter of J. A. & B. E. Loftin."
The footstone for Annie is all that we found. Likely the headstone is fallen and sunken into the soil. Next visit I will probe for it so it can be raised above the ground.
Photo and information by Wayne Austin 23 Dec 2008.
