This cemetery was founded in 1910 and grew up around the Neapolis Methodist Church and the Neapolis Village & community. It is well maintained and has space to continue to expand using the upright style granite memorials. Complete as of 11/29/2005 Wayne Austin Published here 1/17/2008.
