NEAPOLIS CEMETERY, NEAPOLIS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
The number after the name is indicative of the path the surveyor took when this cemetery was photographed. Start at 8310 and progress upward thru the cemetery to see whose lots are beside whom. The cemetery was surveyed starting at the northeast corner with at the time the Camden Young (#8310) monument. Surveyed, by Wayne Austin 29 Nov 2008. There are somewhat more or less than 600 graves here today.
