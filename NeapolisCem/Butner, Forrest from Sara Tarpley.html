Forrest Butner was the only child of my great-grandfather John Scott
Morgan Butner and his second wife Simmie Lorene Russell.  He was born
about 1903 in Maury County, TN, and died on February 12, 1910.

My great-grandmother, John Butner's first wife Lettia Ann Scott, is
also buried in Neapolis Cemetery in an unmarked grave.  She was born
about 1847, probably in Coffee County, TN, and died about 1891 in
Maury County.
