MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


SCOTT, Sarah, 10 Feb 1794 - 4 Nov 1855. 59-08-24. "Daughter of Samuel & Sarah." (Box tomb.) "I would not always live because this world is full of doom. There is for me a brighter place, but ti beyond the tomb. For in the grave I shall not lie But shall be raised again With body free from all? ill From cry? worry? and pain."
