MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


HANNA, Infant son Andrew & Naomi, b. & d. 25 Dec 1835. "Here lies the body of an Infant son of Andrew & Naomi Hanna his birth & Death was on the 25 of Dec 1835." This stone is easy to pick out in the
 upper photo. It is the next stone to our right of the little square stone in the upper left of the photo.
