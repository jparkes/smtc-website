MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

MATTHEWS, Jinny H., 12 Dec 1795 - 28 May 1864, "Jinny H. Matthews - daughter of John & - Margaret Matthews - was born December - 12 1795 & died May 28, 1864. ??Threescore & ten years do sum ?? days & years we see;  ason of more ...me, fourscore??." Wish I had taken more pains to gather the epitaph from the bottom fracture. I almost had to dig this one up. Part of the stone was visible from above ground.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there December 13, 2004.
