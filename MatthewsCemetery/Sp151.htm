MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

SPAIN, Sarah L., 5 Sep 1814 - 4 Jun 1892. 77-08-29. "Lead Kindly Light" (Tombstone has fallen and been on the ground since 1986 when Mr. Fred Hawkins found it in that state.) It has a good solid base that is tilted down hill. The stone will not stand until the base is leveled and supported.
 Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there December 13, 2004.
