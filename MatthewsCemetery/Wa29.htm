MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


WALKER, Adelia J., 19 Aug 1851 - 1 Jan 1876. "Wife Andrew B. M. Walker & dau. P. H. & N. A. Nelson."  I set the two missing top parts of this stone up long enough to photograph it. One can see the bare ground in front where the parts lay undisturbed awaiting slow destruction.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there December 13, 2004.
