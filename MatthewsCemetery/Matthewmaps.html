MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

To find Matthews Cemetery go south from Columbia on Campbellsville Pike and 2 1/2miles beyond Bigby Hollow 
Golf Course turn west or left on Sowell Hollow Rd. Go 3/4 mile to cemetery on right side of road. After about 
a mile on Sowell Hollow road the road will bend sharply to the left then quickly back to the right. After the 
right turn look for the entrance. If you go there handle this cemetery with care. It is old and the stones 
are fragile. Photo and information by Wayne Austin.  Visited there Dec 13, 2004.
