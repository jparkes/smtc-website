MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


COURTNEY, J. (Joseph) H., 1 May 1845 - 9 Apr 1919, Maury County Court; Tenn. Legislature; C.S.A.)
and
COURTNEY, Helen B., 25 May 1855 - 18 Sep 1897. "Wife of J. H. Courtney."

COURTNEY, Sarah Ann, 1810 - 2 Apr 1881. "born Dungannon Co. Tyrone, Ireland." This is probably the mother of Joseph. All of these inscriptions are on one stone just three different sides.
