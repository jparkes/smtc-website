MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


HANNA, Samuel, Sr., d. 1 Dec 1834. Aged about 93 yrs. "He for many years resided in lredell Co, 
N.C.", (Born in Virginia.) Born circa 1744, Samuel has to be one of the earliest born white men in Maury County History, at least with an inscribed stone.
