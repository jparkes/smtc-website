MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
   

MATTHEWS, Luther, 19 Nov 1847 - 4 Jul 1873.
also
MATTHEWS, John T., 1 Jan 1842 - d. in Mississippi Jul 4, 1868. (Co. D. 3rd Tenn lnf., C.S.A.)
 I do not know the relationship of John T. & Luther Matthews to T. Jeff Coleburn but the memorials are beside one another and are identical in style and age.
