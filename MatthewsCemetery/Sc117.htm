MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

SCOTT, Elizabeth, d. 31 May 1829. 31-02-00. "Daughter of Samuel." Full Inscription: " Here lies the body of Elizabeth Scott daughter of Sam'l- Scott who depa-rted this life May 31 1829. Aged 31 years 2 months."
