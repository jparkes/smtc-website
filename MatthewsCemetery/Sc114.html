MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Samuel Scott

Sarah Scott

SCOTT, Samuel, died 2 Mar 1829, in 66th year of his age. (born Jun 1763; Revolutionary War., Charter member of Hopewell Associated Reformed Presbyterian Church)
and
SCOTT, Sarah, died 27 Dec 1845, in 84th year of her age. (Married Samuel Scott on 18 Jan 1786.) These are the eldest Scott progenitors in this cemetery and maybe in the whole of Maury County.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there December 13, 2004. Parenthetical information from MCTC.
