MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

WALKER, Agness L., 9 May 1851 - 1 Feb 1858. "Dau. of A.B. & M.D."

WALKER, Calvert E., 11 Feb 1840 - 8 Sep 1840. "lnf. son A.B. & M.D."

WALKER, Robert B., 3 Jun 1838 - 14 Jun 1839. "Inf. son A.B. & M.D."

WALKER, Joseph M., 18 Aug 1836 - 21 Aug 1837. "Inf. son A.B. & M.D."

WALKER, John A., 18 Jan - 20 Nov 1835. "lnf. son A.B. & M.D."
WALKER, John A., 18 Jan - 20 Nov 1835. "lnf. son A.B. & M.D." (This stone lying 
behind the stone of John A. Walker and is the original stone for John A..The new stone to 
our left is a later replacement stone. The Stone carver's initials is J.H..

WALKER, Mary A., 22 Aug 1845 - 18 Jan 1847. "Inf. dau. A.B. & M.D." 

WALKER, Martha J., 24 Jul 1847 - 21 Nov 1848. "lnf. dau. A.B. & M.D."
This stone is fallen. The above are the children of Andrew Boyd and Mary Daak Matthews Walker who died in infancy. These are not typical 1830s & 1840s stones so I would say they were put there sometime later perhaps as late as 1885.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there December 13, 2004.
