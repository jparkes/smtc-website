MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

MATTHEWS, Luther, 8 Oct 1882 - 27 Jan 1899. "Son J.W. & M." "At Rest" Notice this 
time the mom's initials are M instead of S. M.. M. and S.M. are the same person because there are children here both before and after the above birth date with the Mom's initials S.M. 
 Whew! Somehow I managed to lift this 200 lb. stone out of the earth to make the photo. Afterwards it 
went safely back into the same spot. Unless we can permanently fix things then do nothing. The base behind this top leans too much for the stone to remain standing.
