MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) BIGBYVILLE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

We are looking south at the large monuments of the Matthews family. The little stones in front 
are foot or boundary markers. In the enclosed ornamental fence at the edge of the cemetery is the 
Wilbanks lots with the tall monument silhouetted against the thicket and two short ones. 
Photo by Wayne Austin Dec 13, 2004.

We are continuing to photograph the rows as we progress thru the cemetery toward the back. If you 
study these photos closely you can begin to identify the stones from the listing photos by flipping 
back and forth between the area photos and the individual ones. 
Photo by Wayne Austin Dec 13, 2004.
