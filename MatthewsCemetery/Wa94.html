MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

WALKER, William M., 13 Dec 1811 - 12 Oct 1874. "Each of us hopes to join you at last on that beautiful heavenly shore". If I understand this correctly Kesiah V. Walker was the first wife of Wm. Walker and died in 1845 and Wm and his second wife Margaret B. Alexander married in 1847.
