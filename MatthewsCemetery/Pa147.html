MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

PATTERSON, Agness S., 27 Nov 1824 - 7 Aug 1853. Upper part of stone has a round insignia with the letters in a circle from the top clockwise "F-N-D-O-Z-B-T-K-C" In the center is the letters: "AMYR"
The "7" in 27th of Nov is chipped off leaving us to rely on previous researchers who say the birth date is Nov 27, 1824.
"Fear Not Daughter Of Zion Behold The King Cometh" - Female Masonic Symbol.

Information and photo by Wayne Austin 25 Apr 2005. Visited there 13 Dec 2004. [CWA] - Update: Finally I have found the meaning of those letters. Please add my findings. [Mary Bob McClain Richardson 22 May 2015]
