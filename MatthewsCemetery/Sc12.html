MATTHEWS CEMETERY (Historic) SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
  
SCOTT, J. Walter, 25 Mar 1859 - 8 Sep 1898. This memorial stone is propped up on the back 
side of the Amis G. Matthews stone. It is probably broken and is missplaced. It probably belongs on 
the next row behind Amis G.'s stone.
Information and photo by Wayne Austin 4/25/2005. Visited there December 13, 2004.
