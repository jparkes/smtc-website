Loftin (Shadrack) Cemetery known also as Union Grove Cemetery



Go east on the old Iron Bridge Road from Tom Hitch Parkway about 2 miles to Union Grove Road. Take that and while going up the hill look to the left for the Cemetery signage & Access Road. Stop and open the gate (also close it behind you) and follow up the hill 350 yards and bend back right and the cemetery will be before you fenced in.
