PEAY CEMETERY, POTTSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Location: From Pottsville, go west on Hwy 99 for about 1 mile. Turn right (or north) onto a small gravel road that goes up steep incline. Cemetery is approx. 200 yards off highway across from the old Pottsville School site.
