HOWARD CEMETERY, ENTERPRISE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


HOWARD, Sarah Elizabeth (Brownlow), 30 Jun 1850 - 21 Aug 1875. 25-1-21. "Wife of Bithal Howard", a prominent Banker of Mt Pleasant and Columbia.' (He is buried Lawrence Cemetery in Mt. Pleasant with his 2nd wife Ada.) Inscription: "Sarah though art gone to rest, Thy toils and cares are oer, And sorrow pain and suffering shall neer distress thee." Bithal & Sarah had one daughter named Jennie. Source "Maury County Cousins". Photo & info by Wayne Austin 4/10/04.
