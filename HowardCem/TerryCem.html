Maps of the Cemetery Location

The Terry Grave is on the old Allie Kittrell Home & Farm, now owned by Mrs. Arthur Harris on the road between Mt. Pleasant and Enterprise. This is across road from the point where Arrow Lake Road enters. With permission take the lane into the old home and after crossing the creek in front of the old house take the farm road south and over and around the hill to your left for about 1/2 mile. Cemetery is in grove and the grove can be seen from Enterprise Road above the Sugar Creek.
