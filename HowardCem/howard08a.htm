HOWARD CEMETERY, ENTERPRISE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HOWARD, Louisa, 22 Jun 1853 - 13 Aug 1867. 'Daughter of J. & N. Howard. Stone discovered later, photographed and sent in by David King of Georgia. Thanks David. This stone is in the possession of Mr. King of Georgia and in his back yard leaning against a pine tree somewhere in GA. He says the old family gave him permission to own this stone, (believe it or not!) I think it should be returned to this graveyard as it is against the law to desecrate a graveyard, but at least he had the fortitude to let this be known.
