HOWARD CEMETERY, ENTERPRISE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


HOWARD, N. I., (Nancy Isabel Brownlow) 29 Aug 1838 - 27 May 1883. 'Wife of J. D. (Joseph David) Howard.' J. D. was the son of Willoughby & Frances Howard buried here also. Joseph D. Howard is interred in Rose Hill Cemetery with his second wife Mattie (Beckett) Howard. J. D. and Nancy had one dau Annie wife of W. B. Giddens of Giles County.
Death date was verified though not photographed.
Photo & info by Wayne Austin 4/15/04.
