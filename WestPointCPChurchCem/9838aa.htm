WEST POINT CUMBERLAND PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, THETA PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

DODSON, William, 1816 - 29 Sept. 1899. unmarked grave.

William Dodson died Friday, Sept. 29, at his home south of Columbia, and his remains were interred Saturday at West Point, services being conducted by Rev. Mr. Gupton. Deceased was in the 83rd year of his age. -- The Columbia Herald, 06 Oct. 1899.
