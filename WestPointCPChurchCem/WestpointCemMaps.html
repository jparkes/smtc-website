From Columbia go north on Theta Pike about 5 miles. The cemetery is on the left behind and to the left side of the church building which is on one of the highest hills in the county. As we can see on the above topographical map it is on one of the highest hills in the county about elevation 950.

From this aerial view from MS Bing mapping it is nestled in the clearing of a forest.

This closer up view shows the circular road and the cemetery to the south of the church building. That white line runing across the road is a security gate which can be locked for security reasons.
