BLAIR CEMETERY, Port Royal Road (North East) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Though most of inscriptions are still largely readable in this cemetery we can see from this photo the sad shape of the original memorials. These are old & very soft limestone & other stone monuments that are breaking and crumbling at a rapid rate. This photo was taken by pointing the camera across the cemetery southwest.

Panning the camera a bit more southerly includes the new monument.
Photo 24 Feb 2009 and information 4 May 2009 by Wayne Austin.
