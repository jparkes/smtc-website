BLAIR CEMETERY, Port Royal Road (North East) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 This large entrance monument greets the visitor to the Blair Cemetery. It reads: 
 BLAIR / McMAHON CEMETERY - DEDICATED OCT. 30, 2002 IN MEMORY OF THOSE WHO LIE HERE.
The back side shows a listing of all the known interments in this cemetery. See: The New Monument (backside)
Over on the other side of the cemetery we can see the new road that is being cut thru this place making this cemetery very accessible. Before that road it was  nearly a mile to the nearest road.
