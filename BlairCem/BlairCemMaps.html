BLAIR CEMETERY, Port Royal Road (North East) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


This map showing the latest technology from ACME Mapper 2.0 shows the outline of the cemetery which I have marked with a yellow boundary. Also the corrected GPS is: N 35.72635 X W 86.88177. Map revised 11 Mar 2013

Revised directions now is go north on I-65 and take the Saturn Parkway Exit and go west on Hwy 396 to Port Royal Road. Exit to the south (right then left) there and go south on Port Royal Road to Derryberry Lane which is a left turn (east). Next follow that and when it bends north back to become the Worthington Lane follow that 2 tenths of a mile and look to your right (east) and the cemetery is almost next to the road behind a chain link fence. Revised Instructions dated 11 Mar 2013
Old Instructions: From Spring Hill, take Port Royal Rd. Just before reaching Port Royal Presbyterian Church enter left into a farm lane and go 1 mile further. Cemetery is on Willis Brown place, between house and barn adjacent to the old Blair home which has recently been razed to make way for a new road. The new road which is probably in place now (2009) causes the cemetery to be on the east side of that.

 
 This is an exploded now historical aerial map of the area showing the cemetery pointed out by the yellow arrow. The old Blair Home can be seen above. This aerial image was made before the road was cut thru there and the home bulldozed. The cemetery has a new chain link fence surrounding it.
