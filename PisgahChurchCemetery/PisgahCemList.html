PISGAH CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This historic cemetery is 2.5 miles east of Hampshire on the Mt Pleasant Pike on the north side of the road. The Pisgah United Methodist Church facility still stands as a monument to the historic past.
