LEMAY, Vander Howard, 2 Nov 1930 - 8 Oct 2004. (The Maury County native was the son of the late Jessie H. Lemay and Elizabeth Holder Lemay. He was married to Bonnie Gibson Lemay who preceded him in death on December 1, 2002. Mr. Lemay served in the U. S. Army, 3rd Armor Division during the Korean Conflict.)
LEMAY, Bonnie Elizabeth Gibson, 27 Nov 1943 - 1 Dec 2002. (The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Riley Sr. and Lucille Black Gibson. She was the wife of 40 years to Vander Howard LeMay of Columbia.)
