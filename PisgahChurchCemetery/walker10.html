Kimberly Jane "Kim" Walker, age 45, a resident of Biffle Lane in Hampshire, passed away 6 Aug 2008, at her residence.
Burial was in the Pisgah Cemetery. The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Helen Jane [Turner] Chappell and Wayne (Margie) Chappell and the wife of William "Billy" Walker.
She was a graduate of the UT School of Dental Hygiene and worked as a Dental Hygienist for Dr. M.E. Gillis for 23 years. In addition to her husband and father, she is survived by her children: Allen and Hannah Walker of Hampshire; brothers: Richard (Sandra) Chappell of Pottsville and Jeff (Maggie) Chappell of Seattle, Wash.; mother-in-law: Faye [Biffle] Walker of Hampshire; several nieces and nephews.
She was preceded in death by a sister: Connie [Chappell] Brown; paternal grandparents: Paul J. and Pauline [Goosby] Turner; maternal grandparents: Maury P. and Viola [Adams] Chappell .
Information included in Turner-Austin Newsletter of 2008, by Linda Yates, Carolyn Hicks & Anne Cole. 31 Oct 2008
