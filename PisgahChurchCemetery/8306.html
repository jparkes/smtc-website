PISGAH CEMETERY,  HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

RUNIONS, Freddy Wade,  21 Jul 1949 - 27 May 1995, Pvt US Army Vietnam. 
Fire Ants have taken up residence in this cemetery soiling the stones. They are messy, but harmless to the graves, unless they move enough soil to render a memorial out of balance.
