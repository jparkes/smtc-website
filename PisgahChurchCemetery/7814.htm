PISGAH CEMETERY,  HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McNABB, Children memorial stones, handcrafted.
 There is a caricature that eerily looks like a woman looking to our left in the lower stone. It is formed by a small chip in the paint of the stone about 2 inches above the wing of the little angel on the right. She has short blond hair and white loose clothing on. I analyzed it at 4 mega-pixels and cannot see errors even when magnified. Weird!! Except I know it is not there by design, just a concrete paint chip of accidental formation!
