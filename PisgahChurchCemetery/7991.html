BIFFLE, Fannie, 14 Apr 1811 - 6 Jun 1879. 68-01-22. (Dau of Jonathon and Elizabeth Isom, wife of William Biffle.)
More Biographical information: Frances "Fannie" Isom was born 14 Apr 1811 in North Carolina. She is the daughter of Jonathon and Elizabeth Isom. She is the 6th of 7 children. Her father Jonathon Passed away when she was 2.
She married William Biffle on June 25, 1829 in Maury Co., Tennessee. She was the Mother of 9 children, Mary, Manerva, Adley B., William McHenry, Alice, Jacob D., James Knox, Izora and Ursula.
She passed away June 6th,1879 in Maury Co. Tennessee
 Information by Missy GGG Grandaughter. Photos by Wayne Austin, 12 Mar 2008.
