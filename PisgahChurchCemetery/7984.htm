PISGAH CEMETERY,  HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Stone on the left.

PATTON, Mary Jessie Biffle, 2 Nov 1876 - 29 Apr 1902. (married James H. Patton 16 Dec 1897; daughter of William McHenry Biffle and Julia A. Walker.)
You have my G-Grandma listed in Pisgah Cemetery and I was wondering if you could use this brief Biography for her? Her first name was Mary: Mary Jesse Biffle Patton was the daughter of William McHenry Biffle and Julia A. Walker. She was the oldest of 5 children. On 16 Dec. 1897, Mary Jesse married James Henry Patton. To this marriage 2 children were born, Mary Ozelle Patton (1898) and Jesse Patton (1900). Mary Jesse passsed away 29 April,1902. Jesse patton passed the same year as his Mother, Leaving Ozelle and her Father. [Thank You, Missy. 14 Aug 2008]
