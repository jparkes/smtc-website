PISGAH CEMETERY,  HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GIBSON, Raymond, 19 Aug 1916 - 13 May 1933. (Single, Son of William James and Mollie Odom Gibson; no memorial stone has been found to indicate where he was interred here in this cemetery. Verified by Charlotte Colvett in her survey and also Wayne Austin when he was there.)
All information and Death certificate sent in by Charlotte Colvett 16 Jul 2009.
