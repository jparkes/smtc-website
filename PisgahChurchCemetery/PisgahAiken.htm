It is interesting that Rev. John Akin had a younger brother, William. I have been doing some research on William and his progeny, and I have attached a file of four generations. One can see that he moved from York, SC (actually quite near Clover, SC) to Texas, briefly stopping in Tennessee along the way.

I was in Columbia for Mule Day and visited "family" at Rose Hill. I never have the time to get to Pisgah, but your page on that cemetery is quite rewarding. Thank you again for that work.

Now if you could just locate the graves of William Howard Dedman and wife Elizabeth D. Haile Dedman somewhere north west of Williamsport--possibly along Leatherwood Creek. They are my gr-gr-great grandparents.

Jim Dedman
