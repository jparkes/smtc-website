Columbia War Memorials to those who made the supreme sacrifice

Columbia Daily Herald, 1971

Maury Soldier Said Missing In Vietnam

 A Maury County soldier, scheduled to be discharged from the Army this month, is missing in Vietnam, army officials have notified his mother.

Mrs. Webb Himes of Rt. 1. Hampshire said she was advised that her son, Sgt. Edward Himes, has been missing since Oct. 26.

The 25-year-old soldier, who has served two and a half of his five years military duty in Vietnam, was slated for discharge in November (1971)
