PISGAH CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The GPS Coordinates are shown on this map.

This cemetery is 2.5 miles east of Hampshire on the Mt Pleasant Pike on the north side of the road. The Pisgah United Methodist Church facility was still standing as a monument to the historic nature of this place.
Map from MS Terra Site 11/15/2005 based on topography in the 1950s updated some.
