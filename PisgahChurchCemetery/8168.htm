PISGAH CEMETERY,  HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BROOKS, Mary E.(Mary Elizabeth Dodson), 28 Mar 1830 - 25 Apr 1882. "Wife of W.T. (William Thomas) Brooks.", daughter of William Beverly Dodson and Elizabeth Hamilton Dodson.
Their daughter, Mary Elizabeth Brooks married Hugh Fletcher "H.F." Fariss, and they are buried in Rose Hill in Columbia.
Information by Jim Dedman, 9 Jan 2017,  Photo by Wayne Austin, 12 Mar 2008.
