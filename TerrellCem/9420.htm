TERRELL CEMETERY, (Carters Creek) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Agniss P. Jenkins (fallen and broken memorial, bottom of stone likely is nearby). We see in this photo two footstones of the style that would match Agniss's headstone. I believe one footstone belongs to the headstone of Agniss and the other footstone belongs to the memorial of Elizabeth P Jenkins which is covered by soil in this photo. Later I discovered it below the soil and cleaned and made the photo. 

JENKINS, Agniss P., 10 Sep 1834 - 26 Dec 1835. (the 3 in the death year is covered by a leaf)
