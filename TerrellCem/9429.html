TERRELL CEMETERY, (Carters Creek) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This side and bottom and two ends of a box tomb is likely the box tomb for John Hill. The top is some distance away from these parts. It is next to Mary Hill's box tomb we see in the right edge of the photo.

Fallen & brolen box tomb of John Hill, parts of this memorial are sunken below the ground. 

HILL, John, 1 Jan 1794 - 7 Jan 1850.
