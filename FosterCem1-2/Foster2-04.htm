FOSTER CEMETERY #2 (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Curry children memorial from the backside. Other memorials are in the distance. There on our right is Mr Fred's Pine tree, dead as a doornail. No offense toward this brilliant man, but this is hardly useful for finding a cemetery as pine trees grow fast, die fast, and decay fast.

CURRY, Joseph, 19 Jul 1860 - 10 May 1867. "Son of C.B. & M.A. Curry."
CURRY, Ella K., 22 Apr 1867 - 9 Dec 1867. "Daughter of C.B. & M.A. Curry."
