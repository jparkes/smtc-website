FOSTER CEMETERY #2 (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Benjamin Foster memorial.

FOSTER, Benjamin C., "born in Abberville District, S.C. on 30 Dec 1819, came to Tenn. 21 Nov 1830, married Fannie G. Roberson on 1 Jun 1846 and died 28 Jul 1898." (Son of Joseph & Mary Anderson Foster.)
