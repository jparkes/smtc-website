BOOKER, (MERRIT M.) CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

An aerial view of the Booker Cemetery. The little thicket is so small it almost does not show up in this photo.

Take Mooresville Pike south to Scott Hollow Road turn right into Scott Hollow Road. Follow this road for one mile. When it turns it turns sharply to the west continue on in the home lane of the house and farm and the cemetery is in an open field in a thicket, on the old Merritt-Ingram Historic home. As one turns into the lane of the home place look to your left about 200 yards out into the middle of the field and there will be the thicket where the cemetery is located. Maps are adapted from Topozone map site. Information by Wayne Austin 12/30/2005.
