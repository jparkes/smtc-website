BOOKER, (MERRIT M.) CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
BOOKER, Lucien, d. 21 Apr 1842. (16-01-17.) 'second son of Merritt H. & Martha F." Inscription reads as: DEPARTED this Life on APRIL 21 1842. LUCIEN second son of MERRIT H. & MARTHA F. BOOKER Aged 16 years, 1 month & 17 days."
