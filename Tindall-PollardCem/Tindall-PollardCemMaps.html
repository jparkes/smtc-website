TINDELL-POLLARD CEMETERY, Tindall Lane, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEEÂ 
This Map shows the GPS coordinates of the Tindall-Pollard Cemetery. The speckled area is land that has been scarred by Phosphate mining years ago.

Go west on Williamsport Pike (Hwy. 50) and turn south on Tindell Lane. After passing through the rail road underpass turn
right into the first farm lane. This leads to the historic Tindell House, which is listed on the National Registry and
is now (1987) owned by Mr. & Mrs. Leroy Gibson. The cemetery is located in the field about 100 yards behind the house. [1987 Fred Lee Hawkins]
Directions still good in 20 Sep 2010. House has been restored by current owners, Paul & his wife Cyndy (Hubbell) Gibson.
