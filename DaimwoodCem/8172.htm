DAIMWOOD CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The fallen memorials of Richard & May Maude Caldwell. The bases are on our left the main fallen stones are in the middle and the little light gray stones on our far right are their footstones. Level the bases and set the mid sections back up facing east and these memorials are restored. Well maybe a good bio-wash.

Richard A. Caldwell memorial fallen from its base.

CALDWELL, Richard A., 2 Jan 1876 - 14 Aug 1913. (Son of Wm. A. & Belle D. Caldwell.)
CALDWELL, May Maude, 29 Feb 1884 - 16 Dec 1911. (Dau. of Wm. A. & Belle D. Caldwell.)
