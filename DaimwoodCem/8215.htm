DAIMWOOD CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This little footstone is all I found of the H.B. Daimwood, 1870-1893. It is east of the Holly memorials and is not be confused with the fallen and broken memorial for H.B. Daimwood b. 1814 memorial as that is located north of this place down the same row. If one were to probe for the headstone it could be found as previous researchers detailed it I believe. Nor is this to be confused with the Henry Burton or Henry Burton Daimwood memorial as that is southwest of this row.

DAIMWOOD, H. B., 27 Jan 1870 - 27 Jul 1893.
