DAIMWOOD CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Elizabeth J. Daimwood, wife of Henry Boone Daimwood.

E. J. Daimwood wife if H.B. Daimwood memorial. The empty base further out also has a stone I found buried under the topsoil. I dug it up. it is for H.B. Daimwood, the husband.

DAIMWOOD, E. (Elizabeth) J., 16 Dec 1820 - 26 Dec 1890. "Wife H. B." (nee Eliza Jane Gullett.)
