DAIMWOOD CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Unknown, this is one of many fieldstones that are marking graves. This one clearly does because there is a head & footstone, both being fieldstones standing up on their end and both buried about half way down. This is between the Butler and Daimwood area. I believe the stone to the left of this stone is actually the headstone. That is consistent with the rows in this graveyard.
