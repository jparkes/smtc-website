DAIMWOOD CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

DAIMWOOD, John N., 14 Feb 1857 - 29 Jan 1906. (Son Henry Boone & Eliza J. Daimwood.)
DAIMWOOD, Theresa "Rissie" Haley, 7 Dec 1859 - 25 Nov 1918. "Wife of John N. Daimwood." (Daughter of H. V. & F. T. Haley.)
This stone fallen from its base. The inscription for Theresa appears to be a hand inscribed writing by a non-professional. It may be that her original inscription is on the other side of this stone. This memorial was sunken into the muck too deeply to turn over to determine this.
