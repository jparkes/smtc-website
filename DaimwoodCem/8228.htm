DAIMWOOD CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Our subject stone here is the little small hard to see stone in the center left edge of the photo with the greenish face and a bit blurred here. The stones on its right also belong to the Holly (parents) family. There is also one more Holly memorial further left out of the range of this photo identical to the two large Holly memorials we see here.

HOLLY, Charlotte, 3 Feb 1906 - 22 May 1906. "Infant of R. H. & I. C. Holly."
