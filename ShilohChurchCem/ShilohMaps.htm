SHILOH CHURCH CEMETERY, ROY THOMPSON ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is a topographical map by Topozone showing the cemetery located several hundred yards upon the ledge above the Roy Thompson Road.

From Mt. Pleasant, take Cross Bridges Rd. as it meanders north or west about 1 mile to Roy Thompson Rd. Take this and the old cemetery is marked by several large beach and oak trees, on the north side of the road. It is just west and next to the driveway to Mr. Hickman's entrance to his farm.
Photo and info by Wayne Austin 25 Sep 2008.
