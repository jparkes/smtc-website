SHILOH CHURCH CEMETERY, ROY THOMPSON ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The little short stone is the footstone and the headstone is fallen and shows up 10 feet the other side of that as a light spot. John Nixon Jr.

The John Nixon Jr. stone. It is on the ground and broken. The inscription is also flaking off.

NIXON, John, Jr., 13 Sep 1795 - 26 Jun 1824. 'Son Rev. John Nixon.' (Tombstone toppled & covered with dirt.). Inscription:"Jno Nixon Jr. son -- of Rev. Jno Nixon -- was born Sept 13th -- 1795 and departed -- this life June 26th -- 1824."
This may be the last photo of htis stone complete as it is breaking up pretty badly.
