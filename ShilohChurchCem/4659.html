SHILOH CHURCH CEMETERY, ROY THOMPSON ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

E.R. Jackson memorial is on the left and the right is the light spot where the E. F. Jackson stone is lying on the ground. The M.S. English is in the distance propped up by a pine log (will stand about 1 year that way)
