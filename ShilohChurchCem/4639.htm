SHILOH CHURCH CEMETERY, ROY THOMPSON ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Jane P Briggs. Boxtomb on the end with broken pieces piled on the top.

Boxtomb on the left with the rocks piled upon it. Inscription is on the (our) left side. I battled Red Wasp and Poison Ivy to photograph this memorial.

BRIGGS, Jane P., 19 Mar 1834 - 13 Mar 1851. 16-10-24. "Dau. of John & Annis Briggs; member of Methodist Church at this place." (Box tomb, inscription on side.) Inscription: "Jane P., Daughter of John and Annis Briggs -- born May 13th 1834 and departed this life -- March 13th 1851. Aged 16 years 10 months -- 24 days, and was a member of the Methodist Church at this place.
