SHILOH CHURCH CEMETERY, ROY THOMPSON ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

E.F. Jackson memorial is on the right. It is the light spot lying on the ground. The M.S. English is in the distance propped up by a pine log (will stand about 1 year that way)
The E.R. Jackson stone is leaning to the left.
JACKSON, E. F., died 18 Apr 1844. (TS fallen & broken, covered with dirt.)
