SHILOH CHURCH CEMETERY, ROY THOMPSON ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

These stones are just to our left and slightly to other side of the tree in the upper right corner of the photo and not visible in this photo. The main black cemetery is to our right 400 feet out.
FRIERSON, Mrs Izora, 28 Jun 1915 - 27 Feb 1937. 
This little memorial stone is home made from concrete. Folks if you make your own be sure to use Hydraulic Cement (Concrete) as it is tough enough to last.
