SHIRES (JACOB) CEMETERY, POTTSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map from TopoZone shows where the cemetery is located. The red check marks the spot according to the GPS coordinates. 

From Columbia go west on Hwy 412/99 12 miles to the intersection of Hwy 106 and Hwy 412. Turn north or left and go to you see the cemetery road which is the same road to the old home place. Turn left into the driveway stopping by the home to get permission and the keys to the cemetery as it is locked.

This aerial view shows the cemetery at the end of a road that bend back left at a gate and and then back right to the cemetery. The cemetery is heavily fenced

This (closer-in) aerial view shows the cemetery pointed out by the white arrow at the end of a road that bend back left at a gate and and then back right to the cemetery. The cemetery is heavily fenced. The road goes by the old Chunn & Shires home first in the corner of the road just before the first bend in the cemetery road.
