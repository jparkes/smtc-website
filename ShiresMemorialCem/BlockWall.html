SHIRES (JACOB) CEMETERY, POTTSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Cinder block Protective wall on the left can be seen spanning the cemetery east to west.
I have many relatives buried in the Shires Memorial Cemetery. My father, Lyndon Harris, built the cinderblock wall there. Here is a picture of Daddy laying the blocks. Daddy's brother, Venson Harris is interred there and so is Mother's brother Murray (Jim) Brown.
Thanks for all the work you do for Maury County Cemeteries.
Ruby Harris Thomas
