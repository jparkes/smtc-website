LANCASTER-KERR CEMETERY-BRYANT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Looking south from inside the cemetery at the many stacks of rough cut stones which mark graves. The Smith tower style stone and to the left of that is the four Lancaster stones. To our left just out of the photo is the Kerr Family Stones. On your right is the stone of Nancy Scribner which sits out by itself. Many old stumps of Cedars and other bygone trees are in this cemetery. Photo by WA. 5/31/2003
