LANCASTER-KERR CEMETERY-BRYANT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Looking west toward the road from inside the cemetery at the many stacks of rough cut stones which mark graves. Only two marked & inscribed stones can be seen from here. 1. The small stone under the sign in the middle next to the road. 2. The stone just to the left of the large tree on your right. Photo by WA. 5/31/2003
