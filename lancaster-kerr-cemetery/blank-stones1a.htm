LANCASTER-KERR CEMETERY-BRYANT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 A carved head and foot stone of the old kind that is in the 1840s era without any visible inscription. Between the carved stones are a stack of rough cut stones to cover the grave in the Lancaster-Kerr Cemetery. Stones are in the cemetery first row near the road. Photo by WA. 5/31/2003
