WALKER (Thomas) CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Go out Hampshire Pike one mile past the northeast bound Hwy 50 turnoff and turn left onto Rutherford Lane. Go south past Lindsey Lane, Williamson Dr & Robert Lane. When you see Logan Dr. on your right go to the second Driveway on your left and into that and request permission to visit the cemetery if the owner is amicable. The cemetery is just south of the barn on this property in the trees. The cemetery should be inside a low rock enclosure. Directions developed from Mr. Fred Hawkin's  instructions along with the latest in mapping technique. [WA 7 Nov 2008] This cemetery is on my list of those to visit but I do not guarantee it is in this location.
Instructions per Fred Hawkins, 1987. From Columbia, go west on Hampshire Pike. Just beyond Columbia State College turn south on Rutherford Lane and go one mile. The cemetery is behind the house on the Pat Greene place inside a low rock enclosure. [Wayne Austin 6 Nov 2008.]
