Mack Cemetery, Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries
Sometimes called Walker Cemetery.

Locator Maps

Listing of the Memorials (2 photos)

This is a very old cemetery located in the McCains Community. It is one of the oldest in the county. It is said that John Mack came here before the land was opened up to the early settlers and possibly squatted on Indian lands for a time.
Added here by Wayne Austin with photos and information given by Patty Heath 20 Dec 2002
