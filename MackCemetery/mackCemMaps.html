MACK FAMILY CEMETERY-McCAINS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


This is a Topographical map showing the Walker Cemetery which is known more by the name Mack Cemetery. It is in south Maury County near the McCains community about (to the turn off)
6 tenths of a mile south of the Covey Hollow Road on the east side of the road. Take the farm road shown as a dotted line road on the above map and go 467 Yards or about 1/4 mile and look up the hill about 100 yards. The cemetery is in the forest.
