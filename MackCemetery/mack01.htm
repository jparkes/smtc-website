MACK FAMILY CEMETERY-McCAINS, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


MACK, John, 1740 - 1814. "The first of his name in this state. He was born in Scotland in 1740 and died in the 74th year of his age. An honest man. The noblest work of God." (Large box tomb the top of which is now badly faded & broken. Photo by by Mary P. Engels, 1980, donated Jan 2003, by Patricia Renee Heath 8th generation descendant of the Mack family. Photo was from Family History Book published by Mary P. Engels titled "To Hope, To Labor, And So To Live Descendants of John MACK (1740-1814) With Some Allied Families" A few generations of the charts reads: John Adams Mack Sr. married to Sarah Burnett, 9 September 1766 in Prince Edward Co., VA James Mack married to Sarah Wilson circa 1789 in NC or VA.  Aquilla Wilson Mack married to Serene Hutson (Hudson) circa 1822 in Maury Co., TN.  Young Milton Mack Sr. married to Louise (Louie) Gilbreath 28 Oct 1874 in Independence Co., AR.
