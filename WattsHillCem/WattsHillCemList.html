WATTS HILL CEMETERY, WATTS HILL ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Take Enterprise Rd., turn west (right) on Watts Hill Rd. and go 1 & 1/4 mile beyond Spencer Hill Cemetery. The Cemetery is about 300 ft. from road, on south (left) side, in a pasture, but fenced.)   (Locator Map)
