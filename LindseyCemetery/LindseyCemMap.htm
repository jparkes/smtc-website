LINDSAY CEMETERY, GLENDALE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



This cemetery is located 50 or so yards west but slightly northwest of what was at one time the Roy Cherry Home. Go south on Culleoka Rd. 1 3/4 miles south of Glendale. Take narrow road right, cross R.R. and enter drive on left beside the Rails. This is the Roy Cherry place (1987) and the cemetery is behind his barn.
You will find this cemetery going south on Hwy 373 shown on the above map about 300 yards west of Hwy 373 just before the road begins to curve slightly eastward.
