GARRETT CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


HUBBELL, N. B., 22 Jul 1815 - 21 Mar 1888. 72 07-29. Inscription: "N. B. Hubbell, Born July 22, 1815 Died March 21, 1888 Age 72 Yrs 7 Mo & 29 Days." (Mary J. Garrett married Napoleon B. Hubbell December 23, 1839, Maury Co. TN) 
This stone is dangerously close to falling. When it does it is likely to cause breakage of the parts. Also it could injure the cattle roaming freely if they are near there and cause the fall. This needs urgent attention. The stone will have to be lowered to the ground and the tree cut and then the stone leveled and reassembled upright. However none of this will matter if a fence is not put in place to keep the cattle out.
