GARRETT CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GARRETT, William, 16 Jul 1785 - 16 Nov 1870. 85-04-00. Father.
Inscription: "WILLIAM GAR-RETT, Born July 16th 1785 Died Nov 16th 1870 Age 85 Yrs 4 Mo." Jill Garrett says in "Hither and Yon" that William Garrett was a 10th District School Teacher."  
This stone is on the ground lying on its side and hard to gain access to be able to read it. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 4/4/04.
