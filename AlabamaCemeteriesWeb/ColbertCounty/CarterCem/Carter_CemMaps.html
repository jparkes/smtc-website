CARTER CEMETERY, (River Road, near Gargis Hollow Rd), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map combined with the TOPOZONE mapping data shows the GPS of the Carter Cemetery in the center of the map. ACME Mapper says the coordinates are this: N 34.78797 W 87.49907

From Muscle Shoals go east on River Road for about 10 miles past Ford City and to Gargis Hollow Road. Turn left (or north) and go 2 tenths of a mile and turn right into the Carter Cemetery Road - a new gravel road.

Carter Cemetery Road goes east toward the cemetery but as shown by the yellow pointer the road bends back south before fronting the cemetery. Google's labeling of thee the cemetery is located is wrong. My yellow arrow is correct.
