MT. PLEASANT CUMBERLAND PRES. CH. CEMETERY, County Line Rd., COLBERT COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the Mt. Pleasant Cumberland Presbyterian Church Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.77153 W 87.52922

To reach this cemetery go east out 2nd Street past Wise Aluminum to County Line Road and turn north (left). Follow that for about a mile and look left at the CP Church and the cemetery is just past the church also on the left.
 
Notice the yellow highlighter marks an area that approximates the cemetery. Granted some of that area is too low for a cemetery being in the flood plain of McKiernan Creek.

This view from Google has the complete cemetery shown on the side of a slope down to McKiernan Creek.
