HOBGOOD CEMETERY, (east side of Hwy 133), COLBERT COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Hobgood Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.71834 W 87.63284

To reach this cemetery from the intersection of 6th St and Hwy 133 go south on Hwy 133 7 tenths of a mile and turn into a gravel residential road servicing about 3 homes. The cemetery can be seen from there out in a pasture under the trees on your left. Notice the map above calls this the Blocker Cemetery. I do no know the source of that name and therefore followed the naming of this cemetery as others in the past that listed this cemetery did. 
Notice the red check pointer indicating a group of trees under which is the cemetery exist. From this image you can see part of the rock wall jutting west from under the trees.
