LEIGH CEMETERY, (east of Leighton), COLBERT COUNTY, ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the William Leigh Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 shows these coordinates as calculated by C.W.A.: N 34.69845 W 87.51385

To reach this cemetery from the intersection in Leighton Alabama, at the junction of County Line Road (Hwy 48) and Old Hwy 20 go east toward Town Creek less than 1 mile and look left at a park like area next to the road marked today with a historical marker.

Notice the yellow pointer indicating a group of trees under which the Leigh cemetery resides on the north side of old Hwy 20.
