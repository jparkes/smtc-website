PREUIT CEMETERY (Cotton town), COLBERT COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Preuit Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.67511 W 87.50778

To reach this cemetery from the intersection of County Line Road and the 72/20 Hwy go south on County Line Road Co Rd 48 about 1 mile and  turn left or east on Cottontown Lane. Go about 1 mile and turn into the old Preuit home place left (or north) and and travel back to and past the home to the cemetery. Permission and notifying the residents is a good thing here to keep down confusion and suspicion and prevent the bad guys from admission.

Notice the fence and the tombstones inside the fence. In the center is the three Myatt family box tombs.
