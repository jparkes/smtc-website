HENNIGAN CEMETERY, Ford City, (east) COLBERT COUNTY, ALABAMA.

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Hennigan Cemetery.  ACME Mapper says the coordinates are this: N 34.78767 W 87.52424

This road map shows the directions as about 8 miles east of Muscle Shoals on River Road.  Take River Road to Ford City and go about 1/2 mile and look left (in the wintertime) at the thicket back behind a house about 300 yards.

Notice the Red Check marking the thicket where the cemetery resides. It is completely abandoned and does not appear to have had visitors that were interested in its maintaining it in many years.
