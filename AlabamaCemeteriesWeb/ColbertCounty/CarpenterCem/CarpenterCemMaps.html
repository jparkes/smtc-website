CARPENTER CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Rd aka Brick Church Rd) , COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Carpenter Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.77477 W 87.52197

To reach this cemetery from the intersection of County Line Road and Mt Pleasant Road drive .3 miles east to a gravel driveway of J.C. Holt on the left. this is just past the Old Brick Church on the right. Take the gravel road and get permission from Mr. Holt (293 Mt Pleasant Rd) to go behind his home and back behind his chicken house to the cemetery.
Notice the yellow pointer indicating a group of trees under which is the cemetery just north of the east end of the chicken house. Mr. Holt's driveway crosses over Shegog Creek
