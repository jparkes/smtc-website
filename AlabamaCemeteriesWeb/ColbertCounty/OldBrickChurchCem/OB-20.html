OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
 

MILLIGAN, Eva Ledlow, 1906 - 7 Mar 1941. (d/o George James & Mary Ellen Reeves Ledlow; w/o Jef Milligan.)

Eva Ledlow Milligan was the daughter of George James Ledlow and Mary Ellen Reeves. She is the granddaughter of William Robert "Bob" Ledlow and Milly Angeline Osborn Ledlow. According to Alabama Death Records she was also the wife of Jeff Milligan and is buried at Old Brick Cemetery in Colbert County Alabama. (When I surveyed I did not find a memorial stone for her. WA)
