OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA

JONES, Paula Lynn, 30 Jun 1968 - 26 Jul 2013. Harrison Funeral Home Marker
 
Paula Lynn Jones, age 45, of Tuscumbia passed away on Friday, July 26, 2013. Visitation will be Tuesday from 6-8 p.m. at Morrison Funeral Home, Tuscumbia. The funeral service will be Wednesday at 2 p.m. in the funeral home chapel with Pastor Jeffrey Collins officiating. The burial will follow at Old Brick Cemetery. 
Lynn was a native of Florence and a member of Cornerstone Church of Moulton. She was preceded in death by her father, Leonard Thomas Jones and her mother, Marjorie Thompson Cornelius. 
She is survived by her sisters, Cheryl Shook and husband, Claude and Dawn Letson and husband, Chris; nephews, Jordan Staggs and Canaan Newman and a host of aunts, uncles, cousins and friends. 
Pallbearers are Dale, Blake and Ben Allen, Blake Kimbrell, Johnny Villyette and Jason Thompson. 
Lynn was loved by all and will be dearly missed but never forgotten.
