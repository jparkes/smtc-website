FAIRCLOTH CEMETERY, (River Road east) COLBERT COUNTY, ALABAMA

This Aerial map shows the GPS of the Glenco Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 as calculated by C. Wayne Austin says these coordinates are: N 34.70408 W 87.73565

To reach this cemetery from the intersection of Hwy 43 & 72 go west on Hwy 72  for 3 & 3/4 miles and look for the entrance to the Rock Crusher which was at one time Vulcan Materials Company and I assume they still own it. Turn south or right into the main gate and get permission or be hauled off to jail. HA!. Go behind the office and the cemetery is next to the offices on the south side. Because of the dangers of blasting that goes on here along with heavy machinery all around I would say this visit is only available on certain days with permission and a pass including your signature on a release from liability form, but I have not been here.

Notice the rectangular white rock wall facing east located south of the building where you can visibly see stones in this cemetery.
