KING CEMETERY, Cottontown Lane east, COLBERT COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the King Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.66315 W 87.47836.

To reach this cemetery from the intersection of County Line Road and the 72/20 Hwy go south on County Line Road (Co Rd 48) about 1 mile and turn left or east on Cottontown Lane. Go about 1 1/2 miles and turn south on King Bridge Road. Go a short distance and the cemetery is on your left under double fencing. This is a locked-down private cemetery. Permission & a key is required.

Notice the tombstones are inside two fences both secured. If you go here without permission you could be hauled out in the back of a squad car.
