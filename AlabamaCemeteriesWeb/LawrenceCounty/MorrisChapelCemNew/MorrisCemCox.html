Decatur Daily - April 29, 2001

Annie Pearl Cox
74, homemaker
MOULTON -- Funeral for Annie Pearl Cox, 74, of Decatur will be Monday at 2 p.m. at Lawrence Funeral Home with the Rev. Hugh Compton officiating.
Burial will be in Morris Chapel Cemetery. The family will receive friends tonight from 6 to 9 at the funeral home.
Mrs. Cox died Saturday, April 28, 2001, at Decatur General Hospital. She was born Dec. 7, 1926, in Lawrence County to Todd Pitt and Mary Jane Cartee Pitt. She was a homemaker and a member of Caddo United Methodist Church. She was the widow of Buford Cox.
Survivors include one son, Gary Cox of Moulton; one brother, Aaron Pitt of Tanner; one sister, Myrl Hames of Trinity; three grandchildren; and one great-grandchild.
