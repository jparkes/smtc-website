NEW MORRIS CHAPEL CEMETERY, LAWRENCE COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS of (new) Morris Chapel Cemetery. This cemetery replaced the old Morris Chapel Cemetery when the church was moved here to the new location in the early 20th century.

 This road map from MS Streets & trips shows the directions as: Go east on Highway 24 toward Decatur and turn (left) north on County Rd 217 and follow that road about two miles and pass thru the intersection of Cr 170. This very large community cemetery is on both sides of the road about 150 yards further north of the intersection.

The cemetery on both sides of the road with a circular drive on the west side of the road. Over a thousand graves here.
