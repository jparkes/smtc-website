KELLY CEMETERY, (Co. Rd 434), LAWRENCE COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Kelly Cemetery as N 34.62339 X W -87.15180.

This road map shows the directions: Go west from Decatur on Highway 20 toward Muscle Shoals to Hillsboro and turn south on County Road 434. Follow that until it crosses the railroad tracks. After that turn into the parking space near the tracks and walk south west to the cemetery in the middle of a large cultivated field. Currently in Corn. There are certain times of the year when the corn is tall you will be unable to visit this cemetery due to the high corn. I got lucky and found I was there just after the corn harvest.

Notice the cemetery in the midst of a giant (currently harvested) cornfield. There are no trees in the cemetery.
