AUSTIN-FULLER CEMETERY, LAWRENCE COUNTY ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Austin-Fuller Cemetery.

This road map shows the directions: Go 3 and 1/2 miles west past the junction of the Old & New Moulton Highway on the Old Moulton Hwy (460) to the top of the hill and look left when you are at the junction of Hwy 460 & Co Rd 358.

Notice the Red Check marking the Austin-Fuller Cemetery in the southeast corner of the junction of Co Rd 358 & Co Rd 460 (Old Moulton Hwy) under the the Oak trees.
