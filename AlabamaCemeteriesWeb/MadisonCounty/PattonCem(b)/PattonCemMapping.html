PATTON CEMETERY (Riverton Rd) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Patton Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.84165 X W -86.49565.

From Huntsville go out Winchester Rd to Riverton Road on your left and turn left. Go about 1 and 1/2 miles and look to your right out into a cultivated field at the cemetery (today) under a dying cedar tree.

This close up aerial view shows a yellow highlighted outline of the cemetery premises.
