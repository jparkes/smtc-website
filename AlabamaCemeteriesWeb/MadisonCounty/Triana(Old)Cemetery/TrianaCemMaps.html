OLD TRIANA CEMETERY, Triana MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Old Triana Cemetery Alternate Location: Sec. 22-5S-2W. C. SW SW. In the center of the town of Triana.

 From old Hwy 20 in Madison take Zierdt Road south several miles and look right while in the center of Triana and the cemetery can be seen in the sparsely populated large area under the trees.

Notice the large area under the trees where the cemetery.
