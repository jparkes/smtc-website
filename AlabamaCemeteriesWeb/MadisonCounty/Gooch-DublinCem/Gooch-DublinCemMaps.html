GOOCH-DUBLIN CEMETERY, Hughes Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Gooch-Dublin Cemetery of Madison, Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked. GPS N 34.71026 X W -86.74039.

From Hwy 72 go south in Madison down Hughes Road past the Madison Church of Christ on your right and then the medical building next right then look right (east) at the next two buildings. Between them is the two parking lots serving these buildings. Park in the back next to the curb and draw a line from the center curb (ease west) straight up the incline and walk up that incline thru the thicket 50 yards to the cemetery under today a large Cedar tree. When you see the mourners bench the cemetery is just past that as in the photo below.
