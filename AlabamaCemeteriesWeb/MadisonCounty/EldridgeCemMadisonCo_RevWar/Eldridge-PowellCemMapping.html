ELDRIDGE-POWELL CEMETERY, W. Ridge Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
 The GPS coordinates of the Eldridge-Powell Cemetery are shown here as N 34.83232 X W -86.62208. Alternate Location: Section 27 T2S R1W NE SW SW North of Bob Wade Lane.
 At the intersection of Pulaski Pike and Bob Wade Lane go east about 1.6 miles and turn left on Northgate Dr NW. Follow that for a few hundred yards and turn left (or bear left) on Renee Rd NW. After about 1/4 mile that dead ends into W. Ridge Dr. turn left and after the first house on the left notice the cemetery under the trees on a separate lot also on the left.

This photo view shows the cemetery under the trees. Please do not go here unless you have respect & honor for the deceased.
