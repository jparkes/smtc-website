STONE-FANNING CEMETERY, ( (Rube Robinson Rd), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA



 FANNING, Sarah (Loving), wife of Middleton Fanning, born in Virginia (abt 1817), came to Alabama In 1836, married in her 19th year, Died April 13, 1880 in the 63rd year of her age. (Note: part of this stone is illegible.)
and
FANNING, Middleton, Sep 5, 1812 - Apr 27, 1895. s/o James Wilson & Sarah Franklin Fanning.
They had seven known children:
William Loving (m. Mary Elizabeth Kennard) They lye in the nearby Rice Cemetery.
Julia Connally Fanning Stone. (m. William Mapp Stone). They lye in this cemetery near their parents.
John Abraham (m. Ella Virginia Bayless). They are buried in the nearby Rice Cemetery.
Nancy Jane Fanning Miller, (m. Robert Meredith Miller),  Burial in Forest Park East Cemetery, Shreveport, Caddo Parish, Louisiana
Mary Elizabeth Teague nee Fanning (Hezekiah Emmett Teague),  Burial in the nearby Locust Grove Cemetery, New Market, Madison Co., Alabama
Carroll Mayhew Fanning,  (m. Alice Emma Teague) burial location unknown.
Racheal Alice Fanning. buried here near her parents.
