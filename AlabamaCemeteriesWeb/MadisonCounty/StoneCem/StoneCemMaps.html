STONE-FANNING CEMETERY, ( (Rube Robinson Rd), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Stone Cemetery of Madison Co. Alabama as N 34.85824 X W -86.47709. The location is indicated in the center of the map under the white check. Alternate Location: Section 24-2-1E - N/2 N/2 NW Note: This cemetery is located atop a small hill inside a 2 acre woodland plot.
At the intersection of Highway 72 go north on Moores Mill Road. Cross Winchester Road and the road bends to the right and becomes Darwin Road. That terminates into Macon Lane. Turn left there and go 400 yards to Rube Robinson Road on the right. Follow that until it turns into Stone Cemetery Road. When the gravel road bends to the right go another 150 yards and park your vehicle and walk up the slight slope into the woods and find the memorial stones about the center of the woodland at the top of the slope.

Notice the labeling under the tree line. This marker may be a bit south of the actual location. This cemetery needs care as the small trees growing up around the stones under the big trees will become large trees before long and begin to threaten the stones.
