STRONG (George) CEMETERY, (Meridian St N) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the George Strong Cemetery of Madison Co. Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked with the red check as N 34.81068 X W 86.56471. Across Hwy 231/431 from the Charles W. Strong Cemetery and about a 1/4 mile down a field road, so that makes it about a little over a 1/2 mile southeast of the C.W. Strong cemetery.
Specific Location: Section 6 Township 3S, Range 1E NW. In the valley a quarter of a mile east of the big curve in Meridian Pike before it meets with Highway 231-431 north of Huntsville.


ON Meridian St N pass Winchester Rd and go the steep bend back left. Notice the long field road on the right going east and notice the thicket in the distance down on the right side of the field road. The George Strong Cemetery occupies that thicket.

Here under the red balloon we see the cemetery thicket and long field road needed to get to into this cemetery. A better situation is go to the church north of there and park in their lot with permission and hike to this cemetery.
