WADE CEMETERY (Bob Wade Lane, north side) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

This Aerial map shows the GPS of the Wade Cemetery. Do not go here looking for memorials. I am told there are none remaining. The GPS calculates to N 34.83113 X W -86.60240, but may not be exact.

 At the intersection of Bob Wade Lane and Hwy 231/431 go west about 2 miles and look to your right (north) behind a barn and house at a tree lined thicket and fence row where this cemetery is supposed to be located.

Notice the red balloon indicating the cemetery location occupying a treed area just west of the barn of the old Bob Wade farm. This is private property so permission and notification of the owners is necessary.
