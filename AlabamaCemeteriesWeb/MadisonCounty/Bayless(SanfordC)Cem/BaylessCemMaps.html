BAYLESS (Sanford) CEMETERY, (Winchester Rd), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Bayless (Sanford) Cemetery of Madison Alabama as N 34.84318 X W -86.46310. The location is indicated in the center of the map under the white check. Alternate Location: Section 30, Township 2 Range 2E, N/2 NW NW. This means the north one half of section 30 then draw a box halving that and select the north west part of that and then draw a box selecting the northwest part of that and the cemetery is inside that box. This translates to mean the northwest corner of section 30 in a forty acre area of that 640 acre section. However the cemetery is fenced in about 1/5 of an acre. Another description of the location says behind a decaying barn on the west side of Winchester Pike approximately at the Winchester Pike and County Lake Road intersection. Also in the north section of Hale Blvd and Winchester Road. Hale Blvd is a residential road that serves several houses. Gates are locked to this cemetery.

At the intersection of Highway 231/431 North, at the Winchester Pike go out Winchester Pike 5+ miles to a Hale road on your left Turn there and go about 300 yards to a gate and with permission, a key to the gate, and instructions on how not to disturb the cattle, go due north past the old barn to the cemetery about 175 yards from the gate. There are alternate entrances to the livestock fenced area where this cemetery resides.

Notice the labeling under the tree line but west of the old barn seen as a brown color to the southeast of the cemetery.
