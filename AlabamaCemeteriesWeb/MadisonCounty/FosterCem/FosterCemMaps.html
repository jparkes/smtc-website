FOSTER CEMETERY, J.B. Walker Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Foster Cemetery of Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked. GPS N 34.980409 X W -86.4332423.
Go out Winchester Road  past New Market to Buddy Williams Road and turn left (west). Go to Old Limestone Road and turn northwest (right) and pass the Pleasant Grove Cemetery and the cemetery is between two houses just back from School House Road on the left.

This image shows the cemetery graphically highlighted in yellow sitting between two homes.
