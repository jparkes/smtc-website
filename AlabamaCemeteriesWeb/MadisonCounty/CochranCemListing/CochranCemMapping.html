COCHRAN CEMETERY  (New Market) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Cochran Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map. GPS: N 34.90460 X W 86.42332. Since this is a large sprawling cemetery the GPS is in the center near the entrance.

At the intersection of Winchester Highway and Hwy 231 North go east to northeast out Winchester Hwy to New Market. Turn right or east on Hurricane Road inside New Market (only major eastern road out of New Market) to Davis Street and next turn south or right on Davis street and merge onto Cemetery Rd. Just after that look to your left at the stones and the entrance way to the cemetery. The cemetery fronts Cemetery Street and sprawls eastward & then back to the north.

Notice the entrance road here. Only a small part of the cemetery can be seen due to the trees being leafed out.
