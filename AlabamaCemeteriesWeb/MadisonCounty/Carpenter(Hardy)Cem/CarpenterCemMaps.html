CARPENTER (Hardy) CEMETERY, (Bo Howard Rd), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Carpenter Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map as marked at GPS N 34.89508 X W -86.69792 Specific Location: Section 2 Township 2S Range 2W. NE NE SE. On the barn grounds of the Bo Howard pig farm. Mr. Howard has carefully protected the stones at these graves.

On Hwy 231 go several miles north and turn left on Grimwood Rd. Go several miles and the road will merge onto Tony Rd. Take that and go about 1 1/2 miles and turn south (left) on Bo Howard Rd. When the road bends left go straight ahead into the farm road and with permission to cross the locked gate follow that until the road bends back left (east). After bending left the cemetery will be on the left under a cedar tree behind some old Texas Bear Grass Cactuses that live for hundreds of years and were probably planted after the tombstones were set here.
