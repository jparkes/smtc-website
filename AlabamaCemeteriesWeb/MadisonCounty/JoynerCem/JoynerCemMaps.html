JOYNER CEMETERY, (SE of Junction of Wall Triana Hwy and Nick Davis Road), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Joyner Cemetery. It is given on national mapping, but there are errors not included above. Alternative location: LOCATION: Section 5-3S-2W. S/2 NE NE SE. Southwest of the Intersection of Nick Davis Road and Wall Highway.
At the intersection of Hwy 72 and Wall-Triana Highway to north about 8 miles passing Capshaw & Pine Grove Roads along the way. When you see a gravel/dirt pit on the left behind that upon the hill top is the cemetery now in a grove of tall trees.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location occupying a spot in a round grove of trees across a dirt pit carved around it.
