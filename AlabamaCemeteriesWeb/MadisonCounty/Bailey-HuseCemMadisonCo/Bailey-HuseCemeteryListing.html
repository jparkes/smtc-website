BAILEY-HUSE CEMETERY, (Cecil Ashburn Dr), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

Mapping the location

HUSE, Cynthea, 30 May 1783 - 6 Sep 1847. 8845C/8845/8844/8846 *

Unknown soul, "Adult age 18-24, reinterred at this site 17 Dec 1997. 8840/8842/8843

Unknown soul, "Child, reinterred at this site 17 Dec 1997. 8841/8842/8843
 

* It is likely this is the same name as Hughes/Hughs, but misspelled on the tombstone or just a different spelling.

As you can see the only marked stone is that for Cynthea Huse. Fenced here is about a 1/4 acre area, but I am unaware if unmarked graves are here in the open area. This cemetery is known as the Bailey Cemetery on maps, and I am not aware of the reason, except somewhere in this valley is known as the Bailey Cove along with the Jones Valley and a road named Carl T. Jones Rd..  Maybe someone's Bailey ancestors are interred here in unmarked graves. Also Cecil Ashburn Drive is nearby.
