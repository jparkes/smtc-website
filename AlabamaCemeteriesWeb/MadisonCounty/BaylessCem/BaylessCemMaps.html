BAYLESS-EDDINS CEMETERY, (Old Rail Road Bed Road), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Bayless-Eddins Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map.

At the intersection of Highway 72 and Old Rail Road Bed Road travel north several miles and go past Highway 53 (Ardmore Hwy). From Hwy 53 go two miles and when the road comes under tall trees turn left into the residential dead end road. Immediately cross the creek and the cemetery will be south to southwest of you about 200 yards upon the rise and still into the edge of the woods. It is hard to find so go there only in the winter so you can find the massive monuments because they blend in with the tree trunks. Since this is private land all around get permission.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location of the two separate monuments occupying this woodland cemetery. While there I took out several small trees which should make the stones easier to see from a distance.
