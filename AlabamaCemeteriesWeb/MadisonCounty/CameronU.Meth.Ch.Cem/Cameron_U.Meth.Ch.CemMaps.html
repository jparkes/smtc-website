CAMERON UNITED METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, Hurricane Creek Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Bailes-Broyles Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.84835 X W -86.39856. Alternate Location.

Go out Winchester Road and turn right on County Lake Road. Follow that and turn right (south) on Hurricane Creek Road and go almost a mile and 1/2 and turn left (east) into the Cameron Church Road. The Cemetery is on three sides of the church in the location, 400 yards down that road on the left.

This close up aerial view shows the church and cemetery in close proximity.
