ROWE CEMETERY, (Wall-Triana Highway), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Rowe Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked as Rowe Cem.. Specific Location: Section 8-5S-2W. C. N/2 NE. This cemetery is located in a grove of trees on Triana Blvd. north of Triana in Southern Madison County Alabama.

At the intersection of Interstate 65 & Wall-Triana Highway go south until Wall Triana Highway veers left and continue to follow Wall-Triana Hwy for 2/10 of a mile and look to your left at a clump of trees near the road. A little further down on the same side of the road is the Barclay-Toney Cemetery.

Notice the marker Rowe Cemetery indicating the cemetery location. Be prepared to fight the dense brush to get into this cemetery and also to see the stones.

This zoomed in map with a red check shows where the cemetery can be found in the tree lined thicket. surrounded on two sides by dug out soil from road building.
