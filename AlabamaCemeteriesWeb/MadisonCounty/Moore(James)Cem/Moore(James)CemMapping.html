JAMES MOORE CEMETERY, Elkwood Section Rd., MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
 The GPS coordinates of the James Moore Cemetery are shown here as N 34.97641 X W -86.60189. On Elkwood Section Road.
 North of Huntsville at the intersection of of Hwy 231 & Elkwood Section Road go west on Elkwood Section Rd about 1.6 miles and look to your left (south) at a thicket of evergreen trees where you will see the single monument.

This street view photo shows the single monument and clump of evergreen trees where the this monument exist on the south side of Elkwood Section Road. If you go here be careful this stone is very old & fragile. The strange question comes to my mine of where James Moore a Revolutionary Moore Soldier is interred. Is he here in this place that bears his name. If so why has no-one set a monument to his honor?
