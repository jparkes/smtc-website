MATLOCK CEMETERY, Grimwood Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topozone map shows the GPS of the Matlock Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.9062 W -86.61527. Alternate Location: Section 34 Township 1S Range 1W, W/2 W/2 SE/4.

 At the intersection of Hwy 431 & Grimwood Rd turn left (west) and travel past the Plainview Cemetery on the right. Continue to Matlock Rd on your right and go into the place and the cemetery is in the yard of the first home on your right.

Notice the cemetery as marked on the map inside a white picket fence.
