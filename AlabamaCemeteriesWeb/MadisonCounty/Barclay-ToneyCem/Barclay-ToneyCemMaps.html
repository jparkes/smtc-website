BARCLAY-TONEY CEMETERY, (Wall-Triana Highway), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Barclay-Toney Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked as Cem.. Specific Location: Section 9,5S,2W, N/2, SW SW SW.

At the intersection of Interstate 65 & Wall-Triana Highway go south until Wall Triana Highway veers left and continue to follow Wall-Triana Hwy for another mile and look to your left at a long clump of trees. The cemetery is in the south end of that clump in the center of the cemetery. If you pass Beadle lane you have passed it.

Notice the marker Barclay-Teney Cemetery indicating the cemetery location. be prepared to fight the dense brush to get into the cemetery and also to see the stones.

This zoomed in map with a red check shows where the cemetery can be found in this long thicket.
