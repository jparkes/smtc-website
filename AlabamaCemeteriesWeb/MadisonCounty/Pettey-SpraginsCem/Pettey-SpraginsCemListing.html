PETTEY-SPRAGINS CEMETERY, Mint Springs Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
Mapping the Location

 

PETTEY, In Memory of Eli Williams Pettey, Born May 3. 1833. Died July 1, 1854 Aged 21 Years, 1 Month and 27 Days.
PETTEY, Zechariah Pettey - Born May 28. 1793 - Died July 8, 1854 Aged 61 years, 1 Month, and 10 days.
SPRAGINS, Thomas E., b. ca 1821 death date unknown. (perhaps buried here beside his 2nd wife. 1st married Lucy Crutcher. After her death he married Mary E. Pettey.)
SPRAGINS, Mary E. Spragins, Wife of Thomas E. Spragins, Born Oct. 4, 1823 - Died April 8, 1879. Kitchen Bros., Winchester. Stonemasons. (Mary E. Pettey -m- Thomas E. Spragins April 29, 1847.)
SPRAGINS, Elias Bruce Spragins - Born April 22. 1848 - Died Feb. 6, 1899. Thy will be done.
WALKER, Thomas Covington Walker, Son of James R. & Mary T. Walker, born Jan. 21. 1884, died Jan. 7. 1888.
