ECHOLS-HOLLOWELL CEMETERY, (Cozy Creek Rd), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Echols-Hollowell Cemetery of Madison Co. Alabama. The location is indicated with a check at GPS N 34.89485 X W 86.52681. Alternate Location: T.2 - R.1E. Section 4, NE NE SW

From Hwy 231 take Steger Rd east for something less than 3 miles and at the intersection of Moores Mill & Steger Road turn right into the new subdivision. At the first road left (subdivision circle) go to the end of the road and there in the thicket is the fallen stones of this cemetery. I knocked on the next door house to alert neighbors of my visit and accidentally awakened a sleeping police officer. Now that is a major opps. Shift workers need their rest. Sorry, but maybe I didn't otherwise cause any alarm. The first map above shows the only house next to the cemetery as of now on the south side of the cemetery.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location occupying some of the lots in a subdivision. This map was cut before any houses were built on the Cozy Creek Circle (or road)
