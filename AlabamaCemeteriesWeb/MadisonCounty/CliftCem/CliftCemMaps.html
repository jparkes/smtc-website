CLIFT CEMETERY, (south side of Hwy 72 in present day shopping center), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This aerial map shows the GPS of the location of the original Clift Cemetery of Madison Alabama as N 34.74968 X W -86.75759. It is now lost & abandoned as a graveyard.

Located in the what is now the parking lot of the Shops of Madison Shopping Center south of Hwy 72 prior to being moved (at lest the visible stones) to the old Madison City Cemetery.

This zoomed in map shows the approximate original spot of this cemetery on Google.

This image shows the Shops of Madison Shopping center where the cemetery at one time resided. This is across the road from the Clift Farms and due east of a small dry stream that crosses Hwy 72.
