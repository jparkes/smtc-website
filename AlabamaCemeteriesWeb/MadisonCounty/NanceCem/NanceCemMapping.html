NANCE CEMETERY, Sharps Hollow Rd. (eastern), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Nance Cemetery of Madison Alabama as N 34.8761514 X W -86.378494. The location is indicated in the center of the map under the green check. Alternate Location: Section 11-2-2E. S/2 9/2 SE/4. Behind (100 yards northeast) the old Nance home.

At the intersection of Highway 231/431 North, at the Winchester Pike go out Winchester Pike several miles to County Lake Road and turn right. Follow that to Hurricane Road and turn left (or north). Follow that for several miles and turn right on Sharps Hollow Road. Follow that and when it bends back right (south) turn into the old Nance home and with permission go to the home and back behind and northeast 100 yards to the cemetery in inside a chain link fence.

Notice the long residential road and the necessity of going behind the old home.
