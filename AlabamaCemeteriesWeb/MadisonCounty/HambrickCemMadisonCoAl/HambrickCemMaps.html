HAMBRICK CEMETERY, (Upper Hurricane Road, Hurricane Valley) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Hambrick Cemetery. The GPS as calculated on ACME Mapping by Wayne Austin is:N 34.88903 X W 86.37845. 

 At the intersection of Winchester Rd in New Market go east on Hurricane Road and then veer right continuing on Hurricane Rd. Turn left on Upper Hurricane Rd and bend around to the south and when there is a steep hill on your left look to your right into a cultivated field for the cemetery. On your left far upon the hill is the Baker Cemetery. The cemetery has been recently cleared of brush.
Vandals, treasure hunters, devil worshippers, etc beware - the neighbors, land owners & Madison County Sheriff are watching for your illicit activities.

Notice the cemetery in a triangular shaped area of land in the middle of a field. The old home place is nearby.
