OTEY CEMETERY (Hwy 231/431, Green Lawn Plantation), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the Otey Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map - GPS N 34.83601 X W -86.56957. Alternate Locator: Section 30 Township 2 Range 1E - NW NW SW. This cemetery is on the grounds of the lovely old Otey mansion (Greenlawn Plantation) approximately one mile south of Meridianville, Ala. on Highway 231-431 N.

From Huntsville go north on Hwy 231 almost to Merdianville and turn right in to the Greenlawn Plantation and subdivision.

This close up aerial view shows a red check where the cemetery resides. There is a rock wall surrounding the cemetery.

This close up aerial view shows a green arrow pointing down at the trees under which resides the cemetery behind a rock wall. The old plantation home is the one on the left closest to the Hwy 231/431. This is a private home but if it ever gets opened to the public it will be a great visit.
