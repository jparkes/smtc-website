WIGGINS CEMETERY, (Penta-Star Dr SW), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Wiggins Cemetery of Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked as Wiggins Cem.. Specific Location: Sec. 4-5S-2W. NW NW NW.

At the intersection of Interstate 65 & Wall-Triana Highway go south until Wall Triana Highway junctions with Electronics Blvd. Turn left there and start around the circle until you reach the junction of Pentastar Dr SW. Turn right there and just before that road bends back south (right) look left out in the field at a round grove of trees and just inside the tree line is the cemetery.

Notice the check marked Wiggins Cemetery indicating the cemetery location. As of now the cemetery is fenced and in good shape.
