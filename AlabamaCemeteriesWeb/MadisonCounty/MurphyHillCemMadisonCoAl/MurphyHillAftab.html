MURPHY HILL CHURCH CEMETERY (Murphy Hill Rd), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

Teri Aftab went to be with the Lord, Thursday at a local hospital, she was 50 years old.
She is survived by her husband Mr Aftab, four daughters Sofia Fortney and husband Glenn, Saira, Samina and Allison Aftab; two sisters Marie Simpson and Donna Peterson; one brother Richard Frank Jr.; and her parents Richard Frank Sr. and Janet Frank.
A visitation with the family will be held Sunday April 14, at Hazel Green Funeral Home from 2 until 6 PM. Funeral services will be at 2 PM Monday at Murphy Hill Baptist Church. Burial will follow in the adjoining cemetery. She will lie in state from 12 until 2 PM.
