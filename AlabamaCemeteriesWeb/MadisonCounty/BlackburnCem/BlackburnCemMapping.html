BLACKBURN CEMETERY, Maysville Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
 The GPS coordinates of the Blackburn Cemetery are shown here as N 34.83466 X W -86.45589. Alternate Location: Section 30-Range 2 Township 2E. NW NW NW SE. On Maysville Road.
 At the intersection of Winchester Rd (Hwy 65) and County Lake Road go east about 1/2 mile on County Lake Road and turn south (right) on Maysville Road. Follow that for about 3/4 mile and look west (right) at the clump of trees where this cemetery can be found. Park your vehicle on the field road.

This street view from Google shows the clump of trees where the cemetery exist clearly on the west side of Maysville Road. Careful here. A man that lives next to this cemetery has been encountering burglars recently and the burglars park here and invade their homes. Might want to go past this cemetery to their homes and tell them your intentions. I did not do this and they came out and hung over the fence under suspicion that I was a burglar. That was very strange encounter considering they had concealed guns and blood in their eyes. Probably better to call the law than try to convince a bunch of idiots with revenge on their minds in spite of the fact they did not own the property.
