Mr. W. Howard Norris. a grandson of William E. Norris. escorted Dorothy Scott Johnson to this family Cemetery and the extra information (except marriage records and census) was supplied by William around 1978.  If there ever were a private family cemetery this is it because the folks that take care of it are family of and related to about all the members interred here.  I would advise us researchers to go here less often than an old abandoned cemetery that lines the roads up and down all the counties. Those need our attention more. We may not be as welcome here or at least as needed for help in preservation.  However if you are related by all means visit here. I was shown this cemetery by Gayla Ann Hale Apr 17, 2014. It is difficult to find without an aid. C. Wayne Austin 17 Feb 2015.
