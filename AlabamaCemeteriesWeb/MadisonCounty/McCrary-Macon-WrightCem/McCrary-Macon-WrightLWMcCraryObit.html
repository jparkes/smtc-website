McCRARY-MACON-WRIGHT CEMETERY (Macon Lane) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
Note: The following obituary is from the Huntsville Times of Feb. 7. 1971.
Miss Lucy McCrary. A funeral for Miss Lucy McCrary, 90, of Route 5. Huntsville who died Saturday in a local hospital after a long illness. will be today at 2 p.m. In the McCrary Cemetery with Billy Davidson officiating and Laughlin Service Funeral Home directing.
Survivors Include four nieces. Mrs. A. M. Johnson. Henderson, Tenn., Mrs. B. E. Evans. Weirsdale, Fla.. Mrs. Anna Roseborough. and Miss Alice Thomas, both of Huntsville; two nephews. Thomas McCrary. New Market. and James Watts, Huntsville.
Pallbearers will be Leroy Ford. Shannon Ford. Pickett Eslinger, J. Allison Smith. William Hillis and Tommy McCrary. The family requests no flowers. The body will be at the Rt. 5. Huntsville, address from 11 a. m. until 2 p.m.
