BENDALL CEMETERY, (Hwy 72, Equestrian Farm), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Bendall Graves of Madison County, Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked with the red check. This cemetery is not reported in any county or national mapping or books of the county that I can determine. So basically this is a new find.

At the intersection of Hwy 72 and Jeff Road go west toward Athens and turn north (right) on Christopher Dr. Follow that until it ends at a horse farm and the cemetery can be found in a tree lined thicket after the road branches between a residential home and the horse barn.

This map shows the Christopher Drive layout and where the cemetery is located

Notice the stand of Black Gum Trees labeled above where these graves are located.
