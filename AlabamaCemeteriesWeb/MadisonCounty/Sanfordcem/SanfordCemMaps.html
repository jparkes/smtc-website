SANFORD CEMETERY, (Stone Dr. West), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Sanford Cemetery. N 34.73608 X W 86.42190. Alternative Location: Township 3S Range 2E, Section 33, NE NE SE NW - South of Stone Drive out in a large field.

 Go east out Hwy 72 from Huntsville and after passing Moores Mill Road continue on to Brock Road and turn (left) north. After you cross the Railroad Tracks turn right on Stone Drive and go about 1/2 mile to the cemetery on the left out in a field in a clump of trees. There is a right of way whereby the path is maintained to the cemetery and not cultivated. 

Notice the brushy clump of trees in the distance about 250 yards away where this long abandoned cemetery can be found. Looks like a right of way is being maintained by the land owner, but no visitors who care about its upkeep are going to this cemetery. The name of the cemetery is spelled incorrectly on Google maps as Stanford.
