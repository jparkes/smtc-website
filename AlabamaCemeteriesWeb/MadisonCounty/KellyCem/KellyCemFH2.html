KELLY CEMETERY Kelly Cemetery Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

HOWSE, Ida N., Dau. of J. A. and L. A. Phillips. Born Jan. 17, 1855 - died Jan. 6, 1894 0601/0602

Ida's husband, Isaac Henry Howse, died C. 1891 in Manor, Tex.  Ida Nancy (called "Dolly") Phillips married Isaac Henry Howse, her second cousin. Some of the House family that went to Texas adopted the "w" instead of the "u"  as in House". Isaac Henry House's tombstone in Manor, Texas, states he was born in Williamson County, Tenn.
