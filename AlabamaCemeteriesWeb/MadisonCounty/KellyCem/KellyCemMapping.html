KELLY CEMETERY Kelly Cemetery Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Kelly Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.8106 W -86.6463. Alternate Location: Section 5 Township 3S Range 1W. NW NW SE NE. Located directly behind a large frame house built about 1830 by David Emmit Kelly on Kelly Cemetery Road. The house was sold out of the Kelly family in 1878 but the cemetery is still maintained by the Kelly family.


 Go out Research Blvd to Kelly Cemetery Road and turn right (east) and go less than a mile and the cemetery is behind the a home. The next home has a right of way road back to the cemetery, but I still think it best to get permission from the homeowner in front (due north) of the cemetery.

Notice the yellow Cem indicating the cemetery location occupying an area inside a fence.
