GRIFFIN CEMETERY (Monroe Rd) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

This Aerial map shows the GPS of the Griffin Cemetery under the white check. The GPS is calculated as N 34.85533 X W -86.62726. This map shows the farm road going by the cemetery site. It is a private road.

 Go up Hwy 231/431 past Meridianville to Monroe Rd and turn left (west). When the road bends back left a farm road to the left is available which goes back southeast. There is a locked gate there. You will need to see the farm manager to view these monuments. I think he lives across the road. When I was there he was there too which made the task for me easier. As a rule I don't give out names without permission.

Notice the green balloon indicating the cemetery location occupying an open area to the east of he small barn. One can see the stones as two light spots just above the tree shadow, on the ground under and just north of the green arrow. North out of the reach of this image is the old chimney where the old home stood at one time.
