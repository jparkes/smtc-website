HEREFORD CEMETERY, (W. off Pulaski Pike), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Hereford Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map as marked at GPS N 34.85286 X W -86.64874 Specific Location: Section 20 Township 2S Range 1W. NE SW NE. West off of Pulaski Pike.

From Huntsville go north on Pulaski Pike. Pass by Bob Wade Lane then Liberty Hill Rd. Go about 3/4 a mile after passing Liberty Hill Rd to Sunny Dell Rd which may have a locked gate on it. Turn left into Sunny Dell Lane or get permission from the Farm Caretaker to go here. When Sunny Dell Lane bears back due west the cemetery will be in that plot of fenced land on your right where a small fallen building is located. The cemetery has a chain link fence surrounding it.

This aerial view shows the tall trimmed cedar and the area where the cemetery is located under the big red check.
