St CLAIR CEMETERY, Hurricane Creek Valley, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
St. CLAIR, George Rice, Dec 10, 1914 - May 31, 1977.

Obituary of Mrs. Annie Ruth St. Clair, from the Huntsville Times:

Mrs. Annie St. Clair. A funeral for Mrs. Annie Ruth St. Clair, 49. of Rt. 2, New Market. who died Thursday in the Fifth Avenue Hospital, will be Saturday
at 2 p. m. at the Hurricane Primitive Baptist Church with the Rev. Carl Spraggins officiating. Burial will be in the St. Clair Cemetery with Spry Funeral Home
Directing. Survivors include her husband, G. R. (Doc) St. Clair; five daughters, Mrs. Betty Parker. Mrs. Linda Samples, Miss Karen St. Clair, Miss Melissa
St. Clair, and Miss Malesa St. Clair, all of New Market; a son, Kenneth St. Clair of New Market; father, L. E. St. Clair of New Market; a sister, Mrs. Mildred
Mildred Anderson of New Market; a brother. Leslie St. Clair of New Market; three grandchildren.

Posted by Dorothy by Johnson in her book.
