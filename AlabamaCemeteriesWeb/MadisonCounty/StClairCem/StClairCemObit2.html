St CLAIR CEMETERY, Hurricane Creek Valley, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
Obituary of George St. Clair (from Huntsville News June 2, 1977)

Services for George R. "Doc" St. Clair, 62, Rt. 2, New Market, Ala., who died Tuesday at home, will be today at 2 p.m. at Hurricane Primitive Baptist Church. the Rev. Ben Atkinson
and Howard McNelly will officiate. Burial will be in St. Clair Cemetery. Survivors include his wife, Mrs. Ruth E. St. Clair, New Market; five daughters, Mrs. Linda Samples and Mrs.
Karen Buir, both of South Pittsburg, Tennessee; Mrs. Betty Parker, Miss Melissa St. Clair and Miss Milessa St. Clair all of New Market; one stepson. Eddie Mason of Huntsville: three
stepdaughters, Mrs. Eva Towery of Tuscaloosa, Mrs. Rebecca Buck of Lincoln, Tenn. , Mrs. Carolyn Harris of New Market; three brothers, Homer St. Clair, Raymond St. Clair and
James St. Clair, all of New Market; three sisters, Mrs. Louise Miller of Grass Valley, Calif., Mrs. James Woods and Mrs. Edna Hereford both of New Market; 16 grandchildren.
