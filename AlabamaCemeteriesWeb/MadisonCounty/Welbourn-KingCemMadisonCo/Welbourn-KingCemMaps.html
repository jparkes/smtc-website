WELBOURN-KING CEMETERY (Hwy 431/231) MADISON COUNTY TENNESSEE
This topographical map shows the GPS of the King Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map at the red balloon as N 34.92330 X W -86.57170.

LOCATION: Township 1 Range 1-E, Section 30, NW NW SW. This cemetery is located in front of (now) a used car lot on the east side of Highway 431-231 just south of the main part of Hazel Green.

Here we see the cemetery beside the road next to a car lot and behind a failing cable fence.
