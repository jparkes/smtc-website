TOWNSEND-COOVER CEMETERY, (Hwy 231, Hazelgreen, Madison, Al.), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Townsend-Coover Cemetery of Madison Alabama.

In Hazel Green & from the junction of Hwy 231 & Joe Quick Road go north about 3/4 of a mile and turn left into the field road which used to go to an old home. after traveling 1/4 of a mile on this field road during the dormant growing season you will see the cemetery out in the field to your left. Go here only after crops are harvested to prevent damage to the crops.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location occupying a plot of land in the middle of a large field.
