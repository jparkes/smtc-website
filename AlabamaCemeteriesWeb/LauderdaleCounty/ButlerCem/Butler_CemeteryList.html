BUTLER CEMETERY, (Wheeler Dam Road right on County Rd 84), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA
Mapping the location

Area View: 8492

BUTLER, Lee, 8 may 1829 - 19 Jul 1901. "I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the faith". 8490C, 8490, 8491

Only only stone was found in this cemetery, but the large fenced area indicated there may be several other graves, but without monuments.  This cemetery was the last one photographed late in the evening so the photos all appear dark. The tall cedars appear ghostly in these photos when special affects are applied 8492R,  8492X
