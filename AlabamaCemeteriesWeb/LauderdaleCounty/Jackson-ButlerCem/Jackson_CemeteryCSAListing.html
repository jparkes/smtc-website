JACKSON-BUTLER CEMETERY, (Wheeler Dam Road, behind Stock auction barn), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA
aka BELEW CEMETERY


 Confederate States Army Soldiers interred in the Jackson-Butler Cemetery

BUTLER, William P., 22 Sep 1845 - 1 Jan 1916. "Father." (C.S.A.;William Polk Butler; Born Lauderdale Co., Rogersville, Ala, discharge date May 1865, discharged at Pond Springs Ala. near Courtland, Cavalry, Roddy's Escort, enlisted Sept 1863 as Private; shares stone with Martha J. Butler.) 1475/1482

McCARLEY, J.T., 8 Jan 1835 - 12 Mar 1902. (C.S.A., Cavalry 9th Alabama Regiment Co. E, Pension #26188 by Lucy A. McCarley, Widow. Witnesses W.T. Weaver, M.H. Harvell.) 1453/1454

SIMPSON, Preston, 1842 - 1932. (C.S.A., Presly Simpson, Cavalry, 9th Alabama Co. E, Authority, Letter from Capt John H. Lester dated 24 Jan 1911. Enlistment date 1862 Lauderdale County, Ingrams Crossroads, Private, Remarks: 9th Regiment was sometimes called 7th Regiment; shares stone with Catherine Simpson & Paul Simpson.) 1464/1465
