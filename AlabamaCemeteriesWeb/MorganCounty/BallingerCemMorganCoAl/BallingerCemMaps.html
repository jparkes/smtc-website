BALLINGER CEMETERY, Talucah, MORGAN COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates (under the red & white check mark) of the Ballinger/Ballenger Cemetery located on Sharp Ford Rd at N 34.555317 W 86.71283 in Decatur Alabama.

From Hwy 231 in Huntsville cross the Tennessee River Bridge and turn west (right) on Hwy 36. Follow that for about 5 miles and turn north (right) on Talucah Rd. Follow That for about 4 miles passing thru Talucah. Turn left on Sharp Ford Rd and go about 3/4 of a mile and the cemetery is on your right as can be seen from the road.
Maps from ACME Mapping 2.0, & Google modified for this site by Wayne Austin 16 Mar 2014.
