AUSTIN CEMETERY (Spring Ave SW. Austinville) DECATUR, MORGAN COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Austin Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2:N 34.57023 W 87.00025.

From 6th Ave aka Central Parkway SW turn right on 14th St SW and ago about 1 1/2 miles and just after a sharp bend in the road south where Spring St and Austinville Rd SW merge at the junction of Cedar St SW and Spring Ave SW look to your right at the signage and open space of the cemetery on the front side.

This aerial view shows the larbe open area of this cemetery inside a well maintained which fence line. Charles Austin b. abt 1861 d. 1933 was an important businessman in this area and the area still carries his name today as Austinville and Austin High School.
Maps from ACME Mapping 2.0, & Google modified for this site by Wayne Austin 28 Jun 2013.
