HALL CEMETERY, (East Limestone Rd & Hall Cem. Rd) , COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map combined with the TOPOZONE mapping data shows the GPS of the Hall Cemetery. Location: SW quarter of SE quarter, Section 15 , T2S, R3W on Hall Cemetery Road.

Go east on Nick Davis Road from Athens several miles (about 8) and turn (left) north on E. Limestone Road and go several miles (about 4) to Harvest Road and turn right. As the Harvest Road bends south veer off to the left on Hall Cemetery Road and the cemetery is on that road abt .2 tenths of a mile.

The red check denotes the location of the cemetery on the west side of Hall Cemetery Road. It is under a grove of trees.
