STEWART CEMETERY, Nuclear Plant Road, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial view shows the GPS of the Robinson Cemetery at N 34.71945 X W -86.99752.  Location Section 6 SW quarter, Township 4S, Range 4W, near Jones Cross Roads. This cemetery has a wrought iron fence around it.


This road map show the cemetery north of the Nuclear Plant Road and east of the Lucas Ferry Road in Limestone Co Alabama.

With this aerial view we can see the tree lined grove under the red check where the cemetery is located.
