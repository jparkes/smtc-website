HASTINGS CEMETERY (Hwy 53,near I-65) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Hasting Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2:N 34.975901 W 86.880021.

From Athens go north up I-65 about 14+ miles and take the Hwy 53 aka 7th St. exit (Exit 365). Bear right onto Hwy 53 toward Ardmore. Go a few hundred yards & turn left into the road into the cemetery.

This image from Google shows the road leading to the cemetery off of Hwy 53/I-65. One can see the cemetery from I-65 as you go by on the (right) east side.
