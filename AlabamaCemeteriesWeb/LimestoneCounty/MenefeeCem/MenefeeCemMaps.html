MENEFEE CEMETERY (Capshaw Road), (East) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Menefee Cemetery. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. Alternate location: SW quarter of SE quarter, Sect. 17, T3S, R3W on Capshaw Road.

Go east of Athens on Hwy 72 about 6 miles to Frenches Mill (or the junction of Mooresville Road and Hwy 72). Turn (left) north and go about .6 tenths of a mile to Capshaw Road and turn right (east) Follow Capshaw Road .5 miles and look left (north at a home and the cemetery on the back left location. For access to the cemetery secure permission at the house in front of the cemetery. The gate is accessible on their side.   
This aerial view uses a yellow pointer to show the cemetery located in the small square fenced area in back of a new house, fronting on Capshaw Road.
