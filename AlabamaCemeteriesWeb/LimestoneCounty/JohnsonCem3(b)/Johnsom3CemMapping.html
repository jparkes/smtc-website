JOHNSON CEMETERY #3, Hwy 31, Bee Line Hwy, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Johnson Cemetery #3 as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2.1:N 34.75331 W 86.95654.

From Athens go south on Hwy 31 several miles past Roy Long Road East (on your right). The Johnson Cemetery is just past that on the left near the road and easily seen.

This aerial view shows the cemetery inside a defined and maintained area. This photo took place on a day a burial was taking place.
Maps from ACME Mapping, MS Streets & Trips, & Google modified for this site by Wayne Austin 20 Aug 2017.
