BETHLEHEM CEMETERY, Bethlehem Cemetery Rd, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Bethlehem Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper 2.0: N 34.71089 W 87.05487.
Alternate Location: SE quarter of NE quarter, Sect. 9, T5S, R5W on a gravel road which turns south beside the Bethlehem Church.

From Athens go South on Hwy 231 and west on Brownsferry Road to Lucas Ferry Road and turn south. Go to Nuclear Plant Road and turn west. Go about 2 miles west to Bethlehem Church or Bethlehem Church Road (gravel) and turn south. Go several hundred yards to the cemetery on the right under a stand of trees. There is some maintenance occurring on the grounds, but hardly any on the tombs.

This aerial view uses a green marker to show the cemetery located under a few trees. I attempted to draw a yellow outline to approximate the area of the cemetery.

This view of the cemetery shows the Bethlehem Road branching south off of Nuclear Plant Road. However the Bethlehem road hardly goes further than the cemetery except for capable farmers and bushman. The road goes from being a gravel road to a dirt one.
