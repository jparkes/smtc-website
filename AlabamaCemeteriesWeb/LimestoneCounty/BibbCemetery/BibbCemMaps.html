BIBB CEMETERY (north side of Bibb-Garrett Road), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Bibb Cemetery commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.

Bibb Cemetery is found on the north side of Bibb Road behind the Thomas Bibb House. From Athens Alabama travel east on Hwy 72 for 5 miles and turn south on Mooresville Road for several miles. Pass thru Belle Mina and the Bibb House and then turn right or west on Bibb-Garrett Road. The cemetery is in a clump of trees just a few hundred yards down that road and next to the road inside a fence.
