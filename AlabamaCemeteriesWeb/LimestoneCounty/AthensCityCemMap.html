ATHENS CITY CEMETERY. Location map layout, WASHINGTON STREET, ATHENS, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA


Scroll around to read the various location names and numbers. Lots are numbers left to right from 1 to about 2000. If you are unable to make out the specific location number then get a nearby location number that you can read and count over to the specific location you wish to investigate. Sorry I did not take the time to make it perfectly readable. The  Limestone County Archives has this map and can help you if you call them. This map shows the lot purchaser, Lot Number, and the cemetery Area. This may not be the name on the gravestone and each lot may be larger than one grave. If this map does not display large enough for you to read then copy it to your graphic image program and you should be able to read it or enlarge it more.
