BLACK (J.L.) CEMETERY, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the James L Black Cemetery. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. N 34.73925, W -87.09400.

From Athens go west on Hwy 72 and turn south (left) on 7 mile post road. the road eventually becomes McCormack Rd and when that takes a 90 degree bend to the west (right) go 100+ yards to a field road on left and that leads one to the thicket where this little cemetery can be found.

This aerial view shows the cemetery inside a clump of trees & next to a field road to the crop land.
Maps from ACME Mapping, MS Streets & Trips, & Google modified for this site by Wayne Austin 12 Jun 2014.
