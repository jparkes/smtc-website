HATCHETT CEMETERY, Thach Road, MADISON, ALABAMA
 This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hatchett Cemetery as North 34.91969 X West -86.79948.

Go out Hwy 251 (aka old Hwy 31) several miles to the Thach Road and turn east (right). When that road bends back south (left) go into the road there and the cemetery is at the end of that road on the left.
