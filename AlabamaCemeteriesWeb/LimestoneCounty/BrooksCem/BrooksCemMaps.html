BROOKS CEMETERY (south side of Holt Springer Road), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Brooks Cemetery commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.

Brooks Cemetery is found by going west on out Highway 72 about 12 miles to Hardy Road. Turn south (left) there and go to Holt Springer Road and turn left again and go 1/2 mile and find this very small cemetery on the south (right) side of the road.
Alternate Location: NE Quarter, Sect. 15. T3S. R6W just to the side of the road. 

This aerial view above shows the cemetery located on the side of the road just before the long crooked driveway.
