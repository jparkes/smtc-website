YARBROUGH CEMETERY #2, (Snake Road east off of County Road 33) , LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of this cemetery commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.

Yarbrough Cemetery #2 Location: Southwest quarter, Section 17, T3S, R5W. Going west on Highway 72, turn left (south) onto county road 33, then left at Snake Road, Follow the road until it ends at a gate. Go through the gate and the cemetery is on top of the hill on the left in a grove of trees.

This aerial view above shows the cemetery located in a grove of trees.
I make no claims that this mapping without a doubt will find this cemetery as it is subject to confirmation.
