YARBROUGH CEMETERY #1, (NE of Athens 10 miles) , LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of this cemetery commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.

Yarbrough Cemetery is found on the north side of the road bank of Hwy 72 about 9 miles west of Athens Alabama or three miles east of the Elk River crossing of Hwy 72. It is across from but toward the northeast from Clements Baptist Church. The yellow pointer above shows the location.

This aerial view above shows the cemetery located in the tree line on the north edge of Highway 72.
