MOORESVILLE CEMETERY, Mooresville Alabama) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Old Mooresville Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude (34.6206N) and Longitude (86.8784W).

From Athens go east toward Huntsville on Hwy 72 several miles to Mooresville Road (French's Mill community) and turn south (right). Go several miles to historic Mooresville Alabama (passing over I-65) and turn left at the "T" in the road and then turn back right on Broad Street continuing south in Mooresville. Follow that thru downtown Mooresville until it dead ends. After the pavement ends in the winter park your vehicle and you will cross the (low) gate and walk to this cemetery. Follow the gravel road as it curves around several hundred (about 500) yards and you will see the stone signage on your right for the cemetery.
This aerial view above shows the Old town Cemetery south of the town of Mooresville. Other cemeteries which are labeled above are the Thach-Hulsey Cemetery found on this site. Also I left unlabeled in the far upper right the Peoples Family Cemetery. In the bottom right is the Thatch cemetery (black) which may have been destroyed. The far left red check is the Center Star Cemetery also a black cemetery. You can see some of these cemeteries on the above map.
