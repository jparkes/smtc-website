MITCHELL CEMETERY #2, Wooley Springs Road, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Mitchell Cemetery #2 commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude. GPS: N 34.89834  X  W 86.78849.

Mitchell Cemetery #2 is found by turning east off of Hwy 251 on Wooley Springs Road and traveling until the road bends back south (right), then follow south about 1/4 mile and turn (east) left into a long lane with permission and go to the house where you will find the cemetery on the west side of the home next to the woodland inside a chain link fence.
