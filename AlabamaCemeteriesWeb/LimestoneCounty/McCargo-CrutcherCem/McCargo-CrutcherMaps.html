McCARGO-NEWBY-CRUTCHER CEMETERY (Harvest Road), (NorthEast) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the McCargo-Crutcher Cemetery. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. Another location method is: Section 23 Township 2 Range 3, Northeast Quarter of the Southwest Quarter.  

Go east on Hwy 251 (old Hwy 31) to the Harvest Road. Turn east (right) there and go about 3 miles and near the end passing over Limestone Creek. The cemetery is north of the road in a cultivated field in a round grove of trees. It is marked with a red check on the above map.  
This aerial view uses a yellow pointer to show the cemetery located in the small clump of trees. The cemetery resides in the middle to eastern and southern part of that clump of trees. Evidence is seen of graves in other parts of that grove of trees though.

This is more a view into the area that has few if any graves, except for the lower left corner of the photo where we can see the Crutcher family graves monuments
