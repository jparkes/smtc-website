ANDREWS CEMETERY (French's Mill) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

Alfred A. Sloan, Aug. 14, 1810, Tenn. - Jan. 23, 1888, Limestone County, Ala.

ALFRED (ALFORD) A. SLOAN was the son of SAMUEL SLOAN and ELIZABETH PATTERSON.
He married ELIZA STINNETT 8 March 1836 in Limestone Co., AL.
They had one child, LOUISIANA (LOU) SLOAN who was born 1836/1837. ELIZA died shortly thereafter.
LOUISIANA married TOM CRAFT in Limestone Co., AL and they both died in Texas.
After the death of ELIZA, ALFRED married MARGARET JANE C. HARRISON 27 Feb. 1837 in Madison Co., AL, the daughter of JAMES HARRISON and ELIZABETH HARASON.
