EVANS CEMETERY #2 (Athens Sports Plex) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Evans Cemetery #2 as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2:N 34.81741 W 86.94161 Alternate location: Sect 34, T2S, R4W in the center of the Athens Sports Complex across Hwy. 31 from the east end of Elm Street

From Athens at the junction of Hwy 31 & Hwy 72 go north a couple of miles to Elm Street and you will see the Sports Complex on your right. Turn right (east) there opposite Elm Street and the cemetery is near the parking lot on the right.

This Picture shows the cemetery in the midst under a couple of trees on either end. The corners are fenced.
