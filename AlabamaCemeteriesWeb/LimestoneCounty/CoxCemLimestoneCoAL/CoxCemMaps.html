COX CEMETERY, Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant, LIMESTONE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Cox Cemetery as N 34.71096 W -87.10460 as plotted by C. Wayne Austin usig ACME Mapper 2.1.
Alternate Location: LOCATION: NW quarter of SW quarter, Sect. 7, T4S, R5W in front of the Brown's Ferry Nuclear Plant. Originally located in the SW quarter, Sect.
12, T4S, R6W, this cemetery was moved by the Construction Engineering Division of Brown's Ferry to its present location.

From Athens Go west on Hwy 72 to the Browns Ferry access road (Shaw Road) and turn south. follow that almost to the Plant and turn left onto Nuclear Plant Road and the relocated cemetery is on your right a half mile to the east Nuclear Plant Road behind a fence.
