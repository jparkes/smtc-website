William C. McClellan, military memorial.
McCLELLAN, William C. 21 Apr 1839 - 9 Dec 1869. Pvt Co H, 9th Ala Infantry C.S.A.
(Son of Thomas J. & Martha F. Beatie McClellan. married to Sue Strong, dau. of Charles Strong. Sue was a native of North Alabama. at the time of William's death she resided in Limestone County Al near this graveyard. After his death Sue married David K West of Giles County Tennessee.)
I do not know the details related to William C.'s death, but it is likely related to his war service in some way, nor do I know where Sue or David K. West is interred.
