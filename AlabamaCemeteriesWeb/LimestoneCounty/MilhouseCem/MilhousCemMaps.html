MILHOUS CEMETERY (Wooly Springs Road), N.E. LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Milhous Cemetery. GPS is a common term for the Latitude and Longitude. Location: SW quarter, Section 35, T1S, R3W two miles west of Wooley Springs.

From Athens Alabama travel east on Hwy Hwy 251 (old hwy 31) to Wooley Springs Road. Turn right there and go about a .3 mile to a long drive way that is 250 yards long and terminates at a house. After permission from The Potts family who are the gate keepers walk to your left and back north about 200 yards into a field where the cemetery is under a few trees in a pasture. 
This aerial view above uses a yellow pointer to show the cemetery located north of Wooley Springs Road with access only by crossing private property.
