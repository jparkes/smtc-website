POLLY MALONE CEMETERY,(old white section; Browns Ferry Road), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Malone Cemetery - white section. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

From Athens travel Hwy 31 south to Tanner nd turn east (left) on Huntsville-Brownsferry Road. Go to the Malone Cemetery Road and turn north (left) and follow that to the cemetery about 1/2 mile from the road.
This aerial view above shows the stand of trees hovering over the cemetery and surrounded by a field road allowing access into the cemetery from all sides.
This map shows the layout of the sections of this cemetery. The break in sections will be obvious when you see the cemetery. The upright & boxtombs from the old white section are very old while the black citizens use this cemetery even today - using modern granite monuments.
