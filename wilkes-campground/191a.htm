FITZPATRICK, Lois J., Jan 1828 - 14 Feb 1910. (Maury Co. native; dates taken from 1900 Maury Co. Census; d/o John Fitzpatrick and Lucy Freeman Fitzpatrick [both buried at Friendship Bapt. in Maury Co.]; w/o A. J. Fitzpatrick mar. 24 Mar 1878; This is an unmarked grave and may account for one of the field stones in the Fitzpatrick grave area.)

OBITUARY
The Columbia Herald  - Friday, February 18, 1910

MRS. LOIS FITZPATRICK
Mrs. Lois Fitzpatrick, widow of the late Jack Fitzpatrick, died Tuesday afternoon at her home near Culleoka. She was eighty-two years of age, and for many years had been a faithful and consecrated member of the Baptist Church. She is survived by her sister, Miss Mollie Fitzpatrick, and by a nephew, Morgan Fitzpatrick. Funeral services were conducted Wednesday afternoon from the late residence, interment at Camp Ground Cemetery.
