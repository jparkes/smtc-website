WILKES, Mary Kennedy Amis, daughter of Thomas Amis and Hannah Gordon Kennedy, was born 19 Oct 1828 in Maury County, Tennessee. She died 20 Dec 1855 in Maury County, Tennessee and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Mary married Rev. William Harris Wilkes, son of Richard Ambrose Lipscomb Wilkes and Judith Harris, on 6 Jan 1848 in Maury County, Tennessee. William was born 7 May 1821 in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. He died 15 Oct 1895 in Maury County Hospital, Tennessee and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Inscription: "Mary Kennedy wife of Wm. H. Wilkes and daughter of Thomas & Hannah Amis born October 19 1828 died December 20 1855 she lived an exemplary Christian for fifteen years & died in sure & certain hope of the resurrection unto life".
