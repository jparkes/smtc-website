Family information has it that Andrew M. Kerr born in Orange County NC . 1786 - 8/5/1864 is buried here in this cemetery and one of the above three stones logically markes the place of burial for him along with his wife Clotilda Moreen Kerr birth unknown, died it is believed in 1854. Andrew M. was one of the founders of the old nearby Pleasant Grove Male Academy in 1835.
