WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

 

WILKES, Richard Amis, son of Rev. W. H. Wilkes and Mary Kennedy Amis, was born 1851 in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. He died 1929 in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Richard served as Representative to the General Assembly of the State of Tennessee 1881-1882 representing Maury County, Tennessee. Richard married Mary T. Burke on 28 Jan 1872 in Maury County, Tennessee.  Inscription: Richard A. Wilkes, 1851-1929.
