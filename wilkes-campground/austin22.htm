WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, CULLEOKA, TENNESSEE

 

AUSTIN, Joseph Walter 8 Feb 1910 - 31 Jan 1911, Joseph was the fifth of seven children and the 
son of Charles Harris & Otie Eva Kerr Austin. Joseph died of Diphtheria an infectious disease that was 
prevalent at one time. This stone was placed by Joseph's siblings in the 1970s. All the other siblings made it to adulthood and lived long lives.
Information and 1992 photos by C Wayne Austin.
