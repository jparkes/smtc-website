Jim Kerr in various (cropped photo) poses.

KERR, James E., (2 Feb) 1874 - (20 Dec) 1948, son of J. J. & E. V. Ausban Kerr. James' wife (buried here) was Jettie Dean Kerr, (18 Nov) 1882 - (7 Feb) 1937, daughter of Wiley & Selina Walker Dean. The James Kerrs' had no children. Information and 1992 photo by Wayne Austin's family history records. Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003
