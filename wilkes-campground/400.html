BOWMAN, Louise F., 1907 - (17 Jan) 1992. (Louise Fleming Bowman, 84 yrs, widow of Leon Webb Bowman; Lawrence Co. native; d/o late John Calvin & Minnie Augusta Hancock Fleming. Ref obit.)
Photo and information by Suzanne Davison, May 2003.

The Daily Herald Obituaries January 19, 1992:

Mrs. Louise Fleming Bowman, 84, widow of Leon Webb Bowman, died Friday, Jan. 17, 1992, at Bethany Terrace in Morton Grove, Ill.
Services were 1:30 p.m., Monday at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home. Burial was in the Wilkes Cemetery at Culleoka. Family visitation was 6-8 p.m., on Sunday at the funeral home.
Memorials were to be made to Bethany Terrace Nursing Home, 8425 Waukegan Road, Morton Grove, Ill., 60053, or the PEO Educational Loan Fund, Chapter H.V., in care of Sue Johnson, 310 Satinwood Terrace, Buffalo Grove, Ill., 60089.
A Lawrence County native, she was the daughter of the late John Calvin and Minnie Augusta Hancock Fleming. She taught school in Tennessee prior to living and teaching in Washington for 40 year. She was also well-known as an accomplished artist.
