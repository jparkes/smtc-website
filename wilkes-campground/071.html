KERR, John Thomas, son of J.B. & M.M. Kerr, Born May 23 1887 - Died Mar 29 1889. Parents were John Betts & Mary Matilda Finley Kerr.  I believe they removed to Texas. To the E. is a footstone. Family records of Wayne Austin. Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003
