WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

AMIS, Bruce E., son of John Erastus and Rebecca J. Thomas Amis, was born 20 Nov 1858 in Maury County, 
Tennessee. He died 7 Jan 1927 in Tennessee and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, 
Tennessee.
