ABERNATHY, Cornelius B. Abernathy was Born May 23rd 1839 Died July 7th 1873 Aged 34 Years 1 Mo & 14 Days. Cornelius Beverly Abernathy, husband of Mary K. Wilkes, served in Company A, 11 Battalion (Gordon's) Tennessee Cavalry, C.S.A. He was the son of William David Abernathy and Mary Beckwith Hardaway. Info by Rick Gray 11-11-2003.
