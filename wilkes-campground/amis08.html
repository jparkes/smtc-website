WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

AMIS, Hannah Gordon Kennedy, 25 Nov 1800 - 2 Mar 1878, daughter of 
John Kennedy and Barbara Gordon, Hannah was born in Kentucky & died at age 77 yrs 
3 mos and 7 days. Hannah married Thomas Amis who was the son of John Erasmus 
Amis and Mary Knight "Polly") on 12 Oct 1827 in Maury County, Tennessee. Hannah 
was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee.
