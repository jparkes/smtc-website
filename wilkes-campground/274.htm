WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

AUSTIN, Unmarked fieldstone-between Joseph Austin & the double monument for John S. & Sarah Morgan Pride. (This is probably the marker for Myrtle B. Austin, the daughter of James Walter & Sara Elizabeth Epps Austin. She died of a stomach ailment (probably appendicitis at age 22 about 1912. She was a younger sister of Charles H. Austin) Her parents James Walter and Sarah Elizabeth Epps Austin moved to Ulan OK in 1926. Information by Wayne Austin. Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003.
