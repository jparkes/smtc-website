PILKINTON, Nancy, (Nancy Pilkinton)-Nan..??..., Larki..??, born..??.., died A..?? . (I (Suzanne) talked to the Pilkinton family at the site and they are researching their family. Mr. Pilkinton said they also believe this is Nancy's stone.
) (Mr. Hawkins in Maury County Cemeteries says the dates are b. Dec 7, 1781, d. Apr 4, 1859. Most of the A in April is visible and part of the p in April. (
Note by James Pilkinton 1/28/2004,
Nancy Dickson was born in Guilford County, NC 7 Dec 1784 and died 14 Apr 1859 in Maury County (Culleoka). I believe her stone once read "Nancy Pilkinton wife of Larkin".) This is an 1850s era stone indicating the death was in that time frame which confirms Mr. Fred's & James Pilkinton's confirmation of the death date. I don't know who was carving that style stone in the Culleoka area in the 1850s, but they carved many that crumpled to dust in a hundred years. Shane Cemetery has a couple of these along with Friendship Cemetery also, by Wayne Austin
