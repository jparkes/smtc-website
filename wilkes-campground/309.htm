WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA


RAMSEY, J. D.,"John Dunwoody", son of Thomas Ramsey and Esther Dunwoody, was born 25 Oct 1795 in Charleston, South Carolina and died 26 Jun 1873 in Maury County, Tennessee. He is buried in the Wilkes Cemetery in Culleoka, Tennessee. He was the husband of Elizabeth "Betsy" Kennedy, daughter of John Kennedy and Barbara Gordon. As you can see in the photo the stone is very worn from age the inscription is almost illegible now. Information by Rick Gray 1/11/03. Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003
