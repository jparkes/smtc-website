WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA


RAMSEY, James Albert & Mary Lou Adkisson. James was born 8 Mar 1830 in Tennessee. He died 30 Sep 1908 in Maury County, Tennessee and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. James married twice. I am not sure where his first wife, Priscilla M. Warden, is buried, but it may be in the Shane Cemetery with the other Warden kin. His second wife, Mary Lou Adkisson, is buried here beside him. She was born on 22 Dec 1870 in Maury County, Tennessee. Mary was born 1 Jun 1848. She died 30 Oct 1902 and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Like his parents tombstone, this one is beginning to show its age. A family member is trying to get new tombstones for them. Inscription: James Albert Ramsey, Born Mar. 8, 1830, Died Sept. 30, 1908 / Mary Lou Ramsey, Born June 1, 1848 Died Oct. 30, 1902.
