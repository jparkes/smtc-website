WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

 

PILKINTON, Larkin, Died May 15th A.D. 1858 aged about 76 years. A soldier of the Cross. Larkin was born 1 Mar 1781 in Virginia. He grew up in Guilford Co., North Carolina and moved to Maury County, Tennessee in about 1819 with his second wife Nancy Dickson who is buried here next to him. Larkin Pilkinton died as the stone indicates, May 15th A.D. 1858. Nancy's stone is crumbled but next to his. by James Pilkinton 1/28/2004)
