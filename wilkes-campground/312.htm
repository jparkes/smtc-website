Added info by Rick Gray 10/16/04:Yearby Ramsey was born 21 May 1880 in Culleoka, Tennessee to James Albert Ramsey and Mary Lou Adkisson. He was their 6th and last child. He died a few weeks later on 7 Jun 1880 and was buried at Wilkes-Campground Cemetery in Culleoka. Buried near him are several of his brothers and sisters and his parents.
