FLEMING, S 1/c Fred G. Fleming, U. S. Navy; Born Mar. 4, 1922; Lost In Action off Guadalcanal on the USS Little 6 Sept. 1942. He is listed on the Tablets of the Missing at Manila American (ABMC) Cemetery and Memorial, Manila, Philippines. 
Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003. Updated for the Link to Manila American Cemetery 3-5-07, sent in by Mary Bob Richardson. Thanks ladies, and all for your efforts in helping us to remember this War Hero. [WA 3-7-07]
