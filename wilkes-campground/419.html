WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA


DONALD M. SCOTT
 
SCOTT, Donald Matthews, 23 Aug 1925 - 18 Aug 2011. (Maury Co. native; s/o late Allen Bumley Scott and Bessie Belle Matthews Scott; h/o Jean Trousdale Scott who died 24 Oct 2003; well known grocer livestock trader, farmer in Sawdust Community; owner and operator Riverside Market and Hatcher Lane market for years, Manager of Southern Livestock and a salesman for Herbert Lewis Tractor Company; raised trained and showed Tennessee Walking Horses; member of Graymere Church of Christ, and 50 yr. member of Columbia masonic Lodge #31; preceded in death by brother James Clifford Scott.)
and
 SCOTT, Jean Trousdale, Aug 8 1926 - 24 Oct 2003. (Davidson Co. native; d/o late Maury Vernon and Brownie Hobbs Trousdale; w/o Donald M. Scott for 58 yrs.
Mrs. Jean Trousdale Scott, 77, former co-owner and operator of Riverside and Hatcher Lane Markets, died Friday, October 24, 2003 at her residence on Hilltop Drive.

Funeral services will be conducted Sunday at 2:00 p.m. at Oakes & Nichols with John Vaughan, Wendell Robinson and Vinton Morgan officiating. Burial will be in the Wilkes Cemetery in Culleoka. The family will visit with friends Saturday from 4:00 p.m. until 8:00 p.m. at the funeral home. Memorials may be made to Graymere Lads & Leaders, in care of Graymere Church of Christ, 1320 Trotwood Avenue, Columbia, 38401; the Girl Scout Council of Cumberland Valley, 4572 Granny White Pike, P.O. Box 40466, Nashville, 37204-0466; or the BGA Golf Team, in care of Battle Ground Academy, 366 Ernest Rice Lane, Franklin, 37069.

The Davidson County native was the daughter of the late Maury Vernon Trousdale and Brownie Hobbs Trousdale and a graduate of Culleoka High School. She was a member of the Graymere Church of Christ.

Survivors include her husband of 58 years, Donald M. Scott, well-known livestock dealer and sportsman; two daughters and sons-in-law, Donna Kay and Don Vick of Franklin and Tammy Jean and Bill Riley of Columbia; four grandchildren, Matthew Paul Vick, Scott Andrews Vick, Stephanie Jean Riley and William Scott Riley; a sister, Harriett T. Dugger of St. Louis, Missouri; several nieces and nephews.

Pallbearers Mike Dugger, Dan Dugger, David Dugger, Ben Leibner, Danny Morgan, Brent Turner, and Joe Wakham.
