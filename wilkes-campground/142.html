WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

 

WILKES, Thomas Paul, infant son of Rev. W. H. Wilkes and Mary Kennedy Amis, was born on 25 Apr 1855 and died 25 Jun 1855. He is buried in the Wilkes Cemetery in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Grandparents were Richard A. L. Wilkes & Judith Harris and Thomas Amis & Hannah Gordon Kennedy.  Inscription: Thomas Paul infant son of Wm H. & Mary K. Wilkes was born April 25th & died June 25th A. D. 1855.
