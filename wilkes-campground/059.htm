WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

 

WILLIAMSON, Charles Samuel, Apr. 10, 1837 - Mar. 13, 1913. He was a Maury County native and lifelong resident and the son of George C. Williamson. Charles was killed in the tornado that hit Culleoka on Mar. 13, 1913. His wife Izora was also injured in the tornado. Her sister, Alice Wilkes Peebles, was the only other person killed that day. He served in Company G, 11th Tennessee Cavalry, Confederate States of America in the Civil War.

Izora Wilkes, Nov. 19, 1848 - Nov. 30, 1921. Born in Culleoka Tennessee she was the daughter of Rev. William Harris Wilkes and Mary Kennedy Amis. She married Charles Samuel Williamson on 16 Jul 1868 in Maury County, Tennessee. Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003
Charles and Izora were the parents of 6 children: Alice Brown Williamson, Georgia Florence Williamson, Dr. George W. Williamson, Mary Tabitha Williamson, Charles S. Williamson and James Richard Williamson. Information and update by Rick Gray 5-16-4004
