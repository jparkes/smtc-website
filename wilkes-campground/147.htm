WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

 

 WITT, Florence T., 15 Nov 1853 - 14 Sep 1854, daughter of Carter H. Witt and Tabitha C. Amis, was born & died in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Inscription: "Erected In Memory Of FLORENCE T. Infant Daughter of C. H. & T. C. WITT Born Nov 15th A.D. 1853 Died Sept 14th 1854 Aged 10 months and 29 days". Information by Rick Gray 3/05/03. Photo by Suzanne Davison, May 2003
