WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

AMIS, Emily E., daughter of Thomas and Hannah Gordon Kennedy Amis, was born 15 Jul 1838.
She died 23 Nov 1855 and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee.
I could not make out the inscription except she was aged 17 years 4 months and 10
days old at death.
