WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

AMIS, Thomas, 3 Oct 1803 - 19 Jan 1867, Thomas married Hannah Gordon Kennedy 
He was the son of John Erasmus Amis and Mary Knight "Polly"). Thomas was born 
in Granville County, North Carolina. He died in Maury County, Tennessee and 
was buried here in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee.
