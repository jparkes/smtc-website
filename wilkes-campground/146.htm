WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CULLEOKA

 

WITT, Tabitha C. Amis, daughter of Thomas Amis and Hannah Gordon Kennedy, was born 18 Nov 1832 in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. She died 12 Mar 1854 in Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. Tabitha married Carter H. Witt. Carter was born 18 Nov 1832. Inscription is: "In Memory of TABITHA C. Consort of Carter H. Witt Daughter of Thomas & Hannah Amis Born Nov 18 A.D. 1832 Died March 12 A.D. 1854 Aged 21 years 3 months & 24 days".
