WILKES-CAMPGROUND CEMETERY-CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

AMIS,McCoy Campbell, son of Thomas Amis and Hannah Gordon Kennedy, was born 27 Feb 1841. 
He died 5 Feb 1863 and was buried in Wilkes Cemetery, Culleoka, Maury County, Tennessee. McCoy 
served in the military Private, Company E, 3rd (Clacks) Tennessee Infantry, Confederate States 
of America in Civil War. Besides the name and dates on his tombstone other information includes: 
"Aged 22 years wanting 22 days" and "Beloved in life Lamented in death"
