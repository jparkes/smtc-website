ZOLLICOFFER, E. P., Daughter of J. D. & S. Love. who was born December 28 1812 and departed this life September 15th 1854. Aged 41 years 8 months and 17 days. Elizabeth Petillo Love, was the daughter of John Draper Love and Susannah Caruthers. She was born 28 Dec 1812 in Maury County, Tennessee and died 15 Sep 1854 in Nashville, Tennessee. She married Dr. Frederick Zollicoffer, son of John Jacob Zollicoffer and Martha Kirk on 3 Jan 1830 in Maury County, Tennessee. She was originally buried in the J. D. Love family cemetery but moved here in the 1970s. Her husband was the brother of the great Civil War general, Felix Kirk Zollicoffer, who was killed at the Battle of Fishing Creek in Kentucky. Felix's biography may be found on this site under Maury natives interred elsewhere. Photo and information by Suzanne Davison, May 2003. Updated by Rick Gray 4/20/2004.
