SHEBOSS CEMETERY, SCOTT BRANCH RD., (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

These stone are visible in various shapes out from the bottom of the photo.

ANDERSON, Unknown, two graves marked with fieldstones and not inscribed among the "Anderson" inscribed markers. In analyzing the graves we may have more graves than markers here indicating someone left an old fieldstone after it was replaced with an inscribed marker. Obviously that was the case with the large marker in the center. I see the outline of four graves and five marked and unmarked stones here. The spots where the darker green grasses grow are most often inside the grave outlines.
