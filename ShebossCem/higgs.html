SHEBOSS CEMETERY, SCOTT BRANCH RD., (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

HIGGS, Pagy, (Peggy), d. 22 Mar 1906. Aged 83 years. (She and her sisters are buried in a line in Sheboss.)

Maury County Census lists Peggy Higgs born May 1820.
Another reference that I have shows Peggy Higgs born April 1823 and died March 11, 1906. This source is undocumented. Ref: Faye Jackson (harjac6@comcast.net)
 Information by Fay Jackson. Sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 May 2009.
