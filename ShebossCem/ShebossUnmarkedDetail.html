SHEBOSS CEMETERY, SCOTT BRANCH RD., (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

** Surname spelled "Regen" on Death Certificate.

**REGEN, James Madison, 19 Sep 1871 - 24 Jan 1948. (Son of Joel Marthenes Region and Mary Jane Brickell.)
**REGEN, Sarah "Ellen" Hendricks Turner, 08 Jul 1877 - 24 Aug 1925 (Daughter of Jesse Turner and Carolina Hendricks.)

James Madison **Region was born in Centerville, Hickman County, TN and died in Columbia, Maury County, Tennessee. His wife Sarah "Ellen" Hendricks Turner was born in Alabama and died in Hampshire, Tennessee. They were married 14 Mar 1895 in Linden, Perry County TN. His parents were Joel Marthenes Region and Mary Jane Brickell. Ellen's parents were Jesse Turner and Carolina Hendricks.
