McCLAIN CEMETERY, LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE


HAMM, Raymond H., 23 July 1908 - 29 Oct 1976. (mar. 6 Jun. 1939 to Lavonia McClain (daughter of Matthew & "Bettie" Kilpatrick McClain); father of Raymond McClain Hamm; adopted father of Judy Brooks Hamm.) 
and
HAMM, Lavonia McClain, 31 July 1909 - 14 Aug. 1987. ( daughter of Matthew & "Bettie" Kilpatrick McClain; wife of Raymond H. Hamm ( Williamson Co., Tn.); mother of Raymond McClain Hamm, who was born and died 1 July 1937.) Lavonia & Raymond adopted Judy Brooks (daughter of Edna Earl McClain & James Commer Brooks), after the sudden death of Edna Earl (daughter of John Turley & Allie Mai Kennedy McClain.)
