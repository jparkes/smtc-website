McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE


BROWN,  Melvin  "Mebb",  1880 - 1960. (father of Lillian Brown McClain;  husband of Ollie Goodman Brown; son of A.W. &  Lulu Skelton Goodman.)  Mebb Brown was a pall bearer at the funeral of Martin McClain Sr. 1833-1916. see obit.  He is shown in this picture with his wife Ollie Goodman Brown and child. 
Both are buried at McClain Cemetery.
