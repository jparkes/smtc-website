McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

KENNEDY, William Otto, 15 Jul 1896 - 16 Jan 1963, WWI vet., US Army, son of James William & Nancy Elizabeth 
Hunt. Buried beside him in an unmarked grave is his twin brother, James Otis Kennedy, who died 
as an infant. Photo & Information by Rick Gray 1/11/03.
