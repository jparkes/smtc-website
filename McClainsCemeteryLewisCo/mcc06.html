McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE


McCLAIN, Amelia Velina Elizabeth Kilpatrick "Bettie", 16 Feb. 1875 - 31 Oct. 1959. (christened "Bettie" (born in Perry Co., Tn.; wife of Matthew McClain; mother of William Thomas McClain, Janie Melvina McClain Weaver, Gladys Catherine McClain Duncan, Alice Kennedy McClain Thornton, Birdie Madolia McClain Oaks, Lavonia McClain Hamm, Matthew "Mathie" Elizabeth McClain, Marth May McClain Hurst, and Jessie Reba McClain Russell; dua. of William Allen Kilpatrick & Mary Melvina Cooper Kilpatrick Whitwell; sister of Mary Ellen Kilpatrick Lewis, William A. Kilpatrick Jr., James Samuel Kilpatrick, Eliza Alice Kilpatrick Lamar.) (Our paternal great grandmother), Matthew and Bettie had 12 children.
