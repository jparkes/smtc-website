McCLAIN, Robert Matthew, 10 Feb 1926 - 23 Feb 2003, son of John Turley and Allie Mai Kennedy McClain. 
he was the husband of Helen Cook McClain of the Mt. Pleasant area near Mt. Joy. Robert McClain served during
 WWII from July 1942 -Dec 1945 on the USS Pennsylvania. 
He was the ggggg-grandson of Epraim McLean Sr. of Maury County a Revolutionary Soldier and the ggg-grandson 
of John McClain who served in the War of 1812 He was the gg-grandson of Martin McClain who served 
in the Civil War Robert McClain has a grandson James Richardson who is presently serving in the War in 
Iraq in the 3rd Infantry Armor Division, and drives an M1A1 Abrams Tank. Historic Home of Robert & Helen McClain
