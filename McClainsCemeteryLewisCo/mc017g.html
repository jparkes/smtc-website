McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE
COOPER, Leander Bruce, 2 Sept 1841 Maury Co., Tn. - 28 Mar. 1926 Maury Co., Tn.
(3rd Tennessee Regiment, Confederate States Army; husband to Cornelia Cooper; member of the Mt. Joy Cumberland Presbyterian
Church joining in 1885; son of Col. Robert Melville & Catherine Cooper Cooper.)

Left to right: Katie Cornelia Cooper (daughter), seated on the porch is Cornelia Cooper
(wife of L.B. Cooper), seated in yard Leander Bruce Cooper, and Orin Duff Cooper (son).
