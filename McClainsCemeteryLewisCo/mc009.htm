McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

LINDSEY, Evan, 12 Apr 1884 - 26 Sep 1962, son of William L. Alexander and Rhoda E. Neeley, 
married the former Florence Seaton. I am told that Evan's dad is buried at McClain with him
though there is no marking for his grave. His mother Rhoda is buried at Sheepneck Cemetery 
in Sandy Hook in an unmarked grave. Photo & Information by Rick Gray 1/11/03.
