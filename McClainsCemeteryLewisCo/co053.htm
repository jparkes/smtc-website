McCLAIN CEMETERY, LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

COOPER, Cornelia, 28 Mar 1834 Maury Co., Tn. - 13 Aug. 1917 Maury Co., Tn. (wife of L. B. Cooper; married 5 Nov 1868, mother of Katie Cornelia, Orin Duffe, Alfred Bruce and Henry Otis Cooper. Member of the Mt. Joy Cumberland Presbyterian Church joining in 1885.)

Home of Leander Bruce Cooper, Lewis Co., Tn. (Son of Robert Melville and Catherine Cooper) Left to right: Katie Cornelia Cooper (daughter), seated on the porch is Cornelia Cooper (wife of L.B. Cooper), seated in yard Leander Bruce Cooper, and Orin Duff Cooper (son).
