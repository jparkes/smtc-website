McCLAIN CEMETERY, LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

McCLAIN, Jerry Eugene, 3 Nov. 1941 - 11 Aug. 2006, Birth: Mount Joy Maury County Tn, Death: Columbus Muscogee County Georgia 
Jerry McClain, 64, assemblyman for a private trailer company and former Maury County resident, died Friday, August 11, 2006 at his residence in Columbus, Georgia. Funeral services were conducted Tuesday Aug 14 at 3:00 p.m. at Oakes & Nichols with Larry Johnson officiating. Burial was in McClain Cemetery in the Mt. Joy Community. The family visited with friends Monday Aug 13, 2006 from 4:00 to 8:00 p.m. at the funeral home. Notes of sympathy may be sent to www.oakesandnichols.com. The Mt. Pleasant native was the son of the late Turley and Allie McClain. Survivors included his wife, Margaret Spires McClain of Columbus, Georgia; son, Jerry Eugene McClain of Rome, Georgia; step-daughter, Treva Massey of Chestnut Mound, Tennessee; sister, Helen Pinkley of Horn Lake, Mississippi; brother, James McClain of Pensacola, Florida; two grandchildren; several sisters-in-law, brothers-in-law, nieces, and nephews. Pallbearers are Danny Grooms, Roy Wayne Grooms, Charlie Grooms, and Tiny Yowell. Jerry was the son of John Turley and Allie Mai Kennedy McClain of Maury/Lewis Co., Tn.
Information by his niece Mary Bob McClain Richardson 8/16/2006.
