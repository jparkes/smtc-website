RICHARDSON, James C., 5 Oct 1947 - 19 Apr 2011. (Nettleton, Mississippi native; s/o late Sidney Lee Richardson and Sybil Maxine Hester Richardson Osborne; married 30 Dec 1976 to Mary Robert McClain Richardson; Vet. U.S. Army, Sgt. 101st Airborne serving in Viet Nam 1968-69. Bronze Star Recipient; preceded in death by daughter Tiffany Helen Richardson, brother Danny Carl Richardson, father - in- law Robert Matthew McClain.
