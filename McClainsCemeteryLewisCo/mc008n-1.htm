McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

The historic home of the Robert Matthew McClain family in Mt Pleasant Tennessee. The house was built in 1858. Facing the house you would
be looking in front of the left corner.

This not so great photo is a photo of a Civil War Reunion of old surviving Soldiers which took place about 1905
in the front yard of the above home. This picture hangs today on the walls of this home. The big tree
in the left foreground was struck by lightning and had to be cut down.

Photo & Information by Mary Bob McClain Richardson who lived there. 
Her Mom Helen resides there today 6/20/2007
