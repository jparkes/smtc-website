McCLAIN CEMETERY, LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

McCLAIN, Nancy Jane Beckum, 20 Mar. 1848 - 18 Dec 1881. (wife of Martin McClain, 33 yrs 8 mos 27 days; on the stone it says "From morning on till evening love shines upon her brow, she has gone from earth to heaven - we have no mother now." Census: 1850, Lewis Co., Tn; age 2; b. Tn; fam #511; died of tuberculosis when baby was 11 mo. old; dau of Alexander & Phobe Beckum.) (Our paternal Great Great Great Grandmother.)
