McCLAIN CEMETERY, LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

HAYES, Wanda Gayle Lindsey, 11 Feb 1959 - 6 Jan 2012. (d/o late William Lindsey & Sue Johnson Lindsey; preceded in death y brother Phillip D. Lindsey and sister Deborah West.)

Wanda Gayle Hayes, age 52, passed away January 6th, 2012 at her residence in Mt. Pleasant. She was born February 11th, 1959 in Lewis County, TN, daughter of Sue Johnson Lindsey and the late William Lindsey.

Wanda is survived by her mother: Sue Lindsey of Mt. Pleasant, TN; a son: Michael Seaton of Mt. Pleasant; a daughter: Amber Lynette Miller of Florance, AL; brothers: Mike Lindsey of Mt. Pleasant, TN, Jimmy Lindsey of Nashville, TN and Bobby Johnson of Hohenwald, TN; sisters: Susan Arbrazan of Murfreesboro, TN and Linda Vinson of Atlanta, GA; 5 grandchildren: Haylee Springer, Lyndon Springer, Peyton Miller, Bentley Miller and Hunter Seaton. She is also survived by her special dog: "Pumpkin"

In addtion to her father, she was preceded in death by her brother: Phillip D. Lindsey and sister: Deborah West.

Funeral services will be held 1:00 pm Tuesday, January 10th, 2012 at Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant with Brother Dickey Lindsey officiating. Burial will follow in the McClain Cemetery. Pallbearers will be friends of the family. Friends may visit 10 am till service time at the Williams Funeral Home Chapel in Mt. Pleasant.

Memorial contributions may be made to Williams Funeral Home, 2517 Trotwood Ave., Columbia, TN, 38401
