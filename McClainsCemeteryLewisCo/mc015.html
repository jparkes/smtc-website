McCLAIN CEMETERY, (near Maury line) LEWIS COUNTY TENNESSEE

POLLOCK, James Thomas, 11 Nov 1857 - 4 Dec 1936, married first to Victoria Ann Kennedy, 
daughter of Weakley Gordon Kennedy and Sarah Ann Stockard. Victoria is buried here beside 
him in an unmarked grave. She was born November 10, 1861 and died December 31, 1892. 
His second wife was the former, Nettie Cooper. She is buried in the Zion Church Cemetery.
