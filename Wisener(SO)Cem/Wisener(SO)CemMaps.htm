WISENER (SAMUEL O.) FAMILY CEMETERY, Chickasaw Trace, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Samuel Wisener Cemetery.
Turn left off of Santa Fe Pike onto the Chickasaw Trace. Cross the Knob Creek on the Bridge and take the road to the right up the hill toward the County Landfill. The Cemetery is on the right across the road from the restored Derryberry Cabin and adjacent to the guard gate to the County Landfill.

This is the sign that awaits you as you turn left into the Chickasaw Trace Park from Santa Fe Pike.
