WISENER (SAMUEL O.) FAMILY CEMETERY, Chickasaw Trace, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Wisener Cemetery #2.
In Athendale, turn left on Roberts Bend Rd. and go 2 and 1/2 miles. The small graveyard is on the (south side) left, almost under the T.V.A. transmission line.

This view looking south from Roberts Bend Road shows a small private road branching to our right of the main private residential drive. There is a fence enclosed thicket at the far end of that and that is the place the map indicators are pointing at as the location of the (lost) Wisener Cemetery. That is about 200 yards from where the photographer is standing
