WISENER (#2) CEMETERY, Roberts Bend Rd., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

 Finding Wisener Cemetery #2 - Locator Maps & Instructions     

No listing. Unknown burial ground.

"All that remains of this cemetery is a grove of small trees, some stones that were part of a wall and several fieldstones that might have been grave markers. No record has been found as to who might have been buried there. On maps it was called Wisner's Cemetery. Site visited on 3 Feb 1987 FLH. 
Revisited 24 Mar 2009 WA, nothing new to report.

Published in the book Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries, by Fred Lee Hawkins, Jr. Page 177; visited the site 3 Feb 1987. Added here 26 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin.
