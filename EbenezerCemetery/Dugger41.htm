EBENEZER CHURCH CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

DUGGER, Walter T., 2 Sep 1889 - 14 Dec 1953. Father.


Dugger Funeral Wednesday Morning
Funeral services for Walter T. Dugger, who died Monday morning at his home in the Scott's Mill community, will be held at 10 a.m. Wednesday at the Ebenezer Cumberland Presbyterian Church, with Harold Spencer, minister of the Church of Christ, officiating. Pallbearers will be Jim Elliott, Ship Aydelotte, James Green, Eslick Overstreet, Ernest Crews and Mack Benderman. Honorary pallbearers were Dr. C. D. Walton, Dr. G. C. English, Lawson White, R. U. Swann, Charlie Jackson, Pillow Due, Tom Peebles and C. H. Denton. Burial will be in Ebenezer Cemetery with Oakes & Nichols _ _ _ _ .
This obituary was in a scrapbook and the bottom line or two was cut off. In handwriting is written Dec. 15, 1953, most likely the date of publication. Submitted by Judy B. Forgos 8/20/2009.
