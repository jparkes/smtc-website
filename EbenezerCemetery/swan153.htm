EBENEZER CHURCH CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

SWAN, Sarah J. Spencer, 10 Dec 1838 - 10 Jun 1908. Mother, "Wife of John N. Swan". If John N. Stone is the husband of Sarah the memorial for John N. Stone is high upon the hill above the main cemetery area. Believe the lower main section of the cemetery was established later and since John N. died early in the history of the cemetery then he was buried in the older section upon the hill.
