EBENEZER CHURCH CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
BAKER, Earl James, 17 Dec 1913 - 20 Aug 1982, SGT US Army WWII.
 Photo & information by Wayne Austin 5/20/04.
Obituary:
Earl James Baker, 68, of the Cross Bridges Road near Mt. Pleasant died Friday in Veterans Hospital in Nashville. Services were Sunday at 2 p.m. in Williams Mt. Pleasant Funeral Home. Burial was in Ebenezer Cemetery. The Maury native was a son of the late Thomas W. and Ethel Knapp Baker. He was a veteran of World War II and retired from the army in 1962. Survivors include a brother, Elmer Baker of Mt. Pleasant; three nephews; and two nieces. Pallbearers were M.B. Henson, William Boyd, John C. Bolton, Russell Roberts, Bumpus England and Herschel Bolton.
Mt. Pleasant Record, Aug. 26, 1982, page 3. Obituary submitted by Judy B. Forgos, 8/10/2009.
