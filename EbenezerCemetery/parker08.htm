 PARKER, Patricia Ann Dugger, May 4, 1953, Maury Co., Tenn - Sep 25, 2008, Hermitage Davidson Co. Tenn.  (daughter of Lorenzo Gower & Betty Joann Dugger; wife of John Steven Parker (1955 - 2007)

PARKER, Patricia Ann Dugger
The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Lorenzo Gower and Betty Joann Dugger and attended Spring Hill High School.
She was married to John Steven Parker who preceded her in death June 17, 2007  who is interred in Lynnwood Cemetery listed on a stone with his (living) mother. Survivors include daughters, Vicki Lynn Spain of Dickson and Jennifer Lee (Brian) Baker of Smyrna; grandchildren,
James Spain, Amanda Spain, Amber Spain, Trey Clardy, Cole Clardy, Carson Baker; sisters, Peggy (Royce) Davenport of Murfreesboro and Sharmin (Richard) Rowlett of Hermitage; brothers, Lloyd,
(Jackie) Dugger of Lawrenceburg, Morris (Dot) Dugger and Judd Dugger, both of Summertown; several nieces and nephews.

Active pallbearers are Walter Dugger, Danny Dugger, Steve Morton, Shane Morton, Kevin King, and Terry Brazier. Honorary pallbearers include David Morton, Dylan King, Hunter King, Tyler Morton,
and Cody Morton.
