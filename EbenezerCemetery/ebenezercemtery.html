Ebenezer Cumberland Presbyterian Church & Cemetery bore those names for more than a hundred years. The Church is now the Ebenezer Community Church. The cemetery is located south on Terrapin Branch Road which follows Scott Creek. It is on the Church grounds on the left going south toward Screamer.
