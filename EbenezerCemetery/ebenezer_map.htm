EBENEZER CHURCH CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

     A topo map is shown here depicting the Ebenezer Church Cemetery location.  It lies on the eastern (left) side of the road going south toward Screamer from Southport Rd near Mt Pleasant Tennessee.

From this map from Topozone we see the GPS coordinates of the Ebenezer Cemetery.

 Here we see some of the roads to travel to get to the Ebenezer Cemetery.
