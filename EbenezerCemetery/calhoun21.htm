EBENEZER CHURCH CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


CALHOUN, Rev. James, 1800 - 21 Aug 1883. Inscription: "Rev James Calhoun, born in 1800. A faithful minister for over 60 years. Died Aug 21, 1883. Aged about 83 years." He was the first pastor of the Ebenezer Cumberland Presbyterian Church. "Mason". Photo & information by Wayne Austin 6/19/04.
