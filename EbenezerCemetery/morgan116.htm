EBENEZER CHURCH CEMETERY, TERRAPIN BRANCH RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


MORGAN, Aaron G., 30 Aug 1819 - 7 Nov 1847. 28-02-08. " son of Aaron & Elizabeth Morgan." Stone is on the ground. Photo & information by Wayne Austin 5/7/04.

This footstone lies on the opposite side of the tree from the Aaron Morgan stone which means the tree grew up in the center of the grave of Mr. Morgan.
