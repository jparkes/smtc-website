HEFLIN, Adam Lee,3 Mar 1979 - 2 Dec 1989, (Son of Larry W. & Barbara Ann McKennon Heflin and younger brother to Johnnie Heflin & Kim Heflin-Williams; Died at age 10 of gunshot wound to head; A Bridge on Terrapin Branch Road is dedicated to our brother Adam Heflin on September 9th 2006. "ADAM HEFLIN MEMORIAL BRIDGE" located at the corner of Preacher Holt & Terrapin Branch Road.

When my younger brother Adam was killed in 1989, my mother and father (Barbara & Larry Heflin) had a triple rock made for the 3 of them. My mother joined Adam in heaven in 1995.
