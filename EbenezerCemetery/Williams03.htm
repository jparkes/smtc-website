Margaret Williams
Obituary
Mrs. Margaret Ann Kelley Williams, 43, supervisor for the Impact Center, died October 10, 2007 at her residence in the Culleoka Community.

Funeral services will be conducted Monday at 11:00 a.m. at Oakes & Nichols with Dickie Lindsey officiating. Burial will be in Ebeneezer Cemetery. The family will visit with friends Sunday from 4:00 to 8:00 p.m. at the funeral home. Notes of sympathy may be sent to www.oakesandnichols.com.

The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Edward M. Kelley and Henrietta Randolph Kelley Bolton.

Survivors include her husband, Charles Douglas Williams of Culleoka; daughter, Crystal Dawn Williams of Culleoka; son, Charles Maxwell Williams of Culleoka; sisters, Joyce (Paul) Riggins of Columbia, Mary (Alton) Pierce of Pulaski, Carloyn McCandless of Mt. Pleasant; brothers, William (Robbie) Kelley of Lewisburg, Michael Bolton of Mt. Pleasant, Chris Bolton of Mt. Pleasant; step-father, William Bolton of Summertown; step-sister, Kathy Bolton; step-brother, Edward Bolton; several nieces and nephews.

She was preceded in death by brothers, Paul E. Kelley, Clyde E. Kelley, and Rick Kelley.

Active pallbearers are David Williams, Billy Joe Williams, Jerry Williams, Jeff Williams, Robert Riggins, Joe Staggs, Kevin Williams, and Brandon Holt.

Honorary pallbearers include employees and special clients of the Impact Center.
