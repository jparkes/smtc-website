HILL CEMETERY, HILL STATION (eastern), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Virgie M. Worley memorial. Standing complete. This little stone along with the Floyd Worley memorial is at the far end of the cemetery - north side.

WORLEY, Vergie M., 3 Oct 1890 - 11 Oct 1890. "Daughter of N. D. & Eliza. Worley."
