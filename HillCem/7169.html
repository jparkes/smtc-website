HILL CEMETERY, HILL STATION (eastern), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Jane Hill (wife of James Hill) memorial on left/James Hill Revolutionary War Soldier on right and military marker for James Hill standing further right.

Jane Hill Died Feb 9, 1836, Age about 72 years.

HILL, James, d. 12 Jun 1831 at about 70 yrs. (Revolutionary War Soldier. Original Stone broken and had been covered with dirt & moss. Inscription seems to be intact.)

HILL, James, d. 12 Jun 1831 at about 70 yrs. (Revolutionary War Soldier. Continental Line 1761 - 1831. Original Stone broken and covered with dirt & moss.)
