HILL CEMETERY, HILL STATION (eastern), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
HILL, unnamed infant boy, 20 May 1842 - 3 Jun 1842.
The top of this stone was temporarily set upon the base for photography. I always set the pieces back down with the inscription facing away from the other part of the stone. That prevents the inscription from being gouged off by friction.
