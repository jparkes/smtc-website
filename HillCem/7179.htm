HILL CEMETERY, HILL STATION (eastern), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Sarah E. Hill memorial. Another broken memorial the top resting against the bottom.

HILL, Sarah E., 2 Jun 1834 - 15 Jun 1854.
Memorial is broken and top is resting against the lower half of the stone. Do not try to decipher the death date as 1851. The cross bar on the 4 is hard to see on these old stones.
