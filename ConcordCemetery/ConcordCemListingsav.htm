This cemetery has been well maintained for the past several years by the Monsanto Company. Evidently, this was not the case when the compilers of They Passed This Way listed the stones in the 1960�s , since a fairly large section of the older part of the cemetery was completely omitted from that study. There are many graves that have no markers. A few are marked with fieldstones, but relatively few such stones are now in place.
