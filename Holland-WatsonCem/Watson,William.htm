HOLLAND-WATSON CEMETERY, Stillhouse Hole Duck River Bluff, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Also known as Holland Cemetery, aka Watson Cemetery.
WATSON, William Henry, (20 Jan) 1845 - (1 Apr) 1913, Company G, 9th Tennessee Calvary, & 48th Tennessee Infantry, Confederate States Army

WATSON, William Henry, son of Alexander Watson, was born in Giles Co., Tenn. on 20 Jan 1845. As a very young man he joined the Confederate Army, first enlisting in Co. G, 9th Tenn Cav. and later serving in the 48th Tenn Infantry. Returning home after the war, he married Ellen Journey and to them eight children were born. Ellen died in 1894 and in 1897 Mr. Watson married Miss Lizzie Porter. He died on 1 Apr 1913.
