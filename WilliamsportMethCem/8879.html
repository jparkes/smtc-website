WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Duncan & Hamilton memorials, very difficult to read when wet. Third stone (short one) this way (of four) is for Hance Hamilton.

Francis W. Hamilton, 1817 - 1835., a male name.

HAMILTON, Hance, 12 Aug 1775 - 26 Sep 1843. 68 years. "native of S. Carolina" (Vet. of Creek War.)
