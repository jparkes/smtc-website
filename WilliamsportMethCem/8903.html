WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BULLOCK, John, 1 Oct 1768 - 3 Feb 1850. Age 82 yrs. Mason.. Believe this is the John Bullock monument based on a couple of clues. For some reason I missed photographing it. 

BULLOCK, Howell Collier, 25 Jun 1838 - 17 Mar 1909. 70-8-20. "Son of Effie & John; d. Birmingham, Ala." (2nd. Lt., Co. K, 1st (Field's) Inf., C.S.A.; son of Maj. John & Effie Huddleston Bullock.)
Our focus is on the tumbled memorial on our left. Several clues tells me this is the Howell Bullock memorial though it is fallen face down. 
Photographed on a wet Tuesday morning 27 Jan 2009 and published here 3 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin. Transcribed and Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Feb 2009.
