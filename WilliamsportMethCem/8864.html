WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Wilkins Memorials

WILKINS, Mary A., 11 Sep 1809 - 23 Mar 1876. "Wife of J. A. Wilkins." (Daughter of Hans & Nancy Bell Hamilton of Ireland & N.C.)
Inscription Reads: "IN -- memory -- of Mary A. wife of J. A. -- WILKINS born Sept -- 11th 1809 & died -- Aug 27th 1847 aged -- 37 years 11 months -- & 16 days."
Photographed on a wet Tuesday morning 27 Jan 2009 and published here 3 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin. Transcribed and Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Feb 2009.
