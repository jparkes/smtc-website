WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Duncan & Hamilton memorials, very difficult to read when wet. Stone on end (of four) is for Nancy. Be not confused by the smaller footstones on our right.

Nancy Hamilton, 1779 - 1862.

HAMILTON, Nancy, 7 Feb 1779 - 14 Feb 1862. (Broken.) (Nee Bell, native of Ireland; mar. Hance Hamilton on 31 Jul 1798 in S.C.) 
Photographed on a wet Tuesday morning 27 Jan 2009 and published here 3 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin. Transcribed and Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Feb 2009.
