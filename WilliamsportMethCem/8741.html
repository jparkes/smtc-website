WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Dr L. Eugene & Mary C. Ragsdale.

RAGSDALE, L. Eugene (M.D.), 18 Jul 1875 - 23 Jan 1949. (Son P. A. Eugenia Mangrum Ragsdale; Administrator of Tenn Home for the Feeble Minded, Nashville.)
RAGSDALE, Mary C., 28 Oct 1882 - 19 Jan 1962. (Married to Dr. Leonard Eugene Ragsdale on 19 May 1904; Daughter of J.P. & Cassa Rucker Choate.)
