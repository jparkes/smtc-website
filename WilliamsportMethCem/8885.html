WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Duncan & Hamilton memorials, very difficult to read when wet. Tall stone on this end (of four) is for Samuel Hamilton.

Samuel Hamilton, 1817 - 1835., a male name.

HAMILTON, Samuel, 20 Mar 1801 - 27 Feb 1869. (Son Hance & Nancy Bell Hamilton; deaf mute.)
Photographed on a wet Tuesday morning 27 Jan 2009 and published here 3 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin. Transcribed and Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Feb 2009.
