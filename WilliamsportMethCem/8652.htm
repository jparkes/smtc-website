ALDERSON, Lillie Mae (Russell), 6 Jun 1905 - 29 May 2007 (died in Columbia, Tn.; a Maury Co. native, born in little settlement of Chartersville, near the Hickman Co. line, Daughter of Grover Cleveland Russell, Sr., and Liza Jane Johnson Russell; she was 101 years old, graduate of the class of 1924 from Columbia Central High School, rode horseback to school near Primm Springs where she taught for years; 1925 marred to Thomas Howard "Buck" Alderson, he died 28 Jan 1973; she was preceded in death by a grandson, Michael Alderson, sister, Elmer White, brothers, G.C. Jr., and Bill Russell.)
ALDERSON, Thomas Howard "Buck", 28 Dec 1898 - 28 Jan 1973.
ALDERSON, Howard Eugene "Snooks", 2 Feb 1927 - no other date. Mason.
ALDERSON, Lucy Belle Hicks, 12 Aug 1929 - 17 mar 1981. (Daughter Jimmie Brown Hicks; mar. Howard E. Alderson.)
