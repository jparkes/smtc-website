WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Rachel A. Rumbo.

Rachel A. Rumbo.

Reverse graphic image made to aid in reading this inscription.

RUMBO, Rachel, 25 Aug 1846 - 13 Jan 1857. (Another spelling of this name is RAMBO)
Inscribed as: "IN -- memory of -- Rachel A. Rum -- bo was born -- Augus 25th -- 1846 and -- died January 13 th, 1857."
Photographed on a wet Tuesday morning 27 Jan 2009 and published here 3 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin. Transcribed and Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Feb 2009.
