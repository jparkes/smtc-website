WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Duncan & Hamilton memorials, very difficult to read when wet. Second stone (tall one) this way next to the one on far end (of four) is for Francis Hamilton, (a young man).

Francis W. Hamilton, 1817 - 1835., a male name.

HAMILTON, Francis W., 24 Apr 1817 - 6 Aug 1835. 18 yrs.
