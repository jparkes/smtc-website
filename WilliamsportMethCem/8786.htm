WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Robert Frederick Jones, military memorial.

JONES, Robert Frederick "Bob" Jones, 27 Dec 1924 - 5 Jun 2003 in Murfreesboro, Tn. EMI US Navy, WWII (Milwaukee, Wisconsin native; son of George W. Jones and Ethel Bechtel Jones; grad. of Marquette University, degree in Electrical Engineering; Veteran U.S. Navy; mar. to McLin Moore Jones.)
JONES Anne McLin Moore, 9 Apr 1935 - 27 Jan 2012. Married to Robert Frederick Jones Sr., 17 Dec 1961
