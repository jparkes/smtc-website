WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Wilkins Memorials

James M. Wilkins 2 Feb 1843 - 8 May 1919, Pvt. Co. E. 1 Tenn Cav C.S.A. Awarded "Southern Cross of Honor." (for staying with his cause)

James M. Wilkins

WILKINS, James M., 2 Feb 1843 - 8 May 1919. (Son J.A. & Mary A. Wilkins; Pvt, Co E. 1st Tenn Cav. C.S.A.,(other records say he was also with: 6th Wheeler's Cav., C.S.A., Cross of Honor; last born of 15 children.)
WILKINS, Nettie F., 1854 - 1909. (Daughter of Garrett & Frances E. Fitzgerald; mar. J.M. Wilkins, 3 Dec 1877.)
