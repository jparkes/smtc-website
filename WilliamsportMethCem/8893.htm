WILLIAMSPORT METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, WILLIAMSPORT, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Tyler & White Memorials, Of the three tall stones on the right our subject K.T.S Tyler memorial in the middle.

Inscribed on east side as:TRUMAN TYLER.

Inscription on west side: K.T.S. TYLER.

TYLER, K.T.S., 18 Dec 1831 - 29 Jul 1859. Inscribed on Front as Truman Tyler. Probably the name he used.
Photographed on a wet Tuesday morning 27 Jan 2009 and published here 3 Mar 2009 by Wayne Austin. Transcribed and Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Feb 2009.
