DERRYBERRY - SHARP CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Derryberry - Sharp Cemetery, Exit 46 of Interstate 65.
1986, MCTC Instructions: Take Highway 99 east; just beyond I-65 enter driveway past first service station on right.  Cemetery is east of house on Mrs. Lottie Brown's place.
2005, Al Studman's Instructions: Behind Moses Market just east of I-65 about 300 yards, and just south of Bearcreek Road out in the field there about 300 yards. 
2005 Note by Wayne Austin: At one time I believe the old home place of the Derryberry's was only a short distance to the right of the solitary Oak tree which markes the cemetery today.
