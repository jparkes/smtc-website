DERRYBERRY - SHARP CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

SHARP, Annie May, 20 Dec 1879 - 17 Dec 1922. 63-1-27. (Dau. Newt C. & G. Anne Davis Derryberry; wife of Martin M. Sharp.) One would have to believe that Annie's husband Martin M is interred in this cemetery as she was up in years when she died and produced four children by Martin. See the book MCTC for more info on this family.
