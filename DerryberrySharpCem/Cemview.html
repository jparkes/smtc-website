DERRYBERRY - SHARP CEMETERY, POTTSVILLE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


This view looks at the cemetery in a westerly direction. You are looking at all the memorials and funeral markers. Seen in the distance is the Bear Creek Pike overpass of I-65. Just to the right of the overpass on this side in the north corner of I-65 and Bear Creek Pike is the large and active Jones Cemetery.

Derryberry - Sharp Cemetery. Notice the solitary Oak tree that marks the spot.
