MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE




A. Manson Morrow
#001 Archie Manson / #002 Edith Wall (Double Marker)
12 Dec 1904 / 1 July 1910
14 September 1983 / 25 February 1983
Married 20 July 1931.
In Loving Memory of Daddy / In Loving Memory of Mamma
Archie Manson Morrow is the son of Willie Manson & Matilda Kincade Morrow. Manson taught school in Virginia or West Virginia where he met Edith, who was also a teacher. Edith Walls Morrow was the daughter of Alonzo Marshall & Lula Belle (Dot) Harvey, 1844-1942.
