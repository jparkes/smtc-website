MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
#003 Daniel Warren Thomas
6 January 1970 - 20 June 1988
Son of Jim and Myra Brother of David, James, John

Daniel died in 1988 as the result of complications due to a car accident. Loved and respected by all who knew him. Daniel Warren Thomas attended Columbia Academy (a Church of Christ supported school) in Columbia, Maury Co. Tenn. He played football for the school.
