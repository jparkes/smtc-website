MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
#006 Mary Alice Anderson- Mother
28 July 1914 (1) - 26 June 1977
Mary Alice Anderson, was the daughter of Frank M. & Estelle Blocker Bishop. She was a visitor at the Maury County Jail, in Columbia, Maury Co. Tn. when the jail burned and killed several in the family and others. The jail fire was the result of a prisoner setting a mattress on fire. Smoke was the cause of death in most cases.
