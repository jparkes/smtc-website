MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
#007 Carolyn Anderson Irwin
1943-2006
Carolyn Anderson Irwin was the wife of Frank Irwin Jr.; Frank also died in the jail fire of 1977 & is not buried in the Morrow Cemetery.
Carolyn's burial is on top of her mother's (Mary Alice Anderson) grave.
