MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#099 Blocker (Double Stone)
" Gone but not Forgotten"
P.B. (Peter Bird) Blocker / Ella A. (Gainey) Morrow Blocker
(8 July) 1869  / (24 April) 1875
(16 March) 1908 / (25 July) 1924
Peter Bird Blocker is the son of Elijah & Elizabeth Wilkes Blocker (married on 7 July 1862). He inherited his nearsightedness.
Ella A. Morrow is the daughter of John Wesley & Alice Rebecca Love.
