MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
#008 Margaret Kinzer Roland- Daughter
2 July 1951 - 26 June 1977
Margaret Kinzer Roland was the daughter of Herman I. & Mary Alice Bishop Anderson. She was the wife of prisoner, Buck Rowland, and was visiting the jail in June of 1977. Buck Rowland is not buried in the Morrow Cemetery.
