#100 Alice Estelle Blocker Bishop
17 December 1890
23 December 1927
Alice Estelle Blocker Bishop is the wife of Frank Bishop. She died at age 37 years of heart disease. Four children were born to Alice Estelle
& Frank Bishop. Mary Alice Bishop (married Herman Anderson),
Marvin Bishop, Addie B. Bishop (married Mora B. Baxter), Allie Z.
Bishop (married John Wesley Blocker)
