MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#031 John Andrew Love
27 July 1842 - 1 February 1914
John Andrew Love is the son of Thomas Kendrick (born 11 January 1917) & Virginia Jane Love (daughter of Edom C. & Annie Strickland Love, born 5 January 1817). John A. was also called William (not sure if this was part of his name or nickname).

He was a veteran of Adali Biffle Co. He married Mary Jane Hines on the 14 March 1867. Father of William T. Love
