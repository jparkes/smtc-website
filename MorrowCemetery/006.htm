MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
#005 Stella M. (Marie) Anderson - Daughter
2 February 1949 - 22 April 2007. Interred 24 Apr 2007, Stella was the daughter of Herman and Mary Alice Bishop Anderson. Stella's mother was killed in the jail fire in Columbia.
