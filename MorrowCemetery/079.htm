MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#073 Willie Snow Morrow Scott
(11 Oct) 1899 - (29 Oct)1925. (date Ref: death certificate.)
Willie Snow is the daughter of W. Otey & Rebecca Ballard Morrow
She was the first wife of Walter Scott, who is buried in the Sheboss
Cemetery in Hickman County, Tennessee. He married Mary Lou
Truelove after the death of Willie Snow .

Foot marker- W. M. S.
