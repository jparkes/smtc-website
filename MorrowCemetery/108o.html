MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#113 Grave not marked
Addie Dell Blocker Region
18 February 1898- 24 April 1940
Died at the birth of her baby
Addie Dell Blocker was married to Walker W. Davis, and they had a son Thomas Pete Davis. Her second marriage was to Dixie Roland, and they
had one daughter Thelma Roland. Her third marriage was to Everett
Region on 15 July 1930. They had a daughter names Sarah Elizabeth
Region. May have had other children, Cecia,(died at birth) Dorothy(May 1923, Carl Edward, Evie(born 1937),and Everette Jr. (born 22 April 1940, died 2 or 3 days later, about the time of the mothers death) This information comes from notes of Louise M. Elliott
