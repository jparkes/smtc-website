MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Following Highway 99 west out of Columbia, just before entering Hampshire Community , turn right just past Cedar Hill Church of Christ on the right onto Taylor's Store Road. Follow this road around to Loves Branch Road and turn left there. Following this road will bring you around to the Morrow Cemetery sign on the right just before a large barn. Turn right on this chert road and follow it around to the left. This is the entrance to the cemetery. The cemetery is fenced all the way around the mound that forms the cemetery. This is the only entrance and the only exit.
