MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#032 Headstone with no markings. 
Notes of Louise M. Elliott indicate this is the grave of:
Mary Jane Hines Love
6 August 1847 - 28 August 1917
Mary Jane Hines married John A. Love on 14 March 1867, and after
John A. Love's death, she drew a widow pension on John A. #7336
