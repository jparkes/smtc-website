MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#055 Infant Daughter of Manley W. & Lettie A. Morrow
10 April 1923- 12 April 1923
"A Little Bud of Love"
This little girl was born when Manley & Lettie Morrow lived on the Forrest Cummins farm at Cathey's Creek. This farm is now owned by Bill Brown.
