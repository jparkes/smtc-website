MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#029 Nettie Love Shea
11 March 1868 - 8 June 1930
An inspiration to all who knew her. 
Records indicate that her given name was Jennet Love. She was the daughter of John A. & Mary Jane Hines Love. Notes of Louise M. Elliott indicate that she died of Heart Failure and Tuberculosis. She married Dan Shea. After her death Dan remarried and is buried in Santa Fe, Maury Co. Tennessee.
