MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#054 Morrow (Double Stone)
Manley Walker Morrow / Lettie Pearl Anderson Morrow
25 September 1893 / 5 June 1897
29 April 1977 / 10 February 1951
These are the parents of Lizzie Louise, William Anderson, Gerline Brown, Infant Daughter, Willie Mae, and Betty Jean Morrow.
Louise married William Howell Elliott
William A. Married Marcie D. Collier
Gerline Brown married Willard Brown Gibson
Infant daughter died at age 2 days old
Willie Mae married John Willie Hines
Betty Jean married C. Foster Fleming Jr.

Faye E. Jackson & Sandra Lindsey at the graves of their grandparents. Thanks ladies for your efforts in making this site available to the public. Your works will be appreciated by the many visitors.

This is a few years earlier. Same memorial stone but Sandra as a little six year old child. My grandmother, Lettie Pearl Anderson Morrow had been buried about 6 months. Great family tradition. These photos say something much about family pride and love for grandparents.
