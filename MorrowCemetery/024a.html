MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#024 Adella Love Regeon (Region)
Died age 54
Married to Melvin Regeon (buried Mobley Cemetery 1898-1969 in Hickman County, Tennessee) on 29 November 1903. She was the daughter of John A. Love & Mary Jane Hines Love.
This grave is not marked. There is no marking for her grave, only the notes of Louise M. Elliott that state she is buried in this cemetery, next to the unmarked graves of W. T. & Susie Love's twins that are also in unmarked graves near W.T. & Susie.
