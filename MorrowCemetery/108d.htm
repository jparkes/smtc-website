MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#098 Blocker (Double Stone)
John Wesley Blocker / Allie Z. Bishop Blocker
14 July 1895 / 13 December 1895
23 October 1919 / 15 February 1918
Allie Z. Blocker is the daughter of John & Lula Green Bishop
Daughter of John Wesley & Allie Z. Bishop Blocker was Bessie Mae who lived in West Tennessee.
John Wesley Blocker married Esther Smith after the death of Allie Z. Bishop
John Wesley died at the age of 24 years at Kettle Mills, Maury County, Tennessee
Esther Smith married Eldridge Travis after the death of John Wesley Blocker.
