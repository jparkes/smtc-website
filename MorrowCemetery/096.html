MORROW CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

#086 Leffie Brown Morrow
1897-1927
Leffie Brown Morrow is the daughter of W. Otey & Rebecca Ballard Morrow.
Leffie never married. Requested that children of later generations be named for her. Thus Gerline Brown Morrow Gibson, Edna Brown Morrow Vowles.
