CLYMORE CEMETERY #1, HARDISON'S MILL, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


Located on Hwy 431, about 1/4 mile south of the Duck River Bridge at Hardison's Mill. It is behind a house on the west side of the highway. F. L. H., 1986. Found at these Latitude & Longitude Map Coordinates: 35� 36.31'N, 86� 49.52'W (NAD83/WGS84), per Topozone.
