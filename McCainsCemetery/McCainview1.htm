McCain's Cumb. Presb. Church Cemetery Maury County, Tennessee

The above are two views of the historic McCains Cumberland Presbyterian Church and Cemetery. 
The ladies on the left are Paulene V. Barlar Thurman and her daughter Wanda Jones. Paulene lived about all her 80+ years within a couple of miles of this church and can tell you much if it history. 
There has been a great little history written about this church and it appeared on the Historic Maury Site for a time. I do not know where it is now but it is worthy reading. These photos were taken from a (Barlar, Coffee & Wiley) Family History tour conducted by Wayne Austin, 5/27/2003.
