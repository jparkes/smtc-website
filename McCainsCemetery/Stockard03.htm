McCAIN'S CUMBERLAND PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

I just wanted to let it be known that Alice Stockard is buried at McCain's Cumberland Presbyterian. She and Robert Stockard are interred there in unmarked graves.  On one of my trips down I found the location of the graves in their site map that they have at the gate, but it is no longer there. They are buried next to his parents, Joel Brevard (J.B.) Stockard and Margaret Anderson (Margaret M.) Stockard , and his sister Susan.  
Also according to an obituary that I found at the archives James Stockard a four yr old son of Mr and Mrs Robt. Stockard of Bear Creek Pike who died on 3 Oct 1901 is interred at McCains Cemetery. I did find that the family lived near there at that time, so I am assuming he is my kin. Yes, according to the obit he is buried at McCains.
 
I do not have a date of death for Robt. Stockard. I know that he died after the 1910 census, and Alice is listed as a widow in the 1920 census. I am guessing that he passed prior to the issuance of death certificates, so that is between 1910 and 1914?
Stockard, Robert, died about 1912, birth known (husband of Alice McDonald Stockard). (unmarked grave)
Stockard, James, died about 3 Oct 1901, birth about 1897, (son of Robert Stockard; unmarked grave)

Charlotte
