McCain's Cumbumberland Presbyterian Church Cemetery Maury County, Tennessee

The above are two different maps showing the location of historic McCains Church & Cemetery. Notice the black pointers for finding the location. From Columbia (shown above at the top center of the map) go south on Highway 31 about 8 miles to the McCains Village and turn left on McCains Lane. The church and cemetery will be on the right. Now to the bottom Aerial View notice the white circle on the right side of McCains Lane. That is the circular drive that one takes to get to the church. The long dark object is the church and the cemetery is back or south of that where the arrow indicates.
