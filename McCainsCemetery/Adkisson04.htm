McCAIN'S CUMBERLAND PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE 
Adkisson Eliza (abt 1831) - 3/20/1859

Eliza Adkisson was born abt 1831 according to the 1850 census. Her maiden name was Eliza V Craig. She was the first of 5 wives married to John G Adkisson. I found the inscription you noted from the headstone interesting as I did find a marriage certificate for John G and Eliza. They were married Mar 20, 1849 in Maury County, TN.
From Judy Diaz via E-mail to Mary Bob Richardson. 8 Aug 2011.
