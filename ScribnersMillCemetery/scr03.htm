SCRIBNER'S MILL  CEMETERY, ALSO KNOWN AS BETHANY CHURCH CEMETERY MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 AYDELOTT, C. D. 14 Aug 1830 - 26 Mar 1881. C. D. Aydelott is the brother of Rebecca Aydelott who was the second wife of John Scribner. Rebecca was the mother of Rhoda Scribner. Rhoda was  the wife of William Littleton Young.
