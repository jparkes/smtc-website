SCRIBNER'S MILL  CEMETERY, aka BETHANY CHURCH CEMETERY MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Scribner's Mill Cemetery is located on the premises of the Bethany Church of Christ but across the McKnight road. The stream shown above is Fountain Creek. The old Scribner's Mill was near the intersection of the road and the Creek. These old mills often served as the hub of the community in the olden days when they were used to turn wheat and corn grain into flour & meal.
