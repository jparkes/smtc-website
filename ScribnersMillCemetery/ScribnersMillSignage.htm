SCRIBNER'S MILL CEMETERY MONUMENT

JULY 2009 BILLY RAY OSBORNE STARTED A PROJECT TO REPLACE THE OLD RUSTED & FADED SIGN WITH A MORE PERMANENT MARKER.

THE PLACEMENT OF THIS MONUMENT WAS MADE POSSIBLE BY CONTRIBUTIONS FROM VARIOUS FRIENDS AND FAMILY MEMBERS OF SCRIBNER'S MILL CEMETERY & CHURCH.
