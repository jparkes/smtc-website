SCRIBNER'S MILL  CEMETERY, ALSO KNOWN AS BETHANY CHURCH CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 SCRIBNER, Lewis A., 9 Jul 1884 - 11 Dec 1956, "Father".
and
 SCRIBNER, Lula A., 6 Apr 1886 12 Oct 1966, "Mother".
also
SCRIBNER, Louella D., 18 Oct 1909 - 15 Oct 1951, "Sister".
 Lewis A Scribner is the son of Lewis and Amanda. His wife was Lula A. Roland. Louella is listed on her stone as "sister". My records show her is the daughter of Lewis A. and Lula. The stones must have been placed by children of Lewis A and Lula. Also I believe there is a main monument for these stones which was not photographed.
