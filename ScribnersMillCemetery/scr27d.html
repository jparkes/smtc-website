SCRIBNER'S MILL  CEMETERY, ALSO KNOWN AS BETHANY CHURCH CEMETERY MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
SCRIBNER, Jefferson D., 4 May 1886 - 13 Dec 1939. (Son of George W. & Mandy Scribner.) 
and
SCRIBNER, Myrtle Lois, 29 Mar 1902 11 Aug 1964. (nee Myrtle Lois Shelton.).
Jefferson Davis Scribner was the son of George Washington and Mandy (last name unknown). Mandy was G.W.'s second wife. G. W. is the son of John A. T. Scribner and Huldah Garrett.
