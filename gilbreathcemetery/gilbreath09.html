GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GILBREATH, Martha E., 21 Jul 1863 - 22 Feb 1864. Since she is buried next to Isaiah R. Gilbreath located in the Coffee Family area of the cemetery indicates that she was the daughter of Isaiah & Mary L. Coffee. Inscription reads: "To the memory of Martha E.
