GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

MORROW, Jane, 2 Oct 1795 - 22 Apr 1852. 56-06-20. Wife of Leonard Morrow. (Daughter of John, Sr. & Mary Coffee Gilbreath.) Inscription: "SACRED to the memory of JANE, wife of Leonard Morrow was born Oct 2nd 1795. died April 22nd 1852. Aged 56 yrs 6 months and 20 days".
