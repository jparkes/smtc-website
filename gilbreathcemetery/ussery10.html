GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

USSERY, John G., 16 Nov 1852 - 10 Apr 1878. Son of Joseph C. & Susan M. Ussery. Inscription:" JOHN G. Son of JOSEPH C. & SUSAN M USSERY Born Nov. 16 1852 Died Apr. 10, 1878.
This stone is next to a massive Oak tree. The stone is on the south side of the tree. The tree may well hold some records for size. It is hugh. Judging from the leaves in this photo it is a Pin Oak.
