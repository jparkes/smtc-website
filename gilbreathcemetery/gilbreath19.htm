GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
GILBREATH, Mary, Sep 1758 - 24 Aug 1843. 84-11-24. Wife of John Gilbreath, Sr. Inscription reads: "SACRED To the memory of MARY GILBREATH wife of John Gilbreath who was born Sep 1758 and died August 24th 1843 Aged 84 years 11 months and 24 days." (Mary Coffee Gilbreath was believed to be the daughter of Chesley, Jr & Margaret Baldwin Coffee).
Information about their son Andrew Gilbreath can be found here: http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.ancestry.com/~sybleg/perrymanwebpgs/2422.html
