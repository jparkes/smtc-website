PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Gilbreath-Morrow Cemetery, Preacher Holt Road, Maury County TN

 Looking northwest standing behind and north of the giant Oak tree which is partly shading this photo at the Gilbreath area. Notice the fieldstone marking a grave (hard to see because of the darkness) between the Susan M Ussery grave and the John Baker Gilbreath grave. More than likely someone closely related to Susan or John. Dec 2002 Photo by WA.

 Same view but showing & labeling all the marked stones of the Gilbreath area. One can see the edge of the large Oak Tree on your left. Use your scroll bars to scroll around and see all the stones in this photo. It is a wide panoramic view. Area out back of these stones and to your right have many open areas for fieldstones and other grave markers with no inscriptions. Minerva E. Gilbreath has two stones one is hidden from your view behind the John Baker Gilbreath stone. The foot stones are located on the opposite side of the grave from Head Stone Markers  Dec 2002 Photo & graphics by WA.
