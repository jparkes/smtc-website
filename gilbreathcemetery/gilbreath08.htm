GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GILBREATH, Isaiah R., 1 Dec 1828 - 21 Nov 1864. 35-10-20. "To the memory of ISAAH R. GILBREATH who was born Dec 1, 1828 and departed this life Nov 21, 1864. Aged 35 yrs, 10 ms, & 20 ds". Son of James & Elizabeth Baker Gilbreath. Married Mary L. Coffee on Sept 29, 1862.  Mary L. later married J. J. Fleming and had other children and is buried in Rose Hill Cemetery with her 2nd husband. To Isaah & Mary was born William James Gilbreath.
Source: Lester Galbreath GED Ancestry.com and Cemeteries of Maury County Tennessee by Fred Hawkins.
