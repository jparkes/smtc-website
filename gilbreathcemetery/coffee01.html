GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



Calvin & Elizabeth (Fine) Coffee, circa 1860.

COFFEE, Calvin, 5 July 1805 - 16 Jan 1889. Inscription: "IN MEMORY OF CALVIN COFFEE JULY 5, 1805 - JAN. 16, 1889 Erected by his grandson, W. W. Wilcoxson". Son of Nathan and Elizabeth Gilbreath Coffee. Nathan and Elizabeth are interred in the Reeses Chapel Cemetery. It is believed the Calvin Coffee's lived across Preacher Holt Road in what is now (2002) the Buckner Home when it was once a log cabin.
