GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

MORROW, A. A. (Andrew Alexander), 6 Feb 1836 - 18 Jun 1898. (Son of John Leonard and Jane Gilbreath Morrow; mar. to Martha Coffey Morrow; Early preacher at Bethel Christian Church.) The inscription is on the south side. His 1st wife Susan P.'s d. 1878 inscription is on the north side of the stone.
