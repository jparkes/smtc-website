GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

MORROW, Susan P., 10 Mar 1834 - 7 Apr 1852. 18-00-27. Daughter of Leonard & Jane Morrow. Inscription Reads: IN memory of SUSAN P. MORROW daughter of Leonard and Jane Morrow. was born March 10th 1834 died April 7th 1852. Aged 18 years and 27 days.
