Gilbreath-Morrow Cemetery, Preacher Holt Road, Maury County TN

 Looking east toward the giant (Pin?) Oak tree. In the center next to the Oak is the memorial of John G Ussery a 16 year old son of Joseph C & Susan M. (Gilbreath) Ussery.  Unseen but on the opposite side of the tree is Susan's Stone. We made the photo standing in the Morrow section. Dec 2002 Photo by WA.

 Looking east toward your right of the giant (Pin?) Oak Tree. Another view of the Coffee Area of the Cemetery. Dec 2002 photo & graphics by WA.
