GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

USSERY,Susan M. b. 1832 - 12 Jul 1858. in 26th year. Nee Gilbreath, Wife of Joseph Carrol Ussery; married Dec 14, 1851. Joseph is interred in the Roper Cemetery on Factory Creek in Giles County TN. This stone is next to a massive Oak tree on the north side of the tree. The tree may well hold some records for size. It is hugh with a diameter of at least 10 feet. Judging from the leaves in this photo it is a Pin Oak.
