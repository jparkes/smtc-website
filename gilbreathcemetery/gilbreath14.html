GILBREATH-MORROW CEMETERY-PREACHER HOLT ROAD-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GILBREATH, Emily E., (Emily Esther Morrow), 25 Nov 1831 - 1 Sep 1853. Inscription: "SACRED To the memory of Emily E. wife of ABSALUM M. GILBREATH, was born Nov 25th 1831 and died Sept 1st 1853 Aged 21 years 9 months and 6 days." (The daughter of John Leonard and Jane Gilbreath Morrow)
