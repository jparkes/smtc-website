CHAFFIN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-WILEY HOLLOW ROAD

Inscription on east face.

Photo made Jan 15, 2008, after restoration. North side of Chaffin memorial.


CHAFFIN, Susan, 20 Feb 1820 - 12 Apr 1898. 78-01-22. Wife of H. G. Chaffin.' Daughter of Bennet & Martha Kerby. The large tower style memorial of H. G. & Susan is no longer toppled. This lettering is on one north side and H. G's inscription is on the south side. The initialed footstones mark Susan's as on the west side. Inscription reads: "SUSAN wife of H. G. Chaffin Born Feb 20 1820 Died April 12, 1898 Age 78 Yrs 1 Mo 22 Days."
