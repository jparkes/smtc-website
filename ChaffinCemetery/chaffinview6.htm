PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Chaffin Cemetery-Wiley Hollow Road


We are looking at the western edge of the cemetery. The John & Sue have used all the loose fieldstones to create a rock wall much like they would in the olden days. Jan 15, 2008 photo by WA.

A view looking mostly west over the cemetery shows the central right section of the cemetery. All four of the box tombs can be seen from this view including one on our far middle right. When I first visited this cemetery in 1988 the old dead Beech tree in the upper right of the photo was still alive and a very large tree. It soon started dropping its limbs and upon one visit it had dropped about a 2 ton limb right down on the William & Lucy Chaffin memorial. Luckily that stone is very durable and was not damaged.  Jan 18, 2008 Photo by WA.
