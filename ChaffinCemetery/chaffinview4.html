PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Chaffin Cemetery-Wiley Hollow Road


Another view looking at the graves marked with fieldstones. In studying the history of the Chaffins and Kerbeys. I find no history of slave ownership so these are unlikely to be slave graves. Jan 15, 2008 photo by WA.

A view looking mostly west over the cemetery shows the central part of the cemetery. The Chaffin & Kerby uprights loom on the left. Two of the box tombs can be seen in the lower left of the photo. You are looking at the foot of the graves then to the head on the west the traditional way Christians are buried. Jan 18, 2008 Photo by WA.
