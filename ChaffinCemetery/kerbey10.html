CHAFFIN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-WILEY HOLLOW ROAD

Photo made 1/15/08 after restoration


Photo dated 12/20/2002 before stone was cleaned.
KERBEY, "Bennet Kerbey, Father of Susan Chaffin, birth unknown - died Apr 23 1840." Stone is now upright, but is in remarkably good shape. Small object on the left is a fallen footstone of the Chaffin Children's Graves further to the left. Bennett was listed as a Stone cutter in one of the Censuses.
