PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Chaffin Cemetery-Wiley Hollow Road

A View looking at cemetery from the lower northwest corner near the William Chaffin Memorial. Prior to the restoration the viewing distance was much more restricted as shown in the labeled photo below before the restoration. Photo dated 1/15/2008. The digging above is largely caused by the Brawders'(owner, restorers) removing roots in an attempt to get the land level and also setting up some of the footstones.

A view looking from about the same angle using a photo dated Dec 20, 2002. This was prior to the restoration and contrast the improvement made to the cemetery by the Brawders'.
