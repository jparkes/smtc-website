Photo dated 12/20/2002 Before restoration.
BARNETT, "ANNIE LEE DAUGHTER OF M. L. & F. E. BARNETT. BORN April 12th 1868. died April 20th 1868." The Parents; Marcus L. & Fannie "Frances Elizabeth" Barnett are buried in the Glenwood Cemetery about 2 miles northwest from here.
The Brawders did a great job of cleaning this stone. The difference in readability between the two stones is the sunshine reflecting on the face on the oldest photo or the one dated 12/20/2002. Sunlight angled across the face from the southeast brings out the inscription.
