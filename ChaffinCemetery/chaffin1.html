CHAFFIN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-WILEY HOLLOW ROAD


CHAFFIN, W. J., (William J.) 30 Jul 1853 - 11Aug 1916. Father. (Son of H. G. & Susan.) 
and
CHAFFIN, Lucy Ann (Scott), 15 Sep 1856 -no death date. Mother. Death Rec. say Lucy died 11 May 1921.

This memorial is composed of 8" thick high quality granite. Photo by Wayne Austin, Update information by Pat Cribbett 2 Jun 2008.
