MAURY COUNTY - MEMORIAL - THOSE THAT PAID WITH THEIR LIVES

 
About the same angle as the previous photo but further back showing the decorative wrought iron fencing surrounding the memorial. This area view of the memorial to the War dead of Maury County is located across from the James Knox Polk ancestral home, the 11th president of the U. S., in Columbia Tennessee. The back side of the Maury County visitor center is shown on your right. You should go there first upon visiting Columbia and Maury County. In side is a very helpful person with information and maps for visitors. Photo and info by Wayne Austin July 20, 2003
