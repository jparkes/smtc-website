MAURY COUNTY - MEMORIAL - THOSE THAT PAID WITH THEIR LIVES

 
I was accompanied on my photography trip by the two boys above who posed that day for this photo in February of 2005. They really enjoyed reading the names on the memorial wall. They enjoyed this part of the trip the most, but we visited the Spring Hill Cemetery, the Amis Cemetery in Culleoka and the War Dead Memorial here in Columbia all the same day. The boys are both fast friends and 13 years of age. On our left is Ethan Ingram and on the right is my son, Andrew Gray. Photo and information by Rick Gray on 26 February 2005.
