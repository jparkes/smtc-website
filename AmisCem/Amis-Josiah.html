The Amis Cemetery enclosed in a new wooden fence. Notice the yellow kitty in the right of the photo adding to the show & tell.  Photos by Rick & Andrew Gray 5 Feb 2005.
Box tomb of Josiah D. Amis. Miss kitty (name unknown) posing for the cameraman in all his/her royalty.

 AMIS, Josiah D., 10 Apr 1819 - 4 Apr 1852. Aged 32 years 11 months & 21 Days.) "Youngest son of John & Mary Amis."
Inscribed as: "SACRED To the memory of Josiah D. Amis the youngest son of John & Mary Amis born April 10 1819 departed this life April 4th, 1852 Aged 32 years, 11 months & 21 days." 
Dates do not calculate here. based on the dates given.  Probably should be 32 Years 11 Months and 24 Days. maybe with one day error in the day. This suggest the date of April 4 1852 s/b April 1, 1852 as fours and ones are often confused in translating the old inscriptions.
Photos and research by Rick Gray & his son Andrew. Visited & photographed 5 Feb 2005. Added here 26 Jan 2010.
