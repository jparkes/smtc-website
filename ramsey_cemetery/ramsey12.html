RAMSEY CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


RAMSEY, Elizabeth L., d. 30 Apr 1842. (42-05-17; nee Elizabeth Caskey; mar. Robert Ramsey, 9 Nov 1819). Inscription: "Here lies the Body of Elizabeth Ramsey. Who Departed this Life on the 30th of April in the year of our Lord 1842 Aged 42 years 5 mon. and 17 days." Photo & info by Wayne Austin 12-23-03. Relationship from MCTC.
