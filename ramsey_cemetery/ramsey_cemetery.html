Ramsey Cemetery may be found by taking Martin Hollow Road from Campbell Station and going south 3/4 of a mile. Turn east on the road leading to the Billy Allread Place. This is a very historic home setting far back off of the road. Please get permission to visit this cemetery so all will be comfortable with the purpose of your visit because you must use a private drive to get there. The Cemetery is on the right in a cleared area back 300 yards from the house. Compared to the description Mr. HawkinsÂ  gave in 1987 the cemetery is well maintained but a bit barren without the cover of trees. It is maintained and surrounded by an electric fence today. Thanks to the volunteer who is maintaining this cemetery. If you have relatives here you should consider making a contribution to the upkeep. The (private) home & cemetery has a very historic place in Campbell Station History. Originally it belonged to the Ramsey & Clark families but you may read about that in the other histories.
