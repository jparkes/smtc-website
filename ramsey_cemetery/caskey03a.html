RAMSEY CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


CASKEY, Unknown Grave marked by a Field Stone just to the south of Margaret Caskey's Stone. This may be the stone of the husband of Margaret. It also appears to mark the last grave in the second row. Lest I be a party to speculation I reveal the below information for the consideration of the reader:  

CASKEY, John I., died 1810 in Maury County, Tn. (Native of Scotland; immigrated in 1768 to Port Charles, S.C., Veteran Revolutionary War; married Margaret Coffey in 1785 in South Carolina.) Ref: Jackie W. (famgen@hotmail.com)
