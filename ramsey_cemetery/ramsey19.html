RAMSEY CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


RAMSEY, Margaret Isabella, d. 28 Oct 1838. Inscription remaining: "...... Departed this life on the 28th of October 1838 Aged 5 years and 2 months." Photo & info by Wayne Austin 12-23-03. This stone was compared to the records of Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries page 521 to complete the inscription as that of Margaret's.
