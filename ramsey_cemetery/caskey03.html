RAMSEY CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


CASKEY, Margaret, d. 15 May 1835, In 80th yr. Inscription: "SACRED TO The Memory of Margaret Caskey who departed this life the 15th of May AD 1835 in the 80th...." (year of her Age.). According to info on Find-a-grave she was Margaret Coffee likely a sister of Hugh W. Coffee who also migrated to what was to become Campbell Station.
Stone is fragmenting but appears stable for now. Will you please help preserve this photo and record. It may be the only actual record to survive for posterity. Margaret is probably the mother of Elizabeth Caskey Ramsey. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 12-23-03.
