POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

BELLAMY, Johnnie Ruth King, 3 Apr 1947 - 30 Mar 2010. (Maury Co. native; d/o Shelton M. King Jr., and Alice Hoge King who are buried at Lawrence Cemetery; mar. to Frank Bellamy for 45 years; retired from the Department of Human Services; graduated from the 1964-65 class at Hay Long High School as Valedictorian, and King College in Bristol, Tennessee in 1969; member of the Children of the American Revolution.)
Photo & information compiled and submitted by Mary Bob McClain. Mary Bob & Johnnie Ruth were good friends going back to their school years. 2 Apr 2010.
