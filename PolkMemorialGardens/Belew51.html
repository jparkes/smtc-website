POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

   Gladys Elizabeth [Tyler] Belew, 94, widow of William Elmer Belew, died Monday, 7 Jan 2008 in Savannah, Tn. Burial was in Polk Memorial Gardens. Born and raised in the Culleoka Community, she was the daughter of the late James Amos Tyler and Laura Ellen Austin Tyler and graduated from Culleoka High School. Survivors include a son, Larry (Jackie) Belew of Hohenwald; three granddaughters, Kristie Belew Hedges of Clarksville, Beth Belew Britton of Clarksville, and Tonya Pierce of Hohenwald; four great-grandchildren; several nieces and nephews. She was preceded in death by a daughter, Wilma Ingrum, two sisters Eula Whitehurst & Willie Hight; a brother, James Tyler, and a grandson, David Ingrum.
Obituary from Newsletter from Turner-Austin tidbits, from the records of Linda Sharp Yates, Carolyn Sharp Hicks & Anne Sharp Cole 31 Oct 2008.
