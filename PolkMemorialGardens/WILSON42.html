POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

 WILSON, Earline South, 2 Jul 1934 - 26 Jan 2003. (Limestone Co., Al. native; daughter of Cullen Carter & Prudie Franklin South.)
She was the mother of Shirley Gail, Tammy, Jeanie, Lisa, & David.
Her Brothers were: Hubert, Carter, Lonnie, Roger.
Hubert was also buried here at Polk Memorial Cemetery, Columbia, TN.
Her Sisters were: Margie, Wanda, Judy Gail.
Her Parents were: Cullen Carter & Prudie Viola Franklin South who are buried in Lone Oak Cemetery, Limestone County, Alabama.

Information from Frances Bryson, 11/08/2008.
