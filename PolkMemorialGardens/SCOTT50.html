POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

SCOTT, Joe Hagey, d. 15 Nov 2008 in Knoxville, Tn., (Maury Co. native; son of Burtie C. Scott and Ida Wilkin Cole Scott; 1947 grad. of Columbia Central High School; Veteran U.S. Army, 101st Airborne Div., Korean War; 1980 Maury Co. Circuit Court Clerk serving three terms until 1994; 1998 elected Maury Co. Commissioner, rep of the 1st District; Mason; Al Menah Shriner; Elks Lodge; Kiwanis Club, Gerbert Griffin American Legion Post #19; married 58 yrs to Jewel White Scott.)
