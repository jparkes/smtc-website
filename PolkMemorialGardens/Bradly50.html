POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 
Bradley, Leslie 1887 - 2 Feb 1965, Polk Memorial Gardens, Garden 'H'

Funeral services for Leslie Bradley, 77, were conducted at the Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home. The Rev. James Taylor, minister of the Riverside Methodist Church, officiated. Burial was in Polk Memorial Gardens. 

Mr. Bradley, a retired International Harvestor employee, died early Tuesday morning at the Hillview Haven Nursing Home. 
Pallbearers include Bobby Bradley, Emerson Bush, Frank Ritter, John Williams, Joe Spencer, W. C. Smithson, Leonard Ritter and Charles Porter.

The Columbia Daily Herald, Wednesday, Feb. 3, 1965, page 1.
