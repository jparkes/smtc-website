POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

DUGGER, Jimmy B., 13 Nov 1930 - 3 Aug 2009 in Mt. Pleasant. (Maury Co. native, son of Robert and Alene Alexander Dugger; mar. to Irene Rawdon Dugger on 6 Aug 1954; member of American Legion Post in Mt. Pleasant; Veteran U.S. Air Force, Korea; manager and tour guide for the Mt. Pleasant Maury Museum of Local History for 17 yrs.; preceded in death by brothers, Robert Lee and Richard Dugger, son-in-law Billy Ray "Bubba" Hardison.)  
Photo and information compiled and sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 13 Aug 2009.
