POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CHURCH, Robert M., born 10 Oct 1909 - died 13 July 1991. (dates from SSDI; Son of Walter & Annie Jones Church; married to Nuna Lavender Church.) 

Robert M. Church, 81, of Columbia died Saturday, July 13, 1991 at Maury Regional Hospital. Services were today at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home with Ted Burleson officiating. Burial was in Polk Memorial Gardens. A Maury county native, he was the son of the late Walter and Annie Jones Church. He was married to the former Nuna Lavender who survives. He was a farmer and produce manager for H.G. Hill Grocery for several years. He was a graduate of Central High School and served in the U.S. Army during World War II. Survivors in addition to his wife include three daughters, LeAnn Church and Mrs. James 'Buddy' (Marilyn) Harlan, both of Columbia and Mrs. James (Sara Jo) Farris of Brentwood; and five grandchildren. Pallbearers will be Herbert Church, John Church, Bobby Murphy, Ewell Harris, Ted Prince, Wallace Dillard, Clarence Norman, James Farris, and Pete Harlan.
The Columbia Daily Herald, Monday, 15 Jul 1991. Submitted by Judy B. Forgos, 14 Aug 2009.
