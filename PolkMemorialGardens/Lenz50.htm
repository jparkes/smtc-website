POLK MEMORIAL GARDENS - MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
 

LENTZ, Albert Sidney, 17 Jun 1930 - 6 Jan 2012. (Maury Co. native; s/o late Robert Sidney Lentz & Theola Lemay Lentz Brown; grad. of Columbia Central High School; Veteran U. S. Marines & U.S. Marine Corps Reserves i 1953 with rank of Lance Corporal, discharged in 1959; retired from Columbia Police Dept as patrolman as Lieutenant of the Detective Div; served Maury Co. Commissioner of the 2nd District; member of F.O P.; 34th Degree Scottish Mason and Columbia Masonic Lodge #31; Herbert Griffin Am. Legion Post #19; h/o Martha Hill Lentz who he mar. 12 May 1954; preceded in death by step father Thomas Marion Brown; brothers, Robert Thomas, Leslie Frank, and Herbert Jefferson Lentz; mother-in law Edna Hill, father-in-law Roy Hill; brothers - in - law Bill Howell, Wayne Cothran, Cotton Henderson, Jerry Haddock, and David Greene, sister-in-law, Sondra Hill.)
