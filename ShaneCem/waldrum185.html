SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION

WALDRUM, Calidonia "Donie", 3 Jul 1844 - 11 Jun 1880.
"Wife T. (Thomas) M. Waldrum; dau. Samuel & Mary Coffey."
Emily Caledonia "Donie" Coffey who was married to Thomas
Waldrum. She died 41 days after giving birth to Gladney (Gladna) Waldrum. Her husband
Thomas M. Waldrum was Joseph Seymour Waldrum's youngest brother.
The stone for Mary Coffey (Calidonia's mother) is seen in the background.
