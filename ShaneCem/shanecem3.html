PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Shane Cemetery - Campbell Station

These are the box tombs of a husband and wife who died young. William N. Feb 17, 1814 - July 31, 1847 & Emmaline F. (Campbell) KERR Jan 28, 1822 - May 9, 1844, Box tombs with difficult lettering to read. Pointed shaped stone on the left is that of Susannah Harris. Romanticist would wonder if William N. died of a broken heart after losing his bride Emmaline.

Fallen Memorial of William Kerr d. 1853 in center of photo between his 2nd wife Mary K and daughter Permelia. 1990 photo

Looking at some of the Kerr, Hobbs & others Memorials from the north to south. 1990 photo.
