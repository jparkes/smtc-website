SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

HOBBS, Robert Virge, 24 Aug 1877 - 12 Nov 1959. Robert Virge Hobbs was born 24 Aug 1877 and died 12 
Nov 1959. He married Martha Allison "Allie" Hannah, daughter of James Marshall Hannah and Margaret 
McKenzie in 1908. He was the son of Jefferson Lafayett Hobbs and Elizabeth Lucretia Coffee. He and Allie had 
4 children: Marvin Hannah, Revis Virgil, Margaret, and J. L. Hobbs.
and
HOBBS, Allie Hannah, 26 Aug 1884 - 8 May 1973. Allie was a native of Giles County TN. Her formal name was
Martha Allison Hannah Mckenzie. She was the daughter of James Marshall and Margaret (McKenzie) Hannah and was born 
26 Aug 1884 and died 8 May 1973. She married Robert Virge Hobbs in 1908 who is interred beside her. Allie 
was a great-great-granddaughter of John Kennedy who was one of the signers of the petition to begin Maury County
 and mother of 4 children. Photo by Wayne 6-15-02. This record was revised on 11-1-03 with information 
contributed by Rick Gray and included an error correction.
