SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

WELLS, Pricilla A., 6 Mar 1822 - 7 Aug 1887. This 
  stone was broken and the central section with the dates and surname are missing. A footstone is there with the 
  initials P.A.W., the base of the stone and upper section with the letters "Prisc" is still in place 
  as shown in the upper right inset photo. Priscilla A. (Warden)(Kerr) Wells, (Same Priscilla as listed on the 
  stone with John B. Kerr). The new memorial was placed there by descendents in 1990 and represents as far as 
  the descendents knows the husband of Priscilla who fathered their only child Joseph J. Kerr. See the John B. 
  & Priscilla Kerr memorial. The open Bible in the inset photo depicts the religious faith of Priscilla.
  In the upper inset is the broken stone of Priscilla Warden Kerr Wells who was married 2ndly to David Wells.
