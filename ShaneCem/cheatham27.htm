SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

 CHEATHAM, Martha Ann, 16 Oct 1833 - 3 Sep 1870/(1907). ‘Wife of E. H. Cheatham.’ 
(Stone broken.) Believe the stone clearly says 1870. MCTC shows the date as 1907. 
Evidence indicating 1870 is correct is the her age. If she had lived to a ripe old age of 74 she would 
have not been the only Cheatham interred in this cemetery as often happened to a wife who died prematurely 
young. She would probably have been buried with her husband. Revised 10/22/03 WA.
