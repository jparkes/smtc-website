SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

 MORRIS, Martha Ann, 24 Jan 1818 - 4 Aug 1857. “Wife John T. Morris; dau. John & Sarah Campbell.’ 
This is a magnificent memorial being the largest in the cemetery. If this stone were to be purchased today 
the price would probably be at least $10,000. It is something on the order of 20' high with an angel 
perched at the top which did not show up in this photo. It is visible in some of the area photos.
