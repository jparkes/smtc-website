 TAYLOR, Claborn, 6 Jul 1818 - 26 Feb 1899. Son of James E. & Martha Worsham Taylor. In 1840 
in Maury Co., TN he first married Elizabeth Foster (who is interred beside him here). They had seven children: 
James, William, Sarah, Francis Marion, George W., Jasper N., and Cornelia C. In 1866, in Maury Co., TN, 
Claborn second married Mary E. Thompson b. 1837 TN and they had four children together. Mary also had two 
sons Walter and Clinton Fowler from a previous marriage. Claborn appears in the 1850, 1860 and 1870 Census 
for Maury Co., TN. He was a prominent person in the southern Maury County in his prime.
