WARDEN, (Mary) 11..., 1857. Age 75 yrs. (Stone broken.) Likely to be the lost stone of Mary Warden, wife of James Warden who died 1832 and is buried in the Rise/(Rice)-Warden family Cemetery less than a mile from this place near where their home was located. This stone is identical in shape to the stones of William Kerr & Mildred L. Martin Stones. (Mary Warden was born in Pennsylvania and came to Maury County prior to 1807. She died here in Campbell Station and is the matriarch of a large family.)
