SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 

CAMPBELL, Sarah, 23 Dec 1794 - 11 Jan 1862. (67-00-18.)
‘Consort of John Campbell.’ (nee Kimbrough of N.C..) Inscription reads: 
"Sacred to the memory of Sarah Campbell, Consort of John Campbell, was born 
December 23rd 1794. Died January 11 1862. Aged 67 years & 18 days."
