PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Shane Cemetery - Campbell Station

This is a very large image scroll to the right, left, up or down to view the stones labeled in their settings

 View looking at some labeled stones in the far western section. This is the older section of the cemetery One can pick a stone and go to the individual section and view it up close, in many cases well enough to read the inscription.  June 2002 Photo by WA.

 View looking south from the northwest older section of the cemetery.  The Box Tomb of Martha Carothers is in the center to the left of the Maple Tree. The stone have been labeled for location and to show family relationships. Stones not labeled are the ones I am not aware of the person interred there, however the stones are shown individually on this site. Far distant ones are not labeled also. Hopefully this labeling procedure will aid any restoration efforts needed in the future. June 2002 Photo & Graphics by WA.
