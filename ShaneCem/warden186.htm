SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION 


WARDEN, James, 4 Feb 1811 - 13 May 1878. (son of James Sr & Mary Warden; James had three marriages 1st to Mary McDonald where he had four children according to my records. He then 2nd married Mary Gill and had Moses & Sarah Ann Warden. 3rdly he married Mary A. Ramsey & had four more children.) James Sr. is buried in Rice-Warden Cemetery
