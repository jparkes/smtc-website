PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Rivers Cemetery - The Campbell Station African American community cemetery

 View looking west into the Rivers Cemetery. Photo was made while standing in the western edge of Shane Cemetery. There were temporary Markers that are no longer readable. There are many graves that are unmarked including some recent ones. The marked graves are predominately WWI, WWII, Korea & Vietnam War Veterans. Included below is a list that was donated  by David Farris & Emma Burrell in July 2002. Anyone that has corrections or additions may e-mail these to WayneAL1@aol.com and we will get those entered.
