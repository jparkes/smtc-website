SHANE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION

John N. Warden monument. Just to our right of John's stone is the crumbled stone of his mother Mary Warden. It is not visible here.


WARDEN, John N., 31 Oct 1823 - 6 Jul 1847. Aged 23 years 8 mo and 6 days., (son of James Sr. b. 1775 & Mary Warden)
