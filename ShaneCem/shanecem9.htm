PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Shane Cemetery - Campbell Station

 View looking west from a center of the Cemetery. This is the older section of the cemetery. Notice the massive memorial to Claborn Taylor to the right of center. Basically the stones around his are of his family, including the box tombs.  June 2002 Photo by WA.

 View looking northwest from the center of the cemetery from further north than the above photo. This is a panoramic from just to the right of the above photo. Stones in the forefront are the Taylor family. June 2002 Photo by WA. 

View looking northeast from the center of the Cemetery. You would be looking at the memorials of the Campbell, White, Hobbs, Perry, Harris, Leneave & others. The large Cedars in the photo have sheltered the Cemetery for more than a hundred years. July 2002 Photo by WA.
