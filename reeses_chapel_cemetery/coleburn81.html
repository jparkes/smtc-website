COLEBURN, Calvin L., 4 Aug 1832 - 3 Jun 1834. From the appearance of stone, placement of the grave and dates of birth Calvin L. appears to be the son of Thomas F. & Isabella and the other Coleburn stones here in Reese's Chapel Cemetery probably belong to other family members. Notice the death of three of the children Sarah F., Amanda E., & Calvin L. occurred over a short span of 5 days during June 1834. This probably indicates the ravages of an infectious disease. Most likely it was Cholera. Info and photo by Wayne Austin 6-19-03
