REESE'S CHAPEL CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE


 

ALEXANDER, Sarah, 2 Feb 1778 - 22 Nov 1839. Age 61 yrs. (Dau. James & Elizabeth B. Reese; Wife of Silas Alexander. Silas moved to Fayette County Tennessee, died and is buried there.) 
Sarah REESE ALEXANDER was the wife of Silas ALEXANDER and daughter of James Polk REESE (my 5th Great-Grandpa) and his wife Elizabeth BREVARD REESE (my 5th Great-Grandma). And if I am right James Polk REESE is also buried here in this same Cemetery as well. Hope that helps from a REESE/POLK/ALEXANDER descendant. Also EASTER was Esther Nancy BROWN ALEXANDER wife of Lt. William Anderson ALEXANDER. (Patti Heath) Photo by WA 5/31/2003.
