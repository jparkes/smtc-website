REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This photo is also from the center of the cemetery looking north at the older section of the cemetery. I am located about 75 paces to the south and 20 paces to the west from the previous photo or just beyond the small round-top stone of the Elizabeth Osbourn memorial on your right. Many of the box tombs have long since lost their inscription and they have been concreted over by the restoration committee to stop the erosion. The small stones are the oldest spanning from 1815 - 1840. The box tombs are in the 1830 - 1860 timeframe. By Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
