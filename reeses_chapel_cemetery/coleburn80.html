COLEBURN, Amanda E., 29 Nov 1830 - 8 Jun 1834. From the appearance of stone, placement of the grave and dates of birth Amanda E. appears to be the daughter of Thomas F. & Isabella and the other Coleburn stones here in REESE's Chapel Cemetery probably belong to other family members. Notice the death of three of the children Sarah F., Amanda, & Calvin occurred over a short span of 5 days during June 1834. This probably indicates the ravages of an infectious disease of the time. Most likely it was Cholera. Info and photo by Wayne Austin 6-19-03
