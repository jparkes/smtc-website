COFFEE, Jacob, 14 Mar 1817 - 22 Aug 1844. Aged 27 Yrs 3 Ms. & 8 Days." (Son of Nathan & Elizabeth Coffee).
(Box tomb.) (year dates though faded appear to be, b. 1817, d. 1811. Maury County Cemetery book says 1817-1826, However inscription reads aged 27 yrs 5 ms & 8 days. Fours are sometimes hard to distinguish from ones because the crossbars in the fours are usually cut faintly, so I believe 1817 - 1844 to be correct. Photo by Wayne Austin 6-19-03
