REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BENDERMAN, Elizabeth Davis, 3 Nov 1773 - 13 Nov 1834. This grave is next to Margaret (Benderman) Alexander. It is in the deep woods back in the far southeast corner of the Cemetery almost against the rock wall. The original stone above is now the head stone and the modern granite stone is the foot marker.
