REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Panning even further right from the last photo one sees almost all of the older stones.  This photo is taken from the north wall facing southwest. Surname interred here are Coleburn, Maxwell, Henderson, Amis, Watkins, Thompson, Ridley, Reese, Coffee, Alexander, Knott, McKissick and others. By Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
