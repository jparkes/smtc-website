REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This photo is the another view of the old section looking southwest from the north side of the  rock wall. The tall stones further out are the William Henderson family. Tall stones on the right belong to the Amis family. To our far left is a pile of stones that probably was at one time a box tomb. If not then maybe it is a pile of rough cut stones marking a grave. Photo by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
