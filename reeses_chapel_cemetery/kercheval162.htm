REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

KERCHEVAL, Vance G., 10 Sep 1824 - 3 Apr 1852. This stone is located by itself over against the north edge of the rock wall about 50 feet north of the last Neelley stone. This is also the only stone in Maury County with the surname Kercheval in the book MCTC. Photo and transcription by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
