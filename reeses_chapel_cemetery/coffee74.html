COFFEE, Elizabeth, b. (Abt 1783) d. 18 Jun 1846. Aged 63 yrs. `Wife of Nathan Coffee.' (This is a Box tomb.) Elizabeth's maiden name was Gilbreath. Her parents were Andrew Gilbreath & Nancy Ann Hicklin Gilbreath all interred in the Gilbreath-Morrow Cemetery of this county, about 4 miles southwest of this place.
