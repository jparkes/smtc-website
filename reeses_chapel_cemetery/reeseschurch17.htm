REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This photo is from the center of the cemetery looking northwest at the older section. All the pointed top small stone in the front belong to he Colburn family. further down the same row one encounters the Maxwell, Dooley and others. Other surnames here are: Neelley, Davidson, Harris, Amis, Harwell, Hill,  and more. By Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
