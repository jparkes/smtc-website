REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

LUSK, Katherine Sullivan, 8 Jan 1876 - 14 Mar 1934 (Daughter of Patrick & Mary Hanks Sullivan; Wife of Ernest M. Lusk.)   
This memorial is inside an iron fence next to the Ernest Mayberry Lusk stone and is of the same style stone. This is in the Lusk family plots located outside the main rock fence in the church yard to the south of the Church. Photo by Wayne Austin, Aug 30, 2003.
