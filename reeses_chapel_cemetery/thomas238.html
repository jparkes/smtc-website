REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

THOMAS, lnfant daughter of Isaac & Asenath, 19 May - 9 Jul 1816. "Here lies the body of the infant daughter of Isaac & Asenath Thomas who was born may 19th 1816 and departed this life July 9th 1816 Aged 1 month & 20 days." This is one of the earliest stones in this cemetery and anywhere in the county for that matter. Photo & info by Wayne Austin, Aug 30, 2003. Concurs with the book MCTC.
