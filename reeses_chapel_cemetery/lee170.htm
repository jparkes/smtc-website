BROWN, David Lee "Tom", May 1886 - Jun 1953.

Daily Herald, Columbia, TN, Wed., June 17, 1953

Services Held For David L. Brown, 62, Godwin Farmer

David Lee Brown, 62, a farmer of the Godwin community died at his home at eight o'clock Tuesday evening. While he, had been in poor health for a number of years, he had worked in the
fields Tuesday and his death was unexpected. A native of Giles County, he had lived most of his life in Maury County. He was the son of the late A. J. and Martha Jane Waldrop Brown.

Funeral services were held this afternoon at the Reece's Chapel Church on the Campbellsville
pike, with the Rev. Bob. Roberts officiating. 

He is survived by two sisters, Mrs. Susie Wiley of Columbia and Mrs. Rosa Chapman of the
Mooresville Pike, three brothers, Jesse B. Brown of the Campbellsville Pike and Willie Carter
and Herman Brown of Godwin. 

Burial was in the Reece Cemetery -with Oakes & Nichols - in charge of arrangements.
