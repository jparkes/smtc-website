COLEBURN, William F.. 1 Oct 1809 - 19 Sep 1842. From the appearance of stone, placement of the grave and dates of birth William F. appears to be the son of Thomas F. & Isabella and the other Coleburn stones here in Reese's Chapel Cemetery probably belong to other family members.
