REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This above photo shows the two more box tombs. The first one is visible in this photo in the center left of the photo 
with a white top from fresh coat of cement.




This box stone coated in cement is located in old section. It is in a row of three box 
tombs in a line up from south to north first as this stone then the Susanna Ramsey 
stone and last the Esther L. Henderson memorial. I am not certain who 
it belongs to since the inscription has long since been lost. 
Photo and info by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003
