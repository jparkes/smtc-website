JOHNSTON, Martha A. V., 13 Feb 1820 - 11 Aug 1843. 23-6-3. "Consort J. C. Johnston; dau. Wm. & Elizabeth Knott." (Box tomb.) This is the last in a line up of box tombs of the Knott family beginning left to right or South to North with (1st) George W. then (2nd) Elizabeth wife of William, (3rd) William, (4th) Thomas I., and then (5th) Martha A.V.
