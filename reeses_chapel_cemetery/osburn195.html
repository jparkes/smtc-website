OSBURN, Elizabeth, 28 Feb 1783 - 16 Apr 1817. Located one row east of the 
three identical stones of William Thompson, John N & Nathaniel T. Clanton memorials. 
This stone reads: "Elizabeth Osburn was 
born February the 28, 1783.  Deceased in April E 16, 1817."
