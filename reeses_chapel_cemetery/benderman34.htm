REESE'S CHAPEL CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE
 
BENDERMAN, John W., (2 Jun) 1864 - (24 Apr) 1936. Father. (
Son of William Franklin and Mary Eveline (Hackney) Benderman. Married 1st to Ella Mae Coffey and had 5 children. Married 2nd to Mary Evaline McKissick and had 8 children).


John was born June 2, 1864 in Maury County, TN. He died April 24, 1936 Farmington, Oakland, Michigan, buried here April 26, 1936. John was the son of William Franklin and Mary Eveline (Hackney) Benderman. He married 1st to Ella Mae Coffey and had 5 children. After Ella Mae's death he then married 2nd to Mary Evaline McKissick and had 8 children; Mary Evaline is buried in the McKissick Cemtery.
