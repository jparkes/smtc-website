REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Panning to the left of the last photo this is a wide angle photo from the southeast corner looking west to northwest at the rows of memorials. This is generally more recent burials with a sprinkling of stones older than 1900. Surnames here are Stockard, Chapman, Alexander, Henderson and others. By Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
