REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This photo is a labeled view of the southwest area of the cemetery. The photo is pointed southeast. The small stones in middle right area escape my memory, but back in the thicket on our right is the Henderson family, the Wortham family, the Cunningham family, and the large well crafted stone belonging to James Kannon 1800-1874. Photo by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
