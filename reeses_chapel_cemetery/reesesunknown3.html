REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE






This was probably the remnants of a broken & partially buried base of a box tomb. 
It is located 3 paces south and 8 paces west of the next photo below which is a 
partially buried & broken box tomb top without a base. It is likely these two stone groups were at 
one time a complete box tomb but who would ever know if the two match up? It is
located in the Maxwell and Colburn and Dooley area. 

Photo and info by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003
