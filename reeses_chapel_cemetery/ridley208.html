RIDLEY, Infant daughter of Thomas E. & Asenath, 27 Feb-30 Feb 1839. "SACRED to the memory of Infant Daughter of Thos. E Ridley & Asenath R. Ridley who was born on the 27 of February 1839 and departed this life on the 30 of February 1839." Notice how the threes above look like fives with the top bar turned around. That was a prevalent way to inscribing a "3" on stones in Maury County. Photo by WA 8/30/2003.
