REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This photo is another view of the old section looking at the Coffee (Center Row) and Knott (Left Row) box tombs. The row of small stones in front of the box tombs belong mostly to the Alexander family. The tall stone further out & on the right is the Martha W. Kennedy stone.  In front of that are the box tombs of the Isaac Thomas family.
Many of the older and smaller stones have a footstone nearly as large as the head stone making a photo such as this confusing. For instance o
f the six stones in a row to our left of the Coffee Box tombs only two of them have footstones situated further to our left in front of the box tombs. Photo by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
