REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
(Use your scroll bars to see the drawing in its entirety)  

Looking at a layout of the Church and Cemetery one finds the Cemetery entrance as being behind three iron gates with 
the last boundary encountered being the gates marking the entrance thru the historic rock fence. The progression of the photographer was from east to west beginning with the burials in the upper left corner (Southeast) area of the layout because some of the yard in the front area is being used for burials. Once inside the Rock fence I covered the cemetery beginning with the east edge of the rock fence and progressing row by row to the west. Drawing not to scale by Wayne Austin. 9/20/2003
