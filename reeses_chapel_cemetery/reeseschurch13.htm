REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Panning right from the last photo one sees an open area. It is almost like there is more than one cemetery the way the graves cluster together. This photo is taken from the north wall looking southeast at the separation of the older section from the newer section on the left. The three stones on the left are those of the brown family. The box tomb in teh middle is unknown with the inscription shaled off years ago. By Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
