COFFEE, Nathan, Aug 1780 - 3 Jun 1858. 77-10-00. Photo by WA 6/19/2003. Believed to be the son of Chesley, Jr. and Margaret Baldwin Coffee who also came to Maury County in the early 1800s and is interred nearby in the Chesley Coffee Graves.
