REESE'S CHAPEL CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE


 

STOCKARD, James R., 22 Apr 1810 - 21 Jan 1833. 23 yrs. "Sacred to the memory of (James R. Stockard.) who was born April the 22 1810 & departed this life on the 21 day of January 1833 Being in the 23rd year of his age." Notice how the threes above look like fives with the top bar turned around. That was a prevalent way to inscribing a "3" on stones in Maury County. Photo by WA 8/30/2003.
