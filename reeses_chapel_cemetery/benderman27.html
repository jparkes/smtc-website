REESE'S CHAPEL CHURCH AND CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BENDERMAN, William, 15 Aug 1749 -15 Jul 1829. 80-11-00. (Revolutionary War.) This memorial is in the deep woods back in the far southeast corner of the Cemetery almost against the rock wall. The original stone above is now the head stone and the modern granite stone is the foot marker. Photo by Wayne Austin, Aug 30, 2003.
