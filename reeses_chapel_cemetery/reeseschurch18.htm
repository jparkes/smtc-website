REESE'S CHURCH, CAMPBELLSVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This photo is looking northwest at the box tombs belonging to the Henderson family. Of the five tall stones the three tall stones on the right (left side of photo) are for the Amis family with the two tower stones on the left being for the Henderson family. Some of the box tombs are not readable due to the inscription being weathered away. Photo by Wayne Austin 8/30/2003.
