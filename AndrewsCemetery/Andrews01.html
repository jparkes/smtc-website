ANDREWS CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


ANDREWS, James W., 2 Feb 1866 - 25 Apr 1885. "Son J. P. & H. A. Andrews"; (Drowned at age 19.)  
 This memorial is an example of what can be accomplished when taking an old broken, weathered & worn stone and integrating it into a modern granite memorial. The white part is the original stone.  Faye's closer image shows more detail on this great way of restoring old tombstones. Notice even the once lost inscription was restored across the breakage.
Photo and information by Wayne Austin 27 Dec 2006. 2nd photo provided by Faye Bradford, 26 Mar 2010.
