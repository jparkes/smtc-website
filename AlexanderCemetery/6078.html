ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This old broken stone I dug up and photographed must have been an old original stone that broke and was replaced in the 1800s by the family of John McMeen for him. It has his name John McMeen and other readable items including the "19th of Feb" which is his birth date without the year and a part of the death date which is "and died _ _ _ 16th"

John McMeen.

McMEEN, John, 19 Feb 1780 - 16 Aug 1838. (Aged 58 yrs.)
