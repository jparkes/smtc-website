ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This view is of the oldest stone in this cemetery and indeed maybe in Maury County. That is the Eleazer Alexander 1763 - 1810 memorial in the center against the wall.

This cropped photo looks due north from a different place in the cemetery.

This view is another north view whereby one can see all the open space in this cemetery. It is hard to tell what part of that is occupied by unmarked graves.

This view looks northwest from a wide angle view at most of the stones. Most are too small to make out the detail from this view.
