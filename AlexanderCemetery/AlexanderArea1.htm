ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

As can be seen from this photo this is a very old cemetery with stones from the early to late 1800s. Today it is well kept. However about all of these stones are limestone which is quite soft and are in sever danger of crumbling with age. Therefore move around this cemetery carefully.

What a gorgeous & historical old cemetery. This photo was made from just east & back of the one above.

We are looking at the Boyd, Campbell, Zellner and many other memorials.

One can see the more modern style granite stones in the distance which are the more recent ones here.
Photos by Michelle Batte, July 31, 2004.
