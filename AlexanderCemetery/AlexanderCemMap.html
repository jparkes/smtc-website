ALEXANDER CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Use this map to find the GPS coordinates to the Alexander Cemetery

This Road map shows the place to find the cemetery on Beechcroft Road 2+ miles west of Spring Hill Tennessee.

This map shows the cemetery under a tree lined perimeter enclosed in a rock wall on all four sides.

From Spring Hill, go west on Beech Croft Road for three miles. The cemetery is on the (right) north side of road, well kept, and with a rock wall around it.
