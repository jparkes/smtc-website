ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


ALDERSON, William H., 14 Jun 1842 - 17 Aug 1872. Inscription: WILLIAM H. ALDERSON, Born June 14, 1842 Died Aug 17, 1872." This stone is crumbling badly and in a few months or years it will no longer by readable. Located in the eastern center of the cemetery. It is straight out about 50 feet in front of the white Abdon I Alexander memorial which is against the back northern wall in case it cannot be read in the future.
