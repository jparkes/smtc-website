ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Hardison Memorial. Seth, T. J & Carrie.


HARDISON, T. J., (3 Jun) 1856 -(31 Jan) 1932. (Oldest son of Richard Bates & Nancy Catherine Sowell Hardison; studied medicine at Louisville University, graduated from the University of Nashville; practicing medicine for 50 years in Maury Co; mar. Carolyn Huntley "Carrie" Alexander, dau. of John C. and Mary Sparkman Alexander on 28 Oct 1880. updated Ref: Rick Gray.)
HARDISON, Carolyn Huntly Alexander (Carrie), (9 Mar) 1858 - (28 Jul) 1949. (Wife Dr. T. J. Hardison; daughter of John C. & Mary Sparkman Alexander.)
HARDISON, Seth, 1892 - 1899. (Son T. J. & Carolyn H. Hardison)
