ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This cemetery is also known as the BEECH GROVE Cemetery, not to be confused with the historic old community 3 miles east of Columbia.
Directions: From Spring Hill, go west on Beechcroft Road for three miles. Pass several historic (but private) homes. The cemetery is on the north side of the road, well kept, and with a rock wall around it.
