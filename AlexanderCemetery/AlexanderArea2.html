ALEXANDER CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This view to the northeast of the cemetery shows the historic nature of this place. (Private Property please)

This looks at the Anderson & Burnett Lots, a part of the newer sections. That is Beechcroft road on our left.

This view pans to the southwest more and looks at the Anderson & Burnett Lots, a part of the newer sections. The Sparkman lots can be seen farther out closer to the distant trees

This view looks northeast at the old sections and the Wilson & Jameson lots in the distance out closer to the trees. I can't emphasize too much the historic nature and age of this cemetery. The earliest stone is dated 1810 with the same individual being born in 1763. The styles of the memorials reflect that age.
