GILMER CEMETERY, SOUTHPORT, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

P 239

Joseph Gilmer
Last will & Testament
1 Dec 1862

I Joseph Gilmer of Maury County and State of Tennessee, do make this my last will & testament that is to say.

First of all after all of my just debts are paid, I give and bequeath to my daughter Euphence Coffee One hundred dollars left over to hands of my executor to be paid over when she has the right of using it herself. And also to the too daughters of Adaline Williams, Martha F. Williams and Melvino Williams his interest to be one hundred dollars to bee divided equally between them when they become of age.

Two notes on Jeremiah Gilmer for $ 481.91 cts. Two notes on Tininas? Pullian for $ 58.41 cts. Three other notes for $45 dollars. $85 in cash.  Also one note of hand on J. B. Stockard for $133.55. If any property remaining at the death of the testator the remainder to be equally divided among the heirs of said Gilmer.     

                        Willed and bequeathed ratifyde  and confirmed this and no other to bee his last will and testament.   I hereby appoint Joel B. Stockard sole executor of this my last will and testament. Hereby Scoohing all former wills by me as paid.   In witness hereof I have here unto set my hand and seal. This 7th of April in the year of our Lord Eighteen hundred and fifty eight.

                                                                                                                        His

                                                                                                           Joseph Gilmer             [Seal]

                                                                                                                        Mark
