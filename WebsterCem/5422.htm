WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


WEBSTER, Sarah M., 26 Mar 1877 - 11 Dec 1878. 2nd headstone to Sarah/Sadie Mays Webster, same dates. (daughter of Felix J. & Martha Louise Foushee Webster.) This is the footstone. Her inscription is also on the large Felix J Webster memorial. The birth date is slightly different 20th of March.
