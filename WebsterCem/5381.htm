WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Jonathan Webster box tomb in the immediate front, then Sarah Webster then son John G. Webster.

3rd of the three box tombs in the center of the photo. The third one is not visible. It is hidden the other side of the second one o your right under the cherry bush in the left of the photo.

WEBSTER, Jonathan, 17 Nov 1767 - 25 Apr 1845. "Revolutionary War Soldier." (Box tomb)
Inscription: " SACRED  -- To the memory of -- JONATHAN WEBSTER -- who was born Nov 17th 1767 and -- died April 23rd 1845 in the close -- of a long life he left to a numerous family a name associated with -- the noblest virtues of the human heart to this  _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ "
