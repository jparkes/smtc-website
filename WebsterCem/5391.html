WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Jossey family memorials lined up north (our right to left) to south James (father) Wm. Walker (son) and Nancy Coleman (mother & wife)


JOSSEY, James, 23 Nov 1786 - 12 Dec 1831. 45 yrs. "Native of Georgia." (Lt. in War of 1812; son Henry & Mary Hill Jossey; married Nancy Coleman who is buried here across from him past a son Wm.)
