FOWLER, Halle Webster, 16 Mar 1928 - (2 Jun. 2013)  (wife of James Sterchi Webster) obit
 
Published in The Tennessean on June 4, 2013 

FOWLER, Halle Webster Passed away on the 2nd of June, 2013. 

She is preceded in death by her parents, Corinne White and Robert Webster; her husband, James Fowler and daughter, Corinne Fowler. 

Halle is survived by her daughter, Happy, and son, Jim; her grandchildren, Halle Hayes McLauchlan, Michael Hayes, James and Landon Fowler, and great-grandchildren, Michael and Carson Hayes, and Ella McLauchlan. 

Halle was a lifelong member of Cheekwood and the Junior League of Nashville. She was also on the original Board of Trustees of Ensworth School, a member of long standing of the National Society of The Colonial Dames of America in the state of Tennessee, and of the Stoneycrest Garden Club. Halle was a devoted wife, mother and grandmother. She loved deep sea fishing, was an avid gardener and photographer, and treasured her family's farm in White Bluff, TN, as well as the years she and her husband spent living in Scottsdale, AZ. 

The family would like to thank her caregivers: Dee Dee Herrman, Jean Brady, Judy Dodds, Arleita Marlowe and Jozella Policastro. The family will receive friends on Wednesday, the 5th of June, 2013 from ten-thirty in the morning until twelve-thirty in the afternoon at Marshall-Donnelly-Combs Funeral Home. Graveside services will commence at two-thirty in the afternoon on Wednesday, at the Webster Cemetery in Cross Bridges, TN. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the Alzheimer's Association , 4205 Hillsboro Rd., Nashville, TN 37215.
