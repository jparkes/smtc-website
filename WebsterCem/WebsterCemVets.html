WEBSTER, Jonathan, 17 Nov 1767 - 25 Apr 1845. "Revolutionary War Soldier." (Box tomb)

War 1812

JOSSEY, James, 23 Nov 1786 - 12 Dec 1831. 45 yrs. "Nat. of Georgia." (Lt., War of 1812; son Henry & Mary Hill Jossey; mar. Nancy Coleman.)

Civil War - C. S. A.

TURNER, Edward, died 1864. Aged 18 yrs, C.S.A.; (Son Wm. H. & Caroline Webster Turner; b. 1845. C.S.A., 19th Biffle's Tn. Cav. Co. H. rank in Sgt. rank out Sgt. film #M231 roll 44. Ref: Civil War Soldier's and Sailors.)

WEBSTER, James J., 1843 - 1918. (Son James H. & Sarah Jane Weakley Webster; Co. H, 1st Tenn Inf., C.S.A. ; rank in Pvt. rank out Corporal; film #M231 roll 46, Civil War Soldiers and Sailors; married Fannie Robinson.)

WEBSTER, William Henry, 1840 - 1863. (Son James H. & Sarah Jane Weakley Webster; Co. H., 1st Tenn. Inf., C.S.A.; killed Chickamauga, body returned.)

WEBSTER, Andrew Blair, 9 Oct 1844 - 21 May 1922. "Brother." (Son George Pope & Harriet Blair Webster; Co.. H, 1st Tenn Inf., C.S.A., rank in Pvt. rank out Pvt. film #M231 roll 46. Ref: Civil War Soldiers U Sailors; never married.)

WEBSTER, Henry J., 17 May 1838 - 8 Feb 1865. "Brother." (Son of George Pope & Harriet Blair Webster; C.S.A. 1st. Reg. Field's Tn. Inf. Co. H., rank in 1st Sgt. rank out Capt. film #M231 roll 46, Ref: Civil War Soldiers & Sailors.)

WEBSTER, Felix Jonathan, 18 Oct 1845 - 15 Jan 1899. (Son Felix Grundy & Martha Louise Fouche Webster; Co. H., 1st Tenn. Inf., C.S.A.. Widow's Pension #W9226 by Martha Louise Webster; Ref: Tn. State Library & Archives.)
