WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Jonathan Webster box tomb in the immediate front, then Sarah Webster then son John G. Webster.

2nd of the three box tombs in the center of the photo. The third one is not visible. It is hidden the other side of the second one under the cherry bush in the left of the photo.

WEBSTER, Sarah, 18 Sep 1763 - 22 Jul 1830. "Consort of Jonathan Webster." (Nee: Jossey; Box tomb, inscription very difficult to read.) Inscription: " COMMEMORATIVE OF -- SARAH WEBSTER -- Consort of Jonathan Webster -- She was born the 18th of Sept 1763 and -- Died 22nd July 1830 -- In the 59th year of her age. -- In the life of the deceased who exhibited a bright example of all -- those a level which adorn and decorate the female character _ _ _ _ _ "
