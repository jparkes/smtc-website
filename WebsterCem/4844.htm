WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This monument stands in the southwest corner of the cemetery. Open spaces are all around which may indicate burials without headstones.
Located under the trees in the western area of the Webster Cemetery this memorial pays tribute to those providing much of the labor required prior to 1861.

MONUMENT ON THE SOUTHWESTERN SIDE: "Sacred to the Memory of the Slaves of the Websters."
Memorial set in place prior to 1987.
