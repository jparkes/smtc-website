WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

West side footstones with initials of the sibling & parental relationships. This inscription on the south side of this massive monument.

WEBSTER, Andrew Blair, 9 Oct 1844 - 21 May 1922. "Brother." (Son George Pope & Harriet Blair Webster; Co.. H, 1st Tenn Inf., C.S.A.; never married)
WEBSTER. Carrie Turner, 10 Aug 1842 - 19 Dec 1928. "Sister." (daughter of George Pope & Harriet Blair Webster, never married)
