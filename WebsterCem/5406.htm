WEBSTER CEMETERY, HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

West side footstones with initials of the sibling & parental relationships. This inscription on the north side of this massive monument.

WEBSTER, Henry J., 17 May 1838 - 8 Feb 1865. "Brother." (Son of George Pope & Harriet Blair Webster; C.S.A.)
WEBSTER, Harriet Walker, 22 May 1850 - 1 Oct 1851. "Sister" (Dau. of George Pope & H. Webster.)
WEBSTER, Ellen Pope, 26 Dec 1853 - 21 Mar 1885. "Sister." (Dau. of George Pope & H. Webster.)
