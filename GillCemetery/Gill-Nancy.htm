GILL, Nancy, 3 Feb 1771 - 20 Dec 1815. (44-09-27.)
Inscription Reads: "IN memory of NANCY GILL born February 3rd 1771 & died December 20th 1815. aged 44 years 9 months & 27 days." (Oldest inscribed tombstone yet found in this area of Maury County that I know of. The memorial has been fallen & broken for many years.)
