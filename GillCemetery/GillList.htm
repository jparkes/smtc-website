GILL CEMETERY, CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

In Campbell's Station take Pullen Mill Road north for 1/2 mile. The cemetery is on the crest of a hill before continuation of the climb on up to the top of the big hill. This is above what was at one time the old Gill Home place on the right side of the road. The cemetery is inside a new decorative white wooden picket fence.
