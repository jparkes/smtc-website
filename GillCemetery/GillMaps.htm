GILL CEMETERY, CAMPBELL STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 In Campbell's Station take the Pullen Mill Road north for 1/2 mile. The cemetery is on the crest of a hill in the open field in the livestock area 350 yards east from the home. The cemetery is inside a new decorative white wooden picket fence.
Map adapted from MS Terra site. Information by Wayne Austin 11/30/2007.
