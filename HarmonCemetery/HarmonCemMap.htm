HARMON CEMETERY, Joe Peay Rd, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This Aerial view map shows the the cemetery as placed by GPS Longitude and Latitude coordinates as: N 35.70009 X W 86.87668. That is marked by a white check in the center of the map.

We have placed a yellow pointer on the above map to indicate where we can find this cemetery. On this map the cemetery was still under the trees, but has since been cleared. Notice we are east about 3/4 of a mile from I-65. Joe Peay Road runs parallel with Interstate 65 and spans eastward out toward the cemetery and community.
