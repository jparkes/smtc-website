HARMON CEMETERY, Joe Peay Rd, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Go east on Kedron Road, turn left just after passing over the interstate onto Joe Peay Road. Go 1/2 mile and turn right into the woods on a gravel road. The cemetery is about 1/2 mile further, on the Emory Harmon farm.

This is a beautiful old cemetery that someone is doing a great job of maintaining. There were artificial flowers on some graves and when we visited in March 2010 there were jonquils and wild violets blooming. A very peaceful setting. [Faye Bradford, 25 Mar 2010.]
