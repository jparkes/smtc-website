HARVEY CEMETERY,  KEDRON RD., MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


From Kedron Rd., turn north onto Ollie Chunn Road. Go 1/2 mile & turn left into a small road that goes to several houses and by the cemetery. Keep left and head up the hill. The cemetery is on right of this lane about 1/4 mile up hill. This is a fenced and gated cemetery, you will be accepted here only if your intentions are honorable.
