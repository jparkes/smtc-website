MAGUIRE CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the cemetery behind Hope Clinic (2009) inside a fallen rock wall accessed by going behind the Clinic on the circular road. The green label os slightly off center to the south. It is at GPS: North 35.62579 X West -87.04212.
Topographical map showing the Maguire Cemetery just on the southwest edge of Hope Clinic. At one time this was the Bel Air Nursing Home.
Here we can see the Hope Clinic and cemetery with the yellow arrow pointing at it located in the trees and sandwiched between a business on the southeast, the clinic on the northeast, and residential houses on the west. All this is just off of James M. Campbell Blvd.
