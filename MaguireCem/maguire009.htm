MAGUIRE CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Ellen Leonard memorial. This is the best preserved memorial in the cemetery.

Footstone for Eleanor Leonard. "E. L."

"SACRED to the memory of ELLEN LEONARD, a native of County Fermanagh Ireland. Emigrated to America in March 1841 & died 14 Dec 1841."

LEONARD, Ellen, "Native of the County Fermanagh, Ireland. Emigrated to America in March, 1841 and died on 14 Dec 1841." (niece of Patrick Maguire)
