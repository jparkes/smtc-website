MAGUIRE CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Another part of the Martha Maguire memorial that shows the death year of 1858 on the upper tip of the broken and pointed piece. 

"MARTHA, WIFE OF P. MAGUIRE, BORN MAY 1796, DIED OCT 4, 1858"

MAGUIRE, Martha (Kavanagh), May 1796 - 4(or 9) Oct 1858. "Wife of P. (Patrick) Maguire." (box tomb top broken into many parts.)
