MAGUIRE CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mary Ann Looney box tomb.

"I am the resurrection and the life: he that believeth in me thugh he were dead, yet shall he live:" John 11.25."

LOONEY, Mary Ann, d. 17 Jul 1840. (24-09-17.) "Consort of David Looney. A kind friend, an affectionate wife and mother."
Inscription: "SACRED to the memory of MARY ANN consort of DAVID LOONEY. A kind friend an affectionate wife and mother and more than all a true Christian she lived by faith in the Son of God and slept in Jesus, July 17th 1840 aged 24 years. 9 months & 17 days."
This inscription had to be cut out of the bushes for the photograph.
