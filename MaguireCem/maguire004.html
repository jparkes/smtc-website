MAGUIRE CEMETERY, COLUMBIA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Martha J. Maguire, fallen boxtomb side.

MAGUIRE, Martha J. (Jane), daughter of Patrick & Martha Maguire, 1 Sep 1819 - 24 Oct 1836, age 17 yrs., 1 mo., and 13 days.
SACRED to the memory of MARTHA J. MAGUIRE, Daughter of PATRICK & MARTHA MAGUIRE Born 11th Sept 1819, and died 24th Octbr 24 1836 Aged 17 yrs 1 mo & 13 days. Much lamented by her relations & associates."
