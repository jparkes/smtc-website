RIEVES CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 
 This cemetery was contributed to this site by Al Studtman. According to the maps and Mr. Hawkins you can find this cemetery from Columbia by taking the Sowell Mill Pike over the bridge. After passing Union Grove turn right on Reaves Bend Road and go until it bends to the right 90 degrees. Cemetery is on the left about 200 yards after the bend in the road.  LOCATOR MAP
