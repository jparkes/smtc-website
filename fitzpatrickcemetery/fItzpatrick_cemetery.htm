Fitzpatrick Cemetery may be found by taking the Lewisburg Highway from Culleoka for 1/2 mile. The cemetery is on your right up the slope in a grove of trees about 100 yards behind two nice new brick homes on private land. It is enclosed by a rock wall that is weak in a few places. One of the surrounding landowners have set a wire fence over the rock wall in the low places to prevent livestock from entering the cemetery. They are to be commended for the civic minded efforts.
