FITZPATRICK (Morgan) CEMETERY, LEWISBURG HWY., CULLEOKA, TENNESSEE

FITZPATRICK, Morgan, d. 11 Aug 1860, in 78th year. (b. cir 1782) "Morgan Fitzpatrick died August 11th 1860 in the 78th year of his age." The footstone is behind the grave and is still standing.
