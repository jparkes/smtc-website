POLK LANE CEMETERY (African American) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This is the name I used. I am unaware of the correct name
This map shows the accurate GPS based on the Actual Imagery of Google mapping. It is found at: N 35.55858 X W 87.14365.

To find this cemetery go west on Trotwood Ave (Old Hwy 43 - now 243) and turn (left) south on Polk Lane going past the St Johns Church & Cemetery on left. Go about 2/3 a mile and look to your right on the bank of the road at the stones of this cemetery.

This Birds-Eye View image from Bing Mapping shows the cemetery under a Cedar Tree on the edge of (the west side) Polk Lane. It is being kept free of brush.
