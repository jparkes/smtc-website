WELLS (Thomas) CEMETERY, (Port Royal Road, Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The row in light color is the subject now. There are five stones there, three upright and two flat style memorials. The large stone on the right is actually the footstone for Jack Wells. Our first focus is on the 2nd of three round top stone nearest us.

The Miss Sophia C. Wells memorial.

WELLS, Miss Sophia C., 20 Aug 1873 - 6 Jun 1935. (Single; daughter of James & Sarah Wells.)
