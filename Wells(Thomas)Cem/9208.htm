WELLS (Thomas) CEMETERY, (Port Royal Road, Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Mary Wells memorial next to her husband Thomas close to the fence.

WELLS, Mary, 26 Nov 1804 - 28 Mar 1863. (58-4-2; wife of Thomas Wells)
Inscribed as follows: "IN memory of MARY WELLS, was born Nov 26 1804 And Died March The 28th 1863, Aged 58 years 4 months & 2 Days."
