WELLS (Thomas) CEMETERY, (Port Royal Road, Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Amanda J. Wells memorial next to her husband William J. Wells.

WELLS, Amanda J., 12 Oct 1833 - 10 Sep 1902. Mother (69-10-28.) (nee Amanda J. McCracken; wife of William Jasper Wells.)
Inscribed as follows: "Our Mother Amanda J. WELLS, BORN Oct 12.1833 Died Sep 10 1902. Age 69 years 10 Mo & 8 dys. The Golden Gates were open. A gentle voice said come and with farewells unspoken she calmly entered heaven.)
