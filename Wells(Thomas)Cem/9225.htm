WELLS (Thomas) CEMETERY, (Port Royal Road, Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The row in light color is the subject now. There are five stones there, three upright and two flat style memorials. The large stone on the right is actually the footstone for Jack Wells. Our focus is on the 1st of two flat stones on the end of this row.

WELLS, Whithorne, 1879 - 1945. (Son of James & Sarah Wells; father of John Wells who aided Mr. Fred Hawkins (1987) & Marise Lightfoot & J. C. Parrish (1963) in the listing of this cemetery.)
