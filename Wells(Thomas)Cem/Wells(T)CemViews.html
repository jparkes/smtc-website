WELLS (Thomas) CEMETERY, (Port Royal Road, Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This grafted photo from three smaller one shows the entire cemetery in one view. Our view is toward the south across the cemetery and also at the backs of a few houses fronting Queens Place St. No access from there to this cemetery. Notice the gate in the northwest corner. 

Standing inside the cemetery and looking north this is the view one will get looking at the Somerset Springs Drive. This gives one a perspective of how to find this cemetery, but may change.

Photo and info by Wayne Austin 24 Feb 2009. Added here 13 Jul 2009.
