ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

WADE, Mary Jane Caperton, 1859 - 1914. "Wife John F. Wade.", Source: They Passed This Way, Vol. 2, page 362; from newspaper dated Thursday, April 2, 1914) Mrs. John F. Wade, died at Spring Hill Monday night. She was Miss Mollie Caperton, who in her girlhood days spent much time in Columbia; one dau., Mrs. John Thompson, Jr., of Nashville survives. She was a sister of Admiral Wm. Banks Caperton of the U.S. Navy; funeral at Spring Hill; interment at Rose Hill, where she was laid to rest beside her brother S. J. Caperton and other relatives.
