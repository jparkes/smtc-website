Dear Ms. Bradford,

I was disappointed when I looked at the Rose Hill Cemetery entries. I did not see a listing for David WITHERSPOON (4 April 1789-1 June 1876) or his wife Penelope (Gee) WITHERSPOON (27 June 1792-27 Nov. 1863. As I recollect, the graves were toward the top of an incline. Of course, this was quite a long time ago. I have pictures of both stones. His has a curved top; hers is rectangular and straight across the top.
The top of the incline (Slope) is probably Section "M"  [WA 10/26/2009]

Information by Betty Moore 25 Oct 2009 as she conversed with Faye Bradford 25 Oct 2009. Added here 26 Oct 2009. T
