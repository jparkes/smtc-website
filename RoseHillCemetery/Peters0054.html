ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

PETERS, Robbie N. (or Bobbi N.), 27 Mar 1886 - 28 Feb 1901. (Stone tumbled flat on ground.), Block "B"

Peters, Robbie N.

Mrs. Mack Peters died Wednesday afternoon at 5 o'clock at the home of her brother, Mr. E. B. Gilbert on South High street, after an illness of long duration. Deceased was thirty-five years of years of age, and had lived in Columbia for several months, having come here from Nashville. The funeral services were conducted at the residence yesterday afternoon at 2 o'clock by Elder J. Paul Slayden, and the remains were interred at Rose Hill. -- Columbia Herald, 1 Mar. 1901.
