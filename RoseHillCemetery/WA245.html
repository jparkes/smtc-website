Isabelle Vaughan Walker memorial
WALKER, John Beeler, 14 May 1903 - 10 Mar 1970. "Block E"
WALKER, Isabelle, 22 Jun 1900 - 7 Jan 1994, (Wife of John Beeler Walker) Block "E"
Information by Mary Belle Cross Duvivier, a native of Columbia, resident of New York, Daiyro5662@frontiernet.net, 26 Mar 2008. Any questions may be directed to her. Mary Belle is the Granddaughter of Isabelle & John Beeler Walker. Isabelle is the second daughter of Robert Lee Vaughan and Clarissa Lipscomb Vaughan. The oldest child was her twin sister Theo Erwin Vaughan Cathey.
