Columbia (TN) Herald, Monday, August 15, 1977


Farris Roy "Son" Byrd, a resident of Whatley Street, died Saturday at the Maury County Hospital following an illness of several months.
A native of Madison County, Ky., he was the son of the late Burn and Ida Byrd and a veteran of World War II.
Funeral services were conducted today at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home. Burial was in Rose Hill Cemetery.
Survivors include his wife (Flossie Woodrow Kirkland), daughter Mrs. Barbara Ann Driver, Ringgold, GA, and two grandchildren. Tina and Mike Cobb, both of Texas.
Active pallbearers were Douglas Oliver, Jessie Perry, Kenneth Brown, Glynn Harris, Johnnie Wynn, Otis Jernigan, Gary Dungy, Walter Fitzgerald and O.J. Ingram.
