ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

STAGGS, Hattie M., 1907 - 1981. Block "U"

Mrs. Hattie Mae Fralix Staggs, 74, of 206 N. Campbell Blvd., died early Saturday morning at Bel Air Nursing Center following an extended illness. The body is at Williams Funeral Home, where services will be conducted in the chapel at 11 a.m. Monday. Rev. Don Smith, pastor of Emmanuel Baptist Church, will officiate. Burial will be in Rose Hill Cemetery. A native of Cullman, Ala., she was the daughter of the late Bob and Maggie Fralix. She was a housewife and member of Emmanuel Baptist Church. Survivors include a son, William Staggs, Columbia; a sister, Mrs. Maggie Sue Tibbs, Columbia; two grandchildren; and eight great-grandchildren. Pallbearers will be William Jewell, Loyd Dugger, Terry Lindsey, Jimmy Southall, Charles Myrack and Billy Joe Anglin.
The Daily Herald, Columbia, TN, Sun., 20 Sep 1981. Submitted by Judy B. Forgos 14 Aug 2009
