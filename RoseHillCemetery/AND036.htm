ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

 ANDERSON, Daisy A. Scott, 21 Jan 1909 - (27 Oct 1993). (wife of Arthur Simpson Anderson) Block "U"
Mrs. Arthur S. Anderson
Mrs. Daisy Scott Anderson, 85, widow of Arthur S. Anderson and a former resident of Webster Street in Columbia, died Thursday, Oct 27, 1994, at Clarksville Manor Nursing Home in Clarksville, Tn.
Funeral services will be 1 p.m., Saturday at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home with the Rev. Bob Vassar officiating. Burial will be in Rose Hill Cemetery in Columbia.
Visitation will be 4 - 8 p.m., Friday at the funeral home.
The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Alexander and Martha Runions Scott. She was a member of the Baptist Church. Survivors include one son, Robert Anderson, of Clarksville and several grandchildren.  

 Obituary copied from microfile of The Daily Herald, Columbia, Tennessee published Friday, October 28, 1994. Microfile is housed at the Maury County Archives, Columbia, Tennessee 38401.
