JOHNSON, A.G., (dates unknown; C.S.A., Cross of Honor NOTE: Cross in front of the tombstone of his wife, E.O. Hortense, with the inscription "Wife A.G. Johnson"; there is a Soldier's Pension #S11050 for A.J. Johnson-Giles Co. but no Widow's Pension.) It is unknown if this cross of honor belongs to A.G. Johnson or if there is a grave here for him.
