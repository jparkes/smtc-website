ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

VAUGHT, Nathan, 13 Aug 1799 - 9 Apr 1880. "Master Builder of Maury County. A founder of Rose Hill Cemetery, dedicated Mason, devoted Methodist, Confederate Patriot, he built more fine houses than any man ever in Maury County. Married Lucretia J. Journey and Sarah H. Dale.' --Erected 1867 by Maury Co. Chap., Block "P"
