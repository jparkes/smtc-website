ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
GREEN, Florence Douglas, 1886 - (23 Aug) 1952. (wife of W. M. Green, Dau of George & Sally McKissick Douglas) Block "Q"

Mrs. Florence Douglas Green, 66, widow of W. M. Green, Sr., died unexpectedly at 9 a. m. today at her home on Galloway St. A native of Maury County, she was a daughter of the late George and Sally McKissick Douglas and a member of the Church of Christ. Funeral services will be conducted at 2:30 p.m. Sunday at the Highland Avenue Church of Christ by Elder Paul Matthews. The body will be Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home until time for services. Survivors include a daughter, Mrs. Claude Gillespie of Columbia; four sons, William, Houston and Lindsey Green of Columbia and Virgil Green of Oak Ridge; a sister, Mrs. Charlie Douglas of Enterprise; three brothers, Hugh Douglas of Mt. Pleasant and John and George Douglas of Enterprise; 7 grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. Pallbearers will be George Fowler, James, Hugh and Neil Douglas, Gardner Hubbell, Granberry Hickman, Harlan Harmon and Herman Fox. Burial will be in Rose Hill. Oakes & Nichols in charge of arrangements.
Columbia Daily Herald, Saturday, Aug. 23, 1952.

Obituary compiled and submitted by Judy Forgos, 9 Sep 2009.
