ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
CRUTCHER, Tennessee K. McConnico, (1829 - Mar 21, 1900) No marker reported. (d/o Garner G. & Catherine Kinnard McConnico; w/o James Patterson Crutcher.) obit

Daughter of Garner G. and Catherine Kinnard McConnico. Married James Patterson Crutcher 1 July 1867, Maury Co., TN.

Mrs. Tennessee McConnico Crutcher died Wednesday afternoon at 3 o'clock, at the home of her son-in-law, Mr. O.O. Wellborn, on Galloway street. Deceased had lived to a ripe old age, being in her 71st year at the time of her death. The funeral services were conducted at the residence yesterday afternoon at 3 o'clock by Rev. J.F. Beasley, assisted by Rev. W.T. Ussery, and the interment was in Rose Hill Cemetery. -- Columbia Herald, 23 Mar 1900.\
