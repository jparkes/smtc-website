ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

 HACKNEY, Nettie, d. 20 Mar 1873. (7 years.)(Same stone with: Fredonia E. Carrington.) Block "L"

The little daughter of Mrs. Ann Hackney fell down the star steps recently and received injuries so serious a nature as to cause death. The entire community sympathize with the heartbroken mother in her sad bereavement. -- Columbia Herald, March 21, 1873.
Compiled by Mary Bob McClain Richardson. 6 Oct 2015.
