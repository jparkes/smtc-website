ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
Margaret Elizabeth Frierson on ancestry.com originally submitted this to Melton Family Tree on 18 Sep 2010. On the back of this photo is written "grandmother Engle". C. S. Judd was a portrait photographer in Columbia, Tennessee, starting around 1883. Based on the age of the subject and the notation of "Grandmother Engle", I think this is a portrait of Margaret, not Laura Engle (who would be a Douglas at this point in her life anyway)."
Added by: Wendy & John Corcoran 21 Jun 2011 & their notations
