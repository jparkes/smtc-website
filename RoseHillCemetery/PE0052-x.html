ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

PEBBLES, Bascom Regen, Sr., d. 12 Jan 2009 in Lebanon, Tn. (Maury Co. native; son Martin Arnold Peebles, Sr. and Katherine Regen "Kitty" Peebles, mar. Alicia Yvonne; Veteran U.S. Army 1963-1965; Outstanding athlete, 1961 grad. of Columbia Central High School, All-Conference Team, All Midstate Team, and All-State second team, elected "King of Football" by Senior Class and #42 retired, an honor only bestowed on 5 players in the history of the school; recruited and played for Coach Paul "Bear" Bryant, played with famous Quarterback Joe Namath for the University of Alabama.)
Information compiled by Mary BoB McClain 4 Feb 2009
