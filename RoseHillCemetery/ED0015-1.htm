ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

EDWARDS, Charles Manuel, 12 Jun 1870 - 9 Jul 1953. DEATH NOTICE: "83, d. 9 Jul 1953; one of organizers of Citizens' Telephone Co. in 1896; native of Maury County; son of C.M.. & Mary Rains Edwards: was manager of Citizen's Tel. Co. until 1926 when it was purchased by the Southern Bell Tel. Co. With son and daughter he operated Edwards Grocery on South High; married Ida Mae Griffin; d. on 8 Dec 1949." (D.H., 10 Jul 1953.)
