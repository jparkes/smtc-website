ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
BATTS, Mary Lou Tidwell, 30 Mar 1884 - 12 Dec 1965. (Obit, not dated: " Mrs. Mary Lou Tidwell Batts, 81, widow of Norman Batts and resident of 1022 Woodman St., died early Sunday morning at the Maury County Hospital following several weeks illness. Funeral service were conducted this morning at 10 a.m. at the Oakes and Nichols Chapel. David Davidson, minister of the West 7th Street Church of Christ, officiated. Burial was in the Rose Hill Cemetery. A native of Arkansas, she was the daughter of the late Thomas and Annie Goad Tidwell......." mar. T. Norman Batts, Sr. 19 Sep 1906.) Ref; obit posted by Linda Batts Howell.
