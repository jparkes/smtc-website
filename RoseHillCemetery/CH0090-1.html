Obituary

Obituary, Shelbyville Gazette, Bedford Co., TN. 2-2-1899.
"Mrs. Sallie Chaffin died early yesterday morn at the home of her brother in law, Epps Parker, in West End (Nashville). Age 62 years. She was the daughter of Col. George W. Cunningham of Bedford County. She originally married Dr. James Kincade of Shelbyville. She married 2nd Green T. Chaffin of Columbia. Funeral in Columbia."
