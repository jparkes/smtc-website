ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

 QUARLES, Lucy J., 8 Jun 1855 - 12 Dec 1877. b. & d. in Clarksville, Tn. "Losing her mother at an early age, she found a home, father, mother and sisters in the household of F.C. Dunnington and at her request was buried among those whose love had brightened the pathway of her life. / Her life was one of love and trust.", Block 'C'
