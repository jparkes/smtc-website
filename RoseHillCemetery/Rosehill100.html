ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

Terry Lane "Turtle" Bratton, 50, employee of General Machine Works and resident of Davis Ave., died 3 March 2008 at Maury Regional Hospital. The Maury County native was the son of Bernice [Brown] Bratton of Columbia and the late Clarence Sam Bratton. In addition to his mother, he is survived by a son, Samuel Bratton of Santa Fe; three brothers, Mike Bratton, Steve Bratton, David (Judy) Bratton all of Columbia; and several nieces and nephews. Terry is buried in Rose Hill Cemetery in Columbia.

Obituary from Newsletter from Turner-Austin tidbits, from the records of Linda Sharp Yates, Carolyn Sharp Hicks & Anne Sharp Cole 31 Oct 2008.
