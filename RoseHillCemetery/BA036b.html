BALBEN, Walter H., d. 13 Feb 2008 in Giles, Co. (Boston, Mass. native; son of Harold W. Balben and Annie Harris Balben; Veteran U.S. Army, WWII, Paratrooper, 101st Airborne, 513th Parachute Regiment, 17th Airborne Div., shot down over Germany and was MIA for one year; mar. 38 yrs. to Linda Faye Peters Balen; preceded in death by daughters, Jeanette Kelsey and Anna Balben, brothers, William, Malcolm, John Weston, Warren, and Chester Balben.)
