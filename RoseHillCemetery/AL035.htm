ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

ALEXANDER, Andrew Jackson, 1844 - 1927. C.S.A. Cross of Honor, Block "A", b. 22 Oct 1844, son of Ebenezer C. Alexander and Lucy Sellers. He first married Fannie Williams, and second, Belle Perry. Each of these marriages produced two children. Mr. Alexander served throughout the Civil War in various units of the Confederate Army; Maury Lt. Artillery; 48th Tenn. Inf. and Co. A., 9th Cav Bat. 
  Information by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, Dec 2005. Updated for photo by Kim Shockey 11 Oct 2009.
