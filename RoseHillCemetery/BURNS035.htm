ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
BURNS, Angie McKee, (16 Nov) 1871 - (6 Sep) 1950. (Accident Victim; d/o Mrs. Paul Hawkins. Ref: Judy Forgos.) Block "R"

 Accident Victim

Funeral services will be held at the West Seventh Street Church of Christ here for Mrs. Angie McKee Burns, 78, of 805 School Street, who was killed almost instantly yesterday morning in the auto accident north of Franklin in which Mr. and Mrs. Ernest Tankersley also met death, and Mrs. Burns' daughter, Mrs. Paul Hawkins of Columbia, was critically injured. Mrs. Burns was born and reared in Columbia and spent her entire lifetime here. She was a member from early life of the Church of Christ. Besides Mrs. Hawkins, she is survived by a son, Jasper E. Burns of Gary, Indiana, a brother, Will C. McKee of Nashville, and a sister, Mrs. D. L. Beasley of Humboldt. She is also survived by her step-daughters, Mrs. Beulah Jones of Nashville, Mrs. Evron Jenkins of Tampa, and Mrs. Aultia Foster, of Alega, Florida, and by a step-son, Herman Burns, of Gary, Indiana. The Williams Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements.
Columbia Daily Herald, 9/7/1950
   
An undated obituary from Columbia Herald by E-mail sent by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 16 Jul 2009 from the files of Judy Forgos.
