ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

Columbia, Tenn., March 31, 1875 - John B. Padgett, for fifteen years County Clerk of Maury county, died at his residence near this city on Saturday, and was buried at Rose Hill Monday morning by the Masonic fraternity. Mr. Padgett was well known by every man in Maury county, and had no enemies. -- Nashville Union and American, April 1, 1875.
