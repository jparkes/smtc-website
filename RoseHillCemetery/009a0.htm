ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

Undated (cir 1986) Obituary for Mamie A. Adams.
"Mamie A. Adams
Mrs. Mamie Alexander Adams, 76, of South High Street, died Monday in Maury County Hospital. Services are Wednesday at 2:30 p.m. in Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home with the Rev. Ezell Rose and Murray Alexander officiating. Burial is in Rose Hill Cemetery. The Maury County native was the widow of Elmore Adams Sr. She was a member of the Baptist Church and was the daughter of the late James and Margaret Davis Alexander. Survivors include five daughters, Mrs. William (Margaret) Wells, Mrs. Carl (Dorothy) Coffman, Mrs. Patricia Roland, Mrs. Linda Rose, all of Columbia, and Mrs. Daniel (Mary) Monroe of Logansport, Ind.; three sons, Elmore Adams Jr. of Columbia, James Adams of Thompson Station and Ray Adams of Williamsport; three sisters, Mrs. Floyd (Christine) Chapman, Mrs. Lester (Gladys) Brown and Mrs. Bob (Aline) Dugger. all of Mt. Pleasant; three brothers, Edward Alexander of Chattanooga, Herbert Alexander and Paul Alexander, both of Nashville; 32 grandchildren; and 26 great- grandchildren. Grandsons will serve as pallbearers."
