James Anderson & Anna Maggie Austin Turner on their wedding day Dec 24, 1905
TURNER, James Anderson, (13 Apr) 1877 - (15 Mar) 1944, (son of Absolom & Amanda Turner), native of Cocke County Tenn., operated a restaurant in Columbia several years)
and
TURNER, Anna Maggie Austin, (27 Apr) 1888 - (25 Apr) 1963. (daughter of James Walter & Sarah Elizabeth Epps Austin, She grew up in Culleoka. Her parents moved to Ulan OK in 1926.) They operated a restaurant in Columbia for several years. Information & photos by Linda Yates, granddaughter.
