ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
BATTS, James Thomas, 1848 - Apr 1912. (Orbit not dated : "After an illness that has been of considerable duration James Thomas Batts died at his home on West Fourteenth street from cancer of the bowels. Mt. Batts was a well known citizen of the city, having been a resident for a number of years. He is survived by his wife and two sons, Thomas and John H., and two brothers. Funeral services at the residence this afternoon, conducted by Rev. W. T. Ussery and Elder W.T. Boaz. Interment at Rose Hill cemetery at 4o'clock. Pall bearers: M.V. Howser, Louis Loeb, Dr. Hayes, John Colvert J. P. Rambo, Whit Hight.) 
Obit posted by Linda Batts Howell. Sent here by MB Richardson, 2009
