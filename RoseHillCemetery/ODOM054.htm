ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

ROBBIE ODOM

Charles Robert "Robbie" Odom III, 10, fourth grade student at Baker Elementary School and a resident of the old Williamsport Pike, died Wednesday, June 16, 1993, at Maury Regional Hospital.
Services will be 10 am Saturday at Oakes and Nicholas Funeral Home with the Rev. Telford Tharp and B. O. McKinley officiating. Burial with be in Rose Hill Cemetery.
Visitation will be after 5pm today and after 12 noon Friday at the funeral home.
Memorials may be made to the American Heart Association.
The Maury County Native was the son of Charles Robert "Chuck" and Penelope Rena "Penny" Odom of Columbia. He was a member of the First United Pentecostal Church.
Survivors, in addition to his parents, include one sister, Christie Rena Odom of Columbia; maternal grandparents, Elmo and Margaret Adams of Columbia; paternal grandparents Charles Robert and Louella Scroggins Odom of Columbia; paternal great grandparents, Elmer and Elizabeth Wales Scroggins of Columbia.
Pallbearers will be Loyd Condwell, Mike Jones, Jeff Thompson, Cheyenne McNeese, Joel Roberts and John Stanfill.
Honorary pall bearers will be his uncles and cousins and the 1992-93 Third Grade Class at Baker Elementary School.
