ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

MERIWETHER, Gilmer, 1 Mar 1864 - 3 Dec 1945, Block "G", (On our left) 
and 
MERIWETHER, Hollie Dorset, 11 may 1875 - 11 Nov 1940, Block "G". (On our right)
MERIWETHER, James Hunter 17 Jun 1901 24 Oct 1992, Block "G". (In the middle)
There could be other graves associated with the Dorset - Meriwether Monument as I did not make a complete memorial inventory.
Notice the Rail Road Track in the background. That was the southeast trunk of the Louisville & Nashville Rail Road from Columbia toward Hurricane Switch, Lewisburg and further. Believe it also branched south to Culleoka and further to Decatur Alabama.
