ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

 JACKSON, (Johnnie) Louise Green, 24 May 1905 - 17 Sep 1978. Daily Herald Obituary dated 18 Sep 1978; " Mrs. Louise Green Jackson, 73, wife of Ronald Harris Jackson, died Sunday night at her residence on the Green's Mill Road following an illness of several months. Funeral services will be conducted Tuesday at 1 p.m. at Oaks and Nichols Funeral Home. Rufus Clifford and Gordon Ricketts, ministers of the Church of Christ, will officiate. Burial will be in Rose Hill Cemetery. A native of Maury County, Mrs. Jackson was the daughter of the late Robert M. Green and Lula Scholes Green. She was a member of the Lanton Church of Christ. Survivors in addition to her husband include a daughter, Mrs. Gordon (Katherine) Ricketts of Greeneville, Tenn.; two sons, Robert Harris Jackson Jr. of Tullahoma and Harold L. Jackson of Springrield; a sister, Mrs. Aileen Lovell of Columbia; five grandchildren, Robert Harris jackson Jr., Larry Ricketts, Elliott Jackson, Miss Patricia Jackson, Ronnie Ricketts and Miss Leanne Jackson. Active pallbearers will be Bob Jackson, Larry Ricketts, Elliott Jackson, Ronnie Ricketts, Richard Jackson, Kenneth Jackson, Gene Wright and Jeff Wright. Honorary pallbearers will be the miniisters serving lanton Church of Christ and the men of the congregation.) Ref: Faye Jackson. Block "Q"
