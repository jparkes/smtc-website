ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE
BROWN, John "Major", d. 22 Jun 1858. (82 yrs.) Block "O"
BROWN, Mary Boswell, d. 26 Jul 1849. (61 yrs.). b. in N.C. Wife of John Brown.' Block "O"

John Brown was born 20 Oct 1780 in Wilkes Co., North Carolina and died 25 May 1858 in MAURY Co., Tennessee. He married Mary Basil/Boswell (shown as Boswell in Maury Co Cemeteries book) Gordon 30 Dec 1813 in Wilkes Co., North Carolina His 2nd wife was Sarah (Unknown maiden name). per Nancy Note there is a difference in the tombstone month and day from what Nancy has and we are unaware of the cause. [C Wayne Austin]
