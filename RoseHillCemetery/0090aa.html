Harley Earnest Cluxton

E-mail 9 Jan 2012 from ljsandvik@msn.com - to Mary Bob McClain
Harley Earnest Cluxton born 29 Oct 1885 in Iroquois Co IL was the son of Moses W. Cluxton and his 2nd wife, Ellen Cox Cluxton. Harley died 22 Nov 1971 in Maury Co TN. He married 12 Jan 1911 Ellen Catena. They had 3 children: Areta Faye b 1912 in IN d 1999 in Maury Co TN who married John T Davis, and twin sons Harley Ernest b 1914 in TN d 1987 in Cook Co IL and Horace Hayes b 1914 in TN and died 1991 in NH per SSDI. Harley was a conductor for a railroad until after the birth of the twins when caring for 3 children became too much for his wife while he was away from home. His brother-in-law provided the money and became a silent partner in a shoe repair bus (1971iness which Harley ran until his retirement.


 Ella Mae Catana

Ellen Mae Catena was born 31 Dec 1890 and died 28 Dec 1973. She married Harley Earnest Cluxton 12 Jan 1911. They had 3 children: Areta Faye b 1912 d 1999 who married John T. Davis, and twin boys Harley Ernest b 1914 d 1987, and Horace Hayes b 1914 d 1991.
