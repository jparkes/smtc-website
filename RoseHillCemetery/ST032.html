ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

STAGGS, Mandy Elizabeth Lee, 10 Mar 1872 - 16 Nov 1942. (Wayne Co. native; d/o James & Frances Christian Lee; w/o J. E. Staggs; Ref: obit sent to Mary Bob from karen@oklumber.org.)

Obituary
Mandy Lee Staggs was born 10 Mar 1872 to James & Frances Christian Lee. She died 16 Nov 1942. Funeral services for Mrs. Mandy Elizabeth Staggs, 70, who died at General Hospital in Nashville Monday night following a two weeks' illness, were held yesterday afternoon at the Free Will Baptist Church here. Burial was in Rose Hill Cemetery.

Mrs. Staggs was born and educated in Wayne County, but moved to Columbia shortly after her marriage. About 10 years ago she moved to Nashville with her children. Her husband, J.E. Staggs of Wayne County died two years ago.

She was a member of the Free Will Baptist Church.

Survivors are two daughters, Mrs. Laura Wirsching of Nashville and Mrs. J. E. Roberts of Saginaw, Mich., four sons, Clarence, Hiram and Marion Staggs of Nashville and Lester Staggs of Birmingham, Ala; one brother, George Lee of Columbia; 28 grandchildren and five great grandchildren.
