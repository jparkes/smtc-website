ROSE HILL CEMETERY, COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

GIBSON, Virginia Lorene Henson, 10 May 1908 - 4 Aug 1970. d/o Turner Henson and Sally Gray Henson; w/o Carl E. Gibson; married to him in 1927.

A TRIBUTE
published in the pages of
THE DAILY HERALD
COLUMBIA,TN
AUG 4 1970
MRS CARL GIBSON, Dies, lived on East 9th Street

Mrs. Virgie Lorene Henson Gibson, a resident of 309 East Ninth Street, died early Tuesday morning at the Maury County Hospital following an extended illness.
Mrs. Gibson's body is at Oakes & Nichols where funeral services will be conducted at a time to be announced later. Burial will be in the Rose Hill Cemetery.
A native of Lawrence County, she was the daughter of the late Turner Henson and Sally Gray Henson. In 1927 she married Carl E. Gibson, who died in 1967
Mrs Gibson worked in restaurants most of her life. She was a member of the Church of Christ.
Survivors include 3 daughters, Mrs. Billy (Mary Louise) Batts of Winter Garden, Florida, Mrs. Harland (Ruby) Green of Ecorse, MI and Mrs. Elmore (Margaret) Adams of Columbia; 2 sons, Carl Gibson Jr. of Ecorse, MI and Staff Sgt. Hubert Gibson of Anchorage, Alaska;3 sisters, Miss Berdie Henson, Mae Ella Jones, both of Columbia, and Mrs. Clarence (Lela) Phillips of Ypsilanti, MI; 2 half sisters and 1 half brother; 22 grandchildren; 1 great grandson; and several nieces and nephews.
