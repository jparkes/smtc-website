GRANBERRY CEMETERY,  HAMPSHIRE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Mt. Pleasant, take (Hwy 166) toward Hampshire. Just beyond the By-pass, enter farm lane that goes north go by an old barn and by a turn off to a new house. The cemetery was supposed to be somewhere near that field on the left.  I could not find it in 2008.  It was located on USGS maps in the middle of a green field with no visible sign of a cemetery.

This 2006 map shows a small clump of trees consistent with what Fred Hawkins and the maps show. Today there was only a spot where nothing was growing and it was freshly tilled.
Wayne Austin 11/19/2008.
