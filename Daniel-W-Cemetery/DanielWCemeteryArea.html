DANIEL (W) CEMETERY-MOORESVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

You are looking at a late evening 1/14/2008 photo of the Daniels Cemetery high upon a hill. On our left is the box tombs of Thomas Theobald and the Smiths while the Daniels memorials are scattered in front of us. The more recent Daniels memorials are in front of us further away and almost not visible here. Mr. Kenny has restored this cemetery and found stones that were not previously visible to researchers.

On our left is a better view of he three box tombs. That is box tombs of Thomas Theobald and the Smiths.
