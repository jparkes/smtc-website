DANIEL (W) CEMETERY-MOORESVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


2008 Directions: From Columbia go south on Mooresville Pike to Buck Daniels Road. Turn left there and go about 1/4 mile to the lane (1st turn off right in 2008) of the Kenny place. Travel up that lane to the home and ask for the cemetery. The cemetery is in Mr. & Mrs. Kenny's yard on the hill just behind the house. You must have permission to go there, but they are congenial folks. Mr. Kenney can tell you some of the history of the cemetery.

1987 directions: From Columbia go south on Mooresville Pike to Phinny-Murphy Rd also called Cross Road. Across the Pike is a high hill just south or about 100 feet south of a line drawn straight eastward. The cemetery is on the top of this hill, deep in the woods, on Mrs. Pleas Akins' place You must cross a feeder branch of Hurricane Creek to get there.
