COOKE CEMETERY (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

JOHN O. COOKE, inscription, back of stone: "J. O. Cooke was born in St. Marys Co., Maryland; at age of six months was removed to Culpepper Co., Va. and thence to Greenville Dist., S.C. at nine years of age. Came to Tenn. in 1806, vas married to S. Morgan on 27 Dec 1807. His wife was a
meek, kind, affectionate and devoted Christian, to know her was to love and admire her virtues. These two lived together in great peace and harmony, within one-half mile of this place for more than 60 years. And while their spirits are in paradise their flesh rests here in Hope."

Information presented in Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries. 7 Dec 2008.
