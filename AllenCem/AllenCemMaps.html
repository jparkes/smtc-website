ALLEN CEMETERY, (Double Branch Rd), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Topographical map showing Allen Cemetery just east of double Branch Road on the slope. The main purpose of this map is to give the GPS coordinates.

Road Map showing direction: From Columbia, go north on Hwy. 31; turn right onto Double Branch Rd., just after crossing Rutherford Creek. Go three miles to where road makes a sharp turn to left. Cemetery is about 300 yards further, on right, in a what is now a cow pasture.

Aerial Map. This cemetery is still part of a farming operation along Lee Road. Permission to visit is important.
