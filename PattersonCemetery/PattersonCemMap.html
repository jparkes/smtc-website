PATTERSON CEMETERY, Hwy 31 South, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

1/4 mile south of Hickman Lane on Pulaski Pike. Past Hickman Lane and top the hill. As you descend down other side  you are.25 mile so turn east onto farm lane. Old House is gone but a large barn stands in back. The cemetery is in a grove of trees 50 yards behind (east) the barn on the right next to the farm road. The road leads past the barn. near a few pieces of abandoned farm machinery. 

Patterson Cemetery is 1/4 mile south of Hickman Lane on Pulaski Pike. Go past Hickman Lane and top the hill. As you descend down other side you are .25 mile now so turn east (left) into the farm lane. Old House is gone but a large barn stand in back. The cemetery is in a grove of trees 50 yards behind (east) the barn on the right next to the farm road. The road leads past the barn near a few pieces of abandoned farm machinery. 
Notice above the yellow arrow points to a clump of trees behind a red barn next to the field road. The cemetery is in that clump of trees. Go there only with permission.
