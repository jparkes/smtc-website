PATTERSON CEMETERY, Hwy 31 South, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Just as Mr Fred mentioned in 1988. Near old farm machinery. This stone is actually leaning on the machine behind it.

PATTERSON, Jared, d. 27 Jul 1829 in the 50th year of his age. Very faint inscription reads: SACRED -- To the memory -- of -- Jared Patterson -- Died 27th July -- 1829 in the 50th year -- of his age."
Transcribed and photographed by Wayne Austin, 14 Apr 2008.
