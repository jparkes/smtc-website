PATTERSON CEMETERY, Hwy 31 South, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Locator Map    Area views

1/4 mile south of Hickman Lane on Pulaski Pike. Past Hickman Lane and .25 mile at the top the next hill as you descend down other side turn east onto farm lane. Old House is gone but a large barn stands. The cemetery is in a grove of trees behind the barn on the right next to the farm road. The road leads past the barn. near some abandoned farm machinery where you will find the cemetery. 

PATTERSON, Jared, d. 27 Jul 1829 in the 50th year of his age.
PATTERSON, William B., 5 Feb 1832 - 21 Feb 1833, son of Robert & Matilda Patterson

Published in "Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries", Page 574, by Fred Lee Hawkins, 29 Jan 1988. Not Listed in "They Passed This Way" by Marise Lightfoot, and Evelyn Shackelford, Page D-240, 1964.  Transcribed and photographed by Wayne Austin, 14 Apr 2008.

 Latitude 35 degrees N 32.1392', and Longitude 87 degrees W 2.6174', Delorme Mapping.
