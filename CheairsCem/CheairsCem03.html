CHEAIRS CEMETERY,  (Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The tall stone of the two speckled ones. Our focus is on the one on the left of the two speckled stones nearest to the large tree.
   
Sarah Rush Cheairs.

CHEAIRS, Benjamine F., 16 Apr 1798 - 2 Jul 1824. Aged 26 years 2 months & 16 days.
Inscribed as follows: "SACRED To the memory of BENJAMINE F. CHAIRS who was born the 16th of April 1798. and departed this life the 2nd of July 1824. aged 26 years 2 months & 16 days."
