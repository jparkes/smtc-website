CHEAIRS CEMETERY,  (Spring Hill), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map marks the spot where the Cheairs Cemetery is located with a red check. If the red check reflects the true coordinates then the coordinates have a slight error in there location. The red check is placed (as you can see) just northeast of the cemetery.

 This map with show one the path of the road that spans behind the Rippavilla Plantation home to get to the cemetery. There is an old slave cabin and a restored farm relic area among other things along the path to this cemetery. The home is on south of the lane to the cemetery and just east of Highway 31 (Spring Hill to Columbia Rd.)
This close up aerial view shows the two graveyards here with the road on the west side.
