GREENFIELD BEND CEMETERY, Greenfield Bend, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 
 The above yellow arrow points to the Greenfield Bend Church of Christ Cemetery just east and behind the church facility.

Instructions 1986: Go out Greenfield Bend Road for about 4 miles. This is a new cemetery located behind the old Graham School, now used as the Church of Christ.
 Instructions today 2009: From Williamsport Pike turn left on to Greenfield Bend Road. Go about 3 & 1/2 miles and you will see the church and signage to the cemetery on your right. This is a well maintained cemetery.
