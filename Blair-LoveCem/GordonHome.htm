BLAIR-LOVE CEMETERY, BOOKER FARM RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
known also as the LOVE CEMETERY.

Capt. John Gordon headed up a spy company during the War 1812; John Gordon of Maury County was Captain of the Spies & served under Andrew Jackson in the Indian Wars, his family had a ferry on the Duck River at Natchez Trace on the edge of Maury & Hickman Co, Gordon's old plantation home has been restored and is still standing. (pictured here). One can see the outline across the front of what at one time was a portico.
