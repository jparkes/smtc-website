This cemetery is also called Blair-Love Cemetery. Go out Hwy 412/Hampshire Pike past Cross Bridges and turn right (or north) on Booker Farm Road. Go 2 1/2 miles. After the third sharp (left) bend in the road go another 1/4 mile and look to your left at the cemetery near the road.

 This map shows what you will see as you approach the cemetery.  The cemetery is near the road but before the line of forest up ahead on your left. One can actually see the stones lined up north to south and facing east with this map. There will be a few tall trees lining the road between the road and the cemetery
