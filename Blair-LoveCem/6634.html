BLAIR-LOVE CEMETERY, BOOKER FARM RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
known also as the LOVE CEMETERY.

The little shaft not visible but just this side of the white stone in row #1 the same row as the boxtombs

LOVE, Collins, 12 Dec 1906 - 18 Sep 1910.(Maury Co. native; s/o Lucius Delbrick and Annie Delta Curry Love)

IN MEMORIAM

On September 18, 1910

The angel of death visited the home of Mr. and Mrs. Lucius Love and took for its victim their little son Collins, age 3 years, nine months, and six days. His bright face and sweet, sunny disposition made every one love him who knew him. It seems so sad to part with our dear little darling. But our loss is heaven's eternal gain, for Jesus has said, "Suffer little children to come unto me and forbid them not, for of such is the kingdom of heaven."

Funeral services were conducted by Bro. W.T. Boaz, and he was laid to rest at the family burying ground near Cathey's Creek. Oh! What a sad, sad fate to see the little white casket pass through the gate.
