BLAIR-LOVE CEMETERY, BOOKER FARM RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
known also as the LOVE CEMETERY.

The first stone in the row of box tombs going R to L.: Unknown, Eliza Isom, large skip, Andrew Blair, Hariet Blair. 

Elizabeth Isom and the unknown tomb beside hers.

Unknown possibly the spouse or other relative of Elizabeth Isom. It is the first box tomb you will come to in the cemetery and has no visible inscription. It defies age analysis, but was probably put in place before the more formal stones of the other Blair and Isom families in the 1820s. Being side by side with Elizabeth Isom may mean it belongs to her family. Possibly her spouse.
