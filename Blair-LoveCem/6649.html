BLAIR-LOVE CEMETERY, BOOKER FARM RD, (Western) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


McMINN, Wade Kennedy, 4 Mar 1919 - 14 Feb 1980. 1st Lt US Army WWII. (Nat. of Weakley Co.; son Percy & Fern Kennedy McMinn; 1st Lt., U.S. Army, WWII. Married 3 Jun 1942 to Chloe Love, father to Darius Mark and David Rodney.)
McMINN, Chloe Love, 12 Mar 1915 - 28 Nov 2007 in Columbia, Tn. (Maury Co. native; daughter of Darius E. Love and Evie Sparkman Love; graduate of Hay Long High School; married Wade Kennedy McMinn on 3 Jun 1942)
