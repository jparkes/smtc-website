Christopher Todd memorial. Notice the repaired upper right corner.

LOONEY, James Dick, 23 May 1850 - 2 Jun 1851. "Our little Boy. Affectionately dedicated by his parents, A. M. & Susan K. Looney."
Full Inscription:" To our dear little boy JAMES DICK who was born on the 23rd of May 1850 and died on the 2nd day of June 1851. This monument is affectionately dedicated by his parents A.M. & Susan K Looney."
