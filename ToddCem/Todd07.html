TODD CEMETERY, MOORESVILLE PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE



TODD, James D., d. 22 Nov 1843. "In 21st yr. of his life". Inscription: "This monument erected in memory of James D. Todd who departed this life on 22nd November 1845 in the 21st year of his age. He was respected by all who knew him and lamented by all his relations and friends. He died in hope of a glorious imortality beyond the grave."
