FOUNTAIN CREEK BAPTIST CHURCH CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


RICE, Elder Ebenezer, Mar 1750 - June 1834. My best effort at this weak Inscription: " In Memory of Elder Ebenezer Rice? Born Mar-ch 1750 or 1756 Died Ju-ne 1834 Aged 83 years and 3 months" This stone is not on any cemetery records of Maury County as far as I can tell and the inscription is very weak. Had I known this at the time of the visit I might have read it closer in person. The dates calculate to 84 years and 3 months, but appear to be 73 years & 3 months. The stone might have been dug up in recent years. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 4/4/04.
