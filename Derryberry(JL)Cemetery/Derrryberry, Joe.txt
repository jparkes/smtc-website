Joseph Marshall Derryberry was the eighth child of James L. Derryberry and Sarah Ann Hardison Derryberry.  He was born 17 Sep 1857; died 7 Mar 1951, and married Mattie A. Huckaby.   Mattie b. 12 Mar 1860; d. 31 Jul 1933 was the daughter of John P. Huckaby and Martha M. Williams Huckaby, who were my great great gramdparents on my grandfathers side.    Joe Marshall Derryberry  was a farmer and lived in the Lasea neighborhood.  His original home stood on the same side of the road as the Jones Cemetery, near Interstate #65.  The house has been torn down now.   This couple is buried in the J. L Derryberry Cemetery as are his parents.  He and Mattie had one son Archie L. Derryberry. Born 26 Mar 1884; d. 23 Aug 1937; by his own hand.  He married Lillian Otis Lane (or Lain).  She, b. 15 Jul 1880; d 19 May 1962; daughter of William and Geneva Frances Davis Lane (daughter of Will Davis and Sallie Evans Jones)who were my great great grandparents on my grandmothers side.   Archie is buried in the Chas. Hardison Cemetery and Lillian in the Jones Cemetery.   

Written by Faye Huckaby Bradford
