DERRYBERRY (J.L.) CEMETERY, NEAR I-65, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This shows the cemetery just up on the right bank of the I-65 about 2/3 of a mile north of the intersection of I-65 & Hwy 99. Intersection not quite visible in the bottom of this map.
F. L. Hawkins directions: From Hwy. 99, just west of 1-65, take New Lasea Rd. Turn east on Lee Rd., cross Interstate, take sharp right turn and go 1 mile. Cemetery is on left at end of dirt road.
W. Austin directions: I visited the cemetery from I-65 after pulling far off the shoulder on the right. It is just upon the embankment on the right (going north) about 1/2 mile north of the junction of Hwy 99 and I-65.
 Photo & Information by Wayne Austin 11/29/2005.
