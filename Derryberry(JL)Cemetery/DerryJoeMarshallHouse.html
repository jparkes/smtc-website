DERRYBERRY (J.L.) CEMETERY, NEAR I-65, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Joe Derryberry home was taken sometime after it was abandoned in the 1960s.

This photo of the Joe Derryberry home was taken 2 Sep 2007 when it was still barely standing. It is now abolished. This House stood just east of Hwy 99 from Jones Cemetery. Judging from the chimneys placement and style it may go back as far as the good economic times of the 1890s if not earlier.
Photo by Wayne Austin 2 Sep 2007. Information condensed from the research of Faye Bradford .
