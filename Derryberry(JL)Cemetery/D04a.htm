DERRYBERRY, M. A. (Martha A "Mattie"), 12 Mar 1860 - 31 Jul 1933. (Wife of Joe Marshall Derryberry; daughter of John P. Huckaby and Martha M. Williams)

M. A. (Martha A "Mattie") Derryberry is listed on your site as the wife of William J. According to my history findings and the Hardison and Allied Families Book (Page 445) she is the wife of Joe Marshall Derryberry listed in that same cemetery and her name was actually Martha A "Mattie" daughter of John P. Huckaby and Martha M. Williams page 587 and 588 of the Hardison book. [Faye Bradford 5 Nov 2009]
