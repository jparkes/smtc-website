DERRYBERRY (J.L.) CEMETERY, NEAR I-65, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
DERRYBERRY, Jas(James) L.(Liggett), 29 Mar 1815 - 12 Mar 1908. (Son, Daniel & Katie Long Derryberry.) 
and
DERRYBERRY, Sarah A. (Hardison), 27 May 1818 - 12 Dec 1890. "Wife of J. L. Derryberry.' (Dau. William & Mary Hardison) 
Looking on the left side of J.L.'s memorial one can see the road bed for Interstate Hwy 65.
