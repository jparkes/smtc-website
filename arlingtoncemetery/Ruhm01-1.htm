ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 

A front view of the Home founded by John J. Ruhm Jr., a Phosphate Mining 
Magnate of Mt Pleasant during the late 1800s & early 1900s.

A corner view of the Home founded by John J. Ruhm Jr. & his wife Mollie. The home is in Mt Pleasant Tennessee. Mr. Ruhm was born in Nashville Tennessee and received his early education from the Nashville City Schools. He studied scientific courses from Vanderbilt and the University of Illinois and received an L.L.B. from the law school at Cumberland University.
