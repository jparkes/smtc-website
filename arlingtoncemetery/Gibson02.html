ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 
GIBSON, Vivian Cook, Born: Jan 2, 1916 Died: April 27, 1937, Dau. of Ben F. Cook, Sr. and Mary Little Ridge Cook, Wife of Flem Gibson, Mother of Juanita Gibson Dewberry Bishop McClain. Vivian Cook Gibson was the sister of Margaret Cook Gordon, Cornelia Cook Scott, James T., Ben F. Jr., Mary Ophelia Cook Sutton Bell and Helen Cook McClain and the Aunt of the submitter.
