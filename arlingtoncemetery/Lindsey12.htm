William Henry Lindsey was born 18 Dec 1920 in Tennessee, the tenth of eleven children born to 
Fred R. Lindsey and Josephine Wright. He served his country in the United States Army during 
World War II. He was the husband of Alice McKennon. He died on 24 Dec 1959 and was buried 
at Arlington Cemetery in Mt. Pleasant, Tennessee in the midst of some of his brothers and sisters 
and near his parents.
