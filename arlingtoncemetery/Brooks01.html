ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 


Brooks, Edna Earl McClain, 1920 - 28 Jul 1950 (wife of James C. Brooks; daughter of John Turley and Allie Mai Kennedy McClain). She married James Comer Brooks and they had 5 children. James C. Jr., Linda Gayle, Judy, Nancy, and Johnny. After Edna Earl died James. C. Sr., married Mary________, and they had a daughter Pam. Edna Earl was the sister of Ruth, Mildred, James "Sonny", Robert M., Ara Alton, Helen Jane, David Leo, and Jerry McClain. She was the first of the nine children of John Turley and Allie Mai Kennedy McClain's children to die. Section D. [Mary Bob McClain Richardson 11/20/2004]

Brooks, James C., 1916 - 1989 (husband of Edna Earl McClain; son of Comer L. Brooks and Frances
