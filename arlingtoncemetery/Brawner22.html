ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE

Brawner, Jane Williams, 1 Aug 1957 - 24 Oct 2008 in Davidson Co., Tn. ( Maury Co. native; dau. Mrs. Aldene Patterson Williams and Harry Webb Williams; wife of Tony Brawner; grad. of Mt. Pleasant High School.)


Born in Maury County on Aug. 1, 1957 Departed on Oct. 24, 2008 and resided in Summertown, TN.
Visitation was Sunday, Oct. 26, 2008 & Monday, Oct. 27, 2008
Services were Tuesday, Oct. 28, 2008
Funeral Home: Williams Funeral Home (Mt. Pleasant)
Cemetery: Arlington Cemetery

Mrs. Jane Williams Brawner, age 51, a resident of Highway 20 in Summertown, went to be with her Lord and Savior on Friday, October 24, at Vanderbilt University Medical Center.
Funeral services for Jane were conducted Tuesday at 2:00 P.M. at the First United Methodist Church in Mt. Pleasant with Rev. Phillip Wright and Lance Williams officiating. Burial followed in Arlington Cemetery. 
The family visited with friends on Sunday from 4:00 - 9:00 P.M. and on Monday from 2:00 - 9:00 P.M. at the Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant. This beautiful & pleasant Maury County native was the daughter of Mrs. Aldene Patterson Williams of Mt. Pleasant and the late Harry Webb Williams. She was the wife of Tony Brawner of Summertown. Jane was a graduate of Mt. Pleasant High School and was a member of the First United Methodist Church in Mt. Pleasant. She was a 33 year employee of the Tennessee Farmers Insurance Company serving last as the Coordinator of Incentives and Recognition. She also served on the Maury County Board of the Monroe Carell, Jr. Children's Hospital at Vanderbilt.
Survivors in addition to her husband and mother included one son and daughter-in-law: Ray and Tiffany Brawner and their daughters, Amy Carson and Jancin of Mt. Pleasant; one sister: Joyce (Wayne) Dugger of Mt. Pleasant; one brother: Webb (Lori) Williams of Mt. Pleasant; three brothers-in-law: Barry Brawner of Clifton, David (Karan) Brawner of Five Points and Jerry Brawner of Columbia; three sisters-in-law: Gail (Bobby) Meigs of Summertown, Brenda Runions and Jenny (Bill) Moore, both of Columbia; several nieces and nephews and a special friend: Tracy Ring of Columbia. She was preceded in death by a daughter: Amy Dorene Brawner in 1997 and a brother-in-law: Douglas Runions.
Pallbearers were Clay Gilliam, Patrick Trousdale, Marcos Garza, Neal Townsend, Joe Coble, Bobby Belew and Keith Woods. Honorary pallbearers will be members of the George Anderson Sunday School class of the First United Methodist Church of Mt. Pleasant, Tennessee Farmers Insurance employees, friends of the Monroe Carell, Jr. Children's Hospital at Vanderbilt, and members of the Lorena Bourne Circle of the First United Methodist Church in Mt. Pleasant.
