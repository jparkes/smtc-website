ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE

LINDSEY, Zephna Adaline, was born 24 Jan 1914 in Maury County, Tennessee the 8th child of Fred R. Lindsey 
and Josephine Wright. She never married. She died 4 May 1967 in Maury County and was buried in Arlington 
Cemetery. She is buried near her parents and several of her siblings. Inscription: Zephna A. Lindsey 
January 24, 1914 May 4, 1967  Photo and information by Rick Gray, 10/16/02004.
