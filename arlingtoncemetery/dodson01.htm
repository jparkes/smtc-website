Dodson, Sarah, 22 Aug 1915 - 17 Jul 2000 (dau. of Rev. Albert A. & Sallie Nelson Holt; wife of Herman Thomas Dodson)

Sarah's obituary - Mrs. Sarah Holt Dodson, 84, retired employee of Brown Shoe Company and former resident of Savannah, died Monday, July 17, 2000, at her daughter's residence in Brentwood. A graveside service will be conducted Wednesday at 11 a.m. at Arlington Cemetery in Mt. Pleasant with Rev. Jim Collins officiating. Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements. The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Rev. Albert A. Holt and Sally Nelson Holt. She was the widow of Herman Thomas Dodson who died June 16, 1998. Mrs. Dodson was a graduate of Hay Long High School in Mt. Pleasant and a member of the Methodist Church. Survivors include one daughter, Mrs. Peggy (Joe) Shaw of Brentwood; two step-daughters, Mrs. Mary Frances (Max) Copenhaver of Greenville, Ky. and Mrs. Doris Grace (Jim) Chapman of Manchester; three grandsons, Jeffrey Holt (Dawn) Shaw of Crossville, Gregory Owen Shaw and Kelly Alan (Christy) Shaw, both of Brentwood; one great grandson, Reed Shaw of Crossville; two great granddaughters, Anna Holt Shaw of Crossville and Kellyanne Shaw of Brentwood; and several step grandchildren and step great grandchildren.
Columbia Daily Herald, Wed., July 19, 2000. submitted by Judy B. Forgos 8/14/2009. [Grve listing developed from obituary by Judy Forgos. We are not aware if an inscribed tombstone was set for the above couple. WA 15 Aug 2009]
