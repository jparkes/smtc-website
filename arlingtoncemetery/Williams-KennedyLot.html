ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE
 


It is known that 2 of the sisters mentioned below are buried in this unmarked spot in the Williams-Kennedy lot. It is only believed that W. C. Ratliff and his wife Mary Lou are buried here. Many believe he was buried here and then his buried here later. This can not be confirmed. He may be buried at Hunter and her buried here with her sisters. All the plot says is that Oliver and Nancy Kennedy Williams bought the graves and owned the deed to them. Nancy was the sister of the 3 Kennedy girls. They are all my 2nd Great-grandaunts.
