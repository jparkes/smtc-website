ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE

Morrison, Donald G., 7 Jan 1927 - 14 Dec 2004. (Maury Co. native; son Ronald Stuart & Genevieve Brownlow Morrison; hus. Mary Dale Norman Morrison; Veteran U.S. Navy, WWII, stationed on board the William C. Miler (De259) destroyer escort transported submarine detection equipment, present for the peace treaty signing at Tokyo Bay; member Macedonia Church of Christ on Mt. Joy Rd.)
