ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 

BROWN, Charley Crittingdon, born 4 March 1883 and died 29 July 1964, buried at Arlington Cemetery, 
Mt Pleasant TN. He was the son of Charles B. Brown and Matilda Ann Bell. He married Kate M. Chumley, 
daughter of John & Martha Chapman Chumley. She was born 19 Jun 1905 and died 23 May 1980.
