ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 

George L. Gibson Born: Sept 16, 1879 Died: Mar. 7, 1940. 


GIBSON, Tee G. Born: Oct 28, 1879 Died: Dec. 17, 1961 Buried: Arlington Cemetery, Mt. Pleasant, Maury Co., Tennessee. Miss Tee as I remember her being called, lived in the Sandy Hook Comunity and was the mother of Flem Gibson who married Vivian Cook. Miss Tee was also the grandmother of Juanita Gibson Dewberry Bishop McClain, and the great grandmother of Ricky Dewberry and the great great grandmother of Eli Dewberry who died recently in a welding accident at his auto shop next to Mac's Service Station in Mt. Pleasant.
