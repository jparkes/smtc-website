These 2 photos are taken from the lower left hand corner of Section I. In this corner the first burial is that of Russell 
Roberts and his headstone is visible in the picture. The first known burial in this section of the cemetery was Richard Nolen, 
a veteran of World War II and the Korean War, who died in 1950. The next known burials in this section were not until 
1954 when several were interred in this section. Photo & info by Rick Gray 2/25/2005.
