SUTTON, Mary Ophelia (Cook) (Sutton) Bell, Born: Sept 4, 1920 Maury Co., Tennessee Died: 
July 25, 2004 Maury Co., Tennessee Buried: Arlington Cemetery Mt. Pleasant Maury Co., 
Tennessee Daughter of Ben F. Cook Sr., and Mary Little Ridge Cook. Married 1st to Calvin 
Frank Sutton. They were the parents of William Wayne Sutton, Barbara Jean Sutton Wilmer, 
and Tina Sue Sutton Finney. Married 2nd to Edward Bell of Columbia. Ophelia was the sister of 
Ben F. Cook Jr., James T. Cook, Vivian Cook Gibson, Margaret Cook Gordon, Cornelia Cook Scott, 
and Helen Cook McClain. Ophelia was a member of the First Baptist Church in Mt. Pleasant, 
homemaker, and did Ceramics. She is buried beside her 1st husband Calvin Frank Sutton.
She was listed as Mary Bell in the newspaper Obituary.
