ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE

My husband's great grandfather William Monroe Blair, who was born in Cannon County, Tennessee 29 April 1844 and is buried in Arlington Cemetery, but apparently has no tombstone. My source of information is:

IRWIN BROTHERS FUNERAL RECORDS - copied from originals in possession of Mr. & Mrs. Tom Williams, Williams Funeral Home, Mt. Pleasant, Tennessee. Copied by Evelyn B. Shackelford. "BLAIR, William M., died 29 July 1923; buried Arlington; age 79-4-0; father James Blair, born Tenn.; mother - Caroline MELTON, born Tenn." "They Passed This Way: Maury County, Tennessee," by Shackelford & Lightfoot.

Thank you

Frances Willess
Austin, Texas 

Information by Frances Willess, Austin Texas, 10/2/2007.
