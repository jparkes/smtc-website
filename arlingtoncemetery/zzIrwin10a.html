Irwin, Frank James, Jr., "Buddy", 17 Apr 1942 - 26 Jun 1977 "In loving Memory, Carolyn & children" (Husband of Mary Carolyn Anderson; Son of Frank James Irwin, Sr. and Mary Lee Nance; died in the Maury County jail fire) Section G

Further Information by Rick Gray
Buddy Irwin was visiting his brother-in-law, William Henry "Buck" Rowland, at the Maury County Jail on 26 June 1977 when fire broke out. The fire claimed his life and many others including his mother-in-law, Mary Alice (Bishop) Anderson; his sister-in-law, Margaret (Anderson) Kinzer Rowland; his brother-in-law, William Henry "Buck" Rowland, Jr.; his brother-in-law, Marvin Anderson; and his brother-in-law, Bily Anderson.
