ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 

LITTLE HINTON FRIERSON WEAVER PASSES AWAY Hinton Frierson Weaver, the 16 months old son of Mr. and Mrs. Will Weaver, died at their home near Columbia Saturday, after a few days illness of pneumonia. Mr. and Mrs. Weaver formerly lived in the Mt. Joy community. Burial took place Sunday afternoon in the Arlington cemetery the funeral services being conducted by Rev. E. R. McCord, pastor of the Methodist Church. 
This was taken from an old unnamed nor dated newspaper clipping. Cemetery Section A.
