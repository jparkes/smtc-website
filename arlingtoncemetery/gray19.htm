GRAY, Walter Harrison, 5 Oct 1898 – 15 Jan 1975 (husband of Eva “Evie” Tidwell; son of James Joseph 
Gray and Elizabeth Clemons) Section A 
and
GRAY, Eva “Evie” Tidwell, 7 Jan 1904 – 1 May 1999 (wife of Walter Harrison Gray) Section A Walter 
Harrison Gray, son of James Joseph Gray and Elizabeth Clemons, was born October 5, 1898 and died 
January 15, 1975. He married Eva "Evie" Tidwell on July 18, 1920 in Winston County, Alabama. 
Evie was born January 7, 1904 and died May 1, 1999. They are buried in Arlington Cemetery, Mt. Pleasant, 
Tennessee. Section A. Photo and information by Rick Gray, 10/16/02003.
