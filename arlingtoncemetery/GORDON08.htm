ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 

GORDON, Margaret Cook, Born: Sept 2, 1910  - Died: April 22, 1987 Buried: Arlington Cemetery 
Mt. Pleasant, Maury Co., Tennessee Daughter of Ben F. Cook Sr. and Mary Little Ridge Cook; 
wife of Buford Gordon; mother of Dora Mai Gordon Whisenate, and Billy Gordon. Margaret 
was at one time President of the Mt. Pleasant Professional Business Women. She was the owner 
of Gordon's Dry Cleaners, and later was in the Restaurant business owning Margaret's Din In 
on Main Street. Margaret was the sister of Cornelia Cook Scott, Mary Ophelia Cook Sutton Bell,
Vivian Cook Gibson, James T. Cook, Ben F. Cook Jr., and Helen Cook McClain."


GORDON, Buford Owen, Born: June 29, 1906, Died: Oct 16, 1961 
(Husband of Margaret Cook Gordon)
