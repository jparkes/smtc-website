Andrew Nelson Hicks, 17 years old, son of Mr. and Mrs. N. P. Hicks, died Saturday at his home on Florida Avenue of meningitis. Young Hicks fell from his bicycle some time ago and was injured about the head, which is thought to have caused his death. Burial took place at Arlington Cemetery, with services being conducted by Rev. W. T. Haggard, pastor of the Methodist Church.
