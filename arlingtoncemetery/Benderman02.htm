ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE

Benderman, Loren Lonzo, 22 Jun 1878 - 20 Aug 1945

L. L. Benderman Dies; Rites Will Be Wednesday. Loren Lonzo Benderman, 67, carpenter, died at 11:30 o'clock last night at the home of his sister, Mrs. Lula Walker, near Screamer. Funeral services will be conducted at the Walker home at 2:30 o'clock Wednesday afternoon, with burial in Arlington Cemetery. A son of the late Tom and Sephronia Voorhies Benderman, Mr. Benderman was a life-long resident of the Mt. Pleasant section. Surviving are a daughter, Miss Florence Benderman, of Mt. Pleasant; five sisters, Mrs. Effie Morgan, Miss Maggie Benderman and Mrs. Walker, all of Maury County, Mrs. M. C. Denton, of Nashville, and Mrs. Fannie London, of Oklahoma; and a brother, Otis Benderman, of Mt. Pleasant. Columbia Herald, Aug. 21, 1945.
Obituary & listing submitted by Judy Benderman Forgos 10/18/2007
