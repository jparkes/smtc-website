HENDRIX, John Harvey, 17 Mar 1897 - 2 Sep 1980 (native of Lewis County; son of Samuel James Hendrix and Modie Ann Cagle; married 1st to Katherine McClain and 2nd to Nancy York Sharpe) Section J
HENDRIX, Katherine McClain "Kate", 16 Jan 1877 - 23 Feb 1974 (wife of John Harvey Hendrix, mar. 3 Jul 1914; daughter of Newton McClain and Fannie Roberta Lindsey) Section J

Katherine was the daughter of Newton and Fannie Lindsey McClain. She married John Harvey Hendrix on July 3, 1914, in Lewis County, TN. Their children were Marcus Allen, Robert A., infant Hendrix, Clarence, Dorothy Ellen, Jimmy Jones, Mary Katherine, Linda Jane and Gladys Louise Hendrix. The couple had celebrated their 50th wedding anniversary.
Information and photo by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 6 Nov 2008.
