Annie Mae Bennett Boshers, age 77 of Summertown, TN, passed away Friday, April 4, 2008 at Maury Regional Hospital in Columbia, TN.

She was a member of Whispering Hills Free Church of God, a native of Maury County, TN, and a homemaker. She is preceded in death by her husband: Melvin Arnold Boshers and her parents: Myrtle Olie and Mattie Ruth Parker Bennett.

She is survived by two daughters: Deborah Latham and Sylvia Doreen Shelton both of Summertown, TN; two sons: Melvin Wade Boshers and Jeffery Boshers both of Summertown, TN; three sisters: Ruby Isabell of Summertown, TN, Ruth Ann Shelton of Flintville, TN, and Barbara Bolton of Mt. Pleasant, TN; two brothers: Billy Bennett of Columbia, TN and Olie Bennett of Mt. Pleasant, TN; eight grandchildren: Angela Slater, Jay Shelton, Magen Shelton, Jessica Byrd, Jason Boshers, Katie Boshers, Mary Anna James, Debbie Pierce; eight great-grandchildren also survive.

Funeral services for Annie Mae Bennett Boshers were conducted Monday, April 7, 2008 at 1:00 p.m. at Whispering Hills Free Church of God with Gayle Harris officiating. Interment followed in the Arlington Cemetery in Mt. Pleasant, TN. Condolences may be sent by way of the funeral home's website. Neal Funeral Home was in charge of all the arrangements.
