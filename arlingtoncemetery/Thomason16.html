ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 

 Mrs. Mary Elizabeth Kennedy Thomason, 75, a resident of the Sandy Hook Community
 near Mt. Pleasant, died Monday, April 25, 2005 at Saint Thomas Hospital. Funeral services for Mrs. 
Thomason will be conducted Thursday, April 28, 2005 at 2:00 p.m. at the Williams Funeral Home in Mt. Pleasant 
with Jeff Waldrop officiating. Burial will follow in the Arlington Cemetery. The family will visit with friends 
Wednesday from 5:00 to 9:00 pm at the funeral home. The Maury County native was the daughter of the late 
Johnny and Emma Dugger Kennedy. She was the widow of Lonnie C. Thomason who died in 1996. Mrs. Thomason w
as a retired supervisor of the laundry department at NHC/Hillview Health Care and former clerk at the Sandy Hook 
Store. She was a beloved mother and grandmother and a member of the Sandy Hook Church of Christ. She is survived 
by her children, Johnny Ray (Doris) Thomason of Columbia, TN, Charlotte (Doug) Davis and Pam Davis both of Mt. 
Pleasant, TN; two brothers, Billy (Brenda) Kennedy and Tommy (Shirley) Kennedy both of Mt. Pleasant, TN; 
grandchildren, Lonnie Thomason and Lee Thomason both of Mt. Pleasant, TN, Timothy (Kellie) Davis of Summertown,
 TN, Kevin Davis of Sandy Hook Community, Diana Davis of Mt. Pleasant, Bethany Thomason of Columbia, TN, Holly 
Davis and Mindy Davis both of Mt. Pleasant, TN; great-grandchildren, Sean Davis, Brittany Davis, Cheyenne Davis, 
Ashlyn Craig, Kaylee Davis, Nicholas Davis, Noah Davis, Kolby Boshers and Kinsey Boshers; several nieces and nephews.
 She was preceded in death by two sisters, Liddy Mae Burkett and Betsy Thomason. Pallbearers will be Lonnie Thomason, 
Lee Thomason, Timothy Davis, Kevin Davis, Mike Davis and Tony Cole. Honorary pallbearers will be the men of Sandy 
Hook Church of Christ.
