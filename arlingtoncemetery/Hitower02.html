ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE
Mt. Pleasant Record Jan. 23 1931:

"SID HITOWER, 47, DIES WEDNESDAY
Sid Hitower, 47 years old, died early Wednesday morning (20 Jan 1931) at the home of Jim Grimes, with
whom he had made his home for many years. He was the son of the late Mr. and Mrs. Dock
Hitower. Burial in the Arlington Cemetery Thursday (21 Jan 1931) at 10 o'clock."
HIGHTOWER, Sidney Columbus "Sid", 18 May 1882 - 21 Jan 1931. (s/o Mr. & Mrs. Dock Hightower; source: Mt Pleasant Record 23 Jan 1931; interred in unmarked grave; h/o Susie Ellen Parsons mar. 24 Sep 1905, was separated or divorced prior to 1910; Teamster and Phosphate Miner)
Printed in the Mt. Pleasant Record Jan. 23 1931. Sent in first by Charlotte Colvett 25 Jun 2009. Addition information contributed by Brian Hightower, Feb. 2011 by e-mail to MB Richardson.
