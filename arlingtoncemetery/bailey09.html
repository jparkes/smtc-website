ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE


State of Tennessee, County of Maury, Death Certificate for Thelma Boshers Bailey, 6 May 1939.
Thelma Boshers Bailey born Mar. 3, 1920 - died May 6, 1939. (Daughter of Huey Boshers and Lena Gibson Boshers. Married Carl Bailey June 1, 1938. Twin sister to Elmer Boshers.)
Information and documents sent in by Charlotte Colvett 4 Jun 2009. State of Tennessee Death Certificate dated 6 May 1939.
