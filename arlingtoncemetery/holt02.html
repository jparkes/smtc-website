Holt, Josephine Virginia "Dean", 30 Nov 1928 - 19 Mar 1959. (Maury Co. native; died in Wichita Falls, Texas, from of Cardiac Arrest, lost grave as of Nov 2010)
 Note from H. Braden who is trying to locate her grave site: Josephine V. Holt. Headstone is small flat horizontal. Located below 3 other Holt headstones of similar size and shape. Facing the cemetery, the headstone is along the right fence line about halfway down the cemetery. For a long time the burial spot was lost due to no marker. Williams funeral home had to find the spot and mark it before a headstone could be laid. I still remember the complaint that she was buried next to a tree stump. (stump unless of cedar would be rotted today but may have left unlevel ground as a way of proving this[WA 1 Dec 2010.)
