ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE 
Allen, Doise Vick, 24 Aug 1932 - 4 Jul 2003 (native of Giles County; daughter of John Arlin Vick and Irene Adams; wife of Alton Eugene Allen - married 26 Dec 1952) Section F in the Allen-Vick-Bennett lot
 

Mrs. Doise V. Allen, 70, a resident of Factory Creek Rd. in Ethridge, died Friday, July 4, 2003 at her residence.
Graveside services for Mrs. Allen will be conducted Monday, July 7 at 1:00 p.m. at Arlington Cemetery with Ricky Glass officiating. 
Born in Giles County, she was the daughter of the late John Arlin and Irene Adams Vick and wife of the late Alton Eugene Allen, and a graduate of Campbellsville High School. She was a secretary for Kizer-Fraizer Auto and a homemaker. She was a member of the Church of Christ Church. 
She is survived by a daughter, Kim Allen Ellis of Ethridge; two grandchildren, Sammie Jo Ellis and Lance Allen Ellis, both of Ethridge; two sisters, Sammie Barnes of Ypsilanti, Mich. and Lois (Bob) Lancore of Monroe, Mich.; a brother, Louis J. (Barbara) Vick of Summertown; Brother in law, Bill (Evelyn) Allen of Lawrenceburg and loving nieces and nephews. She was preceded in death by a stepsister, Della Kay Vick.
Williams Mt. Pleasant Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements
