ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE
 
Brawner, Isla Greer, 26 Sep 1890 - 25 Oct 1953 in Lawrenceburg, Tn. (Hickman Co. native; dau. of Howell Greer and Kate Taylor Greer Roth; widow of William Brawner, who died 19 yrs. ago; preceded in death by sisters, Annie G. House, Robbie G. Polk and half-sister, Mable Roth.)
