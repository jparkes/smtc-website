ARLINGTON CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, MT PLEASANT TENNESSEE


State of Tennessee, County of Maury, Death Certificate for Lena Boshers, died 9 Oct 1921.

Boshers, Lena Gibson, 31 Aug 1898 - 9 Oct 1921. (Daughter of Ernest and Annie Odom Gibson; First wife of Huey G. Boshers; Lena died 3 days after giving birth to a daughter, named Catherine. Mother and daughter died with in one hour of each other and are buried together at Arlington cemetery in an unmarked grave.)
Boshers, Catherine, 6 Oct 1921 - 9 Oct 1921. (Daughter of Huey G. and Lena Gibson Boshers.)
Information and documents sent in by Charlotte Colvett 4 Jun 2009. Both State of Tennessee Death Certificate dated 10 Oct 1921.
