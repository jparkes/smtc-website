This photo is taken from the lower left hand corner of Section G, on the end next to Section I. 
The four tombstones on the front row there belong to J. Maxie Roach, Lee Nola Roberts, Clarence and Etta 
Brooks Roberts, and Cecil Roberts. The first known burials in this section are Annie E. Williams Wishart and 
Charles Vance Hendrickson, who were both buried in the upper part of this section in 1941. The oldest burials 
in the lower part are from about 1952. Photo & info by Rick Gray 2/5/2005.
