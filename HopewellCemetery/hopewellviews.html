Use your scroll bars to view the whole cemetery.

This is the best view of the cemetery from the east side looking at most of of the memorials. The stone nearest to us in the photo is for Nell Martin, with the Pigg's, Craigs, Matthews, Emerson, Hindman, Walker, Benderman, Harlow and others nearer the church building. The two stones in the far upper left corner of the photo which are side by side belong to the four sisters of the Maxwell family. Back under the trees and among the Boxwoods are the memorials of the Galloway and earlier Matthews families. The red stone belongs to William L. Craig
