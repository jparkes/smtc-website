HOPEWELL AMERICAN REFORMED PRESBYTERIAN (ARP) CHURCH & CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


WALKER, John E., 30 Sep 1848 - 25 Feb 1916. (Son William & Margaret Alexander Walker, 
both of N. C.) 
and
WALKER, Jennie Peoples, 16 Jar. 1865 - 30 Jan 1934. "Wife of John E. Walker." (Daughter of Reverend James H. Peoples, former minister of this Hopewell Associate Reformed Presbyterian Church.) John E. has the distinction of having the earliest memorial stone in this cemetery. There is always the possibility that there are unmarked graves that are earlier though.
