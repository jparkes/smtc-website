HOPEWELL ASSOCIATE REFORMED PRESBYTERIAN (ARP) CHURCH & CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Use your scroll bars to view the whole cemetery.

This is a view from nearer the church looking back out over the stones. We are looking northeast. There is still more of the cemetery to our left and behind us. Those with a keen eye will begin to notice some of the same stones showing up in other photos and will be able to trace where they are located by their shapes.  A prime example is the little heart shaped stone of John Galloway Matthews who was born & died in 1985. The stone is located in the center of this photo. Photo and info by Wayne Austin 12-13-04. Two photos were graphically combined here to create a wide angle view.
