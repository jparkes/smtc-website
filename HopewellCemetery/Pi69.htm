HOPEWELL AMERICAN REFORMED PRESBYTERIAN (ARP) CHURCH & CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

PIGG, Raymond Edgar, 5 Nov 1928 - 3 Jul 2013, Son of Willis Edgar & Nelle Jane (?) Pigg.

Born Lawrence County, Tennessee
Death Maury County, Tennessee

A native of Lawrence County, he was the son of the late Willis Edgar Pigg and Nellie Jane Cummins Pigg. He began his forty year career with the propane company Southern LP and worked at the same location through name changes including Petrolane and Amerigas. An avid outdoorsman, Mr. Pigg enjoyed raising tobacco, gardening, fishing and bird hunting. He loved people and was fond of spending time with his family, especially his grandchildren. Survived by his wife, Joanne; three children; three sisters; three brothers; and five grandchildren. Graveside service Saturday, July 6 at Hopewell Cemetery with Rev. Buddy Roberts officiating.
