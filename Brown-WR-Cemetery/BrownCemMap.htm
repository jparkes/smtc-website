BROWN (W.R.) CEMETERY, POTTSVILLE


The Brown Cemetery may be found by turning off of Hwy 431 onto Hardison Mill Road about a mile south of Pottsville. Hardison Mill Road makes a sharp left turn and from there go to the first entrance on your right. The cemetery is located behind what was in 1986 the home of Mrs. Edward Peay. It is about half way between the river and Hardison Mill Rd. marked above by the black arrows. If these instructions don't work notice in the topographical map the cemetery is located at the end of a plateau on the south side about 400 yards west of the road.
Information by Fred Hawkins in 1986 and map by Wayne Austin Feb 2006 modified from MS Terra site.
