MOORE, Alexander, 10 Oct 1789 - 29 Jun 1851. (Large crypt)
MOORE, Sarah, 26 Oct 1794 - 24 Jul 1853. (I believe this is a death date of 1853 instead of 1835. Threes and fives are tricky in these old stone inscriptions. Definately the last digit is a three but the cross bar may be invisible to make it a 1833. However generally Husband and Wife died around the same time in those days. as I am suggesting lexander & Sarah were H&W. That is only because the stones are close together, the same style and lettering. Just a strong possibility. [WA 2/19/2008]
