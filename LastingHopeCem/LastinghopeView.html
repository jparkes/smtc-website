A view out across Lasting Hope Cemetery. You quickly get a feel from this photo of the historic nature of this cemetery. The focus of the picture is the stone in the center for Elizabeth Kennedy The average age of these stones is more than a hundred years old and in great condition compared to most old cemeteries. Our hats are off to the surrounding community for all the hard work and care.
