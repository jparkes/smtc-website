KITTRELL FAMILY CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Thomas M. Kittrell. Brush is too thick to get an area photo here.

KITTRELL, Thomas M., 8 Jan 1836 - 1 Jun 1876. 40 yrs 4 ms & 23 Days. "Husband of Elizabeth Kittrell; Mason." Likely he was a Civil War Soldier and fighter for the Confederates. Being a Mason at the age of 25 years old when the war broke out is prime evidence of that.
