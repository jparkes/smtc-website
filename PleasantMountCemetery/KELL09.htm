PLEASANT MOUNT CEMETERY, GLENDALE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


KELLEY, Ralph, 7 Aug 1918 - 28 Aug 1944. Military marker: " Tennessee PFC Med Dept World War II" (killed 28 Jul 1944 in France, and first buried at U.S. Military Cemetery at Draguignan, France. Remains were returned in 1948 for burial at Pleasant Mount Cemetery. MB) (Ralph's name is also listed on the Columbia Tennessee War Memorial to those who made the supreme sacrifice of their lives for the freedoms you and I enjoy. WA) 7035
