PLEASANT MOUNT CHURCH & CEMETERY, GLENDALE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Pleasant Mt Church Cemetery. Go east on new Hwy 50. Just about a mile beyond Glendale turn north on the Pleasant Mt Road and go about a mile to the church on the right.The cemetery is behind the church building.
