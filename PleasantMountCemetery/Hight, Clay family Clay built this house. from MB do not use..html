When my sister and her husband bought the old farm house it was in pretty bad shape.  It was sitting on these hugh bolders   It has a double stair case inside and hardwood floors.  The walls were little tiny slats of wood that looked like hardwood.  I loved it when they first restored it. It has been restore two times by them.   They have redone the walls and added on another bathroom (claw foot tub)  It is lovely.  They had to put inslulation and sheet rock over the wooden walls they were freezing in the winter time, but have maintained best they could the orginial look inside of the house.  I will send you a picture of the house when they bought it.  you won't believe the difference.  It looks like the picture when it was built now on the outside.
