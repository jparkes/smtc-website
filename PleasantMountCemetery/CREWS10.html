PLEASANT MOUNT CEMETERY, GLENDALE, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
CREWS, Wesley E., born 1821 died 21 Jun 1894, born in Granville Co. NC was the youngest son of Littleberry Crews and Elizabeth Earl. He married Nancy W. Collier on 23 January 1840 in Maury Co. TN.
They had three children, Joseph, Mary Ann and Archie T. Crews.
