ORMAN, Robert L.

Speculative interment in consideration of residency and family relationships. No reported marker.

Mr. Robert L. Orman, a highly respected citizen of Spring Hill, died Monday morning at five o'clock at his home. The funeral services were held Tuesday morning at ten o'clock at the residence. Deceased was a half-brother of Mr. J. Sykes Orman of this place, and leaves a wife and four children to mourn his death, and also a great number of friends throughout the country. -- Columbia Herald, 17 Aug. 1900.
