SPRING HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Military Marker for Alton Bradley

BRADLEY, Alton "Peck", 5 Mar 1923 - 24 Sep 2004.
BRADLEY, Wanda W. Barton, 27 Sep 1924 - 28 Jun 1976.
Alton "Peck" Bradley died in Davidson County. Tennessee. He was a Maury Co. native and the son of James Lee Bradley and Mamie Early Bradley. He was a Veteran of WWII & married the former Wanda Barton, 17 Oct 1964, She preceded him in death 28 Jun 1976.
