Grigsby Lots








RAINEY, Mary E., 19 Feb 1858 - 8 Feb 1883. (Wife, Thomas J.; Dau. Ben & Mary E. Grigsby. Born a few days before the death of her mother Mary E.) (Grigsby lots)
RAINEY, Horace, 26 Jul 1885 - 17 Mar 1887. "Son T. J. & L. R. Rainey"
GRIGSBY, Benjamin, died 22 Apr 1900, age 82 years, "Father"
GRIGSBY, Matilda, died 27 Jun 1896, 'Mother". (2nd wife of Benjamin Grigsby, m. after 1858.)
NEELY, J. (John) W., 20 Feb 1877 - 6 Feb 1903. (Grigsby Lot; John W. Neely, married Lenora Rainey. She was the daughter of Thomas J. Rainey and Mary E. Grigsby.)
