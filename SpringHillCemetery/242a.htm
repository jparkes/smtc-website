JENKINS, Etta

Enon Creek, Sept 20...Etta Jenkins, a respectable colored woman of this vicinity, died last Saturday, the 18th, of consumption. Her funeral was attended by a large crowd of colored people, and a good number of white people. Etta had many friends among her color, and was well thought of by all the white people. She was buried at Spring Hill. -- Columbia Herald, 24 Sept. 1897.
