SPRING HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

CRAFTON, Eliza V., 10 May 1840 - 23 Feb 1901. "Mother."

CRAFTON, Eliza V.

Mrs. Elizabeth Crafton died Saturday night at her home at Spring Hill, aged about sixty years. She had been ill a few days with pneumonia. She leaves to mourn her death one daughter, Mrs. Olive Butler, besides a large circle of friends. The funeral services were conducted at the residence Sunday afternoon at 2:30 o'clock by Revs. Harrell and Bose, and the interment was in the Spring Hill cemetery. -- Columbia Herald, 1 Mar. 1901.
