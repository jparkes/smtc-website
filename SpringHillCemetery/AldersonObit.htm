ALDERSON, Madge Sowell Dodson, 20 Nov. 1920 - 29 July 2004. (d/o Roy & Jannie Sowell Dodson; w/o Paul E. Alderson.)

She was a graduate of Santa Fe High School and was a member of the Santa Fe United Methodist Church and the Water Valley Home Demonstration Club.

Survivors include two sons, Norris (Susan) Alderson of Laurel, Maryland; David (Elaine) Alderson of Water Valley Community; two sisters, Mrs. Edith Church of Gallatin; Mrs. Faye (Ray) Locke of Columbia; one brother, Madison Dodson of Columbia; three grandchildren, Anthony Alderson, Brian (Beth) Alderson, Beth Alderson; and three great grandchildren, Kaitlin Alderson, Taylor Alderson, and Grant Alderson.
