ORMAN, Margarette R.

Miss Maggie Orman died at 1 o'clock this morning at her home in Spring Hill. She had been dangerously ill for about four weeks, but the malady from which she was suffering was not exactly known until towards the last. It was then pronounced cancer of the stomach by her physician, Dr. Dorsett. By her bedside were her brothers, J. Sykes and Henry Orman, and her sisters, Misses Janie, Sallie and Julia Orman, and Mrs. Coats of Alabama. Their mother died several years ago, and Miss Maggie, being the eldest daughter, had since taken, as far as possible, her place in the family. Her life has been an unselfish devotion to her brothers and sisters and a beautiful example of noble Christian womanhood. The funeral services will occur tomorrow at Spring Hill. -- Columbia Herald, 27 Sept. 1892.
