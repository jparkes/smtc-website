Dr. Aaron C. White family memorials

WHITE, Dr. George W., 23 Jan 1819 - 4 Jul 1859.
George and Aaron's mother wrote Aaron a letter not long after George died, in which she insisted he bring to her (in Nashville) all
the daguerreotypes (photos) of George, so she could pick one to give to WB Cooper, who then painted Dr. George's portrait.
