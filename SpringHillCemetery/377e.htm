FLOYD, Mollie, (d. Jan. 1897) no marker.



FLOYD, Mollie

Mrs. Mollie Floyd, wife of Mr. Henry Floyd, died at her home in the Enon Creek neighborhood last week. The funeral services were held at the grave by Rev. Mr. Craig, and her remains were interred in the Spring Hill Cemetery. Mrs. Floyd leaves a husband and one child, two years old, to mourn her loss. -- Columbia Herald, 5 Feb. 1897.
