CAPERTON, Mary J.

Mrs. Mary J. Caperton died last Sunday at her home near Spring Hill. Deceased had been seriously ill for only a few days, although her heard had been quite feeble for several years. The funeral services were conducted at the residence Monday by Rev. Mr. Hardin, assisted by Revs. Taylor, Dixon and Fuller, and the interment took place in the Spring Hill Cemetery, the remains being followed to their last resting place by a large concourse of sorrowing friends. Mrs. Caperton was a true Christian woman, beloved and respected by everyone. She leaves surviving her the following children, to whom the deepest sympathy is extended: Mrs. Molloy, Miss Mollie Caperton and Messrs. J. P., J. W. and Thomas Caperton. -- Columbia Herald, 3 June 1898.
