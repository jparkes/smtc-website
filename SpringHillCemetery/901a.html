BLANKENSHIP, Yvonnie June Wheeler, 20 Dec 1925 - 15 Sep 2009 in Davidson Co. (Native of Pikeville, Tn.; dau. of John William and Flora Blanche Hampton Wheeler; Educator, Speaker, Writer, grad. Central High School in 1943; majored in pre-medicine and home economics from U. of Chattanooga; mar. 2 Oct 1949 to Fred Blankenship from Hardin Co. and pastor of Riverside United Methodist Church in Columbia; preceded in death by sister Billie Looney and brother John L. Wheeler of Chattanooga.)
