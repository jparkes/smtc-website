SPRING HILL CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

GUNNEL, Louise, 1900 - Mar 1919. "Sister." (d/o John P. Gunnel & Lucille Turner Gunnel.)

Miss Louise Gunnell, aged 19, died Wednesday morning at her home near Spring Hill of influenza. She is survived by her father and mother, Mr. and Mrs. J. P. Gunnell, four brothers, Stanton, Claude, John and Jesse, one sister, Miss Margaret. Five members of the family are now ill with influenza. The deceased was a member of the Methodist church and a young woman of beautiful character. Services will be conducted today at the Spring Hill cemetery by Rev. J. E. Woodard. Maury Undertaking Co., in charge.
The Maury Democrat, Friday, March 14, 1919.
