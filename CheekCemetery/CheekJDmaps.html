CHEEK (JESSE D.) CEMETERY (Clark Rd, 1.5 mile east of Hwy 431) MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map was added to show the GPS location of the Jesse D. Cheek family Cemetery. It is not detailed in the records of the USGS so I have given here the GPS Longitude and Latitude of this cemetery.  

Clark Road, Marshall County Tennessee (near the Maury/Marshall County line).
Located on Clark Road, between Rutledge Lane and Milltown Road in Marshall County. The cemetery is approximately 1 and 1/2 half miles from the Maury County line and Franklin Pike (Hwy 431) in the Hardison Mill community. The cemetery is located on the late Jesse D. Cheek property. Property is currently owned by the Parish family.

This map shows the cemetery pointed out by a yellow arrow. Clark Road can be seen here and the private property road you turn left on to go here.
The cemetery is pointed out by a red check on private property.  Here we see the dark rectangular shaped area where the John L. Cheek cemetery is located.
