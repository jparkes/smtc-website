CHEEK, Calvin Modrell "Polk", 1840 - 16 May 1864. Son of John Lillie (Jackie) and Elizabeth Rhyne Cheek. Killed at Atlanta in an unlucky exchange of shots between CSA and Federal sentries. He was a member of C.S.A. Company G 24th Tennessee Rifles (Duck River Rifles) commanded by Capt. Robert Washington Tindell, a neighbor, and his sisters brother-in-law, who lived across the Duck River on Cedar Creek. (No marker was ever placed at this grave).
