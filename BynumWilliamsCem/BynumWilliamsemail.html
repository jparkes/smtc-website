Take US 31 (really 43) North from Mt. Pleasant, going 1 1/4 miles past Maury County Airport. Turn left, passing through Rattle & Snap's farm area, crossing railroad tracks and by-pass, follow farm road through fields to cemetery in a grove surrounded by ploughed fields.
