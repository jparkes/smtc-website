POPULAR UNION CEMETERY, HIGHWAY 50, HICKMAN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The Popular Union Road is clear on the map. It is the cemetery after one turns onto popular Union Road.

This cemetery is located just off Highway 50 on the left traveling west from Columbia, Maury County, Tn. At this writing the sign on the highway has been taken down.
