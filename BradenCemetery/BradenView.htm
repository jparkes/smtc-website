BRADEN CEMETERY, CAMPBELL'S STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 


A view looking at all of the monuments of the Braden Family Cemetery. The inscription is listed on the opposite face of the stones except for the James Braden stone in front. The cemetery gets a regular mowing and there is a fence around it. All this is maintained by Mr. Donald Wilkes who owns the farm.
