Braden Cemetery 

PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

In Campbell Station take the Dodson Gap Rd. and go south for one mile. Turn right into Tanyard Hollow Rd. and go 1/4 mile to the Donald Wilkes place on left. The cemetery is on the side of the road, next to Mr. Wilkes barn. Visited there 9/4/2004, Wayne Austin.
