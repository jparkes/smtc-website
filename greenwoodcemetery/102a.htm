GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

RUSSELL, Janie Clark, Little Dau. of M/M W.L., Dec 1899
Janie Clark Russell, the thirteen-months-old daughter of Mr and Mrs W L Russell, died Saturday at the home of her parents on Sowell Mill pike. Rev F B Webb conducted the funeral services Sunday afternoon at the residence, and the remains were interred in Greenwood Cemetery.

Columbia Daily Herald 15 Dec 1899.
