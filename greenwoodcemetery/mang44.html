GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE


MANGREM, Clara, 22 Sep 1779 - 13 Sep 1843. "Consort of Littleberry Mangrem." (Greensville Co., Va. native; d/o Meredith Jordan and Margaret Acres, Nee: Clary Jordan; w/o Littleberry Mangrem/Mangum in 1807; migrated to Maury Co. with husband and parents who settled in Davidson Co. Tn. (Note: Clara Jordan Mangrem's dates appear on her husband, Littleberry's marker. Ref: Dave Johnston - djohnston@byu.net.)
Photo and information by Wayne Austin 7/20/2003. Parenthetical information updated by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 3 Aug 2010. Note by Wayne Austin: I think the Census tends to be less reliable information than tombstone dates, but everyone is entitled to their intrepretation.
