GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE


GRANT, Anna Sanders, 1791 - 29 Apr 1856 "Consort of Isreal Grant and mother of Cyris Webster. She was born 1791 
and died Apr 29 1856. She embraced Christianity when young, was zealous and stedfast in faith, her last words was 
With Heavenly weapons I have fought the battle of the Lord finished my course and kept the faith and await the sure reward."
