GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

SANDERSON, Miss Nancy, died 27 Sep 1851 78 years. Sacred to the memory of Nancy Sanderson 
who departed this life Sept 27th 1851 in the 78th year of her age." She was a member of the 
Cumberland Presbyterian Church and died in full triumph of a living faith beyond the grave. Blessed are 
the dead that die in the Lord". Photo and information by Wayne Austin 7/20/2003.
