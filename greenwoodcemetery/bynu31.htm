GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE


BYNUM, Joseph Wingfield, 24 Apr 1839 - 9 Dec 1861. ‘Embraced religion 1856, joined M. E. 
Church, 1858. Graduated Wesleyan Florence University, 1859. Graduated Lebanon Law School, 
obtained license for practice of law 1860. At the time of his death he was Vice President of the 
Young Men's Christian Association for the promotion and extension of Christianity and his 
life was an example of Virtue & Piety.’ (Co. H, 1st Tennessee lnfantry, Confederate States Army; 
killed Old Millboro, VA.)
Photo and transcription by Wayne Austin. Military record info from book by Fred Hawkins "Maury 
County Tennessee Cemeteries" photos 7/20/2003 & 12/18/03.
