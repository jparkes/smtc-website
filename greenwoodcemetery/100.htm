GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

WELCH, Martha J., 18 Nov 1812- 22 Jan 1879. "Wife F.H." "The grave of MARTHA J. wife of F.H. Welch, BORN Nov 18th 1812, DIED Jany 22, 1879. No more thy gentle voice Loved caring shall sooth my troubled heart. When sorrow some? shall shed hallowed brightness oer the soul. When storms have rent the darkened sky of home rest thou have gone yet not like those whose hopes went out with life. For on yon radiant shore our darling spirit will? fly_ _ to erect the evermore, My Angel Wife"
