GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

WINN, Richard, "South Carolina, Colonel S.C. Militia, Revolutionary War, died 19 Dec 1818. Buried at home place 11 miles W. (west) on Hwy 50." (This commemorative stone was placed here by the "Daughters of the American Revolution, Tennassee Chapter", since the original grave site cannot be located in the family cemetery in Arkland.)
This article appears on the TNGENWEB and says Richard Winn was a Major General in the American Revolution.
