McRADY, Margarett, d. 5 Mar 1871 - Aged 67 years. "Wife of E.W. McRady." (Daughter of Alex Whitt; b. Albemarle Co., Va.) This stone was still under repair. It was being propped up with timbers until the epoxy dried enough to hold it.
