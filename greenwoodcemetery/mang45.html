GREENWOOD CEMETERY-COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

MANGREM, Littleberry, 16 Jul 1783 - 16 Feb 1837. (Isle of Wight Co. Va. native; s/o Briton and Elizabeth Glover; married Clara (Clary) Jordan in Greensville Co., Va. He came to Maury Co. with his uncle Henry Mangum. (Note: Dave Johnston, a descendant of this family states that Littleberry's dates appear to be on his wife's ts. Mr. Johnston say Littleberry enumerated in the 1840 census and the death date on Clara's tombstone is actually his death date.)
Photo and information by Wayne Austin 7/20/2003. Parenthetical information updated by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 3 Aug 2010.
