GREENWOOD CEMETERY COLUMBIA TENNESSEE

Looking southwest at the cemetery from inside on 11/29/2005 finds the cemetery much improved from recent restorations by Cumberland Research Group funded by the Columbia Recreation and Parks Department from a grant. Much of the historical value has been regained. The large wooden markers mark graves found by the Ground Penetrating Radar Equipment indicating there is an unmarked grave there. We see several in this photo though many of them are too small to be noticed from this photo. Photo by WA. 11/29/2005
