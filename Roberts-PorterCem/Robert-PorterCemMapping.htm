ROBERTS-PORTER CEMETERY, Baker Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Use this map to find the GPS coordinates to the Roberts-Porter Cemetery GPS as 35.65093 W 87.00360.

Go north on Hwy 31 to Baker Road and turn right (east) and go about a 1/2 mile and turn into the Trailer Park. Use the map above for GPS to navigate into the trailer park and to wind around to the cemetery which is easily accessible. Mr. Fred Hawkins' directions in 1986: Go north on US Hwy 31, turn east on Baker Road for 1/4 mile to the R. M. Kelly place. Cemetery is inside the trailer park just before reaching the Camber home. It is behind a trailer on the end (northeast)
