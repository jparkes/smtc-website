BALLANFANT CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BALLANFANT, Sarah Turner, 7 Aug 1801 - 10 Apr 1876. "Wife of Joseph Ballanfant, born in Caswell Co., Nc."
 Josiah Turner Ballanfant is son of John Ballanfant and Eliza E Turner.  Sarah Turner Ballanfant was mother of John Ballanfant and grandmother of Josiah Turner Ballanfant.
