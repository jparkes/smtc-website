Maps of the Cemetery Location

Ballanfant Cemetery is just west of Culleoka. Go west on the Mooresville Pike to the Frank Moore Rd. It is down a fence line opposite the point where the Frank Moore Road enters Mooresville Pike. It is about 400 yards south of Mooresville Road. The Cemetery and Stones are well kept with an iron fence surrounding the stones, but surrounded today by heavy cattle ranching. The iron fence will need repair soon to continue standing against the heavy traffic from the livestock.
