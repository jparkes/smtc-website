BALLANFANT CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
    Looking northwest across the cemetery. The iron protective fence is going to need repair to stand long term. The cattle have rubbed against it until it leans and is in danger of falling. By Wayne Austin 03/24/2004.

This view is looking west across the rows of memorials. There is serious cattle ranching going on around this cemetery so be careful not to disturb the livestock if you visit here. The way to do that is to move slowly and deliberately around them.
