BALLANFANT CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

BALLANFANT, J. T. (Josiah Turner), 1 Oct 1845 - 27 Nov 1924. "FATHER". (Son of John & Eliza E. Ballanfant; Co. F, 4th Tenn. Cav. & Co F, 3rd Cav.; C.S.A.; mar. Sallie Turner of Marshall Co., on 28 Nov 1871.) Josiah Turner Ballanfant is son of John Ballanfant and Eliza E Turner.  Sarah Turner Ballanfant was mother of John Ballanfant and grandmother of Josiah Turner Ballanfant.
