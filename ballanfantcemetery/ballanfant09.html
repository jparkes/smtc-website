BALLANFANT CEMETERY, CULLEOKA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


BALLANFANT, Lucy, 9 Feb 1865 - 9 Jun 1865. "Daughter of John & Eliza Ballanfant". 
If you visit this cemetery, your task is to be as inconspicuous as possible. The cattle are largely looking disinterested so we must have been fairly transparent, though they are clustered over our way more than I would have liked. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 24 Mar 2004.
