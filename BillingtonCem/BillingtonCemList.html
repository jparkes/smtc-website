BILLINGTON CEMETERY,  BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Mr. Fred Hawkins, instructions 1986: 1/2 mile west of Pottsville, on Hwy. 99. It it located on the north side of highway in a cedar grove. 
2008 Instruction: About 1/2 mile west of Pottsville on the north side of the road in a cleared area well maintained and watched over by a nearby residence. There is a sign on Highway 99 pointing the way.
