BILLINGTON CEMETERY,  BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Mr. Fred Hawkins, instructions 1986: 1/2 mile west of Pottsville, on Hwy. 99. The cemetery is located on the north side of the highway in a cedar grove. 
2008 Instruction: About 1/2 mile west of Pottsville on the north side of the road in a cleared area well maintained and watched over by a nearby residence. There is a sign on Highway 99 pointing the way. Go into a private drive marked with the cemetery sign but one can continue with permission by the residence into the cemetery straight ahead.
Information by Wayne Austin 8 Aug 2008. Map from MS Terra mapping service modified for this site.
