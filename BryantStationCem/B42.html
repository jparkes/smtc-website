COFFEY, Nancy S., 12 Oct 1834 - 16 Feb 1865. "Wife of John A.; daughter of Benoni & Sarah Gresham, An infant son aged 7 mos sleeps with her in this grave" (1st wife of John A. Coffey.) Complete Inscription: "In memory of Nancy S., wife of John A. Coffey & daughter of Benoni & Sarah Gresham born Oct 12th 1834. Died Febry. 16th 1865. She lived & died a Christian & was beloved in life & lamented in death. Our infant son aged 7 mos sleeps with her in this grave"
