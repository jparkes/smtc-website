WOODY CEMETERY (Santa Fe, Snow Creek Rd.) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Our subject is the memorial with the black arrow pointing at it. It is standing up.

Samuel Woody, 1770 - 1855

WOODY, Samuel, 12 Sep 1770 - 22 Oct 1855. (85-01-10.) "Devoted Christian near 60 years."
Inscription: "IN memory of Samuel Woody, Snr. born Sept the 12th 1770 & died Oct the 22nd 1855, Aged 85 years 1 Mo. & 10 days. He lived a devoted Christian near 60 years."
