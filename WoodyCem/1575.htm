WOODY CEMETERY (Santa Fe, Snow Creek Rd.) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
Our subject is the memorial with the black arrow pointing at it. It is standing up.

Jane Woody,  1774 - 1856, (wife of Samuel, 1770 - 1855)

WOODY, Jane, 18 May 1774 - 16 Jan 1856. (81-07-29.) "Wife of Samuel Woody; devoted Christian for 60 years."
Inscription: "IN memory of Jane. Wife of Samuel Woody, born May the 18th 1774 & died Jan the 16th 1856. Aged 81 years 7 mo. & 29 days. She lived a devoted Christian 60 years."
