GRIGG FAMILY CEMETERY, Maury/Giles County Line.
An endangered Cemetery not shown on any maps or publications
This stone in front of us above belongs to this listing: GRIGG, Infant Daughter Grigg of P.H. and Gille Grigg b. May 6, 1860 d. May 6, 1860. That is the footstone on our left and the grave is in between. The other broken memorials are mostly piled against the old Sugar Maple tree. Many of them also look like children's memorials. I would say the large Maple tree would be a help in finding this cemetery except from the dropping limbs. That means it is probably dying and may not be there today.  [Commentary by WA Oct 10, 2007]
