PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Wells Family Cemetery-Campbell Station, Maury County TN

 A view looking south at the three box tombs of David Wells, 1812-1888 and his two first wives Elizabeth, 1812-1845 and Lucinda 1810-1862. Just beyond that is two tall field stones I presume these mark two graves. Beyond that is an unmarked round top carved stone leaning against the base of a tree. Over to your right of that is the stone that has only a partial inscription remaining of a child who was born and died in 1834. Dec 2002 Photo by WA.
