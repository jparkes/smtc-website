WELLS CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

WELLS, David, 25 Dec 1812 - 21 Apr 1888. Inscription: "In loving Remembrance DAVID WELLS Born Dec. 25 1812 Died April 21, 1888, Aged 75 yrs, 3 mos & 26 ds. We miss thee from our home dear father, we miss thee from thy place; A shadow around us is cast; we miss thee sunshine of thy face we miss thy kind and willing hand. Thy fond and earnest care, Our home is dark".

DAVID WELLS was born 12/05/1812 in Virginia, and died 4/21/1888 in Maury Co TN. He married (1st) ELIZABETH P. FITZPATRICK 9/09/1836 in Maury CO, TN. (Interred here in this cemetery). Elizabeth was born 8/27/1812, and died 1/02/1845 in Maury Co., TN. Next David married (2nd) LUCINDA KENNEDY 1/14/1846. Lucinda was born 9/13/1810, and died 11/01/1862 in Maury Co TN. (Interred here in this cemetery). David then married (3rd) PRISCILLA A. (WARDEN) (KERR) 8/13/1863 in Maury Co TN, daughter of JAMES WARDEN and MARY MCLEAN and widow of JOHN KERR who d. in McNairy Co Tn. Priscilla was born 3/06/1822 in Maury Co TN, and died 8/07/1887 in Maury Co TN. (Interred in Shane Cemetery)

Notes for DAVID WELLS:
No Children were born to the marriage of David Jr and Priscilla Warden Kerr, but the two first wives has children by David.
From the family history records of C. Wayne Austin descendent of Priscilla Warden Kerr Wells. updated 6Jul 2011
