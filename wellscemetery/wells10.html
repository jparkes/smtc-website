WELLS CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

WELLS, Mattie T., 23 Dec 1871 - 23 Dec 1871. Death date is broken off but some of the letters can be made out with careful examination. On the left is the footstone with the initials "M.T.W." The stone was found leaning against the west side of the above Hackberry Tree and the footstone was against the opposite side.
