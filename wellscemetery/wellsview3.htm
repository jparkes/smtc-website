PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Wells Family Cemetery-Campbell Station, Maury County TN

 A more open view of the John & Addie O'shea (tumbled) stones. Excuse the slip from normally good photography of the camera case and clipboard sitting on base of the stone. The tall object at the base of the tree is a post. Maybe the wood post has a purpose but I am unaware of what it is. Dec 2002 Photo by WA.

 Notice the old rusty plow shoe at the base of the tree. It was very old and barely had any steel left in it. Might have been the object with a handle attached that was used to dig graves at one time. Dec 2002 Photo by WA.
