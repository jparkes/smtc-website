WELLS CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


WELLS, Elizabeth, 27 Aug 1812 - 2 Jan 1845. ‘Consort of David Wells.’ (Nee Fitzpatrick). Elizabeth was the first wife of David Wells who in 1845 left him a widower. David then married Lucinda Kennedy. Inscription: "Sacred to the memory of Elizabeth, consort of D. Wells. was born August 27th 1812, Died Jan 2, A.D. 1845.
Elizabeth Fitzpatrick was the daughter of John Fitzpatrick (1780 - 1872) and Lucy Freeman Fitzpatrick (1787 - 1861) who are buried in the Friendship Cemetery.
