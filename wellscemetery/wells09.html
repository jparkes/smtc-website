WELLS CEMETERY-CAMPBELL STATION-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

MITCHELL, Samuel, 25 Feb 1809 - 2 Aug 1849. "ERECTED TO THE MEMORY OF SAMUEL WELLS born February twenty fifth 1809 and departed this life August the second 1849". (Son of David & Christian White Wells; married Mrs. Nancy L. Washum on 23 Nov 1834). Box Tomb. Many inscribed symbols on the bottom of the stone.
