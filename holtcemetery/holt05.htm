HOLT CEMETERY, LEWISBURG ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Allen Holt memorial among the oldest stones in Maury county. Broken and on the ground. 


HOLT, Allen, 1 Feb 1796 - 15 Oct 1817. "Sacred to the memory of Allen Holt who was born Feb 1st 1796 & Died Octr 15 1817. My Home henceforth is in the skies. Earth seas and sun a dieu. All Heaven unfolded to my eyes. (but) I have no sight for you". 
This stone is among the oldest in the county. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 3/24/04.
