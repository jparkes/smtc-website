Holt Cemetery, Lewisburg Highway, Maury County Tennessee
    This is a photo of a cemetery from inside looking across at the main part.  By Wayne Austin 03/24/2004.

This view is looking north at the top of the box tombs and two other rows of memorials on our left. The light image above center left is Hwy 373, Culleoka to Lewisburg. As you can see we are high above the road.
