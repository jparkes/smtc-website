Holt Cemetery, Lewisburg Highway, Maury County Tennessee

This map provides the GPS coordinates of this cemetery. It is my belief this is correct.
Take Highway 50 out of Columbia and turn south on Culleoka Road Hwy 373. Go past Culleoka toward Mooresville about 2 miles and turn into a driveway that doubles as a residential road. With permission climb up the road and the cemetery is next to the road on our left.

     This is an aerial photo of the Holt Cemetery. You will have to use the drives just west of the major southward bend in the Lewisburg Highway to get to this cemetery. There are houses & buildings all around. The driveway does appear to be the right of way marked on the Topographical Maps, but if you believe you have the correct right of way be courteous to the nearby neighbors because you will need to cross someone's front yard to visit the cemetery and you are using roads that very rarely get used as a cemetery access.
