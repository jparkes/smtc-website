HOLT CEMETERY, LEWISBURG ROAD, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


HOLT, Ann K., no dates. Here is where experience and observation begins to pay off. Based on what I have seen as the style and sculpture of this stone, I believe this stone would have been cut in the 1850s. So that suggest that Ann K. Holt died in the 1850s. I know of about ten other stones identical to the style here in nearby cemeteries and they are all 1850s death dates.
