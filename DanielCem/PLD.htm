DANIEL CEMETERY,  BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
DERRYBERRY, P. L. (Patrick Leamon), 17 Jun 1847 - 13 Aug 1927, (son of John Taplan & Phoebe Cornelius Spear Derryberry))

DERRYBERRY, Patrick Leamon: was born near New Lasea, Maury Co., Tennessee, the son of John T. and Phebe Cornelia (Speer) Derryberry.
P. L. Derryberry was married to Mary (or Margaret) E. Amis, daughter of John D. and Mary (Daniel) Amis who immigrated to Tenn. from North Carolina.
P. L. Derryberry and family moved to the city of Columbia and resided in Riverside. He was a rural dentist and also a carpenter by trade. He later became a building contractor and engaged in other business activities as well. He was the owner, for a time, of the farm on which this cemetery is located.
Information from Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries by Fred L. Hawkins.
