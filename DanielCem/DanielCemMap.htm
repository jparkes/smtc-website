DANIEL CEMETERY,  BEAR CREEK PIKE, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mr. Fred Hawkins, instructions 1986: Going east on Hwy. 99, turn right into last lane before Interstate 65 exit. The cemetery is located in grove on hill in front of house on Mrs. C. U. Barker's place. 
2008 Instructions: Change of ownership. Place no longer belongs to Mrs. C. U. Barker. Also turn south into the first white gravel lane back from I-65, about 500 yards, on the west side of Interstate 65, exit 46. The cemetery (after gaining permission) will be on your left down the tree line going eastward about 200 yards just inside the tree line. You could also follow the north side of the fence line eastward for the 200 yards the cemetery from the driveway. The cemetery then was a dense thicket and getting there on the north side of the fence is also a briar thicket.
