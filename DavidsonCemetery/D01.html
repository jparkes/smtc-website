DAVIDSON CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mary V. Cockrill

COCKRILL, Mary V., 31 May 1841 - 17 Jun 1865. Mother. (dau. John Kirk; mar. Marion McDonald Cockrill 23 Mar 1859, died at same day as infant W. N. Cockrill)
COCKRILL, W. N., 22 Jul 1864 - 17 Jun 1865. Sweet babe. 
(Two inscriptions on the same broken stone, broken in two places.)
