DAVIDSON CEMETERY,  MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

KIRK, Martha, 16 Jun 1849. (39-07-24.) (Stone signed: Wm. Shirley, Col.)
This inscription is believed to match this listing below:
KIRKS, Martha (Shores Westmoreland), 23 Oct 1809 - 16 Jun 1849. "39 yrs,7Mo & 24 days". (Born in Dinwiddle Co., Va.. Ref: g g grandson Richard Smith, Arizona.
