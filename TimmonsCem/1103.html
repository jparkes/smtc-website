TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Back side of the Judith Sellers memorial,Transcribed as: "Repaired and reset 1979 by Homer R. and Leslie Sellers  G G Grandsons of Judith and Isaac Sellers." (Stone was encased in (probably Hydraulic) Concrete in 1979 and reset and lettering painted black, but is failing again.) 

Our subject in this page the Isaac Sellers memorial. It is the small memorial with the brown oval top adjacent or just to our left of his wife's Judith Sellers (bottom left corner of photo.) Notice the long slab appearing between the head and footstone for Isaac Sellers. 

The Isaac Sellers memorial.

SELLERS, Isaac, b. 1769 - d. 12 Jan 1830. Age 61.
Inscription: "Isaac Sellers Born in 1769 & Died Jan. 12, 1830, Age 61 years."
