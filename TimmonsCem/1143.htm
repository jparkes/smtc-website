TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The Wisener family memorial. Red pointers indicate the two footstones for Abner A. & Zula Zon Wisener and their head monument. I do not know if there are more graves matching this headstone but I did not see any other foot stones.
