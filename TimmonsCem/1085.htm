TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

Large Timmons Family Headstone with no other markings. Perhaps it serves as family marker for Nancy Evans Timmons and her husband Thomas Jefferson Timmons interred here without a detail foot marker. There is an unmarked grave just south of the grave of Nancy Evans Timmons. Mr. Fred Hawkins also noticed this in 1986.

TIMMONS, Unknown, (likely Thomas Jefferson Timmons), (Large family marker at the head of Nancy Evans Timmons Grave and foot marker with her inscription. There is a Grave on the south side of Nancy Evans Timmons. It is thought to be for Nancy's husband Thomas Jefferson Timmons, Maury Co. Tn., Widow's Pension #W10793 applied for by Nancy Evans Timmons, Ref: Tn. State Library and Archives. mb.)
