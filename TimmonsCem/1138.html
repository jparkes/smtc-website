TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Emma Callie Timmons, 4th slab monument from this end. Footstone on our right and large Timmons monument on our left.

I believe the large Timmons monument on the other side here was installed for A. J. & E. Callie Timmons who are the only interments with the footstone on the far end. Both Oval shaped on tops are on common on the footstone.  

TIMMONS, E. Callie (Emma Callie), 1 Jul 1852 - 24 Oct 1915. (Dau. A. J. & Nannie Ester Kinzer.)
