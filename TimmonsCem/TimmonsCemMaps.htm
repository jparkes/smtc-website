TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE 

The red check places the Timmons Cemetery on this GPS related topographical map. The position on the map for the GPS coordinates are correct. Notice the Hughes Cemetery across the road from here. It too is a very historic cemetery.
 The above yellow arrow points to the Timmons Cemetery in a tree lined north/south row. It is adjacent to a private residence deserving of our respect. 

From Columbia, take Santa Fe Pike, cross Duck River and go 1 and 1/2 miles. The cemetery is north of road beside tree lined hedge row on top of hill. It is on the Billy Walters place, left of the home. (1986).
