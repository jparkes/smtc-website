TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Mary M.D. & Laura Ann Sellers monument on the left with the oval top. This is a mother daughter burial. 
SELLERS, Mary M. D., 7 Jan 1847 - 7 Oct 1865. 18 years, 9 months.
SELLERS, Laura Ann, born 11 Apr 1865 Aged 5 months & 27 days.
In memory of Mary M.D. Sellers, born Jan 7, 1847. Died Oct 7, 1865. Aged 18 years & 9 months And her little Infant  daughter Laura Ann Sellers, Born April 11, 1865 Aged 5 Months & 27 Days.
