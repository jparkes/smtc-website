TIMMONS CEMETERY, Santa Fe Pike, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 Large Timmons Family Headstone with no other markings. Perhaps it serves as family marker for Nancy Evans Timmons and another member of her immediate family interred here without a detail footmarker. There is an unmarked grave just east and south of the grave of Nancy Evans Timmons. Mr. Fred Hawkins also noticed this in 1986.

Stone back against the fence row. There is another grave here beside Nancy's without a marker.
TIMMONS, Nancy Evans, 20 May 1851 - 9 Sep 1950.
