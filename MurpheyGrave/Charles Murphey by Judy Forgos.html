MURPHY, Charles, 3 Jun 1770 - 20 Jan 1852. 81-07-17.  Spelled Murphey on the gravestone.
 
Wayne,
    This grave is 'on the old Benderman place'.  
    Located off of Southport Rd., about half way between Mack Benderman Rd. and Haley Lane, on the right hand side you will find 1574 Southport Rd.  This gravel lane dead ends at the Benderman house and the gravestone is approximately 25' from the front porch. 
    I have attached two items (1) a photo of the gravestone for you to use, and (2) a map of the approximate location.  Hopefully you can make a more accurate map & re-write my location description into a more accurate one that would be suitable for your site. 
I hope this helps you.
Judy Benderman Forgos
March 17, 2008
 
 
  
FYI only Wayne & not to be posted..... 
The numbers 1574 are on the mailbox attached to a post which is sitting on a large milk can.  On the opposite side of the street is a lake.  If the street address does not help you to find it on a map, the coordinates for the Benderman Cave, which is a little further back on the property, are Latitude: 35°29'13" N. Longitude: 87°08'10" W.
The gravestone photo, used with permission, was taken by a cousin in Oct. 2006 and the tree in the background is a large magnolia tree. The property is still in the Benderman family.  A Benderman Family Reunion is held every year, and for the last three years relatives have gathered for a bring-your-own lunch at the Porter's Chapel Church and afterward are invited to visit the home place and cave.
Let me know if you are able to get the exact GPS coordinates for the gravestone location, as I would like to post it to findagrave.
