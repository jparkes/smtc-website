MURPHY (CHARLES) CEMETERY, SCOTTS MILL/(SOUTHPORT) ROAD.

In Scott's Mill community, on the old Benderman place. (large tombstone, near house in 1959, per Allie Berryman & Evelyn Shackelford)

MURPHY, Charles, 3 Jun 1770 - 20 Jan 1852. 81-07-17.

Only one tombstone was found to be inscribed, a few other fieldstones were found but were not inscribed markers.
There is a lot of disagreement as to the location of this cemetery. The GSIS indicators point to a spot in the southeast corner of the intersection of the Mack Benderman and Scotts Mill Road. Clearly there is nothing there today but a cattle pasture so the graveyard is not visible or has either been destroyed or is marked wrong on the map. Others say it is further north up the Scotts Mill Road on the left. Still some say it is back down Terrapin Branch before one comes to Ebenezer Church on the right. Thus far I have not found anyone that can take me to this grave. Single stones like that are in great danger of falling and sinking below the ground unless an alert local person keeps track of the stone and can and is willing to point it out to visitors. We need to find this place and correctly map the Longitude & Latitude so it can always be found.
Above directions and listing condensed from "They Passed This Way" & "Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries."
