ALLMOND CEMETERY, LESEA, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Nina Koelsch grave in a seperate fenced area.

KOELSCH, Nina M., 20 Aug 1950 - 27 Feb 1995. "God Fearing Wife and Mother Thank you.". In 1986 Mr James Koelsch is mentioned by Mr Fred Hawkins as the owner of the surrounding farm. Obviously Nina was a part of that family.
