McKEE-ROUNTREE-JAMESON CEMETERY, Carters Creek, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE
MILLER, Mary Wilson, 21 Mar 1796 - 10 Jun 1822. (Note: The death date is as inscribed on stone, but Maury Co. Marriages gives: Mary Rountree to Joseph H. Miller on 21 Oct 1822. Joseph 1st married Mary Campbell who died in 1822.) 9482 Footstone:"M.W.M." 9485
 Concerning the mystery of the dates for Mary Wilson Miller's death and marriage to Joseph H. Miller, I have found that Maury County Marriage records show that Joseph H. Miller married Maria (Mary) W. Campbell on 22 Feb 1811; and Mary Roundtree on 21 Oct. 1822. So the Mary in the Jameson-McKee_Roundtree Cemetery is actually Mary Campbell, not Mary Roundtree. Joseph H. Miller and family left Maury County, Tn in Feb. 1830 moving to Missouri. According the Histories of Greene County, Mo., Joseph and brother, Daniel B. were pioneer citizens of Springfield, Missouri. Hope this is helpful for Mary's Memorial.
