McKEE-ROUNTREE-JAMESON CEMETERY, Carters Creek, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 This map shows the GPS co-ordinates of the McKee-Rountree Cemetery along Hardison Road.

From northern Maury County in Spring Hill take Beechcroft Road west for about 4 miles. Beechcroft Rd ends at Hwy 246 (Carters Creek Pike) and Gravel Hill Road picks up. Follow Gravel Hill Road continuing west for 1/4 mile and turn left (or south) on Hardison Road. Go 1/4 mile and the cemetery will be along on your right next to the road.

This aerial view shows the cemetery in a rectangular shaped area with trees bordering it on the west side.
