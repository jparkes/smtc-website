HUBBELL GRAVES, (Miller Lake Rd.), MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This is a map to show you the GPS coordinates of the Hubbell Cemetery marked by the red check as 35.45845 X -87.110372. 

In Southport on Hwy 245 take Bethel Church Rd aka Miller Lake Rd west/south about 1 mile. Miller Lake is on your right. With permission find the cemetery just up from the bank of the lake into the woods. It is near the lake in a beautiful location with the lake just 200-300 feet away. I parked in the sharp bend (to the left) on Miller Lake Road, then climbed beside the dam of the lake back to the north side of the lake. The walk was about 400 feet (or 133 yards.)

This is the clearest map of the Lake Area shown here as Badger Spring Lake, but probably known at one time as Miller Lake. We can see the red check marking about where the cemetery will be found back into the woods and up the slope some.
