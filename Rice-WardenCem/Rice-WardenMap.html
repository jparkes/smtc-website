RISE/RICE-WARDEN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION


This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Rise-Warden Cemetery southeast of Campbell Station high upon hill. An easier GPS reading is the decimal one more widely used as given here: N 35.43884 W -86.98464.

 From Campbell's Station take Martin Hollow Rd. south about 3/4 mile. The cemetery is on the right, high upon a hill in a grove of trees, behind what was in 1987 the home of Mr. Charles Graff.

I have the cemetery marked with a red check. It has been recently cleaned so this is an open spot in the woods today. Hopefully everyone will keep it that way.
