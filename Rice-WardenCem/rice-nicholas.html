RISE/RICE-WARDEN CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-CAMPBELL STATION


RISE/(RICE), Nicolas, d. June 1856, supposed to be 78 years old. Nicolas was a soldier in the War of 1812, Capt. Bird S. Hurt's Co. and was born in Virginia.)
"To the memory of Nich -- olas Rise who died -- June 1856 suposed -- to be 78 years old."
According to Fred L. Hawkins of "Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries" there is a marriage record for Jane and Nicholas
Rice in Maury County records. Nicholas and Jane also appear in the 1850 Federal Census.
Nicholas was also known to have served as a Soldier of the War of 1812. We are unaware of the relationship of Nicholas & Jane Rice to James Warden. Could Jane Rice have been a Warden or a more likely a sister to Mary (maiden name unknown) Warden?
