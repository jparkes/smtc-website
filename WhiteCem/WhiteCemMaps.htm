WHITE FAMILY CEMETERY, Parsons Bend Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.
This map shows with a red check the place where the White Cemetery can be found.
This road map shows the place point of the White Cemetery on the Parsons Bend Road.
1987 instructions: In Parson's Bend area, between Montsano Company property and Duck River. Take Mt. Nebo Rd. to Parson's Bend Road. Cemetery is in a small grove on left, just beyond Chirt Bank Ridge.
24 Mar 2009 Directions: From the intersection of Hampshire Pike & Williamsport Pike go about 8 miles. Turn right on Craig Bridge Road. Follow that northeast about 1 & 1/2 miles and turn right on Parsons Bend Road. The cemetery is about 1/2 mile east on Parsons Bend Road on the right just before a sharp turn in the road.

This aerial image shows the cemetery in the crook of the Parsons Bend Rd on the southwest side under a few big Cedar Trees. Cedars show of as a darker spot than other trees.
