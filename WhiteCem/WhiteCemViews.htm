WHITE FAMILY CEMETERY, Parsons Bend Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

Media & Mary P. White memorial is back in the right corner between us and the dark tree trunk.

Media & Mary P. White memorial on the left and back in the shadows on our right is the stone for Mary E. Gray. 

 In the distance nearer the road is first the unmarked white fieldstone and further toward the road is the stone for Luke S. & Nancy J. White. In the far distance hills shown here as right above the Luke & Nancy White memorial I believe is a good chance of finding the lost Reuben Hill Cemetery.
