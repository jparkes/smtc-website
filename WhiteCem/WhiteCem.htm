WHITE, Media, 1785 - 1855. Husband. "Father of L. S. White." (Medy White was a volunteer in Capt. Hudson's Co.
of Tenn. Infantry during the War of 1812.)
WHITE, Mary Panelopy, 1790 - 1860. Wife. "Mother of L. S. White.", (This stone erected in 1961, by granddaughter, Mrs. Kate White Blocker, a daughter of L. S. & Nancy S. White.)
WHITE, L. S. (Luke Summer), 31 May 1820 - 19 Feb 1892. Father. (built the new building for Liberty Church of Christ in 1870)
WHITE, Nancy Jane, 25 Dec 1829 - 6 Jun 1906.
WHITE, Viola, no dates on Tombstone. (no inscribed tombstone found 24 Mar 2009)
