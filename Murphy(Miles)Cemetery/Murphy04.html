MURPHY (Miles P.) CEMETERY, MACK BENDERMAN RD.

UNKNOWN, A fieldstone protruding out of the ground in the row is a sure bet it marks a grave.

It is adjacent to the unknown field stone and next over from that is the infant of Miles P. & L. E. Murphy and is probably that of another infant. Moss covered and in the lower center of photo. This one has a head stone and a footstone composed of field stones.
