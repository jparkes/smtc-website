MURPHY (Miles P.) CEMETERY, MACK BENDERMAN RD.



The above photos show the cemetery deep in the dense forest. Both of the cemetery views above look to the west. The top one is further away from the stones than the second photo. The bottom photo helps one find the place. It is hard to find without help. The field road mentioned in our directions is to our left in the bottom photo. You need to locate that and follow it leading to the green (grassy) spot far out in the center of the bottom photo. To our left will then be the cemetery just about 20 yards left of the crooked Oak Tree (label) and deep in the forest.
