PULLIAM CEMETERY, PORTERS, MT PLEASANT RD.


From Porters going west on Mt Pleasant (or Scotts Mill or Southport) Road take the left fork and turn southwest on the Wheelerfield Road and go about 100 yards and turn right (back northwest) on the Old Polk Lane. Pulliam Cemetery will be on the left out about 300 yards in the middle of an open field
