PULLIAM CEMETERY, PORTERS, MT PLEASANT RD.

PULLIAM, Sallie J., 17 Dec 1852 - 12 Dec 1872. 'Wife of Benjamin H. Pulliam.' 
and her daughter
PULLIAM, Susie M., 9 Jan 1872 - 21 Oct 1872. 9 months 12 days, 'Dau. B. H. & S. J. Pulliam' (memorial broken into several pieces.) Pardon the mud but this broken piece of the memorial which contained most of the inscription was sunken into the mud. Did the best I could to make it readable in the short period of time there. Most of the rest of the pieces are stacked behind this part.
