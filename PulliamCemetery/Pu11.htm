PULLIAM CEMETERY, PORTERS, MT PLEASANT RD.

WORTHAM, Elizabeth A., 11 Aug 1832 - _ _ _ _1834. 2 yrs -??-1 day, 'Daughter of John B. & Mary Wortham.' This little box tomb probably belongs to Elizabeth. It is not large enough for an adult. Just a corner of the top is all that is visibly lying around and that is on our left. The rest of the top is probably under the topsoil nearby. There is a name on that part of the top but I believe it belongs to the stone carver. It is not discernable by me.
