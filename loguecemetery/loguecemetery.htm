Logue Cemetery 

PHOTOS OF CEMETERIES IN MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Logue Cemetery may be found just across the Campbell Pike on the east side at a point opposite where Richardson Road terminates. It is about .25 miles north of the Glenwood Church and Cemetery. You should find one tall style memorial of the Logue Family with inscriptions on all four sides about 30 feet east from the road. This cemetery was on the Logue Farm at one time in the 1800s. Mr. Fred Hawkins in Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries says there are six fieldstones marking other graves. He also has listed three persons two of which bear the surname Logue that are believed to be interred here without inscribed markers.
