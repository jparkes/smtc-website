LOGUE CEMETERY, BROADVIEW, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS location of the Logue Cemetery. It is marked on this map only as CEM.

Here we can see the roads needed to get to this little small family cemetery on the side of the Campbellsville Pike. It is across from Richardson Lane on Campbellsville Pike about six miles south of Columbia Tennessee

 Located one fourth mile north of the Glenwood Church the cemetery is on the east side of the road between the creek and Campbellville Pike Road. One large stone is there to represent the Logue family.

This is the ultimate map since it shows more features than others. The Logue Cemetery is marked here with a red check.
