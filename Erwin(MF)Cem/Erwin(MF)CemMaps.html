ERWIN (Martin Phifer) FAMILY CEMETERY, Snow Creek Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

This map shows with a black leg arrow the place where the Martin Phifer Erwin Cemetery can be found. It is labeled as "CEM" on this topographical map. 
Directions: On north side of Snow Creek Rd., at the point where Craig Bridge Road terminated onto Snow Creek Rd. The cemetery is in a pasture on Mrs. Moore's place (1987). While standing in the middle of Snow Creek and Craig Bridge intersection look north to northwest and the cemetery is on the slope of the gradual hill in the pasture about 350 yards out.

This Aerial view map shows with a yellow dogleg arrow the Erwin (Martin Phifer) Cemetery at the junction of Snow Creek & Craig Bridge Roads on the north side. The cemetery has a wrought iron fence surrounding it with other wrought iron fences stacked nearby for replacement. The Erwin home place is nearby but is still private property.
