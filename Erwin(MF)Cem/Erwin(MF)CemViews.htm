ERWIN (Martin Phifer) FAMILY CEMETERY, Snow Creek Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE.

This distant view of the cemetery shows the large monument surrounded by a Wrought Iron Fence.

This is the view of the intersection of Snow Creek  & Craig Bridge Rd. The cemetery is on our left out of the view of this photo.
In the distance with the yellow arrow pointing downward toward it is the cemetery. That is about 350 yards from the intersection we are standing on to make the photo. Permission needs to be secured to visit here, because the land is private and the gates are locked.
