BROWN (McCLINTOCK) FAMILY CEMETERY, CULLEOKA.


To find this previously lost cemetery, go east on the Culleoka-Lewisburg Hwy 373 and turn left (north) on McKibbon Road. When the road forks, take the east or right fork and that will continue to be McKibbon Road. Go 1/2 mile from the fork and get out and walk 300 yards north into the woods and the two box tombs Mr. Bill Thrasher mentioned in his communication of 5 Apr 2008 should be found. The Geological Survey mapping agrees with Mr. Thrasher concerning the whereabouts of this cemetery. It is shown at Latitude: 35 degrees 28.47' North and Longitude: 86 degrees 55.61 West shown marked above with the red check.
