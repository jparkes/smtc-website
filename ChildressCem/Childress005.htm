CHILDRESS CEMETERY, BRYANT STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The tall straight top stone in front of the big upright Gallaway monument in the upper part of the photo. This cemetery is so abused that it looks like a pig pen with a bunch of broken rocks around it instead of a cemetery.
