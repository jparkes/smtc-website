CHILDRESS CEMETERY, BRYANT STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This Topographical map shows the little cemetery in the bend of Luke Moser Road on the right.

From Bryant's Station, take the Luke Moser Road going north. Go 1.5 miles to what was in 1988 the Harold Crabtree farm, today it belongs to Nona Fox. The cemetery is on the right located under a group of large trees in a pasture to south of the house out from the northward bend in the Luke Moser road.
 Photos and information by Wayne Austin, 17 Apr 2008, Published here 7 Jun 2008.
