CHILDRESS CEMETERY, BRYANT STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

ANDREWS, Maggie Lou Childress, 29 Aug 1897 - 17 May 1922. (Dau. of Lula Childress Fitzgerald and Simon W. J. Fitzgerald, died of Tuberculosis following Influenza.)
 To: Mary Bob
I know that my Great-Grand mother was supposed to be buried there beside a tree that is so big now that it would mark her grave. Sherry Young 23 Jan 2009.
Wayne Austin answers:
Mary Bob/Sherry this cemetery has been so badly abused that there are no stones standing and there may be some stones lost into the earth. Your information is consistent with this large Sweet Gum Tree. Graves are all around it with broken memorials. Wayne Austin 24 Jan 2009
Information sent to Mary Bob McClain by E-mail from Sherry on 23 Jan 2009. Childress Cemetery, Maury Co., Tn. Great Grand Daughter. 23 Jan 2009.
