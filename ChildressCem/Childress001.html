CHILDRESS CEMETERY, BRYANT STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The big memorial with the black arrow pointing to it.

A westerly view, the big stone in the center.


GALLOWAY, Marion, 18 Jul 1804 - 5 Mar 1874. "Our grandmother.' (Wife of James E. Gallaway.)
 From the style of memorial I believe this stone was installed in the early 1900s by her grandchildren.
