CHILDRESS CEMETERY, BRYANT STATION, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

The large stone lying on the ground in the bottom of the photo pointing toward us. A large Oak tree is on our left.

This stone is very near the large oak tree on the west side. 

MAYBERRY, Cornelia E., died, 9 Mar 1858. (30-5-25). 'Beloved wife of Hardin Mayberry." (A big oak tree has grown up beside this grave & may be the resting place of Hardin Mayberry. We can find no stone for Hardin. FLH, 1988)
