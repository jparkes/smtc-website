HARDISON (HUMPHREY) CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
HARDISON, Humphrey, 18 Aug 1804 - 15 Oct 1874. Aged 20 yrs & 2 months. (C. S. A.) "Our Father and Mother are Gone. They lay beneath the sod. Dear parents, tho we miss you much we know you rest with God."
