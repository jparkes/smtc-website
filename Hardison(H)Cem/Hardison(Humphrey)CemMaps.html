HARDISON (HUMPHREY) CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Cross Duck River on Sowell Mill Pike, turn left (north) on Arthur Sampson Road, go about .4 of a mile. Cemetery is 200 yards beyond first house (address 3710) on left in middle of open field 150 yards out. This is the old Arthur Sampson farm. Across the road was the old William A & Mary V. Wilcox farm. It is marked by the old chimneys still standing today. I found this cemetery at Latitude 35 degrees 33.905' North and Longitude 86 degrees 50.884' West.
