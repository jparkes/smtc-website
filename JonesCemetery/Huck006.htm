JONES CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


HUCKABY, Geraldine Alexander, 2 May 1920 - 14 Mar 2011. (Clayton, N.M. native; d/o late Elmer Alexander and Opal Chilcote Alexander; raised in Ethridge, Tn.; 1940 grad. of Summertown High School, B.S. in Special Ed. from U.T. in Knoxville and Masters Degree from George Peabody College-Nashville; certified to teach hearing impaired and multiple handicapped children, taught at T.C. Thompson's Children's Hospital in Chattanooga, Jacksonville, Fla, and King's Daughters' School in Columbia; South Park School and Gregg School in Memphis and Orange Grove in Chattanooga; featured in Who's Who in America, and articles in Reader's Digest and Good Housekeeping Magazines; (2nd) wife of Clifton Allen Huckaby; preceded in death by husband Clifton Allen Huckaby, sister G.G. Alexander, brother, Mac Wayne, Walter, and Ben Alexander and sister in law Mattie Lou Alexander.)
