JONES CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

This topographical map shows the cemetery in the upper right corner of the Exit 46 on Interstate Highway 65 Nashville to Birmingham Alabama. That is on the east side of I-65 & the north side of Highway 99 called Bear Creek Pike in the olden days. Can be seen from I-65. That is some 50 miles south of Nashville Tennessee. It is about a couple of acres in area.
Information  by Wayne Austin 9/02/07
