I just remembered Hattie Bell Galbreath Nicholson is buried at Jones but does not have a marker.  That's why I went over there that day to find her marker but I found the other Galbreaths.  The Shirley Anderson Galbreath is her brother.  Strange name, huh.  Their parents are David Gentry and Isabell Rummage Galbreath.  Hattie was married to Henry Fulton Nicholson who is buried at Hardison Cem on Rock Springs Road.  Hattie was born 29 Aug 1912 and died 23 Feb 1996.
