JONES CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE


JONES, Louise D., 1908 - 1997 (fading Oaks & Nichols Funeral Home Marker). The funeral marker above was found with the fallen white unmarked marker which also had fallen. I set up the funeral marker up in the area of the white marker photographed that and then set up the white marker. On our left is another white marker (photo above). Obviously this was put here by the same family. There are no inscriptions on either of the white markers. On our left we can see the corner of the Mary Satian marker
