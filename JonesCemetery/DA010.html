JONES CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
 


Joanne Bratton Davis, 75, died 31 Dec 2010 at Maury Regional Medical Center. Joanne was the daughter of the late Annie Pearl Bratton. Joanne was survived by her husband, Aubrey L. "Strut" Davis of Columbia, TN, a daughter, Judy (Bruce) Taiji of Columbia, TN; three sons, Terry (Annette) Davis of Columbia, TN, Danny (Cheryl) Davis of Rockvale and Randy (Robyn) Davis of Spring Hill, TN; three grandsons, Jay (Tonya) Watters and Stephen Davis both of Columbia, TN and Patrick Davis of Murfreesboro, TN; a granddaughter, MacKenzie Davis of Columbia, TN; and a great-grandson, Storm Stone of Columbia, TN. She was preceded in death by a grandson, Jonathan Davis.  Interment was in Jones Cemetery.
