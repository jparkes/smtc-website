JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL

 
 
BAILEY, Anderson P., 5 Nov 1803 - 8 Jan 1863. Born Mecklenburg Co., NC., emigrated to Maury County in 1834. Died January 8, 1863 Age 59 years 3 mo. & 3 days. The four pieces of this stone are toppled but appears to be in good shape. The top is lying about 7 paces to  the southwest (shown in the inset photo). The base must be squared and leveled and set on a rock or concrete foundation to stand for long.
