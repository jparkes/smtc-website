HANNAH, Lucretia V., 26 Feb 1821 - 13 Nov 1839. (17-8-17. "In memory, Lucretia V. Hannah was born Feb the 26th 1821 and died Nov 13 1839 aged 17 years 8 months and 17 days." This grave is located next to Martha Young's 1766-1840. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 10-11-03.
