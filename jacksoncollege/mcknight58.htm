JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL


McKNIGHT, Wm. R., died 4 Feb 1838. Age 26 years. 
 "Sacred to the memory of Wm. R. McKnight died Feb 4th 1838. Aged 26 years." This stone is propped against a tree. It was found and uncovered recently by the someone and probably belongs to the footstone located about 15 paces to the south of where this stone rest against a tree.
