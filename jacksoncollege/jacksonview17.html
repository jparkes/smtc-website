JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

A view looking southwest from the center of the cemetery at the toppled stones in Jackson College/Old Brick Church Cemetery. When all the gnarled trees are gone and the memorials restored upright this will be a beautiful place for all to visit and learn of their history and heritage. I don't know yet why there was a hog fence that split across the middle of this cemetery still there today shown as the clump of crumpled fence running across this photo.
