JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL

 There are four children's graves lined up above with small head stones. The first is an 
unmarked grave. These were all the small children of John C. and Emily J. Odil. At the 
left of the Black Cherry tree is the fallen memorial to the parents; John C. & Emily Jane Odil.
