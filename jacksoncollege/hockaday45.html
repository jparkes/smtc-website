HOCKADAY, Mrs., (no dates.) I know the Kerrs and Hockady's intermarried but I don't know who this lady is. She might have been a teacher at Jackson College for a time because this is the only inscribed stone with the name Hockady on it. The name is usually spelled Hockaday.
