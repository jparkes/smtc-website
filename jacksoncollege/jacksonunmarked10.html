JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE




Here are four little uninscribed stones that probably represent the markers for four small children. They are located in the Dunlap, Caskey & Stephenson area of the cemetery. However, we have no evidence they belong to children of those families. Since the difficult process of birth caused as much death in the 1800s there was often a designated area in the cemetery set aside by several of the families where the children were laid to rest.
