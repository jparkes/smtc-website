JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL



SANDFORD, Mrs Winnefred, 17 Apr 1775 - 16 Dec 1844. (Winnefred Speight Turner Sandford, Halifax Co. Va. native; w/o James T. Sandford.)
 "Sacred to the memory of Mrs. Winnefred Sandford Consort Hon. James T. Sandford, A consistent Member of the Presbyterian church for forty years. An example of christian kindness gospel charity and holy benevolence. Born April 17th 1775, Died Dec 16th 1844. - My flesh shall slumber in the ground Till the last trumpet's joyful sound, Then buried the chains with sweet surprise. And in my savior's image rise."
