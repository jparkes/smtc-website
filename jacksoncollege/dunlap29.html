JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL

DUNLAP, Samuel, 10 Nov 1795 - 16 Sep 1869. "In memory of Samuel Dunlap born Nov 10, 1795 and died Sept 16, 1869. Erected in 1960 by great grand children replacing original rock which has been broken." Samuel Dunlap was the son of William Dunlap & Nancy Ann Carnes, who are buried at Waxhaw Presbyterian Church outside Lancaster, SC. There is a nearby broken base which is probably the base for the original stone. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 10-11-03. Relationship from Dr. Julia Ashley a descendant's Web page.
