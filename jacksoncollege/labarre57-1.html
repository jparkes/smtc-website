JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY, SPRING HILL TENNESSE

The Jackson College President's Home was still standing in recent times. It is believed the old College
was located near what is now known as Brown Road. This photo appeared in Hither & Yon in the photo
section of Vol 1. The house already in a sad state of repair was torn away soon after this photo which
was made sometimes between 1960 and 1988.
Translation by Wayne Austin 11-8-2003.
