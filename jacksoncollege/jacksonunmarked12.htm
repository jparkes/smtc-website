JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE



 Above are two foot and head field stones. I think these mark children's graves. I think they belong to children because the space between the two stones is too close to be an adult. Top photo was taken in the Dunlap, Caskey & Stephenson area of the cemetery and the bottom is in the area of many unknown graves. Stone grown into the tree above is that of W. I. L. Johnson, 1834 - 1862. I wonder if he died from a Civil War Battle at age 18. Photo by Wayne Austin. 10/27/20003.
