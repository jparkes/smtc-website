JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL

STEPHENSON, Elizabeth, died 9 May 1850 aged 88 years. Elizabeth Stephenson, widow of Rev. War patriot Moses Stephenson. Elizabeth Stephenson emigrated to Maury Co., TN, from the Waxhaw area of South Carolina.
