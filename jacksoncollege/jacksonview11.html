JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING  ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE


A view looking at the
Jackson College Cemetery at the
memorial to William Hugh Brown, 1844-1912. There is a decorative wrought iron fence surrounding this memorial. However, so far no evidence exist of any memorials other inside. May be others fallen under the dense but cleared vegetation. Most of the cemetery is still behind us. The Sons of Confederate Veterans and the Boy Scouts of America has taken on the difficult process of restoring this cemetery. We have been told but have not confirmed with the records that the last family to own this property before it became public land was the Brown family. That being much later than 1912 had to be a descendent of William H. Brown. Photo by WA. 10/27/20003.
