JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

A view looking west into the older section of the cemetery from near the east edge. These are the graves of the Stephensons', Dunlaps', Mitchells', Kerrs, Blairs', Caskeys' and others.

These stones are leaning badly but still standing. They remain unfallen due to having bases that extend sometimes as far as 4 feet into the ground. Photo by WA. 10/11/20003.
