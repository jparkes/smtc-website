JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

A view looking north from the south west corner of the Jackson College Cemetery at the first three rows of memorials and dense brush of this cemetery even after a days effort at clearing it. Most of the stones from this view are actually just the bases of sometimes very large and imposing monuments. This is because most of the stones are toppled from vandals and years of neglect. Since this photo was taken I understand the brush has been reduced to a level compatible with a well cared for cemetery by the Sons of Confederate Veterans and the Boy Scouts of America. Many thanks are in order to them.  Photo by WA. 10/11/20003.
