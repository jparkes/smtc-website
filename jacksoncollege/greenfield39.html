JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL


 
 

GREENFIELD, Infant T. S., died 31 Mar 1831. Son, Dr. G. T. & Keren T.
"Here lies the infant body of T. S. Greenfield who died 31 Mar 1831. The son of Keren T. and Dr. G. T. Greenfield. Innocence and virtue belong in Heaven."  The photo on the bottom is the tomb after it was set back up by Wayne Austin. Previously Jack Taylor and his helpers had cleaned and leveled the base.
