JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL


JOHNSON, W. I. L., 4 Apr 1834 - 3 Jan 1862. Inscription: "In memory of W. I. L. Johnson who was born Apr 4th 1834 and departed this life Jan 3rd 1862. My youthful friends as you pass by remember me as you are now so once was I. But as I am now you soon may be so prepare for death and follow me. I hope we will meet where death will be no more. Remembered by Elizabeth J. Johnson. Now I guess this stone will fuel a debate concerning what the relationship of W. I. L. was to Elizabeth J. and rightly so. Off in the distance notice part of the restoration crew taking a break after a long days work of building the new fence and clearing the brush. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 11-22-03.
