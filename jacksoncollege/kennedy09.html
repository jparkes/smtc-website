KENNEDY,Nancy McDonald, was born about 1787 and died 8 August 1858. She is buried at the 
historic Jackson College Cemetery. She was the 2nd wife of John Kennedy and mother of 12 children and 
step-mother of the 7 children born to John's first wife, Barbara Gordon. When she married John in 1805, 
the oldest child was 12. Wanda Kilgore supplied the first photo in about 1992. The tombstone was standing 
against a tree and the inscription was very faint. Inscription reads: "In Memory of 
Nancy Kennedy Who Died Aug 13th 1858 In the 73 Year of Her Age".  Information and photo 
supplied by Rick Gray, 1/8/02003.  New and improved photo May 2003 by Rick Gray
