JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL

DUNLAP, Sarah, 15 Mar 1792 - 10 Oct 1861. (69-6-25). Consort of Samuel Dunlap. (formerly; Sarah Stephenson.) "Sacred to the memory of Sarah Dunlap, Consort of Samuel Dunlap, Born March 15. 1792 And departed this life October 10, 1861. Aged 69 years 6 months and 25 days. Blessed are the dead which die in the Lord from hence forth. Yea saith the Spirit that they may rest from their labours; and their works do follow them." It is known one son Thomas Franklin Dunlap is buried in the Brick Church Cemetery in Giles County TN where he died in 1899. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 10-11-03.
