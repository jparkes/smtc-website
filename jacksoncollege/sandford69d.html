JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL


 

SANDFORD, Mary Elizabeth Yancey, 16 Apr 1832 - 15 Feb 1834. "In memory of Mary Elizabeth the daughter of Robert J. Sandford & Elizabeth T. Sandford whose birth was April
the 16th 1832 and departed this life on February 15th 1834. Aged 1 year & 10  months."
Robert J. was the son of Colonel James Turner Sandford.
