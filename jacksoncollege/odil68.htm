ODIL, J. C. (John C.), (13 Apr) 1825 - (1 Mar) 1904, "Father". (Son John & Mary Kerr Odil; Co. C, 19th Tenn. Cav., C.S.A. 
and
ODIL, Emily J., (27 Jan) 1829 - 1912, "Mother". Emily Stephenson married John Calvin Odil on 9 Aug 1858 in 
Williamson County Tenn..)  John and Emily lived near Mallard's Chapel. They were members of the Blanton's Chapel Methodist Church. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 10-11-03. Relationships from MCTC. This stone was set up by the Sons of the Confederate Veterans. It was at one time on the ground. Columbia Chapter.
