JACKSON COLLEGE/OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY-DENNING ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

A view looking east into the older section of the cemetery from near the center of the cemetery at the stones of the Mitchell family. That little short footstone in front of you has the initials W.
