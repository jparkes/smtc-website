JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL

 
BROWN, William Hugh, 1843 - 1912. (C. S. A.) A large decorative wrought iron fence is surrounding this stone but there is no other stones inside. This is in the southwest corner area of the cemetery. I was told by locals that Mr. Brown's at one time owned the property surrounding the cemetery. The property passed to UT Ag center sometime later.
