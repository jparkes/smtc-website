JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL


 

SANDFORD, George Washington Lafayett. "In memory of George Washington Lafayett Sandford, the son of Robert J. Sandford & Elizabeth T. Sandford whose birth was January
the 13th 1828 and departed this life on March the 16th 1833. Aged 5 years & 2  months. Suffer little children To come unto me. And forbid them for such is the Kingdom of heaven."
