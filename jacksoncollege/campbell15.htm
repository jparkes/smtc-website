JACKSON COLLEGE CEMETERY-MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE-SPRING HILL


CAMPBELL, Mrs. Martha, 1 Dec 1784 - 21 Mar 1856. (72-3-20), (Wife of Robert interred next to her to our right.) "Member of the Presbyterian Church." Today 10/11/03 this box tomb is the only one standing of the five box tombs of the Campbell family. It is out of balance and close to collapse though. Photo & info by Wayne Austin 10-11-03.
